    I was just visiting the Dream World for the Mamoswine event, and I had an idea for how to deal with it. It'll hopefully work, since we aren't Doing In the Wizard completely.

    It's a alternate plane of existence somehow influenced by the neural activity of sleeping Pokemon in the normal world, similar to a hybrid of the Distortion World and the plane of Dal Quor from Eberron.     The plane is inhabited, unlike the Distortion World. However, there only seem to be Pokemon, and only those of strains that are normally incredibly rare. These Pokemon are known to cross over to the normal world through a forest in Unova known as the Entralink, but it's not known exactly how it's accomplished.
