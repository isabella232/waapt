    Ditto Line

    Morphs [Oak Catalog #]

    • Ditto [132]

    Notable Biology

    Ditto are small, amorphous Pokemon that look like purple blobs with two small eyes, a mouth, and two stubs that resemble arms. There are rare Ditto that are blue instead, and are prized by collectors. Their most famous trait is the ability to alter their cellular structure to become any species of Pokemon, as well as inanimate objects and even humans. This transformation is complete down to the genitals, allowing Ditto to breed with just about any living thing. As a side effect, Ditto probably has the greatest libido out of any Pokemon.

    Though their transformation ability is normally flawless, Ditto tend to need to see their subject of else they will start forgetting features, often putting their faces in place of the Pokemon they were trying to mimic. They can be knocked out of their form by laughter or by being defeated, though there are some Ditto that can keep it up for longer. [[Trainer's Note: There is a rare mutation in which a Ditto is born without the ability to change in size when they transform, or they grow too much. These Ditto are prized even more than shinies.]] Ditto also have a strange habit of transforming into other Ditto when they meet each other in combat, only to stop when they realize what they're dealing with.

    Habitat

    Ditto are often found in the outskirts of cities, caves, and forests within Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, and Sinnoh, though they are normally never very far from people. People rarely see them due to Ditto disguising themselves as rocks or other such objects to protect themselves from predators.

    Diet

    Theoretically, Ditto's Transform would allow it to pretty much eat whatever it felt like. However, they tend to prefer to eat smaller Pokemon, transforming into their target to beat it into submission before eating it by expanding their mouths and digesting their prey inside their stomachs.

    Hazards

    Ditto are a very shy species, and prefer to keep away from conflict. However, if faced with a threat, they will transform into their aggressor and do battle. Due to their unique body structure, they are incapable of being paralyzed, and even in their normal form they are quite resistant to physical attacks, though they can't fight back due to said shape.

    Courting and Childrearing

    When Ditto want to mate, two parent Ditto absorb each other and exchange genetic information through transposons before separating into both parents and a new Ditto. The three then go on their separate ways.

    Due to Ditto's special ability, they are used in Pokemon breeding to breed with just about any Pokemon, and are most often caught for this express purpose. Folklore tells about Ditto impregnating or being impregnated by humans and having children together. This means that a small minority of humans may actually be part Ditto, though it would be hard to pick up. It may even still happen today, though kept under wraps.

    Social Structure

    Ditto tend to be loners, though they are not averse to groups.
