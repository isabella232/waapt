    Sorry for changing the subject but I had an idea and want to get it down before I forget. I am not sure how much we want to get into history but this idea builds off the idea that the four move limit is an artificial limit. Before this limit was put in place, tournaments were dominated by the upper class. They could afford to use every valid TM on each of their Pokemon and have a move for anything their opponent could throw at them. In order to make things fair, move sets are limited to 4 so that those who can afford all the T Ms don't have an unfair advantage. I was thinking of calling this time the Magnate era because the Champion at the time owned the TM company.
