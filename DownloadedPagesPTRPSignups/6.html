    Trainer Sign-up:
    • Name: Rine Akimiro
    • Gender: Male
    • Age: 14

However, that does not mean he is stupid - he hardly gives up without a battle and develops strategies for each fight, more often than not on the middle of the battle. In short, overly impulsive and 'wild'.


    Pokémon Sign-up:
    • Name: Kyou
    • Species: Combusken
    • Gender: Male
    • Personality/Bio: His appearance is of a common Combusken, except that he uses a white headband and a red wristband.
    • Background: Rine's first Pokemon, which he caught with a friend's help when he was 8 years old. Since then, they have been developping a strong bond, since Kyou shares a lot of traits with his trainer - they're both overly stubborn and impulsive, headstrong and somewhat reckless. The only difference is that Kyou is not as tactical as Rine, leaving all the "complicated stuff" for him while he deals with his opponents.

    • Name: Ryou
    • Species: Monferno
    • Gender: Male
    • Personality/Bio: The fire on his tail is of medium size, and his blue mark is not bright at all.
    • Background: Rine's third Pokemon, who challenged Kyou after he and his trainer were lost on a forest while chasing a Gothita who stole something valuable from them. At some point after the battle, Ryou decided to join them by his own free will. Unlike Kyou, his fighting style is more tactical based, trying to find an opening more than making one by himself and not taking much risks. Oddly enough, he is considered too serious for a Chimchar/Monferno, acting too rationally.

    • Name: Celes (whoever notices the pun gets a cake)
    • Species: Gothorita
    • Gender: Female
    • Personality/Bio: Her appearance is not that different from an ordinary Gothorita.
    • Personality: Basically, a Jerkass with a Heart of Gold. A street smart who apparently only cares about her own profit, not hesitating to use any kind of trick to leave with the upper hand. On the other hand, she tries to do everything for what she values the most. Whether it is a good or a bad thing, the opinions vary.
    • Background: Rine's second Pokemon, who stole something valuable from the team when she was a Gothita. They followed her into a forest, managing to make Rine lose track of her and go deep into the maze-like woods. After his only Pokemon had fought Ryou and other wild ones, the group was too tired to defend themselves. Noticing they were in danger, she helped them trick the wild Pokemons chasing them, and hide somewhere safe. Thus, they started to develop a bond.

    • Name:Sanryu)

    • Gender: Male
    • Personality/Bio: He is the oldest member of the team. Probably the oldest. His abilities suffer from this, but his experience counts a lot.
    • Personality: Probably the most bossy member of the team, he often picks on everyone for their mistakes, which is quite annoying for those who don’t know him. On the other hand, he is always trying to help them improve, sharing tips and helping with training in general, and cares the most about their performance. Acts like a leader, but in fact, he is too overconfident and sometimes lets important details to get by him.
