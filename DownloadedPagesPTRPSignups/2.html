    • Name: Geoff Thompson

    • Personality/Bio: Geoff's always been kind of a simple guy. He wants to see the sights, make some friends, goof around with said friends, and make magical monster pets beat the hell out of each other. He doesn't have any grand ambitions or overarching goals, he just wants to get out there and see all there is to see. He's an adrenaline junky if there ever was one, leaping headfirst into situations that would give most others pause and generally making an ass of himself for his amusement. He's rather flighty as well, though he'd rather you not use that word to describe him. e tends to get bored rather easily, and there are very few things on this Earth that can maintain his attention for too long.
      • Geoff's led a relatively easy life so far, living with his dad in the podunk island town of Dewford. He never had much company growing up. His father, a deep sea fisherman, was usually away at work and his mother had passed away shortly after he was born. A surfing accident, he'd been told. Most of the guys who hung around Dewford Hall avoided him like the plague, and his older brother of twenty six years had long since left on his own journey as an aspiring wildlife photographer. Even when he grew old enough to leave the island, his father forbade him. He had already lost a loved one, he had no desire to risk losing another. It was a dangerous world out there for someone his age. "When you're older", he'd told him before heading out to see again. And yet as the years past and Geoff grew into an impulsive young man, it seemed that day would never come. Eventually, when his fifteenth birthday rolled around ad the sun began to set over the sea, his father took him out to the docks, and handed him three things. A pokeball, a ferry ticket, and a letter, to be opened later. With a short exchange of words and a bone crushing hug, Geoff was off to explore the land of distant Unova, and to experience all the world of Pokemon had to offer him.
