    26119 rmctagg097th Sep 2015 05:47:24 PM from Brooklyn, NY Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I won't say I'm in love
    The Wanderer
    Okay, due to a minor argument in Creative last night, time to open a post on the subject of the Gates, which have been around for nearly a year now.

    So what's a Gate?

    The Gates are among the most powerful of Auric techniques accessible by humans or mons.

    Aura runs in channels throughout the body, but is normally partially blocked so that the body doesn't destroy itself. Through opening these gates in a controlled manner, however, the mind is cleared and the Auric in question gains greater control of their body.

    Normally, the Gates are just a path to greater enlightenment, but they can also be used for offensive purposes. The locks in question are known (At least by Aurics living in the South Asia analogue) as Sahasrara [Crown], Ajna [Third Eye], Vishuddha [Throat], Anahata [Heart], Manipura [Navel], Swadhisthana [Tailbone], and Muladhara [Pelvis], and each deal with certain aspects of the mind, body and soul. A Gated form is created when two or more of these locks are opened at once, with the final fourth form having all seven opened.

    The Gated Forms

    First Form: A general increase of power and strength akin to the boost granted by an Aura Nexus. Increased mobility and speed, sharper mind and stronger attacks with occasional manifestations. (ie, Constantin's arm constructs)

    Second Form: Manifestations become more common, and more advanced moves normally limited to Pokmon are usable by humans. At this point it becomes harder to maintain control while in this state.

    Third Form: At this point things start getting really intense. Things such as advance reflect barriers, variations of more elemental attacks, mass mind control and long distance teleportation (Probably something akin to how Xatu can do intercontinental teleportation) are possible for humans.

    Fourth Form: Absolutely devastating. Even just entering this form can cause collateral damage to your immediate surroundings. In humans, this allows them to perform great feats only thought to be done by very powerful Pokemon such as devastating forms of Hyper Beam and Giga Impact or slight forms of reality warping. (Like say, bending space around them due to the sheer amount of power emitted) In Pokmon its been known to put their natural power levels on the platform of some minor legendaries. Seldom few have achieved to this state due to how dangerous it is.

    How to Activate A Gated Form

    There are two known ways to activate the Gates. The first is through meditation, and the second is to brute force open the gates through overwhelming power. The first is smooth and controlled, the other is very damaging even in the first form.

    This Sounds Super OP lol

    Well, perhaps at first glance, but the Gates have their own (quite major) drawbacks.

    For one, to use the Gates requires either a raw power level or a psychological profile most Aurics lack. (Probably the only Auric on the J-Team besides RP!Tagg who'd be capable of using the Gates with training would be Duke, since it's an outright Enlightenment Superpower when used properly and nearly everyone else lacks the discipline to do so. Maybe RP!Umbra and Lina as well, but I'd lean more towards Lina.) For example, Sol in particular couldn't use the Gates since despite her high amounts of raw power she lacked the discipline, concentration, and mettle to even pull off the first form.

    So honestly, it would be plain impractical for most Aurics to use as it requires a total rigidity of resolve that most people don't have, period.

    Also, to use the Gates takes a major toll on the body and mind even in the first form if used for too long, using the third in particular can cause either permanent power loss in humans or a massive power decrease in mons, and the fourth gate will outright kill you if used save for a miracle or outright divine intervention.

    Constantin for instance only has 11 years left to live anyway due to his overuse of the Gates, and in Salvador's timeline only lived that long after using the fourth gate due to intervention (Plus he lost his powers permanently), so spamming it is a very bad idea.

    Finally, the Gates are considered rather esoteric knowledge, so most people don't even know it exists, making the chances of running into a Auric with those abilities incredibly low.

    Ultimately, however, the Gates are not meant as a mere powerup, but instead are only properly achieved through great understanding of self and a lot of meditation. It's meant more as mental harmony and discipline to the point of being able to control how your own body functions and thus being able to break its own limits, hence why it tends to be so destructive to the user if overused.
