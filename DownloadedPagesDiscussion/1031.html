Multiple weeks of binging Tumblr nonstop beause I've had nothing better to do while I'm so sick had caused all those things to make me more and more depressed and uncomfortable. And the process of liking and reblogging became more of a weird compulsion than something I willingly wanted to do.

Please don't PM or IM any Tumblr links to me until I say I'm ready, and when I get back in the chat (AKA when I'm feeling less horribly sick and unwilling to do anything) don't expect me to look at any links to Tumblr. Thank you.

    I'm not sure how long the hiatus will be. Probably anything from a few weeks to a few months. Probably when the Minions hate dies down by quite a bit, since that's what initially triggered this.

    25769 ConflictedPsyches12th Jul 2015 07:21:03 PM from The Depths of Despair Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: In Lesbians with you
    High Priestess of Fabulous
    So, I had an idea for two new characters that I may or may not start writing for in the future.

    Simone and Sylvia Pascal are twin sisters, age 25, and graduates of Lumiose University. They come from a family of so called "practical members of society" that would like nothing more for Simone to be an engineer or doctor, and Sylvia to be a teacher or nurse. Simone and Sylvia are both very vehemently against their parents' wishes, but for vastly different reasons.

    Simone is against her family's wishes because she is a trans girl, and her family is pushing her down a male dominated career path in hopes that it will "fix" her, while she'd rather work with people and help the world in a more humanistic way. Sylvia is against her family's wishes because she is not patient enough to deal with people's stupidity on a daily basis, and hopes to find a career path where she can interact with other intelligent (read: Pretentious) people like herself.

    Simone Pascal is a soft spoken, cheery young philosophy major who constantly eggs people on to "find themselves" and "follow their dreams". She's very much an idealist with an eye for trying to change the world for the better. She cares very deeply about her friends, and though she doesn't always see eye to eye with her family, she truly does love them.

    Her sister Sylvia, on the other hand, is a loud, aggressively pretentious young literature major who scoffs at her younger sister for many, many reasons. Sylvia is very much a pessimist that envisions the world as a story, where everything's written in stone, and the writer is a cruel master who gives us glimpses of hope to make it harder for the reader when it's taken away.

    So, enough about the boring fleshy humans, let's talk about their mons, the whole reason we came to this RP. :P

    Simone's team:

    • Nietzsche the Honchcrow: Nietzsche is a snarky bird, constantly contradicting the other mons and just being a general troll, but in a lovable way. He really wants the rest of the team to think more carefully about their lives. Except Descartes. That guy talks too much.

    • Descartes the Slowking: Descartes is a mon of many words. Many. Many. Words. He's much more likely to just sit and talk the opponent to boredom than fight.

    • Hegel the Audino: Hegel the Audino is a caring, compassionate mon, that strives to help everymon reach their full potential for the good of the bright shining future!

    • Sartre the Eevee: Sartre is a happy go lucky little Eevee that seeks nothing more than to be able to make his own destiny. He refuses to evolve until he decides which evolution is right for him.

    • Marx the Emboar: MARX WILL FREE THE OPPONENT FROM THE TYRANNY OF THE BOURGEOISIE BY PILEDRIVING THEM INTO THE GROUND.

    • Poe the Gengar: The Odd One Out, Poe is very much like his namesake, eccentric, brilliant and spooky~ He has a tendency to not speak much, but when he does, the people who hear him are usually left more confused or weirded out than they started.

    Sylvia's team:

    • Shelley the Electibuzz: Shelley is a inquisitive young mon, always trying to find more out about the world. She is deathly afraid of fire and labratories, after an incident in her youth.

    • Carroll the Male Meowstic: Carroll is by definition a Troll. Even when he's not spouting pure gibberish, he's actively trying to confuse anyone around by being cryptic, warping perceptions, and vanishing.

    • Melville the White Wailmer: Melville is silent. Melville is merciless. Melville feels no pain or suffering. Melville just is.

    • Hawthorne the Ditto: Hawthorne is a pontificating windbag. He constantly berates the team for various things and claims that whatever they can do, he can do better.

    • Wilde the Banette: Wilde is a very eccentric mon. He's the partner in crime to Carroll, but while Carroll prefers to confuse mons, he prefers to take a more proactive unnerving of people, sometimes actively scaring them.

    • Sun Tzu the Alakazam: The Odd One Out, Sun Tzu, like his namesake cares only for the Art of War. He enjoys any sort of contest and treats everything like a battle or contest.
