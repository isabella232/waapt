    Home Region/Town: Lilycove Hoenn

    Personality: Shy and quiet usually, but fun loving and adventurous. She his almost overly caring, not being able to turn herself away if she so much as thinks as someone may have an issue. She enjoys learning and exploring, her dream being to see every Pokemon in the world. In stressful and sometimes dangerous situations, she prefers to hide any feelings that could be cause for concern under light joking and impassive cheerfulness. All her odd quirks aside, her most often seen disposition is that of a quiet, happily content young woman who just loves life as a whole.
