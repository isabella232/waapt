    Pokesona Start Date

    We now have a solid start date or at least a start by date. Pokesona MUST start by Saturday, February 6th. This is due to Verax notifying me about a trip he's taking.

    That said, the starting event will consist of Philemon successfully a few members of the J-Team to the Velvet Room in their sleep. Those summoned that agree to help Hoopa find and subdue Izanami will be granted a Starter Pokesona - or in Mendel and Lyuri's case have their Starter Persona unlocked for them.

    From there We can do one of two things...

    1) Do a "tutorial battle" With some weak Shadow enemies

    or 2) Proceed directly to when Philemon gives each of the group a Contractor's Key◊ - which allows the group to access the Velvet Room's Portal Doors from the real/physical/waking world - and sends them back and waking them up.

    Pokesona Plot Flow

    !!!ALERT!!!Remember, the group is physically going to the UC! This means that if our PCs can get tired, or get hurt, it will carry over to the real world. But because Time flows differently in the CU completing a Dungeons and events in the CU can take up to 16 Hours IRP.!!!Alert!!!

    Part I - Accepting You Have Issues: Gaining Unique Personas and Unique Pokesonas

    The first part will essentially be group utilizing the Velvet Room to travel to the CU and helping traverse each other's dungeons.
    • The group (or at least some of the group) will travel to a dungeon.
      • To have PCs traverse a Dungeon you will need four PCs including:
        • The PC whose dungeon will be explored
        • At least ONE other PC not controlled by a Plotrunner
        • At least ONE of the plotrunners' PCs in the group
        • At least ONE of the Support Persona users (i.e. Mendel and/or Lyuri)
        • Be willing to GM most of your PC's dungeon - although Plotrunners can help with mook battles.
    • At the end of each dungeon a member of the group will confront their Shadow and reject them, resulting in a Boss Battle against the Berzerked Shadow Self
    • After the these boss battles, the PC needs to accept the Shadow Self.
    • The Group will have the option of returning to the Real World at the end of a dungeon
    This process repeats until all members of the group have their Unique Pokesonas or Unique Personas.

    Part II - Facing and Overcoming Your Issues: The Guardians and Evolved Pokesonas and Evolved Personas

    Part II of Pokesona involves the Group facing trials (read, "traps") set by Izanami's Guardians.
    • The Group enters the CU
    • The group encounters rather elaborate trial scenarios that are designed to prevent the group from Progressing
    • However, each scenario can be broken by at least one member of the group by facing issues and overcoming them.
    • When a member successfully overcomes a Guardian's Scenario, their Pokesona or Persona change in to its "Evolved" stage
    • After a few times of breaking their Trials, a Guardian will personally come to deal with the group.
    • The group defeats the Guardian that confronts them (this can also count as a trial)
    This cycle repeats until Kunio-Sagiri, Hi-no-Kagutsuchi, Mikutama-no-Kami, Kusumi-no-Okami are defeated.

    Note: Remember how I said be prepared to give feedback before? This is where that comes in. In order for the scenarios to work, each participant needs to PM at least one of the Plotrunners a scenario that will for their PC(s) to face their issues and force them to at least start overcoming them. The plotrunners will them GM the scenarios

    !!!ALERT!!!Writers who have meta-aware characters that use their meta-awareness to overcome or escape trial scenarios that aren't targeted at them WILL be asked to edit the offending post(s). This part of the plot is meant to give each of the PCs involved a moment of glory.!!!ALERT!!!

    Part 3 - Moving Forward While Facing The Truth: Endgame

    • The fog that was occurring in the CU will start occurring in the real world, although it will be isolated to a single area Sinnoh - for the moment.
    • The group will got the Velvet Room and hear EXPOSITION!
    • This exposition will prompt the group the enter the CU to ind the source of the fog.
    • The group enters traverses Magatsu Mandala
    • The fights a boss battle against a horde of Reapers and Edward Thames with his persona, Magatsu Izanagi
    • The Group fights the Final Boss
    • The group a break before realizing the fog ISN'T going away
    • The group is lead to where they face down Izanami herself.
    • The group boss battle with Iznami in her Battle Form, and her "Thousand Curses" attack results in the groups Personas and Pokesonas reaching their "Ultimate" stage.
    • The final battle against Iznami in her true form where the group can use their Ultimate Persona's or Ultimate Pokesona's Signature attack.

    Note: Specifics on certain events will be PM'd to writers as needed.

    Final NPC Casting Call

    There are still several opening for NPC controllers. So if you are interested in being a part of Pokesona in some fashion, please contact any of the plotrunners.
