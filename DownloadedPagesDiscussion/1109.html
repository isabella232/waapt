    OK, so bad news and good news on the wild, untamed, Tumblr frontier.

    Bad News

    OK, so I was binging someone else's Tumblr, namely Bitters', because I saw she reblogged Audrey's Robin pic but was unable to get a permalink and wanted to see if there were any silly tags or comments or anything, so eventually started sifting through until I found it (sadly there weren't any comments or tags of the sort), and while I saw plenty of silly things I wound up reblogged I did also stumble upon one seemingly-innocuous thing that wound of setting off a pretty bad anxiety attack (Bitters this is not your fault KEEP READING). Given A. this is hardly the first time something like this has happened binging someone else's Tumblr, and B. I've been avoiding my actual dashboard like hell since last July for related reasons, I figured I'd set my Tumblr Anxiety Trigger Avoidance Rules in stone here:

    • If you reblog anything related to me or my characters, please send me a permalink via Pidgin IM, TVT PM, Tumblr IM, whatever. Or tag me in your reblog (@umbrahighpriestofgiratina or @umbramatic). If I notice you haven't done so in the notes I'll message you.

    • As for other posts, I've been mostly looking at and liking/reblogging more innocuous/fun/silly things other people link me rather than checking my dashboard since last July, and would prefer to keep doing so, since browsing other people's blogs has an unfortunate chance to set me off really badly. As for what's OK and what isn't to link me to...

      • Anything going pretty deep/heavy into the sociopolitical/social justice side of things has a high tendency to set me off for a very wide variety of reasons, so just try to avoid that stuff period; I have a genral policy of never reblogging anything SP/SJ anymore anyway.

      • On a more general note, anything involving bashing/mocking/demeaning/etc. of LITERALLY ANYTHING has a tendency to set me off (the thing that set me off tonight was actually tongue-in-cheek, but it had the misfortune of tying into a subject that REALLY sets me off in the malice department) thanks to both anxiety and what I've grown to believe about the nature of hate, so keep whatever you send me more lighthearted and good-spirited in tone. tl;dr if you just throw me things heavily lacking in toxicity/mean-spiritedness/general negativity you should be good. I happen to generally avoid reblogging that stuff too.

    Sorry I have to be like this, but my anxiety's bad enough I have multiple panic attacks a day, and they often get bad enough that I sink into very deep emotional lows and/or feel actual physical pain in the form of gut cramps, so I'm forced to minimize exposure to what feeds into that.

    On a much more positive note...
