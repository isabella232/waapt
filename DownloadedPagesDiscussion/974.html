    24340 keys2tkingdom11th Dec 2014 04:18:31 PM from a world of my own Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I LOVE THIS DOCTOR!
    I am thou. . . Thou art I. . .
    I would not object to Jason "earning" P->H Armband. So long as we get a general layout/ a statement of how Jason is to "earn" said armband.

    Example

    Here's an example of a PC earning a significant Powerup (In this case the powerup - metawise - isn't on the same scale as an armband although the powerup was most likely every bit as rare):

    When I wanted Ren's former bad leg to be healed so he would have normal mobility, I came up with the Peach Pills. The idea of the Peacha Pills itself fit in with the running Okamiquest plot at the time - as the Peacha Pills are a part of Japanese mythology. With that in mind, I then had Sho(my Okamiquest/Conquest PC) earn them as a sort of "consolation prize" for recovering the Tundra Beads for Ammy. Then, with RL!Dune's help, I was able to show them off at full effectiveness then hide them is a place for Ren to claim later. Once Ren got a hold of them the pills effectiveness had diminished drastically - as the effectiveness of most medicines are wont to due over a long period of time - which in turn failed to heal him as Ren had hoped, so Ren stowed them away because the weren't useful at the moment. The pay off for the Peacha Pills didn't happen until after Ren endured a week of torture and interrogation as a captive of Cipher and escaped; but due to the combination of recently sustained injuries, a lack of a proper diet and rest, an adrenaline crash, and passing out in a cold rain storm would have put Ren in the Hospital for a VERY long time - if RP!Dune hadn't feed Ren the entirety of the partially empty bottle of Pecha Pills and thereby causing all Ren's recent injuries to heal and the bones in Ren's left leg to be restored.

    Again, this is only for an example, but I basically pitched the above plot line since I don't remember the exact wording and I'm too lazy to dig through hundreds of pages of archived posts and conversations to find out exactly what I said in the following bullet points:

    • Sho gets the pills.
    • Sho hides the Pills
    • Ren gets the pills
    • Ren finds out that the pills don't work
    • Ren get captured by Cipher and escapes with injuries
    • [Another PC (which turned out to be RP!Dune)] feeds Ren all the pills in the bottle and heals him

    While this idea was approved, I had to agree to show that Ren - as a more public figure - had to taken extra preventive measures to ensure things were not found out "officially" (since the TV show in the RP makes almost everything known unofficially) by the general public. I showed this by Ren continuing to carry a cane despite not needing one and forging false x-rays and ensuring Ren's former private physician never would talk about Ren's restored leg via post-hypnotic suggestion until Ren had Copperfield lift the implanted suggestion. The latter was to show that Ren has become more jaded since he had a rather idealistic view of morality when he first appeared in the RP.

    End of Example

    Anyway, I hope that anecdote helped give you a few ideas and helped establish my position on this matter.
