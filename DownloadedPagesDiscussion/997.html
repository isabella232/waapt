    Okay, so, at the moment, we've got 6 potential newbies who have expressed interest for when Signups open again. Assuming the number keeps growing, this will probably result in a problem if we just let everyone who asked sign up at once.

    So, in hopes of making it more manageable, how about we implement a queue?

    Here are my suggestions of how it would work:

    - When someone expresses interest in the Signup/Discussion/RP Chat, they are added to the end of the queue

    - When we think we can handle more people (maybe deciding at the start of each new arc?), we PM the first three people in the queue to say they can post a signup in the thread

    - If any people don't respond within a certain time period (either 3 days or a week or something), then we assume they've lost interest and ask the next person/people in the queue

    - If a person isn't able to respond to a PM soon enough due to extenuating circumstances, I wouldn't mind letting them sign up, though

    And one thing I'm unsure on:

    -Since we didn't put this queue system into effect before, I'm wondering whether we should do two waves of signups spaced a week or two apart because we told the people who already expressed interest they could join at the end of the arc and just tell the next group to wait or if we should have half of them wait.

    Thoughts/Opinions?
