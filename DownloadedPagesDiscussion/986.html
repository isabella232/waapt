    24650 Umbramatic10th Jan 2015 11:06:36 PM from WAAPT or PEFE Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: In another castle
    The eternal dusk returns
    So... I've been thinking. And I've decided after I finish my time in WAAPT I'm also giving up on writing completely. As well as anything else creative, for that matter.

    Why?

    It started with the decline of hand-drawn animation in America. The aesthetic of late 90's-early 2000s hand-drawn animated movies is one of my favorite art aesthetics ever, and for more than ten years I've had to watch the art form die and have my hope spots for it surviving crushed because they weren't profitable enough. That is pretty much half the reason I have no faith in pure capitalism anymore.

    For years I convinced myself I could work my way up to a point where I could do something about it, or at least improve my own art style so I could keep the aesthetic alive somehow and maybe even hybridize it with another art style I'm interested in like animesqe stuff. (Though the amount of unnecessary hate anime gets as both a medium and an art style could get a rant of its own.) But tonight I realized:

    I fucking can't.

    My art is shit. Pure unadulterated shit no matter how much I practice so over the past year I gave up trying. Even if I didn't, at the rate I've been "improving" I wouldn't be able to make the kind of art I wanted until I'm 90, no matter what art style I picked. And even if I somehow managed to learn how to draw or animate the old-fashioned way, who would hire me?

    I can't even do the CGI stuff, since I can't program worth a shit. The one programming class I took in high school I only managed to scrape a B because I cheated on one of the tests. This also prevents me from doing video games, but really, what business do I have doing those when I don't beat 90% of games I own due to getting stuck and giving up? Jobs in the video game industry are shit, anyway.

    The only thing I have left is writing, and according to the contest judges at Serebii I'm apparently not good at that either. I can't even finish long projects anyway, considering I have the attention span of a small rodent. How am I ever gonna write a novel like that?

    So yeah. WAAPT is my last hurrah for anything creative. I dunno what I'll do with my life afterward. Maybe work with animals. Or, you know, do shitty job training jobs for the rest of my life. Because according to the job testing coach who I'm pretty sure hated me, competitive employment just ain't my thing. Plus I'm going to community college, and who gives a guy with a community college degree a good job?

    But I need sleep. See you all tomorrow, I guess.
