    And now for some things of my own:

    A beta reader is you!

    Good news! The evil plan worked and the first draft of my latest contest entry is done! While it did unfortunately have the predicted side effect of a small decrease in my sanity (Read: Increasing my already-really-bad anxiety levels noticeably), and I'm likely still avoiding the chat at least until that dies down, the project itself was easily the most fun I've had ever writing one of these contest entries, and now the actual writing's done sooner than expected, I can finally more fully devote myself to properly finishing my WAAPT experience - hopefully starting tonight with TSing some silly shenanigans from over a week ago, the resolution of which will hopefully help re-kickstart a subplot I've been neglecting for even longer.

    However, despite that, the contest entry's not quite ready for submission yet. While the most-distracting part - the writing of the first draft- is over, it's still just a first draft, which as a rule is always in dire need of revisions. And for revisions, I need beta readers, and that's where you guys come in - I want you guys, any of you guys, to be my beta readers!

    However, please dearly realize that reading the story and replying with one sentence saying "That was great!" or "That sucked!" does not count as beta reading. Please, do your best to give genuine constructive feedback on what works, what doesn't, and what needs improving.

    However, some prompts to help you with that:

    • Any suggestions for large expansions or overhauls are unwanted and will likely be ignored if given for the time being, mainly for one particular reason: This contest has a wordcount cap of 10,000, and... this wound up being not only my longest contest entry yet, but the longest oneshot I've ever written and possibly the longest short story I've ever written, to the point it's actually teetering on the edge of passing that cap, making such things unfeasible. By contrast suggestions for cutting or replacing scenes will be taken far more seriously.

    • For you more grammatically-minded beta readers anything in that category helps, but especially proper comma usage or lack thereof, and slipping into present tense instead of past tense, since that's what tends to get me the most.

    • There are "placeholder" bits [MARKED LIKE THIS] in a few places. What I need is suggestions on what to fill them in with.

    • Similarly, sentences marked with a * at the beginning are ones I think might need tweaking, so feel free to ask me why.

    • Finally, this fic has a heavy emphasis on humor, so I'd like to know if said humor works or not. If you feel it does, tell me the highlights!

    Feel free to PM or IM me your interest and I'll send you the relevant docs ASAP. As for what it's about... I'll just say it's a spinoff of two previous oneshots of mine.

    Anyway...

    Staying on the right path

    Now that the contest entry's mostly out of the way... With all the distractions to finishing my run in WAAPT that've happened the past several months, along with my new deadline for doing so (I'm not telling you guys just yet, but it's not all that far away), from now on I am dedicating all my creative output to WAAPT first and foremost, with the remaining planning and prep for Heroes After All (for those of you who don't know a WAAPT spinoff fic of sorts that's my next big project after WAAPT itself) being the only real secondary thing. And I want you guys to enforce that; sure, I can talk to you guys about other projects all any of us want, but if I ever express any intent to actually start writing them down or whatever: STOP ME! Shoot it down, slap me in the face, just do anything, anything to convince me I've got bigger and far more overdue fish to fry. (As a reminder; I announced my retirement over a year ago, with the intention to be done by right about now. Yeah.) I FUCKING MEAN IT.

    There's a related thing I may D!Post about if people keep being annoying about it, but not now.

    Additionally, I'm planning on meeting with some of you guys in a private chat to discuss logistics of the rest of my endgame and possibly even plan out a schedule. I'm still deciding on when and exactly who, but it'll be soon and if I want you in on it I'll let you know.

    On a lighter note...

    Plush Of The Century

    As you hopefully remember, about a month ago I got myself a custom plush of one of my favorite characters from the Pokemon games themselves, Riley, both as a source of stress relief and for the sake of having a plushie of him. :P (He's still doing his stress-relief job very well.) However, a bit later I found out about Eskay's Alyssa plushie and got him to show me the guy who did it, since there happens to be one WAAPT character of mine I've wanted a plushie of for years but never had the means to obtain or even make myself. But now... It's finally a reality.

    As for which character? Well, see for yourself.

    She got delivered Monday, but decided to wait until now to show you all. I'm very happy with her. :3

    I might upload pics of my own later (mainly to show she's bigger than I expected) but this post took me three hours to write, partially due to the aforementioned anxiety) and I need a break to de-stress. Feel free to give thoughts and/or offer to beta read, though.
