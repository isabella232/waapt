    19063 HoobaDooba17th Feb 2014 12:20:03 PM from somewhere in California Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: One Is The Loneliest Number

    Alright, then here's my draft for my next character. I am aware that Im reusing a few elements from Aarons profile (the backstories for Suzu and Tytree are pretty much exactly the same, to the point where all I did was swap a few pokemon names out), but Aaron didnt get that much action so I dont really think that matters too much. Also, Im an unimaginative hack.

    Name: David Weiss

    Age: 23

    Appearance: David is on the short side of tall (510), with thick, black hair and bright blue eyes. He is in good shape, but is not extremely fit. He wears rather plain clothes, to a such a degree that someone very perceptive might think that hes trying very hard not to stand out.

    Organizational Ties: Is a student at Striaton U. Also makes routine anonymous donations to the Aura Users Rights Association (AURA, dohoho), an organization that aims to peacefully end discrimination against Aura users (think NAACP).

    Special abilities: Was born with active Aura potential. However, in his own words, its too weak to be of use, and strong enough to be a problem. In terms of visible manifestation, David can produce light and little else, being unable to enhance his own physical abilities. The main benefit of his gift lies in empathic abilities David can easily read the surface emotions of anyone in a large radius. However, he is unable to reliably shut this ability off, and goes through large periods of each day dealing with emotions that arent his own.

    Personality: While David tries to remain optimistic, a combination of his out-of-control powers, general anti-Aura sentiment in the world around him, the pressure of keeping his gift a secret, and some plain old bad luck has left him teetering on the brink of heavy cynicism (though not suicide, thankfully hes more of a Knight in Sour Armor). The main things keeping him from losing faith in the world are his family, friends, and pokemon team.

    Backstory: Grew up in suburban Nimbasa.

    Pokemon:

    Suzu (female Greninja) (Protean) (Dive Ball) David's first pokemon, hatched from an egg given to him on his 10th birthday an egg that his parents thought was a Panpour, but was obtained (legally) from a breeder who was also (illegally) breeding Kalos pokemon on the side (and mixed up the eggs). Suzu has been with David the longest, and is the one who he goes to when he starts to get cynical. She acts as the leader of the team, and is wise (but prone to hasty decisions). She serves as David's aquatic transport. Evolved into a Frogadier when David was 13, and evolved into a Greninja when David was 18.

    Is capable of reading human language, and loves reading historical fiction.

    Jude (male Lucario) (Justified) (Premier Ball) David's second pokemon, caught as a Riolu on a trip to Flocessy Ranch when David was 11. Jude is stubborn and headstrong, but still extremely loyal to his trainer. However, despite the loyalty (which he still appreciates), David has never totally shaken the feeling that Jude has his own reasons for sticking around after all, why would a Riolu that is clearly not an average wild one seek him out? Which is true, because Jude does have his own motives. As a Riolu, Jude sought out David due to his Aura abilities. However, unknown to anyone but Jude, this is what kickstarted the issues that David has with his own Aura Jude feels guilty, so he remains with his trainer; partly out of genuine loyalty and respect, and partly so that he can one day help fix the problem he created. Evolved into a Lucario when David was 15.

    Is really good at cheating at cards, and at gambling in general.

    Will gain the ability to mega-evolve at some point.

    Meredy (female Espeon) (Magic Bounce) (Heal Ball) David's third pokemon, caught as an Eevee at the Friend Safari when David was 13. Meredy is prim, proper, and somewhat vain. Shes also the friendliest of the team when it comes to dealing with other people and pokemon, and is popular with the children in David's neighborhood. Although it takes quite a bit to truly rile her up, Meredy also has the most vicious temper of the team, and is petty and vindictive when displaying it. Jude in particular is extremely wary of pissing her off, and will do whatever he can to calm her down. Evolved into an Espeon when David was 16.

    Absolutely loves horror movies, the cheaper and gorier the better. Who would have guessed?

    Eugene (male Gogoat) (Sap Sipper) (Nest Ball) David's fourth pokemon, caught near Camphrier about two days after Meredy. Eugene is calm and quiet, and will occasionally over-think things. He (as well as David) serves as the eternal straight man to the rest of the teams dumb antics, and comes across as somewhat humorless. He serves as David's land transport, and is especially adept at carrying people through rugged terrain. Evolved into a Gogoat when David was 17.

    He does not watch TV, because who in their right mind would bring a large goat into their living room?

    Anise (female Crobat) (Infiltrator) (Dusk Ball) David's fifth pokemon, caught as a Zubat on an 8th grade field trip to Castelia. Anise is friendly, boisterous, and eternally optimistic. She is most active at night, though she is perfectly fine with being out in the day (provided that shes wearing her sunglasses). She is David's aerial transport, and very much enjoys carrying people. Evolved into a Golbat when David was 16, and evolved into a Crobat two hours later.

    Loves watching nature documentaries.

    Ruca (male Quilava) (Blaze) (Luxury Ball) David's sixth pokemon, received as a gift from a family friend when David was 16. Ruca is shy, nervous, and has confidence issues in pretty much everything except battling, where he is oddly sure of himself. Along with Meredy, he has the worst temper of the team; however, where Meredys fury is intelligent and focused, Rucas is mindless and almost literally explosive the one time Ruca lost his temper, he reduced a large portion of a beach to glass. Evolved into a Quilava when David was 20, and is probably going to evolve into a Typhlosion soon.

    Loves sports, especially Ice Hockey. Go Nimbasa Sharpedoes!

    Any thoughts?
