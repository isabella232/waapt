      • Miyu is elegant, graceful, mature, humble and quite competent at what she does, she's also quite a casual person, though still very respectful towards others, she's also a bit quick witted and sometimes makes jokes at others expenses, though most of the time she doesn't mean any harm and would apologize almost immediatelly if she ended up offending someone.     • Bad Points: She can be a bit cryptic at times and it isn't unusual for her to act by herself and ending up complicating things.
    • Likes: Tea, social interaction, Pokmon, peace.
    • Dislikes: War, rude people.

    • Perfect Link: Growlithe Line.
