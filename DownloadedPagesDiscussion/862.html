    So, I had an idea for the upcoming Hoenn 2 arc.

    Basically, the weather in Hoenn has gone crazy, with hurricanes, droughts, abnormal temperatures, severe thunderstorms, and the like happening every so often. This is all because of the Weather Trio's sudden dormant state which I'm not sure what the cause is.

    Now, the Mini-trio notice the lack of their bigger counterparts, and attempt to fix the problems...and end up just causing the problems as well due to not being experienced or powerful enough to rebalance the weather.

    You may be asking yourself "But what about Magma and Aqua?" Well, since they're now environmental activists and may or may not be in Ozone, they'd start placing the blame for the weather on the other team. In Ozone's case, it'd cause the team to break up, and return to Magma/Aqua.

    The two soon begin having Political party-esque disputes, with rallies and protests being held to try and prove their cause/discredit the other side. Meanwhile, there'd be extremist groups that would opt to take actions similar to the actions of the original Magma/Aqua. Except no summoning of legends/cloning of legends. Just no.

    The J-Team's job would be to:

    • Figure out what happened to the Weather Trio
    • Wake up the Weather Trio, with my idea being the use of the Primal Evolution method, though that may require more details from ORAS.
    • Return balance to the weather and stop the fighting between Aqua and Magma.

    So, any thoughts/concerns? I had the idea of the J-Team learning of the weather issues via TV/Internet/whatever in their region prior to Hoenn 2.
