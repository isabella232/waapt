    17673 0blivionmobile14th Dec 2013 04:20:25 PM from Oooooooooooooooooooooklahoma~! Get RP Mod
    You Wouldn't Like Me When I'm Bored
    Well, the thing about bracelets is this: my logic was to create something that would allow people interested in long-term plans involving Pokmon-human transformations to have consistent, consolidated access to it for that one particular character while still providing interesting "conflicts" in the form of glitches.

    My reasoning behind "consistent" and "consolidated" is that armbands are extremely important items. In any circumstance, they are never given lightly, and always gravitate to certain characters by default. If someone wants to try and progress a story involving one, they need to ask and then receive one IRP. This can be difficult if those characters with one available are occupied.

    Also, as it has been stated ad nauseum, armbands are Artifacts with a capital A. They are rare, limited, and arcane. Bracelets are not restricted in the same manner, but the rather extreme application process as well as glitch-induced complications are also in place so as not to "cheapen" the value of a transformative trinket.
