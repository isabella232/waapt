    The eternal dusk returns
    OK, now for that idea...

    Holon is a very vague place, right? So, it wouldn't be too outlandish for there to be a small, isolated island somewhere just off the coast... Perhaps one inhabited by a civilization of Delta Golett line members...

    I think some of you know where I'm going with this... For those that don't, let me make it more obvious.

    I'm talking a...

    Holon BIONICLE Homage!

    Now, the idea is still vague, but I'm working some things out. Here's what I have so far...

    • Instead of a Whole Plot Reference, (because, let's face it, Bionicle's got quite the Kudzu Plot , at least if you get into the side stories) I'm gonna go for a homage that mainly focuses on tributes to the world and characters.

    • It will take place on a small island, but said island will have a MASSIVE ruin complex underneath to mirror the Matoran Universe (Though this time it's probably NOT the body of a giant robot.)

    • The Delta Golett and Golurk will draw inspiration from both Matoran/Toa and Agori/Glatorian. As such, they will have elements of both in their society, and will have have mixed mental genders instead of having females confined to one group (Though the water group will still have the most Fem!Letts and 'Lurks).

    • We also need expies for some of the races (Makuta, Skakdi, Vortixx, etc.), Rahi, and other monsters. Suggestions for any of those will be appreciated.

    • It will probably rely less on Always Chaotic Evil than Bionicle proper did, if only because I want to see what a nice Skakdi or something would be like, and it works better with our interpretation of the Pokeverse. (That, and I generally don't like Always Chaotic Evil .)

    • Naturally, Mata Nui will be involved heavily. :P Eskay's Delta Golurk NPC may get involved too, depends on what he thinks.

    Anyway, I'm looking for ways to expand this, as well as participants and people to assist G Ming (because this will be hard to run by myself, especially with the other subplots I have going on). I hope for this to be accessible to people who aren't familiar with the franchise, but if you still aren't interested that's understandable.

    Anyway... Questions, comments, and especially suggestions are all appreciated.
