    I am thou. . . Thou art I. . .
    With Pokesona about a month away, it's crunch time! With that said, here are some topics of importance!

    Plot Runners

    As it currently stands, the Primary Plot Runners are
    • Keys
    • Dune
    • Verax

    We are your GMs! We are here to help you, push your PCs to the limits, and hopefully provide a pleasant sub-arc for all who are participating!

    Secondary Plot Runners are
    • Crow

    Secondary Plot runners primary job will be to help facilitate group battles, "random encounters", etc, but also get to participate in private plot discussions and decision-making. My mind can support two or three more Secondary Plot Runners, so PM me if you are interested.

    Pokesona Applications

    As a reminder, the Pokesona Google Doc will be locked on January 31 before I go to bed. So if you are interested please have your application submitted by then.

    To be eligible to submit an application you must have joined WAAPT prior to September 31, 2015. This decision was made to ensure that newer members are not thrown directly into a plot that uses many elements that are not the norm for the RP (Unique status effects and humans having an active involvement in battles beyond issuing commands, to name the most prominent).

    Again, while joining WAAPT after September 31, 2015 does exclude your PCs from participating, it does NOT exclude new players from controlling a NPC from the list below.

    Pokesona NPC Support

    Also I desperately need people to volunteer to control some of the Pokesona NPCs. Please contact Dune, Verax or myself if you are interested in controlling one of the NPCs not already controlled.

    As a note, most of the NPCs will have multiple short appearances between then main action, so even those not who do not - or can not - participate in the main action are eligible (and honestly, encouraged) to step up and control the NPCs.

    Here the List:

    Velvet Room Occupants

    • Philemon - Philemon is the Master of the Velvet Room and has the power to give humans and Pokemon Pokesonas and unlock human and Pokemon's Unique Personas or Pokesonas. Philemon will be controlled by Keys
    • Igor - An abra-plushie that had a long snout given life and sentience by Philemon. Igor will be controlled by Dune.
    • Nameless - A male Dusknoir created by Philemon who is the Pianist of the Velvet room. Nameless wears a blue velvet blind fold and is an impeccable musician and has the ability of "keeping time". Nameless also has a tendency to use time and number-based references in conversation.
    • Belladonna - A female Gardevoir created by Philemon and a soprano singer who accompanies Nameless' music and covers her ears while singing. Belladona wear a shimmering lavender velvet evening gown a white gems in a Beautifly pattern on the front. Everything she says while conversing with others is spoken in verse.
    • Demon Artist - Or Demon Painter, is a Smeargle given functional immortality by Philemon that wears a black velvet turtle neck, a black toboggan cap, and a stylish pair of sunglasses. He seeks to capture the inspiration of what causes Personas, Pokesonas, Demons, and Shadows to take the forms they do in his art.
    • The Velvet Siblings - A trio consisting of Margaret, a Gothetelle; Elizabeth, a Gothorita; and Theodore, a Gallade. All three wear Blue Velvet outfits and have blues replacing their primary and secondary colors, silver "hair", and yellow eyes.
      • Theodore, often called Theo by others, is a polite and serious young Gallade dressed as a male elevator attendant or bell boy and is the youngest of the Velvet Siblings. Theo will be controlled by Keys.
      • Elizabeth is cheery, inquisitive, friendly, silly, humorous, easily excited and fun-loving Gothorita. Elizabeth will be controlled by Crow.
      • Margaret, befitting her status as the oldest Velvet Sibling, is a professional and quiet Gothitelle - which shows in her choice of clothes (a stereotypical secretary's outfit). Margaret will be controlled by Verax.
    • Marie: - A female Elekid wearing a blue velvet officer's cap and a recent addition to the occupants of the Velvet Room, Marie is When Marie is a cold and antisocial individual. She is cranky, sarcastic, irritable, very foul-mouthed, often prone to mood swings, and will not hesitate to voice out her opinion or express her thoughts - regardless of how it would make everyone feel. However, this is just the way she hides her shyness, insecurity and naivete. Marie often expresses her thoughts in poems which often deal with depressing themes like farewells and existentialism, questioning her own origin. Marie is into fashion, loves nature, and is concerned about her figure (which is why she only eats healthy food).
    • The Twin Assistants- A Pair of two "female" Magnemite with yellow eyes with opposite personalities. Caroline is rather malicious and the more vocal of the pair. While Justine is quiet and more polite. Both wear blue velvet jailers hats - with the letters on their hats spelling out "OXYMORON" - and are in training for duties within the Velvet Room.
      • Caroline can be identified by the red "positive charge" colors on her magnets and her screws being wider than most Magnemite. The symbols on her hat read "X-M-R-N".
      • Justine can be identified by the blue "negative charge" colors on her magnets and that her screws are longer than most Magnemite. The symbols on her hat read "O-Y-O-O".

    Antagonists

    • Edward Thames: Thames, as he prefers to be called, is a lanky man with unruly back hair and dark gray eyes. He was once a brilliant forensic scientist in Orre and was one of the few competent law enforcers in Orre. Thames was a loving family man and hard worker who believed that he could make a difference in Orre. However that competence and enthusiasm caused him to step on the wrong toes at Cipher. So Cipher decided to make an example out of Thames' house while he wasn't home - but while his Wife and child were. Thames, now devastated, vowed that doing good was pointless and everything would be better if no one ever actually cared about anything. These feelings grew and grew within Thames for years. When the J-Team broke Cipher's hold in Orre in 2013, Thames despaired further, as he felt that a group such as the J-Team should have met the same fate as him. Thames was abducted by the Disc One Final Boss because [PLOT REASONS]. Thames will be controlled by Keys.

    • Hi-no-Kagutsuchi: [BACKGROUND INFORMATION REDACTED]. An entity who will give the group challenges based on combat and physical strength.

    • Kunino-sagiri: [BACKGROUND INFORMATION REDACTED]. An entity who will give the group Challenges based on problem solving and mental strength.

    • Mikuratana-no-Kami: [BACKGROUND INFORMATION REDACTED]. An entity who will give the group Challenges based on bonds and emotional strength.

    • Kusumi-no-Okami: [BACKGROUND INFORMATION REDACTED]. An entity who will give the group miscellaneous challenges not based on any particular theme.

    • Final Boss: [ALL PUBLIC INFORMATION REDACTED]. Let's just say it'll be fun. The Final Boss will be controlled by one of the Primary GMs.

    • True Final Boss: Izanami. I believe I've spoken about her before. Izanami will be controlled by one of the Primary GMs.

    Pokesona Participation

    Since there are a lot of participants in this arc, there are a few things to not about participation.

    While is not required - nor is it practical due to    TIME ZONES    and RL - to participate in EVERY event in the Pokesona Plot. Just showing up one or twice during the plot and not actually participating in events beyond what your PC is specifically involved in is a good way not to get the participation reward.

    So, as a guide, try to be around to participate in the following at the very least:
    • Your PC's (or PCs') dungeons and boss fights
    • One the first part's dungeons and Boss fights other than your PCs
    • One of the second part's Challenge other than you PCs
    • The Kunino-sagiri fight
    • The Kusumi-no-Okami fight
    • The Edward Thames fight and the Final Boss fight
    • The Izanami fight.

    If, for some reason, you can't complete the above, PM one of the Primary plot runners and we'll try to find a solution.

    Pokesona Start Date and Other Notes

    With all that out of the way, let's start wrapping up this wall of text.

    Start Date

    In case there are any late applications, I want to start Pokesona no later than the second week of February. Although this may be pushed forward or back depending on the State of Re:Glitch.

    Other Notes

    • To ease wait times, I recommend pre-writing your PCs' encounters with their Shadow Selves and any other text heavy scenes you foresee happening.
      • In a similar vein, be sure to prepare "I am thou... Thou art I..." quotes for when your PCs get their Unique Personas or Pokesonas. The quotes don't need to be shared with others until the post they are required unless you want to.
    • I recommend keeping notes for boss battles, PC notes, etc in a txt file somewhere so if you need to leave you can PM notes to someone and they can fill in for you for a bit.
    • All applicants need to be willing to offer input for the second part of the plot. PMs will be sent out when that time comes.
    • And finally DO NOT BE AFRIAD TO ASK QUESTIONS! The other plot runners and I are here to help you have fun with this plot. So don't shy away from asking for help or asking about something you don't understand.

    Organizational Ties: None.

    Region of origin: Oldale Town, Hoenn

    Personality/Backstory: Brian is friendly, fairly outgoing, adventurous, and likes to explore and analyze things when given the chance. As a result his mom was gone most of the time as he was growing up, but as a perk she got to bring him along on a few trips every summer, so he got to see various different parts of world. Because of this Brian grew to love exploration, as he discovered something new at each location the ship docked. Growing up in Oldale, he and Fletcha grew up as childhood friends, though he left on his journey when he turned 16. He has since gone on and visited/explored each (so far known) main region leisurely for a little over half a year each, and caught/befriended one Mon from each. He and his team do enjoy battling and Brian does want to challenge a league at some point, but its not high on his agenda, he hasnt stayed in one place long enough, and he hasn't had a full team until recently.
