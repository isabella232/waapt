    24753 Fletcha20th Jan 2015 10:39:29 PM from wherever you least expect... Get RP Mod
    This is is a rough draft of my character. Anything in italics is my reasoning as to why things are as they are. Also, not like it's needed at the moment, but Chilly Chinchou.

    Alright, before you read any of the stuff about my character, I just want to let you know one key detail: aside from the backstory, I'm trying to make this character as if I, myself, in the real world, was this character, or at least had a very similar personality to this character. Now, some people may say "that's not original," or "that's no fun," and I have two main reasons why I want to do this: 1. It will be easier for me to get used to Role Playing if all I have to do to think how my character would react to the given situation is to see how I would react to that situation. And don't forget, I'm still coming up with different personalities for my pokemon. 2. I think it's going to be very fun to just be a normal pokemon trainer who gets mixed up in the crazy shenanigans that the plot throws at him and just tries to deal with it as any normal human would. Now that's not to say I might exaggerate this character from time to time, but I will be the basis for it. That being said, onto my character!

    Your Trainer's name: Fletcha

    Age: 18-19

    Gender: Male

    Ethnicity: Caucasian

    Brief description of his/her appearance: Fairly tall, average length blonde hair, slightly thin, definitely not muscular. Likes wearing t-shirts, shorts, and sneakers.

    I will go into at least a little more detail later, just wanted to get the bare minimum down for now

    Ties to any organizations: None

    This character is supposed to be average, or at least start out as average, so nothing special here

    Region of origin: Somewhere in Hoenn, trying to decide

    It seems like everyone is in Hoenn these days, and even if they all leave by the end of the arc, the first pokemon game I played was Emerald, so Hoenn. I don't currently have a preference of which town to start in, though.

    Personality: Fletcha is friendly, has a good sense of humor, likes to crack jokes, and tries to see the best in people/pokemon. He is also fairly introverted, can sometimes be a bit lacking in self confidence,and be a bit socially awkward around strangers. In part because of this he is mostly un-confrontational and has a habit of apologizing for trivial mistakes. However, he is much more outgoing confident among close friends He is more of a follower than a leader, except with things he is passionate about. He generally tries to talk things out before battling, but isn't afraid to defend himself and/or his friends once a battle starts. Can be a bit naive at times. In arguments he tries to state his viewpoint, but he respects other people/pokemon's points of view. Also has somewhat of a hard time explaining things quickly. He can be very competitive, and during battles he tries to think and act strategically most of the time, but he occasionally tries to be spontaneous and unpredictable.

    I most likely put way too much detail here, I just wanted to be really thorough. That's the best I could think of for my personality, soooo yeah

    Backstory: Both of Fletcha's parents are pokemon trainers who already went on their own journeys when they were Fletcha's age, but have since settled down and have average jobs. They have taught Fletcha what they know about being a pokemon trainer and battes while he was growing up, so that he would be well prepared when he starts his own journey Also, They each acquired a fair amount of pokemon on their respective journeys, which in turn means that Fletcha has had a lot of exposure to pokemon while growing up. Fletcha himself has recently begun his journey to collect badges, and looks forward to the upcoming adventures that his journey will bring.

    I wanted a decent background so that Fletcha could act like a competent trainer when starting out, yet have a fairly mundane and average backstory. I should probably add a little more about the journey or something, but I can't think of anything else to put right now

    Pokemon

    Species: Torkoal

    Nickname: Want to think up one any suggestions would be appreciated, I'm terrible when it comes to thinking up nicknames

    Gender: Male

    Ability: White Smoke

    Personality/Bio: Torkoal was originally caught by Fletcha's father when he was on his journey. As such, Torkoal and Fletcha have known each other for a long time. Torkoal has a longing for adventure and decided that coming along with Fletcha would be much more fun and fulfilling than just hanging out at home (To clarify, Torkoal is officially Fletcha's pokemon now). Torkoal also tries to look out for Fletcha whenever he can. While Torkoal is an older mon and past his prime, he has a lot of experience under his shell knows quite a few moves. He is also a physical tank. As for personality, Torkoal isn't afraid of much and has Nerves of Steel (Partly due to the white smoke ability, since stuff like intimidate doesn't work on him). Act's as the Team Dad, and rarely acts like a Grumpy Old Man when upset. Occasionally imparts advice and anecdotes from when he was with Fletcha's dad, which may or may not be interesting and/or helpful. Likes to taunt (not the move, with words) the opposition during a battle.

    While trying to think up of a personalty for Torkoal my mind always drifted to "old" for whatever reason. So that's how I came up with this backstory, and I think it will be fun character to play if I can do it right. One thing that I really like about Torkoal is that he allows Fletcha to have a fairly powerful pokemon that knows a lot of moves even though he only started his journey recently. BUT, since Torkoal is already "past his prime," he probably won't gain that much more power or moves than what he already has, with most of Fletcha's other pokemon probably going to surpass Torkoal at various points along Fletcha's journey.

    Species: Goomy (Starter)

    Nickname: Want to think up one more suggestions, yay

    Gender: Male

    Ability: undecided

    Personality/Bio: Met Fletcha the summer before Fletcha started his journey when he and his family were vacationing in Kalos. Became best friends with Fletcha, so Fletcha's parents caught Goomy so that he could be Fletcha's starter pokemon when Fletcha became a trainer. Is friendly and more outgoing than Fletcha, can be a bit headstrong at times, has a good sense of humor and likes to joke around as much as Fletcha.

    Still need to flesh this out a bit, especially the whole "becoming best friends" part needs an explanation, along with more detail in the personality part. I'm in the process of figuring this out, but of course any feedback is welcome, just like the rest of this character page.

    And that's a rough draft of my character. A couple of other things to note, I chose these two pokemon because they are two of my favorite pokemon. I'm debating on whether or not to start with a third mon, it would probably be the first one Fletcha catches on his journey. And it would probably be either a Lapras, Skarmory, Sableye, or another pokemon that I like. And since I'm wanting to make my character be well, normal, I have no interests in seeking out any auric abilities or human to pokemon stuff. If it happens to my character because of plot, then so be it, of course I will go along with it. One more thing; I would like for my character to eventually learn monese. It could take a really long time, and as of starting my character will only be able to get the gist of what pokemon are saying, just like any other normal person. But sometime down the line, I would like for my character to learn monese.

    That's all I have to say, sorry if it's a wall of text at times, any feedback is greatly appreciated :D
