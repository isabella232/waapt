    Doublepost due to a matter that has been brought to our attention.

    Lately, Verax has been feeling uncomfortable with certain parts of the RP. However, there have been many misconceptions that need to be addressed.

    Verax has had a bad history involving internet interactions with the LGBT Community. Now, this has nothing to do with anyone here, at WAAPT - however, this has left him with a distinct negative reaction to it. Now, he has no desire to cast blame. In fact, he specifically does not want to bring the matter up in chat if he's going to be viewed in an excessively negative light.

    As Every put it, "Basically, in a previous RP, Verax ended up being banned because the RP got really saturated with LGBT stuff and shipping, which he was uncomfortable with. And because of the sudden double reveal, as well as a pretty major focus on shipping lately, he's worried that the same pattern is repeating itself and that he'll end up either being kicked out or otherwise pressured to leave. Consequently he feels in kind of a bind because he likes the RP but is stressed over a lot of the contents, and doesn't want to either leave or pressure people not to write LGBT stuff, but at the same time doesn't feel like there's a way to just stop feeling discomfort over things."

    Verax, right now, accepts LGBT matters, even if he doesn't support them. Unfortunately, he's afraid that this opinion causes people to dismiss his concerns as invalid, which is why he has never once demanded that a matter be taken from Creative - only requested.

    My personal views on the matter are these: We should not be so open-minded as to be closed minded. This essentially means that we shouldn't dismiss people who are not as open to certain ideas. That's just hypocritical.

    Small references and post notifications are fine, but full-on discussions regarding Non-Canon ships are probably best kept to the Non-Canon discussion thread. We're not asking for censorship, just respect.

    19125 ChaoticMarin22nd Feb 2014 01:05:40 AM from Nintendoland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: 700 wives and 300 concubines
    Chiptune DJ
    I feel that there are definitely benefits to limiting how edgy we are I Cly. It's hard for me to explain it without sounding confrontational because I'm used to a very professional principle of RP where mixing IC and OOC is never okay; I'll give it a shot though.

    I feel like it's important that we don't go too far with projecting our sexual morality on the RP, not because anyone is wrong or anything asinine like that but because sex and sexuality are extremely edgy subjects for some people, even beyond violence.

    There will come a time when society as a whole is open minded enough that what we do here doesn't even raise a brow for most people. I feel fairly safe saying homosexuality for instance has reached that point or at least close enough where we shouldn't have to worry about it. Things like transexuality and polyamory have not quite reached that point however... Both of those things still experience a terrifying amount of prejudice in real life; those groups are not remotely free of prejudice and hatred which just goes to show not only that they deserve to be fought for but that society sadly hasn't caught up with our progressive mindsets. It'd also be a disservice to all the people who suffer as a result of being trans or polyamorous to say otherwise.

    Now by jumping the gun and using the RP as a bit of a playground to experiment with these sorts of 'edgy' things (Edgy means overly dark or overly progressive, in this case the latter.) we do kind of heavily shove some people who are still struggling with their feelings about this stuff out of the RP.

    And that's not... ideal. I've seen some things, man. I've been on the other end of this conversation being told the same thing and boy did it suck, but in hindsight a little restraint would've stopped a lot of emotional discomfort and probably a couple of fights / ruined friendships in the process.

    So... There's my feelings on the matter.
