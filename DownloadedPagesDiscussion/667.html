    Crewe: Oh Arceus, I have really treated you badly. Especially during the Hoenn Arc. By the Legends, the Hoenn arc.

    You know what, though? The Reveal wasn't bad at all. Heck, I should have been happy that it was about a Human actually being a Pokémon because we see the other way around enough as it is. It wasn't a bad twist as far as twists go. I only really got unhappy because it was paired with the whole Mindscapes thing - which didn't really seemed as though it "fit" in my idea of Pokémon.

    This, combined with the fact that I hadn't been able to write anything since the tail-end of the Orange Islands arc, just lead to a perfect storm of anger at an otherwise decent plot.

    Also, I really really really' shouldn't have forced you to try and write for a bunch of Pokémon you didn't want. I know darn well how it feels to want to just want to have some Pokémon of mine disappear so I don't have to worry about them, only to have everyone say stuff along the lines of "Nooooo, don't get rid of Blitz! She's such a good character!" Or "Phantom is awesome! You can't just drop her!" It's the worst.

    So I can't hold that against you. Besides, even if you did go without catching any Pokémon ever again, it wouldn't have mattered because you were a Pokémon. Can't be mad at someone for not having Pokémon if they are one.

    All in all, it was a terrible time for me and I lashed out at an obvious target. My bad. :(

    Oh, and you gave the world Clockwise and Likewise. Did I ever mention they're some of the best nicknames ever? Because they're some of the best nicknames ever.

    Straw: I shouldn't have tried to stop you from doing ISB. That was wrong. Even though I don't like glitches, It wasn't my place to dictate whether they could or could not be in this RP. Especially if it wouldn't have affected my end of the story in any way. I have a bad habit of trying to prevent people from doing something if I don't like it. It's not just you this has happened to.

    That, and I shouldn't have said that one time that I didn't want to RP with you. That was wrong too. It's not really up to me who I get to do stuff with, whether you like people or not.
