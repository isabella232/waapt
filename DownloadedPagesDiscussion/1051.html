    As for what I originally came here for...

    Most of you probably have seen that when I'm working on my contest entry my IM status says something along the lines of "Working on something important writing-wise, please don't send me any distracting weblinks".

    Notice how I bolded that last part?

    That's because I REALLY FREAKING MEAN IT

    One, it's really distracting. I already am easily distracted enough doing the contest entry (and WAAPT stuff too for that matter) without everyone and their mother sending me links to SOMETHING I NEED TO SEE OR I'LL DIE, especially if said something is a particularly long article/blog post/Youtube video/etc. Not only that, but the way I'm writing the contest entry adds several levels of annoyance to it all via the following factors:

    • My default browser is Chrome, which I usually have loads of tabs open on.

    • However, I write the contest entry on a Google Doc open in Firefox with minimal other tabs (most relating to the contest entry's subject matter or the contest itself) with Chrome closed to minimize distractions.

    • However, because my default browser is Chrome any link I Med to me automatically opens in Chrome even if I originally had it closed, and having both all my Chrome tabs and my Firefox tabs running with all the usual other programs I run on top has an annoying tendency to make my computer slow to a snail's pace and generally act really wonky, making the viewing of the link and prompt re-closing of Chrome far more of a hassle than it should be.

    Overall, it all amounts to something normally innocuous and fun being a massive thorn in my side when I'm trying to juggle this contest entry (which has a strict deadline for submission not all that far away) and wrapping up loose ends RP-wise so I can finally, properly end my run here (which has its own planned deadline that's mercifully a decent bit further away (I'll tell you guys exactly when a bit later), but still).

    So, please, if there's something on the vast realm of the interwebs you want to show me, TVT PM me it; it renders the browser problem moot and I'm more likely to NOT be working on the contest entry if I'm checking my P Ms at all.

    Furthermore, if I get any unsolicited (read:ones I don't actually ask for) links of any sort (save those important to the RP or its meta I need to see) via IM while my IM status says I'm working on something important, I will immediately remove the offender from my Pidgin friends list until my contest entry is submitted (or if I decide to shelve it). If this happens it's nothing personal; you can still talk to me via TVT PM and I'll add you right back in either case of what happens to the contest entry.

    I'm sorry I have to get this extreme, but I have an autism spectrum disorder and one of my many symptoms is... well, I'll let a cartoon fairy sum it up.◊ So I genuinely need to minimize distractions as much as I can.

    But that's all for now and I'm outta here once again.
