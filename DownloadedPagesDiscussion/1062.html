    Yo, I heard y'all are fond of double posts.

    But anyway, as I said in my last two posts I'm gonna set aside the big questions of when the Delta Episode happens or how long it'll take aside for now and won't re-address them until doing so becomes more urgent, but I still have two side concerns I still want addressed sooner:

    Robin's Alternate Ending

    Part of the reason I'm so concerned about when the Delta Episode happens and how long it takes and especially whether it either goes smoothly or crashes and burns enough to leave it permanently unfinished is that I considered Robin filling the role of the ORAS protagonist or at least having a big role to be perfect for ending his character arc for reasons I'll get to below. However, given recent events and after talking with a few other people I decided I'll need to come up with a Plan B for his character arc just in case any of my worries about the Delta Episode actually happen (or if I become convinced the chances of them happening are too great for me to even attempt my original plan despite the benefits). Problem is, I have no clue what that plan would be, and so am asking you guys for help.

    You see, the main issues Robin has to tackle for his character arc to be "complete" are as follows:

    • His guilt over his past failures and such in general.

    • Related to the above, his worry he'll fail/desert his new friends just like he did his old ones.

    • And finally, that not so deep down he feels like he still doesn't truly belong in this strange new world.

    I felt him having a big role in the Delta Episode would be an ideal way to take care of all three, since he'd be instrumental to saving the new world he calls home after destroying the old one (as well as delicious, nutritious* clashes between his Screw Destiny mentality and Zinnia's You Can't Fight Fate mentality, but just like that sparkly-special deadline for my endgame as a whole, as much as I would love for it to happen if it turns out not to be viable I'll need another option.

    Some additional notes to help you guys help me figure this out:

    • There are three posts I'm planning for Robin regardless of whether I go with the Delta Episode or whatever Plan B is cooked up; Robin confronting the rest of Team Umbra after he learns how Odin really died, another dream sequence going into his past addressing an implication in his home game that completely flew over my head, and a surprise meeting with someone familiar that sheds new light on the circumstances that got him to the WAAP Tverse.

    • I'd be open to this involving other players, but not too many, and preferably by spagging.

    • It should be something that ideally won't take too long both in terms of prewriting and in-RP execution. (hence the "preferably by spagging")

    I'll be open to and willing to hear out any suggestions or especially general offers to help hash a plan out you guys have, either publicly or privately, and would really appreciate them a lot, but at the same time I'll try to be patient waiting for them.

    Now, next thing...

    Three's A Crowd

    Now, this one's directed specifically at you, Skyzerk, and for that I have one very important thing to say beforehand you need to listen to:

    I do not mean ANY of the following as a personal attack on you or your characters, and have no desire to ruin your fun in general. So please, do not make the same mistake I did by overreacting to anything below.

    That said...

    I'm kinda worried that the fact you're doing Leauge challenges for all three of your player characters will derail Delta Episode plans a lot, especially since most of us would rather have it done by Thanksgiving or so for various reasons and there are already 'at least four others in the pipeline. I know you said we could easily go on without you since you're not in the Delta Episode but there's one big problem: The guy your P Cs are facing at the end, Steven Stone, is, in a pretty big role too, and he can't be in two places at once.

    ...However, at the same time, I don't want to shit on your head by making you cancel any long-held plans. So, I've come up with a few possible workarounds:

    • Use Wallace as the Champion instead of Steven. Wallace also has a role in the Delta Episode, but it's far smaller than Steven's and occurs very close to the end, so depending on timing it could work out.

    • Do one of the League Challenges pre-Delta episode and save the other two for after.

    • Just TS the whole shebang.

    • Some combination of the above.

    Also, if you are not already doing so it is a very, very VERY good and highly reccomended idea regardless of whichever of the above you go with to prewrite all those Leauge challenges on a Word document or Google doc or something for ease of getting them out of the way as quickly as possible in the RP itself, to the point it's pretty much standard procedure.

    If anyone else has suggestions for Sky on how to handle all this, please tell him! He'd likely appreciate it a lot! ^_^

    But that's all for now... Except that since I mentioned I have a few more fun D!Posts in the pipeline to ease tensions, I'll give four hints as to what they contain:

    Pidgin, C. S. Lewis, Eevee, and (while this may be a fair bit further down the line than the others) something that's basically Teen Titans meets Doctor Who with a pinch of crack.
