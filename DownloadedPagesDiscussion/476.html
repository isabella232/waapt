    Deltafication

    I don't think so and certainly hope not. I have at least 2 Pokmon I want to evolve then and I'm expecting more to evolve in that time and realized a bit ago my playstyle doesn't translate too well to Delta mons.

    Also...

    Depends on what you mean about working them into scenes. There are a couple ways to handle that IMO...

    • Work on having multiple characters be part of the same conversation

    • Try and get parallel conversations going with your mons

    • Try and have your mons approach people instead of just your P Cs

    • Try making a longpost with two of your mons talking to each other on occasion.

    • Work on making subplots for your mons

    I'm not really the best with the first one. Whenever I try and do that the other characters tend to fade and I just focus on one. Ask other people if you need help on that. Maybe Mezzo?
