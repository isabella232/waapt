    New character bio, here we go!

    Name:Barrigan Crepsley

    Age:32

    Gender: Male

    Affliations: Part time Pokemon Ranger, full time freelance hunter

    Appearance: Barrigan has a tanned complexion, with steely, big grey eyes, rather like an owl's. His brown hair is often hidden by a hat, usually a light brown [Stetson] cowboy hat, along with either a button up vest or a maroon suede jacket, with usually a pair of either jeans or track pants, with some black boots to top it all off. His Styler can be found on a holster on his belt.

    Personality: Barrigan is a rather gruff man who gives the outward persona of a charming, snarky man, while closing himself off emotionally from most people, and thus can be rather ruthless at times, only really opening up to people that he trusts, revealing himself to be a gentle and dorky man. He cannot stand being in one place for too long, as it might eventually convince him to stay there.He's a sensory type Auric, and thus is incredibly weak at Constructs, Aura Sphere, and other such Combat type Auric skills, his speciality lying in Empathic senses, telepathy,(though he prefers to only sense people's minds, not pry into them), and tracking/dowsing.

    Origin: Pueltown, Almia(currently roams around)

    Backstory: Barrigan lived with his parents, Marten and Jivanna Crepsley, as well as his brother, Rockwell. One day, at the age of 16, he went to the old Chroma Ruins for a school field trip, but ended up collapsing when he came into contact with them, as his mind experienced massive feedback from hearing all of the thoughts and emotions of the kids around him at once. A few days later, he would wake up, but when he got back home there was no one there, his family had disappeared, and to this day he doesn't know why. He began to live on his own, but eventually started to help out around the town, by sensing what people needed, or when they needed help, and going to them. Eventually he started doing this for a living, becoming a freelance helper, which eventually lead to him getting a boyfriend, Richard. For a time being, he was happy, and didn't feel alone anymore.

    However, one day, when he came back home with the groceries, Richard was missing too, gone without a trace. After this, Barrigan decided to stop trying to form connections to people, as it'll hurt so much more for him, being an empath.

    After training, he decided to become a Pokemon Ranger, as that would mean he would get to help even more people than before, but(for his own thrills more than anything) he also does hunting on the side, be it man or mon.
