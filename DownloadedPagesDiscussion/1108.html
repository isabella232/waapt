    Ahem sorry, jk. Seriously though, I wanted ask this for a while now but always opted out for... a variety of reasons only one of which being that I was scared, this time I won't chicken out, I won't let myself chicken out:

    So yeah now that that's all done, dk who already read it in Pidgin but I wanted to ask if somebody would be willing to help me with creating a game, currently there are two things:

    • The first is somewhat of my love project, I started with it when I entered GymnasiumGymnasium? In Germany that's a school, not the gym you maybe thinking of, dk what that translates to in the American system, probably High-school but I'm not sure, hence I just wrote the German name, I continued programming it the whole time although work died a bit down after I entered University
      • At its heart its a Dungeon crawler a l Pokmon Mystery Dungeon or Etrian Odyssey
      • At the moment there is only 1 Dungeon, randomly generated every time the player enters it and with a maximal number of floors tied to the players level, maybe there will be more in the future, dk yet
      • The 1st Floor will only be generated once when the player first enters it, and from then on persist, on this floor the player can build his own base with furniture crafted himself or bought from the nearby city
      • The combat system will be somewhat Dwarf Fortress-lite, The body will consist of multiple parts, those all will have multiple layers (Skin, Flesh, Bone) so a light attack maybe only bruise the skin while a heavy hit with a big weapon will go through skin flesh and bone, amputating the whole thing, maybe there will also be inner organs (Heart, Lung, Liver, Kidneys, etc) though I don't know if that will make problems (see point below) hence DF-lite
      • The player will have the possibility to transform his body with elixirs (I'm calling them elixirs now, for all intends and purposes they could be anything, souls of fallen enemies, special scrolls, hell maybe magic stones for all I know, but for simplicity's sake lets stay with elixirs for this description). He will be able to alter his body significantly in this way
        • Now don't think the transformations will be instant, the player will have to drink a few elixirs, some will only increase his stats (for example if you drink a horse elixir you would get stronger or faster while a cat elixir probably would make you more agile, etc), and , if some thresholds are passed a random body part will transform (for example maybe you would grow cat ears after drinking enough cat elixirs. Maybe you will grow a tail instead, so gradually you can transform yourself into any race, or a mixture, centaur body + upper body of a Mantis? If you want it, go for it.
        • The races won't be just for show either, they will come with their appropriate special attacks, so someone with the lower half of a Drider will be able to shoot nets at his enemies, if you have the claws of a cat the player will be able to slash someone with them, etc. Though this comes at a price, don't think the player will be able to fit his fancy human greaves onto that Centaur body he so likes, nope! He will have to order a (pretty expensive as you might expect) armor made especially for horse-bodies from Aicha. Also one shouldn't expect that the player still can equip weapons with that cool and deadly Mantis arms (without hands)
      • The looting system will work like in the Game Etrian Odyssey, monsters won't give you things like 'Gold' or 'Weapons' oh no, they will give you things like 'Fur', 'Bones' etc (you know, things that actually could be salvaged from an animal corpse, the player will have to go to the local Aicha The hell is this Aicha you keep talking about?!? Forgive me or this, Aicha is the smith in Etrian Odyssey, I really like her, so I mostly talk about the smith as Aicha equivalent, guess where the name of a certain Mawile of mine comes from btw, she will use those goodies you bring her to forge new weapons and armor for the player.
      • Now if a human enemy is slain (bandits for example) he might drop weapons or armor he could realistically have.
      • Weapons and armor found or bought from the local Aicha will be generated Dungeon Siege style, so for example a sword may have a slight fire infusion, that would make 'Bronze Sword' to 'Ember Bronze Sword' and so on, idk its hard to explain, basically the weapons and armors can have random enchantments boosting their stats which are reflected in their name (instead a simple background change or a blue outline instead of a white one like in some other games)
      • If it would be possible the payer maybe will be able to catch and tame monsters to aid him in battle, I've coded the basics, but it isn't much right now
      • In the Dungeon are resource nodes, where the player will be able to mine for things like iron and stuff if he has the correct tools.
      • As said I'm coding 3 years on this now, though my ideas are approaching zero while the thing is far from complete, maybe another coder will also bring in a good breath of fresh air, so yeah, if you have any ideas, by all means, please tell me. Doubly so if you want to help me coding, I'll do my best to integrate them
      • Apart from that the thing... probably will need some clean-up, now don't think this is some wreckage, but I programmed it alone for 3 years, I'm pretty sure its bound to be somewhat tailored onto my own style and maybe not too good for a group to program, we'll see.
      • Also, I don't need only coders, artists would also be a great help, currently all portrays for the dialogs are just head that I cut out from the images I have, which of course for one will be a no-go if this thing ever is going to be published and also while they may look good on their own their styles are completely mixed so its not pleasant to look at as a whole. (Did I mention that currently there is no sounds? No? Okay: Currently there's also no sound, not the most important hing in a game, but still a heads up)

    • The second game would be a completely fresh start, it will be a city-builder sim in Steam Punk/Clock Punk style
      • There will be the 'Map Mode' where the city will be represented by a town map (as in those things that where common in the medieval times)
      • Also there will be a 1st person mode where the player will be able to walk in his city, watching as the daily life unfolds, as Steam Golems march past him, shopkeepers go after their business, etc
      • As you can see there will be a real need for a great AI, so I'm not too sure about this.
      • Players can place buildings from both perspectives
      • When in 1st person players will be able to help with the building (carry materials, etc) or do other small chores in the city
      • I've not really anything more planned, if you have more ideas, by all means, please do share them.
      • But alas, as this will be a 3D game and will on the whole be much more complex I'm not sure if I would be able to do this.
      • Considering said complexity I would say if someone were to help me that at first we should focus on exactly two things, not more, building the city from Map-Mode and seeing it coming to life in 1st Person Mode, no complex economics (or economics at all), no fights nothing

    Thanks for the info :) And yes, it needs it, it really needs it. Regarding the stories and languages, the text for the first one is in German though I'd be willing to translate it into English, and the second is just a concept now so there would be no problem for me to start it in english.
