    After some discussion with Eskay, I've been considering resuming writing for Lucius. I'm aware that he was a character who caused a lot of issues last time I wrote for him, and I most definitely don't want him to overshadow my new characters, but even without me realising it he already seems to have become a feature in some of their stories. Eskay has given me permission to take him back, but if anyone else has misgivings about me doing so then I'll hold off. There's no sense in me adopting him again if it's just going to cause more trouble.

    If I do go back to writing for Lucius, I also need to figure out how to tackle his ability to use Aura - or rather, his current lack of it. One of the mistakes I made when writing for Lucius first time around was giving him Auric powers without any prior warning and then failing to define what the limits of those powers were. As such, I don't want to give him any sort of extranormal ability without explicitly confirming that it's okay for me to do so.

    At present, Lucius has been Brought Down to Normal, and has a body made of both types of Obscura. With this in mind, the three options I was considering were 1) he remains a normal, 2) he can use Aura but has to find a way to stop his body's Obscura from locking out his powers, or 3) being made of Obscura now lets him use Obscura once he figures out how. I think 3 is the option with the most potential, but that includes potential for abuse, so I'm more than happy to go with one of the alternatives if people would prefer me to.

    That's all I have to say for now, really. As I said when I rejoined, I'm trying to do a better job of communicating my plans, so don't hesitate with any questions/concerns. (And sorry for hogging Discussion with stuff about me and my affairs again.)
