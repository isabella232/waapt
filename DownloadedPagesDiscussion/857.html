    21414 DuneTheWanderer30th Jun 2014 06:11:17 AM from Nowhere, USA Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Seeking boyfriend-free girl
    Burger Scientist
    Okay, for today's metaphor, I'm going to be using a forest. Let's start when the forest is in it's prime and work from there.

    A full forest is full of trees, nice and healthy. They stand strong and tall, and the forest floor is home to green shrubs and undergrowth that supports the animals within. This is the RP and it's players. One could say that the animals are the characters.

    Over time, however, detritus builds. Fallen branches and rotten logs clog everything, causing things to be difficult on the forest floor. Naturally, healthy fires come by and consume this organic trash while the trees endure thanks to their thick trunks and the animals stay ahead of the fire. The soil left over is now fertile, allowing even more healthy growth to support the animals who reside there.

    Unfortunately, if too much detritus builds, the fires that come won't be healthy - the fires will be far hotter than they should, catching animals before they can react and scorching trees through their bark. This also sterilizes the earth, making small plant growth near impossible.

    The way this is counteracted is, by taking the detritus aside and doing what are called "controlled burns", we maintain the mild and constructive nature of the fires instead of them turning into omnidestructive waves that burn the good as well as the bad.

    Okay, metaphor done. In a slightly different tone, even the Game of Gods has had to do cleanup like this before, and they're probably the longest running RP on the entire site. This just seems to me to be a natural extension in response to RP growing pains, as more steps need to be taken to prevent explosions of meta conflict.
