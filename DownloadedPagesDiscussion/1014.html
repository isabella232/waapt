    The Hero No One Knows
    So here's the bio for Prometheus, his team is a WIP so I just have names, species, and genders. Sorry about being such a pest about him, now that I have him out of my head like this I should shut up until he's actually relevant

    Feel free to share comments, complaints, or thoughts.

    Name: Prometheus Inverno

    Age: Late 20's-early 40's

    Ethnicity: Unknown, but definitely of Eastern [European] origin, family ethnic background does not match geographic location born.

    Appearance: Tall and lean, with neat short copper hair and "radioactive"-green eyes, dressed similar to Ein◊ without glasses, he has pale white skin and soft timeless face.

    Personality: Prometheus is a quiet intellectual and historian, fond of people and situations that are thought provoking, though is stubborn and has an arrogant streak. He refuses to take risks when he doesn't need to and instead prefers to make sure his battles and tasks are won before he even takes his first step. Though not intimidating in appearance, his eerie ever-pleasant demeanor and chilling presence give him an intimidating aura that he uses when working with others. He's not above bullying people to get what he wants and does not take well to being challenged or contradicted. Prometheus has very little concern for the how things are done to get the results he wants, but has no qualms about unnecessary liberties taken if they do not negatively impact the end result.

    Background: Born in [Germany], he led a very unremarkable early life, normal parents, normal friends, exceptional compared to his peers in academics, his true talents for logistics and strategy were revealed after he moved with his wife and newborn son to Orre for research with Professor Mesquite, one that led him on a trip that would take him out of the Region, through Ransei, Holon, among several other Regions. Upon it's completion, and following a series of events of unclear nature, he was moved into Cipher's R&D, after the events of Wes, was transferred to Operations, where he remained.

    Team:

    Vanguard: Male Shadow Tyranitar

    Ability: Sand Stream

    Personality: Vanguard, along with Eulogy was Prometheus' starter. Hiding a hair-trigger temper behind a mask of elegance and kindness. Vanguard is of the mind that the strong should dominate the weak, the strong have to be smart enough to not be outsmarted and strong enough to not be brute forced, and those without power either have no right to it or deserve to be placed at the bottom. He got fed up with Prometheus not holding his ground when he was teased and bullied as a boy and fought back for him, and most of his fights for all his life. First into the fray and the last one to leave, Vanguard has the role of a protector as the foundation of everything he became.

    Birthday: 5/7

    Nature: Adamant

    Eulogy: Shadow Metagross

    Ability: Clear Body

    Personality: Eulogy is nearly always silent, finding that words are useless and inefficient in communicating concepts. Along with Vanguard, Eulogy was Prometheus' starter. The why of everything is what matters to Eulogy, regardless of if something works, or simply is, the reason behind it, and that understanding, are all that matter. He was instrumental in developing Prometheus' intellect and desire for knowledge growing up, and preferred not to battle for the sake of strength, but to learn why others fight. Ever present and always scheming, the presence of Eulogy indicates there is more to the situation than meets the eye.

    Birthday: 1/1

    Nature: Careful

    Enforcer: Shadow Gallade

    Ability: Steadfast

    Personality: Enforcer was Prometheus' first captured Pokmon, and was caught as a Kirlia. At 16, Prometheus already had some experience training Pokmon, and Enforcer was glad to be taken out of the wild. Rash and reckless, he is always eager to prove himself. Growing up he was treated in a very feminine and condescending manner, which constructed a deep-seated anger and misogyny. He still has a conditioned aggressive response to anything he perceives as feminine. He has a blind devotion to Prometheus and never questions him, figuring that there is always a good reason behind what he does. Even as a shadow, with his heart closed and memories sealed, this loyalty refuses to burn. Zealous and hostile, Enforcer ensures the word of Prometheus is carried out.

    Birthday: 6/24

    Nature: Brave

    Requiem: Female Swampert

    Ability: Torrent

    Personality: Requiem is the shy and lonely mother of the team. Finding Prometheus nearly drowned on the shore's of Ransei, she took him and his team to rest. Seeing her as a sign, Prometheus captured her as he continued to explore. The diplomat and comforter, Requiem does not suffer fools or act rashly, though not as powerful as her Shadowed comrades, her tenacity and patience have won her more battles than her allies have brute forced their way through. Peaceful and powerful, even when out of sight, when Prometheus is around, be sure that Reqiuem is near, ready and willing to protect.

    Birthday: 8/12

    Nature: Docile

    Voiceless: Female Crobat

    Ability: Inner Focus

    Personality: Voiceless is the second Pokmon Prometheus caught, exploring caves on his travels. Mute but observant and clever, Voiceless has never forgiven Prometheus for her name. She communicates with a series of bobs and flutters that Eulogy worked out, and can communicate very quickly with this, allowing her to send long strings of messages of a short period of time. She is the scout for Prometheus, and the infiltrator. Finding information and keeping away from watchful eyes, she has a love of inflicting pain on others. Silent and merciless, sighting this Crobat with one lower wing missing and a long scar across it's back is the final sign that Prometheus has interest in you.

    Birthday: 12/19

    Nature: Naughty

    Hyperion: Male Shadow Chandelure

    Ability: Flame Body

    Personality: Hyperion is Prometheus's recent addition, caught 5 years ago. Hollow and empty, Hpyerion sees nothing in the world worth keeping, and no goals of his own. He lets Prometheus give his life meaning, and as such only seeks the destruction of what stands in his way. Hollow and fearless, the flames of Hyperion are often the last sights of a battle.

    Birthday: 10/30

    Nature: Quiet

    Oblivion: Female Shadow Hydreigon

    Ability: Levitate

    Personality: Oblivion is the ruthless mindless sword of Prometheus' will. She communicates rarely, and only to voice needs such as hunger or thirst. Stubborn to a fault, pain, exhaustion, threat of death, nothing distracts her from her goals, it is either accomplished, or never needed to be addressed. Obtained shortly after joining Cipher, Oblivion is the sixth Pokmon Prometheus obtained. A vector of destruction, Oblivion was quite aptly named.

    Birthday: 11/27

    Nature: Serious
