    Well now the truth is laid out in front of me. And it's quite clear to me that if I want to forge some true friendships then I'm never going to find them here.

    I've had enough of trying. I've given WAAPT so many many chances over these past two years. I keep telling myself that maybe it'll be better this time. That I could make some friends and have some fun. But every time, these hopes get dashed against the hard rocks of reality. I've given countless time and effort towards something that has given me almost nothing in return. And I've had enough.

    I really wanted to see things through this time. I was already setting plans in motion to get some cool stuff going again for Holon. I even went through all the trouble of introducing a new Pokémon for goodness sake. Granted, I could just continue in fan-fic mode, but what would be the point? It wouldn't help me. It wouldn't help anyone. And as much as it pains me to leave right in the middle of things, that's just the way things are going to have to be.

    I'll extend my thanks to the people who did try and be my friend. At least you wanted more out of this like I did. Sorry that I have to leave you, but I can't bear keeping in contact, I'm afraid.

    So that's it then. Luck with whatever you're doing in the future. I'm gone, and hopefully for good this time.
