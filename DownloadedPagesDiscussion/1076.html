    WAAPT Proposition Number Nine-Three-Zero, Presented by Keys2tkingdom

    With the ports of RBY being released soon, I thought, as a nod to the event, that Legacy TMs could be released IRP.

    What are Legacy TMs?

    Simply put, they are re-releases of the Gen I TMs made with current technology (i.e. the are infinite use).

    Why propose such a thing?

    Well other than the stated nod to the release of RBY ports, getting Gen I TMs can be a bit of a chore for some characters. Off the top of my head, I've seen "discontinued" TMs been obtained through EevveeBay, been bought from shady backalley merchants, or through exceedingly contrived coincidences. So this allows for them to be more common.

    Okay but what about the mon not included in the original 151?

    Well this has multiple examples so bear with me here,

    • Mon that got evolutions in later Gens (e.g. Magmortar, Electivire, Porygon-Z) should be able to learn most - if not all - of what their pre-evolutions learned.
      • This is more iffy as some moves could cause a line to become broken.
      • Notable possible exceptions: Leafeon, Glaceon, Slyveon due to the fact that Eevee learned some moves from TMs that the can (probably) no longer learn

    • Mon that have Gen I TM moves as Egg Moves or Tutor Moves. (e.g. Petil's Bide and Swaddle's Razor Wind)
      • Think how Stealth Rock became more available through breeding in Gen V because it was no longer a TM.

    • Crow brought up the fact that some mon should be able to learn certain TM moves if the fit. (e.g. like how Scouter was able to learn Nightmare from the Move Tutor in Orre - due to the fact that Watchog can learn Hypnosis and Dream Eater)

    What if I want [x mon] to learn [x Gen I TM ] like that last example?

    That is something that needs to be decided on a case by case basis and should be approved by a majority before slapping, say, Rage on a Chespin.

    If this takes off...

    If this actually goes through, I'd like for Ren to give everyone full sets of Gen I Legacy TMs as gifts for the Holidays. Either or both ideas can be shot down, but I just wanted to put them both out there.
