    Guess Who...?
    Seeing as we seem to have some recurring issues regarding satisfaction with the progress of Hoenn, over at the chat, we tried discussing some possible causes of this, and things that we could do to try and fix it.

    One of the things that we found was that the present TA/TM arc feels a bit like a subarc to some players, due in part to the plot currently being run more or less by a couple players.

    So we tried to ask what could be done to make things easier for everyone to contribute, and one of the things that we found was that a chunk of the playerbase seems to have little/no idea as to what's supposed to be going on with TA/TM beyond their being obnoxious and terraforming.

    So... Perhaps that's something that we should attempt to sort out... So that way we'd have a general direction to work towards.
