    17990 strawberryflavored27th Dec 2013 05:03:24 PM from Virginia Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: If it's you, it's okay
    Princess of Hell
    Okay, I had a new idea that I mentioned in the chat. Also, fleshed out the Mega evolutions idea Here goes:

    Fairy type idea

    There is an organization that, upon seeing the new Fairy Pokemon and the newly recognized Fairy types, notices the happiness and cuteness that many of them possess. This organization starts out as trying to use Fairy Pokemon to bring happiness to those whose lives have taken a turn for the worse.

    Then the board of executives gets a bit insane. They notice that, while their efforts seem to be making people happy, they cannot do anything permanent. This is where they resolve to make the world a place where no negative emotions can exist.

    To do this, they create a superweapon, powered by the attacks of the Fairy type, such as Dazzling Gleam, Moonlight, and Moonblast, that plunges the world into eternal night, the energy from the eternal night, coupled with the Fairy types' power, will project an aura of happiness across the world. It may be forever dark, but to them they have succeeded in their mission.

    The latter part would not become apparent until later in the arc. For a good part of the arc, they would simply be just another activist group/business.

    Mega Evolutions idea

    The Anistar Sundial is connected to the Mega stones in that all of the postgame stones can only be found at 8 PM, when the sundial reveals them or something(I think). Well, this is because it is actually a giant megastone. It was created when the initial awakening of Xerneas scatter the mega stones, one giant chunk of rock became more crystalline, turning into the sundial at Anistar, but retaining the Mega Stone properties.

    Some very wealthy person(think Lawrence from Pokemon 2000) has uncovered this knowledge, and is working on a way to utilize the Anistar Sundial. The J-team likely ends up assisting he/she in their efforts, especially PEFE, for scientific purposes. Through careful research, he/she has theorized that if used with a Mega Ring or something like it, it can allow a Pokemon to retain their Mega Evolved form for as long as it is day, the mon reverting to normal at night.

    As the arc goes on, he/she is constructing a key stone to use the Anistar Sundial and activate its effects.(again, the J-team assisting) Once bonded to the sundial, it causes the expected result, but as a result, Xerneas slowly becomes weaker, the extended Mega Pokemon durations drawing straight from the source that created them. It is eventually theorized that, unless the sundial and the keystone built to harness its power are separated, Xerneas will die. (this would occur in the last quarter of the arc)

    The only issue is that the link can only be severed using the combined powers of every single Mega Evolution known at the time(This would mean the currently known ones, since there will almost certainly be more in the future. Mega Lati@s may or may not be included.) So the J-team would need to find each mega stone, and the corresponding Pokemon in time before Xerneas dies.
