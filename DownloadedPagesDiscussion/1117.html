    And as another derail with almost all of my 20th anniversary stuff being over...

    Well, soon after I announced I was gonna retire from WAAPT in August 2014, I made a list of remaining plans which I'd been keeping up to date and using as a checklist ever since. However, as of late the list's been getting cluttered with the completed plans vs. the remaining ones, I'm not sure how to work a couple new developments into the old list, and... Well, the list was posted Fall 2014 and I was honestly expecting to be done by a year from then but... Yeah.

    So, without further ado, I present...

    Umbra's Endgame Checklist 2.0

    & marks a plan that requires others to participate either through spagging or interaction, though I otherwise know who's involved in what for the most part. # marks a plan can be demoted to the "post-retirement extra" section if necessary. A marks a completed plan.

    General wrapup

    • &Finishing off the Delta Episode, wrapping up Robin's character arc in the process - TOP PRIORITY

    • &Finishing Yveltal's personal subplot (especially going into the mysteries around her origins and resolving intrapersonal issues regarding Salvador and Levi)

    • A look into Arika's past... Or at least one angle of it.

    • Dave and Mia meet the J-Team

    • Three ghostly people have an important talk.

    • &Ammy and Ho-Oh meet and talk of the past, with surprises on both their ends

    • &Waka fully reveals the things foreshadowed in Umbra and Tagg's Shadedrake visit - and another player's characters are involved in more ways than one.

    • #Certain mons meet some surprising and yet not-so-surprising relatives.

    • You all learn who this guy◊ truly is. Prewritten.

    • &?Some old Legendary freinds make a comeback, somehow.

    • &Umbra has a rough time coming out as bi... Thanks to one other aspect of his character. Solo parts prewritten, spagged parts and a few tweaks pending.

    • &Polly dream-visits her other descendant she mentioned in her last talk with Nico - and gives them a bit of info and advice.

    • &#Bartelby learns who Midnight Sun REALLY is.

    • &Silent's conversation with Steven Stone continues, and takes some interesting turns.

    (note: Most of the following was lumped together in the old list due to most of it being lower-key stuff, but I separated it all for this. They're also phrased more vaguely because of spoilers. :P)

    • Chrome does something small but important he never thought he would.

    • Ilya learns what it means to grow up.

    • A reunion unfolds again, but in a different time and place.

    • Someone close to Lina finds a new calling.

    • Umbra learns a bit more about the road ahead from a surprising source.

    • Both Arika and Umbra see themselves from a new perspective. Arika half prewritten, part of Umbra half prewritten with rest pending.

    Curtain Call

    • &Eclipseshipping wedding shebang, with no shortage of shenanigans and suprises.

    • Team Umbra finds a new calling.

    • &The Retirement Post, where everybody gets a happy ending. EVERYBODY (Certain parts will need to be spagged with other people, including Crow, Bitters, Pippi, Tracer, Mezzo, and Moon.)

    • Meta-wise a special farewell/retrospective post in Discussion, posted ASAP after.

    Post-Retirement Extras

    • Cameos via spagpost and "epilouge" interactions in the second forum (to preserve the sanctity of the retirement post being the last thing I personally post in the thread).

    • Epilogue-y oneshots and solo miniplots on the second forum, including:

      • Umbra and Lina find out how the fact both their Pokeforms can Mega Evolve affects them

      • A silly subplot Tracer suggested ages ago starring the Booster Trio, Yomi, Susano, and Issun.

      • Prism's Broken Pedestal moment regarding Umbra's past.

      • &Seishirou and Fuuma's Storytime

      • Anything else that comes to mind when I get the urge to re-visit my characters.

    • Heroes After All: WAAPT Version, a second forum WAAPT gaiden/prequel story and together with the Yangverse Version my next big project after I leave WAAPT proper.

    Stuff to do on side of endgame

    • That Yangverse Ballad of Robin spinoff, Rude Awakening. (Note: Needs to be done by May, so I'll need to get crackin'.)

    • Revising Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Plasbad and publishing the new and improved version on Serebii, AO 3, and beyond.

    • Plan out remaining WAAPT commissions I want to work with budget.

    • Possibly writing a couple non-Pokemon stories I'm doing for my sister and cousin, though that's lowest priority.

    But yeah, I'll keep this thing bookmarked and keep track of and update it as I go along. Stay tuned.
