    16801 Luke992423rd Nov 2013 01:49:40 PM from England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Noddin' my head like yeah
    Alright. So I've made a few posts over the past few days, so I guess you could consider me "back". As much as I would like to say that I'm not going away again, I have been feeling somewhat...concerned about the RP as a whole and my place in it.

    It may just be that I came back at a time that could be described as "filler", but a lot of the recent posts have been about Genderbending or Mindscapes or things like that. Now, I don't really consider these to be the sorts of things you would find in the Pokémon Franchise, since...well, they don't occur anywhere in it, really.

    Thing is - when it comes to Mindscapes at least - a lot of the RP consists of content like this. That doesn't make it wrong, it just means that this seems to be what the vast majority of the playerbase is interested in.

    And then there's me. I've never been interested in Mindscapes, or Genderbending, or the using the Pokémon -> Human armband on my mons. But that's what a lot of content is. So I'm not interested in most of it. And it's lead me to wonder whether I really fit in here or not.

    Again, it's not anyone's fault. I'm not saying that this stuff is bad and it shouldn't happen. It's just that I don't think of this sort of thing when it comes to Pokémon. Which leads to me thinking that I'm not finding what I'm looking for in this RP.

    "But what about your own posts? Can't you just be happy with them?" You say.

    I guess I could? But the above concerns lead me to think that they don't really fit in this RP either. In essence, I'm trying my hardest to fit a square peg in a round hole. That I don't have much in common with the other people here, and that it leads to me feeling like a bit of an outcast.

    Since Kalos is coming up, I'd be lying if I said that I didn't really want any of this sort of thing to take up too much of the spotlight. But that's not ideal at all. Which has made me wonder whether I should stay and take part, or leave to find another RP that suits me better.

    It's all very confusing. What do you think about all this?

    Okay, now that I'm lucid, I'm going to try elaborating on my EBIL TEEM idea.

    The Illusion Project

    To sum it up, these guys want to change the world for the better. One biological entity at a time. They want to make the world they live in a "perfect" world, one without conflict and misery.

    It should be noted that these guys are no Galactic or Flare. They like the world, and they realize the potential for good. Their problem is that the world is so tainted by the bad, the ugly, and the stupid that they want to change people. Not destroy, not create, change.

    They're looking for Mew, a Legendary who has been known for being able to change biology. They want to turn every single living thing into something that doesn't need conflict with others to be able to survive.

    However...

    When in Rome, they do as the Romans do. While their world is imperfect, they aren't afraid to create conflict to get what they want and need, because going against the system they see in the world is useless when it's clear that it's the only way to get anything done.

    Anyway, enough about the overall team. They've gotta have a leader, right?

    Illusion Project Leader

    "It's a shame this world's what it is. It's a shame what I am. It's a shame I'm too much of a hypocrite to be allowed to see my new world when it is innovated."-

    Name: Echo

    Gender: Female

    Affiliation: Need I repeat? :P

    Personality: Echo prefers to participate in her project's actions as little as possible. She oversees everything, but she doesn't want to be involved in the crimes her project members commit. Even authorizing them to do such things makes her guilty, but she does it anyway, just because there's no other way. She acknowledges that, if she had a spark of hope left in humanity's ability to fix itself, she'd be fighting her own project alongside the J-Team.

    Pokemon: I haven't fully decided yet on her full team, but my idea involves them all being based on urban legends. Alien sightings (Beheeyem), the Abominable Snowman (Abomasnow), the Loch Ness Monster (Milotic)... those sorts of mons.

    Note that I'm perfectly fine with this being shot down. I could easily implement them in the "new RP" I had in mind, which would give me a chance to use Dom and Steph as well.

    And who knows, I might use them in that other RP anyway. :P

    Note that they won't be "immediately threatening". In fact, they don't even have to be limited to Kalos, and could possibly seep into following arcs as well. Sure, Kalos would make their presence well known, but it doesn't have to be the last we see of them.
