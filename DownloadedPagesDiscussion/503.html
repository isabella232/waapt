    I vote for "make the battles look prettier."

    Anyway...

    I have some ideas for helping Rt AU along. Since some have calculated it'll take two more years to finish at its current rate, and we shouldn't really let that happen.

    I know such requests by me have been ignored in the past, and given some of my outbursts on the subject I don't blame you for ignoring it again. But I prefer you do not, since I think this can help.

    There are three things I can think of to move Rt AU along:

    1. Generally pick up the pace/do more with it. I recommend doing bits and pieces with the AU whenever there's not much going on with your other plots. Or setting aside days to focus primarily on the AU.

    2. Delegate the big responsibilities to more available players. A big part of the problem is that the people who need to do the big stuff aren't as available. If they can find some people they can trust to help them do said big stuff, things could very well move along faster.

    3. This may wind up annoying some people, but: Tone down/TS your plans. I can understand plans being very important to someone (Just look at me and Okamiquest) but given Rt AU's pacing problems I figure it's kind of necessary at this point to trim any plans that aren't essential and/or TS things in favor of the endgame. Because at this point I'm more concerned with this arc getting some resolution.

    Any suggestions to help any of these? Or other plans to move it along?

    And I realize I should be helping with all three of these things. >_> Fortunately, now that I have regular computer access again, I think I can.
