    And now a little something of my own...

    Arika's Race

    Long before Arika's reveal, (which was the biggest and most elaborately planned plot twist of my entire R Ping career) I always joked that her "canon" race was Ambiguously Brown, and even after said reveal (Laconic version for newbies: She's a MacGuffin Girl, and not only was her initial MacGuffin form magical and already sentient, it had absorbed the soul of a human woman named Ren who initially functioned as a Split Personality post-reveal, though eventually a Split-Personality Merge occurred) I still referred to her as such because of her supernatural origins (and because she was influenced character-design wise by Princess Kida, another ambiguously brown, highly supernatural character).

    However, as I'm struggling to include more racial diversity in my work, and realizing two of my P Cs are already confirmed white (Robin) or half white (Ammy as a human; only reason she isn't out-and-out Japanese is her parentage) and the other three (Nico, Umbra, and Lina) are all ambiguous but looks-wise are likely like either of the other two. So I felt it might be best to upgrade Arika from Ambiguously Brown to an actual RL race or ethnicity. Problem is: Her supernatural origins are still a factor.

    So I'm asking you guys three questions:

    1. Does her supernatural origins preclude her having a defined race/ethnicity?

    2. If not, what races/ethnicities could she reasonably be, given her appearance?

    3. If we pick one... How the hell does she find out?

    I gotta go, but answers to those questions are much appreciated. I'll have some stuff to say about LGBT things related to my characters once we find a solution.
