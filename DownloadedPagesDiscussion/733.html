    Sorry for the doublepost, but something that was mentioned in the chat was deemed important enough to put into Discussion.

    I was mulling over the idea of Clearance Levels, which would give the characters, or at least the authors the ability to know exactly what new characters will be allowed to know at any given point.

    Here's the list as it is right now.

    Level 0 - No real knowledge of the J-team whatsoever

    Level 1 - Watches the TV show.

    Level 2 - Fans of the show.

    Level 3 - J-Team newbies.

    Level 4 - Has "Not in show knowledge".

    Level 5 - Has Armband privileges.

    Level 6 - Has or has had one or more Legendary Item(s) in their possession.

    Level 7 - Has knowledge of Spirit World dealings (i.e. Has died and been resurrected)

    Level 8 - Fourth Wall Awareness

    Level 9 - You're a God-level Legendary.

    Level 10 - You're an Author.

    Thoughts, questions, comments, concerns? If we get this hammered out, I'll make the Wiki page, then people can start putting their characters in tiers if their authors choose to.
