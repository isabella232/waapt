    Sooooo... I've had some creative sparks today and I thought of a little media arc we could do:

    Kagerou Sub-Arc

    Kagerou Project is about a group of characters who have special powers that come from their eyes, who turn red upon being used. In this RP, mon versions of them can have those powers just from some of the abilities or moves that the Pokmon they'll be, so the only difference they would have from normal mons is that their abilities might work a bit differently and/or having their eyes turn red when they use them just for cosmetic reasons.

    The basic plot is that there's a group of Pokmon who apparently are some sort of shady organization, they would initially appear as just NP Cs (Kalos would work since there are already some mon groups already, like the Pawniard Army and the GreNinja Squad so they won't feel too out of place) and sometimes interact with the group, but eventually the P Cs could find more things about this group and later realize that there's more to them than meets the eye.

    Later (maybe in the Ranger Regions?) the arc could really start by having things start to go south and end up with everyone trapped in some sort of "Groundhog Day" Loop (though how I'm not sure, maybe Tagg's Adamant Orb could be stolen?) and having to figure just why it's happening and why it seems to revolve around those Pokmon. The arc might have some call backs to some other arcs in the backstory present in it, but nothing that would make much of an impact, it also sort of fits the theme of recovery, if that's still what we're going with by the time.

    There's the issue of the "Groundhog Day" Loop affecting everyone, even those that aren't going to get involved, but even in the original series itself it just affects an area instead of everyone, so those who don't get want to get involved just would have to avoid going into the area of effect. Once everything is settled up, time will be back to normal.

    Now, of course I'm being a little vague about what will actually happen, though you probably can guess some of it, but that's because if I go to deep into it, it sort of loses it's effect as I think this would work as a little "mystery sub-arc". For the most part, it'll be fairly light-hearted, though it'll hit some very dark spots but even then everything will turn out all right. I'm also going to put my own twists into the plot in a way that it doesn't spoil much of the original work, in case everyone plans on reading the manga or watching the anime once it gets released. If necessary, maybe I could talk over the details with someone in private to see if this arc could work out, but I'd like to keep that to a minimum.

    The arc would be mostly Slice of Life even after the main plot starts, as it's not just a single day that'll be repeating itself, instead it's probably around an entire week and For Want of a Nail would probably be in full play during it.

    So, thoughts on the idea? It's just something I came up with while bored at school, so I don't quite have everything thought of yet, but it seemed like a fun sub-arc to do since we seem to be at a shortage of things to do.
