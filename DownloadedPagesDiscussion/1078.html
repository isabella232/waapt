    Okay, I started thinking about this recently and wanted to give some thoughts on something that hasnt come up recently, but I was thinking about it recently, so I thought Id bring it up in general

    Not sure if Im phrasing this the best way, but Id call this

    Pokemon and Gender Expression

    (And just a note, Im phrasing this almost all in terms of the Gender Binary since thats easiest to explain with/how things seem to be split more often based on impressions)

    Anyway, Ill try and illustrate what I mean with some examples

    First, lets look at Lopunny. Lopunny is generally seen as a pretty feminine Pokemon appearance-wise. However, if you asked a given Lopunny if they themselves looked feminine, you wouldnt necessarily get a yes. See, Lopunny arent strictly female and male Lopunny might not see themselves as feminine. Theyd more likely determine what comes off as masculine or feminine to them in Lopunny terms.

    Second, lets look at Alakazam. Alakazam is a Pokemon with facial hair that would generally come off as masculine to humans. Since Alakazam are skewed male gender ratio-wise, there might be grounds for more Pokemon seeing Abra-line as male in general. Though, it might be a bit weird for Alakazam to consider having mustaches a masculine thing. Alakazam, unlike, Lopunny, though, do have an easily identifiable way to distinguish gender: the length of their mustaches. From that, many things could follow: Alakazam growing out their mustaches to seem more masculine or keeping them short to seem feminine, the Long-haired Pretty Boy equivalent being more of a Short-mustached Pretty Boy, Alakazam in turn viewing humans as generally feminine-looking due to even for those who grow facial hair, most dont grow it that long stuff like that seems to make more sense from an Alakazams perspective. Granted, that probably wouldn't be the *only* marker. Other markers might be there, they'd just be less obvious to humans.

    Lastly, lets look at Sylveon. Pink and ribbony, which generally indicates a feminine appearance. Does that mean Sylveon wouldnt see other Sylveon as feminine in general, though? Well, thats trickier. Since theres a definite choice involved in Eevee evolution, if Eevee perceive pink and ribbons as feminine, then Sylveon will likely be seen as a feminine choice, with everything that would follow. That said, like Lopunny, between Sylveon there would be masculine Sylveon, feminine Sylveon, gender-neutral Sylveon, and all sorts. And if a masculine Sylveon wanted to do something to demonstrate masculinity, it wouldnt be the human (or even Alakazam) way of a giant ribbon mustache, itd be however Sylveon do that, maybe taking tips from RL!foxes if they have a way of doing it.

    Anyway, I hope that was at least somewhat entertaining read. Hopefully itll at least be in peoples minds more in situations like this and maybe lead to some interesting ways of handling this kind of thing. ^_^
