    I'd like to start this post out with a few apologies of my own. Nobody has to forgive me if they don't want to, but I'm apologizing anyway.

    First, a general "I'm sorry" for all the drama I've been involved in, whether I was the source or not. It's done a number on everybody.

    Next, direct apologies.

    Crewe, I'm sorry. I can't tell for sure, but I'm pretty sure I pretty much destroyed what little faith you still had in humanity. And I'm the type of person who wants to restore faith in humanity. I wish I could somehow find a way to do so for you.

    Sixth, I'm sorry for all the hell I've put you through. I've never meant to hurt you, but I ended up doing so anyway, with the Bittersweet hoax. Even with the positive things that came out of that, I'm still being eaten up by the negatives long after I tried to get it off my shoulders.

    To Wolf and Tangent, I'm sorry for the emotional blackmail I've put you two - and everyone else, but especially you - through, when you were trying to keep us level-headed. Especially Wolf, who I've even insulted for managing to keep relatively sane by staying out of the chats. I'm sorry.

    And hell, I'm even sorry to Jho. I'm very pissed about his attempt to repeat my mistake, but even so, I was way too harsh about it. I let the past two weeks, which have pretty much been The Perfect Shitstorm for me even IRL, get to my head, and I broke down yet again.*
    I'm still incredibly paranoid about who I can trust, but that's also partially due to something that happened IRL; I'll explain in PM for those who want to know.
     Let it be known that, whether he does get this message or not, I mean every single word I'm saying right now.

    The next section is not meant to push blame away from myself. That kinda defeats the purpose of the above apologies. I am trying to prevent it from happening to any of us in the future. It's probably a futile attempt, but I'd rather go down trying to pull us together than pushing each other away.

    A lot of the drama is caused by burnout. Burnout caused by Arc Fatigue. We all know the reception previous excessively-long arcs have gotten, and this one leaves all others in the dust.

    I know Tangent is probably thinking something along the lines of "we need to take a minute to calm down to an operable level." I appreciate the thought... but the thing is, we may very well have been pushed past the point where we can possibly think clearly. If we have, no amount of "calming down" will fix it, because each minute we wait means another second we have to deal with having sensitive moods and having to be surrounded by other sensitive moods.

    The only way we can solve it is, we need to stop talking about change and start doing change. I can't exactly contribute to discussing ideas for Unova 2, but those who have more availability need to have a nice, long brainstorming session, and pull stuff out of that to use. As it stands, we've got minor ideas, but no reasons to go there or to stay there once we do get there. And we need to get something done quick, because I don't know how long we can last before our burnout destroys us completely.

    As for our current stuff, if you don't want to abandon ship for it, time shenanigans may be necessary for everyone's sanity. Rather than feeling like people are holding each other back, get both done. Kanjoh, Magic Mirror, and PEFE can be "past", and Unova 2 can be "present". Obviously, it'll only take effect once we get something figured out for Unova 2.

    I'm sorry if this section sounded at all harsh. I'm kinda desparate for a way to fix it, because the truth is, the stress has done a number on my mind, and even almost destroyed me IRL. I don't want that to happen to me again or to anyone else. If anyone thinks my train of thought is on the wrong track, feel free to say so. I'll understand.

    And one final message...

    Luke.

    I'll be honest with you.

    Yesterday morning, I was fully prepared to attempt to arrange with everyone else to give you exactly one more chance. One mistake, and you'd be gone. I was prepared to see if you'd actually be able to redeem yourself, and I admittedly had my doubts.

    Then suddenly, Perfect Shitstorm Chapter 14 was published, and you never even took that 50th chance you wanted.

    But now, it seems you didn't need that chance. You redeemed yourself without it. Perhaps not 100%, but it's clear you're starting to change. In a good way.

    Thank you.

    I don't know what the others think, but... I'd kinda like you to come back. I want to see you prove yourself, prove that you're a better guy than the one who left so long ago. I honestly think you can do it. It may be something everyone needs - something that makes us feel proud.

    Although if you really think you should leave, I understand. You'll be ending on a high note, that's for sure.

    ...And I think that's all I have to say. Maybe I'll think of more later, maybe I won't. But I promise all of you, I wouldn't have spent several hours typing this up last night if I didn't mean it all.
