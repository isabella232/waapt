(It's funny, here I'm the grizzled veteran and there I'm an up and coming newbie, despite having a Serebii account years before I joined WAAPT. But anyway...) One person I've been getting particularly, surprisingly close to is Dragonfree, author of such fics as The Quest for the Legends and Morphic, and webmaster of Cave Of Dragonflies. Despite her reputation for grim and depressing fics and being a very tough fic reviewer she's actually really nice and we've been chatting it up and she's been giving me constructive feedback on my fics and even helping me with advice on future projects - including HAA.

    Now, Dragonfree is one of two people on Serebii (aside from a couple WAAP Ters on there I've told) who knows HAA's true premise. And when I first told her what it was... she essentially fangirled.

    Now you're probably wondering, exactly what could get a grizzled veteran even remotely excited about the up and coming newbie's project? I'll give you a hint: As much as Dragonfree does the grim and depressing stuff one of her favorite tropes in all of fiction is strong platonic friendships.
