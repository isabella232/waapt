    I agree with Tagg. And honestly, the way some people ignore each other, we may as well be running several separate R Ps sometimes. Granted, it may not be that bad at the moment, but it's still rather appalling.

    Even is events don't directly pertain to your character, they can still have effects on you at some point down the line. Even if you can't read someone's posts (Which is acceptable), or don't want to (Which isn't), you could at least ask the author to sum things up for you so that you at least can grasp what's happening.

    Also, from what I may have gathered (Which may be grossly wrong, so sue me if it is), apparently certain people going out of the way to world-build is "hogging the RP." Personally, I'm saddened that the very act to treat this RP as more than twenty people and their Pokémon going around causing wanton property damage having adventures as hogging the RP saddening.

    You have to remember that this isn't a forum game any more. Back then, it was fine to have legendaries and do the sort of thing that would end up with half the region fearing our character's names because we weren't so fussed about continuity back then. But now that we're an RP and we're dealing with an established continuity, we have to remember that:

    • 1) Our actions have consequences for other peoplemons, whether good or bad.
    • and 2) Worldbuilding is good, but if you can't do it, please don't stop others from doing it.

    Okay, that's everything. Feel free to Tell me how much this post sucks provide constructive feedback.

    Oh well, here we are at it again.

    There was some private discussion today (yesterday), of which logs will be made available somehow, when I'm less tired. Don't worry, should be afternoon today localtime anyway Some of the conversation went around the issue of the chatroom and its usage.

    Bottom line, the chatroom promotes instantaneousness as well as certain not very likable behaviours. For which we have behaved immaturely. Immaturity leads to GIFT. GIFT leads to suffering. Overused reference is overused. But it causes some serious issues. For example, the chatroom is supposed to help us planning. But with that much drama and randomness going on, new ideas, questions and suggestions are more or less swallowed and torn by the tide, just ask Umbramatic about it (no offense pal; I'll get to answer your newest Giratina concerns sometime). Add to that that we aren't reflecting the decisions that we do manage to make in chatroom into the Discussion thread a.k.a. here. Likely the excess noise has prevented most serious attempts at discussing potential plots recently, in particular as to what comes next TA/TM wise.

    So, the usage of the chatroom has to change. Two plans, at least, were discussed as potential strategies, and we would like your input for them.

    The first plan is to move each of creative discussion, off-topicness and drama to their own chatroom. This involves the creation of a third chatroom, which would probably be "waapt-offtopic". The normal chatroom will become dedicated to actual, honest to Arceus creative RP and/or Pokémon related discussion, while all the YOUR FACE jokes, favourite Legendary and similar stuff would go to the offtopic one.

    The second plan is to separate into only two chatrooms, which is what we have now: a creative one and a drama one. Offtopicness and randomness would go into the normal chatroom as it has always been, but they will become more or less moderated as with all discussion. In what sense? Well, we'll have established mods (still to be chosen) who will pick subjects of discussions as well as lock the /topic for easier searchability and record. That way we can switch between discussion about a particular subject or the randomness of the day, accordingly.

    Regardless of choice, two key points will be enforced (somehow): first, drama will be moved to the drama chatroom, and second, important decisions and process about the RP discussed in the chatroom MUST be mirrored into Discussion a.k.a. here. Ideally messages of the kind of "Silentran♂: by the way guys, remember to post in Discussion, g'night" should never have to happen again.

    Also we consider a key factor in the choice the fact that for Speequers, switching across yet another channel would be a burden.

    So, thoughts on this? There were other subjects, but we wanted to bring this particular one to the front.
