    So, one problem I deal with frequently in this RP is that often I can't do much about my characters due to the RP's quick pace and I frequently making things up as the RP goes, so, with that in mind after watching this video, I had an idea of a small Spin Off/Fanfiction of sorts.

    Diary of a Blooming Flower

    The plot is basically what you expect: Sakura goes back to Floaroma and discovers and old diary that she had when she still lived there and starts writting on it everything that happens to her from that day, most of the narration will be in Sakura's POV, I'm doing this mostly to put more depth on my characters, especially Sakura since everything is from her eyes, and also to improve my writting a bit. Of course, when all that happens will be left completely ambiguous, be it in the near future or not, or even if it's canon or not, YMMV. The chapters all follow a pattern, the first one after that prologue will be telling what happened to Sakura and her group on that day, the second one will be a shorter one where Sakura writes about a specific character, basically telling the reader everything she thinks about the character in question, the next chapter is another "adventure" and so on.

    Nothing noteworthy, but I was thinking of including other P Cs in the story, with permission of course, having them have a more developed interaction with Sakura and her team in one of the chapters as a guest star of sorts, followed by Sakura's thoughts on the PC in question, I think that would be good way to expand a bit on the characters in certain aspects, but maybe that's just me. Do note however that I'm not sure how that would work, it's just an idea that I had for now and also that each chapter may take a long time to be done considering school starts soon, but it's something mostly just for fun.

    A less important matter, I'm open to suggestions of things for Sakura and the team to do, I can think of ways to adapt the ideas in a way that it works for the story
