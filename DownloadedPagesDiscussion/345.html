    A Dilemma

    OK, as you can all probably tell, Chrome's shipping partner is currently Arika. As you can also probably tell, this relationship is made possible (and generally more feasable) by Chrome borrowing Ammy's armband. Normally, said relationship of Chrome having to depend on something of Ammy's is fine by me, as it creates an interesting dynamic and ballance.

    However, I have encountered a problem with this dynamic. Namely, that Ammy will be separated from the others for at least a good chunk of two arcs.

    In light of this, I have determined two solutions:

    1. Have Chrome borrow another armband while Ammy is separated. This situation allows for a similar dynamic and is less likely to rile parts of the playerbase. I was thinking of using Skye's, because her realtionship with Diane isn't dependent on it (Bird cuddles are cute!) However, this requires me to be HEAVILY dependent on another player, which may not be fesable due to RL and such and can be annoying in general for both sides.

    A. Chrome goes on an elaborate sidequest I'm planning to get his own armband. On the bad end of things, this solution may rile up parts of the playerbase, add to the already bloated number of armbands, and mess with dynamics/balance. On the other, I have precedent for where he could find one, he'd definitely have to earn it, and I wouldn't be dependent on other players. (Plus, then he, Arika, Umbra, and Ammy could go on double dates! :3)

    So, any votes, thoughts, concerns, and alternative solutions will be appreciated.
