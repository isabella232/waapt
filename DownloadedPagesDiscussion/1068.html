    I got bored while playing Yugioh so I've taken to making WAAPT character decks. This one is Ren's.

    Yeah screencaps suck like that >.>

    Anyways because of Ren's fabulous sense of style and his skills as a coordinator I thought Performapals would be appropriate for him so I literally took one of every Performapal card and threw it in a deck along with some Odd-Eyes and Magician cards.

    Performapals are thematically circus animals and performers who have effects based around level manipulation, attack boosting and drawing. They're a pendulum based deck so they have a lot of swarm potential and are reliant on combos and they work well with the Odd-Eyes theme. Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon, while tricky to actually summon, basically guarantees game with it's board clear and burn damage and 3 attacks. But Ren's a show-off so he should have no problem.

    More character decks to come.
