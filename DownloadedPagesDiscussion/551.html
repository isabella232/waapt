    Regarding the recent tensions, I think that in any given major story arcs, somethings just are unable to fully work with. Especially in an arc like this. Between Holon and Conquest, there are too many side characters to keep up with. I'm personally dealing with upwards of 20 already, and that's just the ones that have been shown on screen. I have described a good 40 characters in the process of these arcs. It is impossible to expect anyone to be able to use every idea that they have for every character, especially if the character in question is something they are not used to portraying. I think it is unreasonable to hold people to every idea for 40 characters. I think it is unreasonable to hold people to every idea for 3 characters. A lot of ideas will come and go, and sometimes, it just doesn't pan out. It happens.
