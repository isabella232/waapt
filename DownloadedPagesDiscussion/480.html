    Guess Who...?
    So... Tangent brought up a fun proposal for introducing some inevitable mechanical shakeups in the RP over PM earlier today. And there's been a little bit of discussion regarding it...

    So... Here's the gist of it...

    Introduction of the Fairy-Type to the WAAPTverse

    • Starts in RtAU as a response by the Legendaries countering Rules Lawyering with their own Rules Lawyering
    • May start out as a ill-defined typing for who gets to have it, enabling one-offs such as a temporarily part-fairy Inka.
      • May be stolen wholesale from a part of the multiverse that already has had Fairy Typing on the ground for ages.
    • By the end of things, the typing chart would even out to be what it is in X and Y when it launches.
    • In the midst of all of that, the legendaries of the AU are busy spreading the word to other legendaries in the multiverse through cosmic imageboards. (For now, we will presume that Giratina, being the Renegade Pokemon and connoisseur of reality-warping media, would be the most likely offender. On what will temporarily be known as "108chan".)
    • Legendaries from other universes decide that it's a cool idea whether by getting annoyed at every competitive team on TV being half-dragon or simply having one too many sips of some spiked punch, start implementing the typing chart retooling in their own verses.
    • Fairy-Types become a meme among the multiverse's Legendaries, and ultimately work their way into the WAAPTverse, much to every Dragon-type's consternation.

    It's a little goofy... But I figured that given our credentials... That it might merit doing in place of a dry cosmic retcon for the story / gag value.

    In anyone supportive or opposed to this proposal?
