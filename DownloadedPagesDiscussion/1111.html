    27755 Umbramatic7th Feb 2016 10:31:48 PM from WAAPT or PEFE Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: In another castle
    The eternal dusk returns
    Double post because... There was something special that I meant to do yesterday, but dumped that awful ranty shit instead, so while it's a bit late I felt I still would drop this:

    It didn't start with me. It started with some plane tickets to Unova.

    Those little tickets sparked something. And it started crude and simple and kinda dumb, but it always, always, had potential. And as it went along, it lived up to it. It grew from a crude little assemblage of some posters dicking around with wild abandon and used hat as a base, an oh so wonderful base, to turn into one of the most fun interpretations of the Pokemon world I had ever seen. And when I was roped into it all in the midst of that blossoming I was pulled along for the ride and grew and improved myself, and this RP went from something I thought I would grow bored with and fade out from into a few moths into an over four-year adventure that despite all the difficulties - and there have been ungodly amounts, as you all saw just yesterday - I deeply have grow to personally care about and view as one of the most wonderful experiences of my life.

    I've learned so much in this RP. About writing, about characters, about stories as a whole. About freindship and trust and enjoying the silly and fun things in one's bonds with others even as you learn to deal with arguments and diffrences and moving past them. And it's given me drive to move onward to what's next, my wrapup I'm still trying to complete and fortunately (though no guarantees, I could be delayed by awful crap to still being here a year from now from all I know, but knock on wood, fingers crossed, all that, and I genuinely don't have too much left to finish) see the end in sight in the near-ish future - but so I can take my time here and use it to build on my own interpretation of the Pokemon world in the Yangverse further, and from there go beyond to write stories more thoroughly my own.

    But as I head out, you guys are throwing back to those crude old days in a way making hem new and bigger and even better - once again fighting a glitch in Sinnoh threatening all of reality, but taking all the RP has grown and changed in with us. And while I sadly cannot take part due to trying to pack my bags to give my grand finale and head off to new horizons as soon as possible, I salute and support the rest of you all in doing so.

    Heh... Five years. Most R Ps are lucky to last five months. And it's the same month the franchise this RP is built around is celebrating 20 years.

    For that, the wider franchise has adopted the mantra "train on" This RP's called We Are All Pokemon Trainers. So that goes double for us, former evil overlords and immortal wanderers and powerful mons mons giving humanity a try and legendary artifacts giving humanity a try and teen psychics with more potential than anyone would think and even guest star Fire Emblem characters allong for the ride and everyone in between alike.

    Train on, WAAPT. Train on.

    - Umbramatic, wishing well for WAAPT's fifth birthday

    "Did you hear about those plane tickets to Unova that just opened up? Just got some for me and my Pokemon from Prof. Oak!"
