    Let me just say three things

    • 1) We are not saying that you can't have characters who are ambiguous or have a white heritage. We just want more people to think about it when creating characters.

    • 2) Researching stuff for creating minority characters isn't that big of a barrier as you might think. Tagg has linked the wonderful Writing With Color blog that you can all have a peek at. I have also generally found Googling "____ Culture" or "____ History" are good jumping points. From personal experience, I have found some great websites such as this one by googling "Romani Culture" and having an afternoon read on a rainy day. And googling specific phrases and ethnic specific food recipes are also a great jumping off point. Plus you get to learn some real cool shit. (Have you guys tried Shakshouka? That shit is the shit)

    • 3) Nothing exists in a vacuum. Saying "it doesn't matter" is not gonna help things. Again, this is just to get more people thinking, don't take it as an attack.

    It's been simmering in some of our minds (Tagg, Every, Myself etc.) for a while now. We just decided to bring it to the forefront as of recent.
