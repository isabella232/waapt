    The basic oremise is that there are dozens of small potential rifts around Pokepan, made by the Time Train's travels in its past, like a dent a pen makes in a piece of paper. Other things, like Pokemon and people, came through later, widening those rifts. The job of the J-Team would be, put simply, to put the displaced entites back through the rift, allowing those rifts to properly fade. However, a secondary force is trying to prevent this, having his own reasons for keeping the rifts open, and there are plenty of others that could gain from open rifts.

    For example, say a herd of ancient Mamoswine rumble through to the present from the ice age. One of those Mamoswine is the one who opened the rift up, and it essentially has pieces of time on it. If it's pushed back through the rift, the pieces will stick to the rift and reknit itself. But perhaps Cipher wants to keep this rift open, to capture any migrating ancient Pokemon and shadowfy them. So you might have Cipher also involved for this part.
