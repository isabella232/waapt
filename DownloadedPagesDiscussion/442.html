    So, I'm starting to see the signs of the fact that our current story arc is going to fall apart as we get close to the end (like what has happened with Hoenn, PMD-R, Kanjoh and may possibly happen to Rt AU), and from what I can tell from the discussions on the matter, Holon and Orre are likely to suffer the same fate.

    I don't think it's wise to enter a plot, and then begin trying to figure out the cause of it all. I mean, these things need to be decided ahead of time, but since we haven't planned anything together as a team in a long time, things just get awkward and the plot gets a disappointing conclusion.

    Really, guys. We need to better plan things out ahead of time. I mean, if there's going to be a time limit on things, we need to at least have things planned out so the climax doesn't just end up as a rushed and awkward mess thrown together at the last minute.

    I know we're capable of some pretty great story telling, so surely we'll be able to figure out how to increase the quality of the story telling when it comes to our main plots?

    I'm sorry if this comes across as whining, but I really am getting concerned about how this arc will play out if we don't manage to sort out certain details and when certain things will happen.

    So, should we start by trying to figure out a timeline for when things will happen for the rest of this arc? Since we've reached a certain point earlier than I expected, I'm in kind of an awkward spot since Setsuna and Mirage logically should've already evolved.
