    Li'l Punk
    Okay, so I need to work on explaining myself before leaving, but I was getting increasingly strong feelings of "don't want to be here/do this", so I decided to bail and do biology homework.

    But it turns out biology homework is actually super boring, so I looked at the logs and realized I did not explain myself at all.

    The reason I wasn't feeling up for a group battle isn't because it would be too intensive. It's because it would mostly boil down to fidgeting in my seat while occasionally typing out "yep, so-and-so is still fighting", and that just has no appeal to me. "Reassurances" that all that would be expected of me is an occasional existence post are actually confirming my fears. I don't want to make existence posts. They don't involve any sort of challenge or creativity. They're boring. If it's an existence post outside of battle, at least it has the possibility of leading to something fun. But a "uh-uh, totes there and contributing" is just a token participation while waiting for the inevitable conclusion to happen. Which just seems pointless and uninteresting to me.

    I could try and come up with something to shake things up, sure, but I find the more people involved in the battle, the less effect any efforts to shake up the circumstances will have.

    So, that's my rationale for not wanting to bother with the Dragnor battle. Also, I am impulsive and easily bored, so there's that.
