    Anyway, we're just about ready to kick things off, but there are a few important things that need clarifying first:

    Where do we start?

    We've already determined that we'll be starting off in the Unova region, but the question is, where exactly? A town of city would probably be the best place for a bunch of trainers and Pokémon to meet casually, so perhaps one of the major population centers like Castelia or Nimbasa City would be our best bet?

    On Black/White 2 Spoilers

    Considering that the upcoming Pokémon games are set in this region, it raises the issue of whether to incorporate material from these games. Some of us may not be keen on having any facets from these games - be it plot details, or simply where you can catch certain Pokémon - spoiled. Perhaps it would be for the best to forbid using anything from these games until a few weeks after their release come October, but I am open to other options.
