    39698 Luke92415th May 2011 11:17:47 AM Get RP Mod
    "...Phew, that's everything. Any questions?"

    A certain trainer has just finished explaining the plan of attack to his gathered Pokémon, Ho-oh, and Reshiram.

    Ho-oh: No questions from me.

    Reshiram: <None from me.>

    Luke's team all indicate that they have nothing that needs answering about the plan either.

    "Alright...but there's one more thing, Lake?"

    Lake: <Yes?>

    He takes his belt off, offering it to the Feraligatr.

    "Take it."

    Lake blinks, taken-aback.

    Lake: <Huh? Me?>

    "Yes. Reshiram can't carry the whole team, so you should keep everyone else in their balls while she's carrying you there."

    Lake: <Oh...alright, that sounds reasonable.>

    Lake inwardly sighs with relief. For a moment there, he thought Luke was asking him to take over as the trainer or something! Happy that it wasn't the case, he takes the human's belt, and despite being several sizes too small, manages to clip it on around his waist, with only some minor discomfort.

    Woolly: <How is it?>

    Lake: <Oof...it's a bit tight, but I think it'll be okay.>

    A certain Zoroark, however, doesn't seem so happy about this situation.

    Phantom: <Please don't tell me that this guy's our trainer now.>

    Luke responds with a frown aimed in Phantom's direction.

    "Well, yes. He will be for a little while, at least."

    Wait...what?!

    Lake almost jumps in shock as the team exchange awkward glances, unsure of what to make of this.

    "You're all okay with that, aren't you?"

    Before Lake can say anything, Woolly nods cheerfully.

    Woolly: <Yep! I'm fine with it!>

    Ynite chips in with a nod of his own.

    Unite: <Yeah, I think Lake's got what it takes to be in charge!>

    As does Wulfric, if very shyly.

    Wulfic: <.I think so too.>

    Rather than a nod, Ad Min flips in mid-air, his detached body swilling around as the virtual bird emits a cheerful beep.

    Ad Min: I've processed the calculations, and after preforming 2.4 million simulations this past second, I have determined that Lake has a 82.6 percent chance of successfully completing the mission!

    A few of the less technologically savvy Pokémon give Ad Min weird looks, Ho-oh and Reshiram in particular, who are naturally stumped by any sort of technobabble.

    Reshiram: <...In monese, please?>

    Ad Min: Oh! My apologies. It means that I fully support Lake in this executing this operation!

    Ho-oh: Ah, that cleared things up.

    Reshiram: <He sounded just like Zekrom whenever he has a new gadget to fawn over...

    That done, everyone present turns to Phantom, who has remained silent up to this point. At first she doesn't notice, but after seeing that everyone is giving he an expectant look, she sighs as she dismissively waves a paw at them.

    Phantom: <Okay, okay, I'll do whatever you want. Sheesh...>

    It seemed if all of a sudden, Lake had a lot to live up to. The entire plan's success seemed to hinge on his not messing up, and it was a huge responsibility. If he failed, he wouldn't just be letting Luke down, but his entire team, Ho-oh AND Reshiram.

    He really didn't want to look bad in front of the legendaries. Especially his patron deity.

    "So, are you okay with this, Lake? And is something wrong? You haven't said anything for about a minute."

    Lake blinks at Luke...before giving him a nervous smile and a nod.

    Lake: <Yeah...I think I can handle it. And I'm fine, don't worry.>

    Luke responds with a grin of his own.

    "I knew you would."

    Looking rather uncomfortable at drawing attention to himself, Wulfric nervously raises a paw.

    Wulfric: <You do know how to use our Pokéballs...Right?>

    Lake nods at the Lucario.

    Lake: <Um, yeah. I've seen Luke do it enough times to know what to do. Even if it is a bit awkward with my large hands.>

    Pokéballs were not built with Feraligatr in mind.

    "Well, you'll need to recall everyone before we can get going, so why don't you practice?"

    Lake: <I guess I could give it a shot.>

    Lake glances downward at Luke's, now his, belt, locates Woolly's Pokéball, then pulls it off...only to fumble and drop it on the floor.

    Lake: <Oops!>

    He bends down to pick it back up, giving everyone a sheepish look as Phantom facepalms in the background.

    Woolly: <Go on Lake! Just point it at me!>

    Lake does so, pointing it at the Ampharos. Instantly, a beam of red light emerges from the ball, engulphing Woolly and pulling her into the sphere, successfully recalling her.

    "There you go, that wasn't so hard, was it?"

    Lake: <Yeah...I Guess not.>

    The Feraligatr proceeds to recall the rest of the team to their balls, thankfully without dropping any more in the process. As he does so, Reshiram and Ho-oh watch from the side with interest.

    Reshiram: <Heh, if I didn't know any better Lake, I would say that you we're a Pokémon trainer at the sight of you wearing that belt!>

    Ho-oh chuckles at the thought.

    Ho-oh: I admit, the idea of Lake acting like a trainer is certainly a novel one!

    Hearing the legendaries comments, Lake nods awkwardly.

    Lake: <So, should we go and meet up with everyone else now?>

    "Yeah. As far as I know, they're on the Sophian Road, so I guess we should be making our way over there."

    With that, the group heads off to meet with the others.
