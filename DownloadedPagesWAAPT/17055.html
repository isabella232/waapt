    426373 ILoveDogs3rd Nov 2014 06:23:41 PM from Lunn Guyland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Hounds of love are hunting

    Office, 12 AM

    -Munn picks up the phone-

    Munn: Hello?

    Phone Guy: ~Hello? Uh, you the new guy?~

    Munn: ...I literally woke up here thirty seconds ago, so I guess?

    Phone Guy: ~Uh, OK. Listen, I worked this job right before you did. I'm finishing up my last week, actually. I just wanna, um, show you a few things. I know this job can seem overwhelming, but I'm gonna tell you that there's nothing to worry about. Especially a guy like you, uh, what's your name...?~

    Munn: Oh. I don't have one.

    Phone Guy: ~Oh, that makes things a lot easier. Anyway, uh, I'm just supposed to read a few things to you, you know? Legal things.~

    Munn: Keh. OK.

    Phone Guy: ~All right, ahem: "Welcome to Freddy Teddi's Pizza. A magical place for kids and grown-ups alike, where fantasy and fun come to life. Teddi Entertainment is not responsible for damage to property or person."~

    Munn: Yeah, yeah. I'm working at a Pete E Pika's?

    Phone Guy: ~"Upon discovering that damage or death has occurred, a missing person report will be filed within 90 days, or as soon property and premises have been thoroughly cleaned and bleached, and the carpets have been replaced."

    Munn: Death? Oh, this just got interesting.

    Phone Guy: ~...OK, I think you'll do pretty well if you keep that mindset. Just keep that in mind, OK? Good. Now, check the security camera for a second, OK? Go to the Show Stage camera.~

    Munn: Alright...

    -he does so. He sees three human sized animatronics, all three of which must have looked incredibly jolly in the light. As it stands, however, the Teddiursa, Buneary and Torchic serve only to vaguely unnerve-

    Phone Guy: ~Those animatronics get a bit quirky at night, but do you really blame them? If I had to sing the same stupid songs for 20 years and never got a bath, I'd be a bit irritable, too.~

    -he chuckles the odd sort of chuckle that people do when they make a bad joke that they've made many times before. Munn halfheartedly chuckles along-

    Phone Guy: ~Anyway, these characters do tend to wander a bit at night-~

    Munn: Wait, they do? That's not how animatronics works. I think...? Aren't they rooted in one place?

    Phone Guy: ~These aren't. They're left in free roaming mode at night, something about their servos locking up if they stay in one place for too long. Uh, they used to be allowed to walk around during the day, too, but then the Bite of '87 happened.~

    Munn: ...the bite of what now?

    Phone Guy: ~Did you know that people can live without a frontal lobe?~

    Munn: Ah. That I did know.

    Phone Guy: ~OK, uh, that's great! Well, concerning your safety, the only real concern is if the characters catch you after dark, they probably won't recognize you as a person. They'll probably see you as an endoskeleton without a costume.~

    Munn: -observes his metal arms and legs-

    Phone Guy: ~Now, that's against the rules here at Freddy Teddi's, so they'll probably forcefully stuff you into a Freddy Teddi suit. The thing about those suits is that they have a bunch of crossbeams, wires and animatronic devices, especially around the facial area. So...that could lead to some discomfort...and death.~

    -he gives a nervous chuckle; Munn, annoyed, mimicks it-

    Phone Guy: ~Yeah, they don't tell you that at orientation-~

    Munn: I didn't get any orientation! I just woke up here, and it looks like I've been working here a long time!

    Phone Guy: ~Anyway, first day should be a breeze. Um, check those cameras, and remember to only close those doors if absolutely necessary. OK? See ya.~

    -he hangs up. Munn silently lowers the phone and shakes his fist to the heavens-

    Munn: TIIIIIIIIINNNNNNNYYYYYYYYY!
