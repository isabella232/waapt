    Sootopolis City

    Lucius is resting outside the Pokemon Center, humming under his breath, when he receives a message following up a conversation from several days ago.

    From: Lyuri To: Lucius

    Heya, I've gotten the location of the thing from Tagg. Meet up with Duke outside the Pokémon Center and he'll fill you in.

    Reading through the message, Lucius sighs.

    Lucius: Well, I suppose I had to do this sooner or later. Let's get this over with, shall we?

    He pockets his Pokegear and starts heading towards the Pokemon Center. At the entrance, Duke was stretching himself and looked like he had just gotten up, he soon notices Lucius coming over and waves at him.

    Duke: Hey there!

    Lucius: Hey. You know where we're going, then?

    Duke: Not the exact location, unfortunately. Tagg was a bit vague about it.

    Lucius facepalms.

    Lucius: Great. So in other words, we're in pretty much the same situation as we were before asking him about it. What are we going to do, teleport to the north of Sinnoh and just hope we stumble across an ancient, well-concealed ruin that's successfully remained hidden for centuries?

    Duke: Well, he said that it was north of Stark Mountain and that it's a pretty hard to miss Cathedral, shouldn't be too hard to find.

    Lucius: ...I dunno how to break this to you, Duke, but north Sinnoh is a pretty big place. Visibility's not great either, what with all the freaking blizzards blowing around.

    Duke: I guess there's just not too much we can use for reference when trying to point out where exactly it is. But we can give it a shot, right?

    He rubs the back of his neck awkwardly.

    Lucius: It's not like we have any other options. Still, I am totally blaming Tagg if we both end up freezing to death.

    He looks Duke up and down.

    Lucius: Are you sure you're dressed for a polar expedition? That shirt looks way too tight to insulate you against sub-zero temperatures.

    Duke looks down at his shirt.

    Duke: Well, I guess I could just button up my jacket.

    Lucius: ...I guess being born a Lucario provides some natural resistance to the cold. Having not had that privilege myself, unfortunately, I need to go change.

    He heads into the Pokemon Center. A few minutes later, he comes out looking like a black-clad, heavily bundled-up Sheik.

    Lucius: This'll do. You ready to go?

    Duke: I'm good. If nothing else I'll be able to detect a huge concentration of Aura with my sensing abilities once we start getting closer.

    Lucius: That's something, I suppose. I still can't get used to not having Aura Sense... I took it for granted when I was alive, and now that I've lost it, it's like losing an eye or something.

    He sighs, then sends out Maul.

    Lucius: Still, if all goes to plan, this is our chance to change that.

    Duke: I can imagine. If I lost my Aura sense, I would probably be very lost even when I can still see just fine.

    He chuckles.

    Duke: Anyway, we should get going, right?

    Maul: ~I agree.~

    They all disappear with a blip.

    Spagpost Part 2: Electric Boogaloo

    Sinnoh, Far North

    The trio of Lucius, Duke and Maul find themselves in a featureless expanse of white forest with snow billowing all around them.

    Lucius: ...Yup, we're in Sinnoh, alright.

    Duke: I kinda missed it.

    He chuckled some and then started sensing for any large concentrations of Aura. The forest is filled with the Auras of wild Pokemon, but the strongest concentration of energy appears to be further north.

    Lucius: Can't say I did, really. Though the cold's not as bad as I remember... that might have something to do with being undead, however.

    Duke: In any case, we should keep going further north. There are quite a few wild Pokémon here though, so stay alert.

    Lucius rolls his eyes.

    Lucius: I'm still a trainer, Duke. I know that I ought to be careful in the tall grass. Though I'd think that wild Pokemon would prefer live prey.

    He starts fording his way through the snow. Duke sighs and just follows behind Lucius. For someone of such diminutive stature, Lucius sets an impressive pace, seemingly unfazed by the cold and the snow. Eventually, he disappears from sight in the blizzard, though his Aura can still be sensed up ahead.

    Duke: Hey, Lucius! Try to slow down a bit, I can't even see you anymore without my Aura sense!

    Duke then begins speeding up his pace as well in order to catch up. As if in response, Lucius's Aura does stop moving forwards... and starts heading back towards Duke. A few moments later, he comes back into view, running frantically through the treeline.

    Lucius: Sorrysorrysorrysorrysorrysorry!

    He's immediately pursued by a giant Ursaring bursting out of the forest behind him.

    Ursaring: <WHO DARES DISTURB MY ETERNAL SLUMBER?>

    Seeing that, Duke quickly gets an Aura Sphere out and fires it at the Ursaring, if only to stall it in order for Lucius to get more distance from it. After firing it, he takes a Pokéball out and sends Recette out.

    Recette: <Yayfications~ What are we do->

    She then notices the Ursaring.

    Recette: <Eep!>

    The Ursaring lets out a fearsome roar upon being hit by Duke's Aura Sphere, though this only serves to draw a horde of wild Pokemon to it.

    Ursaring: <COME, MY FOLLOWERS! THE STARBORN ONES HAVE SEEN FIT TO BLESS US WITH MORTAL SACRIFICES! PREPARE THE RITUAL!>

    Sneasel 1: <...Who are the Starborn Ones? And what ritual?>

    Sneasel A: <Does it matter? I'm not about to ask questions when there's a free meal in front of me.>

    The Sneasels pounce, only for one of them to be blinded mid-leap by a Dazzling Gleam and crash straight into a tree.

    Maul: ~Wrong way.~

    Meanwhile, the Ursaring charges Duke with a Hammer Arm whilst Lucius ducks down into the snow, apparently hoping it's deep enough for him to disappear beneath. Duke quickly leaps out of the way of the Hammer Arm

    Duke: Recette, put him back to sleep!

    Recette: <'kay!>

    She then floats over in front of the Ursaring and begins using Hypnosis on him.

    Recette: <Sleep... Sleep...>

    The Ursaring stares at Recette for a moment.

    Ursaring: <...NO.>

    It promptly swats her out of the way, sending her crashing into a tree and falling on the ground face first.

    Duke: Recette! Are you okay?

    She lifts up a bit, looking a bit dazed.

    Recette: <I think I'm the one who needs sleep now...>

    She soon falls down again, though still not unconscious. Lucius blinks.

    Lucius: Did... did you just hurt my squidgy widdle Inkay friend?

    Slowly, he gets to his feet. Then, in a move that's either stupidly bold or boldly stupid, he charges the Ursaring and leaps onto the back of its neck, clinging to it like a particularly persistent flea.

    Lucius: YOU HURT MY SQUIDGY WIDDLE INKAY FRIEND!

    Ursaring: <OW! STOP YELLING IN MY EAR!>

    In its attempts to dislodge Lucius, the Ursaring ends up stumbling away from Duke. Unfortunately, its Sneasel friends are more than happy to fill the gap, one of them hurling a razor-sharp Ice Shard in Duke's direction, which he manages to dodge though it grazes his arm. He quickly takes out his armband, switching back into a Lucario and firing an Aura Sphere at the Sneasel. One gets knocked out and the rest gape at Duke's sudden transformation.

    Sneasel 1: <By Uxie! He's a skinchanger!>

    Sneasel A: <He's come to steal our skins! Run!>

    The Sneasels all turn and flee as quickly as they came. The Ursaring, however, is not so easily discouraged.

    Ursaring: <GET OUT OF MY HAIR!>

    It finally succeeds in throwing Lucius off, who lands in a crumpled pile in the snow, and rounds on Duke. All of sudden a Psybeam is fired at the Ursaring with the intent of distracting it long enough for Duke to rush in and start beating it down with a Close Combat. The Ursaring withstands several blows before staggering backwards, swaying on its feet.

    Ursaring: <YOU SHALL NOT DEFY... THE STARBORN ONES... I need a nap...>

    It topples over backwards, snoring heavily. After several long moments, a very faint noise comes from somewhere beneath it.

    Lucius: Um... little help...?

    The Ursaring then slowly starts levitating due to Recette's psychic powers while Duke pulls Lucius out from under it before putting his armband back. Recette then gently places the Ursaring on the ground again.

    Duke: You okay? That must've hurt.

    He then winces and places a hand on his arm, which had a noticeable cut on it, thankfully not a deep one. Lucius staggers to his feet and picks his scarf up off of the ground. Though he's slightly unsteady, there's also a sheepish grin spread across his face.

    Lucius: Don't worry, I'm fine... that guy was surprisingly soft, actually. Man, it's been a while since I got into a tussle like that...

    He sees the cut on Duke's arm and his grin fades.

    Lucius: Damn, that looks nasty. You okay?

    Duke: It's nothing too big. I should've taken out the armband sooner.

    He sighed and then gave a look to Lucius that seemed almost disappointed.

    Duke: Lucius, you knew it was going to be hard to see around here. Why did you go ahead of me when I'm the one who has the sensing ability? If you had just stayed by my side you wouldn't have bumped into that Ursaring and have to go through all this.

    Recette then floats over to them, but says nothing. Lucius bows his head.

    Lucius: Sorry. I... I guess I just assumed you'd be able to keep up. Ever since we got here, I've felt... stronger. More energised. Like I could keep running for hours without ever having to stop. I thought it was something to do with all the Auric energy around here, but... if it was, surely you'd be feeling it too.

    He thinks for a moment, then looks back up, smiling again.

    Lucius: ...You have to admit we handled ourselves pretty well back there, though.

    Recette: <I wouldn't call hiding in the snow while Mr. Duke does most of the work exactly handling yourself well.>

    She sweatdrops.

    Lucius: Yeah, Duke did most of the work, but you missed the bit right after that Ursaring hit you and he was like "RAR!" and I was like "nobody hurts my Inkay friend!" and then I jumped on his back and he was like "no way!" and then...

    He realises he's rambling and shuts up, looking vaguely abashed.

    Lucius: ...But anyway, you're right. I screwed up. Sorry.

    Duke lets out a couple of chuckles.

    Duke: Just stay close to me and let me lead. You're right, there's a strong flow of energy here, very empowering.

    He gives Lucius a more serious look.

    Duke: It's no excuse to go ahead and put yourself at risk like that though. It might be giving you more energy but you need to control yourself.

    Lucius: Right...

    He bows his head again, looking for all the world like an eight-year-old who just got caught stealing from the cookie jar. Duke does a small smile before petting Lucius on the head a bit

    Duke: It's all right, we're still fine and the worst that happened was that I got a cut on my arm. We should keep going, yes? You think you can still walk around or do I need to carry you?

    Lucius scowls.

    Lucius: Of course I can still walk! I don't need you hefting me around in a bridal carry, unless you're worried I might fall into a Sneasel nest or something.

    Recette giggles.

    Recette: <It's like Duke is the older, relaxed and wise brother and you're the younger, impulsive bratty one>

    Lucius: Hey, I'm eighteen now! I'm technically older than he is!

    Recette: <Really? You don't look like it... Or act.>

    Lucius folds his arms.

    Lucius: Can we just get back to looking for the Cathedral without insulting me any further, please? You'll have plenty of time to belittle me once we get there. We'll be nice and safe, too, since the only possible danger would be you setting the place alight with your sick burns.

    Recette then gives Lucius a hug.

    Recette: <Aw, sorry! I just thought it was funny and cute how you two act toward each other!>

    Duke: You do seem to hold me in high regards. Even with just a small scolding you looked like you were so ashamed of what you did.

    Lucius doesn't resist Recette's hug, but doesn't seem quite as enthused about it as he would usually be either.

    Lucius: Yeah, well, keep going on about it and I won't for much longer.

    Duke: Anyway, so you're being affected by the Aura of the place, huh? That's good, it means the place isn't actually harming you and that you likely do have an affinity towards something in the spectrum, whether it's Aura or Obscura remains to be seen though.

    Lucius: It doesn't feel the same as the last time I was here, that's for sure. Maybe the Aura's reacting to my body rather than my powers? I dunno. I don't have a degree in this stuff.

    He shrugs.

    Lucius: Let's just get to the Cathedral and figure things out from there. Could be that we'll be suddenly enlightened the moment we step over the doorstep. Or could be that nothing'll happen at all. Only one way to find out.

    Duke: Right, let's move on. Stay close to me.

    Duke then begins walking ahead, though he stops a few steps ahead to wait for Lucius to follow him. Meanwhile Recette is still clinging on to Lucius. Lucius rolls his eyes and mutters beneath his breath.

    Lucius: Will do, Dad.

    He follows behind.

    Recette: <I heard that~>

    He raises a curious yet concerned eyebrow.

    Duke: Do you drink that much coffee in two hours?

    Lucius: Sometimes. Only if there's something really important I need to do before falling asleep. Or if there's a livestream I want to stay up for. Or if I just don't want to deal with whatever I'll see after I fall asleep.

    Duke grimaces.

    Duke: That doesn't sound healthy.

    Lucius shrugs.

    Lucius: Probably not. Then again, a healthy lifestyle didn't stop me from dying at seventeen, so I don't think it really matters.

    Duke: To be fair back then it's debatable that your health was worse

    Lucius: Eh. Might've been.

    He looks up at the Cathedral.

    Lucius: Anyway, are we gonna acknowledge this or what?

    Duke: Right, I distracted myself. Well, we'll have to get in there. Shouldn't take too much longer.

    He then begins making his way to the cathedral, Lucius following behind. Eventually, they come to the threshold, and Lucius looks over at Duke.

    Lucius: ...After you.

    Duke just makes his way inside and begins looking around. The huge, expansive emptiness of the entrance chamber creates an impressive echo, and the walls are lined with eclectic and bizarre artwork. Not that either of its visitors pay much attention to these intricacies of the ancient temple, however.

    Duke: It's been awhile since we've last been here but it still looks the same. Guess it's not that surprising.

    Lucius: Yeah, I can't imagine this place sees a lot of traffic. So... what now? I was kinda hoping there'd be a big glowing circle in the central chamber with the words "stand here to regain Auric powers" inscribed on the floor in ancient runes, but I guess it's not that simple.

    Duke: Any powers you have should be considerably easier to perform with this very strong Aura field. Why don't you try to make a construct and see if you can get it to work?

    Lucius: A construct? I haven't even figured out how to make sparks yet... still, I'll try.

    He closes his eyes, takes a deep breath, and tries to focus on creating an Aura staff in his hands. After about a minute, he sighs and opens his eyes again.

    Lucius: ...Nope. Nothing.

    Duke: Hm... You still can't sense my Aura, right? Not even if you focus?

    Lucius's expression creases in concentration before he shakes his head.

    Lucius: I can't sense anything. I can feel the Auric energy in this place, but nothing more than that.

    Duke: Then... I guess you really can't use Aura anymore. Obscura, though... That's still a possibility. I'm not exactly sure what one can do with it, though. You have any idea?

    Lucius: Uh... illusions? I know Crewe used to make those cool shadow claw construct things... other than that, though, I have no idea. I know exactly squat about Obscura.

    Duke: Obscura constructs maybe? With Aura you can use moves like Bone Rush, how about trying something like Shadow Claw?

    Lucius: Alright, I'll try...

    Once again, he takes a deep breath and enters a state of concentration, but in spite of the power coursing all around him nothing happens. His expression becomes increasingly frustrated.

    Lucius: Still nothing. It's like my body is actively trying to suppress any powers I might have.

    Duke stops to think, scratching his head as he does so.

    Duke:... Maybe we could unlock it with some adrenaline. How about we "spar" with me attacking and see if I can get you to unlock that energy?

    He smiles.

    Duke: Don't worry though, I won't hurt you for real. If nothing else it's a good test of dodging skills.

    Lucius thinks for a moment, then nods.

    Lucius: ...Might as well. It's been a while since I had some exercise, after all.

    Duke nods and then produces a large staff made out of Aura.

    Duke: Very well then. Ready to go back to the basics?

    Lucius: Assuming I can manage the basics, then yeah, ready.

    Duke nods and gets in a ready stance before dashing at Lucius and swinging his staff at him diagonally. Lucius ducks sideways and retaliates with a well-aimed, yet entirely mundane, backfist strike which Duke quickly evades followed by pushing Lucius away in order to get some space from him.

    Duke: Good to see you're not too rusty.

    Lucius: What, did you think dying would slow me down?

    He darts forward with a quick jab. Duke easily senses it coming and dodges before attempting to trip Lucius with a leg sweep.

    Duke: Well, you did change a lot after coming back. You even got smaller.

    Lucius goes stumbling, but regains his balance quickly and glares at Duke.

    Lucius: Hey, it's not my fault that whatever brought me back from the dead decided to screw with me!

    He hurls himself at Duke once again, who once again dodges and this time he decides to take the offensive, trying to strike Lucius multiple times in quick succession with the staff.

    Duke: Don't worry, pipsqueak, I'm sure you'll grow up more some day.

    Lucius manages to evade Duke's first few blows, only losing his cool when one finally makes contact.

    Lucius: Quit PATRONISING me!

    He swings at Duke - not with a fist, but with a claw of solid shadows wreathed around his arm. This surprises Duke long enough for him not to react fast enough and barely get out of the way in time, with the claw slashing through his jacket and shirt and also making some new cuts on his left side. He stumbles and fall on the ground, the staff bouncing off the ground and soon disappearing.

    Duke: Ugh....

    Lucius, stunned by both his own powers and Duke's sudden injury, spends a moment in startled silence before he finally reacts.

    Lucius: Duke!

    He kneels down next to Duke, fear and concern written on his face.

    Lucius: Are you okay? Arceus, I didn't mean to... I'm so sorry, I had no idea I could do something like that...

    Duke places one hand over his new injuries, he winced for a bit and took a deep breath before answering.

    Duke: Ok, I'm fine. It wasn't anything major.

    He then gives him a smile.

    Duke: More importantly though, you did it. You really do have Obscura powers now.

    The impact of this statement doesn't seem to get through to Lucius, who is still staring at the gash in Duke's side.

    Lucius: ...You're bleeding.

    Duke: Yeah, that tends to happen when you get cut. I'm fine, really.

    Lucius: Speaking as someone who's been cut open more times than I care to count, you certainly don't look fine. We need to get you back to a Center. Maul?

    Maul: ~One medevac coming up.~

    The trio are teleported away from the halls of the Cathedral with a blip.
