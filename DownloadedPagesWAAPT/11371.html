    Imperial Commissar

    AU, Virbank City, Outskirts - Several weeks ago from a chronological standpoint because the author is too lazy to pace his posts properly

    -A masked Lucario is looking over the city, gazing over at the several wrecked airships still burning in the dock. He smiles, admiring his handiwork, as the trails of smoke from the debris blot out the rising sun.-

    Kirk: They're not going to be flying anything in or out of this city for weeks... if that didn't get Bahahkun's attention, then I don't know what will.

    -Eventually, he stands up, turning towards the makeshift rally point that the fake Resistance members have put together. Down in the city, several large groups of dragons can be seem gathering, but Kirk ignores them. He knows that by the time they begin their assault in earnest, he and his troops will be long gone.-

    Kirk: <Report!>

    -Within seconds, a Scizor has arrived in front of him, saluting.-

    (Ex-)ForLeg!Scizor: <Yes, sir?>

    Kirk: <You can dispense with the "sirs" whilst we're on missions like this. After all, this isn't a military operation, is it?>

    (Ex-)ForLeg!Scizor: <Understood, si- I mean, understood.>

    Kirk: <Good. Now then, I'd say we've caused enough trouble for our draconic friends for the time being. Now all that's left to do is let them know that this supposed Resistance is responsible for all their problems.Who's in charge of the prisoners that we were able to capture?>

    -The Scizor waves a claw in the direction of a Bisharp.-

    (Ex-)ForLeg!Scizor: <She's just over there.>

    Kirk: <Excellent. Now all we need to do is let our captives go, explain to them who we are and just how generous we were for letting them live, and they'll do the rest themselves. Come with me.>

    -Kirk heads over to the Bisharp, the Scizor tailing behind him. As he approaches, the Bisharp looks up from the Haxorus she was interrogating and salutes.-

    Enterprise!Bisharp: <Ah, there you are, Captain!>

    Kirk: <Forget the Captain part. We're not using military ranks here, are we?>

    Enterprise!Bisharp: <Oh, right, got it. Sorry. Anyway, I guess you're here to deal with the prisoners we captured, correct? I've got one right here!>

    Officer!Haxorus: <...I have a name, you know.>

    Kirk: <I don't care.> -to the Bisharp- <Anyway, I assume you know how to deal with them?>

    Enterprise!Bisharp: <We're just going to execute them, right?>

    -There is a sudden silence. The Bisharp notices Kirk glaring at her, then looks over at the Scizor, who has started twitching nervously.-

    Enterprise!Bisharp: <...Right?>

    (Ex-)ForLeg!Scizor: <...You're new around here, aren't you?>

    Enterprise!Bisharp: <Um, yeah. Why does that->

    -Kirk suddenly darts forward, striking the Bisharp in the stomach using a Force Palm with one paw and grabbing her by the neck with the other.-

    Enterprise!Bisharp: <ACK!>

    Kirk: <I don't know if you've forgotten who I am or if that draconic freak on the bridge has been giving you ideas, but I'm going to remind you whilst I'm in command of this unit we're going to do things properly. We're soldiers of the Foreign Legion, not just a group of savages with an airship. Our prisoners are soldiers, just like us, and we're going to treat them as such. That's what we've always done.>

    Officer!Haxorus: <Wait a minute... airship? Foreign Legion?>

    -The Bisharp is dropped as Kirk turns to face the Haxorus, having forgotten about the possibility of being overheard in his rage.-

    Officer!Haxorus: <You're not Resistance at all, are you? You're that militia Cassandra rounded up a few months back! This is all just some big plan to->

    -He is cut off mid-sentence by a Dragon Pulse from Kirk, and KO'd.-

    Kirk: <...Okay, you can execute that one.>
