    Lagoon the Feraligatr♂: <Yeah, remember that one time that Ariados woke her up early one morning? That was so not cool.>

    The larger of the three Feraligatr sighs.

    River the Feraligatr♀: <This is just silly, guys. Mom would never try to eat us, despite her rabid blood-lust.>

    Lagoon: <Yeah? Well she didn't try and eat you when you were just a hatchling, sis. Unlike me.>

    River: <And she's said a million times already that it was an accident!>

    Lagoon sighs, crossing his arms.

    Lagoon: <If you had seen the hungrily look in her eyes, you would have thought differently.>

    River: <Okay, I'll go and talk to her, you guys bring it in when I give you the signal, okay?>

    Swamp: <I hear ya'.>

    Lagoon: <Got it, sis.>

    Swamp and Lagoon hide out of sight as the female Feraligatr steps somewhat cautiously towards her dozing mother. Even though she knew that he had nothing to fear from her, she still couldn't help but be slightly skittish.

    She stops a few feet away from her, then slowly reaches out and nudges the dozing Feraligatr gently with her hand as Swamp and Lagoon peak their heads out to watch.

    Swamp: <Jeez, I can't watch!>

    Lagoon: <Yeah, it would be total bummer if sis loses an arm.>

    Getting no reaction from the Feraligatr, River nudges her gently again. This time, her eyes shoot open, giving River a start.

    River: <WAH!>

    The Feraligatr jumps back in shock, dropping to all fours instinctively. Her mother, however, simply yawns loudly as she sits up.

    Deluge the Feraligatr♀: <River? What's wrong?>

    Seeing that Deluge was more groggy than hungry, River relaxes, returning to her bipedal stance.

    River: <Nothing! Nothings wrong, it's just...>

    Deluge tilts her head curiously as she stands up.

    Deluge: <Yes?>

    River: <Well, apparently the humans like have this holiday called Mother's Day today to celebrate...well, moms. So, since you're our mom, and that there isn't anything to say that Pokémon can't celebrate human holidays, we'd thought we'd do something nice for you, so...

    She turns to Swamp and Lagoon's hiding place.

    <Bring it in, guys.>

    A few seconds later, the two male Feraligatr emerge, carrying what appears to be Stantler. Judging by it's size, it appeared to have been the Stag of it's herd. But not any more. The bite marks on it's neck indidcated that the Pokémon had been killed by having it's neck snapped.

    At the sight of the large Stantler, Deluge can't help but stare, her jaws a-gape.

    Deluge: <WHOA! He's a beauty!>

    Swamp, Lagoon and River can't help but grin as the two male Feraligatr throw the dead Stantler down in front of her mother.

    Lagoon: <We totally knew you would like it, Mom.>

    Swamp: <We spent all mornin' huntin' for him.>

    River: <It wasn't easy luring him away from his herd, but we managed in the end. We know how you love to tear the flesh apart, so we killed him as cleanly as possible.>

    Deluge can't help but grin widely.

    Deluge: <Well, I would have preferred him alive. I just love ending my prey's life with one hard snap, but this is just as good!>

    She then spreads her arms and warps the three Feraligatr in a hug.

    <Thank you so much, kids.>

    Lagoon: <No problem, Mom!>

    Swamp: <T'was the least we could do.>

    River: <It was no trouble at all, really! Now go on, eat him before he starts attracting flies.>

    Deluge nods, letting go of her kids and staring hungrily at the Stantler. Before she can savagely rip it apart though, she glances back at the three.

    Deluge: <You know...I don't think I could possibly eat all of him on my own. Would you three like to share?>

    The three Feraligatr exchange glances, then give Deludge a grin.

    Swamp: <Well, if ya' offerin'...>

    River: <Sure, why not?>

    Lagoon: <Dibs on the flank!>

    The four Feraligatr then proceed to savagely tear the Stantler up, which is an image best left to the imagination.
