All the lights at the other end of the hall go out, leaving him running in total darkness.-

With no air left, Zeal is forced to take the water into his lungs, but to his surprise, he can breathe. He keeps swimming toward the necklace, and the light it gives off grows.-

A voice whispers something that echoes through the hall, but Zeal can't quite make it out.-

    ???: Since... have... reali...

Zeal is still alive. He's in the entry hall of a mansion. He hears a gunshot and a bullet flies toward him in slow motion. Another flicker. His room in Mauville. A giant Archeops opens its mouth and lunges toward Zeal. A flicker. Olivine Lighthouse. The lighthouse vanishes, and Zeal falls to the solid ground below. A flicker. Shoal Cave. His feet freeze and the ice starts to spread. A flicker. A desert. The ground opens beneath him. A flicker. A flicker. A flicker...-
