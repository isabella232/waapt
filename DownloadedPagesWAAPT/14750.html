I've done what I can to try and repair things, but I don't think it's enough, and I don't think it's the best I could've done. Once again, I'm sorry, and I don't expect you to forgive me for my mistakes.

    You've been one of the best friends I could've asked for, but instead of treating you with the respect you deserved because of that, I ended up taking you for granted. I never properly thanked you for everything you did for me back in Orre. If I hadn't had your help and support to rely on... Arceus only knows what I would've done. Probably offed myself, or worse. And yet I never offered you the same support in return. So for what it's worth (which is probably very little) I just wanted to give you my thanks, let you know how much I appreciated everything, and tell you that I'll be willing to return the favour should we ever meet again.

    You won't have to worry about me messing things up any more, thankfully. I'm leaving the J-Team. This doesn't mean that the group's falling apart - if I stick around, the issues between me and all the others are only going to get worse. I don't think I'll be leaving any loose ends behind. There's still stuff I need to do, and I need to do it alone. I'll be sure to look after myself, even if I don't keep in touch.

You're definitely talented enough, after all. I still wish our relationship could've ended on better terms... but hopefully this letter goes some way towards fixing whatever damage I might've caused. Even if it doesn't, you can at least rest easy knowing that I'm not around to cause any more trouble.
