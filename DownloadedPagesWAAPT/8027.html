    Vault Dweller: Well Tagg, it was a very...interesting experience traveling with you guys, but now we have to part ways. The outside was something else, but there's no place like home.

    -The Vault door opens, and the Overseer steps out with a severe look on his face-

    <I have a feeling I'm not going to like the sound of this.>

    Vault Dweller: The Super Mutants will terrorize us no longer, sir.

    Overseer: You've done it! That's . . . wonderful. Amazing! I'm so proud of what you've accomplished, what you've endured. There's no way the people of the Vault can ever thank you enough for what you've done. You've saved all our lives. Who knows, maybe you even saved what's left of the human race.

    -Sighs-

    That makes the rest of this even harder.

    Vault Dweller: W-What do you mean?

    Overseer: Everyone will want to talk to you. Every youngster will look up to you, and want to emulate you. And then what? They'll want to leave. What happens to the Vault if we lose the best of a generation? What if we are the only safe place in the world? You just gave us back all these lives...I can't take the chance of losing them.

    <He's hiding something, I know it.>

    Vault Dweller: You can't be serious...

    Overseer: I've made a lot of tough decisions since I took this position. But none of them harder than this one. You saved us, but you'll kill us. I'm sorry. You're a hero...and you have to leave.

    -Fists frost over as two security guards train their weapons at us-

    <You're hiding something, I know it! I could always sense something slimy coming off of you!>

    Vault Dweller: Tagg, stop.

    -Looks up at the Vault Dweller-

    <You can't be serious, we can't let this slimeball get away!>

    Vault Dweller: -Sighs- We'd have to fight through them, and I really don't feel like doing that.

    Overseer: -Changes the code for the door and leaves behind him, but not before a certain Furret comes out as well-

    Furret: -Climbs on the Vault Dweller- <I didn't want to stay in there anyway.>

    <So...shall we go back to the others?>

    -And so the three head back-
