    Raspberry: <Fascinating! Lumiose humans seem to love coffee... That Prism Tower is amazing! I'm surprised it's not a Mystery Dungeon though...>

    Teksab: -smiles- <I'm glad we came here too. Let me know if you hear anything about Mega Evolution? The genetics of the process must be fascinating...>

    -Crow happens to notice the two Tropii mention humans, Mystery Dungeons, and Mega Evolution and decides to investigate-

    Crow: "Oh, hey, what are you two talking about?"

    Raspberry: <Humans!>

    Teksab: <Mystery Dungeons!>

    Raspberry: <Mega Evolution!>

    Crow: "Oh, okay."

    "I know a bit about two of those, but not much about Mystery Dungeons. What are they?"

    Raspberry: <They're a feature of our home universe.>

    Teksab: <Locations where space regularly reconfigures itself.>

    Crow: "Ooh! What universe is that?"

    Teksab: <Hmm... I'm not aware of any universal universal naming system... but it's a universe without humans. Like, humans apparently turn into Pokemon while they visit.>

    <You might become a Corphish or something if you went there, for example.>

    Teksab: <Don't ask me how, it's genetically inexplicable.>

    Crow: "Huh."

    "I'd hope I'd turn into a Murkrow, though, given that's what I've turned into under other conditions."

    Teksab: <Oh..? Were they magical or biochemical?>

    Raspberry: <What about ray guns?>

    Teksab: <Silly.> :P

    -she noogies her mate with a Magical Leaf-

    -which, as a leaf, is also Biochemcal-

    -but not a Ray Gun-

    Crow: "Hm..."

    "I... don't know how they worked really, I just know I first turned into a Murkrow about a year and a half ago when I put on an armband and it stopped when I took it off."

    "Then until a while ago, I ended up getting bitten by an acquaintance who was a Drilbur at the time, then ended up switching between Murkrow and Human in moonlight and sunlight."

    Raspberry: <Sounds like magic to me. Though... was that bite thing like one of your viruses?>

    Teksab: <Mine never reacted to the moon, no...>

    Crow: "Makes sense..."

    "But oh? Virus?"

    Teksab: <Eh-heh, yeah...> ^_^;;

    <I once tried to take over the world with an army of virally-transformed Pokemon in a hivemind.>

    <Bit of an embarrassing hobby to admit.>

    Crow: "Odd..."

    "Too bad you said they don't react with the moon, though, I kind of missed that."

    Raspberry: <Oh?>

    Teksab: <Missed what?>

    Crow: "Well... being a Murkrow, mostly. I was sort of fine with losing it when it was just a short bit..."

    "But this last time, maybe I was more used to it, maybe it's sort of a feeling of something I can't do as a human, maybe it's just how it was taken away, but it hurts a bit more, I think."

    Raspberry: <You liked it?>

    Crow: "Yep."

    Teksab: -getting a mischievous gleam in her eye- <Hmm... got any Murkrow DNA on you?>

    Crow: "Oh? Maybe I've got some feathers..."

    -He starts searching his backpack and eventually finds a few feathers-

    Teksab: <Excellent...>

    -she pulls out some beakers and does eerie glowy potion stuff with some liquids and the feathers-

    Crow: "Ooh. What are you doing?"

    Teksab: -produces a vial of black liquid with gold sparks in it-

    <Tada! Murkrowvirus.>

    Crow: "Murkrowvirus?"

    Raspberry: <Murkrowvirus!>

    Raspberry: <What's Murkrowvirus?>

    Teksab: <A mostly-defanged version of my old hybridizing viruses. Infect yourself with this, and you should become a Murkrow!>

    <This version shouldn't be as contagious as my old stuff, since I'm not exactly going to hand A-class bioweapons to random people on the street. But try to not get your blood on other people, OK?>

    <And if I find you starting a hivemind, I'm sending in my vampire hunters. :|>

    Raspberry: <You have vampire hunters?>

    Teksab: <Well, I'd have to recruit some and THEN send them, but you know.>

    Crow: "Ooh."

    "...Wait, hm..."

    "I know I want this, but... well, I wouldn't be able to change back."

    "Being human's nice too and it means not being accused of being a    thief    by some Gym Leaders..."

    Teksab: -shrugs- <Your decision.> -she passes over the vial-

    <I imagine if you have second-thoughts, treatments for that "Charizard Fever" disease I hear is going around might work.>

    Raspberry: <Oh, they're about to light the tower!>

    Teksab: <Well, we have places to be, see ya?>

    -Crow takes the vial-

    Crow: "See you, then?"

    -the Tropii nod as they fly off-

    Crow: "Hm..."

    "Well, guess I'll find out after I..."

    "...Wait, why did I come here again?"

    "Oh well, probably nothing important."

    -Crow then begins heading back towards Route 22 and the Badge Check gate while pondering the vial-
