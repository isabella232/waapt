    Herbert: ...You never were very good at lying, Alice.

    -Herbert gives a sigh, and closes his eyes.-

    Herbert: I used to think I didn't need anyone but you, as well. And we were happy together, for a while. I improved over the four years we were travelling together, however slowly. I went from an antisocial, aggressive loner who woke up every night screaming his lungs out to someone who could actually talk to people. And travelling with the J-Team... it kind of reminds me of all the adventures we had together.

    Darek: <Are you done yet?>

    Shut up, Darek. I need to make sure I have all my stuff, all right?

    Darek: <I know, but are you done yet?>

    No, I'm not done yet!

    Darek: <Uuugh... this is boring...>

    Then go find some Butterfree to chase or something.

    Darek: <I scared the last one off yesterday!>

    Well that's your own fault, now wasn't it? Shut up and stop bothering me.

    -the Sneasel groans and the girl goes back to her backpack, muttering to herself-

    Darek: <Where's Ace?>

    Talking to Biton.

    Darek: <Well, where's Biton?>

    Talking to Ace. Shut up, would you? I'm trying to think.

    Darek: <Why is Ace coming with us?>

    So I can get around. Ace is important. The better question would be, why are you coming with us?

    Darek: <'Cause I'm not gonna stay home.>

    -grunts and goes back to her backpack-

    ... Have you seen my phone?

    Darek: -appears to be judging the distance to the nearest nest in a nearby tree- <Nope.>

    Leave the birds alone. We made a deal with Ace, remember?

    Darek: <I was just gonna knock it down, I wasn't gonna kill anything or whatever.>

    Yeah, sure you weren't. Are you sure you haven't seen my phone?

    Darek: <Yes, I'm sure. Can I see your slingshot?>

    No. C'mon, I think I left it at the cave, I better go get it. And I don't trust you around these woods.

    Darek: <I wasn't gonna kill anything!>

    Yeah, whatever.

    -she gets up and slings the backpack over one shoulder, then grabs Darek by the scruff and drags him off the rock. The two walk off-
    Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
