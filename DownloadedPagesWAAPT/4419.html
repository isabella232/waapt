    [AU] Johto Nidoran Colony

    Some of the Owsla are reunited atop a rock formation, mostly the high-rankers. One of them steps forward.

    Maestro Vinollo — <I'm calling you here because I'm going on a very long hrayao.> — turns to one of the others — <I'll be outside for as long as I have to be.>

    Some of the Nidorin@ start talking about the current matters and asking some questions. Finally another of the high-rankers steps in.

    Veterano Hamada — <Maestro, what important mission is this? We haven't been informed.>

    Vinollo -paces around — <Well, I got some information from a human, about an important item I must fetch from Sinnoh.>

    Random Nidorina — <Sinnoh...? That's very far, not to mention somewhat hellish...>

    Random Nidorino — <Not to mention the winter.>

    Hamada — <What kind of item?>

    Vinollo -blinks, blushes a bit — <What... kind? Errr... a... a [key item]! Yes, a key item! Very important one!>

    Random Nidorina — <Can we assist?>

    Other Nidorina — <It's not like we have anything awesomely interesting to do at the moment...> — suppresses a yawn.

    Vinollo drops his ears, a bit nervous with all the questioning. He shakes his head.

    Vinollo — <Well... no, it's something very delicate that I must bring myself.>

    Hamada — <Oh? Like what, Maestro?>

    Vinollo — <Errr... it's a... a...> — looks around, noticing only rocks and some flowers — <...a very important flower... rock! I mean rock!> — blushes.

    Some of the other Nidos look at him but say nothing. After a moment of awkwardness Vinollo paces around and walks besides Hamada, who lowers his head.

    Vinollo — <You are going to be in charge at least until the springtime, Hamada.>

    The other Nidorino bows to Vinollo and then turns to the group, who make a reverential gesture to him. Meanwhile, some of the Nidos are chatting.

    Random Nidorina — <Vinollo said flower?>

    Random Nidorino — <Oh yeah he totally said flower.>

    Another Nidorino — <Perhaps one of the marli covets weird flowers...>

    Random Nidorina — <Weird... it's Rize who's been holding the breeders' attentions the last weeks...>

    Another Nidorina — <Poor Maestro must be feeling inferior or something...>

    Fifth Nidorino — <He better it's a very awesome totally-not-a-flower, because it's a long trip...>

    Random Nidorino — <What's the point though? The flower's gonna be dead by the time he gets back here... why not just go to Goldenrod to ask for one at the flower shop?>

    Other Nidorina -rolls her eyes — <Idiots.>

    Third Nidorina -scratches her mate — <Why don't you get me one of those flowers too, ah?>

    Nidorina's mate — <...Wha? But that's like... like... a hellhole winter!>

    Third Nidorina — <...That's the idea...>

    Vinollo, feeling uncomfortable with the comments and not very sure about shutting them down, steps back and tries to avoid staring at the others so they don't notice him blushing. Hamada takes the center stage and receives some congratulatory comments, then he starts talking about the instructions for the following days.

    Vinollo makes a hasty offscreen teleport exit and goes drink from a pond.
