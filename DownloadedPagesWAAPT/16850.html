    No, not the Rayquaza Corps. Me. Am I helping people?

    Chad: <You founded the Rayquaza Corps.>

    Yeah, and you run it! I sit around all day doing nothing!

    Chad: <So? None of these Pokemon that have been helped by the Corps would have been were it not for you.>

    But I've already done that. Now I'm just… useless.

    Chad: <First of all, you are not. Second of all, what brought this on?>

    I did nothing during the raid on Illusion.

    Chad: <And? Your friends took care of it. You do not personally need to be responsible for the downfall of all evil in the world.>

    But I should at least be trying! If I'm not out helping people… what am I?

    Chad: <First are foremost, a teenage girl with unreasonable expectations for herself.>

    Yeah. Right. Because I'm totally a normal teenage girl who should be worrying about normal teenage girl problems.

    Chad: <You are half right. You are not a normal teenage girl.>

    -Crewe rolls her eyes-

    Chad: <You are the founder of the Rayquaza Corps, the chosen one of Lord Rayquaza, a longtime member of a team of Trainers responsible for the downfall of many a threat to the world, and a costumed superhero. But you do deserve to worry about normal teenage girl problems.>

    -Crewe grumbles-

    Well fine, if you're gonna just ignore all the bad stuff...

    Chad: <I'm not going to insult you while you sit and brood on a rooftop.>

    … Whatever.

    Chad: <<… Crewe, you need a break. Go home.>

    Yeah, that's a terrible idea. No matter what "home" you're talking about. Though I haven't seen Jon in a while…

    Chad: <… All right then. Visit your father, and if you still feel the need to be out there helping, come back to the Pillar. Get more involved with the Corps. Take my son and Dapper and meet the Pokemon besides the representatives. Get to know your people. Come up with ways to make the Corps better. And for all our sakes, stop brooding. You never help anyone when you brood, least of all yourself.>

    …

    … Maybe you're right.

    Chad: <Yessir. <I am very nearly always right.>

    Heh.

    … I should probably talk to Straw about this.

    Chad: <That I can not help you with. But I'll be there when you're ready.>

    -blip!-
