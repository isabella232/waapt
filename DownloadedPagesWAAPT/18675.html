    Imperial Commissar

    Helion's Secret Base, last night

    Helion is sitting at her workbench and making adjustments to her gauntlets with a screwdriver when something blue and froggy comes barrelling through the entrance.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Helion? Helion! Helion Helion Helion Helion Helion I know it's super late but you have to help me!>

    Helion: Oh, sure. Let me just drop everything and get on that, because it's not as though I was in the middle of something.

    She sighs, spins around on her stool, sees Greninja!Batastrophe, and tilts her head.

    Helion: ...Wait, L- Batastrophe? Is that you?

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Yes! I was just minding my own business when this creepy Mew-thing showed up out of nowhere and zapped me with this weird beam and then I turned into a Greninja and it feels really really weird and I->

    Helion: Whoa, slow down a bit. I'm too tired to parse run-on sentences right now. Could you go over that again with punctuation this time, please?

    Greninja!Batastrophe does so. Helion blinks.

    Helion: ...You're serious?

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Does this sound like a joke to you?>

    Helion: Honestly? Yeah, it kinda does.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Well, it's not! Jokes should be funny, and this isn't funny because it's happening to me! You're a scientist who does science-y things, right? Can't you fix this?>

    Helion: I'm not a scientist. I'm an electrical engineer who messes with computer systems on the side. Whatever this is, it's not my field. I don't think it's anybody's field.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <But what if I'm stuck like this forever? The love of my life's never going to notice me now that I'm an ugly, oversized frog!>

    She starts to cry, tear ducts being one of the few features her new form shares with her old one. Helion, unsure how to react to all of this, awkwardly pats her.

    Helion: Hey, calm down. It's not gonna last forever.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <R-really?>

    Helion: If it was, why didn't you transform the moment the beam hit you? There's got to be something that triggers the change. For someone who's so obsessed with frog-themed heroes that you've taken to impersonating one, you're overreacting a bit. Actually, no: you're overreacting a lot.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Yeah, you're right...>

    She brightens up.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Maybe it'll be like the Princess and the Frog, only this time the brave and noble prince has to lift the fair maiden's curse with a true love's kiss~>

    Helion: ...Let's not get ahead of ourselves here.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <I suppose I could get used to this, as long as it's not permanent. Think of all the stuff I could do as a Pokemon! I could sneak onto Shingeko's team, or keep screwing with his dumbass sidekick, or->

    Helion: Or you could go to sleep. It's almost midnight.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Good idea!>

    She rests her head on Helion's lap.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <How did you learn to talk to Pokemon anyway?>

    Helion: ...It's a long story. I'll go over it in the morning, if you're still interested.

    Greninja!Batastrophe: <Are you kidding? I'm always interested. You're an interesting person, after all~ Anyway, night!>

    Helion: ...Goodnight, Batty.
