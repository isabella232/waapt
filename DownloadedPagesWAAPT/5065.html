    Route 120 - Secret Base

    "I don't get this at all. Why hasn't it hatched yet?"

    In the past few days, we've been on the edge of our seat waiting for the egg Fool gave us for Christmas to hatch. But despite being kept comfortable and safe, it doesn't seem to be any closer to hatching at all...

    "Maybe there's something wrong with it?"

    Lake bends down and looks closely at the egg, sniffing it gently.

    Lake: <It doesn't smell bad. And it's been moving occasionally, hasn't it?>

    "It has shaken every now and then. But what if there's a different problem?"

    Treeo: <I think you might be worrying too much, Luke...>

    Treeo glances up at us, the Sceptile having been busy sunning himself under the heat-lamp I had given him as a Christmas present.

    <You see, eggs are like seeds. When you plant a seed, you have to give it just the right amount of water, food and sunlight in order for it to sprout and grow into a plant.>

    "I know that..."

    Treeo: <Of course you do. But...you're forgetting one more very important thing...Patience. Even if you give a plant everything it needs, it still needs time before it can grow. And some eggs, like plants, take much longer to grow and hatch then others. Just like how it doesn't take long for grass to grow, yet trees can take many years to grow fully.>

    Lake: <Huh. That's a very elegant way of putting it, Treeo.>

    The Sceptile grins, feeling rather proud of himself.

    Treeo: <I do try...>

    I, meanwhile, gaze down at the egg, rubbing it gently.

    "...I suppose you're right, Treeo. I guess I could stand to be a bit more patient. But...maybe we could go down to the Fortree Pokémon Center to get it checked?"

    Treeo: <No harm in that.>

    Lake: <Perhaps we could see what everyone else is doing while we're at it? I can't help but feel we've been missing something important these past few days...>

    "Oh...we should probably do that, yes. Everyone's probably been wondering where we've been..."
