Somehow, she got the feeling that Rex had treasure somewhere, even if he didn't realize it or was just hording it for himself.     Yuki: <Stupid forgetful trainer...>

    She was preparing to Ice Beam some Magikarp to vent her frustration, when a glint on the shore caught her eyes. Noticing a red sparkle, she floated closer to the shore, noticing a small scale. As she picked it up, it glowed with a numinous light, releasing a full spectrum of colors. Yuki stared in surprise, never having seen anything so colorful before. The object was small enough to fit in her hand, and interesting enough to captive her attention. And that shape...why it looked just like

    Tite Kubo: THE HEART

    Yuki: <...>

    Freezing the strange man who had shown up and ruined her sentimental moment, Yuki floated back to camp, deciding to spend some private time eying her new treasure.
