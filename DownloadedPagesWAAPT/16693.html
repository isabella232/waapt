    Ray nodded, and jumped into bed, giggling."Tomorrow's gonna be awesome!" By the time Marie had changed and gotten into bed, she was already snoring away.

    Marie smiled at her, laying down close to her, and was just about to nod off when her Styler phone rang.

    She groaned, and quickly picked it up, listening."Yeah? Yeah? Really, now? Ugh...all right, gimme a bit."

    She sneaks out bed, looks at Ray, and sighed.

    Maray's Room

    The light poked Ray in the eyes as she got up, yawning, and stretched her arms forward to snuggle with Marie, but found herself groping empty air.

    She opened her eyes, and saw a note lying on the pillow."Hey, honey, I am so sorry about this, but an assignment came up and I had to leave. I'll be back by tomorrow, I swear. Love you so much; Mar."

    Ray sat upright on the bed and frowned.She then brightened up, and texted"Hey Jay, wanna play a game with me?"
