Röntgen has gotten better since I met him but this kind of thing takes time to treat.

    Röntgen: <Sorry...>

    Winona: -making a not of that in the case appeal's file- I see... So the reason for using him in a Gym Battle?

    Winona: -thinking- Hmmmm...

    Winona: I have reached my decision. Mr. Renard Clavis, in the matter of this appeal, and in light of the information available to me, as the as a certified member of the Hoenn Pokemon League and Leader of Fortree Gym, I hereby approve your appeal to use the Delta Luxray, Röntgen, in a gym challenge.

    -Röntgen breathes easily again as Ren smiles as he pats the electric lion.-

    Winona: I will mark you as a special exception ruling, Mr. Clavis. This decision stands for 30 days. You may use Röntgen in League battles within this gym for that time. After which you should reapply for a special exception ruling again.

    Winona: I look forward to your challenge Mr. Clavis.
