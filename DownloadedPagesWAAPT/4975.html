    Somewhere in the building

    Maekrite is pushing a bowl of food to a room where Treeo has taken some of Crewe's mons to cheer them up a bit. As he does, he stops by a door to check what lies on the other side, and sees Shahinne being [cuddled] by Copahue and Harriet.

    Shahinne: <Eeeeeee~ I'm sleepy, mom...>

    Harriet: <Don't worry, this will be a quick trip, lil'bird...> — nuzzles her.

    Copahue -to Silent: <So we speak with any of the psychics there?>

    "Yeah, don't worry. Zukov is in charge and he will get an adequate Pokémon for the task. Sol and Sandslash will be occupied in other matters. Now, Shahinne," turns to the Starly, "let go."

    Shahinne: "<No. :3> — yawns, tries to hide inside Harriet's plumage.

    Copahue and Harriet spend some private time cuddling Shahinne to sleep.

    From the door, Maekrite watches with admittedly growing envy. While he does remember being cuddled once or twice by both his parents when he was small, this is different. He doesn't know why. It just is. Perhaps it is not the love Shahinne gets that angers him a bit but that he does not understand.

    Maekrite: <It is not fair...>

    A second look however forces him to admit that it is. It is Copahue and Harriet who decidedly love Shahinne a [lot]. They dedicate themselves to her now now. Copahue went over his fears for this, Harriet traded a heart's desire, or so he has heard.

    Shahinne: <Sleep~> — closes eyes.

    They deserve to give their child, even if adopted, love. So they can do, perhaps.

    He doesn't.

    Does he?

    Because he hasn't one.

    Does he?

    Maekrite takes a last glance at the room, growls and then returns to the corridor, only to notice an evil ashcloud trying to sneak into the room. That same ashcloud that has been pestering Copahue for a while. The ashcloud that has been trying to make his life a hell every time he tries to be [happy] these last days.

    Maekrite: <Sure you're insistent... You deserve something...>

    The ashcould has taken shape again and prepares to try and enter the corridor. Maekrite realizes he could just let it in. It is not that [difficult]. He drops his head in anger, then steps back to let the cloud inside...

    ...

    And as soon as the cloud approaches just a bit closer, Maekrite roars and Ice Beams it. The cloud reacts again turning white and letting out some ashy snow, and after some convulsing, it retreats again.

       Ashcloud   : <   He's MINE!!!!   >

    Maekrite: <Not until Shahinne evolves!> — buffs. — <Then you come and talk to her!>

    The ashcloud leaves to the skies of Fallarbor.

    Because that's what parents do. That's the love they give their offspring. It is usually not much, not in a family that grows by four to six a year.

    But a Nidoran is not raised alone by their parents, and neither is a trained Starly. In Maekrite's view, Shahinne deserves more than just paternal love. She deserves that her parents can give her this love in a secured, [unhindered] manner.

    As Maekrite turns around to check the room, where Silent is giving Copahue some last-minute instructions, the Nidorino nods as he pretty much can read his Trainer's lips. It is Silent's job to make Shahinne happy as well, and as such, it is Maekrite's responsibility to allow that to happen.

    Maekrite's ears flicker and he moves his head only slightly, to meet a yellow Pokémon standing on two legs, nervously staring at him, blinking. Perhaps because Maekrite has just iceblasted a weird cloud inside the building, perhaps because he was talking to the cloud... perhaps because the cloud was talking back. Heck, perhaps there was an apparently sapient cloud inside the building.

    Maekrite -half-leers: <Any matter?>

    Electivire: <No. Well...> — hesitates, steps back — <You were taking too long with the food and... the others...>

    Maekrite stares at that Pokémon for a while. She deserves something too, and until the world around her and her partners is fixed, it is his responsibility to care about it. Because Silent would.

    Maekrite -bows: <My fault. But not anylonger- there, that is the bowl with the food.> — points to a nearby door with a paw.

    Electivire blinks and nods, and with some hesitation goes grab the bowl. Then both Pokémon head to another room at the other end of the corridor, where Treeo, Empoleon and the others are waiting.

    Shahinne: <zzzZzzz... teammate... family...> — lets herself sink into her "mom"'s plumage.
