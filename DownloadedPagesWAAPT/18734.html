Silver, square-framed spectacles sit on his nose, behind them a pair of dual toned chartreuse eyes.

    Maria: Oh, hello Marcus.

    Marcus: How you manage such a position and rank, Ms Boehm, is beyond me.

    Maria pouts as she moseys over towards the desk. Placing one hand down on it and tilting her assistant's head up with one of her filed and painted nails.

    Marcus: Going from one self-absorbed, vain spymaster to another must be my punishment for some heinous act I committed in whatever past life I possessed.

    Maria swings around the desk and positions herself behind Marcus.

    Maria: Ouch. So cold~ So brutal~ That's exactly why I chose you over the others.

    She runs her fingers up his upper arms and onto his shoulders.

    Maria: Tilly wouldn't play ball with me and give up the dossiers pertaining to your records. So I had to play guessing games with you lot. While you are certainly not bad on the eyes (I doubt Tilly would hire anyone who isn't nice on the eyes initially), you weren't the prettiest boy of the bunch.

    However, while your co-workers cried and begged for my mercy when it came to our interrogation. Not so much of a hum passed your lips, you just glared at us with those gorgeous eyes of yours~ Not budging an inch~

    She gives a menacing chuckle runs a nail over his lip.

    Maria: But it's just like the [British] to keep a Stiff Upper Lip, isn't it though?

    Marcus adjusts his glasses, unfazed by Maria's touchy-feelyness.

    Marcus: It's merely a job, and there are is no place for weakness in a job. I would be a poor spy if I gave in.

    Maria "boops" Marcus on the nose with the same finger that just ran over his lip.

    Maria: You got caught in my web, though~ Märkie~

    Marcus clears his throat.

    Marcus: As much as I wish it were not so, there is always a margin for human error. And, unfortunately, it so happened that I was faced with circumstances that I was foolish enough not to prepare for.

    Lucius: Some Neo-Galactic nutjob who's still bitter about the drubbing I gave them.

    Bishop: <Ah. Say no more.>

    He charges forward and hits Matrix with a Night Slash for significant damage.

    Matrix: <Physical damage... my one weakness...>

    Iapetus: Flamethrower.

    Despite barely remaining conscious, Matrix manages to let out a gout of flame that strikes Bishop directly in the chest, bringing him to his knees.

    Bishop: <Urk...>

    Lucius: Hang in there, Bishop! Go in for another Night Slash!

    Bishop hauls himself back to his feet and slashes Matrix with his claws again, which finishes the job nicely.

    Iapetus: Hrm. Your Pokemon have some strength, at least, even if the one they follow lacks it.

    He recalls Matrix and sends out his Golurk.

    Array: <My apologies. I find little joy in the pursuit of violence and revenge, but I have my instructions and I will not deviate from them.>

    Iapetus: Begin the attack with a Dynamic Punch.

    Array swings at Bishop, who manages to dart to the side to avoid the attack and retaliate with an Iron Head.

    Bishop: <If you find this whole business so distasteful, then why continue to enable it?>

    Array: <Because I am loyal to my trainer, and hope to see him succeed. I imagine you share a similar attitude.>

    It slams a Fire Punch into Bishop's thorax, which immediately knocks him out.

    Iapetus: Two down already. You are losing Pokemon at an alarming rate, Avenger.

    Lucius scowls indignantly.

    Lucius: I'm not finished yet.

    He recalls Bishop and lets out Kirk, the Lucario tensing slightly as he prepares to fight.

    Lucius: Bring the big guy down with Dark Pulse, Kirk!

    Kirk: <Which big guy? They're both taller than you.>

    Lucius: -annoyed sigh- You know what I mean!

    Kirk shrugs and sends a wave of darkness to wash over Array, bringing the Golurk down to about 30% health.

    Iapetus: Retaliate with an Earthquake.

    Array nods, then stomps on the ground with enough force to knock both Kirk and Lucius flat with the shockwaves.

    Kirk: <Oof!>

    Lucius: That was a nasty one... don't just sit there, Kirk, use Shadow Ball!

    Kirk jumps to his feet and fires a swirling ball of Obscura directly into Array's chest seal.

    Array: <I must admit... you fight well...>

    It faints, and Iapetus sends out a Mismagius in its place.

    Mismagius: <Finally! I thought we'd never get a chance to tear this little twerp apart.>

    Iapetus: Have some restraint, Null. Use Mystical Fire.

    The Mismagius cackles as he bathes Kirk in flames, leaving him on his last legs.

    Kirk: <Grrg...>

    Lucius: C'mon, Kirk, don't faint on me now! Hit him with a Shadow Claw!

    Kirk: <I... I won't fail you here...>

    He darts forward, paw wreathed in shadowy purple energy, only for Null to float out of reach.

    Null: <Too slow, chumpass.>

    He fires off a Shadow Ball, and Kirk, too weary to withstand another attack, faints.

    Lucius: Kirk!

    Iapetus cocks his head to the side.

    Iapetus: Is that all? I expected more from the architect of Neo-Galactic's destruction. There was no subtlety or strategy there... as always, you simply throw yourself in and expect things to go your way.

    Lucius: Maybe... but I think you've forgotten that I can fight perfectly well without Pokemon!

    He lunges forward and tries to tackle Iapetus to the ground. Unfortunately, Iapetus sees it coming and simply steps to the side, swinging his baton twice into Lucius's shins and face. Lucius yells out in pain and topples over, clutching his nose.

    Lucius: OW! Son of a bitch, that hurt...

    Iapetus: Case in point.

    He kicks Lucius over onto his back and rests his boot on his chest.

    Null: <C'mon, quit monologuing and start letting him have it!>

    Iapetus: Anything more you'd like to try, Avenger?
