"<Today, we are going to demonstrate Horn Attack for our guests.>" — he motions for Maekrite to step forward and face the tree while he continues explaining. "<For a Nidorino, a Horn Attack is a very good way to deal with a threat. Simple, yet precise and decisive... but it comes with a risk.>"

    Maekrite finishes rubbing his horn against the ground, then bristles up and charges at the three to demonstrate.

    THUD!!!

    Maekrite tries to remove himself from the tree, his horn dug deep past the first layers of bark. After a moment he uses his foreleg to pull himself out, and shakes his head.

    "<That was a good work and exemplifies the strengths and weaknesses of a Horn Attack.>" — turns to the group — "<You'll assemble in pair for practice. One rams, the other blocks. Teala and Pierce will be only attacking for today. Come on!>"

    "<Don't Druddigon know to use some head attack with those crests? Head Smash, perhaps?"> Maekrite sees Teala shrug. "<Oh well, go with a Tackle...">

    The Nidorinos start pairing up and ramming at each other in turns while Hamada paces around and observes. Maekrite is checking on Teala and Pierce's partners, for whom things aren't exactly going easy.
