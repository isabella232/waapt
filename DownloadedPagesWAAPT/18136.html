    Postponing writing the actual scene of Zeal informing his family about Lanie because all my attempts so far have been unbelievable and narmy. I'll come back to actually writing it later, but it happened!

    Cianwood City

    -Zeal is on the beach with his feet in the water with Gui at his side. He grabbed his Pokédex and Iulianus's tablet from his room before leaving home, and they're in his bag behind him.-

    Gui: So how do you feel?

    Zeal: ...I guess I'd say "light".

    Gui: Ooh?

    Zeal: This has been weighing me down for almost a year now. And I've finally managed it.

    -Zeal looks out at the water.-

    Zeal: ...Yeah... "Light"'s a good word. In fact, I feel better than ever. It's like I've found my lost resolve or something.

    -Gui playfully punches his arm.-

    Gui: Your lost zeal, you mean?

    -Zeal groans. Gui giggles.-

    Gui: I really had to do that.

    -She lies on her back.-

    Gui: So what now?

    -Zeal lies on his back too.-

    Zeal: Well, we've still gotta figure out what's going on with Mr. Knox. And now that that one guy's Scizor attacked me again, I think I'd better spend some more time reading the notes Iulianus left behind. Finding the guy who took Mel's important, too. More than anything else, even. But that's impossible on my own. And finding Fajro... and Glavo now, too...

    Gui: Still a lot, then.

    Zeal: Yeah. Definitely. Come to think of it, I found out recently that my old red-eyed aspect's still alive somewhere. So that's probably another problem to add to that list. Plus whatever else the J-Team inevitably gets itself involved with.

    Gui: But if we want to start simple?

    Zeal: The Scizor guy comes first, probably. Well, for me, anyway. That'll just be reading. You could probably do something with Savvy instead if she's still around when we get back if the alternative'd be boring for you.

    Gui: Okay!

    -She sits up, and pauses.-

    Gui: But wait! I'm not supposed to let you out of my sight again! Besides, Savvy's so quiet.

    -Zeal sits up.-

    Zeal: Er, right... I'd be quiet, too, if I'm just reading.

    -Gui jumps to her feet.-

    Gui: I've got it, I've got it! You can come with me and Savvy with whatever we do and just read where I can see you! How's that? Wonderful? Magnificent?!

    Zeal: Probably overcomplicated, but I doubt there's any point in arguing.

    Gui: Then that's what we'll do!

    -She grins.-

    Gui: Hope it's not too bothersome.

    Zeal: ...So do I.

    Gui: Ouch... That's a colder response than I expected. Well, I'll try to take her to only places that aren't too loud. How's that?

    Zeal: Better, I guess.

    -He stands up and looks back at his house.-

    Zeal: ...Thanks for taking me here.

    Gui: But that's just part of our deal, right? I fly you places!

    Zeal: I don't think you were expected to fly to Johto! Really, it means a lot. I mean, really... I can't exactly put it into words.

    Gui: ~No need to, sillyhead!~

    Zeal: Oh, right...

    Gui: ~I'll try my best to keep being awesome, though! You can count on me when it comes to that!~

    -Zeal bops her. She laughs.-

    Gui: Thank you, too.

    Zeal: Huh?

    Gui: All this has been a lot of fun for me. All the stuff you've gotten involved with and everything. I really mean it, it's been a blast, and I don't want it to end anytime soon. I really feel like I'm a part of something, and it's great. So thanks for that.

    Zeal: I'm... not sure if I had anything to do with any of those things.

    Gui: Course you did, sillyhead! Now, c'mon, stop being so pensive! Back to Hoenn, right?

    -Zeal picks up his bag.-

    Zeal: Yeah... right!

    -And off they go.-
