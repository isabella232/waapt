    407411 ILoveDogs8th Aug 2014 08:54:13 PM from Lunn Guyland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Hounds of love are hunting

    Off the Coast of Almia

    Once, there was a Carvanha named Harold.

    Harold was a normal Carvanha in almost every way. He was red and blue and yellow, he had rough skin, and he liked to bite things. However, he was different from other Carvanha. For you see, Harold had twin passions. Biting things was all well and good, but at the end of the day all you achieved was a bite to eat and making a swimmer rather cross. But Harold...wanted to create. He wanted to innovate, and challenge. He wanted to be an artist.

    Harold started to practice. He found some rocks at the bottom of the ocean, and pushed and positioned them so that they formed the rough shape of a Sharpedo tooth. He showed it to his friend Max, but Max was unimpressed.

    "So, you formed the shape of something that bites things. That's nice. Why don't you go and actually bite something?" said Max.

    Harold, a little defensive, answered that it wasn't really his best work. "It's only practice!", said Harold. "When I evolve into a Sharpedo, I'll really come into my own."

    So, he swam around in the ocean for a while. Every so often, he'd make a design out of rocks of something like a piece of coral or a bit of jetsam. Sometimes, he'd try and capture his abstract feelings and make them concrete. The other Carvanha weren't really mean about it, surprisingly, but they were a bit suspicious. "He doesn't bite things as much as we do," some of them would say. "He just thinks about what it means to bite, or why we bite. Why doesn't he just do it?"

    Well, he did bite, and one day he bit enough things for long enough that he evolved!

    But something changed with that evolution. Harold began to see art as not just making things out of rocks, but making things out of other things, too! He could get enough Clamperls to stay still and make a picture of a Gorebyss, or maybe a bunch or Wailmer could float in the exact way that resembles a Wailord. Or even...or even...

    Evidently, making pictures out of rocks is a harmless quirk, but making pictures decrying the Biting Institution by posing Whiscash corpses is the work of a deranged madman. Harold saw the writing on the wall and left.

    He still does his art. If you're scuba diving off the coast of Almia and you see a picture of a coral made of rocks, that might be Harold's work, still left untouched. If you see a diatribe against the ocean life written in neat cursive with dead Milotic, that might be more some more recent Harold's work!

    He could even still be around! Say hi to him if you see him, he's a rather friendly guy. Just don't bring your Gorebyss and her 500 sisters.
    A tooth for an eye!
