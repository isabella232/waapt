    Roc: <...> — yawns.

    Ichtacya: <...?> — :3s.

    Meanwhile, with a sudden burst of energy, Teala catches up to Tracer right as he is talking to some others of his mons for the trip.

    Teala -nudges Tracer: <Uhm... Tracer? Can I ask a favor?>

    Tracer turns around, Audrey also lends an ear while keeping Zoltan busy.

    Teala: <Uhm... I... I have been unable to help much these days...>

    Tracer: "Oh, it is not your fault, Teala, come on." — pats her, brushing her head carefully — "These days with those strange lawyers have been hell."

    Tracer notices then Teala grumbling nervously, and leans to her in a cue to trust him with what is going on.

    Teala: <Uhm...>

    She shifts her gaze around, meeting Audrey's for a moment; the Lucario gives a barely perceptible go-ahead gesture before returning to her own matters.

    Teala: <I need to be more fit... and was thinking, perhaps, I could go learn from someone else.> — shies away a bit.

    Tracer: "Learn? You... you want to be switched to another team?"

    Teala: <Uhm... Maekrite says he is returning to Goldenrod to train another mon. And he speaks of his place and his bosses... and I thought perhaps he is a good guardian because...?> — turns to Tracer, seeing him look at her sternly for a moment — <Ah, Tracer, I don't mean to imply you are not... uhm...>

    The Druddigon shies away more, but Tracer is quick to reach to her, rubbing her arm carefully.

    Tracer: "You have to be OK, Teala... if you want to become a better Pokémon there are many things you can try."

    Teala -blinks: <So you are fine with it?>

    Tracer: "I'm sure you'll get something useful from it. Be aware though, where Maekrite lives is a very different place that what you had in the Dragonspiral Tower."

    Teala: <I-I know. He and Silent say things I don't understand... but they sound like they have the right peoplemons...>

    Tracer -steps around to see Silent trying vainly to convince Roc to go with him so that he does not have to stay at PEFE: "What did he say about it?"

    Teala sweatdrops, if that is possible.

    Tracer: "Forgot to ask, eh? Hurry then, because Silent is recalling his Pokémon."

    Teala turns around, then turns back to Tracer for a moment; it takes the human a bit more prodding to finally convince Teala to walk to where Maekrite is idly resting on the ground.

    Meanwhile, Silent has left Ichtacya to deal with Roc again, he picks the Pokégear to confirm if Casval and Artesia are coming.
