    467787 PippingFool13th Sep 2015 09:04:59 AM from A BEAUTIFUL DUWANG Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I get a feeling so complicated...
    A Bad Egg

    Neo-Flare HQ, ???

    After the debacle at the Hoenn base, the surviving Neo-Flare personnel stationed there have relocated back to the true Neo-Flare headquarters via the self-destructing teleporters Odin designed.

    One of the more secluded rooms of the facility is in use for the first time since it's construction. Slick black panels coat the room, only a few polished stainless steel panels break up the monotony of black. Various machines also blink, beep, pulse and hum. What each one does is not obvious, but one can infer their purpose as they all lead towards a hospital cot. And the patient contained there-in.

    The honey-haired patient is wired up to all sorts of contraptions, tubes flowing in and out of his body. Considering the deathly pallid color of his usually sandy skin and the complete and utter lack of movement from his body, one may make the assumption that he is dead. However, he is indeed alive. Albeit barely.

    Currently a young, black clad man stands by the cot. A table beside him containing a sethoscope, blood pressure monitor, thermometer and a watch.

    Lex: Time to check on your vitals, boss. Hopefully it's better than yesterday's recordings...

    Lex gentle grabs the lifeless arm of Constantin and places his index and middle fingers against his radial artery. Lex's brows furrows, unsatisfied with the reading - or more accurately, the lack thereof - he is currently receiving.

    Lex: Mrrf. Still to weak too read via the radial artery...

    Lex grabs the stethoscope off the table and places the buds in his ears. After a gentle crack of the fingers in his left hand, he places the chest piece gently over Constantin's heart.

    Lex: Mmm... His heart rate certainly hasn't improved. It's still very weak and now it's erratic as well.

    Lex jots down a few notes.

    Lex: Not good.

    He removes the stethoscope and places it back down on the table, replacing it with the thermometer.

    Lex: Temperature next.

    He places the thermometer in Cons arm pit, waiting a moment for the device to record the current temperature.

    Lex: Hm... 32 °C. An improvement on yesterday's 31°C. However, still not good, and has a chance of plummeting back... Mmm...

    He removes the thermometer and grabs the watch, writing down notes in the meantime. As he begins to monitor his patients respiratory rate, thoughts waft in and out of his mind.

    Lex: Salvador told me of the dangers of these forbidden arts known as the Gates, though I've never truly witnessed the devastating effects on the body and mind until now. Over the last few days, it almost feels like he's... wasting away... slowly rotting away even...

    Is a moment of absolute power really worth all this? If so, he's a fool. Though, I have to admire his ability to live despite all of this. I don't imagine, however, it will be for long...

    Lex eyebrow twitches as his train of thought is broken by incessant humming coming from the other side of the room.

    Lex: Must you do that here, Frau Boehm?

    Maria is standing by one of the polished steel panels in the room, admiring her reflection in the makeshift mirror as she dons the vivid red longcoat that usually drapes Constantin's figure.

    Maria: (Admiring herself) It really is a ravishing color, red? And it suits me most excellently~ Really, I should lay claim to this piece if Connie dearest would allow me~ Tailor made is also a good option, though the last thing the boss would want when he gets out of that coma of his is chastise me for "poor management of company finances". Again.

    Lex: We did not need a hottub.

    Maria: (Fiddling with her hair) Au contrare freund von mir. A hottub is exactly what we need in testing times such as now~ Stress is a killer after all.

    Maria gives a small pout, slightly unhappy with how the closed coat looks on her. She quickly unbuttons the thing and lets it flow loose around her body. Still unpleased, she slacks her shoulders, letting the coat slip and hang around her scapula.

    She gives a mischievous grin as she grabs the end of her favorite halberd, twisting it around before slamming it into the floor. Emitting an eek from Lex on the other side of the room

    Maria: Now this is a look to die for!

    Lex: -With a slight scowl- Boehm. Decorum. Geezsuz, so this is the woman of who Freyja picked up all her bad habits from?

    Maria finally turns around to face Lex, a coy smile on her face.

    Maria: I must say Lexie, your sewing skills are impeccable. You've fixed up this old thing to the point where you can't tell it was ever ripped at all~ How delectable~

    Maria twirls a loose strand of black hair in her finger. Lex inwardly cringes at being called "Lexie".

    Maria: You could make a pretty penny as a tailor, tinkerer.

    Lex: Frankly, my dear, I am quite happy to be an inventor. I see no need to change professions.

    Maria: Though right now you are playing a fine nurse, Lexie-boy~ You really are a man of many talents~ A true Renaissance Man, if I do say so myself~ How do you have the time to learn it all?

    Lex leers at her with a look that reads "stop calling me that" as she saunters over to the cot.

    Lex: I studied medicine most diligently years back for personal reasons, is all.

    Maria purrs as she rubs a finger over Lex's right arm.

    Maria: I'm sure it has to do with this lovely thing~ Doesn't it?

    Lex instantly flinches and retches away from the touch.

    Lex: Don't touch me.

    Maria: Jumpy, aren't we?

    Maria laughs, Lex obviously annoyed by her antics. Though after a while she turns her head towards her boss, a look of worry splashed on her face.

    Maria: ...So how is the old boy doing anyway? He looks absolutely dreadful!

    Lex sighs

    Lex: Not well, I'm afraid. His condition hasn't seemed to improve ,really.

    There is a slight eye twitch of annoyance as Maria fiddles with the stethoscope on the table.

    Lex: Though I would have a more accurate reading if someone wasn't here being a nuisance, Frau Maria von Boehm.

    A Noblewoman's Laugh emits from the raven haired beauty.

    Maria: Teasing you is so much fun, Lexie~

    Lex sees no point in giving into her bait again and ignores her, focusing his energy on his patient.

    Lex: Remember when he got like this in the first place?

    Maria: Hmm? Oh yes~ We were dragging him away towards the teleporters when he suddenly, and violently, retched out blood and his stomach lining before jerking violently, having a seizure and falling into a coma.

    She hums.

    Maria: And now he's here...

    Lex: Mmm...

    Maria: What are you thinking about Lex? You're tense, I can tell~

    Lex: His condition. His blood flow is slow, his pulse weak, breathing shallow and way below the average and his temperature low. But more than that, there are more insidious things underneath that skin of his...

    Maria: Oh~ Do elaborate on the gory details, liebling.

    Lex: ...His welts and wounds aren't healing as they should, it's very slow. A few were almost infected had I not dressed them there and then, no doubt a result of a compromised immune system. The tears in his muscles are also not healing correctly. Not only is it slow, but on top of that the way they are healing is irregular and haphazard. His skin is starting to blotch in places, often a telltale sign of impeding or actual death and if I didn't pump his body full of antihemorrhagics and anticoagulants he would have bled out by now, a curious condition I would only associate with hemophiliacs, which from my records he does not have. Speaking of fluids, I had to drain his lungs before he drowned in his own body.

    Maria: Jesus Horatio Christ.

    Lex: For once I agree with you, Frau Boehm.

    Maria: Would it kill you for once to address me by my first name, Lexie?

    Lex: Yes.

    Maria: Tch. Typical.

    Her thoughts drift back to the man in the cot.

    Maria: To think, all this over a simple power boost. You'd wonder why they'd bother in the first place? It's obviously not worth it.

    Lex: He always seemed to type to dance with Death.

    Maria: Hmm, go on.

    Lex: He chases after it, covets it and yet it eludes his embrace each time. I've seen the scars that litter his arm and throat indicating attempts to take his life, and yet here he is. For whatever reason, he remains alive, as if Life and Death take their turns in tormenting him.

    And when you come from a background of abuse like he does, after a while, you learn how to play dice.

    Maria: Ooh, I do love it when you wax poetic drivel like that Lexie.

    Lex: -Ignoring her- Though I don't suspect he'll last much longer. I give him eleven years, if he's lucky.

    Maria tilts her head.

    Lex: The blotches on his skin, the low temperature and heart rate, his lack of clotting blood. He's literally decomposing, slowly, painfully, day after day. And yet on the surface he shows no sign of how much of a fragile thing he really is.

    Maria: To use one of his own metaphors, he's the ferocious, crumbling Bastiodon.

    Lex: -Biting his lip- Yes. Indeed.

    Maria clings on to his arm.

    Maria: I do hope he makes it out of this mess though~ Otherwise he'll never be able to get to use that wonderful cybernetic eye that you made out of the kindness of your own, sappy heart.

    Flustered by the touch, he tries to pry her off.

    Lex: BOEHM! REMOVE YOURSELF FROM ME AT ONCE!

    Maria: Hmm~ No~

    The fussing and fighting continues on for some time, before Lex gives up.

    Lex: Maria von Boehm. I need to finish the rest of the checkup. So if you don't mind removing yourself from me and the premise so I could finish that would be grand, s'il vous plait.

    Maria: -Pouting- Fine. Have it your way.

    Maria places her hands behind her head, moseying out of the room with the bosses red longcoat still draped over here. Lex sighs, facepalms and goes back to the checkup he was doing earlier.

    Lex: She really is a nuisance, that girl.
