    9 AM

    In one of the many rooms in the Pokémon Center was Lyuri, combing her hair in front of a mirror while sitting on a small chair. Soon Duke comes in, fully dressed but with his hair still wet, having just returned from a bath himself.

    He raised an eyebrow at Lyuri "You're still at that? You've been getting your hair ready since I left to take a bath."

    She let out an annoyed sigh "You're a dude with short hair, so be quiet. Long hair is suffering." she vented, looking frustrated.

    "Why don't you cut out some of it then?" he asked as he sat on the bed to wait.

    "I wouldn’t look good with short hair." she sighed again.

    "Really? I think you would look great no matter the hairstyle" he bluntly stated with a sincere tone.

    Lyuri stopped for a brief moment before resuming to comb her hair "Hold back the charisma, Duke, we just woke up." she joked, her tone being impassive, but still a step up from how frustrated she sounded before.

    Duke let out a chuckle and fell into silence and so the only sound in the room was that of the comb running through Lyuri's hair.

    She then stood up "Alright, done," she sighed, but then smiled at Duke "Breakfast time?"

    Duke stood up himself and put a hand on his belly "Definitely. I woke up with a bit of an appetite today." he smiled

    "Well, big guy, I'll make something to satisfy your hunger." she teased, leaving the room.

    10 AM

    Lyuri and Duke are now eating pancakes with strawberry syrup together, Duke having eaten about four while Lyuri barely ate two, all the while they were sitting on a couch right next to each other..

    "Man, you really weren't kidding were you?" she said, as she caressed his belly a little "You better watch out before this athlete body of yours ends up compromised."

    Duke chuckled “Don’t worry, I’ll burn whatever calories I’ll get from that later.” he said, as he wrapped his arm around and pulled Lyuri even closer to him.

    “Eh…” said Lyuri, leaning against Duke, relaxing as she enjoyed the contact “You’re a big boy, you probably need to eat more than average.” she smirked.

    “If you say so.” he then let out a satisfied sigh then looked at Lyuri “What do we do now?”

    She shrugged “Dunno, I don’t really feel like going out today.”

    “You’re being quite lazy today already.” he chuckled.

    She rolled her eyes dismissively “Hush, I just don’t feel like walking around.”

    With that, Duke just shrugged the whole thing out and stayed there, looking at the surrounding area while he could feel Lyuri head on his shoulder. Soon, however, an idea sprouts in his head and he smiles as he suddenly got up and made Lyuri fall on the couch.

    “You could at least warn me before getting up like-”

    Then before Lyuri could get up, Duke picked her up and started carrying her on his arms.

    “Whoa whoa whoa! What are you doing?!” yelled Lyuri.

    “Well, I figured that since you don’t want to walk, I could just carry you around like this.” said Duke, a mischievous smile making itself very apparent.

    “Duke, no. Put me on the ground right now!” said Lyuri, pointing towards the ground.

    Duke pretends not to hear her and begins carrying her out of the Pokémon Center, doing so with relative ease outside of Lyuri trying to break free by basically flailing around like that with little to no success.

    “Duke!” she yelled, to which he responded just by laughing.

    11 AM

    Outside, Lyuri and Duke were now walking together around the street, the former looking quite grumpy while the latter was quite amused.

    “I think some people even thought I was kidnapping you.” said Duke, trying to contain the laughter.

    “You don’t just begin carrying someone else around like that, you just don’t!” said Lyuri, sighing in annoyance “That was embarrassing…”

    “Come on, it was a bit funny wasn’t it?” Duke snickered some more “You’re not angry at me, are you?”

    “Not angry, but annoyed.” she grunted

    Duke’s expression quickly changed from amused to apologetic “Sorry then, I just thought it would be a fun, silly thing to do.”

    Lyuri looked at Duke’s expression of a kicked puppy and just sighed “I just can’t stay upset with you for too long. Don’t do that again though.” she folded her arms and narrowed her eyes a bit.

    “Right.” he smiled and pulled her closer “You did look kinda cute flailing around like that though.” he then glanced at her, only to meet with a look of complete disapproval “Alright, alright, dropping it.”

    “Good. The only thing I got from this was hearing you laugh that hard for the first time in a long time.”

    “Do you want me to laugh like that more often?”

    “Nah, if you do it too often it stops being special.” a small smirk began showing up in Lyuri’s face.

    2 PM

    Duke and Lyuri are now eating some pizza for lunch at the pizzeria Duke and Sarah went to before, as neither of them was particularly hungry they just asked for a small one.

    “So... has Lucius talked to you recently?” asked Lyuri as she took a bite off her slice

    Duke gave her a knowing look “Yeah, he talked to me yesterday at your suggestion.”

    “Oh,” she shrugged “Well, how did it go?”

    “He told me to stop holding myself back, to not treat him like a little kid anymore.” he smiled “I feel a bit more at ease now, I guess I really was holding myself back for nothing.”

    “Yeah, dude. Don’t pretend everything's okay and speak up when you have a problem with something.” she grinned “Glad he helped you though.”

    “Do you have any problems with the way I act yourself?”

    “Well, as long as you actually speak up and stand your ground more when something is bothering you, I’m good.” she drank some soda “You’re already a really disciplined guy, but it doesn’t hurt to raise your voice every now and then.”

    Duke nods “Right, I’ll try to change that.”

    “So, let’s finish eating and do some training yeah? It’s been awhile since we did something Aura related together.” she grinned.

    Duke was taken aback for a moment with the sudden request, but he nodded again “Sure thing. It really has been a while.”

    “Yeah and that way I can go ahead and do some exercise for the day.” she then finished her slice.

    4 PM

    The couple is now standing at a wide, more isolated area of the city, both of them holding a staff made out of Aura.

    “So, since you’re likely a bit rusty, I’m gonna go easy at first and then step up the difficulty.” said Duke, smiling

    “Oh come on, I don’t need to take the tutorial. I know how to do this Duke” she then sighed in exasperation

    She then took a defensive stance while Duke also adjusted his stance accordingly.

    “Give it your all, okay? I’m ready for-”

    And so in just a few seconds, without Lyuri even being able to react to it Duke struck her staff off her hands and sent it flying away while Lyuri herself was just standing in place as if she was stunned.

    She raised her hands up like some sort of criminal “Okay, okay. Do hold back, please.” she said, not amused at what Duke just pulled off on her.

    Duke chuckled and got back in position while Lyuri went on to get her staff back.

    “All right, try taking the offensive yourself and break through my defense.” said Duke

    Lyuri smirked “I’m sure I could think make a dirty joke about that if I put enough thought into it.”

    Duke grimaced a bit “Well then, I guess I shouldn’t be surprised.” he shook his head “Anyway, let’s start, yes?”

    Lyuri nodded and almost immediately started to rush towards Duke with her staff in hands, ready to strike… Only for her to trip just as she was getting to him and flying into him. He catches her, but the impact is enough to make Duke lose his balance and fall, with Lyuri falling right on top of him.

    There was a bit of silence before Lyuri spoke up “Uh… Does this count as victory?”

    Duke then pats her on the head “No. No, it doesn’t” he said, snickering a bit.

    “Aw come on...” said Lyuri, pouting.

    Duke then kept caressing the back of Lyuri’s head “This just isn’t going to work today is it?”

    “Well, I have to say, I’m feeling pretty comfortable here” she smirked “You’re pretty warm and soft, I almost feel like falling asleep right here.”

    “Do that and I’ll have to carry you all the way back to the Pokémon Center.”

    “... Nevermind.” she said Lyuri, satisfied with Duke’s answer.

    8 PM

    The couple is now walking around the city being at around the center of it and passing by the big dead tree found there. Lyuri had her hands behind her head and looking up at the stars in the sky, while Duke just seemed to be lost in thought and with one hand in one of his pockets.

    “There’s not much to do in this city now is there? I guess there’s the gym and the caves, but those are kinda meh.” commented Lyuri, sounding quite bored.

    “I guess... We seem to have that issue of running out of things to do a lot.” said Duke, with a chuckle that sounded a little awkward

    “Yeah, when we go through some crazy stuff like we have then everything seems a little more boring.” she then looked at Duke “What’s with you though? Thinking about something?”

    “Uh, well... It’s nothing really.” said Duke, rubbing the back of his neck as he usually did when nervous.

    “Come on Duke, no secrets please” she sighed “Remember, it’s okay to speak up if something is bothering you.”

    “R-Right... Speaking up... Ok...” said Duke, taking a deep breath

    Lyuri was quite taken aback by it “You’re kinda worrying me now. I can sense that you’re extremely anxious. Did something serious happen?”

    “It’s... kinda going to happen...” he then pulled his hand from his pocket and shows a little box, a ring box at that “... right now.” he blushed a bit

    Once she saw the box, Lyuri’s eyes went wide and she took a step back, covering her mouth with her hand “D-Duke, are you really-”

    “Yes.” he answered as he opened the box, showing a gold engagement ring set with a diamond “I... We’ve been together for a while now. We both talked about future plans in the past, when we’re married and settled down so... Why not just make it official then?”

    “We’ve only been dating for two years, Duke, don’t you think it’s a bit early?” said Lyuri, shaking with nervousness.

    “We’ve been travelling together for a lot longer than that, even if there was nothing quite like we began having two years ago. Even when all the things in Orre happened our relationship never really faltered, so is time really going to change anything at this point?” he then started to hesitate “Though if you don’t want to-”

    “No! It’s not that! I was just... I wasn’t ready.” said Lyuri, now blushing.

    “O-Oh... Um… Should I just ask again later then?”

    Lyuri just stops for a moment and soon begins giggling “Duke, you’re ruining it, you moron”

    “I-I am? I don’t know how to do this so I’m just saying whatever comes into mind and hoping it works.” Duke frantically spoke, now blushing really hard

    Lyuri now laughs a bit more, before stopping and taking a deep breath herself “... Just ask. Make that very important question.”

    Duke stops for a moment, his face going back to its normal colors as he regained his composure. He then smiled and nodded before kneeling in front of Lyuri and offering her the ring.

    “Lyuri, will you marry me?” asked Duke, trying his best to keep his composure

    “Yes. Yes I will, no doubt about it.” said Lyuri, starting to get teary-eyed

    Duke gave a warm smile and then stood up, taking the ring out of the box.

    “So...” said Duke, not quite sure how to say it

    “Yeah, do it.”

    Duke nodded and then puts the ring on her finger and after it’s done Lyuri looks at it for a moment before suddenly hugging Duke very tightly

    “I’m... I’m so happy right now, you have no idea.” said Lyuri, burying her face on Duke’s chest

    “Are... Are you crying, Lyuri?” he asked, hugging her back and running his hand through her hair.

    “Duh! You came with all this out of nowhere! I wasn’t ready for this Duke!” she yelled and sniffed a few times before calming down “I’m so glad it happened though… Thank you, Duke.”

    “Anytime, Lyuri.” he gently replied, hugging ever so slightly tighter than before.
