    Pummelo Stadium

    Drake stood, arms folded in the center of the arena, awaiting his next challenger. The audience roared in delight, anxious to see what kind of battle was in store for the young man. 'Hopefully an interesting one,' he thought to himself, frowning at the memory of yesterday's battle. The trainer had several type advantages, but his moves were sloppy and lackluster. A textbook case of compensating lack of skill with TMs. He hadn't even needed to use his signature Dragonite, a fact that the creature seemed similarly irritable about. He nodded to himself, hearing the great beast cry out from its ball, eager to demonstrate its power once more.

    Drake: Yes...I know. I, too, hunger for a challenge.

    Pummelo Stadium - Stands

    Rex made his way through the crowd of people, wondering how on Earth they were so excited when all the guy seemed to be doing was brooding.

    Rex: People will cheer for anything, won't they?

    Zippy: <Seems that way.>

    As they pushed past a group of shirtless teens with letters of Drake's name painted across their chests, Rex became aware of something in the sky.     Pummelo Stadium

    Drake gave a respectful nod to Silent, holding up a Pokeball of his own. He studied the other man's face for a moment, as if scanning him for something. After several tense seconds, he spoke up, the crowd's noise immediately dying down.

    Drake: My challenge is, as you are no doubt aware, a full battle. You are allowed to switch out; I am not. The arena ring will change after three of a trainer's Pokemon are rendered incapable of battling.

    Without a further word, he tossed his Pokeball, a flash of light signifying the appearance of...

    Ditto: :3

    Pummelo Stadium - Stands

    Rex blinked in surprise, the cottonweed Pokemon burbling happily in his hands. Zippy crawled across her trainer's arm, giving Breezy a sniff just to make sure it truly was her.

    Rex: What are you doing all the way out here?

    Breezy: <Watching your Gym Match~>

    Rex: My...what?

    Breezy: <...?>

    Breezy tilted her head in confusion, shaking her stalks absently.

    Breezy: <Aren't you having a match? Your mom said you were~>

    Rex: She did? How did she get that ide- Oh, is mom here too?

    Breezy: <Yep yep! Follow me~>

    Leaping from Rex's arms, the Jumpluff hopped into the air, riding the air currents over towards another section of the stands. Rex blinked before shrugging and following after her, wondering just what was going on.
