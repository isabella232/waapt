    384453 PippingFool22nd Jun 2014 06:25:38 AM from A BEAUTIFUL DUWANG Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I get a feeling so complicated...
    A Bad Egg

    Fall City

    Fool: How?

    Memory Lane

    Lumiose City, around a year and 3 months ago

    Fool is walking down Vernal Avenue with a folder in one arm and a map of some sort in another.

    Fool: Oh, where IS this blasted apartment? I had an easier time finding places in Castelia City. Urgh...

    Too busy looking for the place she's seeking out, she accidentally ends up bumping in to someone and drops all her stuff.

    Fool: Ack!

    She quickly drops to the ground to scrounge for that paper on Kalosian phrases. Only getting a very quick glance of the man she bumped into.

    Fool: Erm... Désolée monsieur...

    Constantin: Puis-je vous être utile?

    Fool looks up at the man she bumped into. He is tall, around 6'3 in height, with a mop of honey-blond wavy hair (not to mention a notable pencil-line moustache and goatee) and dark, coffee-brown eyes. He’s currently wearing an impeccably kept black three-piece suit with fancy black kid gloves. In his right hand, he holds a gold tipped cane.

    Fool: Aha. Um... (Quickly looking back at the paper) Est-ce que vous parlez anglais?

    The man gets a soft and welcoming smile on his face.

    Constantin: Indeed I do.

    Fool: Oh! Splendid!

    Constantin: (Laughing) I can tell that you are most certainly not from around these parts then. Anyway, would you like me to help you?

    Fool: That would be very much appreciated...

    The man kneels down and helps Fool pick up her belongings. He takes a quick glance at some of her papers and gets curious.

    Constantin: So what brings you here to Kalos? I gather from your papers you’re a... researcher of some kind?

    Fool: -Nods- Yes. I'm here to study some of the unique social structures of the Pokemon groups here in Kalos. As well as general investigation of the recent oddities with a select species of Pokemon''

    Constantin: Ah, I see.

    Constantin hands Fool her papers and helps her up.

    Fool: Since you seem quite friendly sir, could you help me with something else?

    Constantin: Oui?

    Fool: I'm trying to look for... This address (Shows him the paper that reads "23 Vernal Avenue")

    Constantin: Ah, I see where you’re heading. That fancy apartment that one of the Archaeology tutors at the University of Lumiose rents out to fellow researches when he's not staying in town.

    Fool: How does one afford a fancy apartment in what is essentially the city centre?

    Constantin: (In a slightly dour tone) He comes from a very old, auspicious family. One that can afford such dwellings.

    Fool: I see.

    The man slaps his forehead.

    Constantin: Where are my manners! What kind of gentleman am I to not even introduce myself yet! My name is Constantin. Constantin Minucius Rufus Perilloux. Addressing me by my first name is fine.

    Constantin gently grabs her and raises it.

    Constantin: May I ask what name the lovely lady standing in front of me goes by?

    Constantin kisses her hand as Fool gives a slight chuckle at the display.

    Fool: My name is Solana, Solana Malkewitz. (‘‘Thinking) So Kalosians do tend to be romantics. Fancy that~'')

    Constantin: Solana? Such a pretty name. Befitting of a woman such as yourself~

    Fool: (Chuckle) You Kalosians really know how to be charming, don't you?

    Constantin: But of course. It ‘tis what we are known for~ Now then...

    Constantin holds out his cane in a South-Easterly direction.

    Constantin: The place you are looking for is in that direction. I could help you there if you wish.

    Fool: Say that would be nice.

    The two start making their way in the direction of the apartment.

    Constantin: Hmm. It looks like you could use some help around here.

    Fool: Oh?

    Constantin: Kalos is a large and fascinating place. However, it can be quite daunting to the outsider just arriving in the city. What do you say if I offered to be your guide during your tenure in Kalos?

    Fool: -Smiling- I would appreciate that a lot!

    Constantin: (With a chuckle and a warm smile) Then it's a done deal~

    The two walk in the direction towards the apartment

    Battle Château, a year and a month ago

    Fool flops down on a table chair where Constantin is drinking a cup of coffee.

    Fool: Aw yeah! Who knew that pummelling nobles and raking up cash would be so fun?

    Constantin: Au contraire, pummelling nobles and raking up cash are just my idea of a good time~

    Fool: It feels - how do you say it - très bien

    Constantin laughs at Fool's attempt at Kalosian, which causes her to fold her arms and pout at him.

    Fool: Hey, what's so funny?

    Constantin: Oh nothing, I just find you various attempts at Kalosian to be quite endearing, mon amie. Especially with your unique little Hoennese/Unovan hybrid accent.

    Fool: (Raises eyebrow) My "attempts"?

    Constantin: Well, first off: "Très Bien" is not pronounced "trays bee-yen", but rather "tray byang".

    Fool: (huffing) Hmph.

    Constantin: If you like I could help you with your Kalosian. It will be an asset to you considering you have a long tenure here.

    Fool: You would really do that?

    Constantin: Of course mon cherie. After all, you are my friend, non?

    Fool: That's rather sweet of you, you know. Are you sure I'm not imposing on any of your time though? I've noticed you've been busy lately.

    Constantin: (Smiling) I will make time.

    Fool: ...You really are a good friend.

    Constantin: (slight chuckle) For you? A thousand times over.

    Fool smiles back as she orders some tea from the menu.

    Cyllage, 1 year ago.

    Fool and Constantin are attending some sort of do together. Currently, Fool is trying to talk him into participating in the next event.

    Constantin: ...So the next "dance" involves a group of us trying bend underneath a bar that gradually gets lower?

    Fool: Yep! That's Limbo~

    Constantin: That... sounds rather silly to me.

    Fool: (Tugging at his sleeve) Aw c'mon Ruffles, It'll be fun~

    Constantin blows some of his hair in annoyance before rolling his eyes at his companion.

    Constantin: Oh all right...(sighing) The things I do for you....

    Fool: Yay! Now let's go! The event has almost started!

    Fool pulls Constantin up by his sleeve, which emits a short sound of surprise from the man as he's lead over to the limbo area, where, fittingly enough, an old, cheesy and yet fun ditty starts playing. The line starts moving as each member of the line attempts to get under the bar.

    Fool: It's almost our turns~

    Constantin: I am going to look like a downright fool doing this.

    Fool: It's all a part of the dance, Cons~

    When it comes to her turn, Fool effortlessly dips under the bar. As she bounds back up, she turns around and sticks her tongue out at her partner

    Fool: Let's see how you go, Ruffles

    Constantin gives Fool a playful smirk as he dips underneath the bar just as effortlessly as she did.

    Fool: Huh. You are full of surprises today Cons.

    Constantin: I am a man of many secrets, you know~

    Fool: Let's see how long we can keep this up for!

    As the dance goes on, the line gets smaller and the bar gets lower until it's only Fool and Constantin left and the two start getting pretty competitive.

    Fool: For someone who has never done this before, you sure have lasted a long time.

    Constantin: -Smirking- And now I'm in this to win this.

    Fool: Let's just see!

    Fool starts to lean down under the bar, but has trouble keeping her balance and halfway, falls onto the ground.

    Fool: Damnit!

    Fool slips under the bar and stands back up, while Constantin tries to subdue his laughter.

    Fool: Oh yeah! Let's see how low you can go.

    Constantin approaches the bar and bends his knees forward, shuffling forward at a steady place before clearing it and bounding back up.

    Constantin: And that's how you clear the bar.

    The crowd cheers as Constantin bows.

    Constantin: Thank you~ Thank you~

    After the crowd goes back to idle chatter amongst themselves, Fool approaches Constantin. Shaking her head and giving him an amused smile.

    Fool: I never knew you were this flexible man.

    Constantin: Well, I was never one for brute force. So when I trained, I tried to heighten my physical strength, flexibility, power, agility, coordination, grace, balance and control.

    Fool: In other words: Gymnastics?

    Constantin: That and Yoga.

    Fool: Learn something new every day...

    Fool and Constantin go back to conversing over the food and beverages provided

    Later, Fool and Constantin are walking down the street, humming and singing parts of that limbo ditty and laughing into the night.

    Fool: Jack be nimble, Jack be quick~!

    Constantin: Jack go under limbo stick!

    Fool: All around the Limbo clock~

    Both: Let's do the limbo rock

    The two laugh as the sit down on the pier, finishing off their Sitrus Sherbet and Mint Chocolate Chip ice-creams they brought along the way.

    Constantin: You were right Solana. That was fun! The most fun I've had in a while, actually.

    Fool: Told ya'! You shouldn't knock things until you try them~

    Constantin: Say, where did that dance come from anyway?

    Fool: From what I've read, it was an [Afro-Caribbean] dance originating in [Trinidad]. Originally the bar was raised rather than lowered, to symbolise going from death into life.

    Fool goes back to licking her ice-cream

    Constantin: Interesting. You really do learn something every day.

    Constantin finishes off his ice cream and lies down on the pier, looking up at the stars.

    Constantin: You're always a pleasure to be with Solana. You're just so... ‘‘jubilant''.

    Fool: Oh?

    Constantin: You have such a natural curiosity, a zest for life. You're full of boundless energy and you are always interested in finding out new things, even if it is a trivial as the origins of some dance. That's what I find so... endearing about you.

    Fool: Well, I find you to be extremely charitable. You're always finding spare time for me even though you said it yourself that your workload is getting heavier due to an upcoming event or something or other.

    Constantin: I may be in the higher ranks, but I have slightly more leeway than my schedule than some of my cohorts.

    Fool: Well thank ya' fore spending some of that time with me.

    Constantin: Any time mon piaf.

    Snowbelle, 11 Months ago

    Constantin: So! This here is Snowbelle! The snow here is permanent thanks to presence of a cluster of Abomasnow. If I recall correctly Solana, you wanted to examine the Zoroark populations in the nearby forest-

    He is suddenly hit by a snowball to the face.

    Fool: Gotchya~

    Constantin gives a sly smile to Fool, a retaliates back with two snowballs of his own.

    Fool: Hey!

    Fool notices that Constantin has around twenty snowballs hovering around him and chuckles in response.

    Fool: Oh you sly dog, I see what you are doing there. Two can play at that game!

    Suddenly, black obscuric tendrils burst out of the ground and start curling around some snow and forming snowballs with it. No long afterwards, a fully fledged Obscuric v Psychic snowball war breaks out.

    Coumarine, 7 months ago.

    Once again, Fool and Constantin are attending an event of some kind. This time it seems to be more of a black tie event rather than the rather casual festivities of Cyllage. Currently, Fool is watching some of the other couples waltzing.

    Constantin: (Raises an eyebrow at Fool) I see you've noticed some of the dancers up there.

    Fool: Yeah...

    Constantin: Would you like to learn how to waltz?

    Fool: Me? Dance fancy? Surely not-

    Constantin: (Chuckling) Solana. If you can teach me how to Limbo, then surely I can teach you to waltz.

    This time it's Constantin who helps Fool up and takes her to the dance floor and takes up position.

    Constantin: Now, just follow my lead.

    Keeping in time with the music, Constantin and Fool begin their waltz. After a while, Fool seem to get into rhythm and the two begin to waltz the night away.
