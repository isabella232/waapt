    Holon Museum

    -After getting past a hangover and changing back to his normal gender, Umbra is staring at his exhibit, thinking-

    -Sakura was also looking at the exhibit, though she was rather distant from Umbra-

    Umbra: -without looking at her- Oh, hello, Sakura. Here to marvel at the gravity of my past mistakes?

    Sakura: ... -in a very calm tone and smiling- It's still a bit hard to believe that you're the oh-so-evil Ghost Lord, you know.

    Umbra: ...Huh?

    Sakura: -not looking at Umbra- I know we're not particularly close or anything, so I don't know much about you, but all that time I thought you were a kind person who wouldn't ever do something like what you did in the past.

    Umbra: ...Yes...

    Sakura: And... Well... What you did was unforgivable and I certainly don't forgive you for that.

    -Umbra hangs his head-

    Sakura: -starts looking at Umbra and smiles- But I can see that you've changed for the better, even if what you did was horrible, you're a better person now and I can respect that.

    Umbra: -Starts looking at her as well- Thank you. That means a lot.

    Sakura: I admit that I wasn't sure of what to think of you, but I've realized that even if you have a big stain on your past that you can't clean no matter what you do, we have to keep living and move on, you did awful things before, but it's what's going on now that matters.

    Umbra: Yes... That's what's kept me going, despite everything.

    Sakura: -starts approaching Umbra- So... I know there are people like Herbert that would go out of their way to keep reminding you of your mistakes and judge you based on them and that it's hard to deal with it, so... -hugs him- If you need someone to talk to, just look for me, all right? I'll be more than willing to listen to what's troubling you.

    Umbra: -hugs back- Thank you...

    Sakura: -breaks the hug and starts giggling- Yes, it's hard to believe you were such a tyrant when now you're such a sweetheart.

    -Umbra blushes and smiles-

    Sakura: -giggles some more and looks around for a bit- So... It was a White Arcanine that stopped you wasn't it?

    Umbra: Yeah, why?

    Sakura: It's name was Amaterasu wasn't it? -stops to think for a bit-

    Umbra: It... was? ._.

    Sakura: Isn't that also Ammy's name? -starts giggling and smiling again- Who knows? Ammy might be her ancestor, even if it's a bit hard to believe that a Volcarona is descended from an Arcanine, wouldn't it be interesting if it was true?

    Umbra: ...What. I don't even, just... What.

    Sakura: -giggles- What? It's not as weird as some of the stuff that happens with this group.

    Umbra: Point taken.

    Sakura: It would be funny if your girlfriend turned out to be descended from the very Pokémon which haunts you to this day, such irony! -grins-

    Umbra: Considering my life I wouldn't doubt it.

    Sakura: Oh come on, don't be like that. -playfully flicks Umbra on the nose- Someone who lived a long life like yours is of course going to experience more bad things than most, and admittedly your life as the Ghost Lord must be haunting to you now, but now you're in another time, with different people and with all the changes we had since 600 years ago, it must be like an entire new world for you! Your life as the Ghost Lord may have sucked, but your new life now can't be too bad can it?

    Umbra: ...

    You're right. It's not.

    Sakura: I know it must be hard to, but you should forget the past, or rather, keep in mind what you learned from the experience and move on, you still have a lot to see and experience, you became a historical figure as the Ghost Lord, now you have the chance to make up for it and become famous for good reasons, the TV show was the first step. -smiles-

    Umbra: -laughs- Perhaps, Sakura. Perhaps.

    Sakura: Anyway, I hope you don't mind, but I'm totally going to paint Grant white for Halloween -giggles-

    Umbra: Um... I'll stay away, then. ._.

    Sakura: All it takes is a White Arcanine to make you start trembling like a little kid who saw a horror movie isn't it? -giggles-

    Umbra: ...Yes. >_>

    Sakura: I guess everyone has their fears, so... I gotta go now, it was really nice to talk with you here, Umbra, if you need help with anything, let me know, all right?

    Umbra: Alright, see you.

    Sakura: See ya~

    -Sakura then starts walking away, waving as she goes-
