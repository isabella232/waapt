    -Dapper is still here, nervously spinning and weaving silk. All of a sudden, he is tackled by a cave-colored blur that abruptly turns into Flashlight-

    Flashlight: <Hihihi!>

    -Dapper looks cross and tries to push him off-

    Dapper: <Flashlight, go away.>

    -Flashlight is oblivious and doesn't move-

    Flashlight: <Whatcha doin', huh?>

    Dapper: <I was weaving, until you tackled me. Get off, please.>

    Flashlight: <Okay okay okay!>

    -he hops off of the Swadloon, who gets up and untangles his weaving from his legs. Flashlight is all but hopping around in his excitement. He wags his tail, his frills open and quivering-

    Flashlight: <Play with me!>

    Dapper: <No.>

    Flashlight: <Pleeeeeease?>

    Dapper: <No.>

    Flashlight: <Pleeeeeeeeease?>

    Dapper: <I said no. Go away.>

    Flashlight: <C'mon play with meeee!>

    Dapper: <Get someone else to play.>

    Flashlight: <I wanna play with you!>

    -he tackles Dapper again-

    Dapper: <Flashlight, I said get off.>

    Flashlight: <Imma tell Auntie!>

    Dapper: <Fine. Do that.>

    Flashlight: <Meanie!>

    -Flashlight seems to disappear as he runs off. Dapper grumbles to himself as he once again gets up and tries to start weaving-

    -a few moments later, an elderly Xatu blips in, Flashlight perched on her shoulder-

    Xatu: <Dapper, are you being mean to your brother?>

    Dapper: <... No, Auntie Ava...>

    Flashlight: <Yes he was! He wouldn't play!>

    Ava: <Flashlight, did you ask Dapper to play with you nicely?>

    Flashlight: <Yuh-huh!>

    Dapper: <Nuh-uh!>

    Flashlight: <Yuh-huh!>

    Dapper: <Auntie Ava, he tackled me!>

    Flashlight: <Nuh-uh!>

    Dapper: <Yuh-huh!>

    Ava: <Quiet, both of you. Flashlight, is it nice to tackle people?>

    Flashlight: <... No...>

    Ava: <No, it isn't. Dapper, is it nice to ignore your brother?>

    Dapper: <... No...>

    Ava: <No, it isn't. Now, Flashlight, I want you to apologize to Dapper.>

    Flashlight: <Sorry, big brother...>

    Ava: <Dapper, are you going to play with Flashlight?>

    -Dapper looks from his (now covered with dust) weaving to the despondent Helioptile-

    Dapper: <Okay, Auntie Ava...>

    -Flashlight immediately perks up. The Xatu gives him a long look-

    Ava: <Dapper, dear, what's wrong?>

    -Dapper glances at Flashlight-

    Ava: <Flashlight, go find a toy for you and Dapper to play with.>

    Flashlight: <Okie!>

    -he disappears and runs off-

    Dapper: <Auntie, I'm worried about Crewe. She didn't tell me why she had Chad take me back here.>

    Ava: <I see. Well, dear, I'm sure it's nothing to worry about. Crewe can handle herself, and she has Chad to look after her if there's real trouble.>

    Dapper: <I guess...>

    Ava: <She'll come back for you soon, sweetie, as soon as things settle down.>

    Dapper: <I hope so...>

    Ava: <Don't worry, dear. If she doesn't come back soon, I'll go and yell at her myself. We'll see if she thinks twice about leaving you behind again after that.>

    -Dapper looks considerably cheered up-

    Dapper: <Thanks, Auntie Ava.>

    Ava: <It was nothing, dear. Be nice to your brother.>
