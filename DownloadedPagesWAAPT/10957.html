    -Lucanus watches Herbert attack the dummy for a while, though he is not so much paying attention to Herbert's method as he is the sword itself.-

    Lucanus: May I ask where you acquired that blade? It is unlike anything I have ever seen.

    Herbert: Erm... it's a family heirloom. We're not really sure where it came from. It seems to be able to sense the presence of Obscura, though, since it glows blue whenever there's a source of it nearby.

    Lucanus: Fascinating! I would very much like to acquire such a blade for myself. Of course, unless I were to stumble across one somewhere, then I am stuck with this rusty old thing.

    -He gives his own sword a couple of idle swings, then looks back at Herbert.-

    Lucanus: Still, a family heirloom, you say? Where do you and your family hail from, out of curiosity?

    Herbert: Well, um... I'm not originally from Ransei. Like you said, I'm a traveller. I was pretty sure the Cain family were Sinn, but that seems a lot less likely now...

    Lucanus: I guessed as much. I too hail from a different region, though I doubt you would have heard of it. It is quite unlike Ransei. I must say, I had some difficult adapting to this new culture that is so centred around war... but Pugilis is my adoptive homeland, so adapt I must. But I digress. My apologies, I have been dominating this conversation somewhat, haven't I? Please, tell me a little more about yourself.

    Herbert: Um... actually, I'd prefer to hear more of your story. I'm not particularly interesting, after all...

    Lucanus: You want to know more about me? Very well, I suppose I will start at the beginning.

    -He sheathes his sword, and sits down on the ground, inviting Herbert to do the same.-

    Lucanus: I was born to quite a prestigious noble family, one with a proud history of service to our king and powerful Auric abilities that manifested themselves every few generations. From a young age, I demonstrated almost unlimited Auric potential, though I had... difficulty... controlling my powers at times. To keep me from inadvertently harming myself or others, my parents ensured I was supervised by their Kadabra, Revan. He has been my companion ever since.

    -He gives a faint smile.-

    Lucanus: My noble heritage and Auric abilities meant that I was a natural candidate for a knighthood. Sure enough, at the age of nineteen, I was knighted by the king and given my own ship. My task was to explore new and uncharted lands such as Ransei. Of course, it was only once we arrived on the shores of Pugilis that we realised that Ransei was very much charted, and far from being new. Thankfully, the old Warlord Takuma took me under his wing, and eventually left the kingdom to me - provided that one day, I would slay the Ghost Lord of Spectra. I'm still working on that, though.

    -He chuckles slightly.-

    Lucanus: Well, that's my story. It isn't particularly interesting either, really. The things that have happened to me since I arrived here in Ransei have been far more out of the ordinary. But again, I find myself dominating the conversation. Did you have any questions?

    Herbert: ...Yes, actually, Just one.

    -Herbert holds up his hand, and makes a spark of Aura jump from finger to finger.-

    Herbert: Whilst I'm here, could you give me some training in how to use Aura?
