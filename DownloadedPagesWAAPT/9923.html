    Unlike Lake and Treeo, Comet and Stratus trudged through the cave in awkward silence, not saying much to each other. Much to Stratus' discomfort, as he was getting the impression that the Charizard didn't want him around.

    Trying to relive the tension, he decided to try and strike up a conversation.

    Stratus: <Soooo...what do you think of Unova?>

    Not bothering to look up at the Dragonite, Comet gave him a half-hearted shrug.

    Comet: <It's alright. Haven't seen enough of it yet to judge.>

    The Dragonite nodded, understanding what she meant.

    Stratus: <Yeah, yeah. Gotta' see a bit more before you decide fully...>

    He trailed off, not sure how to continue the conversation.

    Comet, meanwhile, stopped as the light of her tail-flame illuminated some odd markings on the cave wall. Taking a closer look, she found what looked to be...a painting?

    Stratus: <Wow. What's that?>

    Noticing the painting himself, Stratus plodded over, gazing at it in awe.

    Comet: <It's a painting, what else would it be?>

    Stratus: <Hmm. I wonder who made it?>

    Comet: <I dunno. Probably some bored teenagers from Driftveil. Human kids, I swear...>

    Shaking her head dismissively, she started to head onward.

    <Come on, we don't have time to be hanging around.>

    Stratus frowned as he gazed after her, took one more look at the painting, then followed after her, disappointed.

    Stratus: <I thought it was pretty...>
