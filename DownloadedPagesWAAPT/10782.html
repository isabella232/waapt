    -Herbert is sat in front of a group of his own Pokemon, inspecting the Pokeballs on his belt.-

    Samus: <Yup!>

    Leia: <Um, y-yeah...>

    Herbert: Excellent.

    Elan: <Aww, how come I don't get to come along?>

    Herbert: Because I can only feasibly take six Pokemon. And together, the rest of the team can learn any HM I need them to. Besides, you're a Water-type, I didn't think you'd be so eager to travel to a land of violent electric storms.

    Elan: <But you're letting Leia come!>

    Herbert: That's because I trust Leia not to go climbing up trees in the middle of thunderstorms.

    Elan: <That was one time! Okay, maybe two. Or five. But still!>

    Herbert: I rest my case.

    -He turns to the Bisharp next to him.-

    Herbert: Falchion, you'll be staying here to look after the local population. And Harkness as well, of course.

    Falchion: <...If that Chimchar were to, say, wander off, I wouldn't be held responsible, would I?>

    Herbert: Falchion...

    Falchion: <Fine, fine. Whatever you say, sir.>

    Herbert: Remember, you'll have P-1 and Dirk to help you run things around here. Hopefully they'll be able to provide some support.

    -Falchion laughs, before noticing that Herbert's expression is still completely deadpan.-

    Falchion: <...Oh, you were being serious, sir.>

    -Herbert sighs.-

    Herbert: Just remember that if I come back here and find the place collapsed, you're going to be the one held reponsible.

    Falchion: <Yes, sir. Understood, sir.>

    Herbert: Good. So... I do believe that's everything.

    -He stands up.-

    Herbert: Well then. I suppose this is goodbye, Falchion. And to you, Elan. And Harkness as well, even if he's not here right now.

    Elan: <When will you be coming back, though?>

    Herbert: Once we're finished in Holon, we'll be straight back here to pick you guys up. It'll probably be a couple of months. But for now, Maul?
