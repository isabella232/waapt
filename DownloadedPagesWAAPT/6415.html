And stand in the way of any march eastward...-

    <Lileep: "My liege! Are you okay?!">

    <Archeops: "And just what do you suppose that you're doing here?">

    <Croconaw: "M-MEEP! Th-The captain said that y-you needed e-extra security... A-And I-I'm a-about to e-evolve, I sw-swear!">

    <Aerodactyl: "He was really helpful today, my liege... Also, what is this, the third attempt on your life this month?">

    <Archeops: "Fourth. That still doesn't explain why you are in the ranks of my personal guards in place of a true ancient! One who isn't fully evolved at that!">

    <Croconaw: "O-Okay! I-I'll l-lea-">

    <Archeops: "But since you are here, you might as well make yourself useful...">

    -Motions at the three would-be assassins-

    -And claiming the center for ourselves doesn't appear to be a strong possibility... The very mention of its residents is enough to make some of this empire's soldiers quake in fear... There's some sort of history there. Some legendary demon apparently terrorized their kind thousands of moons ago...-

    -And the three drag the attempted assassins before the Archeops-

    <Lileep: "(*sigh*) Knew I should have stuck with marine service.">

    <Kabutops: "Wh-What are you going to d-do with us?">

    <Archeops: "That depends... If you tell me who sent you, I might decide not to make the lot of you into the centerpiece for next week's banquet...">

    -At the mention of this, the Omastar weakly retreats into its shell, and the Kabutops and Venusaur both begin to tremble-

    <Venusaur: "B-B-But I'm n-no good f-for e-eating! M-My b-body and pl-plant are full of p-poisons!">

    <Archeops: "Oh, I'm sure that I'll find a use for you at the table... Perhaps as a garnish...">

    <Venusaur: "A- G-Gar-Ur-Ur-Urk...">
