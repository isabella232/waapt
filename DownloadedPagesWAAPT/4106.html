    -There were several factors to take into account of this escape attempt was to go off without a hitch.

    Firstly, the guards. Off the three of them, the Dragonite seemed the strongest, but Holly, being a Dragon type, had an advantage against him. However, it did work both ways, so if a fight did break out, taking him out would be her first priority.

    Slightly less worrying, but still a potential threat, was the Gallade and the Ariados, the Gallade being slightly more dangerous, as due to her Delta mutation, Holly was part Poison rather than Ground like the vast majority of her species. So if he hit her with a Psychic attack, it would hurt. Badly.

    The thing was, though, that it seemed unlikely that the Gallade and the Ariados would be able to fight her in their weakened condition.

    Even if she did manage to beat them, though, the noise from the scuffle would likely alert the other Pokémon on the boat to her presence. Granted, she could probably release the others to help her take them out, but considering that the three of them working together wasn't enough to beat all of those Pokémon in a fight, it didn't seem likely that her help would be enough to turn the tide.

    So, if this mission was to have any chance at success, Holly would have to do everything that she could to avoid conflict.

    And since it was morning, and everyone would likely be waking up soon, she would have to act quickly.
