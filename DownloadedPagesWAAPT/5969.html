    Mt. moon; B2

    Rex was dropped down in front of Suana. He was a large man in a snake-skin hood. Rex squeaked as the large, pot-bellied man picked her up off the ground and sat her on his knee.

    "Uncle Suana!"

    "Well if it isn't lil Rex, well lil'er Rex!" The large man bellowed. He tugged at the yellow, snake-skin hood as he snorted some, taking a bite out of his sammich. Loudly chewing he would explain their dilemma.

    The Idohori triplets sat out a picnic blanket as they took out their thermos to pour out the tea for lunch time. Uncle Suana pulled off a piece of his sammich and offered some to Rex, who happily took it and nommed at it.

    "Well it seems girl they found your old hoodie down in the Plant. They had gone back to investigate, but couldn't find anything proving your innocence. They managed ta get yer hoodie though."

    Uncle Suana snapped his fingers and motioned to the twins. They scrambled to their feet, bumping into each other before dashing in opposite directions. After a moment they came back holding a deep orange hoodie. Little Rex squealed out and jumped off Uncle Suana's knee. When she dashed toward the triplets they let the hoodie fall down.

    The orange hoodie fluttered down and draped over the tiny trainer.

    "Ah it's a bit big." He held out his hand, the triplets set the thermos cup in his hand. He lowered it to Rex. "Well take a sip, seein' as ya can't fit in it."

    Rex took the cup and drank up.
