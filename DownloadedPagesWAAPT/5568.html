    Across Different Places in Sinnoh

    •The region is shrouded in darkness, yet in the form of collectible McGuffins, there is the light•

    Canalace City

    Three Spiritombs are constantly circling the Move Deleter house. Inside, stuck into the kitchen's fluorescent lamp, a faint glow marks the position of a [Tear].

    How will you notice it is there? Ask your creativity! Oh and don't forget to slap those Spiritomb silly~

    Floaroma Town

    The town is a disaster, as without human or machine intervention, the bad weed has invaded the town from the western and northern valleys and covers the entire northern third of the town. Somewhere, there is a [Tear] randomly lying on the ground, but it is so deeply covered by vines and leaves that one would have to pretty much trip on it while walking into the weed for some plot-convenient purpose.

    This one is more or less designed for silliness. Of course, if you want a Fire mon to BURN! the place, that's another approach. ^_^

    Diablo's Ocean link

    In one of the many craters hidden underwater, a [Tear] is stuck barely peeking out of the sand after having been carried there from Arceus knows where. At the shore, there are two Armortombs making guard, and two groups of three Spiritombs each "protecting" fake plot points so as to ambush any possible opposition.

    How would you even suspect there could be a Tear underwater? You are not supposed to know the traps are traps... but we are Genre Savvy... or perhaps Wrong Genre Savvy?

    Route 219

    In a small nest in the sand island where previously a Buizel family lived, all that remains is some food, some wisps of the young tubers roaming around, and a faint glow of a [Tear].

    Not much to say here, except why would anyone take this paath?

    Amity Square Pond

    A [Tear] is stuck on the pond, held in place by some random vines in a position that is only clearly visible from the northern, elevated site of the landmark.

    This one is more or less designed for a "check everywhere" scenario. You know, some videogames are pretty insistent with those...

    All these Tear location suggestions were checked by Tagg. Of course, feel free to create more! There's still over 80 to be collected! (IN ORDER) Remember to at least have one per settlement. Glad to be of help. And have fun!
