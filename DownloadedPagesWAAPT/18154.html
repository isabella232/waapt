    "And yeah, of course I won't catch you right now, that wouldn't be fair."

    Brandon: <I know, isn't it great!>

    Fletcha: -to the Skarmory- "Yeah...long story..."

    "You do look pretty banged up though, would you like me to take you to the Fortree Pokemon Center?"

    Skarmory: <Wouldn't that require me to be transported by Pokeball?>

    <How do I know you won't just run off with me once I'm inside?>

    Brandon: <Oh, Fletcha would never do something like that!>

    George: <Brandon's right, this Kid wouldn't hurt a flygon>

    Fletcha: -to his Mons- "Thanks for the sentiments," -turns back to the Skarmory- "but you are right, you don't know me and it's perfectly reasonable for you to be concerned about that possible outcome..."

    "But the only other option I can think of is just me trying to patch you up with potions, which definitely wouldn't be as good as treatment at a Pokemon Center."

    "Whichever you think, I won't be offended in the slightest if you don't decide to come to the Pokemon Center." -he smiles-

    -The Skarmory spends some time thinking before coming to a decision-

    Skarmory: <Alright, you can take me to the center, but if you try any funny business I'll be more than mad next time I see you.>

    George: <Wise choice.>

    Brandon: <I hope you are able to recover quickly.> -he smiles-

    Fletcha: "Ok, this will just take a second..."
