    Spaghettipost with Silent, and Crow as Olympia.

    Anistar City

    Silent is standing at the entrance of the Gym, accompanied by Dintel, his Sandshrew♂ who sits on his head. Eridanus on the other hand, has Denshi (Raichu♀) on his left, and Forge (Magnemite, masculine) floating on his right.

    Eridanus: So, who goes in first?

    Silent: I think we can go in together.

    Eridanus: Okay... We go in three... two... one...!

    Anistar Gym

    Eridanus: So, how do we progress forward, Silent? Gyms usually have gimmicks

    Silent: I think we can go through... [looks around] that way, or that way, or...

    [Dintel climbs down to the floor]

    Dintel: <The floor is yellow that way! I want to go that way!>

    [Forge floats around]

    [Eridanus walks bit through Gym... then stops]

    Silent: What happened? [sighs]

    Eridanus: Wait... is this... outer space?! As, in THE outer space? [squeals like a fanboy] [beat] ...did I just do what I think I just did?

    Silent: [looks around] I think so? I'm not sure, I mean we are breathing...

    Eridanus: It could always have artifical atmosphere, or something along these lines.

    Silent: Okay, I guess we can live with that. [to Forge] Can you see a good path?

    Forge: <Hmm... The path branches once in a while... I can see other parts floating there just fine... But I can't make out the path that would directly lead to Gym Leader, because the entire place is too large. You would have to do this from bird's view.>

    Denshi: <This place is so pretty and lovely, don't you think?>

    Dintel: <Yeah, I really like it! c:>

    Silent: Hmmm.... bird's view. I have one with me. [And sends out Shahinne (Starly♀) who goes flying around]

    Eridanus: Bids on birds being limited. Because where would be challenge if you could just fly straight to the Leader...

    Shahinne: <You're silly I can fly where I wa-> — and crashes against an invisible barrier.

    Eridanus: You owe me a drink. Or something. Wait, what can a bird give me? Its feather?

    Forge: <Well that removes bird's view out of the equation. We're going to do it the old-fashioned way. Because I am NOT going to test if I can float around freely...>

    Silent: You'd be surprised. [to Shahinne] Why don't you just go along with Dintel?

    Shahinne: <...No, I can go around it...> — and starts pecking the invisible barrier. [spends some time flying around and pecking the barrier, while Silent just waves his hand and walks away]

    [Fox pops out]

    Fox: <Girl. Don't do that. You'll just hurt yourself. We aren't supposed to break through this, y know.>

    [Eridanus and the rest follow Silent]

    Shahinne: [frowns] <I'll hurt this wall. You'll just see...> — and continues pecking.

    [Silent follows Dintel for a while until the Sandshrew reaches a small platform opening to two paths, with a Trainer standing around]

    Silent: So… what do we do?

    Eridanus: Battle Trainer?

    [They go onwards, Eridanus is still... being amazed. And then the path branches] lol skipping Trainer

    Eridanus: Now what? Who goes which way?

    Silent: Hmmm.... [Looks around, and defers to Dintel]

    Dintel: [looks around] <I'm going to go that way because it goes downwards and I can roll!> [Curls into a ball and slides down the path to the right]

    Forge: <I'll better follow him. He might hurt someone, for the sake of->

    Silent: [shrugs] He'll be fine, don't worry. Still, we need to continue.

    Eridanus: Uh, dude. Forge is already away. Who are you talking to?

    Denshi: <Wait, where's Sol?>

    Eridanus: So, we're going the other way, then? [goes the other path]

    Fast forward to Olympia

    Olympia: [Notes the two challengers walking in] Two trainers at once? Here for a double battle, or one at a time?

    Eridanus: Double battle.

    Silent: That's right. I'm Silent and I'd like to assist in the challlenge of this young Trainer here. [Produces a couple of Pokéballs]

    Olympia: I understand then. Now for your fates and futures - a double battle! [Sends out a Sigilyph and a Lunatone]

    Eridanus: So... Silent... Do you have any tactic?

    Silent: Okay... let's see. [Thinks for a moment, decides to send out Sol (Espeon♀)] The best is to get to know the opponent I guess. Sol should be able to draw their strategies out. Who do you intend to send out?

    Eridanus: Hmm... I think I'll start with... This! [sends out Forge] He has a thing to make their lives harder if we played it correctly, I think.
