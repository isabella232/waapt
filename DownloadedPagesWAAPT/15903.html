    Vien Forest, Past

    Ray coughs."...Some of them were really flashy. I mean, seriously, some of the Unfezants strutted around with rubies on their plumes, there were Herdiers bragged about being owned by movie stars and millionares, you wouldn't believe how many drunk Pansears I heard, their woes about not being picked up by the circus or that they couldn't learn Solar Beam, and so many complaining about eating at a four star restaurant instead of a five star..seriously, it was annoying after a while."

    But now, she started to get worked up and even a bit angry,"But some of the worst were the crossdressers. And no, I'm not against them, except some of the flaunted about how they were doing it for fun. Like, Female Unfezants putting on head plumes and painting their underbellies because they thought it looked glamorous, Mincinnos pretending to be females because they thought they would get less work, and the like. This really bugged me because there were real mons and people, some of whom I actually knew, that had gender and identity problems and I...I really wanted to help them figure themselves out," she was tearing up a bit now,"but I couldn't, I just couldn't....sometimes I was scared that I had made it even worse. And then, these people and mons doing it the way they did felt..it felt like they were treating it as a joke when it's anything but." She finished, and calmed down a bit, sheepish, taking in deep breaths.

    "I'm sorry, guess I got a little carried away."
