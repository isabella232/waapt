    Goldenrod Research Wing

    Silent awakens after a long night of sleep. He performs his usual routine — then getting out of bed.

    "Owwww... *yawn* Let's take a walk around."

    Takes a walk around to check what is going on. First he peeks on the room where Copahue and Shahinne are playing around.

    Copahue -roosted near the window — <...now, little bird, you should be having breakfast, please? We can go play later, I'm still too sleepy...>

    Shahinne -flutters around — <No. I want to go play around.> — flies around Copahue's legs.

    Copahue — <...You are not going to peck me, please?>

    Next, Silent goes to the lounge to check on the interns. Since the new TV has not arrived yet, the interns have brought a radio from the basement and are trying to get it to work.

    Dëvich — "...and what is this weird thing for anyway?"

    Timbú — "Looks like an antenna. How would one use it anyway?"

    Rodríguez — "Uhm... there seems to be something like a cartridge inside. Perhaps if we press those buttons we can get it out?"

    The interns start pressing buttons.

    Somehow they stumble on the tape player's [▶] button instead of the [⏏] button, and then it starts playing.

    [[center:~Playing link~ ]]

    The internst start looking at each other.

    Silent shakes his nead.

    Newbies...

    Afterwards Silent goes check the room where his Pokéballs are stored. As he left them last night, Lileep's, Dintel's and Algira's balls are there.

    Finally, he goes walking around until he hears something coming from the yard. He peeks out of a window to notice Inka and Baluarkos sunbathing. Maekrite is with them, ears down. Some of the kits are playing with the random boxes and containers there.

    Inka -yawns — <...and you should be more proactive when it comes to battle. You are not a patroller for nothing, you know!>

    Maekrite — <...yes, marli.>

    Inka — <Not to mention,> — bares her teeth — <you are adamant damn it. Should be pummeling that thing with your own fistpaws instead of beaming from afar like a lazy Nidoran.>

    Maekrite — <...yes, marli.> — sighs.

    Inka -irises glow red — <I HEARD THAT!>

    Hmm... some things never change. Let's see, I better go check the whole thing with the TV and then call Tangent and the others to say hi...
