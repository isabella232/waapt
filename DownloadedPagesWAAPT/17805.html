    Lucius: ...Eskay, what happened?

    Eskay: I was caught by a horde of fans—

    Lucius: That's not what I meant.

    -He points to Eskay's scar, which had grown bigger as a result of the events in Psyche's Mindscape.-

    Lucius: That wasn't that big before. I know it wasn't that big. What the hell happened to you?

    -Eskay blinks, then sighs as he sits down.-

    Eskay: ...I got stabbed in Psyche's mind a few days back. She was the one to make sure I stayed alive, but somehow the wound came with me out of mentalspace.

    -Lucius frowns-

    Lucius: Eskay, I should not have to be the one to tell you to be careful. I've already been down that road, and I can tell you from experience that it does not end well.

    Eskay: I know, I know, I wasn't expecting it to turn out that way... I just wanted to help. I was on my own road to recovery and I wanted to help her out as well.

    -Lucius prepares to say something... then sighs and sits down next to Eskay.-

    Lucius: ...I guess I'm the last person to blame you. Been there, done that, got the t-shirt and all. I just... holy shit, Eskay...

    -His eyes widen as he notices a second, identical scar running down Eskay's back.-

    Lucius: Did that thing go all the way through?!

    Eskay: ...Yes...

    Lucius: Dear Arceus, Eskay, you've gotta get some sort of medical attention for that! There is absolutely no way that could have happened without some serious internal damage! It's a wonder you're still alive at all, considering what happened to—

    Eskay: Lucius, relax! Psyche already healed most of the internal damage before it all got transferred to my real, physical body. It took almost all of her energy, but now it's nothing more than a pair of marks and a bit of soreness.

    Lucius: ...If you say so...

    -He sighs-

    Lucius: Just... please, be careful. You do not want to end up how I ended up.

    Eskay: I will...

    Maul: ~Incidentally, one hour.~

    -Eskay shoots Maul a glare-

    Eskay: I said I give up, okay?

    Lucius: Okay, seriously, what's with the whole "one hour'' thing?

    -Eskay hesitates, then sighs and looks down.-

    Eskay: ...I've had absolutely no idea what to do for you today. It's Valentine's Day.

    Lucius: ...That's what you're so worked up over?

    Eskay: Yeah... I should have done something, even if it wasn't big and flashy and—

    -Lucius chuckles-

    Lucius: Seriously, quit worrying about it. Why would I be disappointed, when I didn't even realize it was today?

    -He pats Eskay-

    Lucius: You're fine. It's the thought that counts. And besides, an hour of remembering the fact that it exists is better than nothing.

    -Eskay smiles-

    Eskay: Yeah...

    -He kisses Lucius's cheek.-

    Eskay: And besides, I get to spend that hour with you anyway.

    -Lucius blushes, but smirks-

    Lucius: Do you have any idea how sappy that sounded?

    -Eskay grins-

    Eskay: Of course. Incredibly, sickeningly sappy.
