    High Priestess of Fabulous

    Holon City

    Psyche is here and is meditating. She remembers her experience in meditation several months ago, while she was training with Caitlin.

    Psyche's Mindscape

    Reason, Diligence, Superego, and Kindness are looking at a door expectantly.

    Kindness: "I don't like doing this."

    Reason: "We have to. For the good of the mind."

    Diligence: "And so we watch. Until we are relieved."

    Superego: "You virtues and your sense of honor and emotions. We don't do this because we want to. We don't do this for glory, or because it feels good. We do it, because if what's behind this door is released, it would cause a series of events that would be rather unfortunate. If the mortal dies, we die. It only goes to show, then, that we protect her. From herself."

    Holon City

    Psyche meditates deeper.

    Mindscape

    Psyche opens her eyes, to find herself in her own mind. "This is an interesting occurrence. I've seen other people's mindscape. Been a while since I've been in here. And that door's new."

    Superego looks at her. "..."

    Diligence looks at her. "..."

    Reason looks at her. "..."

    Psyche: "What a warm reception."

    Kindness smiles. "Welcome, mistress! Is something wrong?"

    Psyche shakes her head. "But I'm getting in that door."

    Diligence scoffs. "Over my dead body."

    Psyche blinks. "You... really just said that. I guess I have to say, 'that can be arranged'."

    Diligence crosses her arms. "Try me."

    Psyche shrugs, and tk's them to the top of the library. "Thank you. I need something in the restricted section, thank you. Might I have the assistance of Reason? She's the librarian here."

    She opens the door with the help of Reason...

    She sees a girl curled up in the corner, barely breathing, whimpering softly, battered and bruised, twisted and mangled. She looks at Reason. "Memories," Reason explains. "A potent thing, and we try to lock up your mental trauma. Including..."

    A girl dressed as a Jellicent walks up to Psyche. "Hey. I'm you. From... well, you know. I didn't get to talk to you much."

    Psyche bites her lip. "Why are you locked up here? If anything, I should be."

    Caroline: "You manipulate. You scheme. I lead. I strategize. The difference? I do it large scale, you do it subtly. Why does this make me better than you? You gave up that past, I keep it up."

    Psyche: "What happened? Why do you care for those you manipulate?"

    Caroline: "I was raised by them. I in turn gave respect to those I raised. You didn't manipulate those that raised you, nor did you raise any others. You manipulated those who didn't know you. Who couldn't know you. I had different experiences than you. That's it. No less heartrending, no more tear-jerking. Just... Different."

    Psyche sighs. Caroline speaks up. "But we both know that's not why you came."

    Psyche grits her teeth. "I want the pack."

    Caroline shrugs. "You got it."

    She waves and a film projector plays a reel. "This is what happened to you."

    Psyche shudders during the whole ordeal. "And to you?"

    Caroline: "That's not why you're here."

    Psyche grits her teeth. The film shows the Scrafty vanishing into the sandstorm.

    Caroline waves the film projector away. "Time to confront it."

    Psyche shudders. "No..."

    Caroline: "Yes. You need to do this, or you're never going to get through this. Maybe one day we can keep that door open to visitors... Reconciling this will be the best thing you can do for now."

    A Scrafty and its buddies pop out of nowhere. Psyche locks up. "You can do this... You can do this... You can do this..."

    She begins weeping. "I can't do this." The Scrafty go for the girl in the corner. "No... I can't let her get hurt worse. She's been through too much as it is." She leaps into action, moving surprisingly quickly. She uses Miracle Eye on the mons, and spams Extrasensory, getting more and more furious until they are all out. Caroline slow claps. "Interesting. A good first start. But you need to react quicker. It needs to be a knee jerk when you realize you're in danger. Even if you ARE afraid after that, your reflexes will kick in before your freeze response does. But, I think this is going to hurt more than it's going to help if you stay here much longer. Wake up." She flicks her on the forehead.

    Holon City

    Psyche continues meditating, the memory over.
