    Are those...Gills?!?'

    He tried to swim back up to the water's surface. He crashed into it as if it were something solid. It felt like a thick pane of glass trapping him underwater.

    He screamed and banged on the surface, but there was no use.

    Do you see the truth now?

    Not even once? Not one choice in your whole life?

    "If I had a choice in my life, I'd be rich, famous, and I certainly wouldn't be talking to you."

    You poor, misled boy. When will you ever learn?

    Suddenly, Martin felt himself falling, plummeting in a deep vortex.

    He looked down at the ocean floor and saw a hole. An unplugged drain sucking everything into it.
