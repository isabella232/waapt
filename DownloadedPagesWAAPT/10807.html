    Drake: Right... So how are you still..?

    John: If I had to guess, I think it's my faith. Got a small shrine to the mistress of time out in the yard.

    Drake: Hmph, doubt it, I'm rather devout to Celebi and it didn't protect me, had to resort to ancient magics to retain my humanity...

    I quickly showed the armband on my wrist.

    John: Maybe she's testing you? Thinks you've done something wrong and needed to take a different path?

    Drake: Perhaps...

    I tried to put my mask back on, it still fit, only covered half of my face though...

    John: Need directions out of here?

    Drake: That would be good, yes...

    John stood up and walked out of the house, I hobbled after him.

    John: Let's see, to the north there's a dragon camp, to the east too. Though I suppose you knew that, given you were likely taking that path already. 'Course I doubt the dragons can hold power forever, and when they lose it... The results won't be pretty, especially for their leaders... Or, you could walk west, off the Route. It's a long, lonesome road and I can't be certain where it leads... But it's better than a place that's falling apart...

    Drake: Guess I have a choice to make...

    John: And you're the only one that can make it. But if I had to make a suggestion, I'd suggest going west, take a chance and see if things can be better than they are here...

    Drake: "Two roads diverged in a yellowed wood..."

    John: Well, I'm headed back inside. Feel free to take your time, don't want to make the wrong choice...

    John walked back inside and I stood staring at the route for a while, mostly at the abandoned wreckage of the convoy.

    Drake: Hey, you got any supplies I could have?

    When I turned around, the entire building was gone, only the trail west left behind.

    Drake: Ah, makes sense... I suppose that was more of a passive-aggressive warning than anything else... Might as well take her advice...

    I went and scavenged what I could from the wreckage before heading down the thin trail leading west and out of the route.
