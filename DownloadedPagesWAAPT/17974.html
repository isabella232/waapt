    Mossdeep City

    -Anima is loading the first batch of groceries into the boat she takes to Shoal Cave when a familiar-looking Bulbasaur looks up at her and cries happily. She sets her groceries down and pats the Bulbasaur on the head.-

    Anima: You look just like...

    ???: Vewe! There you are!

    -Anima's heart skips several beats due to a combination of the familiar-sounding voice and the name being called. A sixteen-year-old blonde-haired girl that looks disturbingly familiar to Anima runs toward the two.-

    ???: I'm sorry if he bothered you, miss.

    Anima: N-no, it's not a problem.

    -She smiles and resumes loading the groceries into the boat, but is still a bit shaky and drops a few.-

    ???: Oh, let me help with that!

    Anima: Y-you don't need to do that.

    ???: No, it's the least I can do to make up for Vewe. He always goes off bothering people, the silly thing.

    -The girl smiles. Realizing nothing she can say will make the girl go away, Anima nods.-

    Anima: Thank you.

    -The girl beams and helps load the remaining groceries into the boat. Anima thanks her again, and the girl starts to run off, but the Bulbasaur jumps out of her hands and approaches Anima again. The girl runs to pick up the Bulbasaur.-

    ???: I'm so sorry! Vewe isn't usually like this!

    -She picks him up again and looks at Anima again.-

    ???: You look a lot like this girl from those pictures of my sister. I think he's mistaking you for her.

    Anima: Your sister?

    -The girl nods.-

    ???: Vewe was her starter, but when she passed away, my parents started taking care of him, and now I do.

    -Anima is trying hard to keep from crying. Her voice may crack a bit.-

    Anima: That must have been terrible.

    -She smiles softly.-

    ???: Not really. I don't remember her. I was only two when she died, after all. But it would still be nice if there was some way to meet her.

    -She shakes her head.-

    ???: Sorry, I shouldn't be so bothersome. Vewe's rubbing off on me today, I guess! Hope you have a good day, miss!

    -She runs off. This time, the Bulbasaur doesn't come back to Anima. When the girl is out of her sight, she cries.-
