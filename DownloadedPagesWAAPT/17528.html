    Sekra Range, Past

    -After Pent and Crow take their shots at Mammor, Mammor is now beyond the border of the Anti-Teleport field. The area goes quiet as a steady clink, clank, clink, clank fills the area. Eyes stare out from the nearby foliage, all focused on Mammor.-

    Mammor: -wincing in pain- Now what

    -A steady chant starts up.-

    Pawniard: <Princess of Steel Princess of Steel>

    -The chant catches Rios attention and turn to see one portion of foliage bending back. Mammor grabs a stick with his left hand, wincing from the pain from his right arm and ribs.-

    Mammor: -weakly- Leave now. You do not know who youre dealing with.

    -A voice, echoing as if being spoken through a metal tube, enters Mammors mind.-

    Devilotte de Deathsatan XIII: ~I know exactly who I am dealing with, insect.~

    -A steel-grey Gardevoir steps out from the bending foliate, her face framed by a blonde-golden crest.-

    Mammor: I have no time to deal with the likes of you. -deep breath- Get out my way if you know what is good for you.

    -Mammor throws the stick at Devilotte and starts to crawl away. The stick is quickly intercepted by a steel ball-bearing, shattering the piece of wood with a sickening *CRACK* before it gets anywhere close to the advancing Gardevoir. The sound of Devilottes steps and the chanting of the Pawniard are overcome by the massive stomps of an orange Golurk.-

    Eight: <Yknow, youre just making her angrier with stunts like this.>

    -The Golurk puts himself in front of the crawling man. Mammor, not understanding Monese, just looks up at the massive Delta in front of him, a revelation dawning on his face.-

    Mammor: -at Eight- You. You were at Citadark...

    Eight: <Ah, NOW he gets it.>

    Devilotte: ~You are correct. Now, however, I shall collect the debt that remains - you attempted injury of my royal consort. On top of many other crimes against those that I have deemed worthy of my protection, there will be reckoning.~

    -Mammor, now worried and panicked, turns on his back and clumsily uses his left arm to try to move away from Devilotte.-

    Mammor: No you will not. Pokemon are merely tools. I am destined for greatness. I will crush you underneath my heel

    -Delphox!Ren and his team have reached the area near the border where the confrontation is happening and is just watching - Rio made it abundantly clear that Devilotte is not one to raise objections to when angered. Although a part of Ren and his teams minds are rolling around laughing at the mention of my royal consort.-

    Devilotte: ~Do not turn away while your betters are speaking to you.~

    -A high-pitched whistle can be heard as a ball bearing flies through the air, before coming down just mere centimeters from Mammors left hand.-

    Devilotte: ~Because I am a fair ruler, you get one warning for that transgression. However~

    -Devilotte motions to Eight, who wraps a massive and rather hot hand around Mammors waist - it feels as if an active car engine is grasping him.-

    Mammor: ARRRGGGH! -panting while glaring murderously at Devilotte- What gives you the right to offer me a warning? -panting- I will be the one to end you... Clavis... the J-Team... and anyone else who stands in my -pant- my way

    -Devilotte motions to Eight again. The Golurk cools his hand and tosses Mammor back to the ground. She then motions around her, and a group of Bisharp step forward, all different colors. They move to flank Mammor from all angles, silently watching as the Pawniard continue to chant. Mammor struggles to a semi-sitting position facing Devilotte with defiance on his face-

    Devilotte: ~It appears that an animal like you cannot be reasoned with.~

    -Devilotte barely moves as the whistling picks up again, and a ball bearing begins to spin over her head. Her eyes begin to glow a brilliant pink light as the world seems to fall away from Mammor, leaving an empty, steely grey void in every direction.-

    Mammor: What is this?!

    -Images seem to materialize out of the void - images that Mammor may find familiar. One of them is a white-haired man, dressed in a black hat and jacket and carrying Mammors own whip on his belt, laughing while sharing an ice cream with a strange girl with curly pink hair.-

    Devilotte: ~Everything you are is transient. Everything you have ever done will be just a memory.~

    -Images of an older Ren appear. He is walking with a lady with golden hair - wedding rings on their hands. In front of them is a small girl of about six playing with a metallic grey Ralts with a pink crest and an adolescent Ninetales that has hair on the side of its head that is similar to Rens hair style watches from a tree nearby where an older Rio stands with a Riolu. Ren signals his children and his friends and their children and partners as they head back to a manor in Hoenn as the sun sets.-

    Devilotte: ~You do not even warrant as much thought as a giant rampaging tortilla.~

    -An image of a Murkrow sitting on a branch looking up at the stars flashes by, and a young man training with a staff made of Auric energy comes shortly after.-

    Devilotte: ~Your name will be forgotten, and history will forget you, as it has with so many before.~

    Mammor: -eyes wide in horror- No! This isnt how it will end!

    Devilotte: ~Yes.~

    -The mental image disappears with a rush of sound and light, putting Mammor back into reality. His face is being illuminated by a massive, collecting orb of light - the latent form of a massive Hyper Beam.-

    Devilotte: ~It is.~

    -Mammor face pales.-

    Devilotte: ~Disappear.~

    -Devilotte fires her Hyper Beam straight at, through, and beyond Mammors left leg.-

    -Moments pass...-

    -Ren and his party gape as the scene. Mammors face is now a mess and has blank, yet terrified expression. His eyes are wide as his have rolled back into his head as his broken glasses are barely hanging on to his head by one ear. His tear ducts and sinuses seem to have emptied onto his face and the contents are mixing in with the heavy sweat caused by fear and not pain. Mammor body is slack and his hair is no longer relatively neat despite the beating hed just received, but as been reduced to a limp, greasy, hanging mess. His jaw is hanging wide open, showing off the white insides of his suddenly dry mouth.-

    Mammor: -whimpering, utterly broken- Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me Please dont kill me

    -Devilotte takes a deep breath, feeling slightly drained from her dramatic showing.-

    Devilotte: <Eight, bring him. We have a couple of friends who would just love to have him.>

    Eight: <Yes, maam.>

    -Eight grabs Mammor again - who seems completely unaware of being picked up and moved by the Delta Golurk as he continues to mutter and whimper - and as Devilotte turns to leave, she looks over at where Ren and his team stands. She winks at a certain Gardevoir. Pierre, oblivious to the true meaning of this gesture, blushes slightly and bows to Devilotte. Ren and his team, following Pierres example, bow or curtsey to the Queen-in-waiting. Devilotte giggles at Pierres blush before she motions to two steely-colored Kadabra, and the entourage is gone with one, large *blip*-

    -Ren looks at Pierre.-

    Delphox!Ren: <You never told me she fancied you, Pierre.>

    -Pierre stiffens with shock and turns bright red.-

    Pierre: <W-wait. W-w-what? S-s-she does?!>

    Delphox!Ren: <Pierre... After we get ourselves and the others back and settled, Im going to have your parents sent over and the four of us are all going to sit down and have a nice chat.>

    Keys: <The fact that hes paired with her has me worried for our health if things go aspear-shaped.>
