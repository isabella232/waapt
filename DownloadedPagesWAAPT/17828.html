    Mauville

    -ah, am I still narrating? Good, good, good-

    -now, we find a girl, alone in her Center room. She's been doing far too much of that lately, and on some level she realizes this. At the same time, however, she is wracked with angst, jealousy and impotent rage. There is nothing that Samantha Muirgheal Kearney hates more than feeling so impotent and helpless, and yet she's been feeling it a lot lately-

    -talking to no one in particular, she states:-

    Samantha: What happened? When did I become so...so pathetic? I used to be strong, like he said...confident. And then I started traveling...

    -her head lolls back, staring at the ceiling-

    Samantha: ...searching...

    -she shuts her eyes for a very long time, and then she opens them. Distantly, in her head, she hears soft, otherworldly-sounding music; cold, and distant, yet possessed with a particular sort of fury and determination-

    -she opens her eyes, and sings in a way that sounds more like speaking-

    Samantha: If travel...is searching...

    -thinks back to Lucius—

    And home's...what's been found...

    -casts them aside in her head. They won't dominate her thoughts now-

    I'm not...stopping...

    -she practically spits out those last words as she blinks, rapidly-

    I'm...going hunting...

    -her head thrashes about, slightly, and as she sings the next words a marked change takes place. Her skin goes alabaster pale, her eyes black and deep, and fur shoots out slightly-

    I'm the hunter...

    -the fur recedes, and all changes go back to normal for the time being-

    I'll bring back the goods...

    -the fur shoots back out, Samantha wincing in pain and thrashing her head about as it does so, even further than the last time-

    But I don't know when...

    -faint echoes of cellos ring as she grips at her head, trying to slow the growing of the fur. From her head, a small horn pokes, causing Samantha to grip at her thighs, digging in to them with her nails to distract herself and steel her nerves-

    I thought I could organize freedom...

    -the next word, she screams out as she feels the next wave coming-

    HOOOOOOOOW very feminine of me!

    -as the wave comes, the fur growing up to her head and the horn poking out, she wails out incomprehensible words, thinking of Lucius, how he had gone away from her and figured out his true happiness. As the transformation is almost total, Samantha howls-

    YOU sussed it out, didn't you...!

    -concentrating with all of her being, the transformation recedes to almost normal. Samantha calms herself, singing-

    You could smell it...

    -thinks of Eskay-

    So you left me on my own...

    -snarls, feels another wave, more powerful than all before-

    To complete the mission...

    -smiles-

    But now I'm leaving it aaaaaaaaall behind...

    -relaxes for a moment, feeling it come-

    I'm going hunting...

    -repeats it to herself. The fur resurfaces, the eyes blacken, the horn grows-

    I'm the hunter...

    -and again, as it reaches a fever pitch...-

    I'm the hunter...

    -she snaps up. Almost all is still. An Absol woman, some strange, blocky combination of the two, stares up-

    I'm going hunting...

    -as the music swells, she battles, trying to get it back. She thinks of what she will do once she's through; challenging all of the gyms, battling her mons with others to improve, training herself most of all...-

    -finally, the fur, the horn, and the eyes go back to normal, and she sighs. She goes to sleep-
