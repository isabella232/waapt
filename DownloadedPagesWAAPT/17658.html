    Mauville, Near the Food Court

    -Ren and Rio are walking along the corridor.-

    Rio: <The place has really changed, hasn't it?>

    Ren: Oh yeah.

    -Ren looks at his watch.-

    Ren: We should probably find Jacob. It's about time to meet him.

    -Jacob and Regina are likewise walking along the opposite corridor-

    Jacob: Interesting, by renovating the city into a shopping district as well as a living area, you manage to raise the standard of living while at the same time making Mauville the center of Hoenn it was supposed to be. Everyone passes through here at some point, so it make sense

    Regina: <Well, I thought it was nice being able to see the sky before. Also youre running late to see Ren.>

    Jacob: Never late, Regina. Never late. See, the food court is right there.

    Regina: <I have a bad feeling, but okay. Let's head in.>

    Jacob: Oh come on, whats the worst that could happen? It's a battle food court, well be right at home. And hey, he's a professional, hes on par with Wallace in terms of coordinating abilities, he'll be able to help us out.

    -Jacob and Regina enter and see Ren and Rio staring into the food court with their mouths agape.-

    Jacob: Oh hey, Re-

    -He stops as he looks at the food court-

    Jacob: ...... I harshly underestimated what exactly this place was.

    Ren: You too, huh?

    -In addition to the battles going on over seating, food fights dot the food court. Salad bits, drinks, and puddings fly through the air and splatter on the floor as some patrons make the way to the non-battle seating area - stained and exhausted by the time they get there.-

    Rio: -dryly- <Anyone got four ponchos handy?>

    Jacob: -Just as dryly- Sure, let me just get my laptop out and pull out four ponchos that I conveniently own for these exact situations.

    Regina: <Not helping.>

    Jacob: Well, I mean, I have one, but I had it since I was eight. I dont think it fits any more.

    Regina: <Why are we getting so worried about this? We've faced armies and demons and giant tortillas, are we going to let food stop us?>

    Jacob: The difference between Cipher and this is that Cipher wont stain my suit. They might shoot holes in it, but those can be patched.

    Ren: -Agreeing with Jacob- Yeah, it's the stains I'm worried about - and Seamstress.

    Jacob: -Remembering Seamstress at his home-  Okay, now I have another thing to be terrified of.

    Regina: -Sighs- <Okay, well, your reserved table is on the other side of the court, so what are you going to do?>

    Rio: -jokingly- <They could always waltz through.>

    Ren: ... That's...

    Jacob: ...

    Regina: <.... I know that look. Jacob, no. No. You're not seriously considering->

    Jacob: Who has a metronome and a good singing voice?

    Ren: -pulling out his MP3 player cane- I can do better than a metronome.

    Jacob: Okay. Now then, we just need a song. Let's see...

    Ren: Preferably a song all four of us know. We dont want to crash into each other as we move.

    Jacob: Musically I'm in a very [Peter Pan]-ish mood today, but thats not going to help. Hm...

    Ren: Mary Martin or Disney?

    Jacob: Former.

    -Ren grins. Rio Face Paws. Ren raises his cane near his mouth.-

    Ren: Been a while, but... Play Track 51. Repeat song.

    Ren: -as the pirates start to play- Off we go!

    -Ren, using cane for extra balance, and using every of trick from his dancing lessons to their fullest, started to use hop steps and switching between which leg supported him frequently to avoid the food and drinks splattering on the floor. Ren put his cane behind his back as he spun left and right to dodge some airborne food. Ren had to tilt his whole body to dodge larger food items - and even bent himself backwards with an impressive kick step at one point to dodge a burger. As Ren hopped, spun, and kick-stepped to make his way to the order counter, Rio, using his Aura senses and reflexes, kept in perfect step with Ren. While little short on breath, Ren had no trouble waving an "okay" sign to Jacob and Regina-

    Jacob: Well we cant be shown up, can we?

    -Smiling, he sets off towards the counters, Regina twirling behind him. Jacob, while having no formal dance lessons, was smart enough to observe Rens "performance" and try to mimic it. Jacob realized quickly, however, that unlike Ren, he was better using footwork to dodge, rather than spinning out of the way, as he got dizzy after his second turn and was too late dodging a surprised girl jumping up and splashing her water. Jacob's above-standard footwork paid off when, with a quick backwards step, dodged a carton of fries with ketchup that splattered on a column, and only getting a bit of ketchup on his face. As Jacob and Regina approached the order counter, another customer with a try with a nacho salad on it walks out to the seat area, and lifts his try up seeing the pair - only for Jacob and Regina to pull a spectacularly synchronized limbo move to avoid the tray. So with only some minor water splotches on his jacket and a dab of ketchup on his face, Jacob slumps over the order counter.-

    Jacob: Out... of shape... wow...

    Ren: You should have see me the few weeks after I got my leg fixed in Orre, I wasn't used to walking without a bum leg, so I used more energy than I thought I needed and was out breath just walking downstairs from my room.

    Jacob: I guess, but wow, I used to be one of the most fit people in this group. Swimming across Hoenn and climbing mountains while carrying Lity was good for muscle building. And oh hey I just have water spots on my suit. It worked!

    -Rio hands Jacob a napkin.-

    Rio: <On your face.>

    Jacob: -Sighs and slumps more, but takes the napkin- Thank you Rio... -Wipes his face off-

    Ren: You still did rather well though.
