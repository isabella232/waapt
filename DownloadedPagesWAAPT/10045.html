    Sophia: <It was big enough to fill a small village. We kept close together during travels to discourage attackers. It didn't always work, and our numbers reduced every month or so despite our psychic powers amplified by each other's concern for one another.

    Nathan: <My condolences.>

    Nathan: <They were desensitized to the fact.>

    Sophia: <My species feel emotions in an amplified way, so it was a tough task to fulfill, as we could kind of the pain we inflicted.
