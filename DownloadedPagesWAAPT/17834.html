    Dewford Gym, During the week before Valentine's

    -Ren has Challenged Brawly with Vanamonde, Pierre, Conifer, and Shantotto. Brawly used a Hawklucha, Scrafty, Medicham, and Hariyama. Vanamonde was able to defeat the Hawklucha by inflicting a burn and using Hex and Confuse Ray. However Vanamonde was Easily KO'd by with a Feint Attack from Brawly's Scrafty whose Shed Skin prevented the burn inflicted by Vanamonde from hurting it too much. The Scrafty, in turn, was countered by Pierre who used Safegaurd to prevent his Flame Orb from burning himself and KOing the Scrafty with Moonblast. Pierre was also able to KO Brawly's Medicham in a similar fashion despite being forced to take some Burn damage from a Trick and Fling combo before his Traced Shed Skin cured it.-

    Brawly: -recalling Medicham- Wow! Swamped again! Time to ride the big one, Hariyama!

    -Brawly then sends out his Hariyama with a Sitrus Berry-

    Ren: With no more Flame Orb, Trick is useless. Sooo...

    -out loud- Wil-o-Wisp!

    Brawly: Bullet Punch!

    Hariyama: <Tachi-ai!>

    -Pierre conjures ghostly blue flames he has conjured as Hariyama strike his side with a palm strike from hand with metallic gleam. Unable to evade Pierre's conjured flames, Hariyama's ability activates.-

    Hariyama was burned!
    Hariyama's suffering makes him stronger!

    Hariyama: -irritated- <AAAAAH!>

    Brawly: Bullet Punch again!

    Hariyama: <Tsukitaoshi!>

    -Once again, Hariyama rushed forward with his hands gleaming and delivers a massive palm strike that sends Pierre flying and KOing the Gardevoir in the process.-

    Pierre fainted!

    Ren: -recalling Pierre- Thank you Pierre. You gave a spectacular performance.

    -Ren sends out Conifer, a Grass Gem in her claws.-

    Ren: Conifer, give us a repeat performance and end the show with your speed! Leaf Blade!

    -The leafs on Conifer's arms lengthen and in to blade-like leafs and then grow sharper as her Grass Gem glows and then vanishes, also activating her ability.-

    Conifer: -grinning- <This will only sting for a bit.>

    -Conifer shoots forward as Brawly calls out-

    Brawly: Dodge and counter with Force Palm!

    Hariyama: <Henka!>

    -To Ren and Conifer's surprise, the stout fighting type deftly evades Conifer's slashing moves and delivers a massive palm strike to Conifer's back.-

    Hariyama: <Hatakikomi!>

    Conifer: <ARGH! Guh!?>

    -Conifer's muscles spasm and become difficult to move.-

    Conifer was paralyzed, she might not be able to move!

    Conifer: <Ren... *spasm* Got a problem here. I can't move well and I don't think I can take another hit like that.>

    Ren: And there goes Conifer's advantage...

    -Out loud- Change gears then, Worry Seed!

    Brawly: Keep riding the wave! Arm Thrust!

    Hariyama: -dashing forward- <Tsukidashi!>

    Conifer: <Hopefully this will stop you from yelling in [Japanese]...>

    -Right before five rapid palm strike her, Conifer spits a small seed into Hariyama's face, which breaks and covers Hariyama's face with a powder-like substance that changes Hariyama's ability to Insomnia.-

    Hariyama: -now feeling his burn in full force.- <Itai! ITAI! ITAI!>

    Conifer and Ren: Seriously?>

    Conifer fainted!

    -Ren recalls Conifer and pulls out the Premier ball with the six stylized elemental emblems on it.-

    Ren: Shantotto! You're the only one who can make this show a success!

    -As the diminutive Braixen takes the field, she adjusts the Psychic Gem tied to her wrist.-

    Shantotto: -in bored tone- <Once again I must say, "It's up to me to save the day".>

    Brawly: A Braixen, huh? Looks like I'm going to keep on riding the waves you made!

    Ren: Shantotto! We're going to stall! Use Psybeam and try to confuse it!

    Shantotto: <The bigger they are, the Harder they fall, so I'll do my best to worsen your pall.>

    Brawly: Ride it out then use Earthquake!

    Hariyama: -lifting his right leg- <Shi— OOOOOH!>

    -Before can get Hariyama moving, Shantotto had swung her staff-stick and zapped Hariyama in the faces with a powered-up multicolored beam of psychic energy. Hariyama shakes his head to clear it, obviously not confused and intending to eat the Sitrus berry around his neck - but sees that it no longer there. Hariyama frantically looks around when he sees it missing, until a mocking voice calls out...-

    Shantotto: <I'm sorry, are you looking for this? Is a berry so bright really that hard to miss?>

    Hariyama: -furious, while raising his right leg higher than the last time.- <SHIIIIIII-KO!>

    -Hariyama brings his foot down with a ferocious stomp, creating shock waves so powerful that causes the dust in the gym's floor to be kicked up in a massive cloud. As a few grunts signal Shantotto taking heavy damage.-

    Brawly: -waiting for the dust to settle before making a call- Whoa, what a storm! What happened?

    Ren: ... Come on, Shantotto... Let me know if you're okay...

    -A faint crunching sound can briefly be heard as a bright light begins to shine in the cloud as a new, but familiar laugh rings out.-

    Delphox!Shantotto: <O-hohohohohoho! So glorious, so spectacular, so marvelous is this new power! And with it, I shall ring out your final hour! O-HOHOHOHOHOHO!>

    Hariyama: -sweat drops- <Nani?>

    Brawly: -sweat drops- What.

    Ren: -sweat drops- I honestly don't know whether to shout in victory or flee in abject terror.
