    "Eventually, I got better. The egg hatched into the Beldum I named Argentum. I still was going no where. Then, of course, was the point in which I decided that I would use brains to overcome brawn."

    "Five years. Five years of winning and saving money. I bought clothes. I bought food. I finally could live on my own and not have to rely on the charity of others. It was around this time I decided I would see every Pokemon in Hoenn. And I set out to do so."

I decided to move on."
