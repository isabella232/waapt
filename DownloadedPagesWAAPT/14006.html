    Chiptune DJ

    Route 12

    Valorie was wandering her way through the route with Haxorus by her side, a rather standard approach to any adventure by her rule, when surprise... a pachirisu leapt out at her!

    The squirrel pokemon got about one word into a sentence before Haxorus was upon him.

    <Wrong choice, bub!> the predatory pokemon hissed out. The pachirisu emitted a high pitched scream and ran right back the way it came with Haxorus in hot pursuit.

    Valorie was so used to Haxorus being able to take care of herself that it wasn't until the terrified Pachirisu's voice abruptly stopped that something she had never particularly liked thinking about to begin with occured to her.

    Haxorus was going to eat that pokemon.

    Valorie's stomach dropped and she suddenly felt very ill as the gravity of something she'd previously passed off as not worth thinking about hit her like Mike Tyson. She refused to even look at Haxorus once the armored raptor pokemon returned, returning the pokemon wordlessly and stumbling off to go find a place to sit down and deal with her somewhat overwhelming emotions.

    ...

    Valorie did her best to seclude herself and sat down, burying her head against her arms and knees. She shuddered. How did she even solve a problem like this? She could ask Haxorus not to hunt, but she knew that would strain their relationship rather hard. Worse yet, she was pretty confident taking meat out of Haxorus' diet entirely would be extremely unhealthy.

    She supposed she'd just have to... deal with it, somehow.

    Nature was cruel.

    Later

    Valorie was now working her way off the beaten trail with Dragonite by her side in a much worse mood. A now very depressed part of her noted that Dragonite was the only one of her cardinal team of pokemon she could trust not to hurt anyone anymore.

    How had this happened..? Oh right, Pokemon were suddenly people. That had changed a few things. It had used to be that she could trust her pokemon not to hurt humans, but now with Charizard having betrayed her and her newer pokemon having issues of their own everything earnestly felt like it was going to shit.

    An aching pain in her back and arms told her not to show weakness however. She's just have to... make something happen. If she... pushed hard enough her pokemon would have to listen to her! They'd have to change. She'd make this work.

    ...Somehow.

    She had a long day ahead of her.
    I dropped out of school to become a pokemon master, but then they pulled me right back in to get my doctorate!
