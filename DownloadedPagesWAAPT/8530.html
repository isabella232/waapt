    Swail: <Yes!>

    Swail: <Because! I'm learning to control the weather!>

    -he summons a tiny thundercloud in a Thunder Wave attack-

    <There, you're paralyzed!>

    -he Wraps around her, squeezing weakly-

    Tiamat: >.<

    Tiamat: >.< <no, it's->

    -Swail Wraps her again-

    Tiamat: <I have to stop getting distracted by stupidity... ENOUGH!>

    -she whips up a Twister, throwing Swail off and into the air-

    Tiamat: <That's weather control!>

    Swail: <Ack, so dizzy @_@>

    -he lands in a pile, as Tiamat starts to glow...-

    Carathan: <Very well, that is all for- oh? Congratulations, Tiamat!>

    -Tiamat emerges from a bright light as a Dragonair-

    Tiamat: ... <Yes! It's just like I dreamed!>

    -she swishes back and forth, admiring her new body-

    Carathan: <Well, I think we can consider you graduated from my tutelage now.>

    Tiamat: <YEEEESS! I'm gonna see how well I can swim now!>

    -she darts into the water, and makes a beeline for the passage to the open ocean. Unfortunately for her...-

    Guard!Dragonite: <Stop! The exit restrictions are still a thing, you know.>

    Tiamat: <Ugh... come on, I just evolved, and the war's over!>

    Guard!Dragonite: <It's still a dangerous world out there. Rogue Scolemis goons, battles to fill power vacuums, human construction projects that risk exposing Dens... nothing we'd want to see a neophyte Dragonair risking.>

    Tiamat: >.<

    -a Latias swims closer, having been waiting in the passage for a moment-

    Messenger!Latias: <Cheer up, young one, things will get better before you know it. Even if the current management of the world is... incompetent.>

    Tiamat: <Management..?>

    Messenger!Latias: -chuckles- <Why, the humans! Though you wouldn't know from the way they've been handling things. Trust me, Dragons would have whipped things into shape long before now.>

    <...but perhaps I am saying too much for the moment, opsec's the word and all~>

    Guard!Dragonite: <What brings you here, then?>

    Messenger!Latias: <I have a message for your elders, and the elders alone...>
