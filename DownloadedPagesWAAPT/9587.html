    "Hey Lyuri, did you have any luck?" he asked as he handed her one of the Castelia Cones, trying to keep a positive attitude.

    "Nope, but thanks anyway." she said, taking the ice cream cone and starting to eat it "You're taking all this surprisingly well." she commented, a bit surprised.

    "Don't get me wrong, I'm just as worried as you, we just need to keep our cool while doing this search."

    The two of them then start looking for a bench for them to sit down and they do so when they find one.

    "Let's take it easy, all right? Me and Sakura, we're both going to try our best to find you find them." said Duke, smiling to Lyuri.

    "Heh... I know you two will, one is my sister and... The other is one of the best friends I've had in my life." said Lyuri, not looking directly at Duke as she said so.

    "Well, am I honored by having you think of me like that." Duke answered, rather playfully.

    "Yeah, you should, you know how I am with that kind of thing, you mess up and you go a lot down on my list." she warned, also playfully.

    "Like you would let me forget that, just thinking of you and Jacob reminds me of that." he said, with a chuckle.

    "Yeah, him." she said, now feeling rather annoyed.

    "Anyway, let's enjoy this little break and resume the search, yes?" asked Duke, trying to keep the mood light.

    Lyuri just nodded as she ate some more of her Castelia Cone and the two just stood there for a while, resting from walking all around the place.
