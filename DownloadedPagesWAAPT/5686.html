    Crerah: "...Rawr~"

    The two Nidos get closer to each other for a moment, but then the female steps back and gets elusive; Maekrite knows he'll have to perform quite a gesture if he wants to earn her back.     Maekrite stands back up and offers his berry, a not very rare but still valuable Shuca Berry. He walks up to the Nidorina and sits, while she stares at him for a moment. Then she closes up and with her muzzle grabs the berry; when Maekrite tries to push a little bit closer for a kiss and cuddles, he is met with a Scratch to the face.

    The two Nidos's ears flicker for a moment and Maekrite's body stiffens, he turns to the northeast to check the direction of the call, which he has recognized as a call geared for him (or for Silent).
