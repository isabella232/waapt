    421633 Uilleam1st Oct 2014 12:11:10 PM from Bonnie Scotland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Pining for the fjords
    little silver knives
    This was inspired by two things. One, Spalje, Alex's Venusaur, never appears ever despite being one of the three mons I started out with in those halcyon days, and I've decided that needs to be fixed somehow. Two, Dune's post on improvisation inspired me quite a bit. With that said...

    Alex's Room, A Hotel. Somewhere. Exact times and locations are irrelevant here.

    (The hotel TV is not a very good TV. It's a small, blocky thing, ten years old at the very least, and old age has not been kind to it - it makes a disquieting whirring noise whenever it's turned on, the colours have become so saturated that it occasionally looks like a dwarf is hiding inside the set and crayoning the pictures onto the screen, and the sound is quite tinny and muffled by the aforementioned whirring. Vattenfallet can live with the first two, since she only ever watches telly when the BBC have a particularly intriguing documentary on. On the other hand, the third factor is currently a rather large problem, as the sound of somemon eating messily and noisily is constantly interrupting David Attenborough's dulcet tones.)

    (Finally, the Vaporeon has had enough. She shuffles over to the other side of the (surprisingly clean and comfortable) hotel bed to glare at the Venusaur on the floor.)

    Vattenfallet: <Spalje, could you please->

    (She trails off, her eyes fixated on a Jenga tower of at least ten scrunched-up crisp packets.)

    Spalje: (gulp) <Could I please what?>

    Vattenfallet: <...on second thought, it's not that important. What we do need to sort out are your eating habits, and the detritus they leave scattered around.>

    (Several Beats pass before Spalje's ears prick up again.)

    Spalje: <Wait a mo. Did you basically just call me a fat lazy bastard?>

    (Vattenfallet's face goes through various stages of emotionally pained consideration before she finally sighs and hangs her head, fins drooping slightly.)

    Vattenfallet: <Effectively.>

    Spalje: <Aaaand you plan to do some shiz or other and make me someone who isn't a fat lazy bastard, amirite?>

    Vattenfallet: <That would be the agreeable choice, yes, but if you happen to enjoy your current condition, I won't judge.>

    Spalje: <'Cuz you're too polite, amirite? You'll just let Svavel do the judging for you, amirite?>

    Vattenfallet: <...you appear to have me sussed out perfectly.>

    Spalje: (vineshrug) <I may be fat, lazy and a bastard, but I sure as hell ain't dumb.>

    Vattenfallet: <Now, now. Could you please stop putting words in my mouth? We need to handle this in a civil manner.>

    Spalje: <Whaaaadevver.>

    (He reaches out a chlorophyll-laden appendage to grab another packet of crisps... only to end up swinging a vine at empty air. It seems his voracious appetite has completely emptied the hotel's considerably large food cupboard.)

    Spalje: <...okay, maybe I do have some kinda problem.> o_O

    Vattenfallet: <It's nice to see you accepting this initiative, considering how I have nothing but your best interests at heart.>

    (She feels the urge to glance at David Attenborough's televised image and make a "sshhh" sign in his direction, but resists it.)

    Vattenfallet: <Now, when would be a good time to start?>

    Spalje: <...some other time?>

    Vattenfallet: (facepaw)
    Please don't use the term "life hacks" unless you happen to be a Reality Warper.
