    The Reef

    Foreman!Tentacruel: <Come on, come on! We don't have all day! We need 250 pieces of Kelp by starlight or we won't have enough to feed all the guests at King Nautilus' New Years party!>

    Tempest: <Okay, okay! We're working on it...>

    The Tentacruel nods, then swims away to check on the other workers, allowing Tempest to return to his designated section of Kelp, where a certain Serperior was working hard harvesting the aquatic plant.

    Thyme: <I can't believe they're making us work today of all days. I wanted to spend the night alone with you...>

    Tempest: <I know, it sucks...but look at it this way: it's one less day we have to do community service.>

    The Serperior nods, but lets out a sigh.

    Thyme: <I know...but It's a such a special night. New years eve is always so romantic...>

    Tempest: <Well...when you're not drunk, that is.>

    Thyme gives Tempest a dirty look.

    Tempest: <Not that I was planning on doing that or anything!>

    The Feraligatr looks awkward.

    <Sorry...>

    Thyme: <No, it's fine...We'll have all the time we want to spend together once this is over, anyway.>

    Tempest: <...Yeah...>

    Tempest picks another Kelp plant, now feeling bad that he and Thyme would miss out spending time together later. At that point, he could help but feel bad for getting the two of them caught up Ferno's plan. If it hadn't been for him...

    ...They probably never would have met, let alone fell in love with each other.

    Darn it.

    Tempest sighed, and was about to pick another plant when he felt something approach him from behind in the water. He turned around, expecting to see the Tentacruel Foreman there to tell him to hurry up again...

    ...only to find a baby Lugia in his face.

    Pleo: <Hi!>

    Tempest: <Oh, Pleo!>

    Thyme: <Hey there, Pleo! What brings you here?>

    Pleo: <Oh, well I heard you two were working here today so I just came over to say hello!>

    The young legendary swims up and settles on Tempest's head, craning his neck down to make eye contact with the Delta Feraligatr. <How's it going?>

    Tempest: <Hmm. Okay, I guess. Community service is really boring...>

    Thyme: <Beats jail-time, though. Thanks again for getting us out of that, Pleo.>

    Pleo: <Aw, that's okay! Hey! It won't be that much longer until you guys are done, right?>

    Thyme nods.

    Thyme: <That's right! We should be free sometime in the end of January!>

    Pleo: <Oh, yay! That's great! But...why are you working today? Shouldn't you have New Year's off?>

    Tempest: <Well...we'd like to have today off...>

    Thyme: <But they needed us in to harvest the Kelp for King Nautilus' party tonight.>

    Pleo: <Aww...that's so sad...>

    Pleo hangs his head, a sorry look on his face.

    <Isn't there anyone else that can do it?>

    Tempest: <Well, there is the Tentacruel that runs this farm...but he wants us to do it because we still have community service time left.>

    Pleo: <Oh? But...that's not fair! He can do it, can't he?>

    Thyme: <I don't see any reason why he couldn't...>

    Pleo: <Then why is he making you guys do it?>

    Tempest: <...Er...>

    Tempest and Thyme give each other awkward looks, both of them knowing Pleo well enough to know what was coming next. However, before he could say anything, a certain Tentacruel returned.

    Foreman!Tentacruel: <Come on! I could do better than- OH! My prince! What an honor it is to have you here!>

    The Tentacurel bows reverently to Pleo...only for the young legendary to glare at him.

    <...Eep...Is something wrong, my Prince?>

    Pleo: <Yeah! Why have you got Tempest and Thyme working on New Years eve when you can od your own work?!>

    Foreman!Tentacruel: <Well...you see, my Prince, they're on community service...>

    Pleo: <I know that! Don't you think they deserve today off?!>

    Foreman!Tentacruel: <Er...>

    Pleo: <WELL?!>

    The Tentacruel shrinks back, quivering in fear, partly in fear of provoking Pleo's wrath...and partly at the thought of his father getting involved.

    <...Yes. I do think they deserve the day off.>

    Pleo glares at the jellyfish for a few more seconds, then calms down, giving him a smile.

    Pleo: <Okay then! You can harvest all of this Kelp by yourself, right?>

    Foreman!Tentacruel: <Yes, of course I can! I'll get right on it!>

    Pleo: <Good! Because you wouldn't want to get King Nautilus mad...>

    The Lugia gives him a sly look.

    <...Or my Dad...>

    The Tentacuel quivers, then shrieks in terror and swims away quickly, rushing off to the nearest patch of kelp and beginning to hastily harvest the plants with his tentacles. Satisfied, Pleo turns his attention back to the tow Delta Pokémon, who look slightly shocked at what's just happened.

    Pleo: <There you go! That mean old Tentacruel won't bother you anymore today! Now you two can go and have fun!>

    Thyme stares at Pleo for a second, then gives him a big smile.

    Thyme: <Thank you so much, Pleo.>

    Pleo: <Aw, it was the least I could-WHOA!>

    The legendary is interrupted by Tempest suddenly giving him a big hug.

    Tempest: <Thanks, little guy! You're the best!>

    Pleo smiles awkwardly.

    Pleo: <That's okay, Tempest...It was the least I could do after you two helped everyone safe me from Ferno.>

    Thyme: <Speaking of which...whatever happened to him?>

    Meanwhile, in a cave in Holon...

    The Delta Charizard in question is trying to sneak out of his cave without his mother noticing. After a daring escape from his chamber, he's managed to sneak through the house undetected, and is now right near the entrance.

    All he had to do was make it down a short tunnel, and he would be home free. Literally, in this case!

    He leans down, flapping his wings and preparing to take off...

    ...when he is suddenly paralyzed by a Thunderwave attack.

    Ferno: <Huh? What the-?!>

    ???: <Going somewhere?>

    Hearing a familiar voice, the Charizard slowly turns his head to see his mother standing there, her body crackling with electricity.

    Ferno: <...Hi Mom...didn't see you there...>

    The Charizard smiles awkwardly, trying to look innocent, only for his mother to grab him by the tail and begin dragging him back to his chamber.

    Ferno's Mother: <Well, I guess that's another two months you'll be spending in your room then.>

    Upon hearing this, Ferno sighs.

    Ferno: <Darn it, Tempest and Thyme. You're gonna' pay...if I ever get out of here...>
