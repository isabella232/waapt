    Not Ruins of Alph - PMD-B

    Having his mind combed through for memories was an...interesting feeling. If Lake were to describe it, it would be like having another presence inside his mind, as if there were two Pokémon sharing his body.

    As the Espeon searched, he felt one of his more recent memories being pulled up. Although "recent" in this case, was about a year ago. In it, he was describing to the group on how he wouldn't kill another Pokémon or person unless he or she really deserved it, out of fear of getting Luke sent to prison. Or worse.

    Lake: <Well, where I come from...it's not considered a good thing to kill in anything but self defense.>

    Then, perhaps as a part of the mind link, he feels the Espeon become confused as he reads his experiences of the human race. Unsure of what to say (think?) about them to him, he keeps quiet for the time being.
