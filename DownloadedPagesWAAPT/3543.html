    Skarmory (slids his wing against Maekrite's face) — <Now, you better had nothing to do with it.>

    -Maekrite rolls his eyes-

    Maekrite — <Technically, he left because he has work to do elsewhere.>

    -The Heracross glares, inquiring what does Maekrite mean with that, but as he's about to answer, they all hear some sounds coming up the stairs.-

    -However at the last moment Maekrite turns his head and unleashes the Sludge Bomb right in the face of the now unprotected Skarmory-

    Skarmory — <AAAHHH!! My eyes...! Can't see...! Urgh...!> — flails around.

    -Before Weavile can jump in, Maekrite swipes the Skarmory's wing and Double Kicks the Heracross's horn, forcing it down enough that Maekrite can touch the ground and gouge at the Heracross, leaping on his back to reach the Weavile, who is already leaping up to attack with an Ice Beam-

    Weavile — <Oh not aga~> — is hit by a Shadow Claw on the belly.

    -Maekrite lands on the ground as the Weavile spins in midair, balance lost, and crashes against the Skarmory; however the Weavile and the Heracross recover quickly-

    -Weavile casts a Swords Dance as the Heracross dashes to the side to use a Stone Edge attack, but suddenly a faint blue glow envelopes Weavile-

    Weavile — <...What the...!>

    -Maekrite glows too and absorbs the light from Weavile, Snatching the Swords Dance bonus; then he dashes ahead in time to avoid some of the Stone Edge attacks, tanking the others, and rams Weavile sending him through a wall, already unconscious-

    -A couple of moves later and after managing to counter a Pursuit, a Brick Break and a Reversal that left him bleeding from the flank, Maekrite manages to slide his horn through one of the Heracross's legs, slowing him down enough to stick him to the wall with another Sludge Bomb.-
