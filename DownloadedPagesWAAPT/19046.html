    476149 PippingFool17th Nov 2015 05:45:30 PM from A BEAUTIFUL DUWANG Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I get a feeling so complicated...
    A Bad Egg

    Neo-Flare HQ

    In one of the more spacious areas of the facility, a line of new recruits stands in firm regimental order, a chose Pokemon standing beside them. As they stand motionless, a bespectacled fellow in a black suit paces down and observes them, a Hypno following and observing their Pokemon.

    Marcus: Everyone is here, I trust?

    Marcus stops and turns around to face them.

    Marcus: For those who don't know, I am Marcus C. Rothschild. I'm your instructor. I'm here to mold you, to whip you into shape. To evaluate if you really have the mettle to be a member of this organization.

    On whip, he flicks the ruler in his left hand to tap the frame of his glasses and adjusts them.

    Marcus: I will be with you for the entire duration of your training as new recruits. In fact, you are the first batch since we change the managers of the hiring division to Mister Liú.

    There is some whispering among the recruits at the mention of " Liú"

    New Recruit #1: Sir, who was the manager before Liú?

    Marcus adjusts his glasses again, his Hypno mimics the action.

    Marcus: My division was, and we were quite picky with who was and wasn't allowed in the organization. Even those who payed the 1,000,000 P to bypass that initial inspection were rejected if they proved to not have what it takes in the eyes of management.

    He looks down the list of names on the clipboard.

    Marcus: Should you find something unclear, you can come to me for questions. My shift ends at 6 PM so I will answer any questions answered before 5:30. Now to be-

    Marcus's speech is broken by the sound of a woman humming a tune similar to "There's a Hole in my Bucket", to which Marcus's eyebrow twitches in annoyance as he looks over to the woman in question. Who is currently filing her nails while her Jynx cuts out names on her trainers clipboard with a very sharp icicle.

    Marcus: -Sigh- Aren't you also supposed to be an instructor, Maria? After all you are the Admin in charge of this division.

    Maria flashes her nails, proud with now sharp she made them.

    Maria: Oh honey, you know I'm not into children. They just aren't my thing.

    She zips over to Marcus, slinging her arms around his shoulder and her right leg around his.

    Maria: But you, Lieber Märkie, I could nibble on any day of the week~

    She goes in to kiss his cheek, but Marcus stops this by jabbing Maria's cheek with the ruler and pushing her head away.

    The new recruits are horribly confused.

    Marcus: Yes, unfortunately, this is going to be your new boss. Unless your abilities are more suited to a different department or you wish to transfer. If it wasn't the truth or could get me killed, I would say it's because the highest up has collectively lost his mind.

    Maria: -Slightly muffled due to the ruler- But Märkie, you just said it~

    Marcus: Moving on.

    Marcus begins walking away, letting Maria flop to the ground.

    Maria: Oof! So cold~

    Marcus: Before we begin with a tour of the facilities, I would like to assess your combat skills. Both with and without your Pokemon-

    Marcus is interrupted again by one of the new recruits breaking form to burst out into laughter.

    Marcus: That is one strike against your name, Liú.

    New Recruit #4: You!? Assess combat!? I'm sorry Suit, but that's gotta be the funniest joke I've heard all day! Hah! A Namby Pamby office boy like you wouldn't know a shred about combat! At least Miss Vanity over here seems to know about weapons lookin' at her belt and the Halberd on her back.

    Maria: Hey!

    The Leavanny next to him nods with a maniacal smile on her face. Marcus turns around.

    Marcus: ...It seems that you are offering a challenge, to your superior nonetheless. Very well then.

    Marcus looks over the rest of the recruits.

    Marcus: Your classmate here has offered himself up to be a demonstration. You should be thankful to get a front row preview of what is to be expected of you as members of Neo-Flare. We will be heading to the Combat Training Facility, so properly follow me.

    Maria: ''Oh this will be interesting.

    Neo Flare HQ, Combat Training Facility.

    On the proper training floor stands Marcus with his Hypno and the Recruit with his Leavanny. The Maria and the rest of the newbies stand on the sidelines, out of harms way.

    Marcus: This battle will be twofold, first starting with your skills as a Pokemon Trainer and second as a combatant in your own right. We'll start with the Pokemon battle as I'll want you in peak physical condition for our own spar.

    The recruit makes a Tch sound as he spits to the side.

    Marcus: Strike Two, Liú. Phineas? Take your place.

    New Recruit #4: Well this is going to be easy. You're up Leavanny.

    Marcus: Since Phineas is Psychic, I will not need to relay commands to him. If any of you wonder why I don't shout commands at him.

    New Recruit #4: Well ain't you fancy, Suit.

    Phineas: ...

    Marcus looks over to Maria

    Marcus: Maria, you may call the start.

    Maria: Oh you bestow the honors onto me? How flattering~!

    Anyway. Gentleman~ You may begin your battle!

    New Recruit #4: Leavanny! Start with-

    Before the recruit could even shout his first command, Phineas's eyes glow purple as he uses his telekinesis to pick up his opponent, and begins slamming her against the walls of the facility with the flick of his wrist. Hard.

    New Recruit #4: W-Wait! Th-that's cheating! You-

    Marcus: And here, recruits, we have our first rookie mistake. All the rules and regulations regarding trainers and how to approach Pokemon battles? Ignore them. Discard them. They are nothing but hindrances in this line of work. You have no time to "take turns" and let the opponent damage your Pokemon.

    Marcus watches the Leavanny be tossed around like a ragdoll.

    Marcus: Otherwise you have given your opponent an opening, an you'll be played for the fool.

    Phineas finishes his slamdown with one last smash drop on the floor. The Leavanny; batter, bruised and broken, struggles to get up.

    On the sideline, Maria and her Jynx Qandisa look over to Marcus and Phineas with hands clasped to their cheek and hearts in their eyes.

    New Recruit #4: L-Leavanny!

    The recruit goes over to the Leavanny and observes her festering wounds. While Marcus walks over to his Hypno's side.

    Marcus: Now.

    The recruit looks up at Marcus with a sneer of pure hatred.

    Marcus: For hand-to-hand combat.
