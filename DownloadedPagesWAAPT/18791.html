    Sholto: <No, seriously, Shun's not going to like it. I can't see him trusting me if I'm lying about what you're doing and he finds out.>

    Sundancer: <I can't say I'm too fond of this.>

    Sholto: <Hey, I mean, the treasure hunting thing. There's nothing wrong with that if you're not stealing. That was fun, wasn't it?>

    Sundancer: <...Fine, I'll stick with the innocent stuff.>

    Sundancer: <Mm.>
