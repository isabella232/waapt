    Anistar Pokémon Center

    Sakura and Hikari are now in the latter's room, sitting on the bed.

    "So, here's the plan. You wear a Greninja-themed Super Sentai suit, get the GreNinja Squad join you and then become a heroine and fight crime with them!" said Hikari

    Sakura blinks "... Are you serious?"

    "Hey, come on! That would be really cool! I can even provide you with some wonderful toys!" said Hikari, looking really pumped.

    Sakura sighs "Hikari, I'm a normal girl. I'm not fit to be a heroine. Besides everyone in the J-Team would know it's me under the suit because at least some of them know Teishi's the leader and he's my Pokémon."

    Hikari stops to think for a moment "... I know! I could have you use some kind of accessory that can change your voice and make you sound like a man! Everybody would be fooled, even the Aurics!"

    "... So you want me to pull a Samus on everyone?"

    "Yes! That would be so fun!"

    Sakura winces "Except for the part where I can get killed by criminals."

    "Hey, I'll make sure to give you help and the Squad is going to make sure you stay alive. Besides, ninjas are about stealth, not fighting! You just need to be careful and you won't even get seen, much less killed."

    "And where will I get the training to do that?"

    "Train with them, duh! I bet they can help you with some stealth techniques!" Hikari then stops to contain the excitement "Come on Sakura, if we work together, this way we can make a difference! We'll be able to help deal with the problems that arise from time to time and with some effort on our part, we're going to be a great team! At least give it a try and see if you can handle it."

    Sakura sighs and tries thinking it over for a moment

    "... Acting like a vigilante can cause me some trouble with the law, right?" she asked

    "Well, you're a Pokémon Ranger, it's your job to enforce the law no? Then I guess you'd be fine."

    "I don't think it works that way..." Sakura then sighs again "... So, the plan is to have me pretend to be a man, have Teishi's group train me and assist me at being a heroine, dressed in a Greninja suit, keeping my identity hidden from everyone and fight crime? All the while you'll be guiding me by communicating with me from afar?"

    Hikari nods "You don't really need to keep your identity hidden though..."

    Sakura narrows her eyes a bit "Oh, but I will. This idea is just embarrassing."

    "Well... Are you up for it or not?" asked Hikari

    "... I can give training a shot, maybe see if I'm fit for this kind of work or not. If I'm going to become an actual heroine, that we'll see later."

    Hikari fist pumps "Yes! My childhood dreams are becoming reality!"

    Sakura just plain sweatdrops.
