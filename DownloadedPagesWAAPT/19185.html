    -Rustle, rustle, rustle.-

    Gabite 2: <...Did you hear that?>

    Gabite 1: <Oh, come on, are you trying to scare me into leaving now?>

    -Rustle, rustle, rustle.-

    Gabite 2: <No, seriously, there's something else here.>

    Gabite 1: <Look, it's probably nothing.>

    Charizard: -from another room- <What's going on in there?>

    Gabite 2: <Uh, nothing!>

    -Rustle, rustle, rustle...-

    Gabite 2: <Under that box!>

    Charizard: <What are you whining about!?>

    Gabite 1: <...A stupid little Skorupi, apparently. I told you there was nothing to worry about.>

    -Sure enough, the Gabite pulls out a Skorupi, which hisses at them. The Charizard glares at the second Gabite, unamused.-

    Gabite 2: <I... I...>

    Gabite 1: <Ridley! Sir!>

    Charizard Ridley: <Gah...>

    Gabite 1: <What's going on, sir?!>
