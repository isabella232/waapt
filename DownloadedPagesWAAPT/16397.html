    ...was that knock magic?

    Rattata: No. Just looks imposing.

    -the two walk on, and are greeted by more trees. Their huge branches make it impossible to tell if the glass roof still remains. Joseph observes this before his attention is immediately caught by something else-

    Joe and Munn's Room

    Munn: "A cute mon?"

    "Hush, you."

    Munn: o3o

    Forest

    -on a nearby branch hangs a bright scarlet coat with an Inverness cape. There is a similarly red hood on it. Most importantly, it's clean. Joseph dashes over to it-

    Rattata: Just what do you think you're doing?

    Joseph: My shirt is a mess. Pity, I liked it, too. This'll cover it up, and I won't feel all wet with it on.

    Rattata: No, but do you know what you're putting on?
