    398738 Crewe20th Jul 2014 09:46:35 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
    Li'l Punk

    Rasp Cavern

    Yeah. Me neither.

    ... Damn I wish I still had ice cream right now.

    -she coughs-

    Anyway. Seventeen years ago, a runty Sneasel was born in northern Sinnoh. Her parents were dead, her siblings were nowhere to be found, and her vision was so bad she was nearly blind. Now normally, a Sneasel like that would just die. It happens all the time. But that day, someone happened to stumble upon the Sneasel wandering the wilderness before she died. This someone happened to be a Mew.

    Mew, being... who she is, had the bright idea that to save the Sneasel's life... she would turn her into a human.

    -she takes a deep breath-

    So that's what she did. She turned the Sneasel into a human baby and left her on the doorstep of a couple who wanted a kid but couldn't get one. She made the baby look like the parents, and nobody ever suspected anything — least of all the kid herself. So she grew up. I'll spare you the details of her childhood. Suffice to say, eventually she ran away from home to become a Pokemon Trainer. After some years, she fell in with a group of Trainers. She ended up getting into some crazy adventures with them, and these adventures eventually brought them to Hoenn, to stop Team Magma and Team Aqua from... well, doing what they do.

    While they were in Hoenn, they... ran into the Mew again. She was "helping" some Goldeen and Seaking in Meteor Falls after the water had been drained out by transforming them into Zubat. The Trainers—they were called the J-Team at this point—took issue with this. They got angry with the Mew... so she defended herself. By pointing out the girl and telling them all her story.

    -she stops for a long moment-

    ... So yeah. As you can probably tell—you don't look stupid—that girl was me. What happened after Mew told me the truth... I don't like to talk about it. But it left its scars. I hurt a lot of people and Pokemon that I cared about. I don't know if I can ever make it up to them.

    -she pulls her Moon Stone pendant out from under her scarf-

    This thing here lets me transform between Sneasel and human forms. I... made a deal with Mew, and eventually ended up with it. I'm pretty dependent on it, too. This Cipher scientist stole it, once, and... that didn't end well.

    -she clears her throat-

    Anyways... there you have it. Mew and I had a few run-ins afterwards—she revived my boyfriend once after I, um... well, I killed him. It's a long story. But I've always had this... phobia of Legendary Pokemon. And she really doesn't help.

    -she looks over at him-

    By the way, everything I just told you? Confidential. I don't keep secrets from my pack, but nobody outside the J-Team is supposed to know. We have enemies, after all this time, and I don't want any of those enemies getting to my family.

    -she briefly shifts to Sneasel form and back again-

    So that's me. Freaky Sneasel girl.
