    PI MODE

    Pi's Mindscape (The Desert)

    Here, at the heart of Pi's mind, Baron Samedi, Pi, and Madness meet.

    "There you are, fiend."

    Madness: "Who, moi? I must say I am surprised to see you in here."

    Baron Samedi: "We've come to put an end to the chaos you've sown."

    Madness: "Aw, but I was having fun!"

    "Shut up."

    Pi draws the memory-sword.

    "Come. Meet your fate."

    Madness: "What can you know about fate? Pup, weakling, mere glimmer of brief awareness? WHAT CAN YOU KNOW OF ME?"

    "I know that you are a sick freak, limited to piggy backing on other's minds."

    He charges the feline, swinging hard and true.

    Surprised, Madness takes a hit.

    Madness: "Ho ho. That was a good one."

    Baron Samedi leaps at him from the other side, only to miss completely.

    Madness: "Ah ah ah. This is the kid's fight, boyo."

    They rage across the sands, claws and sword clashing and crashing in showers of sparks.

    Attack, attack, defend.

    Block, counter, and repeat.

    Forwards and backwards.

    Shuffle to the side.

    Dodge forwards and roll.

    Jump up, swing.

    Take a hit.

    Retaliate.

    Score a blow.

    Strike, strike, and strike again.

    Success.

    At last, a Pi scores a mortal injury on Madness.

    Madness: "Tsk. A set back."

    "No. An end."

    Dashing forward while dropping his sword, Pi grabs Madness.

    Struggling with the feline, he fits the memory-harness onto him, then let's go and jumps away.

    Madness: "What is this accursed thing?"

    Baron Samedi: "A yoke. Your power is to be harnessed. Used."

    Madness: "..."

    Madness: "   DAMN YOU.   "

    "Oh believe me. I already am."

    "I already am."

    With a rushing, swishing, moving noise, Pi leaves his Mindscape.

    Calor Desert, Mountain Side

    Pi opens his eyes, having crossed the desert.

    <Shell Sage: "So, boy? You cured?">

    <"...">

    <"Aye. Thank god. I am.">

    Pi collapses from sheer relief.

    <"I'm free.">
