    Darn it, why didn't he bring Izzy with him?

    But Luke had no time to ponder such a question as Link had just grabbed a Smash Ball! he felt a tugging on his coat, turning his head to find that a pair of talons had grabbed hold.

    Luke: Wha-?!

    While Unite had been busy with one fake Skarmory, the other one was now trying to carry him off to who knows where!

    HEY! Let go off me, you overgrown blob!

    He tried to struggle free, but the bird's grip was far too tight.

    Unite, I hate to be that guy, but HELP!

    Raising a Protect shield to block yet another blow, the Braviary glanced over his shoulder at his trainer.

    Unite: <Okay, okay! Hang on!>

    Dropping the shield, he spun around and flew up at the other Skarmory, trying to think if he had any moves in his moveset that could do some decent damage.

    <I've got to have something that could do something! Think! THINK!>

    He then suddenly recalled back in Oblivia, where Luke had used a TM to teach him Shadow Claw. A move he couldn't recall using that much. He knew that Ghost type moves weren't very effective against Steel-type Pokémon, but it anything was worth a try right now.

    <Here goes nothing!>

    A shadowy mass grew around Unite's left talon as he flew up to the Skarmory's height. The gel bird reared back to peck the Braviary away, but Unite was faster.

    Unite used Shadow Claw!

    The dark mass on his talon extended into a long shadowy set of claws as he slashed at the Skarmory's talons. To his surprise, instead of clanging against them for little damage, they sliced clean through, leaving the false bird without a leg to stand on.

    Literally.

    Luke: WAAAAAH!

    THUMP!

    Luckily, the Skarmory hadn't carried him very high, so Luke wasn't hurt from the drop. With another Shadow Claw, Unite sliced the false Skarmory's head off, the bird falling to the ground and collapsing into a pile of jelly.

    Unite: <Huh. I didn't expect that to work!>

    Pulling himself up off the floor, Luke gazed up at his Braviary, just as perplexed.

    Luke: I thought Steel was resistant to Ghost-type moves?

    Unite shrugged as he turned to face the remaining false Pokémon.

    Unite: <Apparently not, but at least I can fight 'em now!>
