    141418 Crewe1st Mar 2012 02:06:04 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
    Li'l Punk
    Ashley, Cole, and Domino are wandering around the tall grass outside Twinleaf Town, Cole intimidating the small Pokemon out of attacking, when they're distracted by loud, distressed noises. No, really, they're loud. Seriously, I can hear them from wherever it is I narrate from. Gah... go on, Ashley! Go shut it up! Please!

    Ashley hurries towards the sound (as opposed to most of the Pokemon, who are hurrying away from it), to find a Noctowl, bustling around and hooting very loudly. Ashley nudges Cole and gestures towards him with an elbow, covering her ears with her hands.

    "Go find out what's wrong, Cole," she says.

    Reluctantly, Cole hops towards the Noctowl, with Domino trailing behind.

    <Hey! Hey, buddy!> he calls, his shoulders hunched up a little. <Calm down! What's the matter?>

    <Late! Late, late, late, late! Late! Late! Late!> the Noctowl flaps his wings a few times in distress, hooting even louder.

    <Whoa! Take it easy! What are you late for?> Cole draws back a little, bumping into Domino, who shies off to the side.

    <The night! I'm late for the night! Noctowl should be awake for the night!>

    <You're not late for the night,> Cole says, looking confused.

    <Actually, you're pretty early,> Domino adds, looking up at the sun.

    <False statement!> the Noctowl abruptly stops hooting and runs up to Cole, getting in his face. <I slept all through the night, and now I'm late!>

    <Gah! Get out of my face! Um, please!> Cole ducks away from him, his feathers ruffled. <Okay, so you slept in. What's so horrible about that?>

    <I. Am. A. Noctowl. I do not sleep in. I'm late!>

    <Yeah, yeah, we know you're late. Just calm down. Can't you just sleep all day and get up again tonight? Just knock off that Arceusawful noise.> Cole quickly uses his beak to fix his ruffled feathers.

    <No! Once you wake up, you're awake. Once the sun's up, it's bright. I can't sleep now! I'll have to be a Noctowl awake during the day! When the sun is out!>

    <Okay...> Cole takes a few steps back, then turns around, speaking over his shoulder. <Well, since you've stopped screeching, we'll just go. Good luck with your whole "late" thing. Come on, Domino.>

    <But, Cole, shouldn't we help him or something?> Domino looks at the Noctowl with concern, but Cole buffets him with his wing and pushes him along towards Ashley.

    <Why? He's not bothering us anymore. C'mon, Ashley doesn't want to stick around any longer.> Cole nudges Domino along, then starts hopping after him, but is stopped by the Noctowl suddenly appearing in his face. <GAH!>

    <You're leaving? I'll come with you!> The Noctowl, evidently perfectly happy now, hops up and down a bit. <Where are we going?>

    Cole dodges around him. <We're going back to town. You're going your own way.>

    <Oh, okay! So I should go around and enter town a different way? But how will I know where to meet you?> He tilts his head, eyes wide and bright.

    Cole facewings. <Ugh.>

    So I guess Cole decided that if he ignored him, the Noctowl would go away, 'cause he just keep heading back towards Ashley. Apparently no one told the Noctowl that this was supposed to happen, though, so he follows him. Oh no, I wasn't supposed to tell him, was I? Is that part of the job? No one mentioned this at school, either!

    "Oh, look, he wants to come! Did you convince him to join the team, Cole?" Ashley asks, smiling and patting the Pidgeot. Cole "hmphs" and starts heading back to town. Chuckling, Ashley follows, with the Noctowl and Domino in tow.
    Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
