    "Waah! Rex-chan!" Usa-chan made his way over to his friend, his arms held out, over-extended sleeve falling over his fingers. "I missed you!" He pulled her into a hug.

    "I was gone for like, ten minutes Usa-chan." Though her words were ignored as he continued to rub his cheek agianst her own. "I brought the Hydregion...." With that Usa-chan dropped her to the ground.

    "Wai!" He gave a cheer and looked over to Jasmine. "Waah, ohayou Jasmine-chi, I think kumichou is in the back room with the others." Jasmine grunted and shook out her leaves.

    Zap slipped off her back and hopped over to Rex's side.

    Jasmine: (Don't'cha screw up. Everyone will be watching. Don't treat 'Ebony' lightly.)

    "Wah, speaking of Ebony," Usa began. "He and Gan-chan are about ready to begin the match, are you sure you want to enter the Hydregion? Ebony's so far been undefeated."

    Rex smirked and held out her thumb in acceptance. Zap mimicked the action.

    "No problem, ya? Ready Chary? If you lose Zap'll clean your wounds!" She gave a chuckle and headed toward the main ring. Zap rubbed teh back of his head. He wished they really not goad the poor girl, she must miss her master. The Raichu dropped to all fours and bounded off after his master.

    The ring was a massive platform with no rope around it. Many of these 'eared' individuals stood in their uniforms with their chosen pokemon at their side. Standing on the ring was a large man wearing heavy, steel pauldrons and shot, blue hair. He wore a loose, grey shirt and black tights. His masculinity was further bolstered by the heavy, knee high boots of grey and black.

    "It's about time!" The deep voice called as the shadow stepped forth to reveal the infamous Ganseki. Behind sat, coiled, a massive black Steelix. "You finally showed up, thinkin' you had drowned overseas!" The man laughed.

    Rex glanced off to the side. "Your tummy's showing..." Rex offered with a sly grin.

    Gan-chan fell for the trick and blushed, moving quickly to pull down his shirt. Rex pulled her sleeved hand over her mouth, trying to hide that Cheshire grin.

    "Damn you! Enough! Let's just start the matches already!" Gan-chan demanded and turned to the crowd. "Alright, put your bets up! Time to get started!" The crowds cheered.

    Among the crowds were not only these uniformly dressed individuals, but some civilians, and the shady looking sort....
