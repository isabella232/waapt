    <We have convinced the Emboar to use the power that sleeps behind the Hundred Thousand Year Door to rebuild the Alakagross Society.>

    <In addition, we have also learned that the seal keeping the door shut this whole time is weakening. Regardless of whether we gather the Stars in that time, we will be going through the door and claiming the power that lies within it for ourselves.>

    <You are also to prevent Shirley, Thwap, Goodfellow, and anyone associated with them from getting the other half of the Crystal Stars.>

    <Though, I must make something clear: As of now, they are to be dealt with without serious harm. If any of them are to be harmed, it will be by the Emboar's hand.>

    <Is that understood?>

    <Then get back to work!>

    -He waves the staff and the transmission ends-

    Grodus: <Was that sufficient?>

    Squealer: <...Yeah, thanks.>

    Squealer: <No, uh, Sir Grodus.>

    Hopefully TEC isn't too put off by the sudden turnaround.
