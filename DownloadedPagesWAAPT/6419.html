    -Creatures that do not live in the sea always have the most peculiar thoughts of our realm... That it is a navigation obstable, a feeding ground, a barbaric waste of ferals seeking nourishment from their drowned corpses...

    But almost never is it seen as an expanse with mons that could possibly be alike them... And when it is, it's usually by alike groups of mons such as that realm to the far east where our god spent his youth-

    <???: "I see that the gardeners haven't lost their touch...">

    -We do not interact much with the land dwellers. We do not wish for much... To be able to protect our own from the ferals that dwell the shores near our eastern frontier and that lurk the waters beyond our others. To be able to keep the advances of those impudent western expansionists at bay. To maintain our few trading posts on the surface in peace and have their borders respected... To be able to live out our lives in peace as land-dwelling 'civilized' mons do-

    -A Qwilfish hastily swims after the Milotic-

    <Messenger!Qwilfish: "Queen Oto! Queen Oto!">

    <Oto: "Hmm? Is something the matter?">

    <Messenger!Qwilfish: "Uh... The court said that there was a matter to tend to... Something about the southwestern outpost?">

    -I cannot fault the land-dwellers for what happened to my husband, the gods bless his soul... Even if some of my ministers have urged me to exact vengeance upon those that slew him in the southwest... To reduce the 'Slateport' that claimed ownership of the deed to a swamp devoid of surface life... His mind had departed him in his last days, and he had become someone other than himself... I cannot fault mons for seeking to defend themselves... And there is no point in exacting a further toll beyond that what fate dealt to those that slew him. Our realm lost a beloved leader... And the one that opposed us did as well... If not by our efforts-

    -Frowning-

    <Oto: "Mmph. Does any good news ever come out of that accursed swamp? What happened this time?">

    <Messenger!Qwilfish: "Uh... There were some minor spats between our realm's forces stationed there and those of our rivals...">

    <Oto: "So the usual business, then...? Tell them that I'll be there in a moment.">

    <Messenger!Qwilfish: "Yes, my queen!">

    -And swims off-

    -It still troubles me... That in the end, Wada will be remembered outside of this realm as a monster... A sinker of ships... As opposed to the wise and just ruler that my subjects knew him to be... Fierce in appearance and in temperament in battle... But kind and down to earth... A protector of even the feeblest mons who sought our realm's safety-

    -Drawing near to a secluded patch tended to by a Lanturn-

    <Guardian!Lanturn: "Oh... You've come to see it?">

    <Oto: "I have... But are there not more of your number?">

    <Guardian!Lanturn: "We cannot all be visible, my queen... Sometimes the best guardian is the one that lurks unseen...">

    <Oto: "A fair counterpoint...">

    -And swims on, entering a relatively small cave-
