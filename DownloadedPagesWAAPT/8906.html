    UTINNI!

    Somewhere North of Kanto/Johto

    Jack: "Runt, I need to borrow your goggles."

    Runt: (TP) Why's that?

    Jack: "I'm going swimming. I saw a statue at the bottom of the lake I want to take a look at. It would be much easier if I had something for my eyes."

    Runt relented and took off his goggles, then tossed them in Jack's direction.

    Jack: "Thanks, buddy."

    Runt: (TP) Bring 'em back in one piece.

    Jack: "Got it."

    Jack couldn't help but think of the irony of having just recently putting out his clothes to dry and then going right back into the water, but at least he had changed back into his already wet pants before doing so. No sense getting another pair wet.

    As he went back out into the water, Runt, Groot, and Penny watched. At least, Runt and Groot watched. Penny just kind of sat there. She had been surprisingly calm since the battle with the Gligar, so Jack decided to take a chance and let her remain out of her pokeball for the time being.

    As Jack tried to take a look at the half-buried statue, his three pokemon were joined by a trio of Mudkip who showed up from nearby.

    Moe (Who is a Mudkip): <Who's that guy supposed to be?>

    Larry (Who also is a Mudkip): <What's that guy supposed to be?>

    Curly (He too who is Mudkip): <I think it's a human.>

    Larry: <How would you know? You ever seen a human?>

    Curly: <He ain't a pokemon, I know that much.>

    Moe: <Quiet, ya knuckleheads.>

    Larry: <Who you callin' knucklehead, knucklehead?>

    Moe: <You, you knucklehead.>

    The Mudkip then curled his forearm into a knuckle and bonked Larry on the head.

    Runt and Groot looked at the three Mudkip as they argued amongst themselves.

    Moe: <Say, you're not from around here, are you?>

    Runt: <No, we're from out of town.>

    Curly: <Town? What town? I don't see no town.>

    Larry: <I think it's a figure of speech.>

    Runt: <That would be accurate.>

    Curly pointed in Jack's direction.

    Curly: <So he a human?>

    Runt: <That also would be accurate. His name is Jack. He is our trainer.>

    Larry: <Trainer? What he training you to do?>

    Runt simply rolled his eyes. Not that anyone could see it beneath his eyelids.

    Moe: <What's your trainer doing out there?>

    Runt: <He's looking at a statue at the bottom of the lake.>

    Curly: <A statue?>

    Larry: <Yeah, Curly. You know, that ancient cursed statue at the bottom of the lake?>

    Runt's ears perked up at the word 'cursed', and even Groot began paying more attention.

    Runt: (TP) <What do you mean 'cursed'?>

    Moe: <He means 'cursed'. As in, there's a pokemon in the lake that attack people who start looking at the statue too closely. Everyone knows that.>

    Larry: <He didn't know that.>

    Moe: <He knows it now!>

    Curly: <But he didn't!>

    Moe: <Why I oughta...>

    A minor, comical brawl broke out between the three Mudkip, but Runt was suddenly far more concerned about what kind of danger Jack might have gotten himself into.

    Dunt Dunt Duuuuuuun!
