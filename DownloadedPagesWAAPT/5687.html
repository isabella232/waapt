    Cool Blue Magic

    Sunnyshore Gym, past

    Volkner's Charizard bristled slightly at Salamence's comment but ignored her in favor of firing another Dragon Pulse at Spathi, this one striking closer to him, but still not hitting directly.

    <Rrrr~> Spathi growled appreciatively to Salamence, not taking his eyes off of his opponent. Soaring towards his opponent, he opened his maw wide for another Hydro Pump, whipping his head around wildly in an attempt to hit. The Charizard hissed in anger as he was slightly doused, taking some damage, but not enough to put him fully out of commission. However, the Salamence had put himself in a bad position with his attack, something he intended to capitalize on.

    Diving below his opponent, Charizard let out a Dragon Pulse at the Salamence's belly, Spathi roaring in agony as he was stuck towards the ceiling. He unclenched his eyes just in time to notice four pointed rocks materialize above him, slamming him down to the ground with a loud crash.

    <It's over,> Charizard snorted, crossing his arms.

    However, when the dust cleared, Spathi was still hanging on, wincing in pain as he shook rock fragments off of his body. The dragon sighed, his Roost having kicked in just in time, though he was clearly in no condition to be doing much more flying. <Like I'd lose to you...> he growled.

    Charizard leered, his claws burning with blue fire as he swooped in on the Salamence, preparing to end the match once and for all. Spathi snarled in response, sucking in a deep breath for what was undoubtedly another Hydro Pump. Naturally, due to possessing the aerial advantage, the flame dragon struck first, a roar of pain emerging from Spathi's maw. However, this hardly kept him from fighting. Instead, he lowered his jaw onto the Charizard's exposed arm, causing it to yell out in pain.

    That pain only intensified once the electricity began to flow, the Thunder Fang draining that last of his health.

    Both combatants growled weakly...and slumped to the floor, both fainted from their battle.

    "Taken down so quickly..." Volkner mused in surprise. "Impressive."

    "Finish him with Fire Blast!"
