    -Gui lies down on her back on the couch. Kraus looks down.-

    Kraus: Have you remembered anything more about the incident surrounding that underground Cipher lab?

    -Zeal shakes his head.-

    Zeal: The red-eyed aspect was in total control then. I can't remember a thing about anything other than the aftermath, and doubt I'll ever be able to.

    Kraus: Of course. I'm sorry to have asked.

    Zeal: It's fine, don't—

    Kraus: No, that was my fault. It was a needless question.

    Gui: ~So he's like this a lot, huh?~

    Zeal: Yeah... He tends to blame himself for anything that happens to the people around him, big or small. It makes me feel bad for him.

    Kraus: Actually... if I may confess, I'm glad you can't remember. It gives me a much better feeling than any bad news would.

    Zeal: Oh? This is new. What have you learned?

    Kraus: Well... it's not really...

    -He smiles slightly.-

    Kraus: There's a chance Leander may have survived. When they were identifying the bodies, his wasn't found.

    -His smile fades.-

    Kraus: Of course, some of the bodies have gone unidentified. But even then, I supplied the forensic scientists with every one of Leander's records in my possession. If one of the corpses was his, they probably would have found something to show it. But I can't be as hopeful about this as I'd like to be. Even if he somehow lived, he was caught in the middle of an explosion. I doubt he'd have gotten far with those burns, and there's no records of him turning up in any Orren hospital.

    -He sighs.-

    Kraus: I'm rambling. Sorry.

    Zeal: There's nothing to apologize about! I asked you to tell me about this! Actually, if you want to talk about it, I'd be interested in hearing if there was anything more.

    Kraus: Yes, knowing you, there is another thing you'd have found interesting.

    -Zeal grins.-

    Kraus: To my knowledge, they still don't have an answer to this puzzle. One of the more badly burned bodies found in that lab belonged to someone known to have been died about a month before. His body was stolen from his grave a few days before the explosion. And to add another layer on top of that, they found hints that his body may have been set ablaze sometime after the explosion that destroyed the lab. It's almost as if someone was trying to disguise the number of corpses. And there's no clues as to why.

    Zeal: ...Huh.

    Kraus: I'm sorry. I was rambling again, wasn't I?

    Zeal: No, it's fine! It's just a lot to take in. It's not the sort of story I was expecting to hear today, that's for sure.

    -He smiles.-

    Zeal: Do you have a place to stay? We have an extra bed, now that... Well, never mind that.

    Kraus: If it's no trouble...

    Zeal: Not at all! It'd be nice to have a chance to talk to you about some non-serious stuff, too. Like we used to. It feels like it's been forever since the last time that happened.
    "Sing with me a song of birthrights and love."
