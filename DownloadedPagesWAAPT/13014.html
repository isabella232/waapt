    Gateon Port

    Nathan has been strolling with stiff strides for a while now, beckoning Sophia to investigate.

    Sophia: <Pardon me, but you seem tense.>

    Nathan: "What? No, I'm not tense, just keeping my guard up."

    Sophia: <You're stressed out, admit it.>

    Nathan: "How do you figure that?"

    Sophia got closer and placed a hand on Nate's chest.

    Nathan: "What are you doing?"

    Sophia: <This is not a calm mind's pulse I'm feeling.> She took her arm off his chest. <Is something troubling you that's causing your alertness?>

    Nathan: "...Okay, I'm scared. Ever since I almost got shot at Citadark and how it reminded me how fast I could lose my life out here, with the high velocity objects and the freezing and burning and impalement and ripped to shreds..." Nathan paled at the thoughts and got off his legs. "I'm just one man, in a middle of a desolate land with some mean Pokemon and people. It gets to me."

    Nathan got up and continued walking, silent for the most part. Before reaching the building, Sophia had an idea.

    Sophia: <Have you tried meditation?>

    Nathan: "Meditation? Lying in place while listening to a CD? No thanks."

    Sophia: <That is not what I mean! I meant calming your thoughts and forget your worries for just a few minutes.>

    Nathan: "I don't follow."

    Sophia: <So follow me instead!> She swooped toward a bench.

    Nathan "Oh dear..." He followed, unsure of what to expect.
