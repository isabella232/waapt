    Rebecca: <And when are you going to stop spouting such nonsense!? I did not receive any letters from you!>

    -sighs- Well, I'll let you know once I figure out what I'm going to do with myself.

    Rebecca: -lifts up her nose a bit higher and glares at Jane unsatisfactorily. In the brushes, she hears something, but she decides to leave it alone for now. The two stay in silence for several minutes until Rebecca just can't take it anymore-

    Rebecca: <Uuuuuurgh! You make me so angry, trainer!>

    Heh. You don't even know my name. I must be slipping up more than I thought...

    Rebecca: <Oh, is that what it is? You think you're slipping up as a trainer?>

    No...

    Rebecca: <Then what is it!? I refuse to continue with a mopey trainer like you who doesn't know how to have fun, and has trouble paying attention to what is happening all around her!>

    I do too know how to have fun!

    Rebecca: <Then why are you so quiet and mopey!? When I met you, you were bubbly and smiling and more energetic than an Emolga on a sugar high!>

    I don't want to battle another Eldritch Abomination!

    Rebecca: <But I thought battling was supposed to be fun! This creature is supposed to be a challenge!>

    I just don't want to go on an epic high-stakes adventure right now!

    I only took the gym challenge because Frillish wouldn't obey me unless I had a gym badge!

    Rebecca: <Then release her!>

    No!

    Rebecca: <Why not!? You released the Mandibuzz, and the Scraggy, and the Blitzle, so why not release the Frillish!>

    What about Portales? He's only got one friend!

    Rebecca: <Oh sure, what about Portales! What about ''Me!?

    Ugh, it's just like you to think only about yourself!

    Rebecca: <Oh yeah!? What about Buster? Huh? Did you think of him at all?>

    Uh...

    Rebecca: <You haven't paid any attention to him at all since the day he evolved! Don't you love him anymore!?>

    Of course I love him!

    Rebecca: <Then why did you back out of the Gym Challenge!?>

    Because I don't want to take on Gyms anymore, okay!? I chose my path and I want to be a researcher, not a battler! I want to go out and see the Pokemon World in its fullest! I want to learn about the legends of Unova, Hoenn, Kanto, Johto, Sinnoh! I want to see the pokemon in their natural habitat and draw what I see! I don't want to battle tooth and nail for the sake of my life! And I don't want to battle another Eldritch Abomination!

    Rebecca: <But you're missing out on half the fun! The whole point of this is to take on gyms, battle monsters, Save The World! If you wanted to just walk around you should have brought your pokemon from before you came here!>

    I did bring them! They're in my boxes!

    Rebecca: <THEN WHY DON'T YOU TAKE YOUR STUPID POKEMON AND RELEASE US ALL!? HUH? IF YOU DIDN'T NEED ME IN THE FIRST PLACE THEN GO AHEAD! ABANDON ME! IT'S NOT LIKE I CARE OR ANYTHING!> -her eyes start to water- <IT'S NOT LIKE I EVER WANTED A TRAINER ANYWAY! YOU AND YOUR STUPID GROUP ALWAYS HATED ME!>

    What? Hey! Wait!
