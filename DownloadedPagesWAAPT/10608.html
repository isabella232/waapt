    "Then it's all right, as long as you're happy it's fine to me, I saw that our call list was full of calls from you, just knowing that you've been trying all this time to wish me a happy Father's Day makes me happy."

    "But me going there would still be better, right?"

    "Not really, we have been out all day, if you came here you would just be disappointed."

    "Oh... That explains things... So, how's mom doing?"

    "She's doing very well, talkative as ever too, so everything is fine with her. Normally when she's quiet it means she's either tired or sick."

    Hikari then giggles a bit "All right then, say hello to her for me!"

    "I will, I'm sure she'll love it."

    "Thanks!" said Hikari, she then pauses for a few seconds and gives a small smile "I love you dad, I'll make sure to make another visit soon."

    "I love you too, dear. You'll always be welcome here at home, just make sure to warn before coming."

    "Okay, bye!"

    "Bye!"
