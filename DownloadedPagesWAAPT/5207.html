    Jemma, noting that she was getting attention, swallowed the food in her mouth and mewled cutely before running around and chasing her tail. The woman was clearly amused, a small smirk coming to her lips. "Heh..." Her eyes flicked over to Rex. "Hey, thief. Is this your Pokemon?"

    "So what if it is?" Rex grumbled, feeling more than a little upset at the name. This woman was really starting to bug him.

    Satisfied with his response, she nodded. "Alright, thief...let's have a battle to decide what we should do next."

    She stood, brushing her dress clean as she stepped away from Jemma, turning to face them both. "If I win, you pay me for the fish you stole. If you win, I'll drop the charges."

    "Fine!" Rex scowled, glaring at her in determination. "You're on!"

    The woman blinked, feeling a small sense of deja vu at his response. Shaking away those feelings, she smirked and produced a Pokeball.

    "Alright then...you're going down."
