    Herbert: Well, she didn't use a knife or anything, and she's not willingly working for Cipher. She's... look, I'll explain later when she's in a better state of mind, okay?

    Alice: Of course. You can hardly expect me to handle a task of such colossal administrative proportions by myself. I have assembled a few local volunteers eager to assist with the redistribution of Pokemon stolen by Cipher.

    Herbert: ...So basically you've managed to put your own workforce together within the space of a week.
