    Now, Guardé wasn't sexist. But he had been in the mindset of a typical Knight for so much of his life that the idea of a Princess saving herself was unthinkable to him.     Guardé: <So...are you saying you wouldn't save her if she was in trouble?>

    Chord: <If she needed my help? Sure. But she's more than tough enough to take care of herself. Especially with that steel carapace of hers.>
