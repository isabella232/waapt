    There are many, many Pokémon in this world.

    While many of them choose to live their lives, many others choose to partner with humans known as Pokémon Trainers. Hand in hand, they work together to preform many incredible things.

    This is not their story.

    Instead, we follow the tale of a Dragonite who wanted nothing more out of life than food. And lots of it. There were few lengths this Dragonite wouldn't go through to get his claws on the most delicious dishes he could find.

    Which had brought him across continents to reach the crown jewel of Unova - Castelia City...

    "Good morning, Unova! This is Monochrome Radio reporting live from outside the Castelia City Burger Monarch, and I tell ya', it's a riot down here!"

    They weren't kidding. People and Pokémon alike were lining up around the block at the doors of the fast-food chain, waiting impatiently for the doors to open in a few minutes time.

    "Some of these guys have been waiting almost a week in line to grab a taste of the highly anticipated Monarch Rib! A new sandwich to be available at Burger Monarch for a limited time only!"

    In truth, the limited availability of the burger was merely a marketing ploy. In fact, the Burger Monarch CE Os had already decided that the re-release of the Monarch Rib would be a annual event. Not that anyone knew any better at this point.

    As everyone continued to wait the last few agonising minutes before they could finally lay their hands and taste-buds on the thick, juicy pork sandwich (Burger Monarch had declined to mention whether it was made from Spoink or Tepig meat), at the very front of the line - much to the annoyance of many - the odd sight of a Dragonite was curled up outside the door, snoozing soundly.

    This Dragonite is the "hero" of our story. His name is Stratus, a kind-hearted but somewhat childish and gluttons Dragon who had flown all the way from the Sea of Kyogre a few days ago to wait in line. Getting there hadn't been an issue - Dragonite can easily circumnavigate the world in a matter of hours - but the waiting? The waiting had been torturous.

    But if there was one thing he was willing to wait for, it was food. And that steely resolve had lead him through the long days and nights, and now that the hour was at hand…he was sleeping like a log.

    Stratus: <Zzz…>

    ”Oh, it looks like the long wait is over! The doors are opening up, and…wait, an I seeing this right? Is that a Dragonite blocking the line?”

    The now disgruntled customers were now shouting and screaming in anger at the Dragonite that stood between them and their long awaited meal. But while their complaints did little to rouse him, a swift kick from the person behind finally knocked him out of the land of nod.

    A shot of this event would later appear in that day’s Castelia City Times under the headline “Local man kicks Dragonite.”

    Stratus: <Wuh…WHA! I’M UP! I’M UP! I’m->

    Blinking wearily, the Dragonite’s soon noticed that the doors were now open and that now was finally time for him to grab a Grand Rib for himself after so much time.

    <OHMYARCEUS IT’S TIME! IT’S TIME!>

    At a speed that would make even Sonic the Sandslash blink, the Dragonite had darted in, and slammed a paw full of cash down on the counter in front of a shocked clerk.

    <ONE GRAND RIB PLEASE!>

    Even though he didn’t speak monese, Stratus’ message came across loud and clear.

    Clerk: “…C…coming right up…I knew this would be crazy, but a ‘’Dragonite?’’ I knew I should have gotten my degree…”

    A few minutes later...

    Finally.

    Perched on the roof of a tall building where he could get some peace and quiet, Stratus carefully liberated his precious Burger from it's paper bag, staring in awe at the rich pork patty wrapped in a freshly baked bun and topped with Barbecue sauce, onions, and pickles.

    Stratus: <It's just as beautiful as I imagined it!>

    Licking his lips, the Dragon opened his mouth wide in preparation for culinary ecstasy, when...

    <YOINK!>

    ??? used Thief!

    His burger was suddenly snatched from his hands by a large blue and black blur. Of course, this came as a bit of a shock.

    Stratus: <What the-?!>

    <GWAHAHA!!!>

    Gazing upwards, he found the thief hovering in front of him, a very smug looking Hydreigon, clutching his pilfered meal in the jaw of one of it's head-hands.

    Stratus: <Hey, get your own rib, you lousy thief!>

    Hydreigon: <And wait in line like a chump? Pah! I 'ain't got time for that!>

    Snarling, Stratus clenched his claws in anger as the Hydreigon opened his mouth wide to devour the Grand Rib...

    Stratus: <Give it back now or I'll fight you!>

    Pausing for thought, he lowered the burger, a sly smirk crossing his face.

    Hydreigon: <Well, if you want it so badly...>

    Flapping his wings, the Dragon turned around, gazing back at Stratus.

    <Then come and get it!>

    And with that, he darted away, flying north towards the edge of the city. With not a second to lose, the annoyed Stratus flapped his wings and shot off after him.

    This guy had made things personal.
