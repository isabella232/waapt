    Polly: You ever heard of "practice", kid? I've heard rumors it apparently makes perfect.

    -Nico thinks to himself, then nods.-

    Nico: I'll do that, sure thing.

    Polly: Good. But one bit of advice from me?

    Nico: Oh?

    Polly: You seem to already have some talent for one aspect of physical psychic combat; using telekinesis to improvise weapons from your surroundings, and you should certainly hone that. But given your strength seems to lie in more physical combat... Have you tried other aspects of that?

    Nico: ...Like what?

    -Polly smirks as two pink, scythelike energy blades extend from slits in her gloves just above her wrists-

    Polly: Like that.

    Nico: ...Holy crap. ._.

    Polly: -retracts the blades- It's a variation of Psycho Cut. For learning how to use it... Try concentrating that sheer psychic force you use to grab and swing shit around and concentrate on focusing it physically through your wrists. It'll take a lot of that "practice" hooey I mentioned earlier but it'll pay off.

    Nico: -nods vigorously- Thanks!

    Polly: Now... One thing I've been saving for last...

    Nico ...Oh?

    Polly: Nico... I deeply admire, respect and support your desire to be a hero and make a name for yourself in this world... But at the same time, you've got what being a hero's all about completely wrong!

    Nico: Huh?! What is it all about?

    Polly: I'm not the one to answer that question, kiddo. But I can tell you this: Look to your friends in the J-Team. Look to all those who you helped - or helped you - through this whole adventure you've been having over the past year. Look to that pink-haired chick you hooked up with. They all are the key to the answer, in more ways than one.

    Nico: Wait, I don-

    Polly: Sorry, wakey wakey time for you.

    -she whacks his head with her staff again...-

    Rougeport Inn, this morning

    -And Nico wakes up, bright and early in the morning, utterly dazed and confused-

    Thoughts?
