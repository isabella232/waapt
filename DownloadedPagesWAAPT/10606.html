    -Ren slowly places his hand on her waist-

    Borked!Kitsune: -in thought- (Thank Arceus for the low light and this mask.)

    Ren: and place your left on my shoulder.

    -Kitsune places a slightly quivering hand on Ren shoulder as instructed-

    Ren: Now, follow my lead as I start from my left

    -Ren starts to turn and Kitsune follows his lead. Her training in movement and rhythm aid her greatly, as soon she is keeping in perfect step with Ren.-

    Ren: See? I knew you could do it.

    -They continue to dance until the waltz ends and another song starts-

    -most of the older couples leave the floor, leaving new and younger couples in prominence-

    Ren: Different tune, different step.

    -Ren takes his hands and guides Kitsune's hands to his shoulders then places his hands on her hips-

    Ren: Just turn in a circle and sway in time with the music.

    -Ren and Kitsune smiled at each other as they continued their dancing lesson-

    Later, on a veranda

    -Kitsune looks at a clock across the street and sighs Ren walks up to her concerned-

    Ren: What's wrong?

    Borked!Kitsune: -sadly- It's getting late, this will all end soon...

    Ren: -Ren chuckles softly- You sound like Cinderella.

    Borked!Kitsune: Who?

    Ren: A young lady who fell in love with a prince and went to a ball to see him in an enchanted outfit that disappeared at midnight. So she ran from the prince so he wouldn't see her.

    Borked!Kitsune: -seeing her plight in the story- What happened then?

    Ren: The prince had fallen in love with her at the ball and found one of Cinderella's enchanted shoes. He searched for her until he found her, and he married her. Sound familiar?

    -Kitune smiles and nods and turns and Ren a peck on the lips-

    Borked!Kitsune: Thank you Ren. I love you.

    -She turns and to leave but Ren Holds her in a tight hug from behind-

    Ren: But unlike the prince, I won't let you leave the ball by yourself.

    -He turns Kistsune around with his face near hers-

    I don't if this is true love, but I do love you Kitsune. And because I love you, I want you to be happy. So let's go find Pentigan and return his amulet and Armband. Then we will ask him together where we can find you or me an armband.

    -Kitsune looks up at Ren-

    Borked!Kitsune: Renard Valenum Clavis, you are so cliche and corny. and I love you.
