    -Herbert hesitates for a moment, now pale and shaking slightly, and Iris gives him an odd look.-

    Herbert: You ready for this, Kirk?

    Kirk: <...Yeah. I can finish this off. Just like I did the last four times.>

    Herbert: Excellent. Aura Sphere, Kirk!

    -Kirk charges up one of his characteristic balls of bright orange Aura, before hurling the crackling sphere at the Archeops and sending it flying backwards.-

    Kirk used Aura Sphere!

    Archeops: <Caw! What did I ever do to you, huh?>

    Kirk: <Shut it, bird. I didn't come this far just to listen to your babbling.>

    Iris: Dragon Claw, Archeops!

    Archeops used Dragon Claw!

    -Kirk braces himself and endures the attack, glaring back up at the Archeops.-

    Kirk: <You're going to need more than that to beat me, I'm afraid.>

    Herbert: Stone Edge!

    Kirk: <This, on the other hand, should be more than enough to deal with you.>

    Kirk used Stone Edge!

    Archeops fainted!

    Iris: Not bad! You're pretty tough! But I still have two Pokemon left, whilst you only have your Lucario! Do you still think you can win this?

    Herbert: ...No. I don't think I can win. But I need to win. I'm not giving up here! Not after everything I've been though to get to this point!

    Iris: Hmm... I think it's time. Time to show you just how strong the bond between me and my Pokemon is! Haxorus, go!

    Iris sent out Haxorus!

    -Haxorus eyes Kirk up and down for a moment, before adopting a battle stance.-

    Haxorus: <So you are the challenger's final Pokemon. Rather surpising that he would stake everything on such a frail mon.>

    Kirk: <"Frail"? I'm not sure what kind of Lucario you've been fighting, but I'm tougher than that. Tough enough to get this far, at least.>

    Iris: Haxorus is my best Pokemon! But on top of that, she's also my best friend! Without her help, I wouldn't have been able to beat Alder, and I wouldn't be champion! I owe her everything! Our bond is strong enough to beat even the toughest of challengers!

    -She gives Herbert a cheerful look, but there is a meaningful glint in her eyes.-

    Iris: Do you and your Lucario have the same bond?

    Herbert: ...Kirk, Dragon Pulse! Take the initiative!

    -Kirk hesitates for a moment, before opening his mouth and breathing a gout of draconic fire at Haxorus.-

    Kirk used Dragon Pulse!

    -Haxorus disappears behind the flames, only to step forward as they disperse, virtually unscathed. Kirk stares in disbelief.-

    Kirk: <How did you->

    Haxorus: <You don't get to be a champion's starter without a certain level of endurance.>

    Iris: Now, Haxorus! Earthquake!

    -Haxorus stomps her foot down on the ground and creates a massive tremor, knocking Kirk off his feet and slamming him to the floor.-

    Haxorus used Earthquake!

    Kirk: <Oof...>

    Herbert: Damnit! Kirk, are you okay?

    Kirk: <I'm... I'm fine. Gonna take more than that...>

    Herbert: Thank Arceus... alright, use Close Combat! Give it everything you've got!

    -Kirk heaves himself to his feet, his fists starting to glow with Auric energy. He charges at Haxorus, punching every inch of her that he can reach.-

    Kirk used Close Combat!

    -Haxorus doesn't flinch. Kirk continues futilely punching for a moment before easing off, looking up at Haxorus with an expression of dawning realisation on his face.-

    Kirk: <...I'm not good enough, am I?>

    Haxorus: <Not yet. I'm sorry to disappoint you after coming so far, but I fight to win. And I'm not going to make an exception for you.>

    Iris: Haxorus, Draco Meteor!

    Haxorus: <You fought well, Lucario.>

    -Haxorus gives another heavy stomp of her foot, and a massive c☄met descends out of the starry backdrop with a rumbling roar, slamming into Kirk at full force.-

    Haxorus used Draco Meteor!

    Herbert: NO!

    -Kirk is sent flying against the wall by the attack and falls to the ground on his knees, panting.-

    Herbert: Come on, Kirk, we can still do this! Use Close Combat, or Force Palm, or Dragon Pulse, or something!

    -Kirk continues to breathe heavily, trying to regain his strength.-

    Herbert: Just the one more move, Kirk... one more move and we can win here...

    -Kirk collapses onto all fours, and whatever traces of determination were present on Herbert's face immediately disappear.-

    Herbert: Please, Kirk... one more Close Combat, p-please...

    Kirk: <I... I can't...>

    -With a sigh, Kirk's arms give way and he crumples to the ground, defeated.-
