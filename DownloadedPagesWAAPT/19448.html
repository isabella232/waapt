    Kiaran: <Yeah, bug types are seriously scary. Like have you seen their cold, dead, eyes!?>

    -After a moment of silence, Barclay commented-

    Barclay: <Wow, that's a new record. I think that' the longest sentence Ark has said in human since i've known her.>

    -Audra interrupted with a cough-

    Audra: <Anyway, where, pray tell, did you two disappear off two, hm?>

    Ralli: <Oh yeah.... sorry fer dissapearn' on ya Ark. Verigan was feelin' like he failed ya, so wanted to make it up to you.>

    -She presents Ark with the accessory case full of the things they gathered. Ark took it tentatively and checked what was inside, before shoving it her backpack. Kneeling down she looked at Verign. Verigan was looking away, his previous mirth haven given way to nervousness.-

    Ark: <It's fine, you did not fail.>

    Verigan: <I... Lady of the Midnight Hunt, why are you forgiving me? I did my best, but it still wasn't good enough! How can I call myself a knight and proper hero if I can't even win one battle! If anything you should just cast me out now before I cause anymore trouble!>

    -Ark huffed and hesitantly patted Verigan's head like her adoptive mother did to her when sad. She mentally scrambled for all the forgiveness speeches she heard between other pack members back in Penumbra Clan for reference on what to say.-

    Ark: < I'm not forgiving you.>

    -Verigan seemed to deflate and Ralli opened her mouth to protest.-

    Ark: <I have nothing to forgive you for, because you did nothing wrong. You did your best, and that is what I think is important. If you think that's not good enough, then do even better then your best next time. You are stronger now, so it should be no problem.>

    -Verigan began to slowly tear up as Ark went on. He tried to wipe the tears away and stop sniffling, but they just kept falling-

    Ark: <You didn't fail me. You didn't back down and kept going even when you were going to lose. You did me proud out there. You are apart of Ark Pack no matter what you do, and I am supposed to protect you, so don't you dare disappear again.>

    -Ark paused thoughtfully before adding-

    Ark: <Also... please just call me Ark. I'm not some all powerful being like the Ghost King.>

    -The waterworks finally broke as Verigan launched himself into an awkward hug with Ark. The poor girl froze, obviously having no idea what she was supposed to do at the moment, before awkwardly wrapping her arms around him. It was then that the glowing started.-

    Ralli: <Is he...>

    Barclay: <He is.>

    -Limb elongated and stretched. Colors changed and two pairs of ribbons grew and wrapped around Ark in a proper hug. When the lights finally died down, there was a Sylveon hugging Ark with all his might.-

    Verigan: <I-I... Okay, La-..... Ark. Okay, Ark. Thank you so, so, so, much.>

    -After a moment, Verigan released Ark, and picked up his sword with one of his ribbons. He pointed the sword towards Route 204.-

    Verigan: <Now, let us be off at once! Together we shall persevere and repel the dastardly bug types as we protect He who Guides from the Swarm of the Forest! Tally Ho!>

    -With that Verigan ran off towards Route 204, Ralli soon taking off after him yelling to slow down. Ark stood up slowly and shrugged. sometime In his crying or the hug, Verigan had knocked Ark's scarf eksew. The scarf no longer hid the bottom half her face, nor the hesitant smile that had spread across her face. Ark looked fondly at the advancing figure of Verigan, before seeming to realize others were watching. Flushing, Ark hastily re-adjusted her scarf to hide the bottom half of her face.-

    Ark: L-let's go! No time to waste!

    -With that she ran after Verigan. Kiaran beamed from his perch on her shoulder all the while.-

    Kiaran: <I'm proud of you Ark. You've really started to come out of your shell here.>

    Ark: <Yeah, yeah, whatever.>

    -behind them, Audra smirked.-

    Audra: <I dare say that I might be growing fond of these idiots. It's like having a soap opera play out in front of me. They really are quite entertaining.>

    Barclay: <You watch soap opera's?! I never took you for the type! In the shows I managed to glimpse back in Castelia City, I dare say that Days of Our Pokemon was one of my favorites!>

    -Realizing what she said, Audra looked embarrassed. Eyes darting side to side, looking for a way out, she quickly said.-

    Audra: <Erm... well I better catch up with them, lest they get into some trouble without me. We will continue this line of conversation never.>

    -With that, Audra sprinted off after the rest of the pack.-

    Barclay: <Well, I suppose that we best be off, after you my Lady Ashley.>
