    you really like hot animals don't you?

    Prism Tower

    Lyuri and Duke are now there, looking over the city at night and just admiring it. Duke made a point to stay very close to Lyuri and wrap an arm around her to make her feel comfortable

    "... Can I tell you something?" asked Lyuri, her gaze fixed at the city

    "Yes?"

    "Can we keep it just between us?"

    "Yes."

    "All right..."

    Lyuri then takes a deep breath and just keeps watching the city from above.

    "I'm afraid. Afraid that someday I'll drive everyone I know and like away from me just because I can't contain my emotions when I should. But at the same time, I feel that driving people away from me will make me a stronger person. I feel helpless... I'm afraid of being helpless. Because if I can't do anything to help myself, I can't expect others to do it for me, even if they do it just leave me feel angry at myself for needing help from others. That's why I'm afraid of heights. When you're falling to your death, there's nothing you can do but watch as you get closer to the ground unless someone comes to save your life somehow. Every time I get to a tall place, I can't help but think of how helpless I'll be if I slip and fall. As much as I tell people that they can count on me, I don't like to count with others to do something for me. I want to do it by myself."

    Lyuri then looks up a bit

    "Yet I'm a Pokémon Trainer and regularly need my Pokémon to do stuff I wouldn't be able to normally. I really don't understand how my head works." she added.

    "I guess you're just conflicted, like you just said. But, why are you telling me all of this?" asked Duke

    "Because you're my boyfriend. This means I rely on you just as much as you rely on me. You're the closest person to me here. The last thing I want is to drive you away from me, but... If we settle down like we said in the restaurant and I have to depend on you so much, will that help me improve at all?"

    "You're thinking too far ahead, it's not like that's going to happen so soon. Nothing is set in stone, but even if they happen like we said earlier... You're already an independent and strong girl, I don't think spending time taking care of a house would do much to change that. Maybe living like that would make you a bit calmer and more gentle. I don't know, we just have to wait and see." Duke then pauses and just looks at the scenary "I just hope I never make you feel like you're helpless."

    Lyuri sighs and gives a hint of a smile

    "I'm in love with you. Right now I'm not even afraid of falling even from this freaking high tower, what worries me the most is if I'm going to mess this relationship up like I do all the time with my friendships, but... You always stay by my side, so I guess that won't be happening. I only hope that this relationship can actually last long for us to actually marry someday."

    Duke chuckles "This will last just as long as you want to. I'm here to stay, for how long you want to."

    "Then I guess we'll stay together forever."

    The couple stays silent as they keep gazing at the city from atop the Prism Tower, Duke pulling Lyuri closer to him

    "Lumiose is quite the thing at night isn't it?" asked Lyuri, smiling

    "I think I could stand here watching it all night." said Duke, chuckling

    They remained there for a bit longer, until it started to rain.

    "... Yeah. Not happening." they both say, in unison.
