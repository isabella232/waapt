    Lilycove Fan-Club

    Out of curiosity, Woolly and Sol have gone to visit the fan-club, having heard that it was a good place for Pokémon to visit. To their surprise, they had immediately been cuddled and petted on their arrival, much to their pleasure, only to then find out that the fan-club was now mostly a gathering of J-team fans.

    After a while of being pampered, the two have begun to examine the various J-team merchandise on display. Sol, in particular, takes notice of a certain machine that look like a certain Leafeon she knows.

    Sol: <What's this?>

    She sniffs it curiously, flicking her tail in pleasure.

    <Smells like Ictacya...>

    Woolly gives the machine a close look.

    Woolly: <I think you're supposed to put your flipper or hand or...whatever on here...>

    The Ampharos does so, the machine beginning to whir for a few seconds, before printing a small slip of paper.

    <Ah! There we go. Now what's this?>

    Woolly takes the slip of paper, showing it to Sol, who can read, unlike her.

    Sol: <...It says that you'll evolve into a Jolteon...Huh.>

    Woolly scratches her head.

    Woolly: <Well, the type's right, at least. Why don't you give it a try?>

    Sol: <Wouldn't hurt to try...>

    Sol balances up on her back legs, placing a paw on the machine. As before, it begins to whir, printing another small scrap of paper. Lifting it telekineticly, Sol holds it up for both her and Woolly to see:

    ":3"

    Woolly: <...Wow. This machine's actually pretty accurate.>

    Sol: <Indeed, it is.>
