    <Unseen!Zoroark: "Er... Wait, what's the point of this again?">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "So here's a chance for you to own up to those words... We will be deciding the fates of everyone else here ranked lower than you...">

    <Scolemis!Leavanny: "Shut it!">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "Hmm... Well that makes things convenient for us, does it not? We will be defaulting to your assumption that everyone here would have been choosing as you did, and would have preferred death...">

    -Starting to cry-

    <Captured!Druddigon: "(*sniffle*) N-No... N-Not l-like th-this...">

    <Unseen!Zoroark: "Er... Isn't that a little mu-">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "Quiet! And remember who's your superior here!">

    <Unseen!Zoroark: "Sorry! My bad!">

    <AU!Nimbus: "B-But that's- And-">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "If you decide to remain intransigent after we're through with these pathetic worms, you shall join them!">

    -To the Leavanny-

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "Dispose of this wretch!">

    <Scolemis!Leavanny: "Gladly... Let's have a little fun with this though...">

    -Pulls Leaf Blade away from throat and back into air, aiming to decapitate its unlucky victim in a fell swoop through momentum-

    <Captured!Druddigon: "AAH!">

    <AU!Nimbus: "NO! Pl-Please! Don't!">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "Hold your blade!">

    -Stopping the Leaf Blade just a couple inches from the Druddigon's neck-

    <Scolemis!Leavanny: "Oh come on! Am I going to get to kill something or not?!>

    -Eyes closed, shivering-

    <Captured!Druddigon: "(*sniffle*) (*whimper*)">

    -Feeling utterly sick-

    <AU!Nimbus: "I'll join...">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "A wise choice... For you, anyways.">

    -To the Druddigon-

    <Scolemis!Leavanny: "What about you, O great servant of the gods? You feel like living for another day?">

    -Nodding, utterly miserable-

    <Captured!Druddigon: "(*sniffle*) Y-Yes...">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "Good then, let's check up on your pathetic comrades...">

    -Dragging the two out of the illusion, before a group of disbelieving and fairly demoralized group of local guards-

    -Avoiding eye contact with the other guards-

    <Captured!Druddigon: "Nngh... (*sniffle*)">

    <AU!Nimbus: "I'm sorry... I'm so sorry...">

    <Scolemis!Ninetales: "So... You've heard these two... Now which of you will be making the sensible choice along with them?">

    <AU!Nimbus: "Not a single one of us who were captured had the courage to defy them after that... I undermined the bravery of a bunch of rookies to stand up and not dishonor their shifts... When they needed it most... Is that what you wanted to hear?!">

    <Nimbus: "N-No...">
