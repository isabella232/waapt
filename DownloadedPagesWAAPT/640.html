    Drapion: The enemy has shown themselves, soldiers! Get in formation and CHARGE!

    -Drapion leads the charge. Many forces are lost on both sides. Before long the human soldiers of both sides arrive and join the battle. Drapion has found the head Pokemon of the opposing army and is engaging him-

    Machamp: Foolish Drapion. You're fighting on the losing side! Our forces greatly outnumber yours, both in human and Pokemon counts!

    -He punches Drapion in the face, but he quickly recovers-

    Drapion: That may be so, but we're better trained! I've drilled discipline and coordination into my men, while your forces can barely march!

    -Drapion swings his stinger at Machamp, who jumps over it-

    Machamp: Is that so? Then care to explain why your side already has twice as many casualties?

    Drapion: We have just started this fight! And this... this is the last time I want to see your face on the field of battle! Today, I put an end to your command!

    -He successfully slashes across Machamp's upper right bicep-

    Machamp: Argh! You can still fight, huh?

    -He lifts up a large chunk of the ground and hurls it at Drapion. Drapion smashes it with his shoulder, but Machamps begins picking up smaller clumps and throwing them like bullets-

    Machamp: You're getting old, you coot. I aged gracefully; I'm stronger than I ever was!

    Drapion: Which is still weaker than me!

    -He catches a rock and nails Machamp in the head with it-

    Machamp: Enough!

    -He charges at Drapion, and the two begin to grapple. Machamps lifts Drapion and slams him on his back, but Drapion uses his stinger to push Machamp off. Both get up and this time they begin punching each other. Machamps attempts to punch with both right fists at the same time; Drapion catches his fists and drags his other claw across Machamp's stomach, eviscerating him-

    -Before long, the enemy forces, both human and Pokemon, are forced to surrender their stronghold. Drapion retires, and is released by the General into the wild. Despite his retirement, Drapion cannot help but feel the need to battle and fight for a cause-
