    Glaceon, #471 in Oak's Categorization System

    Pure Ice-type

    Evolves from Eevee when exposed to Ice Rock's radiation.

    Measures 2'7"/0.8m

    Weighs 57.1 pounds/25.9 kilos

    Its best field is special offense (Ice Beam, Shadow Ball, Swift, etc.).

    Males severely outnumber females

    Grows at a medium-leaning-on-fast pace.

    Known Abilities:

    Snow Cloak - User's evasion is increased during a hailstorm or a blizzard.

    Ice Body (Hidden) - User can heal its wounds during a hailstorm.

    [Range of moves]
    Accelgor, #617 in Oak's Categorization System

    Pure Bug-type

    Evolves from Shelmet when exposed to a radiation emitted whenever Shelmet and Karrablast meet each other in the trading machine.

    Measures 2'07"/0.8m

    Weighs 55.8 pounds/25.3 kilos

    Its best field is speed, although its special attack is decent as well.

    Males and females seem to be distributed equally.

    Grows at a medium-leaning-on-fast pace.

    Known Abilities:

    Hydration - User can heal its burn, poison or paralysis, thaw out or wake up if it's raining.

    Sticky Hold - Pokémon's held item cannot be stolen. It also has use in fishing.

    Unburden (Hidden) - Whenever user loses or uses its held item, it's Speed is doubled.

    [Range of moves]
    Absol, #359 in Oak's Categorization System

    Pure Dark-type

    Is not known to evolve from or into any other Pokémon. Capable of Mega Evolution.

    Measures 3'11"/1.2m

    Weighs 103.6 pounds/47.0 kilos; 108.0 pounds/49.0 kilos as a Mega Absol.

    Its best field is physical offense (Aerial Ace, Night Slash, Stone Edge, etc.). Mega Evolution seems to slightly boosts its already high physical power, while bringing its special attack and speed to decent levels. Has a wide array of moves, giving it chance in either physical or special field.

    Males and females seem to be distributed equally.

    Grows at a medium-leaning-on-slow pace.

    Known Abilities:

    Pressure - Opposing Pokémon get tired faster.

    Super Luck - Pokémon is more likely to score a critical hit.

    Justified (Hidden) - Pokémon's physical attack is powered up each time it's hit by a Dark-type move'

    Magic Bounce (Mega) - Reflects most non-damaging moves back at the opposing Pokémon.

    [Range of moves]
