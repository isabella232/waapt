    368768 Herbert40k21st Apr 2014 11:20:13 AM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
    Imperial Commissar

    Neo-Galactic HQ

    -Titan has returned to filing through paperwork when Io enters the room, a sheaf of paper under one arm and a test tube full of a murky, congealed substance in the other.-

    Titan: I have very little patience for those who pay very little attention to punctuality, Commander Io. This report was supposed to have been completed several days ago. It speaks volumes about the condition of our organisation when Allen is the most diligent of our Administrators.

    Io: I can promise you that there's a reason it took so long, but I'll get to that later. Presumably you want the general status overview first?

    Titan: Indeed I do.

    -Io hands him the sheaf of paper, which he starts reading through with a frown on his face.-

    Io: Bottom line is, things aren't looking good. Illusion have us beat in terms of both manpower and resources. They also have a bonus on the PR front, since they don't have an established reputation and aren't walking around with bowl cuts and spacesuits. Not to mention that they have some pretty influencial allies, whereas we only have our sponsors in Almia, and even they would prefer to avoid publicly affiliating with us.

    Titan: ...And are there any positives to our situation?

    Io: Oh, there are.

    -Her scarred face contorts into a mockery of a grin as she lifts up the test tube.-

    Io: We've finished synthesising our first prototype.

    -A flash of something approaching surprise crosses Titan's face as he raises an eyebrow.-

    Titan: I thought you said you'd require a-

    Io: That's because we thought we did. But I was smart enough to develop a formula that didn't rely on any past samples. Not quite as potent as the original, but it has the same effect.

    Titan: How long until we can begin mass-production?

    Io: A while. Even producing a single sample cost large amounts of time, research and resources. This is only the first iteration of the formula, after all. It needs further refinement before it can reach optimum effectiveness. We'd also need to find a third party willing to take care of manufacturing.

    Titan: That can easily be arranged.

    -He stands up.-

    Titan: I suppose we have no reason to remain here, then. A more remote facility would be far more suitable for our purposes.

    Io: That it would. I've already told the Grunts to start packing up and burning all non-essential files.

    Titan: Then all that remains is to transfer our operations to our new location.

    -He starts talking into the speaker on his desk, making an announcement that plays throughout the base and through the personal communicator of every Neo-Galactic member.-

    Titan: Attention. This is a message for all members of Neo-Galactic. From this moment onwards, our facility in Kalos will no longer serve as our main base of operations. We are transfering our operations elsewhere. Coordinates are currently being sent to your personal communicators. All Commanders and Grunts are to continue serving Neo-Galactic's interests to the best of their ability, and disrupting the activities of the Illusion Project wherever they arise. Whether or not you you do so from our new location or whilst remaining in Kalos is irrelevant. That is all.

    -Io raises an eyebrow as Titan finishes the announcement.-

    Io: ...You really trust the other Commanders enough to leave them to their own devices?

    Titan: Of course not. But decentralising our operations leaves us far less vulnerable to sabotage. I need only a handful of scientists and a third-party manufacturer to complete the next stage of our current plan. All the rest is merely smoke and mirrors to distract our adversaries. Irrelevant in the grand scheme of things.

    -He sighs.-

    Titan: And in the end, all of our petty conflicts shall become irrelevant.

    -He turns to Io.-

    Titan: Come. We should be departing this region shortly.

    -The two of them start marching off down the corridors.-
