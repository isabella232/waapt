    Somewhere Above Unova

    | Zeta stands up by Cetus's head, away from the group.

    | She is deep in thought, staring up at the night sky.

    | For a moment, she glances back at Dopants.

    Zeta: Heh. So innocent, so fresh, so... young. Was I ever that young, that new, that unworn? I can't remember. Some days, my heart just sits there, in my chest, weighing me down like a two ton chunk of frozen steel. Can I get a better one? Huh? Will I be able to thaw this ice, melt this steel, and forge something better? Am I destined to be an old, beaten, rusted blade? God, I hope not. That's how Gramps was. He never really left the battlefield. Damn. I pray that my heart will forge something better than just another blade. I beg you, whoever and whatever, that I might become a tool of peace, and construction, and creation, rather than a weapon of unholy, destructive, all-consuming war. I fear it may be too late for me.

    Zeta: And Pi. Oh god, Pi. Fate is a cruel and heartless bitch, to shape my life so. It is a miracle that he managed to escape with his soul unsullied, to dance through the mud and gore and flame and shadow of the cruel lands, and emerge unscathed. I marvel at the sheer light that pours from him. He is a beacon in the dark. I love him, I want him, I need him. But I cannot have him, for my touch would darken that very light I so crave. Lord, what fools these mortals be, eh?

    Zeta: But if I cannot hold him, then at least I can defend him. I shall lie in the shadows, just beyond his light. I shall ensure that none shall reach him, that none shall step out of the darkness to drag him down into the depths. I shall be his shield, his sword, his silent guardian in the night. For his is an existence worth fighting for, to preserve, to cherish, even if only from afar. This I swear to do, on my name of Bismarck, on my honor as an Orren, and on my own, dirtied soul, little value though it may have.

    | She resumes staring up at the stars, reveling in the moonlight. But it is not the light of the moon that she feels tonight.

    Zetta: Pi, I am coming back for you.
