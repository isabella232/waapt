    Blue grumbles, then pulls out a battered laptop, and hooks up the hard drive.

    Blue: Hmmm. Huh. Heh.

    <Gong: What is it?>

    Blue: He used our old secret encryption code. Lemme see... and there!>

    She decrypts the hard drive. The first file is a video, which she opens.

    Video

    An older man sits at a desk, filming himself. His face shows his life in wrinkles - smile lines, worry lines. Unfortunately, the worry lines far outnumber the smile lines. He is of Korean persuasion, with high cheekbones. His hair is wispy, thin, and white.

    Dr. Asmond Cho: My dearest flower... if you are watching this, it means I died a coward. It means I was unable to speak to you of the things I did while living. I am sorry.

    Blue takes a sharp breath.

    Dr. Cho: We... we never set out to Shadow Pokemon. At least, Dr. Warden and I didn't. It was Ein, the bastard... Ein.

    He sighs, then composes himself.

    Dr. Cho: We were actually researching the hearts of Pokemon, how to better understand them. But Ein, damn him, he was in it for the power. Before we knew what was happening, we were signed on with Cipher, and sealing the hearts of Pokemon! Dr. Warden tried to get out, and they killed his wife for it... but that's another story. As for me, I was too much of a coward. I stayed in until the first collapse of Cipher. With Ein gone, they had no hold on me.

    Silent tears trickle down Blue's cheeks.

    Dr. Cho: Despite all that... despite all that, it was not the worst thing. No. My worst crime, I committed against you. When I saw that you inherited the gift, I was afraid. I was scared that some bad man, some Ein, would find you and drag you down those dark paths. And then I saw how you would surpass me in all ways... I. Arceus forgive me. I had Enigma seal away your gift. You have a Psychic construct in your head, strangling your gift. I am sorry, my precious flower. I am so sorry.

    The man on the screen is reduced to tears.

    Dr. Cho: I did what I did out of love, but I am damned for it nonetheless. Arceus have mercy on my soul. My own daughter. I am sorry.

    Sootopolis

    The video ends.

    Gong is shocked silent.

    Blue is silent, save one broken sob.

    Her world has been destroyed.

    Again.
