    "Aline! Where are-"

    She then spots the little group and starts making her way towards them.

    "There you are! Don't leave like that! Unless you want to conclude your birthday by getting grounded!"

    Aline then breaks the hug with Sensei and gives her mom an embarrassed and apologetic look as she ran her hand through her hair in sort of an instinctive reaction.

    "Oh, I guess it's a family trip. Good to see you again Ms..." said Sakura, though she soon struggles to remember her name

    "Monique. You're that one girl who came to our house aren't you? Wasn't expecting to meet you again so soon." she smiles at her

    "Right, I'm Sakura, if you didn't remember that." Sakura's eyes then widen for a brief moment "Wait, birthday?"

    Monique grins "Yup. Our little girl is 9 now!"

    "Oh my, I didn't know that! Happy birthday, Aline!" said Sakura, smiling

    "<Happy birthday!>" said the Squad.

    Aline smiles, though she looks somewhat puzzled and also a bit disappointed. She then begins writing something on a piece of paper she was carrying with her.

    "I guess Mr.Dune didn't get my letter then, I'm sure he'd have told you about my birthday if he did..."

    Sakura raises an eyebrow "Letter?"

    "She wrote a letter to that young man she had been travelling with, talking about her birthday and other things. I guess that letter must be lost now." said Monique.

    "I told you to tell you I said hi! But I guess it didn't happen." Aline then folds her arms, looking mildly upset.

    "Come to think of it, it's been a while since I last saw him in the first place." thought Sakura, then smiling at the little girl "Well, don't get too upset about it, Aline, it was just a little bad luck."

    Aline does nothing for a moment, but then she unfolds her arms and gives Sakura a little nod and smile.

    "Well, shall I help you two find your way across this place?" asked Sakura, looking at Monique

    "Sure, dear! We just need to wait for that sluggish husband of mine to appear first." she answered

    "Oh, I thought it was just you two."

    "Nah, honey wouldn't miss a special family trip like this for nothing. He's really slow though, good thing I'm fast enough to keep this hyper girl in place."
