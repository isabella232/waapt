    The Wanderer
    Thanks to Silent for helping me with this.

    Sometime between Tagg's victory against Diantha and leaving for Fiore

    -Silent is sitting on a bench somewhere, accompanied by Ichtacya on lap. Not very far from there Roc is perched on a tree, observing as Shahinne plays with her [bag of souls] below-

    Silent?

    -Ichtacya raises her ears and barks a happy hello as she recognizes the voice. Silent looks to the direction of the call to see Tagg-

    Silent: Ah, Tagg. Long time no see.

    Yeah it's been a while hasn't it?

    Silent: Long enough, yes. Since the meeting, I think?

    -Shrugs-

    How have you been?

    Alright, I guess, all things considered. You?

    Silent: -Getting Ichtacya off his lap- Fine for the most part. We met some interesting new people.

    Ichtacya: <I'm sure they'll get used to... things.>

    A lot of the others are heading over to Fiore, probably because of Illusion.

    Sol: -Peeks from somewhere, tilts head- <Illusion of what? Gaia?>

    Silent: Hmmm... well, they can probably count me out of that one. I’m not really in the mood for going around knocking people. You...?

    I'll probably be heading over there myself, but not yet.

    Aaron: <There's still stuff we want to do in Kalos.>

    Silent: Ah, good to hear. You know...

    -Looks around-

    Because I still have Roc around. And Algira's here. Though she hasn't done much since we went through Snowbelle.

    I guess she might want Helios to be with her or something.

    -Jokingly-

    At this point we practically have joint custody over Roc.

    Silent: Hmmm, yeah, now that you mention that. Now, if that worked for something...

    -Snarks, points to Shahinne playing with the mysterious bag-

    Ichtacya: <Actually we all haven’t had many fights. The last one I saw was a tie.>

    -Sol and Ichtacya glare at Silent as a reminder of battles-

    That bag feels like...souls.

    Silent: -Frowns- That sounds weird. Still... Shahinne got it from someone in Rt. 16, and Roc refuses to tell who.

    Sol: <He does keep good watch of it.>

    Wouldn't happen to have been a big red and black bird, would it Roc?

    -Roc looks down at Shahinne and then gives Tagg a half-hearted glare-

    Roc: <Yes...>

    Shahinne -picks up the chatter, trills: <Oooh birdie! Huge, red, huuuuge antlers! And with fluffspace!> ^.^

    Roc: <Uhm, she means the horns.>

    Silent: -Crosses arms, displeased- Hmmm... never told me anything about that.

    Yveltal I presume...

    Silent: Great. With that there’s now what... three Legendaries Shahinne has met?

    Aya: <What's fluffspace?>

    Lepi: -Does the Vivillon equivalent of a shrug-

    -Shahinne notices Aya and quickly flies closer-

    Shahinne: -Hopping around the larger bird- <Oooh, you don’t have fluffspace?>

    Aya: -Uses her wings to move a short distance away- <Careful, I don't want to burn you.>

    -Shahinne nods obediently and stays a small distance from the bird, looking at her in amazement-

    'Silent: -To Tagg- So... I guess your plans to head over there are more or less'' immediate. And that you might want Roc back?

    Roc: -gravely- <Shahinne has advanced very little in her training here.>

    Well yeah, pretty much.

    M-hm...

    -Silent checks his Linoone-themed lappy and Pokédex for a moment-

    Roc -sharply: <Shahinne, come here.>

    -Shahinne gives Aya a last look and hops back to her father-

    -Meanwhile, Ichtacya and Sol go sit besides Tagg-

    Sol: <Say, how are Ana and Bohr doing?>

    They're...alright to my understanding. Hopefully they don't end up creating a meltdown over at PEFE HQ one of these days.

    Sol: -happily- <Oh I guess they won’t~>

    -Tilts head-

    <Do you think they’d be interested in going to the university like I am?>

    Doc: <University? I've never heard of a mon going to one of those.>

    '''Silent: -Gets closer and pats Sol- She’s interested in pursuing a degree. In that place humans go to learn a lot, pretty much in the same way Gym Trainer’s Pokémon train a lot, Doc.

    -Roc meanwhile nuzzles Shahinne and tells her to behave and to keep an eye on the bag of souls-

    Doc: <Uh huh...>

    Silent: -Shifts on his seat and turns to Tagg- Well, I guess we are going on our ways then.

    -Sarcastically-

    You could send me Helios for a couple of months now.

    -Smirks-

    I'll see what I can do.

    Silent: -Lets out a "hmmm" then calls Shahinne in. The smaller bird cautiously and somewhat sadly hops her way up to Silent's bench- Okay then. Be seeing you sometime soon, I guess.

    Ichtacya: -Barks happily- <Have fun!>

    Sol: -Flatly- <We should go explore more catacombs with puzzles one of these days...>

    -Roc flies over unto my shoulder-

    Yeah, maybe some other time. See you guys.

    -Shahinne trills a sad note as Roc and I depart-
