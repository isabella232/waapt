    447627 PippingFool3rd Mar 2015 03:41:16 PM from A BEAUTIFUL DUWANG Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I get a feeling so complicated...
    A Bad Egg

    Pueltown, Almia

    A bunch of whizzes and whirrs emerge from a particular hardware store in the Pueltown Arcade plaza. Inside, two employees examine a machine made by the youngest employee.

    Employee #1: So, what's this new contraption 'ere Lex?

    Employee #2: Is it going to blow up in our face, again?

    Lex: Oh, no no no! It's just something I've been working on for a with the spare cash and parts I've saved over the last few months~!

    Employee #1: So, what does it do?

    Lex: It a Meal Printer~

    Employee #2: ...Pardon?

    Lex: Like a 3-D Printer, but for food~

    Employee #1: You developed a machine that... prints food?

    Lex: Yep! Think of all the practical applications for such a device~! Well, actually, I know the practical applications. They're dime a dozen where I come from, I thought it would be interesting to try developing one with the more basic tech available-

    The two employees laugh.

    Employee #1: Oh Lex, you big dreamer you~

    Employee #2: You need to get that head of yours out of the clouds.

    Lex: But... I haven't even demonstrated it yet...

    Employee #1: Lex, by the sounds that thing is making, it's not going to work.

    Employee #2: Besides, the last machine you "demonstrated" for us blew up in our face.

    Lex: H-Hey! I worked the kinks out of that one! It was just some faulty wires! It does works...

    The first employee gives a hearty slap to the back.

    Employee #1: C'mon now. We're on break, let's not waste it on meaningless tat.

    Employee #2: Wanna go get coffee?

    Employee #1: Sure. Coming Lex?

    Lex looks down somewhat dishearteningly at his invention.

    Lex: Nah, I'll get one later...

    Employee #1: Suit yourself.

    The two leave Lex in the shop alone, Lex sighs as he slumps over the machine.

    Lex: Darned Philistines...

    He looks up with a rather sad look.

    Lex: I wish there was someone around here who actually appreciated my inventions... like Salvador... or Lucrece... or Phos-

    ???: That invention of yours looks like quite the contraption.

    Lex: -Suddenly moving his head up- Doy?

    ???: Would you mind giving me a demonstration?

    Lex examines the woman in front of him. She is wearing a stylish, expensive looking black suit - it must have cost her a fortune, unless it was covered by company expenses. A pair of dark black shades rest on the bridge of her nose while her jet black hair. A quick glance at her cuffs and sunglasses frame show some sort of red insignia, though Lex can't quite decipher it at the moment.

    Lex: Oh! Sure! Any particular meal you want...?

    ???: A sandwich will do.

    Lex practically beams.

    Lex: Well! It works like so, you pick a few of the capsules up top like so. These are filled with pastes that will make the basis of your meal. Then you fiddle around with the settings and image to get the shape you want and the layer structure. Then you just...

    After fiddling around with the interface, Lex starts the machine. After a few minutes of startup whirring, the machine beings to print the lady her sandwich. Painstakingly and carefully crafting each component from scratch. After a few minutes, a "ding" rings through as the sandwhich is pushed out for consumption.

    The woman quickly picks it up, examines it, and takes a bite.

    ???: Hmm... It's quite delicious~ How unique~ You are a very talented young man.

    Lex smiles.

    Lex: Why thank you!

    ???: Say, I'm on the look out for people who show a great interest and talent in invention. You, kiddo, a certainly no slouch in that department. How would you like to work for my boss?

    Lex: Your boss?

    Lex looks around the store.

    Lex: But what about here? I kinda have a commitment by contract to this place.

    The woman waves.

    ???: We can handle that. But really, do you want to spend your time at a backwater town in the middle of nowhere surrounded by people who don't appreciate your talents?

    Or, would you rather a job where you get to invent for a living? We operate on a commission basis, the more you invent the higher your wages.

    Lex: Invent for a living? That's...

    Lex twiddles his fingers.

    Lex: You really mean it?

    The woman nods.

    ???: Yes. So... do you accept our offer?

    Lex gives a silly smile as he claps over the woman's extended hand and gives a hearty shake. The woman gives a small smile, noticing the metallic feel of his left hand

    Lex: Of course! Let me just gather my things and-

    ???: No need, we will handle that as well. We already your details, Alexandre Cain

    Lex blinks.

    Lex: O...kay.

    ???: Now, come with me.

    The woman pulls out a small device and throws it on the ground. After a few moments, three prongs jutt out and dig into the store's floor, followed by an expanding plate emitting an ominous light blue glow.

    Lex: Is that a..?

    ???: Don't worry. No-one has been telefragged yet.

    Lex gulps as he follows the young woman on to the plate, teleporting away in the blink of an eye.

    Teleportation Machine: This machine will self destruct amusingly in 3... 2... 1...

    ~*KA-BOOOOOOOOM*~
