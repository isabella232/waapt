    ETA

    Ancient Ransei, A Road

    A Messenger Corps caravan is trundling down the road, kicking up dust.

    In the lead wagon rides Eta, the leader of the Messenger Corps.

    Next to her sits her second in command, Taika.

    "So, there are bandits in these hills?"

    Taika: "Yes ma'am. They've raided at least two of the nearby villages twice in as many weeks."

    "Tsk. Well, let's put them down."

    Taika: "Yes ma'am."

    Suddenly, they come across a log in the middle of the road.

    "Ah. An ambush. Excellent."

    "Shomei, to me."

    <Shomei: "Here.">

    A Chandelure rises from the wagon bed.

    Bandit Leader: "Your money or your life!"

    "Fly the flag, will you?"

    One of the Messengers raises a flag, bearing the same stylized lantern as is depicted on the back of Eta's longcoat.

    Bandit 1: "Shit! It's the Lanterns!"

    Eta leaps off the wagon, sliding her bisento from underneath the rags with which she had disguised it at the same time.

    "Alright you sons of bitches, you've been raiding across borders. You know what that means: you're our jurisdiction now!"

    "Up and at 'em, folks!"

    The coverings on the wagons fall away, to reveal heavily armed members of the Messenger Corps.

    They leap into battle. Blasts of moves fly across the road as both sides clash.

    Bandit Leader: "Dammit!"

    He draws an abnormally large chain sickle.

    "Here we go, Shomei!"

    She begins concentrating her energy in tandem with her Chandelure.

    "HAAAAAAAAAAAAH! Weapon Draw!"

    Her bisento doubles in size, catching fire as well.

    "RAAAARGH!"

    She pounds across the battlefield, charging the enemy warlord.

    Leaping high in the air, Eta swings down at him.

    "Go! FLAME SLASH!"

    Fire arcs out from her blade, scorching her target.

    While Shomei clashes with his Hydreigon, she continues to fight the bandit leader.

    Quickly, she spins her bisento around to block one of the thrown sickles.

    "Not today, bastard."

    She falls, blade pointed straight down.

    "OOOOOOORYYYYAAAAAA!"

    She slashes right through the chain, continuing on to hit the ground.

    Swinging the butt of her bisento, she knocks out the enemy warlord.

    Standing, Eta looks around her, as her forces finish disabling and disarming the bandits.

    "Good work, people! Round them up and tie them up! We're taking them to the nearest capital to be judged."

    Her bisento shrinks back to its normal, if still respectively large, size, in order for it to be more easily stored. The fires also go out. She sheathes it on her back, and throws the bandit leader over her shoulder.

    "Let's roll in ten!"

    She lugs the unconscious body over to her wagon, then ties him up.

    "Ahhh. That was fun. Much better than paperwork."

    Taika: "I'm sure, ma'am."

    "Are you snarking at me?"

    Taika: "Never, ma'am."

    "Good."

    They both hold serious faces for half a second, before bursting into laughter.

    "Nope, couldn't keep a straight face."

    Taika: "Heh heh."

    All of the bandits restrained and loaded onto the wagons, they set out for [Schrodinger's Capital].
