    [Looking back to Riptide] <...I don't know. I'm rambling, aren't I?>

    Riptide: <Nah.>

    Vesuvius: <On top of that, I'm a fire type. I'm sure you can understand why I might be a little apprehensive about the ocean.>

    Riptide: <...Oh, yeah. I guess that was a kinda dumb question.>

    Vesuvius: [Shakes her head] <It wasn't that dumb. I'm sure plenty of fighting type Pokémon are perfectly comfortable around birds, for instance. And I've never seen you tense up when in close proximity to grass, or... I don't know... lettuce.>

    [Riptide snickers, and Vesuvius can't help but laugh herself.]

    Riptide: <Not quite the same.>

    Vesuvius: <No, it really isn't.>

    [The pair hear the muffled sounds of conversation coming from the cave mouth, and a few moments later, notice Lustrix speeding by overhead. Riptide turns to Vesuvius, who shrugs.]

    Vesuvius: <With all that aside, I can see how one might find it calming. There's a tranquil kind of beauty to it.>

    [Riptide nods. A hint of a grin washes over him for a moment, and he moves.]

    Vesuvius: <Are you going to say something to the effect of "It's even more beautiful up close" and push me in?>

    Riptide: [Withdraws his arm] <No!>
    [Pause]
    <...The thought had crossed my mind.>
