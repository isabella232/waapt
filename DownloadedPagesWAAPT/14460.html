    They then air-surfed towards the Kabutops, Locke riding on the side of Mirror, at breathtaking speed, Mirror being surrounded by yellow, the sign of a Heavy Slam.

    The Kabutops flew a bit, but was then picked up into the air by a Hawlucha, and promptly Flying Pressed. It fell to the ground,knocked out. Locke landed in a three point pose, and, longcoat billowing in the wind, turned to Geo and Denise, grinned, and said:"So..who missed me?"
