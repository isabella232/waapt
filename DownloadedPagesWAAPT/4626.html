    Interviewer #1: "What is your overall opinion on the challenges across the archipelago?"

    Silent: "Well, they are original. But a bit too constrained despite the setting, I mean, I guess most Trainers would want to travel from island to island by ferry."

    Interviewer #2: "Why a semi-NFE team?"

    Silent: "Well, this is my second run as a Trainer, I decided that it would be good if my partners got some training to help me on the field. You never really know when you've made a nearby Tyranitar angry, after all..."

    Interviewer #3: "Still, isn't kinda dickish to send a Starly against our famed Dragonite?"

    Maekrite mini-Inka Stares™ the Interviewer into meeping.

    Silent: "Not at all. She was actually the best option I had, since she is a very good Gap Prober and she can be very good when it comes to being annoying."

    Interviewer #2: "Well she seems to be able to Peck very fast... how good is she?"

    Silent: "Well, I haven't conducted hard research on it but I'm sure she can easily clock over 40 pecks per minute."

    Interviewer #1: "How does it feel to earn the Winner's Trophy?"

    Silent: "Pretty good, considering that back at the mainland, all you get for beating the Elite Four and the Champion is a lil'star and a simple color change of your Trainer Card... plus, Shahinne, my Starly, seems to like it."
