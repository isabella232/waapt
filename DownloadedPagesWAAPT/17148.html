    A Heliolisk with her eyes shut.

    Slowly, she opened them again, blinking a few times as she gazed up at everyone else.

    Izzy: <...Is it over? Did it work?>

    Izzy: <Ah!>

    <WOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOO!!!!>

    She ran a fair distance away, then back again, stopping just a few feet away from the group as she flared her frill, sparks of electricity cracking and dancing across it.
