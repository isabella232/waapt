    Not with Shahinne

    "Aaaawwwwww, thanks." Silent hugs the Miltank.

    With Shahinne

    The lil'bird is tidying a bed of loose [grayish-feathers] while Copahue and Harriet are arranging some sticks and branches for a training session on Wing Attack. Harriet looks like she hasn't sleep that well during the last few days, but she calms down after looking at Shahinne as the Starly completes her bed.

    "<There!>" the Starly announces proudly as she hops into the feather bed and looks at her adoptive parents.

    "<That is a very nice bed, Shahinne,>" answer Harriet, patting the Starly's head, "<now you have to keep it like that.>"

    Copahue yawns, then motions to Harriet for the two to go outside and catch some fish. Right as he does that though, something hits the rooftop of the birdhouse and starts walking over there.

    "<Oh? W-what was that?>" asks Copahue. "<Sounds large...>"

    Suddenly a large shadow covers the entrance and a second later Roc lands there, looking inside.

    "<eeeeeep~>" Copahue gets intimidated and hides behind Harriet, the Honchkrow reprimanding him with a leer. "<Uhm... sorry, sorry...>"

    "<What are you doing here, Roc?>" Harriet asks nervously, giving the Staraptor some room for him to come inside. "<... Is that a [fish]?>"

    Shahinne sees her father and flies up to his chest, trilling. "<Daaaaaad! You came visit!>"

    Roc stays immobile for a moment then sets the fish aside as Shahinne hugs him. "<Got here from the Lake of Rage because Silent and the others were fishing. How has she behaved?>" he asks Copahue and Harriet.

    The two birds give a nod and point to the different stuff around the house. "<Uhm... she's more helpful now. Also collects some berries.>" answers Harriet.

    "<We are still going slowly in teaching her attacks...>" adds Copahue, retreating further behind Harriet. "<Still stuck with Endeavour, actually...>"

    Roc assents and then sets Shahinne down. While the little Starly hops around talking about all the stuff she does offscreen every day, Roc inspects the birdhouse with Shahinne following eagerly; the Staraptor's eyes set on the down bed, noticing the particular size and coloration of the feathers and the shortness of their calamus. "<You OK?>" he asks Harriet, glaring at her.

    "<Uhm... I... I'm fine... really.>" Harriet avoids Roc's gaze for a moment.

    Roc glares at her more deeply, is about to open his beak and comment on something about Harriet's nervousness and some minute bruises hidden under her feathers, but decides to not do it in front of Copahue and Shahinne. He simply lets out a "<Take care>" and holding the fish up calls Shahinne.

    "<Oh! Fish! Gimme!> :<"

    Roc stares her down for a moment, the Starly not really getting it. "Shahinne, don't," he says, prompting the Starly to sit and blink.

    "<B-but...>"

    "<Shahinne, I brought this fish to you as a [gift]. That means I give this to you because I want to, not because you want to. You understand that, right?>"

    Shahinne tilts her head for a moment, thinking about something. "<...Like for Xmas?>"

    "<Yeah, like for Xmas.>"

    Shahinne rests on the ground and nods, now her eyes focused on her father.

    "<This fish is yours because I like how you have behaved.>" Hands over the fish. "<It is not a reward.>"

    Shahinne drags the fish to her makeshift bed and starts pecking at it slowly to catch a bit of its taste. She trills and Roc steps in to nuzzle her for a moment.

    "<This is a new kind of fish!>" Shahinne says excitedly.

    After a moment, Roc turns around to notice Copahue and Harriet are busying themselves in other things. He carefully covers Shahinne with his body and gets a hold of her.

    "<[-...eeee! Playtime!>" — Shahinne struggles to get free.

    "<Shahinne...>" Roc pins Shahinne down carefully, waiting for her to calm down. "<[No], Shahinne. I want you to listen to me.>"

    Shahinne stops struggling and stares at her father intently. Only after Roc senses she has calmed down the Starly is let free to be put on the nest. Roc picks one of the feathers and examines it cautiously.

    "<You found these lying around the birdhouse, right Shahinne?>"

    "<Yes! They are very pretty and warm!> — nests on the feathers.

    Roc places the feather before Shahinne and stares at her for a moment, calling Shahinne's attention.

    "<You do well helping your parents in the house, my girl... Shahinne, you remember what Maekrite and Ho-Oh told you, right?>" — narrows eyes as Shahinne blinks at him — "<About teammates.>"

    "<Big beautiful bird, yes!>" Shahinne trills, puffs her chest. "<I remember! Fight together, big Nidorino said.>"

    Roc gives Shahinne a soft peck and then shows her the feathers again.

    "<Take [care] of them outside the house too.>"

    Shahinne stares at the feather, holds it in her beak trying to understand. Then she looks at her father looking for an explanation, but she gets none. After Roc stares at her for a long while, Shahinne settles for setting the feather down and assenting to her father.

    With that Roc brushes Shahinne's head with his wing, then turns around. He gives a goodbye to Copahue and Harriet (staring again at the Honchkrow for a while).

    "<Are you coming to visit again, Roc?>" asks Copahue, now more calm.

    "<I don't think so... you are doing well for the moment.>"

    Harriet mutters a thanks, then the two birds see Roc step outside and take off, to return to Mahogany.

    Shahinne meanwhile simply stares at her adoptive parents and the exit, then picks the feather and returns it to the nest to line it up. Only after she is done she decides to taste the fish.
