    Constantin twitches his nose in irritation.

    Constantin: So you must be one of Mercedes "friends". I know who you really are. Be thankful I'm not turning you all in on the spot.

    Benz: You worry too much about us~ But the effort's appreciated. Can I offer my    help    for anything?

    Constantin: Help? I know of your "help" and I wish not to have any of it.

    ...

    You were the one who changed the faucets, weren't you?

    Thanks for the heads-up! I owe ya one. Are you suuuuure you don't need my help? :3
