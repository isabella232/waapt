    One Quarter of a Dollar

    Violet City, Johto

    Carol had been thinking for days.

    The news on the TV in the Pokémon Center was strange, and no one was sure what to believe. It had been reported that some sort of airship had been observed in Celadon City, Kanto. Carol wasn't sure if this was true, of course, as no reporter in Johto had seen it for themselves—some person had sent in a photograph. For all anyone knew, it was all just a hoax.

    Since the report had aired, Carol had been contemplating on making the journey to Kanto. A preposterous idea at first glance—from Violet to Celadon wasn't exactly a walk down the street. But Carol had her Fearow, Seven. She hadn't done much since she left New Bark, anyways. She had traveled around, up to Goldenrod City, caught some Pokémon along the way, then traveled back. She had battled a few people, evolved the mons, and had now made her way back to Violet. She wasn't the newbie she had been several months ago.

    Carol decided she'd try to get to Celadon. She walked solemnly out the door, released Seven from his ball, and gave him a pep talk. "Seven, you can fly, and you're decenty strong. I need to get to Celadon—can you manage?" The Fearow blinked, preened his feathers, and gave a small nod. Seven was silent. Seven was arrogant.

    Several minutes later, a girl was reportedly seen flying on a bird Pokémon into the sky.
