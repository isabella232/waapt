    475765 FullMoon12th Nov 2015 05:18:25 AM from Snowdin Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Squeeeeeeeeeeeee!
    you really like hot animals don't you?

    Ever Grande City

    Those in the J-Team might find a video trending around social media regarding the J-Team

    Video

    "Are you really going to record this?" asked Lyuri

    Lyuri and Duke are sitting on a chair each, in front of a camera, both holding notebooks and a pen.

    "I thought this could be a fun little game that I could share the results of, come on, it's all pretty harmless." said Sakura, from behind the camera

    "So, what's this game about?" asked Duke

    "It's a game to see if which of you knows the other the best. So I'm going to ask one of you a question and you have to write down what you think is the answer while the other writes down the response at the same time and obviously you can't see it. Once we see if you got it right, we invert the roles for the same question." Sakura explained

    "Oh, sounds simple enough." said Duke, with a smile

    "And I'm guessing we'll be showing you what we wrote." said Lyuri

    "Right! So, ready?" asked Sakura

    "Go for it." they both answered

    "Okay, so, you'll guess first, Duke.What's your crush's favorite pizza topping?" she said as the subtitles appeared in the video for clarity.

    "All right." he began to write it down

    And with that both wrote it down, Lyuri writing the true answer while Duke was making his guess, they quickly finished.

    "So, what is it?"

    They both flip their notebooks "Chocolate." they said in unison as both notebooks had that written on them.

    "Chocolate pizza? Really." questioned Sakura

    "Hey, it's a pretty good dessert." said Lyuri, placing a hand on her hip.

    "It tastes better than I thought it would." said Duke, chuckling.

    "Well then. Now Lyuri, you try to guess Duke's." said Sakura

    They both nodded and wrote their answers down.

    "Pepperoni." they both answered, then exchanging a smile with each other.

    "Honestly though, I'm fine with most of them." said Duke

    "Especially if there's meat in it." added Lyuri, with a chuckle.

    "All right! So, next up in the same order... What's your crush's biggest fear?"

    Lyuri sighed and began writing her question down while Duke took a few seconds to think about it before beginning to write it down.

    "You know pretty well what my answer is going to be, Sakura." she rolled her eyes

    Sakura just giggled and then noticed Duke nodding at her to confirm that he's done. They then both flip their notebooks.

    "Heights." they both said aloud

    "Though not as much as I used to." said Lyuri

    "Going inside an airplane with you is always a fun experience." Duke chuckled

    Sakura giggled "Now, you do Duke's."

    "Well, this is easy." said Lyuri as she began writing down

    And soon both of them flipped the notebooks to show it saying "Hurting others"

    "I would feel really bad if I hurt anybody, physically or emotionally." said Duke

    "He's the team's huge teddy bear." joked Lyuri

    Sakura giggled "Third question... What chore would your crush hate to do the most??"

    "Well, I bet we're both going to get this wrong since we don't really do any chores but..." said Lyuri as she began to think of something.

    After spending five seconds thinking about it both began writing something down.

    "All right, what is it?"

    "Laundry." said both of them as they flipped their notebooks.

    Lyuri turned to Duke with a very surprised look "How did you get that?!"

    "Laundry can take a while since you have to wait for the washing machine and all. You're a lot of things but patient is not one of those." Duke chuckled.

    "Wow, you know me better than I thought." said Lyuri, looking quite impressed.

    "Let's keep going, guys." said Sakura

    They went back to thinking again but soon Duke began writing his answer down while Lyuri was still thinking, it took a few more seconds before she could come up with something.

    "All right. And..."

    "Washing dishes." said Lyuri, showing her answer

    "Vacuuming." said Duke, showing his different answer

    "Wait, why?" asked Lyuri, raising an eyebrow

    "Those things can get pretty noisy." Duke sighed "Washing dishes is fine, it just takes a little bit. I can deal with it."

    Lyuri's expression changes to one of realization "Ooooh. How did I not think of that?"

    "Well, looks like Duke has a lead now. Anyway, fourth question... If you became a billionaire, what would your crush do with the money?"

    "That's tough." said Duke, wincing a little

    "Actually I'm pretty sure I know." Lyuri smiled

    Lyuri immediately wrote something down while Duke took quite a while to do it.

    "All right, so... Comfort at home." said Duke

    "Charity" said Lyuri, smirking and noticing Duke giving her a curious look "I'm not a girl who needs a lot of stuff. A billion is a lot so I figure I would do something useful with it while making sure I can still live a comfortable middle class life."

    "Uh... Wow." said Sakura, sounding a bit surprised herself "... Anyway!"

    They then wrote the next part down, Lyuri quite confident in her answer

    "Opening a dojo." they answered in unison.

    "We talked about this a lot before." said Duke, smiling

    "Yeah there was little room for doubt there." said Lyuri

    "Well, that ties things up. So let's for the last one: What's your crush's favorite pastime?"

    "That's easier." said Duke.

    "Oh come on..." Lyuri sighed

    They both wrote something down while Lyuri was looking pretty bummed out by it.

    "Drawing." they both said as they flipped the notebooks.

    "Looks like I won't be losing this one." Duke chuckled.

    "This is an easy question to end on." criticized Lyuri.

    "No matter. Let's just finish it now." said Sakura

    And so they began writing down for one last time.

    "Working out." said Lyuri, looking bored

    "Reading comic books." said Duke

    Lyuri's eyes widened up "What?!"

    "Oooh. Looks like Duke won this one." Sakura giggled

    "You're kidding. You did that on purpose to trip me up." Lyuri accused

    "You can tell if I was lying or not can't you?" asked Duke, calmly

    "... Yeah, and you weren't lying but... Come on!" said Lyuri, quite upset

    Sakura giggled some more "Better end this now before Lyuri gets a little too salty about this..."

    She then ended the recording.
