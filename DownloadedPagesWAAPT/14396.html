    Wobble...

    Wobble...

    Click!

    -Ealsh is running around in circles. Lanie had been sleeping, but Ealsh has woken her up.-

    Ealsh: <Nightnightnightnight, I love the night~!>

    -Lanie yawns.-

    Lanie: Huh...?

    Ealsh: <Isn't it great? The stars. The moon. THE EVERYTHING! Night is wonderful!>

    Lanie: Ah... yeah...

    Ealsh: <That's why I take naps all the time during the day! I don't want to miss a second of the niiiiiiiight~!>

    -He continues running in circles and talking about night as Lanie tries to fall back asleep.-

    Route 22, the next day

    -Lanie has been training with Ealsh for a while.-

    Lanie: That's strange... don't Scatterbug usually evolve faster than this...?

    Ealsh: <...I won't evolve unless it's night. The moon will grant me its power.>

    Lanie: Er... right...

    Route 6, not too many days after the previous section

    -Lanie is walking back from visiting Parfum Palace when she notices that an Oddish is following her. She turns around and kneels down, looking at the Oddish.-

    Oddish: <Hello!>

    Lanie: Hi!

    -The Oddish stares, wide-eyed.-

    Oddish: <You're the first person all day to reply! That's awesome!>

    Lanie: Huh? Ah, right, most people don't...

    Oddish: <I'll keep following you, then! My adventure will continue!>

    Lanie: Hang on...

    -She throws a Poké Ball at the Oddish.-

    Wobble...

    Wobble...

    Wobble...

    Click!

    -She lets the Oddish out.-

    Oddish: <Hi again!>

    Lanie: ...Hm... How does the name Misery sound?

    Oddish: <Hmm...>

    Lanie: Come to think of it, there's already a Misery, and that name isn't remotely fitting, anyw—

    Misery: <No! That name is perfect! Do not change it, please!>

    Lanie: Oh. Okay, then, Misery!

    -She smiles. Misery smiles back.-

    Misery: <Yay! Let's go!>

    -She starts walking behind Lanie again.-

    Route 7, later that same day

    -Lanie is battling a Ducklett with Misery. The Ducklett is very weak-looking.-

    Misery: <Aw, poor ducky... Here! I'll make it feel better!>

    -Misery is leaving the Pokémon Center when she notices Ealsh.-

    Misery: <Hello, Ealsh!>

    -Ealsh says nothing.-

    Misery: <Ah... I'm—>

    Ealsh: <Sure.>

    Misery: <Yay!>

    -She leaves the building, and Ealsh follows.-

    Ealsh: <...So, where are we going?>

    Misery: <Hm... For today's adventure... I think I'm going to take three circles around the whole city.>

    Ealsh: <Oh?>

    Misery: <Yeah! I can't wait! I wonder what'll happen! This will be fun!>

    -The two walk off to take three laps around the city.-

    Lumiose City, a few nights ago

    -Misery is sleeping. Ealsh, awake as usual, nudges her. She opens her eyes and yawns.-

    Misery: <Hello, Ealsh.>

    Ealsh: <Hey, Misery... Do you want to go on... an adventure?>

    -Misery jumps to her feet and smiles.-

    Misery: <Of course! Where to?>

    Ealsh: <You'll see. Just follow me!>

    -The two of them leave the building and start walking through the city. The walk through a gate to exit the city.-

    Misery: <Oh? We're leaving?>

    Ealsh: <There's too much light in a city at night. We're going somewhere else.>

    -They walk a while more, and end up on a hill.-

    Ealsh: <Here we are.>

    Misery: <Ooh, I know of a hill that's closer to the city! It's even taller, too! Here, I'll show—>

    Ealsh: <No, not just the hill! Look up!>

    -Misery looks up, and sees the glistening night sky.-

    Misery: <...Wow...>

    -Misery lies down on her back.-

    Misery: <It's beautiful...>

    Ealsh: <I wanted to show you why I love the night so much. I thought this would be a good way to do that.>

    Misery: <Yeah... It's... amazing...>

    -The two watch the stars together for a while more. When Misery falls asleep again, Ealsh picks her up and carries her back to the city.-
