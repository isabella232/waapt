    From: Ren

    Just to let you know the Johto Grand Festival has been rescheduled, although the date has yet to be set. I don't regret my choice to become a were mon, but if you want me to, I would be happy to give it up if we ran into Coyote - regardless of the price I would have to pay to have him reapply and remove the spell as needed. Our relationship means that much to me.
