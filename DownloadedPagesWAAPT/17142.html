    To: Ever

    As Luke hit "send", Lake and Izzy were lying on the grass nearby, basking in the warm sun.

    Izzy: <So I think it's about time I ask: does Luke have only four Pokémon? Or do you have others somewhere?>

    Lake turned his head to look at his friend, smiling.

    Lake: <Oh, he's got loads of Pokémon! We're just the ones with him right now.>

    Izzy: <Uh-huh. And how many is "loads?">

    The Feraligatr gazed up as he counted them all in his head.

    Lake: <At least twenty.>

    This came as a shock to the Helioptile.

    Izzy: <Twenty?! Most trainer's I've seen barely have time for six! How does he cope?>

    Lake: <He just let's us do whatever whenever he doesn't need us for anything important. Of course, since I'm his best friend->

    Izzy: <I thought I was your best friend?>

    Lake: <Best human friend, we do nearly everything together. I think I can count on the fingers off one hand that I otherwise wasn't on his active team.>

    Izzy considered giving the Feraligatr a small electric shock, but decided just to nudge his nose playfully instead.

    Izzy: <You're lucky I'm not the jealous sort!>

    Lake: <You're joking! You are so the jealous sort!>

    The little lizard smirked.

    Izzy: <You're right. I just can't be bothered to shock ya'.>

    With a toothy smirk, the Feraligatr chuckled.
