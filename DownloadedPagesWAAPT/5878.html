    Li'l Punk

    That route between Veilstone and the lakefront place

    Ashley, Domino, Ion, James, and Cole are wandering around, doing nothing of importance.

    <Domino, I'm just saying, you gotta step up your game. I mean, losing to that Kricketune? Pathetic.> James gives the Fearow a shove on the shoulder, and he staggers to the side, ducking his head.

    <James, don't...> Ion says, putting a hand on Domino's shoulder.

    <You're not still going on about that dumb battle, are you?> Cole asks, swooping down and flying low overhead.

    <What? I haven't done anything since!> James snaps, taking a half-hearted swing at the Pidgeot. <Just because you lost to that Jerk Ass Luxio—>

    <Shut up,> Cole snaps, Wing Attacking the Heatmor in the back of the head.

    <Oh, you did not—> James stops short and lights his fists.

    "Hey! You two, knock it off," Ashley says, stepping between the two.

    <My, my, aren't we a dysfunctional bunch,> a highly amused voice sounds from behind a bush. Ion nervously turns to face it, and James and Cole look up from each other.

    A Houndoom steps around the bush and looks at the group like a highschooler might look at a preschooler acting stupid. <Don't tell me your actually on the same team.>

    Ashley steps around James, eyes lighting up. "A Houndoom! Those things are awesome!"

    The Houndoom glances at her briefly, then turns back to the Pokemon. <Where's your team leader? Stayed at home, I presume?>

    <I'm team leader,> Cole says indignantly, landing beside Ashley. <What do you want?>

    <Oh, you can't be serious,> the Houndoom says, chuckling a little to herself and sitting down neatly. <Surely you could do better than that. As for my motives, I heard the sounds of a scuffle and wondered if there was a problem.>

    <Yeah, there's a problem! You!> James snarls, taking a step towards her.

    <Oh, don't start a fight, James,> Ion pleads, putting a hand on his shoulder. The Heatmor smacks it off.

    <Back off, Ion. Just because your a coward doesn't mean—>

    <Enough!> the Houndoom barks and snaps to her feet. <You may be angry with me, but insulting a team mate is deplorable. Apologize.>

    <Oh, you've got to be kidding me, lady.> James lights his fists again and starts advancing.

    "James, I don't think you're exactly the right choice for this battle," Ashley says, grabbing his arm. "Let Ion take care of it."

    <Wait, what? Me?> Ion hesitates, taking a step backwards. James glares at the Houndoom for a second, who continues to look amused, but backs down at his Trainers assistance.

    "There'll be other battles. Ion, c'mon, let's do this!" Ashley motions for her other Pokemon to move back as Ion nervously steps forward to stand across from the Houndoom.

    <Relax. I do my best not to injure my opponents,> the Houndoom says, crouching down in preparation.

    <Uh, sure... I'll, uh, do the same,> Ion stammers, taking up a battle stance.

    BATTLE! ION vs. Wild HOUNDOOM!
    Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
