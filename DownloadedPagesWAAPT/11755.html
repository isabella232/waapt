    If you're reading this letter you are now in the possession of a Class A illegal device commonly known as a Snag Machine. Normally I wouldn't use such things, but desperate times call for desperate measures. A Snag Machine overwrites the ID of a captured Pokémon with your own, making them catchable as if they were wild, though as I'm sure you realize that this ability doesn't equal Master Ball generator. These particular models have been modified to only work on Shadow Pokemon, and are biolocked as soon as you put one on to prevent theives from using them. Hell, I even had the R&D guys put in a self-destruct sequence on the things if they're gone from your arm long enough.

    On the not so illegal side is this Aura Reader, which allow those who wear them to see the Auras of Pokémon like an Auric can. Shadow Pokémon have a noted purple aura, which your Reader will automatically pick up.
