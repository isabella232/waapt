    Constantin: A place like this is the last place someone would look for people like us.

    Odin: Excellent point, cousin.

    Constantin: Now, down to the nitty gritty business. Though I assume you already know what I am going to ask of you, oui?

    Odin: Remind me?

    Constantin: Ah, so it may have escaped you. Let me reiterate.

    Constantin pulls out an old Team Flare badge and twirls it idly within his gloved fingers, allowing it to reflect small fragments of moonlight filtering down from the weathered ceiling.

    Constantin: I humbly request if you would like to join my cause. Nepotism states that you will be given a cushy position as admin, and free reign on holding whatever experiments your little heart desires. So will you take me up on my offer? I am lacking in the experience department so far...

    Odin: Oh yes, this offer! Of course I will, you can give me exactly the resources I need! With your backup the Arika girl is as good as mine!

    Constantin: Then it's a done deal~ Though I must warn you that my relationship with family per see is... tumultuous at best. We seem to be of kindred spirits, so I would hate to have you end up facing the barrels of a sawn-off shotgun. Also, you will be sharing the rank of Admin with one Maria von Boehm. A skilled fighter, if overly touchy-feely and affectionate for my tastes.

    Odin: I can deal with her. Though I'll avoid your family at all costs.

    Constantin: No need. They've been dead for over a decade now. One for over two decades. Regardless...

    Constantin holds out his hand.

    Constantin: I can see this as the start of a beautiful partnership, dear cousin of mine.

    -Odin grins and shakes-

    Odin: A beautiful one indeed.

    Constantin himself gives off a sinister smirk as he looks around the ruins of the building he and Odin are currently standing in.

    Constantin: A few years back, when the original Flare was in in developing stages, I was sent here for scrouging all I could about the history of this place in particular, and how it related to the history of both the company and Hoenn as a whole. It was of particular interest to us because they apparently had devices that resembled part of the Devastating Machine used in my home region 3,000 years ago...

    Constantin places a curled finger on his lip.

    Constantin: It proves that time is of the essence if we are to make things work. Otherwise bases will be nothing more than the hollow wreck we are standing in and hard work will be all for naught.

    Constantin looks back to Odin with a more serious glare.

    Constantin: Hence why these plans of ours need to be a hidden as possible. The issue with Flare and those like it was that it was too open, too noticeable. We don't want to drag unwanted attention our way, especially not of those J-team rags and their violent, unstable leader.

    Odin: Hmm.., I will admit, subtlety is not always my strong point, but if it helps our goals it's all but necessary.

    Constantin: Glad we can come to an agreement about this...

    Constantin glances around before glancing back to Odin.

    Constantin: Let's adjourn. This place brings up unpleasant memories due to it's... similarities... to a place back home...

    Odin: Eh? Alright...

    -They leave-
