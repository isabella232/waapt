    347919 Herbert40k5th Feb 2014 03:59:39 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
    Imperial Commissar

    Cyllage City, Leonard's Apartment - Two nights ago

    -Having accepted Leonard's offer of a chat behind closed doors, Herbert follows his raven-haired relative into a rather lavishly decorated apartment.-

    Herbert: ...Wow, this place looks fancy. You live here?

    Leonard: Not most of the time, no. I'm only staying here for a few weeks because my rather needs me to supervise a business deal.

    Herbert: Wait, so you're saying that you own more than one apartment like this?

    Leonard: One in every major city in Kalos. Why?

    Herbert: ...Ever heard the phrase "idle rich" before?

    Leonard: Yes, and I'd rather you refrained from using it.

    -Before Herbert can make another remark, he is interrupted by a girl about two or three years his senior coming out of the kitchen with a Glaceon in her arms.-

    Lucille: Hey, Leo, mind if I crash here for another night? I kinda need to... wait, did you collect a stray on the way back or something?

    Glaceon: <Stray! Stray!>

    -Herbert looks vaguely peeved for a moment.-

    Herbert: The name's He... Lucius. I'm a Cain, so I guess you can think of this as a family visit of sorts.

    -Lucille groans.-

    Lucille: Don't talk to me about family visits...

    Leonard: No need to worry, he's only a distant relative. From Sinnoh, apparently. I decided that he and I ought to have a quick chat, since foreign contacts always come in handy.

    Lucille: Oh, that's a relief.

    -She extends a hand to Herbert, still cradling the Glaceon with her other arm.-

    Lucille: I'm Lucille. The Glaceon's Hail - don't mind him, he's just a kid. You've probably already met my brother, assuming he didn't abduct you or something.

    Glaceon Hail: <Abduct! Abduct!>

    Herbert: Hmph. I'd like to see him try.

    -Lucille chuckles, and this time it's Leonard's turn to look slightly peeved.-

    Leonard: If you don't mind, I'll be stepping outside for a moment in order to attend to some business.

    Lucille: That's his way of telling you that he's going to take a dump, by the way.

    -Leonard glares at Lucille, then sighs and disappears into the bathroom. Lucille smirks, sitting down on a sofa in front of a rather weirded-out Herbert.-

    Lucille: Say... you're that one kid from that TV show, aren't you? The Auric one.

    Hail: <Auric! Auric!>

    Herbert: ...You're not a fan, are you?

    Lucille: Nah, but the kids I babysit watch it every so often, so I've seen a couple of episodes. It's pretty amusing if you don't take it seriously.

    Herbert: That's what I try to do whenever it comes up. Doesn't usually work, so I tend to avoid the subject.

    Lucille: Right. I see.

    -She goes quiet for a while, now looking vaguely pensive rather than vaguely mischievous. There is silence for a while until Herbert decides to break it.-

    Herbert: Soooo... nice place you got here.

    Lucille: You think so? I hate it.

    Herbert: ...Really? Why? This place looks like it cost more than most people would make in their entire lives.

    -Lucille sighs.-

    Lucille: That's the point. Kalos is the kinda place where you can work your ass off for years and never even come close to living in a place like this, whilst me and Leonard get it without having done a thing to earn it. Why? Because seven centuries ago some pompous prat decided that he was top dog due to wearing a slightly fancier hat than everybody else. That's what it boils down to, really.

    -She rather suddenly stands up and starts pacing up and down, causing Hail to hop out of her arms and sit down on the table.-

    Lucille: I mean, it's not like I ever asked to be born to a noble family. I do my best to earn my keep. But according to dad, girls aren't cut out for business, and anything other than business isn't "befitting" of a noble. Well, screw that. It'd be nice to see him brought down to everybody else's level for a change, rather than-

    -She pauses mid-rant when she realises that she is receiving strange looks from Herbert, Hail, and a Scizor stood in the doorway.-

    Bishop: <...Did I choose a bad time to interrupt?>

    Herbert: ...Actually, I'd say you chose a pretty good time. Bishop, this is Lucille, Leonard's sister. Lucille, this is Bishop, my Scizor.

    -Lucille cocks an eyebrow.-

    Lucille: ...You two can talk to each other?

    Herbert: Yeah. It's kind of a long story.

    Lucille: Huh. Interesting...

    -She trails off, before looking over at the door to the kitchen.-

    Lucille: ...I'm gonna go get myself a coffee. Do you want some?

    Herbert: Uh... yeah, sure. That'd be great.

    -Lucille nods, and heads off into the kitchen.-

    Herbert: ...Well, that was a weird way to end the conversation. She seemed alright, though. Kind of cute, even.

    Bishop: <There might be about thirty generations between you, but she's still technically your cousin, remember.>

    Herbert: Er... right. Yeah. I knew that. Just making an observation.

    Bishop: <So, how did your little family reunion go? Well, perhaps "reunion" is an inaccurate term, but you understand what I mean.>

    Herbert: It... I suppose it's going okay so far. Sure, one of my hosts is kind of an asshole and the other's a bit weird, but for Cains they seem surprisingly well-adjusted. I guess it wouldn't hurt to hang around for a while...

    Cyllage City, Leonard's Apartment - Now

    -Herbert is asleep on the sofa in the main room after two days of chatting with the Cains and lazing around watching TV. Bishop is dozing next to him.-
