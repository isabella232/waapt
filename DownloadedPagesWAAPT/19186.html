    Ansien: <In fact, I'm the only one you can trust. You need to trust me. If you don't, this goes nowhere. And you don't want that, do you?>

    -Glavo still doesn't respond. Ansien taps the button again, and Glavo screams again. Ansien grins.-

    Ansien: <What is it you're trying to do here, anyway? You don't gain anything from pretending. You can't run away. We both know who you are. We both know what you did.>

    <Why won't you admit it?>

    -He pauses for a moment, then the big grin returns.-

    Ansien: <I'll just leave you alone a while. You've had enough of this—>
