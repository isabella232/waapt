    Vyvyan: Must you do that so loudly, Levi?

    Levi looks over to Vyv with big, watery eyes.

    Levi: But Vyv! These noodles are just so good!

    Vyvyan: It's low rent.

    Levi: Hah! You can't put a rent price on me, baby!

    Vyvyan rolls his eyes as Levi keeps slurping. Though soon, he finishes.

    Levi: But hey! I'm glad you and me can enjoy merry Ol Festivus together!

    Vyvyan: ...I do celebrate Christmas, Levi.

    Levi: Oh, you're Pokepanese Catholic?

    Vyvyan: ...[Irish] Catholic, Levi. [Irish].

    How do ya think I ended up with a name like Vyvyan?

    Levi: Touché.

    ...

    ...Though if you don't mind me asking, why are you spending your holidays all four here instead of with your family? I mean, Sandy spent the long stretch of November and December with his folks, but you seem content to be... Here?

    Vyvyan: Me and my folks, we haven't gotten along in a long time...

    Levi: Ah...

    Levi slings one sleeved arm around Vyvyan's neck.
