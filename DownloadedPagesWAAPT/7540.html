    Doopliss: <Perhaps I should have tried it on that Liepard I read about... That probably would've had better results...>

    Fuscia Beach

    Diane pulls Skye into a hug. Skye slips on her armband so she can return the hug properly.

    Human!Skye: See, there are good parts about this thing.

    Diane: You can see why I haven't been wearing my armband that much.

    Human!Skye: Yeah, hugs tend to be better when you're not at risk of cutting someone's limbs off.

    They both laugh.

    Diane: That said, it really wouldn't work out for you and Caliburn.

    Skye blushes and lets go of Diane

    Human!Skye: Well, I, um...

    Diane: Undeniable proof. You like him.

    Skye's blush gets even brighter.

    Human!Skye: I do not like him! Well, I like him, but I don't like him, and you don't believe a single word I'm saying, do you? ._.

    Diane: Not one bit~

    Human!Skye: You're hopeless.

    Diane: So are you.

    Human!Skye gives her a light shove. Eskay finally finds where they ended up.

    Eskay: Hey, are you two okay?

    Skye gives him a bright smile

    Human!Skye: Never been better!

    She takes the armband off.

    Skye: <All we needed was a bit of ocean water, apparently. Silly scientists, thinking everything takes some sort of special chemical!>

    She puts the armband back on

    Human!Skye: And I even got this out of it for free!

    Eskay: (Not at all concerned by her sudden extreme mood change) I'm glad you're feeling better. Come on, let's find the others.

    They go to do so, Skye getting a joyful hop in her step as she walks.
