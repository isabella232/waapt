    Girl: ...How do Seishirou -points to the Gible- and Fuuma -points to the Trapinch- sound to you two?

    -The two Pokemon look at each other and nod-

    Gible: Rawr! :3

    Trapinch: Rawr! :3

    Girl: -pets them- Glad you like them!

    Several years later

    - The girl is in Hearthome, going to the contest hall with her Gabite and Vibrava-

    Gabite!Seishirou: Aww, this is gonna be stupid...

    Vibrava!Fuuma: Come on, it might be fun! And Monica will get us burgers afterwards!

    Gabite!Seishirou: ...Alright.

    -He follows his "brother" and Moinica-

    A few years later

    - Fuuma, now a Flygon, is cuddling a baby Trapinch with his Trainer and Garchomp!Seish watching-

    Fuuma: Wow...

    Monica: -pats- Congratulations, big guy.

    Seishirou: Has she said anything yet?

    Fuuma: No... But I bet it'll be special when she does.

    Baby Trapinch: -nuzzles Fuuma-

    A few months later

    Fuuma: She ABANDONED HER?! Over what she thought was a birth defect?!

    Seishirou: I'm sorry... I knew that Trainer Monica gave your daughter to seemed shady...

    Fuuma: ...She's all alone out there... Some predator will probably get her...

    Seishirou: -pats Fuuma- There, there... Things will get better, I know...

    A few years ago

    - Monica, Seishirou, and Fuuma are touring Jublife when they see something forming on the mountain-

    Seishirou: ...

    ...Fuuma, grab Monica and fly. Now.

    Fumma: -does so-

    Seishirou: -follows-

    - They fly away, far from Sinnoh, looking back to see it swallowed by the Distortion-

    A few days later

    - Fuuma, Seishirou, and Monica have returned, and are wandering through the wasteland that Sinnoh has become-

    Fuuma: ...What happened?

    Seishirou: You tell me... Wait, where's Monica?

    -They hear a scream, and rush over to see Monica has been grabbed by ragged-looking thugs. One is pinning a knife to her chest-

    Thug 1: Give us your food! NOW!

    Monica: -sobbing- P-please, I don't have anything!

    Thug 2: Nonsense, you look too well-off to not-

    -Is promptly pounced upon and pinned by Seish-

    Thug 1: OY! Dragon!

    Seishirou: -stops, listens-

    Thug 1: Touch any of my guys and what I assume is yer trainer gets it!

    Seishirou: -growls-

    Fuuma: -strikes another thug from behind-

    Thug 1: Gah!

    - Is startled... and accidentally jabs Monica in the belly-

    Seishirou: NO!

    Fuuma: MONICA!

    - He lunges at the leader thug, who jabs his knife into Seishirou's eye in desperation-

    Seishirou: -roars in pain... then promptly rips the thug's arm off-

    Thug 1: AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!

    - The other thugs start fleeing-

    Seishirou: -growls- Don't let them get away!

    Fuuma: I wasn't planning to.

    -they pursue the thugs-

    A few minutes later

    - The two dragons return to Monica, who is mortally wounded-

    Monica: I-I'm sorry... Both of you...

    Seishirou: -puts a claw on her-

    Fuuma: -does as well-

    Monica: I-I love you both... So much...

    -she closes her eyes and grows still. -

    Seishirou: ...

    ...Fuuma? Take her to where our home was. I'll bury her.

    Fumma: Understood.

    - Fuuma picks her up and carries her off. Seishirou follows-

    AU Kanto, now

    Fuuma: ...Strange that after Monica died, we were able to raise our ranks in the Dragon clans...

    Seishirou: ...It wasn't worth it. Losing the one good human we had in this world...

    Fuuma: Nothing is...

    -they sigh-

    Fumma: Look on the bright side, though...

    -he leers at the Kanto cities in the distance-

    Soon we won't have to deal with humans any longer.
