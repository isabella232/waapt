    Geosenge

    Wesker looks at the Keystone in his hand, and sighs, feeling the three others in his jacket pocket. As always, he felt..empty. And also a bit annoyed. He had gone to all the trouble to snatch this curious object for his employer from an old lady(something that Wesker did not like), but now he comes here on his way and finds out that there is a virtual trove of these things hidden in the ruins.

    He looks at the Bisharp, and frowned. He asked the Pokemon, "So, where did the boss say next?" The Bisharp points at a location on the map it had, careful not to poke a hole through it, and Wesker just muttered," Right, right... man I wish there was something fun to do around here..." His pleas were answered by a bellowing sound of: "WESKEEEEER!!!"

    He turned and saw Locke rushing towards him, all of his Pokemon out, pure rage on his face. Wesker was puzzled at first as to who this boy was(obviously he hated him, he would have to join the queue on that one), but then a sneer crept onto his face, as he ordered his Bisharp to attack.

    Locke told Geo to use Aura Sphere on the Bisharp as it rushed towards them, but he was stopped when a Gallade teleported(with a BAMF sound) in front of him, and smashed his tonfa-arm on Geo's, causing Geo to lose control of the Aura, which then exploded in his face, as the Gallade teleported away to safety.

    "A Pokemon of deception and a Pokemon of honor, both bound to his will? Why are they not fighting each other?" Locke wondered, but then he saw the Gallade's eyes as it BAMFed away. They were blue, different from the normal red, meaning it was under Hypnosis, and then Axel directed his eyes a bit further away, where he saw a Malamar, which was likely the caster.

    He ordered Axel to attack the Bisharp again, but from the air, having Mirror use Psychic to throw him up, and then told Denise to attack the Malamar with Flare Blitz. He then used Potion on the wounded Geo, and then watched in horror as his attack went awry.

    Denise was charging at the Malamar, sheathed in flames, when she got hit with what felt like a bulldozer as the Bisharp hit her with Iron Head, throwing off her balance and causing her to hit an abandoned house beside her, while it sneered and said,"<<Not today, mutt.>>

    Axel was diving at where the Bisharp was supposed to be, his arms glowing with the power of Hammer Arm, when the Bisharp suddenly dashed away and jumped up, using Night Slash on an unsuspecting Mirror with almost blinding speed, while the Gallade BAMFed directly underneath Axel and used Sky Uppercut, cancelling out the Hammer Arm and causing a rather impressive shockwave, while the still airborne Axel found himself hit by a Dark Pulse from the Malamar, knocking him into the ground, who had smirked and said" <<Uh-uh-uh, can't have any of you still conscious. Sorry.>>"

    Weakly, Geo pointed to the back of the Gallade's head, which had a chip attached to it. Locke realized, shocked, that the chip was how the Gallade was being controlled, not Malamar.

    Wesker tutted, as he looked certainly disappointed." I get my first piece of action in so long and you get taken out this easy? Pitiful."

    Then, Locke smirked, and yelled out:" Battle Plan Theta, now!" Upon those words, each member of Locke's team hit with a long range attack; Denise used Flamethrower, Lucario used Aura Sphere, Mirror used Flash Cannon, and Axel used Hydro Pump, all directed at Wesker's team, which were now crowded in the center, while Locke himself, covered in a Barrier field, courtesy of Mirror, charged at Wesker, ready to take him down hand to hand.

    Wesker smirked in return, but his managed to seem much more creepy and menacing, and said:"Counter it."

    The Bisharp and Gallade both used sprung into action, Gallade tanking the Aura Sphere, and then, after staggering for a bit, hit Lucario with a Psycho Cut, knocking him into the poor abused house. Bisharp took its stance on the ground, and used Metal Burst when the upcoming Flash Cannon hit, shooting it at Axel, who got hit with the full force of the Flash Cannon and then some, while Mirror dropped to the ground, with unexplainable burn marks on it. Denise was starting to move forward, but she was stopped in her tracks when a Dragon Pulse hit her in the muzzle.Everyone nearly fainted, but barely hung on.

    As Locke rushed forward to hit him with a Lariat, Wesker grabbed his arm, twisted it, and then threw in a judo flip on the ground, and then held his foot with his hands, and twisted it until it popped, causing Locke to howl in pain.

    Locke's team struggled, but managed to get up, and all slowly moved forward to try and save their leader. Wesker saw this and smiled again." Did you think that was all of the team I had?"

    As he said so, each of Locke's team got hit with a Draco Meteor, from the sky. Locke weakly looked up, and saw a Salamence, roaring in triumph. It was most likely the cause of the Dragon Pulse and the burn marks on Mirror.

    "Had him out for air patrol, but I think he enjoyed that more." He held Locke up by the shoulders, patted the boy's cheek, and said, "I don't know who you are, but come back when you're stronger. You have potential, and believe me," he licked his lips, "I live to find good fights."

    The boy was crying. Every amount of effort and training he had done, up to this moment, had all come down to naught.

    He then called his Gallade, and said:" Not in the mode to kill right now. Send these guys away, would you?" The Gallade nodded, and brought the fainted Axel and Mirror towards Locke, ready to BAMF them away.

    He failed to notice Geo and Denise, slowly crawling towards them, barely hanging on due to Endure and Guts respectively, but both leaped towards them and yelled"<<Locke!>>" Locke held his hand up reaching towards them. They would get him, they would take down Wesker, and/or hopefully, they could get away, together...

    This was crushed when the Gallade used Substitute, made a copy of himself, and the copy jumped towards the two and BAMfed them away in a flash of light, while the original BAMFed Locke, Axel, and Mirror to an entirely different place, with Locke's cry of "GUYS!" hanging in the air.

    Wesker smiled, happy, but realized something." The Keystone from the old lady was gone. He then shrugged, and thought," Oh well, still have more. Should probably look up that guy, too..."

    Man, poor Locke and his team got thoroughly OWNED.Also, Marin, as you were looking for someone to RP with, can I send Denise and Geo towards you?
