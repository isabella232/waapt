    <Grunt!Dragonair: "Who do those foreigners think they are, swooping into our region. Making us responsible for the place that we keep telling them's a lost cause!">

    <Grunt!Charizard: "I've already got everything figured out... If I get slapped with a summons, I'm laying low in that valley west of the mountains! At least I'll be able to do something beyond being a prisoner or dead down there-!">

    <Grunt!Dragonair: "Hey! Keep it down!">

    -Passing by, not bothering to make eye contact-

    <Vektor: *sigh*>

    <Grunt!Dragonair: "So... As you were saying?">

    -To explain why I am the worm that I am. Why others hate me... That under this armor, I'm still a spineless weakling that should have been killed by worthier mons years ago...

    And it's a she right? How could she possibly not think that way?

    ... But... Would she?

    ... I mean... Carol didn't... And she had every right to make my life miserable... But... She didn't lord over being able to push me around...

    And... Randy. I... I don't understand... He was really ready to let me fight with him...? Even after everything? ._.

    Why do they forgive me...? Even when I still fail so? Why do they want me near them...? When I'm so completely meaningless and peripheral to their lives?-

    -The Haxorus passes a wall... With a simple line of grafitti etched into it-

    Vahr lost hiu meyz het?*
    "Why have you come here?"

    -Followed by myriad responses-

    Zu laan stinun nol hahvullonei*
    "I want freedom from my nightmares"

    Wah tinvaak voth jaahmaar wo vodreh wiif zey fah kosvon himdahstaavek*

    Wah tinvaak voth jaahmaar wo fen helt liinvon zey anhk helt weridvon zey fah kosvon aan vol*
    "To talk with someone who will stop screwing with me and stop praising me for being a monster"

    Wah rim fin ahkrin wah grind fadonnei*
    "To gain the courage to face (lit. meet) my friends"

    Wah mindoraan miildovahhe anhk wah nid dir au aan diist lokalgeind!*
    "To understand females and to not die on a first date!"

    Wah kos tok volk klovi vok kriis*
