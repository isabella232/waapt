    Holly: <I've got everymon together as you requested, Ferno. They're waiting for you outside.>

    -The Delta Charizard finishes straightening himself up, before turning to the Garchop and her Shedinja companion.-

    Ferno: -Grins.- <Excellent. I'm sure the sight of our little captive here will be quite the moral booster.>

    -Ferno gestures to Lake's bag, which is currently slung over the Charizard's shoulder.-

    Shedinja: <Don't act so smug. It was a fluke that you and the others managed to get that Lugia. I don't understand why you couldn't have just asked me to do it. It's obvious that I would have been the most suitable Pokémon for the task.>

    -Ferno shoots a glare at the Shedinja, a few flames escaping from his mouth.-

    Shedinja: <Urk! On the other mandible, a luck based victory is still a victory!>

    Holly: -Rolls her eyes.- <Okay Ferno, if you're just about ready...>

    Ferno: <I am, Holly. Come, let us speak to our comrades.>

    -The three head outside Ferno's cave, where a group of about thirty Delta Pokémon are gathered. Upon seeing Ferno, they immediately begin chattering excitedly amongst themselves.-

    <"Look! It's Ferno!">

    <"Oh wow, he came back! I thought he was killed or something...">

    <"He was only gone for a few days!">

    <"Yeah, that's my point!">

    <"Ooh, look at that stylish bag he's wearing! I wonder if it comes in a smaller size?">

    -The Charizard flaps up onto a rock and clears his throat loudly, attracting the gathered Pokémon's attention.-

    Ferno: <My fellow Delta Pokémon! I'm sure you are all aware of our mission to convince the Legendary bird Lugia to aid us in our crusade for equal treatment for Delta Pokémon the world over! In the past few months, we've all worked day and night to attempt to find the child of the legends...But now, I am pleased to inform you that the child has finally been found!>

    <"Oh! You found Lugia's son?">

    <"Where is he? Did you catch him?">

    -The Delta Charizard smirks, reaching down and unzipping Lake's bag.-

    Ferno: <Well, as a matter of fact, the child of the legends is right...Um...>

    -Not being able to find the young legendary in the stolen bag, the Charizard reaches his hand in deeper.-

    Ferno: <Bear with me, peoplemons...Hmm...He has to be in here somewh- ARGH!!!>

    -A sharp pain shoots through the Fire-type's hand as something clamps down on them hard. Roaring in pain, Ferno pulls his hand out of the bag, finding Pleo hanging onto it by his mouth, and angry look on the baby Lugia's face.-

    Ferno: <YOU LITTLE RUNT! LET GO RIGHT THIS INSTANT OR-!>

    <"Oh! That's it! The baby Lugia!">

    <"D'awwwww, look at him, he's adorable!">

    <"I want one!">

    -Realizing that he would not be popular if he throttled the baby legendary in front of the audience for biting him like he had planned too, the Charizard pulls Pleo off his hand, then tucks him under his arm before beginning to pet his head, much to his surprise.-

    Ferno: <Quite an adorable little thing isn't he? It pains me that we have to kidnap him in order to make our voices heard, but it must be done, I'm afraid...>

    -Pleo glares at Ferno, prompting Holly to come over and whisper into his ear.-

    Holly: <I wouldn't do that if I were you. Trust me on this one.>

    -Even though he was weary of the Garchomp, Pleo decided to do what he was told and relax, hoping that it would prevent Ferno from getting any more riled up with him.-

    Ferno: <My fellow Pokémon, we have all had to do things that we regret in order to get to this point. But now that we have this adorable little legendary, these dark times will soon be over, and hopefully we shall all be able to return to our former lives!

    So go and rest, my friends, for this comming Saturday, the Delta's shall take their rightful place as rulers of- I mean, take our rightful place in society as equals!>

    -The gathered Pokémon cheer loudly, excited at the prospect of no longer having to live in fear of being persecuted by their fellow Pokémon. His speech over, Ferno turns around and begins to head back to his cave with Holly and the Shedinja, a huge grin on his face.

    A grin that gave Pleo the chills.-
