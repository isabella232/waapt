    Cool Blue Magic

    Oreburgh City, the City of Energy (Future)

    "Wow...I don't think I've ever seen a city like this before."

    Unsurprisingly, the sight of a mining town was something new for Rex. Near their position were a few vents leading from under the ground, the air around it stale and acrid. No doubt they were used to keep the air filtered in the mine. Further away, Rex could see several houses nestled together near a cliffside, a few businesses scattered here and there as well. A bit further off was a large building...a museum, according to his Pokegear's information.

    Rex looked the town over and shrugged. "Reminds me of Pewter City...only a lot bigger and more industrial." He turned to Ponyta and tilted his head. "Have you ever seen this place before?"

    <Only from afar.> She admitted, kicking at a nearby stone. <My home was to the north of here.>

    "Oh...I see." He nodded, folding his arms. "We'll probably pass through your home at some point, if you want to stop and see your family or friends."

    Ponyta nodded at that, smiling a little at the thought.

    "By the way, I haven't forgotten." She blinked, turning to him with a quizzical expression. "I'm still thinking of a good name for you."

    <Ah...that's unnecessary,> she murmured. Her last trainer hadn't exactly been too good at coming up with names. She didn't want to get stuck with something even more ridiculous...

    "Hmm...well, let's cross that road when we come to it, alright?" He gave her another pat, the horse whinnying softly as her flaming tail twitched happily in response. Perhaps being pet wasn't so bad. At least his gloves felt nice...

    "Anyway, I need to recall you for the moment. See you in a bit, okay?" The horse whinnied softly as she was recalled, Rex placing her ball back onto his belt. "Now..." He looked at the building not too far off from where he was, the unmistakable Pokemon League symbol boldly emblazoned across it's rooftop.

    "Let's try this again."
