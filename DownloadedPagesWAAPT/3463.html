    PEFE HQ

    -wanders around thinking whether I should go take part on the (let's hope) final boss battle.-

    I don't think I can make much of a difference there. Maekrite can survive whatever someone'll throw at him, I guess, but beyond that the only relatively distinctive thing he has to offer is the Limit Break... then there's also Inka and ____. Surely they're coming too. And while they are very powerful and have an incredible array of techniques at their disposal, they'll be not at their peak for a while. Plus even with the special armor I'll be somewhat of a sitting duck there, I hardly think it can take a time-erasing hit...

    -turns around a corner, wandering around without paying much attention, not noticing the Pokémon hovering behind me-

    Perhaps I should return to the Colony. At least there we're gonna be safe for a while. It's not like we can no nothing to prepare for the worst-case scenario where we are simply rewritten from existence, again. So maybe a couple of days of happy rest, forgetting about all these things going on, and let them handle it...

    But on the other hand... that lunatic has to be stopped. I have already seen Luke and Tagg, I know how they operate, and if push comes to shove they surely can take out Cyrus for good, but... peer pressure might prevent them from doing so. And Cyrus is too much of a problem to be simply kept under guard, after what has happened.

    -wanders around to reach the food service area, decides to turn around-

    — Perhaps I should go back and ask them what they- Aaaaahhh!

    -I recoil in surprise as the single eye is staring at me-

    Magneton — <Oh, sorry about that...>

    — Ma-Magneton? How long have you been...?

    Magneton (blinks) — <Oh, trailing?> — makes some sounds — <For 6,823 microfortnights percisely... now. There.> — blinks — <Hope I haven't been intruding...>

    — Oh, no, it's just... wait, I have been walking around for over two hours?

    -Magneton hovers up and down-

    — Damn... I should go back to check on Inka and her husband... er... this way.

    -We walk (well, I walk, Magneton hovers) back to the training grounds-

    — And what are you doing here anyway?

    Magneton — <My power is not too high, but when I heard the Nidos in the colony talking about Inka staying here, I calculated that my help would be required.> — turns to me — <I know the others would like to be here too... but ever since last week you have kinda dismissed us, Trainer...>

    — Oh... — shies — It's nothing like that, I swear.

    Magneton — <You better it's not, hmph.> — screeches.

    -I pat Magneton on the main head and then we continue our way back-

    I guess they are right. Perhaps if Dintel and Algira were here, they would also be understanding about the situation and complaining that I don't let them protect me. Well... at least Algira would be understanding. Dintel would be all like, "my, a road trip with all our friends!" and then goes around hugging everyone. But in this universe Shahinne doesn't even exist, and if we don't fix this, then she won't ever again.
