    Spear Pillar

    A figure in a black hoodie stood with his back turned to our heroes. The wind whipped past him but he did not seem affected by it at all. Des and Sparkers stood there. Their eyes were transfixed on the figures back. Finally the figure spoke.

    "Such chaos in Orre. A former friend over come with paranoia. A young woman separated from her lover and companion. A corrupted mind tryin' to piece it's memories back together...."

    The figure turned around. His face was obscured by the shadows that his hood cast, leaving only his mouth and hairy chin exposed. Said mouth has curled up in a smirk.

    "Such wonderful chaos indeed. And where are you in all of this?"

    Both Des and Sparkers growled at this.

    "Yes. Where is the legendary 'Emerald Fuckr'? The one who swore to protect the peace? The one who swore to protect his friends? Where is he?"

    "Who are you? Why have you called me here?" Des barked out at the hooded man. The man replied with a chuckle.

    "Your first question will have to wait. As for your second it's simple. If chaos and destruction are 'sposed to reign free, then justice must be removed."

    "What does that have to do with me?"

    Another chuckle. "Des you have been on both sides of the law. You are capable of sympathizin' with both the victims and the perpetrators. Your judgement is quite possibly the most unbiased on the planet, makin' you the ultimate force of order. In human terms anyways."

    Des and the hooded man began to circle each other. Sparkers intended to circle with them but Des gestured him to sit tight and stay out of the way.

    "Those eyes of yours. The ability to see the hardships of others with but a mere look." The hooded man continued. "Not the mention a certain blessin' from a certain underground dragon."

    With a metallic sound the hooded man drew a nasty looking sword, it's hilt a dark twisted mess. Des undid his coat revealing a pair of short swords with blue tassels. Both men continued to circle, eyeing each other. Sparkers waited with baited breath on the sidelines.

    "That all? You want to kill me so chaos can rule unimpeded?"

    "Oh I have a few other reasons..."

    The hooded man struck a pose, his blade poised and pointing straight at Des.

    "Have at you, 'Knight'."

    The two swordsmen charged at each other. Their blades flashing.
