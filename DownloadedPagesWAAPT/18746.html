    The Internet

    The article about Lucius is as follows:

    The Mystery of the Undead

    A long time ago a shocking video made it's way into the internet, showing two teenagers being killed by an unknown assailant in what seemed to be some sort of warehouse. The victims of the murder were not only young, but were also the known superheroes, The Auric Avenger and Gaia, who were identified as Lucius Cain and Alice O'Sullivan. The video itself can still be found in the internet, though I won't link to it myself for personal reasons, but you should be able to find it if you're daring enough to watch it.

    This video caused a huge shock thanks to the victims being known as both superheroes and members of the notable J-Team and many mourned their fate. However, not too long ago news among J-Team fans seems to be that Lucius is still alive or somehow came back from the dead. The teenager was apparently spotted among a fellow J-Team member in a convention that was being held in Mauville, looking very much alive and well.

    The video clearly showed the dead duo for all to see, so without doubt they must have died that day, yet many people confirm seeing Lucius that day. The closest to a logical explanation would be that he came back as a ghost, as there have been stories of sightings of such ghosts in both Sinnoh and Unova. At the same time, though, Lucius seemed to be truly alive.

    Theories range from him being now undead or went through a strange case of reincarnation. Some have noticed that he seems noticeably shorter now, which would imply that this Lucius is different from the one that died, further complicating the whole mystery.

    As for myself, I cannot make a clear judgement until I have enough information on the matter, but from little data I have it does sound like Lucius somehow came back into the world of living in a different body.

    However it's unknown if Alice O'Sullivan went through the same thing, as there are no reported sights of her.

    This is truly one of the most perplexing phenomena I've heard of in my life.

    M.R.B
