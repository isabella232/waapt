    Closing 2011!Megapost, part Ⅰ

    Former Mirage Tower, Two minutes before New Year

    Silent stands on the desert, looking at the large mound of sand around him. Some Pokémon can still be hearing wailing nearby, and Dintel and his sister are also examining the place, sniffing and growling unhappily. Maekrite walks around, checking some pieces of equipment and random [coins] that are scattered across the surface. Algira and, surprisingly, Jemma, are watching around as well.

    "...I didn't expect to spend the New Year like this, honest..."

    Sol -rubs against Silent's leg: <I feel this is in part my fault. I should have expected that to happen...> — looks up — <It always happens in those adventure stories I read, you know? Kat takes the important object and then the whole place crumbles down.>

    "...Betcha at some point it became predictable," Silent answers while petting Sol.

    Sol: <It still doesn't detract from the fun, purr~>

    Silent looks around at the desolation around him, while Jemma pulls some sticks and a tire.

       Jemma   : <Nya... this is so sad... even for me. Those poor Pokémon took [care] of this place!> — hops around vigorously.

    Suddenly, the conversation is interrupted by some lights that begin to flash in the distance, in short bursts comine from the south, as Silent and the others can see now, for some reason the sky has cleared and the sandstorm has fully subsided. The people of Mauville are celebrating the beginning of a new year. Sol and Maekrite sit and recognize the event with flickering ears and a smile.

    "Oh well what the hraka..." Silent walks up to the two shrews who are more or less ignoring the lights and picks them up. "Happy New Year, Dintel... and you too, Dintel's sister."

    Sandslash♀: <...Eeeeee?> — surprised by the hug, she only hugs back when she sees Dintel doing as well — <Thanks, I guess... Uhm, I can has wuv you too?>

    Dintel: <Wheeee~! It's like having a party with all my family and friends in!> — looks around — <...Where is lil'bird?>

    As soon as Silent explains that Shahinne is staying in Goldenrod and lets the two shrews down, Maekrite and Silent share a knowing embrace. They don't speak to each other though, as they don't need to.

    Sol is about to open her muzzle to ask about Shahinne and her "family"... when she is tackleglomphugnuzzled by a certain Leafeon.

    Ichtacya: <Wheeeee~ Happy holiday beautiful Espeon!> — licks playfully.

    Sol]: <T-thanks.> — blinks — <I- uhm, congratulations for getting Dintel back, Mr. Silent. Now can I breath please?> — she adds, more or less flustered.

    Meanwhile, Algira flutters around Jemma as the two of them look down at a dead cactus tree on the ground.

       Jemma    -swings tail, looks up to Algira: <Uh...>

    Algira: <...Errr.....>

    The two casually look at each other for a moment and then turn their own way.

    Algira: <...I miss Helios.> — buzzsighs — <We were finally starting to spend time together...>

    Surprisingly, Jemma responds.

       Jemma   : <I miss [Rex] too...> — swings tail as Algira turns to her — <Hey, he is a good servant, can keep me healthy and amused entertained...>

    The two Pokémon stay silent for a while.

    Both: <So Yeah...> — they nod to each other.

    After Ichtacya finally leaves Sol alone to go hug Dintel and Maekrite as well, to which the Sandshrew responds with a hug efusive enough that it is hard to know which of the two is happier, the Espeon moves a couple of steps back and closes her eyes. Meditating, she focuses her psychic power. After a moment, a small glowing blue ball of Psychic power materializes in front of her, floating against the dark sky barely illuminated by the explosions to the south.

    Sol: <These are my good wishes and my hopes for the future for you, [Luke]. Now, power of good will, go!>

    Sol rears back, prompting the quasi-Future Sight ball to fly high to the sky. After a couple of moments of circling around, the psychic ball shrinks and becomes white, then flies northeast, towards Fortree.

    Meanwhile, Silent walks to his backpack and after some brief hammerspace fiddling, produces a shovel. He walks to a point on the mound near to where Dintel and his sister are, and starts digging. This surprises most members of the team enough, as well as the guest.

    Sandslash♀: <...What is he doing?> — pokes Dintel.

    Maekrite rushes in and stands besides his Trainer. In his eyes is drawn the tacit question that Silent lets drag for a moment before answering.

    "Holiday is over, so let's start building a new home for Dintel's sister." Watches Maekrite start digging as well. "You'll help me hold the wall?"

    Maekrite: <Sure... and you'll tell me why you have a shovel in your backpack.>

    Sandslash♀: <Uhm... I'm fine. I got my brother back...>

    "But you lost your home..." Silent digs. "That's a sacrifice that needed not to be made..." Digs more. "Less if it was... for human intervention..." Turns to Maekrite and leans. "This was to help Juanito dispose of some evidence back in the OI... but don't worry about it anylonger... and don't tell Rex."

    Silent keeps clumsily trying to dig a trench.

    Sandslash♀ -to Dintel: <Uhm... does he even know how to dig an adequate tunnel?>

    Dintel: <More or less... Probably not.> — sits and swings tail.

    Sandslash♀ -sits as well, pondering for a moment: <...Is he always like this? He just jumps in and tries to help wild Pokémon?>

    Dintel: <All the time. He calls it "being a PEFE reseracher". It is very fun!>

    Dintel jumps right in to help digging as well, speeding up the task considerably, which his sister observes for a moment, evaluating the Sandshrew's fluidity and strength in movement.

    Sandslash♀ -to herself: <Then I guess you made the right call by leaving...>

    In a moment, the rest of the Pokémon jump in as well - Lileep anchors to Silent's head to serve as a hat, Algira and Ichtacya start compacting some of the sand to create more solid walls, Jemma and Sol have started using their powers to hold the trench stable, while also roaming around looking for things for the mons to eat.

    Sol: <...This year... perhaps I can find even more books to read. Anad hopefully I can find more cultured mons with whom to strike a conversation... I wonder if I can learn more about Eeveelutions?>

    Ichtacya: <This year... well if I got a Trainer for the meantime then I better take advantage of it. I will become a good walkalong! And hopefully we can get to meet the God of [Wind]...>

    Maekrite and Silent share a knowing look. The two get closer.

    "I can ask Pentigan for the armband, when we get to have some spare time in between apocalypses..."

    Maekrite -keeps digging: <I can wait... I chose to wait when Ho-Oh chose me...>

    After a while, Silent turns to Dintel.

    "This totally beats last year's celebration, don't you think?"

    The Sandshrew smiles and hops around happily, drawing random Trapinch figures in the sand.

    Meanwhile, some of the Pokémon who were relocating around the former tower peek in from the distance, lured back to the site by the sound and the presence the random human who out of nowhere has started trying to dig a house for a random Pokémon.

    Flashback to Unova, same time last year...

    Under some trees in a totally nondescript place in Unova, Silent, Maekrite, Algira and Dintel are having a picnic. Out of nowhere the researcher has produced a glass [flute] and a half-empty bottle of champagne.

    "Happy New Year me!" His Pokémon stare at him. "Well this better now than never. Maekrite, Algira? Want to try this?"

    The two Pokémon shake their heads and continue drinking milk and tree sap while Silent is getting the champagne ready.

    Suddenly, a streak of white crosses the picnic area and and the flute disappears.

    "...eeeeeep!" Silent turns to a tree where a Minccino is climbing away while skillfully handling the flute. "Come back here!"

    After rushing his meal, Maekrite follows and circles the tree, while Dintel goes "play" fetch with the Minccino on the tree.

    Whitestone Town, Johto, 2 weeks before New Year

    A Pelipper is flying around a makeshift nest on top of a building, near the sea. Inside the nest, made of hen, leaves and sticks, three Wingull chicks are playing around, pecking each other, crying loudly and otherwise annoying their parents. Said Pelipper tries to calm her chicks by presenting them the most recent acquisitions hidden inside her beak.

    Pelipper♀: <There you have, you will have fun with these...> — opens beak for the chicks to explore.

    Pelipper♂: <I hope...> — flies around observing — <Because the humans don't let us drag much things hereeither. I'm gonna go for a fish.>

    Wingulls: <   TOYS!   >

    As the male flies off, the female roosts on the nest for the chicks to take the random objects she has collected. Pretty minor things, actually: some algæ, a wet Oran berry, a white shoelace, two [Heart Scales], tap for a milk bottle and a piece of a Macho Brace. As the chicks start infighting for the different objects, the mother fails to notice a set of claws clinging to the edge of the roof, soon followed by a violet-colored paw.

    Nidoran♀: <...ufff... ufff.... almost made it!>

    A battered female poison rabbit Pokémon manages to pull herself up the edge, and then shakes herself up. A similar rabbit, but of pink coloration and a larger horn, tiredly manages to clib up as well.

    Nidoran♂: <Uggh... I'm sure they came... this way...>

    The two Nidoran walk around and explore, until they notice the nest. As they stand on their hind legs to observe, however, the Pelipper hears something and turns around.

    Pelipper♀: <....Aaaack!> — tries to look menacing — <Who are you?>

    Nidoran♀: <...Oh look some Wingull! Awwwww...> — walks in — <I'm a Nidoran and my name is—>

    Nidoran♂: <Aha! There's the prize! LEEEEEROY JENKIIIIIIN~> — rushes to the nest.

    The young male rushes past her, horn down trying to cast a Leer at the bird to sway it away from the nest.

    Wingull chick: <....Eeeeeeeep! Mom!>

    Nidoran♂: <Shoo! Shoo! Give those Heart Scales back!>

    Pelipper: <Those are my chicks's! I picked them from the sea for them!>

    Nidoran♂: <Rrrrrr..... they were in our river! Give back!> — Poison Stings the bird.

    The chicks, horrorized at the attack, cry and try to hide, but that is unneeded as the Pelipper stands before them and Protects against the attack. She scowls at the young rabbit and then stands tall before him.

    Pelipper: <Approach my chicks again and I'll nom you whole!>

    The male stands before the Pelipper, lunging and demanding the sclaes back, but getting a direct wing slap from the Pelipper for his troubles. As the larger bird roars (more or less) that no weird rabbit is gonna come threaten her chicks, the female Nidoran looks away at her brother trying to stand up and facepaws.

    Nidoran♀: <...Betcha big bro Maekrite isn't anything like this...> — sighs and goes assist or stop her brother, whichever she can manage first.

    Somewhere in a non-canon region south of Angela; 5 days before the New Year

    In a city at the edge of a mountainrange, already late at night in a very populated neighborhood...

    Among all those white houses with green fences however, one is special. If only because of a tidbit of plot from this otherwise for some reason unknown region to other player characters people.

    The door of the house opens, and a man enters. As he walks in he hears someone more or less crudely playing the piano. It is a good sign to his ears and to his heart.

    A tall brunette woman steps out from the staircase to the basement, a Serperior slithering behind coyly.

    Juanito's Wife: "Honey! You are home! And just in time!"

    Juanito: "The bosses were very good despite their own problems... and I could make it here on time." — smiles — "I got a nice bonus too."

    The couple hugs and kisses, the Serperior coiling around them warmly.

    On the living room, a young girl is practising with her new piano, much to the enjoyment to her new Pokémon companion, a Tranquill, who is taking advantage of the distraction to eat the snacks without anyone noticing. The married couple walks into the living in silence, staying quiet as to enjoy and not perturb the fascination their daughter has with the recently acquired (and very costly) [Piano].

    Route 35, 4 days before the New Year

    Near the entrance to the National Park, a couple of Nidoran families are spending time, playing around and collecting random items under the careful watch of Hamada, the leader of the Owsla, and four other Nidorino who are roaming around the bushes.

    One of the Nidorino, standing atop a fountain on the road, drinks cautiously and hears what is going on in every direction, calling the attention of some humans around. It is very rare for the bulkier, rarer Nidorinos in the Colony to get down to the route, let alone to spend that much time in the open.

    A clicking sound and a flash of light call the Nidorino's attention and he turns around, narrowing his eyes at the human who has just managed to aim at him with a camera. Before the human can get in position for a better shot, the annoyed Nidorino leaps away and runs to some nearby bushes where other Nidorino are also peeking around.

    ???: "You got him, that was pretty interesting."
