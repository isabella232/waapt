    Imperial Commissar

    Ransei - Around 600 years ago

    -Ayame has finally left Illusio, and is now heading along the roads to Yaksha with Thor and Libitina by her side. She is just making her way around the Terreran borders when she notices a familiar figure with a Toxicroak by his side, who hails her with a wave.-

    Brandon: Good afternoon, Ayame.

    Ayame: Oh, hey Brandon! It's been a loooooooong time since we has saw each other, hasn't it? I kinda lost track of everyone after that big battle back in Pugilis a few months back. Glad to see you again!

    -She starts (carefully) petting Sumac.-

    Sumac: <Hey, just wondering, but do you think there's any real difference between a pet and a pat? I can never tell the two apart.> :3

    Ayame: So, how have you been holding up? I've just been hanging around. You know, staying in Yaksha, keeping Aiura company, defeating crazy evil ice bullies, that kind of thing.

    Brandon: Oh? At least you have been keeping yourself amused. Myself and Takuma were merely passing time in Illusio... until recently, at least.

    -Ayame tilts her head at Brandon.-

    Ayame: Did something happen?

    Brandon: You haven't heard yet? I would have thought you would be the first to know, having just come from Illusio yourself... still, it stands to reason that they would attempt to hide the news from you.

    Ayame: ...What's going on? What are you talking about?

    Brandon: Takuma has passed away.

    -He pauses for a moment to let the impact of his words sink in, and is rewarded by Ayame's face falling.-

    Ayame: ...Are... are you sure?

    Brandon: Sadly, I could not be more certain. I saw the body myself. My condolences, of course. I understand that you admired the old Warlord greatly. He was the one that granted you the title of spymaster in the first place, was he not?

    Ayame: Yeah... I was just a servant up at the castle until he took me in...

    -Brandon's hand curls into a fist, then relaxes itself, but Ayame is too distracted to notice the motion.-

    Brandon: ...I see.

    Ayame: ...How did it happen, though?

    Brandon: The Illusian authorities are saying that the cause of death was liver failure. However, I have my reasons to suspect that there is more to the story.

    Ayame: What do you mean?

    Brandon: It seems highly unlikely to me that Takuma would be able to drink himself to death in a kingdom where strict prohibition is enforced. Foul play would usually be out of the question, given that Illusio is quite possibly the safest region in Western Ransei that still maintains open borders... but considering Aiura's volatile mood as of late-

    Ayame: Wait, are you trying to say that Aiura bumped him off?

    Brandon: Unfortunately, that is the conclusion I am forced to arrive at.

    Ayame: That's crazy! Brandon, I think you've gone nutty. Round the bend. Off to cloud nine. I mean, we were allies with Illusio before... well, you know, that stuff with the ghosts.

    Brandon: But that was only whilst Aiura and Lucanus were engaged in their little, ahem, partnership. No doubt you are too young to remember, but the two kingdoms were bitter rivals during Takuma's reign. There is no love lost between the old Warlord and the region he once warred with. And I fear what little goodwill Aiura maintained towards the people of Pugilis departed with Lucanus.

    Ayame: But Aiura wouldn't-

    Brandon: Aiura is a master manipulator and takes pride in it. You of all people should know that. She may say one thing and mean another, extending goodwill with one hand even as she holds the knife between her back. You should not trust her so easily until you are certain that she has no hidden agenda. This could well be the first step of a plan to annex Pugilis once the war is over. And even if she has no cohesive plan, her emotional state is highly unstable as of late. Who's to say that she is not striking out at the surviving members of the Pugilian court in order to exact revenge on Lucanus via proxy?

    Ayame: But.. guh...

    -She thinks for a moment, looking down at her feet as uncertainty clouds her face.-

    Ayame: ...I still think you're wrong about something, you know. Somewhere along the line.

    Brandon: Understandable. Still, I feel that we should avoid returning to Illusio in the immediate future. You were heading back to Yaksha, were you not?

    Ayame: Yeah.

    Brandon: Then allow me and Sumac to accompany you. We have been journeying for some time now, however, and I feel that some refreshments are in order before continuing.

    -He pulls a canteen of water off of his belt and offers it to Ayame, who eyes it suspiciously.-

    Brandon: Would you care for a drink?

    Ayame: Hmm... nah, I think I'll pass.

    Brandon: Really? Well, if you are certain.

    -He lifts up the canteen and takes a long chug from it, before clipping it back to his belt with a refreshed exhale.-

    Brandon: Now then, let's get moving, shall we?

    Ayame: ...Yeah. Sure.

    -Brandon turns and starts to head east along the road, Ayame and Sumac following behind him. Libitina and Thor hesitate for a moment.-

    Thor: <Grunt.>

    Libitina: <Me too, big guy. Me too.>

    -With a sigh, she and Thor reluctantly head after the group.-
