    Long post is loooooong.

    -The Night Before...-

    - Glances up at the starry sky. Is outside, and is feeling rather tired after the day's events, but still has one more important thing to do before I can sleep.-

    -Pulls Pokéballs off of belt-

    Everyone! GO!

    -Pokéballs hit the floor and open, releasing Lake, Crowley, Woolly the Ampharos, Sol the Espeon, Flare the Blaziken and Pepper repsectively.-

    Hello guys.

    "Gatr! "Krow!" "Ampharos!" "Espeon." "Blaziken!" "Ganium!"

    Nice to see you too. Now, the reason I've sent you all out so late at night is that I have...Something rather important to tell you guys.

    -Explains everything that has happened so far, up to the point where we met Palkia on Fullmoon Island-

    ...So we managed to get Palkia to appear again by saying she was fat or something (I don't really know either). But what she told us was incredible. She said that there was a Parallel dimension where this world is inhabited only by Pokémon!

    -The team are shocked by this.-

    I know right? That's how me and Lake felt at the time too.

    -Lake nods in agreement.-

    Anyway, Palkia said that she could open a Portal to this world. But...She said that if a human we're to go through they would change into a Pokémon and lose their memory.

    -Collective gasp from the team.-

    I know, shocking isn't it? But Palkia did say that we could send Pokémon through without any problems. So, with that in mind, I had a talk with Lake and...He volunteered to go.

    -Another collective gasp from the team, followed by collective panicked chattering-

    -Glances at Lake-

    Maybe you should talk about it with them.

    -Lake nods, then turns to the others.-

    Lake: <Guys! Calm down for just a few seconds!>

    Pepper: <Calm down?! How do you expect us to keep calm after we've just be told you're being sent to another world where there could be just who knows what could be lurking there?!>

    Woolly: <Actually, I think it's kinda' exciting! A whole world of only Pokémon! Imagine the kinds of wonderful stuff that could be there!>

    Sol: <I agree with Woolly, there could be all kinds of brilliant things there just waiting to be discovered. This is a golden opportunity for both human and Pokémon kind!>

    Lake: <Guys...>

    Flare: -Snorts in disgust- <Don't be ridiculous Sol! The humans are just getting Lake to risk his life needlessly for, what, a few pages in a stupid book? If they really want to find out so badly, they should do it themselves rather then having their Pokémon take an uneccesary risk!>

    Lake: <Guys!>

    Crowley: <Oh cool down for once Flare! You're always going on about humans put us in danger, but Luke has never once done anything of the sort to us!>

    Lake: <GUYS!!!>

    -Everyone stops, staring at Lake.-

    Lake: <Thank you. Now, I think a few of you are forgetting something very important here.>

    Flare: <And just what would that happen to be?>

    Lake: <Well Flare, as a trainer's Pokémon, isn't it our duty to help out our trainer?>

    Flare: <...Yes...>

    Lake <And in return for that help, doesn't out trainer help us to become the best Pokémon we can be in return? I mean, where would we all be if Luke hadn't come along? I'd probrobly still be stuck in Professor Elm's Lab back in New Bark Town.>

    Crowley: <He's right! If Luke hadn't caught any of us, we'd probably never have reach our final evolved forms by ourselves! Especially me, it takes a special kind of stone to trigger a Murkrow's evoloution that can't normally be found in Johto. Without Luke, I'd never have become a Honchkrow.>

    Woolly: <Yeah! Before Luke came along, I was just a Mareep who could only use Thundershock. Now, I'm a really powerful Ampharos!>

    Sol: <I do believe that Lake has hit the nail on the head excatly.>

    Pepper: <How could we forget something so simple? It is what we do, after all!>

    Flare: <...>

    Lake: <Do you understand now Flare?>

    Flare: <...Yes.>

    Lake: -Grins- <Heh, I knew you'd come around eventually. All you needed was a bit of convincing.>

    Pepper: -Raises a Vine- <I'm still bit worried though Lake. What if something happens to you in this world? There'd be no way any of us could help you if you got into trouble!>

    Lake: <Come on Pepper, I'm perfectly capable of looking after myself. Besides, I'm not going alone. The other trainers are going to send one of their Pokémon through as well. Plus I think that Gamer person is going through too.>

    -The other Pokémon give Lake a blank look.-

    Lake: <I dunno either. I reckon he must a have a few screws loose or something.>

    Sol: <Then it's settled. Tomorrow, Lake shall embark for the world of Pokémon. Everyone in agreement?>

    Crowley: <Agreed.>

    Woolly: <Agreed!>

    Pepper: <Agreed.>

    Flare: <...Agreed.>

    Sol: <It's is unanimous. Lake, on behalf of the team, I wish you the best of luck on your mission.>

    Everyone (Except Flare): -Cheers-

    Lake: -Smiling- <Thank you guys. I'll go and tell Luke about this then.>

    -Lake walks over to me, having been standing nearby watching.-

    So...I guess you managed to convince them then?

    Lake: -Nods- Feraligatr!

    -Smiles-

    Good going Lake.

    -Watches the others as they talk among themselves.-

    You know, it's times like this that I really wish I could understand what you Pokémon are saying...
