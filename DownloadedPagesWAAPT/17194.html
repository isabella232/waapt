    Six Hours Later

    Sinclair: <Approaching Goldenrod City. Moving into position, sir.>

    To the Metagross' surprise, there was no immediate response from the Tyrantrum.

    <Sir? Are you-?>

    Looking away from the helm ever so briefly, the robotic Pokémon turned around to come across the sight of Torque asleep on his throne.

    Torque: <Zzz...Mom, I wanna' eat the Spoink!>

    Sinclair: <SIR!>

    The sudden shout awoke the Tyrantrum with a start.

    Torque: <AH! What time is it?>

    Sinclair continued to stare at his superior with his usual dead-pan expression, before turning back to the controls to continue piloting.

    Sinclair: <We're here, sir. Moving into position as we speak.>

    It took a few moments for Torque's sleepy brain to process the information.

    Torque: <Oh, right! My imminent conquest of the Johto region!>

    Sitting up straight on his chair, he pointed a claw straight ahead at the city visible through the immense wind-screen beyond the helm.

    <Bring the ship to a hover over the bay, where everyone can see us!>

    Sinclair: <Understood, sir.>

    Goldenrod City

    For most, it was impossible to ignore  a huge black ship had appeared on the horizon and was slowly moving into position above the Goldenrod Bay. People and Pokémon alike stopped to stare up at the immense shape in the sky, in a mixture of awe and terror.

    Just as suddenly as it had appeared, the engine slowed it's approach, then came to a complete stop, the whine of it's huge jet engines dying as the immense vessel hung motionless in the sky.

    Waiting.

    Bridge

    With his large claws, Sinclair pressed and swiped various instruments as the hum of the engines slowly died away.

    Sinclair: <Engines stopped, fuel burn at zero percent. G-diffusion system active and stable. Now hovering at an altitude of five-hundred meters above sea level.>

    Torque nodded in approval of all of this.

    Torque: <Good, good.>

    Sinclair: <Ready to proclaim your arrival?>

    He hesitated. Now that he was here, were the entirety of the human settlement of Goldenrod was watching him closely, it was hard not to feel a little stage-fright. It didn't help that this was also Torque's first proper attempt at conquest. The dinosaur fidgeted nervously in his chair.

    Torque: <This is it, Torque. Stay clam, and remember what the book said.>

    He closed his eyes as he recalled the sage advice on the first page of How to Conquer the World in ten simple steps.

    <The first and most important rule to asserting your dominance is to have fun and be yourself. Have fun, be yourself. Have fun, be yourself.>

    Feeling a bit more confident as he repeated this mantra in his head, he nodded to the Metagross.

    <Lower the mike!>

    Sinclair flipped a switch, a boom-mike descending from the ceiling from a line, stopping just above Torque's head.

    Sinclair: <Microphone enabled. Translation protocols active. Ready to go.>

    Steeling his courage, Torque took a deep breath, than began to speak.

    Torque: <People and Pokémon of Goldenrod City...>

    Goldenrod City

    Thanks to some complicated translation software developed by Sinclair, Torque's words were broadcast loudly in perfect English.

    Torque: Do not be alarmed! My name is Torque the Tyrantrum, and I am here to gladly accept the position of ruler of the Johto region!

    Now I know what you're all thinking: Torque, we already have a suitable  or not so suitable  ruler already! You can't just barge in like you own the place! Of course, I'm well aware of that. Which is why I'm here to take your region by force.

    It's not ideal. In a perfect world, there wouldn't be any need for such unpleasantness. But that's just the way it is, I guess. Thankfully, unlike most would-be conquers, my demands are entirely reasonable.

    All I ask is that the acting government, monarchy or what-have-you surrenders to me, and that I am promptly declared Emperor Torque, lord of Johto!

    That said, I wouldn't just arrive at your doorstep making such brash demands without any form of leverage, which is why I brought this with me!

    Bridge

    The Tyrantrum pulled his head away from the microphone to bark an order to Sinclair.

    Torque: <Sinclair! The laser!>

    Sinclair: <Lowering the laser.>

    Goldenrod City

    With a mechanical whir, the underside of the airship open as a very large and very powerful looking cannon was lowered into view, pointing directly at Goldenrod City as the massive gun powered on, glowing eerily with a powerful energy.

    Torque: Neat, isn't it? This Wave Motion Cannon is powerful enough to obliterate an entire city in a single shot! So I think you can see where I'm going with this, can't you?

    As much as I would like to avoid wiping Goldenrod off the map  it looks so lovely from up here  if my demands are not met within two hours, Goldenrod  and anyone or anything in it  will be atomized.

    Still, two hours is more than enough time to make peace with your loved ones. Or, if you're smart, to get as far away from here as possible. I think that's fair, isn't it?

    Anyway, your two hours starts now. Um...talk to you later, I guess?

    ...Bye!

    Bridge

    As Sinclair pressed the button to retract the boom-mike for the time being, the Torque rubbed his head, sighing in relief.

    Torque: <I think that went okay. Do you think it was okay?>

    Sinclair: <It was fine, sir. Although, I must warn you that retaliation will be imminent.>

    Leaning back in his chair, the dinosaur Pokémon smirked confidently.

    Torque: <Then let them come. With all we have at our disposal, who could stop us?>

    Goldenrod City  1:57 until Cannon Fire

    Of course, what just transpired has not gone unnoticed by a certain Feraligatr, Dragonite, Braviary, Heliolisk and their human, who were gazing up at the massive airship and it's huge cannon, ready to snuff out Goldenrod City with a press of a button.

    There was a few seconds of fearful silence before someone finally spoke.

    Luke: Well...what are we gonna' do about this one?

    Stratus raised a claw nervously.

    Stratus: <I vote running so we don't get vaporized. All in favour?>

    Unite: <NO WAY! We're not gonna' run away like cowards just because some Pokémon's pointing an over-sized laser pointer at us! If we give into fear, then he's already won!>

    Clenching a wing into a fist, the Braviary punched his other wing held flat with it. Ignoring that it was anatomically impossible for him.

    <I say we go up there and give this...Tyrantrum thingy a lesson in why it's rude to order people around! Who's with me?>

    Perched on top of Lake's head, Izzy raised a hand.

    Izzy: <This bird's speaking my language! If he's using force, let's respond in kind!>

    Stratus still wasn't keen.

    Stratus: <But, but...Giant laser! Big boom! ALL OF US DEAD!>

    Unite: <Hey, if it helps, the Laser won't get us if we're on there when he fires it. Not that we want him to fire it. But by that logic, that airship's the safest place to be right now.>

    Stratus: <...Oh.>

    The Dragonite scratched his chin.

    <Good point.>

    Izzy: <What about you Lake, huh?>

    She gazed down at the Feraligatr from her perch atop his head.

    <You told me you and Luke took down a crime syndicate, huh?>

    Lake nodded.

    Lake: <Yeah, Team Rocket.>

    Izzy: <And Two Glitch Pokémon.>

    Lake: <Yep!>

    Izzy: <The Sky Fortress?>

    Lake: <With a bit of help.>

    Izzy: <Three different armies?>

    Lake: <That was a different universe. Does it count?>

    The Heliolisk shrugged.

    Izzy: <For the sake of the argument, sure. Crazy Delta Pokémon?>

    Lake: <Oh yeah.>

    Izzy: <Neo Team Magma and Aqua?>

    Lake: <Just a little bit?>

    Izzy: <Two insane computers in a post apocalyptic future?>

    Stratus: <OH! And a Holographic Mewtwo! That was me who did that!>

    The Dragonite folded his arms, looking proud.

    Lake: <...Yeah, that too.>

    Izzy: <Now, how confident were you when you took all those guys on?>

    Lake thought, rubbing his chin.

    Lake: <That depends. They were all different, but I'd say I was mostly confident that we'd come out okay in the end.>

    Izzy: <And how do you feel about taking on this guy?>

    Lake: <Well, now that you're here...>

    He smirked up at the lizard.

    <I'd say he doesn't stand a chance! Besides...I can't just let him blow up Goldenrod and kill everyone here. It wouldn't be right!>
