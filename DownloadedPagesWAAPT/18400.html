    Joseph: ...well, that explains it!

    Joseph: ...a gate?

    -a gate. Made out of crossed bars, and enough to block the whole hallway. Peering through, Joseph could see the circle of bright light that was the way out, but the stubborn, obstinate, damnable gate stands in the way-

    Joseph: ...if this is locked, I will be seriously upset.

    Patrick: Ssssshhh...

    Patrick: We were close. We should be proud of that. Temporary setback. One day, we'll-
