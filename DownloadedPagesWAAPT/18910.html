    [Astrid, a young Tynamo ♀, hops up onto the table at which Riptide is sitting.]

    Astrid: <Hi, Uncle Rip!>

    Riptide: <Hey there, squirt.>

    Astrid: <I am not a squirt!>

    Riptide: [Grins] <Yeah y'are. You're a tiny lil' baby squirt.> [Extends an arm] <C'mere.>

    [Astrid climbs up Riptide's extended arm until she is perched on his shoulder. The Feraligatr pats her on the head, and she wiggles happily.]

    Astrid: <Uncle Rip, can I ask a question?>

    Riptide: <Shoot.>

    Astrid: <When am I gonna evolve and get bigger and stronger and where'd Ava go and what do you know about my Dad?>

    Riptide: <Whoa, slow down there. I only got one brain. What happened to "Can I ask a question", anyway?>

    Astrid: <I only meant to do one but I got excited!>

    Riptide: [Chuckles]
    <Alright. It happens. Now, how about we go over those questions one at a time?>

    Astrid: <Ok!>

    Riptide: <First of all, you get stronger by winning fights. Now, that don't necessarily mean you should run around starting fights. What it means is if you have to fight someone, you do it right. You know, kick their ass.>

    Astrid: <How do I do that?>

    Riptide: <You'll learn. Believe it or not, you're getting there already, ya little scrapper.>

    Astrid: <I am?>

    Riptide: <You are! Go on, try your Tackle attack on that fiendish monster over there.> [Gestures at a sleeping Slouch]

    Astrid: <Grrr!>

    [The Tynamo charges the unsuspecting trainer, knocking him out of bed and prompting an undignified shriek. Riptide and Astrid make no attempt to hide their ensuing laughter.]

    Riptide: <And so the beast was slain, and the kingdom rejoiced.>
    <What was that second question again?>

    Astrid: <I wanna know what happened to Ava.>

    Riptide: <...The Beedrill? The one that used to be on our team?>

    Astrid: <Yeah.>

    Riptide: <Hmm...>

    [Pause]

    <Hey Vess, can you come over for a sec?>

    [Vesuvius approaches.]

    Vesuvius: <Hi, little one!> [She pets Astrid, who wiggles contentedly]
    <So what's up, Rip?> [Teasing] <Do you have a problem that can't be solved by punching it hard enough? Or are you perhaps trying to think of a word longer than two syllables?>

    Riptide: <H-hey!>

    [Astrid giggles while Vesuvius chuckles]

    Riptide: <I'm gonna pay you back double for that one, y'know. I'm gonna come up with the greatest comeback of all time and hit you with it when you least expect it.>
    <Buuut I digress. Astrid wants to know about Ava- where she went and so on- and I figured you'd explain it better.>

    Vesuvius: <Is that so? Alright then.> [Turns to Astrid]
    <I don't know if you knew her long enough to know this, Astrid, but Ava... Wasn't nice. Ava was the kind of Pokémon who took pleasure from hurting others. Not like the joke you and Riptide played on Slouch a moment ago, either. This was different, and much worse.>
    <I tried to help, tried to change her ways- we all did; well, most of us, at least- but she didn't seem to want to be helped.>
    [Vesuvius sighs.]
    <Slouch didn't think it was safe to keep her around any more, especially since you were still very young at the time, so he let her go.>

    [Pause]

    Astrid: <She sounds scary...>

    [Riptide and Vesuvius nod]

    Vesuvius: <Well, does that answer your question?>

    Astrid: <Yeah, it does. Thanks, Vesuvius!>

    [Vesuvius smiles and nods as if to say "You're welcome", then walks away.]

    Astrid: <Can you answer my third question now, about my Dad? It's the last one.>

    Riptide: <Alright. What do you wanna know?>

    Astrid: <Well, you've already told me that his name's MGMT, that he talks by singing, and that he's a big jerk to you all the time. You said that last part a lot. You even called him a bad word once!>

    Riptide: <Whaaat? No I didn't.>

    Astrid: <Yeah you did, I heard you! But anyway, I wanna know some good stuff about him, too.>

    Riptide: <Oh yeah?>
    [Hesitates] <Well, even though he's a jerk, he isn't scary or dangerous like Ava. He's just an annoyance.>

    Astrid: <That's not a compliment and you know it!>

    Riptide: <Yeah, you're right. Let's see, then...>
    <Your dad's strong, I'll give him that. Kicked my ass, in fact.>

    Astrid: <Uncle Rip, that's crazy! You're the strongest guy I know!>

    Riptide: <There's plenty stronger than me. He's probably one of 'em. Although... We are at 1-1, if memory serves...>
    <...Anyway, he's clever, too. Too clever for his own good, I've always thought.>

    Astrid: <Why?>

    Riptide: <...I dunno.>
    <Too clever for my own good, then.>

    Astrid: <Anything else?>

    Riptide: [Shrugs] <Good singer?>

    Astrid: <I think you can do better than that...>

    Riptide: [Sighs] <I think so too.>
    <Y'know, I never really got to know 'im outside of his messing with me. For all I know, he could be an otherwise great guy who just loves targeting me in particular. On the other hand, he could be as big of a jerk as he seems. I'd like that.>

    Astrid: <He might be the biggest jerk in the universe.>
    <I'd still like to meet him some day, though.>

    Riptide: <...Some day, squirt. Promise.>
