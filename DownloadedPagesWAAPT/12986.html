    you really like hot animals don't you?

    Lumiose City - Kalos

    Eventually Aline gathered the courage to go inside the alley, she was scared and the continuous whimpering was just making her more nervous, luckily there wasn't anyone there and nothing jumped at her. She eventually came to a dead-end where she found a Furfrou, a rather small one, so it was probably still a puppy, he was very dirty and looked hurt in more ways than one as it was shaking a lot. That sight completely wrecked Aline's heart and she tried to approach the Furfrou to give it help, but when she got too close it noticed her and start growling, making her stop.

    She just stood there, thinking of what to do, before remembering the contents of her bag. She sat on the ground, smiling as she took a galette out of her bag, it wasn't that hot so it was safe to touch, she approached the Furfrou, trying to give it some food. It smelled the galette and started eating it, but still seemed suspicious as it took a bite and then took a step backwards before trying again. Aline kept a gentle smile on her face to try and get the puppy more comfortable around her.

    Some time and one galette later, Aline tried to touch the Furfrou and, while it looked rather wary and shy, it didn't stop her from doing so. She started petting it on the head, right in the forehead and the little thing seemed to be very happy with it. A while later they were just staring at each other until Aline started tapping on her lap and then gesturing to the Furfrou to come at her, it didn't take long for it to jump at her and start licking her. It tickled a bit and she was giggling in her own way as she pet it.

    She then grabs the Furfrou, puts it on the ground, gets up and starts walking away, once she got a certain distance, she whistled and gestured for it to follow her, it was still looking scared though so she had to appeal to food, taking another galette out of her bag and luring the puppy out, she let it take a bite, but then started walking away to make it go out with her. It worked.

    Once she was out of the alley, she started rollerskating a bit away, the Furfrou followed her all the way, despite being injured, it was running really fast. She then started leading him towards her house, looking behind to see if he was still following her from time to time, one of those time however resulted in her not looking ahead and once she did, she slammed her face on a tree again, the same one as before even. Again, her face slided down on the tree until it hit the ground and a few tears started to form in her eyes. The Furfrou caught up to her and pounced her, licking the tears away and making her smile.

    They sat there for a while, Aline petting her new friend for a bit. She then started to write on her sign a message that read "Can you read?"

    There was no response whatsoever from the Furfrou, so she gave a sigh, but even then she started to write message after message.

    "I'm sorry I startled you, but you looked so lonely."

    She then gets rather sad and grumpy at the same time "You were probably abused by some mean person."

    "I hate that!"

    "But..."

    "I promise I will take care of you. I'm sure Mom and Dad won't mind!"

    "I don't even know why I'm writing all of this, since you can't understand a thing."

    She sighs "But I guess this is my only way to talk to you now..."

    She smiles "I'll get Spring to translate everything for you later though!

    She then starts petting the Furfrou again while writing with her better hand

    "You're my new friend and I'm going to get you all fixed!"

    "But you need a name..."

    "I'm gonna call you 'Scout'!"

    "So... Let's go home so Spring can tell you all of this!"

    Aline then gets up and starts rollerskating again as Scout started to follow her, she couldn't wait to show him to everyone back at home.
