    Hazel: < Speedwell! Blackberry! How has the scouting gone?>

    Blackberry: <Hazel-rah. The north has provided to be too difficult for us to cross without risking going to close to the humans. The south, however, can provide us with a sufficant detour from the City.>

    Speedwell: <I don't see the problem with going through the human dwelling at Inle. They sleep just like us. It shouldn't be that troublsome...>

    Blackberry: <As I told you before Speedwell. Humans tend to spend odd hours awake or have Pokemon with sensative ears to wake them up. Dashing right through the city is just begging for unspeakable trouble.>

    Speedwell grumbles and Hazel nods.

    Hazel: <I see, but the night is nearly over and we are tired. Have the party found a decent place to rest.>

    Speedwell: <A group of hedges down a bit south sir.>

    Hazel: <Very well, we'll come along with you two now.>

    Laurel: <And if we're lucky, Dandelion just might treasure us with a story of El-ahrairah and his journeys...>
