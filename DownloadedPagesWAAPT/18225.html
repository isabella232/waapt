    455613 BlitzTrain16th May 2015 06:21:49 PM from The Other Rainforest Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I'm just a poor boy, nobody loves me
    Jiraiya of the Modern Era

    Two days ago

    To: Fletcha
    From: Blitz

    I'm guessing you mean training for battles? Hard to give a definite time-frame, without seeing the mon in person. Some pokemon are practically ready as soon as they come out of the egg!

    I'd say the best way to tell is to let them watch some battles or your team practice, that should give you a good hint for how physically/mentally ready the new member is. Other than that, maybe try some simple exercises or basic moves sometime in the first 1-3 weeks and see how they take to it?

    Obedience training should start almost right away, at least making sure bad habits like using clothing for chewtoys, flooding the house or digging in the garden don't develop.

    Good Luck!
    If you want to be able to do something, then go do it! You learn more from mistakes corrected than perfection never attempted.
