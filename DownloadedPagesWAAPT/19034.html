    Ever Grande City, more than a week ago

    -Zeal stands outside the door to the room Gui is in.-

    Zeal: Is it really okay for me to knock now? Have I given her enough time alone?

    -He debates to himself for a few moments before deciding to knock after all. There's no answer.-

    Zeal: Gui?

    -There's still just silence.-

    Zeal: Gui?!

    -Still no answer. Panicking, he quickly forces the door open. Gui's still in the same corner of the room, still in Latias form. Zeal breathes a sigh of relief.-

    Zeal: Are you— No, of course you're not okay...

    ...What's wrong?

    -Gui says nothing. She puts up her human disguise and turns away from Zeal.-

    Zeal: Please, tell me. I want to help you, Gui.

    ...Well, if you want my help, anyway.

    -Gui says nothing.-

    Zeal: I'm sorry. I'm just... I'm really worried about you.

    -She's still silent.-

    Zeal: Sorry... I'll... I'll give you more time.

    -He slowly turns to leave and walks back toward the door he came in.-

    Gui: Wait, don't...

    -Zeal turns back around. Gui is crying.-

    Gui: Please. Don't go.

    -Zeal walks back toward her and sits down on the floor next to her.-

    Zeal: I won't. I'm here.

    -The two sit in silence for a while.-

    Gui: Did you mean what you said?

    Zeal: Huh?

    Gui: When you said you didn't hate me. Did you mean that?

    Zeal: Of course I meant it! Why... No, what's making you think otherwise?

    Gui: I'm not sure, exactly... I'm sorry.

    -Zeal shakes his head.-

    Zeal: There's nothing you need to apologize for.

    Gui: I...

    -She forces a smile.-

    Thanks.

    -Zeal smiles back.-

    Zeal: Did I really do anything to deserve that thanks?

    Gui: Of course you did, sillyhead!

    ...You came back.

    Zeal: Well, yeah. Why wouldn't I?

    Gui: I'm not sure. I just... got it in my head that you wanted me gone.

    Zeal: I definitely don't. I love having you around.

    In fact, I'm dreading leaving Hoenn. Most of the J-Team's already done with their challenges, it's bound to happen sometime soon. And you'll have to go back home then, won't you? This was always supposed to be a temporary thing.

    Gui: But why do you want me around?

    Zeal: I... why do you keep asking that?

    Gui: I've done nothing for you, why would you care about me?

    Zeal: What are you talking about? You've don tons for me! Heck, you even saved my life back in Petalburg Woods.

    ...You've done way more for me that I've done for you.

    -They both sit silently for a little while again until Gui breaks the silence with a laugh.-

    Gui: Guess we're both idiots, aren't we?

    -Zeal grins.-

    Zeal: Yeah, I guess we really are.

    Gui: ...Do you think it would be that big of a deal if I stayed with you? After Hoenn?

    Zeal: I don't have a clue.

    Gui: This was all supposed to be for my protection. But... I guess you've really been the one who's needed protection, more than me. I just wonder...

    Well, anyway, I don't want to think about leaving yet. Can we not worry about that for now?

    Zeal: Yeah, no problem. I don't really want to think about it either. We've still got some time left.

    Let's try to make the most of it, at least, and wait and see where things go from there.

    -They keep talking a little while longer, until they both fall asleep right there, exhausted from their general lack of sleep the night before.-
    "Sing with me a song of birthrights and love."
