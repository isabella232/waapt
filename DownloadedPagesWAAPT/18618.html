    Imperial Commissar

    Sootopolis Geothermal Plant, Main Floor

    Whilst the two heroes are busy taking care of villains on other levels, Shun - accompanied by Gregson - is slowly and methodically poking his way through the machines on the main floor until he finally comes across one with a big, red button sticking out the front. The text above it reads "MAIN POWER - ON/OFF".

    Shun: ...Hrm. I always assumed that operating a power plant was a more complicated affair than pushing one singular button. Regardless, this should hopefully-

    Suddenly, the nearest wall comes crashing down as an armoured figure mounted on a Steelix bursts through it.

    Earthwyrm: So, it seems that a hapless INSECT has stumbled into my lair! TREMBLE before me, hero of justice, for I shall-

    He pauses mid-rant as he takes a look at Shun.

    Earthwyrm: ...Wait, you're not a superhero. Who are YOU to face the might of EARTHWYRM?

    Shun: I... I'm a police detective, and quite frankly I'm getting tired of dealing with the antics of costumed caperers. Particularly when they threaten the existence of half a city. This needs to end.

    Earthwyrm: END? How can you hope to INTERFERE with my plan when you barely even UNDERSTAND it? You cannot comprehend my genius, for you are a FOOL! You are a foolish fool who has foolishly decided to fool around like the fool you are!

    Shun opens his mouth to respond, then realises he has nothing to say to that statement and closes it again.

    Earthwyrm: Yet even if there WERE a chance for you to defy me, it is far too late for you now! My masterstroke will soon be complete.

    Gregson: <Let me guess: it involves blowing up the plant to destroy the city.>

    Earthwyrm: I will destroy Sootopolis City-

    Gregson: <Called it.>

    Earthwyrm: -'s INTERNET CONNECTION!

    Shun and Gregson both blink in surprise.

    Gregson: <...Okay, that was not what I was expecting.>

    Earthwyrm: You surface-dwellers have grown WEAK and COMPLACENT, unable to survive without constant exposure to the wonders of the web. NO LONGER! My minions have developed a sophisticated jamming device that, when powered and connected to a suitable transmitter, will create an internet dead-zone that extends across the ENTIRE CITY! All of Sootopolis will be at my mercy!

    He gestures grandly with his arm.

    Earthwyrm: After just a few hours without their precious internet, I will have the people of this city on their knees, willing to WORSHIP me if it means I will grant them a few minutes of their precious connection. I shall become their OVERLORD, their-

    Shun: Could you stop shouting for a moment, please? I need a few moments to process all of this.

    He massages his forehead.

    Shun: If I have this all correct... your intention is not to destroy the city, but to take it offline instead.

    Earthwyrm: Of course! I could hardly rule a city that has been reduced to rubble!

    Shun: Even though running the plant at full capacity could potentially cause a catastrophic meltdown anyway.

    Earthwyrm: Irrelevant. My plan shall not be stopped by a few technical imperfections.

    Shun: You are aware that all the city's inhabitants have to do to remedy their situation is send someone to deactivate the plant, correct?

    Earthwyrm: HAH! As if any of your ilk would be capable of besting me!

    Shun: Unfortunately for your ego, that is exactly what I intend to do.

    Earthwyrm: Then you shall be PUNISHED FOR YOUR INSOLENCE! Steelix, use Iron Tail!

    The Steelix spins around and slams into Shun and Gregson with its tail, sending them flying. They both crash into the console and slump to the ground, unconscious, leaving Earthwyrm looking almost disappointed.

    Earthwyrm: ...Huh. That was easy. I always thought crushing inferior foes beneath my steel-toed boots would be rather more satisfying.

    Behind him, Shingeko - who has quietly been making his way upstairs - arrives just in time to see the whole thing. He then commands Ryu to fire a strong Hydro Pump at the Steelix while their presence has gone unnoticed, trying to wear it down so that they can take Earthwyrm down later. The Steelix roars in anger upon being struck and wheels around to face Shingeko and Ryu, Earthwyrm still mounted on its back.

    Earthwyrm: What the-?! Where did you come from? My minions were supposed to have this place secured against any intruders!

    Shingeko: They failed, simple as that.

    Earthwyrm: Blast and confound those wastrels! I suppose I'll just have to deal with you MYSELF!

    As he yells, the Steelix slams its tail into the ground, sending an Earthquake rumbling towards the intruders. It hits both of them, with Shingeko falling to the ground and Ryu managing to keep himself standing. He starts dashing very quickly across the area and then attempts to leap on the Steelix's back to join Earthwyrm, only to be smacked away by another Iron Tail.

    Steelix: <You think you can oppose me? A field of wheat trying to oppose the scythe would have more chance of success.>

    Earthwyrm: Is this the BEST the forces of justice have to offer? Children and weaklings?

    A Water Shuriken is thrown at the back of Earthwyrm's helmet and a set at the Steelix from Shinobi's hiding place on the other side of the room, drawing their attention away from Shingeko.

    Earthwyrm: GAH! My armour's one weakness... RUST! You will pay for this INSULT with several of your major organs, you surface-dwelling sleaze! Steelix, Stone Edge!

    The Steelix fires a hail of razor-sharp rocks in Shinobi's direction which are caught on a protect barrier from the now normal-type Greninja

    Shinobi: <Gotta catch me first!>

    He makes a "bring it" gesture at Earthwyrm before hopping away to keep their attention away from the control panels and the others. Meanwhile, Shingeko spots the unconscious detective duo and goes to help them whilst Ryu just watches what Shinobi is doing. Shun stirs weakly as Shingeko approaches.

    Shun: ...no, Mom, I don't wanna play with the others...

    Earthwyrm and his Steelix go slithering after Shinobi, still believing him to be the most immediate threat.

    Earthwyrm: Only COWARDS and TAPS run like that! Bulldoze the area, Steelix!

    Steelix: <Gladly.>

    It slams its tail into the ground again and again, throwing Shinobi in one of the seismic shockwaves as he lands, The Greninja quickly gets to his feet and claps his hands together, turning grass-type as the vines of a Grass Knot attack and attempt to pull the Steelix's head to the floor. It roars as it comes crashing down, bringing Earthwyrm along with it.

    Earthwyrm: NO! I will not have my reputation as an evil overlord RUINED by defeat at the hands of a mere sidekick! Rise, my steed!

    The Steelix groans and doesn't move.

    Steelix: <In a minute...>

    Earthwyrm: I said GET UP!

    He digs his heels into the Steelix's sides - a pointless gesture, given that he's riding a Pokemon covered in thick metal armour. Nevertheless, it annoys the Steelix to the point where it's able to break free of the Grass Knot and shake itself off.

    Earthwyrm: This is too much to handle without minions... you may have won this round, heroes, but I will return! It will take more than a couple of frogs to defeat the EARTHWYRM!

    His Steelix turns and crashes through a nearby wall, tunnelling away. Shingeko notices that, but prioritizes Shun first and begins shaking him awake.

    Shingeko: Come on, kid! It's still not the time for the little children to sleep!

    Meanwhile Ryu tries to go after the Steelix, firing another Hydro Pump at its back, but by then it's already burrowed out of reach. Shingeko's attempts to rouse Shun meets with rather more success, however, as the boy blearily starts to regain consciousness.

    Shun: Urgh... is it over...?

    Shinobi: <Almost had him...>

    He pats Ryu's shoulder before looking over the controls and pushing the big, shiny OFF button. Almost immediately, the flashing red emergency lights are replaced by normal non-flashy yellow ones and all the whirring machines shut down.

    Shingeko: Yeah, we took care of things while you were busy taking a nap. Two of them got away but at least I took one of them down.

    Ryu sighs, but then gives Shinobi an impressed look.

    Ryu: <When did you get so good at fighting? You're making me look bad.>

    Shinobi: <Sometime between almost getting assassinated, working for Mew and now I guess?>

    Shinobi chuckles and rubs the back of his head awkwardly.

    Shinobi: <I figured you'd have to bail me out after that Protect.>

    Shun groans, then heaves himself to his feet, leaning against the nearest available console for support. Perhaps thankfully, the exchange between Shinobi and Ryu simply passes him by.

    Shun: At least we have a culprit...

    He glances around, still looking rather out of sorts, then turns back to Shingeko.

    Shun: ...What happened to your sidekick?

    Shingeko: Don't worry about him, he's all right, but... He went out to get some more help.

    He makes a dismissive gesture with his hand.

    Shingeko: Anyway let's go downstairs and check up on our friend down there.

    Shun nods and starts hobbling towards the stairs, whilst Ryu hangs back with Shinobi.

    Ryu: <You really didn't need it from the looks of it. Man, you're good at being a Pokémon.>

    Shinobi: <Guess that means you guys are good teachers. But thanks, it means a lot to me.>

    Shinobi gives a smile as they follow behind Shingeko and Shun.

    Imperial Commissar

    Sootopolis Geothermal Plant, Server Room

    Down below in the server room, whilst the exciting climactic battle with Earthwyrm is drawing to a close, Helion is still being restrained by Strider and Teishi.

    Helion: ...Don't suppose either of you know any good card games?

    Teishi and Strider just look at each other for a moment and then shake their heads in response. Suddenly, Batastrophe and her Clefable materialise in the corner of the room with a blip.

    Batastrophe: Hiya, Helion! I- oh hey, you didn't tell me you'd made new friends!

    The Clefable immediately uses Dazzling Gleam on Teishi and Strider, who are caught off guard and hit by it, Teishi letting go of Helion in the process. In spite of her own surprise, she quickly gets to her feet, readjusting her gauntlets.

    Helion: I wonder if these have recharged yet...

    She points them at Teishi and Strider, and an arc of electricity comes shooting out, which they both manage to dodge.

    Helion: That answers that question.

    Teishi quickly then attacks the Clefable with a Hidden Power Steel, which sends it bouncing backwards.

    Clefable: <Oof! That was uncalled for.>

    It uses Metronome, which results in Teishi being dusted with sleep-inducing Spores and he soon collapses on the ground, asleep. Strider then attempts to use Lick on the Clefable, trying to paralyze. Unfortunately, that doesn't happen.

    Clefable: <Better luck next time, old chap.>

    It retaliates with a Moonblast which hits, but thanks to the type change it doesn't do as much, though at this point Strider was barely holding on.

    Strider: <Tch...>

    He then falls to the ground, not unconscious but without any energy left. Helion breathes a sigh of relief, then turns to Batastrophe, who flashes her a broad grin.

    Batastrophe: You look surprised to see me. What, didya think I'd bail on you?

    Helion: ...Of course I didn't-

    Batastrophe: You did! You thought I'd bail on you, you big fat jerk.

    She punches Helion on the arm.

    Batastrophe: Luckily for you, I'm an awesome friend, so I won't hold it against you~ Plus, I got to beat up Shingeko's stupid sidekick, so I'm in a good mood right now.

    Helion tilts her head at the object in Batastrophe's hand.

    Helion: Is that his helmet?

    Batastrophe: Yup~ I dunno whether to put it on display or start wearing it myself. Either way, you'll help me get it fixed up, right?

    Helion: ...Sure, if I can't find anything better to do.

    Batastrophe fist-pumps.

    Batastrophe: Yes! Aw man, you're the best.

    She pulls Helion into a hug, taking care to avoid the electrified bits of her costume.

    Batastrophe: C'mon, let's get outta here. We need to find out where the boss ran off to. Hold on tight!

    Helion: ...If you say so.

    The Clefable grabs hold of the conjoined pair and blips all three of them away. Moments later, Shingeko drops down to see the two Greninja on the ground.

    Shingeko:... What? She escaped?!

    Shun, now looking somewhat less dazed, sighs.

    Shun: Perhaps we ought to pay more attention to containment next time around.

    Shingeko also sighs.

    Shingeko: The other girl must have come here and caught them off guard...

    He then moves over to Teishi and shakes him awake, though it takes a few tries. Once he wakes up, Teishi frantically looks around for a few seconds before realizing what happened.

    Shingeko: Yeah, she got away.

    Shun: At least you were able to stop them before their harebrained scheme caused any real damage.

    Shingeko: True.

    He then goes over to Strider and helps him get up.

    Shingeko: Man, you guys got really beaten up today. I'll have to compensate you all for this later. They might have gotten away but you still did great today.

    He then gives Strider a Sitrus Berry and turns to Shun.

    Shingeko: Guess now we should leave before this whole place falls apart on us.

    Shinobi, who had been looking over the damage once he saw Shingeko treating the other Greninja, nods.

    Shinobi: <Yeah, looks like a real mess down here.>

    Shun: Hopefully, there's no longer a chance of that happening. I do agree that we should leave with all haste, however, as this plant has consumed quite enough of my time already. Any further discussions can wait until we are are outside.

    He turns and starts heading back up the stairs.

    Sootopolis City

    Soon, the detective/superhero/Pokemon trio emerge from the plant entrance, where they are met with a wave from the scientist.

    Scientist: Oh hey, you're finally out! Looks like you shut down the plant, too.

    Shun: Indeed. With any luck, the city is no longer in danger.

    The scientist looks confused.

    Scientist: ...Wait, the city was in danger?

    Shun: ...That's what you told us. You said that the plant could blow up, which in turn could trigger a landslide that would level the city.

    Scientist: Well, yeah. I said the plant could blow up... if you got it generating electricity. But the volcano's dormant, so even if the plant's active, it's still going to be producing exactly the same amount of power: zero. Sure, hitting the "on" button might make the machinery rattle a bit, maybe turn on the scary red warning lights, but nothing dangerous is gonna happen. I thought you knew that.

    She gives a smile.

    Scientist: But hey, at least you caught the villain!

    Shinobi facepalms. Shun's expression at this point can best be described as "100% done".

    Shingeko: Well, good to know there wasn't any danger. Even if there wasn't any harm in turning the plant on, at least we stopped three people who are going around causing trouble. What made it a waste was just that we couldn't catch any of them.

    He sighs and shrugs.

    Shingeko: Anyway, I'll need to get a new helmet for my sidekick and take care of my Pokémon, so I think this is where we'll be parting ways.

    He begins walking away and waves at Shun as his Greninja follow behind him.

    Shingeko: Be sure to call me when something is up again, prodigy.

    Shun: Certainly. I'll be in touch to discuss our continued partnership.

    He runs his hands down his face.

    Shun: For now, though, I think I need to lie down for a while and lament the idiocy of the world.

    Gregson: <Sounds like a plan to me.>

    The boy and his Sableye start heading back towards the Pokemon Center, leaving the plant behind them.
