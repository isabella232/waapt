    Copahue — <Hmmm... you, the one with the bag...>

    Pidgey — <But... we're nice, eeeeeep~>

    Copahue — <So you are nice?> — strongly pecks the ground right besides the Pidgey, missing his neck by an centimeter and change — <Then you'll have no issue leaving those nuts to her, right?>

    Pidgey — <Err...> — blinks — <Well, no... just don't kill me please I'm cute~>

    Shahinne — <Yay! I love those nuts!>

    -Copahue extends his beak and grabs the bag with his beak from the nearest Pidgey, swings it to pick it up with the other talon, then extends his wings, scaring the other Pidgey-

    Copahue — <Say bye to your... "friends", Shahinne... We have to go see Trainer.> — to the Pidgey — <I better not see you again.>

    Shahinne — <Bye Pidgeys!    Who can't sing as pretty as I do~   > — waves a wing to the Pidgeys.

    -The Pidgeys, under a sort of trance, wave a goodbye as Copahue flies back to the front yard of the lab.-



    -A couple of seconds pass-

    -The Pidgeys start chatting-

    "That was unexpected"

    "We almost had her..."

    "Awww...."

    Lead Pidgey — <Well, that was close. But we are alive and pretty, peoplemons!>

    -The Pidgey stands up and ruffles his plummage, when he is suddenly grabbed by some vines and held up, the other Pidgeys staring in disbelief as their Leader floats above the still hungry Victreebell-

    Victreebell — <...Well?>

    Lead Pidgey — <...Oh! Well, there- er... we have nuts and...>

    Lead Pidgey — <coo~>

    Victreebell — <I was told there would be noms...>
