    Thanks to Dogs for spag! ALSO WARNING THERE IS SOME BODY HORROR SO LOOK OUT.

    FUAM, Abandoned Manor, Basement

    Samantha is still strapped to the repurposed dentist’s chair. The lights flicker on as Lazarus and Glendower come down the stairs.

    Lazarus: So, dear, have a nice time down here? Everything’s satisfactory?

    Samantha grits her teeth in frustration and anger, glaring over to Lazarus.

    Samantha: Do you think being strapped to a dentist chair is satisfactory?

    Lazarus: Well, so long as you give us five stars on [Trip Advisor], I’m happy.

    Samantha: Yeah, I’ll make a point to do that.

    She couldn’t sound more deadpan and miserable if she tried.

    Lazarus: Still have some sass in you. Let’s fix that. Glendower!

    The Metagross picks up something behind Samantha, out of her vision. Lazarus walks over and now secures her head, in addition to her already strapped in limbs.

    Lazarus: Don’t want you dislodging anything.

    Samantha looks, bewildered with anger and (though she would loathe to admit it) fear.

    Samantha: What are you doing, bastard?

    Lazarus: Just a little experiment. You like that sort of thing? Baking soda volcanoes, screwing with things man was not meant to know? Good times. Here.

    Glendower passes the object, which turns out to be a breathing apparatus attached to a hose.

    Lazarus: Let’s get you gassed up. Any last words before we start the test?

    Samantha tries to decide exactly what to say to properly express her anger and hatred, but figured that a simple “fuck you” could suffice.

    Samantha: Fuck y-

    Lazarus: TOO LATE!

    He slaps the breathing mask on her, strapping it on quickly.

    Lazarus: Glendower! The average dosage, please!

    The Metagross pushes some buttons, still out of Samantha’s view. Gas starts flowing through the hose, and Samantha breathes it in.

    Lazarus: Sweet dreams!

    Samantha opens her mouth to say something, but the words get stuck in her throat as she looks down at her arm and sees a small lump moving down her bicep towards her forearm.

    Samantha: HMMMPH!

    She hyperventilates, rapidly, trying desperately to remind herself that this is probably just some sort of fear gas, the sort that the Scarekrow uses. It’s hard to remind yourself that you’re in no real danger, however, when you see your skin bulge out obscenely at your wrist, feeling a deep, vibrating buzz inside you.

    Samantha: MMMMMMMPH!

    It had to be the Spinarak dream, didn’t it? If she remembered it right, it would be about here that the Poison type’s brethren would migrate down towards her hands, moving rapidly with each other. The difference between the dream and here, of course, was that any sensation Samantha could feel was cloaked in a dreamlike haze. Here, she feels everything, as sure as her hair being red. Panicked, she thrashes about, scrunching her eyes shut to blot out the sight, if not the feeling.

    Lazarus: Hmm. Interesting. Symptoms at this dosage include active, if not knowing, movement. Glendower, what is she seeing.

    <‘Glendower: A generic nightmare, milord. Spinarak, I believe.>

    Lazarus: Fascinating. Continue observation.

    Samantha is vaguely aware of clinical observations taking place, but she’s too terrified to give them much thought. Rattling about in her binds, she does her best to try and block out the horrid crawling sensation beneath her skin. That fails, miserably-it’s the only thing she can think about, the chittering and crawling and skittering and poking and biting and weaving. So, in lieu of that, she hopes they don’t do anything else; this was where she woke up when she had this dream, after all.

    <Glendower: Milord, the dream is about to end.>

    Lazarus: Hmm. Keep the gas flowing. Let’s see if we can ‘’push’’ it.

    The crawling beneath her skin becomes an even worse sensation. There is a straining feeling on her skin, as though something is pushing itself outwards in an effort to free itself. Samantha knows it’s coming, but can’t help staring at the small, black Spinarak poking out from her arm and crawling up towards her. When she sees the second, third, fourth and fifth, however, is when she starts screaming.

    Lazarus: Wonderful! Glendower, shut it down. Let’s bring her out.

    Glendower pushes some more buttons, and the flow of gas stops. Lazarus waits for Samantha to show signs of lucidity before speaking.

    Lazarus: So, how was that? Did you have a good trip?

    Breathing quick to either calm herself or to expel the gas, Samantha briefly goes limp, staying that way for a half-minute before she answers, weakly and distantly.

    Samantha: Does it look like I had a good trip?

    Lazarus: Well, it was good for me. Glendower, tell me when she’s recovered. Don’t want to contaminate the next test.

    <Glendower: Aye, milord.>

    Lazarus waits in the dim light, content to keep silent for a while. Samantha makes a point not to speak or even acknowledge the two of them, taking it upon herself to calm herself.

    <Glendower: She’s ready, milord.>

    Lazarus: Wonderful! This time, up the dosage. Maximum non-lethal.

    Buttons are pushed, and the gas comes rushing back down the tube. Samantha tries to open her mouth to scream, but her lips won’t part. Come to think of it, normally she’d be thrashing about and wriggling like a Wurmple on a hook, but she can’t seem to move at all.

    Samantha: Stun Spore…?

    Alas, not so merciful. Stun Spore doesn’t cause everything surrounding you to slowly melt.

    Samantha: ...oh no.

    It was as though the room was a painting splattered with turpentine. The damp, moldy walls start to run. The machinery, Musharna and all, melts together in a mess of pink and gun-metal gray. Lazarus himself, though he seems unaware, becomes gelatinous and mushy.

    Lazarus: Hmm. No movement this time.

    <Glendower: That may be due to the nature of the nightmare, milord.>

    Lazarus: Ah, impotence. A fascinating fear. We’ll have to test someone else, make sure the paralysis is not a fluke.

    The room and all its occupants melt into a large puddle, colored a dull brown from the muddle of colors. There’s only white surrounding Samantha as she lays paralyzed, alone and useless.

    <Glendower: She’s at the nadir, milord. Shall I shut off the flow?>

    Lazarus: Might as well. At that dosage, she’ll be locked in it for a few hours anyways.

    The gas stops. Hours pass, Samantha still in the depths of the nightmare. Hours, as it happens, pass slower when you’re alone. A few hours in such a nightmare feel like an entire day alone, locked in your own body and unable to do anything but panic. In the back of her mind, Samantha tells herself to hug Maria once she sees her again, but a smaller part of her tells her that such a possibility isn’t a certainty. Finally, she begins to surface.

    Lazarus: There we go, nice and easy. That’s it sweetheart.

    He finally unstraps the breathing mask.

    Lazarus: That’s all I wanted, darling! And now we can let you go. Eventually. But remember - I know your deepest fear. Come Glendower.

    Glendower shuts off the machine and moves back into Samantha’s field of view.

    Lazarus: Sleep tight. Don’t let the nightmares bite.

    He climbs the stairs and flips the lights off, leaving her alone in the dark again.

    Please, give me and Dogs feedback once you've read! We really appreciate it!
