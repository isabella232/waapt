    Joseph's Dream

    -Joseph's eyes snap open, but he's still in darkness. His arms are tied above his head, and attached to the very low ceiling so that he stands ever so slightly on his tiptoes. Thrashing, he finds his legs bound together, too. He starts to hyperventilate-luckily, he's not gagged-

    Joseph: HELP!

    -a soft, girlish giggle in his ear-

    Kyrie: What's the point, Joseph? This is a dream; it's not even yours. There's nothing you can do. There's no boyfriend to trade his life in to save yours. No cavalry of J-Team members. I doubt you could save yourself even if I didn't tie you.

    -she drags her bat against the ground, circling Joseph-

    Kyrie: I look at you, Joseph, and do you know what I see?

    Joseph: You see the one that will take you down someday, Valkyrie.

    -another laugh-

    Kyrie: You've learned nothing, haven't you? It's not your dream. Perhaps I can teach you.

    -she raises her bat and swings; a blinding pain goes through Joseph's leg, and he screams before biting his lip to muffle it-

    Kyrie: No, no, no, Joseph. Don't shush yourself. I didn't gag you for a reason. I wanna hear you before I make your teeth fall out.

    -Joseph shakes his head, blinking back tears. A mock exasperated sigh from Kyrie-

    Kyrie: You want to play that game? Fine. I'll make you scream.

    -a loud grunt and another swing; pain, worse than anything he's ever felt, goes through his other leg. Joseph tries not to scream. Joseph can feel the crazed grin, even in the darkness-

    Kyrie: Aw, what's the matter? I'm sure you scream louder than that for Munn. Let me hear it!

    -she takes out a knife and begins to cut through the rope suspending him. Once she saws through, Joseph collapses to the ground, his legs broken and useless. Another laugh from Kyrie-

    Kyrie: Do you know what I see when I look at you, Joseph? I see someone who thinks that he's the heart of all his friends. He thinks he ties everyone together, in good times and bad, in Kalos and Orre, through his love for everyone.

    -a combat boot stomps on his arm; he screams louder-

    Kyrie: But that's not really it, is it? They've lived without you their entire lives; they don't need anyone to feel for them, let alone an oversensitive fairy boy with the maturity of a six year old.

    -she begins to bend down, slowly, her face lowering to Joseph's-

    Kyrie: And when you get right down to it, that's all you are, isn't it? A little white-haired faggot. You're barely even a man. Half woman. Half man.

    -now, in the darkness, Joseph sees her. Her teeth extend in fangs from her mouth, digging into her lower lip. Her eyes, once a light blue, are now a searing red. She grins-

    Kyrie: Some people need you, but not them.

    -she lowers her bat towards Joseph, her mouth opened in a silent shout-

    ???

    -Joseph opens his eyes. He takes a few deep breaths. He sits up-

    -and he panics, clutching for his coat, hugging it to his chest, and sobbing-
    A tooth for an eye!
