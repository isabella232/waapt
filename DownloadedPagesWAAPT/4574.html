    Felix: May? May?

    Surprisingly, there was no answer.

    Felix: May? Are you in there?

    His question was answered by a sudden snore and some mumbling, both having a distinct feminine sound.

    Felix: ...Is she still asleep?

    It was odd that he'd wake up before May... Shrugging to himself, Felix left the inn, leaving a note under May's door explaining that he had stepped out for the moment.
