    Flashback! Dewford - Early morning

    -Tori has fallen asleep on the bed with a Cyndaquil, Floatzel and Skiploom while watching a romantic comedy on the hotel's television. The Skiploom however has yet to sleep, and wriggles out of Tori's grasp to venture out into the chilly morn. Eventually Float settles on a quiet area to watch for the eventual sunrise. It's pretty obvious for anyone to see she's got a few things on her mind-

    -Crow has woken up early as well and has decided to fly around, ending up near Float. He notices her and offers a greeting-

    Murkrow: <Hey!>

    -Float quietly turns at the greeting, then hops back slightly at seeing the bird-mon- <Eeep!>

    -Crow blinks-

    Murkrow: <Uh... something wrong?>

    Float: <U-um, no. Sorry...> -She shakes her head- <Just... Not used to friendly Murkrow>

    -She looks away for a moment, possibly in search of any other birds, possibly from feeling sheepish-

    Float: <Er, good morning?>

    Murkrow: <Oh, huh. Personally most Murkrow I've met have been friendly for the most part.>

    <Anyway, are you from around here? I know there are Skiploom further south, but...>

    Float': <Well, I came from an island, er What did Tori call it?... Oh, from Oblivia. But the Murkrow there were kinda mean. They'd attack me sometimes when I tried to pick berries...>

    -She looks a bit uncomfortable at the memory- <B-but I had a friend, a Shinx that drove them off sometimes>

    Murkrow: <Oh, that's unfortunate. Good you had someone to help you, though.>

    Float: <Yeah, he's been watching out for me for a while now... I think he really likes me>

    -The Skiploom sighs- I kinda feel bad for him, though... Since we can't really be together

    Murkrow: <Oh? What kind of like? You seem kind of down about it, though.>

    Float: <Uhm, well... A lot. I guess. But, he's a Luxio, and I'm a Skiploom so... I kinda don't want him to end up in one of those tragic 'star-crossed lovers' situations>

    -She rocks back and forth slightly- <He said he had an idea... But I don't now if it's really possible>

    Murkrow: <Hm... I'm not sure if you have much to worry about. I'm kinda close to a Venomoth and Primeape who seem to have made it work between them. And a Gardevoir I know is apparently dating a Scizor too.>

    <But what do you mean?>

    Float: <Really? That good to hear> -She stops for a moment, wondering about whether to continue or not-

    <I-it's kinda weird...> -She blushes a bit- <He, um, heard about somemon who could change from pokemon to human... And I think he wanted to find a way for us to do that>

    Murkrow: <Hm... Yeah, that's not an uncommon thing among the peoplemons I know. But... have either of you ever been human before?>

    Float: -Tilts curiously- <Oh?>

    -She looks elsewhere for a moment- <Um, no... Actually the idea sounds a little scary. You know, to just... stop being like this>
