    Neo-Flare HQ, ???

    Lex and the woman beam into the main teleporter room of the rusty old, vine-covered building Neo-Flare has made it's HQ.

    Neo-Flare Scout: Here we are. The HQ is a still a work in progress, but we are working on it. Mainly, we need a sizeable amount of funds to actually get this place running as the boss sees fit.

    Lex: Oh! Who is the boss anyway?

    Neo-Flare Scout: Oh, you'll meet him soon enough. In fact, we should be heading to his quarters right now.

    Lex begins twiddling his fingers.

    Lex: Is he... nice?

    The scouts flicks her wrist.

    Neo-Flare Scout: Depends on your definition of "nice". And his mood, he's an erratic one.

    Lex gulps as he begins following the woman around the building. On their way, he observes the various peons scurrying around and making busy of themselves; cleaning up some of the vines, moving boxes and unpacking their wares, installing machines and making meaningless banter around coffee machines and water coolers.

    Lex: Huh. It's a little bit rustic.

    Neo-Flare Scout: Indeed. We intend to remove all the unwanted growths and bring the place up to our leader's high standards.

    Lex: Though I quite like the vines and plants, it makes the place feel less... sterile and lifeless. It's quite pretty, actually.

    Lex goes over to examine one. He takes a good look at the leaves and the stem before giving a small smile.

    Lex: Looks like a Lablab purpureus, also known as the Hyacinth Bean Vine. Native of the [African] continent. Their beans and flowers are the most beautiful shades of purple you know...

    Neo-Flare Scout: A botanist and an inventor. You are a man of many talents, Alexandre.

    Lex: Oh Lex is just fine, but yeah. Growing up, mother made sure we had a wide, scholarly reading. Which is funny because Pakhet always said she hated studying herself as a young person.

    Neo-Flare Scout: And you studied flowers?

    Lex: And gastronomy. And clothing design, sewing and textiles. Though the latter is because of the many days we would spend with my Tatie Louise at Ton-Ton Lawrence's place in Cyllage...

    Neo-Flare Scout: What feminine pursuits for a young man. Strange.

    Lex bites his lip.

    Lex: Well, everyone has their own way of being themselves. Regardless if they are a guy or a girl, right?

    Neo-Flare Scout: Hmph. I suppose.

    The woman stops in front of a door and turns around, Lex seems a bit on edge.

    Neo-Flare Scout: These are the bossman's quarters, if you don't mind waiting here. I will announce your arrival and have him examine you to see if you are really up to snuff to work for Neo-Flare.

    Lex: Oh that al- wait, what?

    Lex falls into a babbling stutter as the woman knocks on the door and enters the room. While whatever discussion is going on inside, Lex breaks into a panicky sweat as his mind races with worry.

    Lex: Neo-Flare? I got hired by Neo-Flare!? I only know about snippets of this, but Sal always said they were ruthless, heartless monsters who would rather see the world in ashes than have people around that they didn't like. How do I get out of this? Where is Lucrece and Salvador when I need them? Why do I always get into bad situations when I'm on my own?

    As Lex's breathing becomes quicker, the scout walks out of the door and gives Lex the signal that he's good to go. Lex attempts to regain his composure, with not much success, as the woman walks out.

    Neo-Flare Scout: You are free to see the Leader now.

    Before she walks off to do some other job though, she leans over and whispers into Lex's ear.

    Neo-Flare Scout: You better pull yourself together if you're gonna survive here. The boss can pretty much smell fear from a mile away. Like a Sharpedo~

    This emits a high pitched sound from Lex as the woman smiles and walks off. Mustering as much courage as he possible can, he walks over to the door, slowly turns the handle and peers his head through the door.

    He gets a view of the room. Nice wooden walls, baize mattings and antiquated furniture dotting the room here and there. It's obvious that the leader is a man of wealth and taste, at least when it comes to interior design. At the desk, a woman with long black hair curled in ringlets is leaning, picking her fingers with a boning knife and chatting to the man behind the desk, who is idly maneuvering his cigarette holder between his long, gloved fingers.

    Lex: Um, hello?

    The woman looks over to the head in the door and gives a creepy, wide eyed smile.

    Maria: Well, Well! Fresh Meat, I'm assuming you are the wonderful scientist that our scouts reported on?

    Lex: Wait they've been following me for months? Um, yes, well, I-

    Constantin: Come in! Come in! I can barely hear you from back there.

    Lex: Okay... Okay...

    Lex inches ever so slightly into the room and shuts the door quietly behind him, before tiptoeing slowly Constantin's desk.

    Maria: Oh looky here, we send the scouts out to find a scientist and they bring back a mouse. You aren't normally this skittish, are you?

    Lex sits down as he looks over to Maria and frowns.

    Lex: Well, erm, I, yes.

    Maria: Oh me, oh my. Thankfully, you're not gonna be out on the front lines~

    Maria starts picker her teeth with her boning knife as Lex turns his attention to the tall man going over the papers on his desk. Lex tilts his head at him, he looks awfully familiar...

    Constantin: So. You are the inventor we got reports about in Peultown, oui?

    Lex: Oui monsieur, je suis l'homme de Peultown. Et qui êtes-vous?

    Constantin looks up from his papers and raises an eyebrow at Lex.

    Constantin: Another Kalos native I see. With the name on your document, you did strike me as someone who was born and raised in fair Kalos. May I ask where from?

    Lex: Lumoise sir.

    Constantin: Lumoise? Are you - in any way - related to the wealthy Cain family that was brutally slaughtered last year?

    Lex: ...doy?

    Constantin: Oh that backwater you found yourself living in didn't get the news? The prestigious Cain family's youngest child went mad, absolutely insane. Murdered her father, her brother and the Pokemon that were with them. She's now incarcerated indefinitely, and the family business picked at like a Madibuzz feasting on a corpse. Of course, the silently but equally decrepit Kalosian-Arabs in the de Niles and al-Gala's got the Pyroar's share of that carcass.

    Your Lex.exe has stopped working.

    Constantin: Is some thing a matter, Alexandre?

    Lex: No. No. Nope. Nopity. Nope, No. Just fine. Peachy really. Just... sudden and unexpected. Anyway, no. No relation. Just a coincidence really. Yep.

    Constantin and Maria exchange a short glance, before both raising an eyebrow in conjunction with each other.

    Maria: Told ya he was skittish.

    Constantin: Anyway...

    He goes back to his notes.

    Constantin: From the contents of your room, we found that there were a number of small robotic devices just lying around, as well as a number of... textiles oddly enough. The functions of the devices were simple, but there we're a lot of them. Are they prototypes towards a bigger invention?

    Lex: Oh those things? Naw, I just build them for fun! Takes my mind off things, yannow. The textiles however, are a project of mine~

    Constantin: So you build quite functionable machines... for fun?

    Lex: Yeah, and they don't take long to build either! I've literally boiled it down to a science...tific equation! Lemme break it down for you-

    Constantin waves his hand

    Constantin: No need. But if these are just machines created as pastime hobby, what do you create as a fully fleshed project?

    Lex: Well...

    Lex looks down at his right arm and places his left hand on his "bicep".

    Lex: There is... something... I can show... I guess...

    Maria: Oh? Why the hesitation love? We're not going to dismember you~

    Lex: -Gulping- I don't normally like showing this to people... but, yannow, situation calls. And you did ask nicely...

    Lex pulls up his sleeve and takes off his right-hand glove as Constantin and Maria's eyes widen at the state of the art mechanical limb a la Lex.

    Maria: I take back what I said about dismemberment! I would KILL for that arm!

    While Lex stares at Maria with an expression of absolute horror, Constantin grabs Lex's mecha-arm and examines it.

    Constantin: Is this a titanium carapace?

    Lex: Tungsten-Titanium alloy actually. Incredibly durable. As for some of the aspects of my arm, well.

    Lex opens up the panel on his wrist.

    Lex: A fully functioning on board computer touchpad with in-built wifi.

    Lex presses a button on the touchpad which causes another panel on the top of his robo-hand to slide open

    Lex; A High Energy Laser. I mainly use it for cutting through thick substances, though.

    Lex does a few more touchscreen taps as the panel on his hand slides shut while another on his upper arm slides open, revealing a small turret of sorts.

    Lex: A Compact Infrared Targeting Turret. It's a peashooter (but a lot more lethal), and I don't know why I installed it in the first place, but it has helped my scare off a number of street thugs so that always helps.

    A few more taps as the turret pops back in and panels at the end of his fingertips open one by one.

    Lex: And my personal best invention, Zero-Point Energy. Best for moving heavy objects-

    Lex points his hand to the empty armchair on the left side of the room and flicks his index finger, causing a white-blue light to beam out and cover his target. He lifts it up, left and right before placing it back down again.

    Lex: And No Selling annoying, Auric-capable younger siblings who try to hit you on the back of your head with an Aura Sphere. Or lift you off the ground with their Psychic powers. Or spooky you with chill-shadows...

    Constantin and Maria are flabbergasted. Though very much impressed.

    Maria: I must say I had my doubts when I saw yah walk in, but these are VERY impressive indeed.

    Constantin: Excuse me I need to make a call to someone.

    Constantin walks over to the intercom and begins making a call for "The Head Scientist" to make his way to the Leader's Quarters as Lex just sits there, twiddling his thumbs and occasionally glancing at Constantin.

    Constantin: Yes, yes. He's well worth your time. Arrive ASAP. Bye *click*

    Constantin walks back and sits down at his desk tapping his index fingers together.

    Constantin: I could feel you staring at me from over there, have anything you wish to say?

    Lex snaps out of his trance and shakes his head.

    Lex: No! No! I was just thinking of how much you look like someone I-

    __Realization has hit threshold_

    Lex: Nooooo....

    Constantin: -Raises an eyebrow, again-

    Lex: Oh, nothing of importance sir!

    Lex bites his cheek and breaks his eye contact with Constantin. Maria looks confused but brushes this off as one of the man's eccentricities.

    Odin: -is suddenly right behind Lex- So, this is the new twerp?

    Lex: GUH!

    Lex looks behind at Odin. At first he seems a bit surprised, but then he gets a closer look at Odin and seems to take a mental Double Take before looking back at Constantin.

    Then back at Odin

    The back to Constantin

    Before making a whine and slumping slightly.

    Odin: -giggles- I love when they jump.

    -starts circling Lex-

    Odin: My dear cousin said you had things to show me... Well?

    Lex seems preoccupied with staring at Odin for the moment, blinking occasionally.

    Lex: -Snapping out of it again- Oh, sorry. It's just that you reminded me of someone I knew. My best friend, his youngest sister - Freyja - she has the same heterochromia you do.

    Odin: -blinks- One red eye and one blue eye?

    Lex: Yeah. Apparently her grandmother had the same strange combination. Then again, that family always had strange genetics popping up here and there.

    Odin: I see... Anyway, what have you got that Cons wanted to show me?

    Constantin: Show him the Zero-Point Energy. That one I find is most intriguing.

    Lex: Alrighty then...

    Once again, he flicks his index finger at the armchair which covers it in a harsh white-blue light, and once again lifts it up, left, right and back down again.

    Constantin: Apparently, not even those on the Auric Spectrum can break it's grasp~

    -Odin stares in shock for a few seconds before grinning and clapping-

    Odin: Wonderful, wonderful! You did find a good one. What else can he do?

    Maria: Don't get too worked up Spunky, we want this fella in good shape. You gotta play nice with yer toys or else they'll break~

    Constantin: Lex, do show cousin that High Energy Laser and Compact Infrared Targeting Turret of yours s'il vous plait~

    Lex nods as he shows off the laser and the turret in quick succession.

    Odin: -giggles maniacally- Brilliant! This kid has it all!

    -leans in close to Lex and grins-

    Odin: You'd make a damn good apprentice...

    Lex is holding his hands together quite tightly as he bites his lower lip.

    Lex: Thinkhappythoughtthinkhappythoughtsthinkhappythoughts!

    Maria, meanwhile, pulls out the black fabric collected from Lex's Pueltown apartment.

    Maria: But, what about the clothes? Odd thing for an inventor to focus on, makin' dresses.

    Lex furrows his brow at Maria

    Lex: Actually, that itself is a work in progress. Here, give me the fabric.

    Maria hands Lex the fabric.

    Lex: Now on the surface it just looks like regular, old black fabric. However, it's actually covered in nanoscopic lenses and light refracting cones. Triggered by a small current or pulse, you get...

    Lex sends a light shock through the fabric, causing it to go temporarily invisible.

    Lex: Nothing

    Maria: ...Wow.

    Constantin gestures for the fabric, as Lex obliges. He holds it up in the sunlight with his right hand, and covers his left with weak psychic energy before pressing it against the cloth as he watches his hand disappear right before his very eyes.

    Constantin: Mon Dieu...

    Odin: -giggles again- This kid really does has it all!

    Constantin repeatedly removes his hand from the fabric before touching it once more and giving his own, chilling childish giggle.

    Constantin: Now you see me, now you don't~ Now you see me, now you don't~ Eh..hehehehehe~

    Lex: That's it. I'm dead. I'm dying and currently on a journey to the afterlife where the Gods that be throw every bad thing at you to see if your worthy for eternal rest. *whine*

    Constantin: Lex!

    Lex: -Perking up- Yessir!?

    Constantin: I want you to make an entire suit out of this. Just one for now. But I do expect nothing but the highest quality from you even in a prototype phase, oui?

    Lex: O-oui.

    Constantin hands Lex back the fabric and looks up at Odin.

    Constantin: Cousin, do take Lex here down to the labs so he can familiarize himself with the equipment. Oh, and do be careful with him. A hair split on his head and I'll use your innards as Christmas decorations.

    Odin: Oh cousin you tease~ Would my intestines be the tinsel or the lights?

    Constantin: The tinsel. Unless your intestines have bioluminescent spots on them that glow in intervals.

    Odin: Got it!

    -Grabs Lex by the non-mechanical arm and hauls him off to the labs-

    Lex: This is going to be a rough haul...

    As the door shuts behind them, Constantin goes back to finish off his notes with a large smile plastered on his face. Maria tuts, as she stares back at the now closed door.

    Maria: Not gonna lie, that kid is a genius. But something about him... it's off, yannow.

    Constantin: I understand Maria, but I'm willing to put that "off-ness" aside for that brilliant mind of his.

    Maria: Couldn't you scan his mind or somethin'?

    Constantin: Actually no. He is one of those rare humans who's mind when attempted to be read by others is nothing but a blank space. Makes him a good subject to tell secrets to, as he seems like the fellow who would follow orders. But also means that we have to do our own digging to find out more about him.

    Maria: I see.

    Constantin: Regardless.

    Constantin places his pen down.

    Constantin: We have just solved both our money issues, and have just out-competed our rivals in one fell swoop. For once, things are looking up in my favor.
