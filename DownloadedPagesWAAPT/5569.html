    Goldenrod City, some hours ago

    "Seeing you later then guys."

    Silent leaves into offscreenness with Sol and Ichtacya following.

    Up the rooftop, Copahue and Harriet wake up when they hear Latios speaking.

    Inka: <And you, mysterious dragon, look pretty affable to me. So we are even :3...> — to Copahue and Harriet — <About time you woke up.>

    Copahue: <...Oh? W-what happened?>

    Inka produces Copahue and Harriet's Pokéballs.

    Inka: <There is a small problem with Silent's Pokéballs that I have to correct~>

    Copahue: <P-problem?> — Harriet and Copahue look at each other — <We didn't... we didn't know.>

    Inka: <Oh it's fine because you're living happy here... so, once we're done here, which one of you will bring me the chick?>

    Harriet -remembers something: <...Errr.....>

    One time skip later

    Inka enters the birdhouse to see Roc talking to Shahinne. The Nidorina stares at the Staraptor, who stares back.

    Roc: <...>

    Inka: <...>

    Shahinne -looks at them both quizzically: <...chirp?>

    Another short time skip later

    After a short impasse regarding letting Teala walk around freely in the hills, Silent is back at the Nidoran Colony, petting some kits and talking to Maekrite and some other Nidorin@s in search of information about ghosts.

    Random Nidoran: <Rawr!> — bumps onto Teala's tail — <....Oooof!>

    Teala -shifts nervously: <...This place is so different... It is not like I had imagined.>

    Maekrite: <What had you imagined?>

    Teala: <Uhm... no kits running around... less chaos perhaps? More order?>

    Some kits start bumping onto Teala and chasing Ichtacya around looking for experience, while Silent and Maekrite are having a talk with Taisho and Mayonaka, Silent a bit surprised at how different they are from the other timeline.

    Mayonaka the Nidorina -lies on the ground: <So you say then those ghosts came up and gave this Eevee powers?>

    Maekrite: <Yeah... We wanted to talk to mom about this because I supposedly have to train this Eevee...>

    Taisho: <I guess Hamada won't oppose to the idea.> — shrugs — <I don't really see the concern.>

    "Ahem... Ghosts? Giving powers and instructions?"

    Maekrite -flattens ears: <Not to mention I am assigned to Silent's protection... I shouldn't really be leaving him to train another Pokémon...>

    Mayonaka: <Well we can ask the lorekeepers about that...> — points to Silent — <So long as you are OK there should not be an issue.>

    "So... what do we do first? I was expecting to talk to Inka already but I haven't seen her."

    Mayonaka -shrugs: <Inka went to the city to do stuff... she'll be back whenever she pleases, you should know better...> — sees Silent grimace, smirks — <In the meantime, you should be returning to the Owsla to give your report either way, Maekrite.>

    Maekrite: <Ah yes...> — drops head — <I, uhm... I will be back later I guess?>

    "You don't have to worry, Maekrite. I have the rest of the team, and I'll be a just short flight away, next town or two." — pets Maekrite — "Besides, don't you want to spend some time with Pierce?"

    Teala steps forward nervously, rubbing her paws together.

    Teala: <Uhm... s-sir, madam? D-do you think I can stay with Maekrite as well?>

    Mayonaka: <...Oh?> — looks up at Teala, sizing her up — <And what would you want?>

    Teala -looks at Maekrite for a moment, then at the two Nidos: <I'd like to be trained by Maekrite. By his peoplemons, I mean...>

    "You sure about this, Teala? Tracer said you should stay with me, I should keep you safe."

    The two Nidos look at each other, murmur for a moment. They seem to have trouble coming to a decision. After a moment, Taisho stands up and goes before Maekrite.

    Taisho: <Well, it might be uncomfortable to have this large... uhm... dragon here.> — looks up at Teala — <You won't be able to move comfortably through the denser parts of the forest, and are too big to be inconspicuous...>

    Teala lowers her head, as does Maekrite.

    Taisho: <That said, it is not my choice. If it is Maekrite's responsibility to train two Pokémon, then it falls to the Owsla to judge and provide. You are free to go in with Maekrite, dragon, until we get official word from the Owsla.>

    Mayonaka -growls: <In matters of protection and training, we defer to Hamada's experience.>

    Maekrite and Teala nod and look at each other. Then Maekrite looks at Silent, a bit saddened.

    "I'll be fine, Maekrite. I have Ichtacya, see?" — points to Ichtacya who is happy playing tag with some kits and a random Furret.

    Maekrite: <...Fine.>

    "Well, I should also go fetch some things that I wanted to bring to Inka... so 'll leave you to organize yourselves as you find convenient."

    Yet Another Time Skip Later

    In approximately present time plus ten minutes, Silent is sitting at the National Park preparing for his meeting with Steven and Jeff. He has already met with Inka, who has returned Silent's Pokéballs and after a chat have discussed varios matters. Silent has rearranged his team in different groups for the remainder of the week.

    At PEFE Office: Shahinne the Starly♀, Copahue the Fearow♂, Harriet the Honchkrow♀, Lileep♂

    At the Colony: Maekrite the Nidorino☼, Teala the Druddigon♀, Python the Ekans♂
