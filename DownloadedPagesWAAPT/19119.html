    The eternal dusk returns

    Ever Grande

    -Robin is intently studying his Ranseian strategy book when someone taps him on the shoulder-

    ???: Hey, Gameboy. We're back.

    -Robin turns to see Arika and Nico are right behind him-

    Robin: Oh! You two are-

    -Quickly notices Nico seems much more feeble and frail than normal-

    -And that he's holding a strange staff and wearing a strange helmet-

    -...And that he's riding a rather irritated-looking Metagross-

    ...Um, Nico? You seem... Different. ._.

    Nico: Let's just say... things got pretty hectic since I last saw you.

    Arika: Yeah, we would've come back yesterday but the poor guy needed a lot of rest after what happened near the end...

    Robin: ...Okay then, exactly what did happen on that adventure of yours and Maggie's since I last heard from you?

    Nico: Well...

    One explanation later

    Robin: ...Wow. ._.

    Nico: Yeah... As I told you and as you can probably see I'm not exactly in good shape from purifying the Crystal Stars and making sure the Shadow King's no longer a problem... But otherwise, I think things turned out pretty damn well.

    -he thumps his staff on the Metagross he's atop of-

    Plus, I've got Grodus here as a pretty sweet ride until I can walk better on my own again.

    Grodus: <...I am not a "sweet ride".>

    Nico: -thumps him harder- The Tremendous Thwap decrees you are in fact a sweet ride and that you shall not question it.

    -Grodus grumbles and grows silent-

    Robin: -Smiles- Definitely good to hear.

    -Nico smiles back before his look becomes more puzzled-

    Say... Arika told me plenty of what the J-Team's been up to since I've been gone... But she's not sure what happened after she joined the party...

    Arika: Yeah, I was in enough of a rush to answer Maggie's summons I forgot my cell phone. Not to mention the moon and the Palace of Shadow wouldn't exactly have good cell service. >_>

    -To Robin-

    Mind giving us a recap?

    Robin: -blinks and rubs the back of his head nervously- Oh, er, well...

    One explanation of recent events (particularly those of a still-being Time Shenaniganed Delta Episode) later

    Arika: ...Wow. ._.

    Nico: So while we were preventing one apocalypse... You were preventing another? ._.

    Robin: ...Pretty much.

    -turns to Nico-

    But hey, not only did we both succeed, we both found what we wanted in a sense, right?

    Nico: -smiles- Exactly.

    -Robin smiles back and approaches Nico, but stops upon reaching Grodus-

    Nico: If you want to get up to me, go ahead and step on his face.

    -Robin does so - Grodus making an annoyed grunt in the process - and sits next to Nico, after which the two promptly hug-

    -Arika smiles at the proceedings before realizing something-

    Arika: ...Hey Nico?

    -Robin and Nico break the hug to turn to Arika-

    Nico: ...Yes?

    Arika: You know how Maggie and I kept making all these comments about chapters and boss fights and knowing at least how things should go and such?

    Nico: ...Definitely.

    Arika: Well, Maggie went off on her own before she could explain, so...

    -walks up to Nico and pulls out a copy of a certain Gamecube game before handing it to him-

    Nico: -stares at the box in shock- That whole adventure... Was...

    Robin: I was pretty shocked when I found out too, for... Obvious reasons.

    Nico: ...Ayep.

    Robin: But on that note... You know how you were upset that the Great Gonzalez title went to me in the Glitz Pit because the "hero" was supposed to have that title? Well, turns out that's because Mario gets that title in the game...

    -smiles-

    ...But while I was Gonzales during that chapter... You and Maggie were still Mario the whole time.

    Nico: -smiles back- Considering that's coming from an actual video game protagonist I can definitely say I'm flattered.

    -Robin grins, and the two hug again-

    -Once they break the hug again, Nico looks at the game box again and furrows his brow at one character in the corner-

    Nico: Say... Now that I think about it...

    Arika: Oh?

    Nico: Our version of Cortez said he knew Umbra back in the Ghost Lord days... Says that's how he got his extra arms... But he was pretty shocked when I told him Umbra was still alive...

    ...I'm wondering if he's gonna meet up with him again...

    Elsewhere in Ever Grande

    -Umbra is sitting around listening to music when something approaches behind him-

    ???: <Hola!>

    -Umbra yelps and his headphones fall out before seeing the being addressing him is a floating Aggron skull with one red eye and a pirate captain's hat-

    Umbra: -blinks- ...Wait... Cortez?

    Cortez: -cackles- <The one and only! Aye, when that Nico kid told me rumors of your death were greatly exaggerated and those immortality rumors were true I knew I had to stop by and visit sometime soon, and it's the perfect time of year to do it! Bet you're just as surprised to see me, eh, Señor Fantasma?>

    Umbra: -binks then rubs the back of his head-

    Admittedly.. Not quite as much as I should be.

    Cortez: <Eh? Why not?>

    Umbra: You're the fifth literal Ghost of my past from the Ransei days I've run into in the past few years. One was... invoked, but you and the other three were crazy coincidences.

    Cortez: <Arrrgh, I'm late to the fiesta! Who from the glory days beat me to yeh?>

    Umbra: I wouldn't call 'em glory days, but... Celzvet, Revan, Duolis, and Izumi, in that order.

    Cortez: <Quite the assemblage... Though your old wife's among them, so that's good to hear!>

    Umbra: Yeah...

    -he frowns-

    There's some things she's worried about I need to talk to her about...

    -smiles-

    ...But I'm confident she can find the right path.

    Cortez: <Good luck to her then.>

    Umbra: -nods- Thank you.

    -smirks-

    Though current romantic involvement has been with someone with even more convoluted ties to the old days.

    Cortez: <...Aye? Who would that be?>

    Umbra: Remember how everyone thought I died?

    Cortez: <Some sun goddess Arcanine supposedly managed to do you in, why?>

    Umbra: Well, to start, she was actually my girlfriend from the future and we're currently working on wedding arrangements.

    Cortez: ...

    <...We've got a lot of catching up to do, amigo.>

    Umbra: -smiles- Then let's start, shall we?

    -they start going over the past 600-ish years together-

    Thoughts?
