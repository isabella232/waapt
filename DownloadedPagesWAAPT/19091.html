    After yet-to-be-shown events

    Battle Frontier Hotel

    -Sarah walks into her room, to find Eskay awake and pacing. His hands are scarred.-

    Sarah: Still can't sleep, huh?

    Eskay: Of course not. You can't blame me, after...

    Sarah: ...That. Yeah.

    Eskay: Ugh... geez, I was stupid. You tried to warn me about that sword, but I didn't listen. Even after seeing how it affected Umbra, just in... what, ten seconds of holding the damn thing?

    Sarah: Hey, it's gone now. You're sane again. You've got nothing to—

    -She's interrupted by Obscura literally exploding from one of Eskay's hands. He yelps in pain, pulling his hand back and breathing heavily.-

    Sarah: ...Okay, that's still a problem.

    Eskay: Yeah... after all that, I still can't control it alone, and it does that.

    Sarah: Hm...

    -She digs through her bag and pulls out the Obscura fabric gloves Crewe gave them before the split.-

    Sarah: Try these.

    -Eskay nods, and puts the gloves on. He holds out a hand toward open air where he's unlikely to break anything, and tries focusing...-

    -And yelps as another burst of Obscura makes its way out of his arm, but instead of exploding forward, the glove simply glows purple for a second before fading.-

    Eskay: ...Well, it'll keep me from breaking anything at the very least.

    -He sighs, and sits down on the bed.-

    Eskay: ...Anyway... it doesn't change the fact that I still went around acting like a complete asshole. To Legendaries. If it were any others, I'd probably be dead now.

    -Sarah sits down next to him.-

    Sarah: But they knew it was just effects of the sword. It wasn't you, Eskay.

    -Eskay pauses, mulling something over.-

    Eskay: ...Hmmm... Kieran.

    Sarah: ...Wha?

    Eskay Kieran: Not Eskay, not anymore. "Kieran". Call me that.

    Sarah: Ooooookay... but why...?

    Kieran: You had to change your name to a new one when we split, no? You're just as much "Eskay" as I am, why should I pretend I'm the "original" or anything? Especially after I made a complete ass out of myself.

    Sarah: ...If you say so.

    -She hugs him-

    Sarah: But I know you're back to normal now, either way. You're better now, Es— Kieran.

    -Kieran hugs back-

    Kieran: ...Thanks...

    Sarah: ...That said...

    -Sarah picks Kieran up, and THROWS HIM INTO THE NEAREST TRASH CAN.-

    Kieran: AAAAGH!

    Sarah: Don't you DARE do something like that ever again! Having that sword is no excuse for ignoring your Arceusdamn BOYFRIEND when he told you to STOP USING THAT THING! I don't care if it was screwing with your mind, you're the one who still had the super empathy! YOU SHOULD HAVE CARED WHEN HE WAS CREEPED OUT! IT'S THE FIRST FUCKING RULE OF HAVING A SIGNIFICANT OTHER, DAMMIT!

    -Kieran pokes his head out of the trash, dazed-

    Kieran: Urk... didn't you hate him, though...?

    -Sarah smacks him in the face-

    Kieran: OW!

    Sarah: I was frustrated! Eight years of fleeting crushes on more people than is healthy, and you threw out our second rule of significant others, "don't act on attraction so quickly", faster than we did when we got with Psyche! At least we waited two weeks before getting with her!

    -Kieran rubs his cheek-

    Kieran: You definitely hit as hard as you did before this all happened...

    Sarah: If I were you — and I am you — I'd go back to him and say "hey, maybe we should hold off on this whole relationship thing until I can ACTUALLY THINK ABOUT A CRUSH BEFORE I START MAKING OUT WITH THEM!" Okay?

    Kieran: Okay... geez...

    Sarah: Good! Now, throwing you and shouting at you has made me really sweaty so I'm taking a shower.

    -She heads into the bathroom. Suddenly, echoing through the halls, is the sound of a trombone going off to a very familair and... one may say "memetic", tune. Kieran stares blankly in the direction of the noise, starting to rise out of the trash bin-

    Kieran: What in the world…?

    -Suddenly the hotel door begins slamming in tune to the dooting. As the door opens for each slam, Kieran recognizes the face behind it and sinks back into the trash, pulling the lid over his head.-

    Kieran: ...Hello again.

    -Suddenly, the lid of the trashcan opens. Revealing the all too familiar, smiling, chubby face of Xander in only the most haute cuture◊ that one can describe.-

    Xander: Geeeeeeeeeeeet dunked on, kid!

    -Kieran makes a sound out of a combination of fear… and utter befuddlement.-

    Kieran: ...Uh…

    Xander: Ey! Remember me? Of course you do, you did kinda get blasted in the gut after making me mad, but I'm not angry at you anymore kid. Even if ya' broke a few of my rules.

    -Xander leans his arm on the edge of the trash can.-

    Xander: That bein' said, I don't think I got a formal apology you. Though, others have already dished out your just desserts. News travels fast among the legendaries, you find. Especially those of whom you were in cahoots with~

    -He winks, getting across his implications quite clearly.-

    Kieran: ...I, uh… see…

    -He pokes his head back out of the trash can, brushing off some of the garbage clinging to him-

    Kieran: Anyway, er… I'm… really sorry about all that. About… yeah. I was an idiot.

    Xander: Yeah, you were a dummy, but you're remorseful about it AND you've gotten your comeuppance.

    And hey! Look on the bright side! At least you and yer cetacean sweetheart can't get stuck in any more feedback loops!

    ...

    Which reminds me, gotta talk to bro about that before he heads off on his date...

    Kieran: ...Riiiiight…

    -He fidgets awkwardly-

    Kieran: ...Uh… so… new look…?

    -He gestures vaguely at Xander's outfit-

    Xander: You know of Santiago, right? The Groundon of your boyfriend's little ragtag band?

    Dude's a champ. Lent me some of his clothes since I was planning on disappearing into the [Bermuda Triangle] for some good ol' R 'n R and also to get off bro's back since while we do care for each other deeply - we tend to get on each other's nerves quite often. 'sides, he needs to figure a few things out for himself.

    But yeah, that explains the recent venture into only the finest of modern day clothing.

    -It should be noted that Xander looks like he stepped out of the late 70s/Early 80s. Which is surprising to no-one considering he got fashion tips from Sandy, of all peoplemons-

    Kieran: ...Uh… yyyyeah.

    -He goes quiet for a moment, and sighs-

    Kieran: Hate to be a downer, but… I'm gonna need to be a bit of a downer for a little bit. Sorry to cut this short, but do you mind if I just… y'know, sulk for a bit? Alone? Need more time to regret everything and contemplate whether I can ever let Levi see my dumb face again. Nothing against you.

    Xander: 'Kay kid. I'll allow you to pretend you're garbage for the time being.

    -Xander turns around and waves goodbye.-

    Xander: Oh, but do say hi to your "sister" from me. She's a good kid, after all~

    -He winks.-

    Xander: Toodles~

    -Xander disappears, continuing his habit of disappearing and reappearing as he pleases. You wonder if he can secretly manipulate time and space, but shake off the thought as those are Dialga and Palkia's areas of expertises. Not Xerneas's.-

    -Yes, you wonder this. Not Kieran. You.-

    -This happens to be conveniently timed with Sarah finally coming out of the shower. She blinks when she sees Kieran still in the trash.-

    Sarah: You're still there?

    Kieran: Yeah. I'm sulking.

    Sarah: ...Well, go sulk in the shower. You stink and I didn't throw you in the trash for you to find it comfortable.

    -Kieran grumbles and pulls himself out of the trash can.-

    "She's drunk, but she's no fool. She knows what she's doing better than some of us do sober."
