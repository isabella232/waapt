    -Amhlaidh reins up a good distance away from the warehouse, dismounts and leads his Rapidash into a somewhat secluded spot. He pauses a moment, then unbuckles his sword and sheath from his back, wrapping it in his cloak and placing it on the ground-

    Kori: <That could be a very, very foolish idea.>

    Amhlaidh: I didn't come to Dragnor to fight Lord Kaius. I don't want to hurt him.

    Kori: <I doubt he has the some reservations.>

    Isolde: <If he hurts Amhlaidh, I'll hurt him!>

    Amhlaidh: No. Isolde, I want you to stay here.

    Isolde: <But—>

    Amhlaidh: I don't want your emotions to get the better of you. Stay here and stand guard. If I do not return by the day's end... leave. Find a way home. Tell Aislin what came of me.

    Isolde: <Amhlaidh, no! I can't let you go in defenseless!>

    Amhlaidh: I won't be defenseless. Kori will come. Hopefully I will be able to talk things out with Lord Kaius and it won't come to blood. If not, I will handle it.

    Isolde: <But what if you die?>

    Kori: <I won't let him die.>

    Amhlaidh: If I die, someone needs to tell Aislin and the boys. Let them know everything that's happened since we left home. I can only hope she can forgive me.

    -Kori digs through the saddlebags and pulls out the practice sword carved for Halim-

    Kori: <If you won't bring your own sword, bring this one. It won't be much, but...>

    -he snorts if quiet amusement and takes it from her-

    Amhlaidh: A wooden sword, to save my life against Lord Kaius. I should tell Isolde to keep this and bring it home so Halim will have something from his father.

    Isolde: <No! You come back with that sword, and you can give it to Halim yourself!>

    Amhlaidh: -turning the sword over in his hands- Perhaps I should have made something for Faolan, as well... I hope he doesn't think his father forgot him...

    Isolde: -distressed- <Stop talking like that! You'll come back just fine, and you can make Faolan a sword then!>

    -Amhlaidh gives her a small smile and rests the back of one hand on her cheek-

    Amhlaidh: Good bye, Isolde. If I don't return, please tell my family how very sorry I am.

    -Isolde makes a small sound of distress. Kori gives her a long look-

    Amhlaidh: -softly- Come, Kori. Lord Kaius is waiting.

    -he gives the Rapidash a pat on the neck and she nuzzles him briefly, then he walks off towards the warehouse. Kori hesitates before following-

    Isolde: <Keep him safe. Bring him back to me. You have to.>

    Kori: <I will do my very best. Good bye, Isolde.>

    -she turns and starts to follow after Amhlaidh-

    Isolde: <Bring him back! I love him...>

    -Kori hurries to catch up, and the two of them approach the warehouse-
