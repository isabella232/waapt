    Alys: They also told me who you work for.

    -Kirsten freezes, before her eyes begin to dart. Alys just laughs, quietly-

    Alys: Don't worry, I knew you were a spy for someone. But you weren't doing anything spy-worthy if I gave you a project.

    Kirsten: ...hmph. You know me too well, don't you?

    Alys: I know everyone well.

    -she takes a wax apple from her bowl and takes a large bite, staring Kirsten up and down-

    Alys: I could kill you right now, you know.

    Kirsten: You couldn't.

    Alys: Idiot girl. Of course I could.

    Kirsten: No, I'm not bragging. You can't.

    Alys: What, because of your eye?

    -Kirsten blinks and snarls before Alys speaks, in an incredibly calm and soothing voice-

    Alys: Now, I want you to listen carefully, Kirsten. Whatever you do, don't move a muscle until I tell you to. Understood? Do you understand me, Kirsten?

    -she nods, slowly, suddenly relaxed and complacent but having no idea why. Alys smiles, and rummages into her desk-

    Alys: Good...very, very good, Kirsten.

    -there is the click of a hammer, and Alys produces a revolver from her desk drawer-

    Alys Now, stay still...

    -Kirsten goes wide eyed and goes to move, before Alys chuckles and puts the gun away-

    Alys: See? That's how.

    -the blonde woman snarls, balling her fist-

    Alys: Now, I've been told about your boss. I've heard of him before then, of course. "Ineffable plans..."

    -takes another drag from her cigarette-

    Alys: He didn't give you a lot of straight answers, did he? He didn't think you were the smartest, did he?

    -Kirsten shakes her head, still glaring-

    Kirsten: I could say the same about you.

    Alys: I've looked over your records, Kirsten. The fake ones, that didn't use your real name. They gave your IQ as...129, if I recall correctly. Obviously, I can't get an accurate reading unless I tested you here, which is inaccurate, but you're clearly rather bright.

    Kirsten: ...you do know that the IQ is about as accurate as a Zubat playing darts, yes?

    -Alys beams-

    Alys: Very good! Now, my point is, you have no love for your employer, yes?

    -Kirsten nods-

    Kirsten: Hate the bastard.

    Alys: And if he was no longer your boss, you'd have no one to spy to?

    Kirsten: There's the J-Team.

    -beat, then laughs-

    Kirsten: Couldn't say that with a straight face.

    Alys: And of what I've seen of you, you are constantly developing plans to kill anybody within a ten foot radius of you.

    Kirsten: ...yeah, you got me.

    Alys: Good.

    -slides a stiletto knife across the desk to Kirsten-

    Alys: Here's your new job. You work for me and only me, from now on. You will use this knife to do your job in this case. Quickly. Don't gloat, don't have your fun. Return to me. I'll be able to tell if you pulled something, and it will be dealt with accordingly.
