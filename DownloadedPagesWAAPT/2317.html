    Stratus: <Um...You've lost me Lake. Could you give me an example?>

    Lake: <Sure, take me, for instance. I'm very good when it comes to using Physical attacks, as well as taking them. But at the special side of things, I'm not really up too much. Plus I'm not really very fast either. Those are the sorts of weaknesses I'm talking about.>

    Stratus: <Oh, now I understand!>

    Lake: <It's not hard when you wrap your head around it. So, when I'm fighting, I take my weaknesses into account and try my best not to let my opponent take advantage of them. For example, seeing as I'm not good with taking or receiving special attacks, I try to keep things "up close and personal" as they say.>

    Stratus: <And you do that because it's important to take advantage of your own strengths too, isn't it?>

    Lake: <That's right! That's the key to any battle, really. Using your own strengths to the best of your ability. As I just explained, I'm good at Physical stuff, so I use my brute strength and endurance to win my fights. But it's different for every Pokémon.     Stratus: <Yeah. That's why Espeon use mainly special attacks. That's what they're good at!>
