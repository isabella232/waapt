    Lake: <Oh, don't worry about him. We won't let him hurt you. Besides, he means well. Most of the time.>

    Lugia: <Er, okay.>

    Lake: <Oh, my name's Lake, by the way. Would you like to tell me yours?>

    Lugia: <...Um...It's Lugia.>

    Lake: <Huh. Lugia is your species name, though. Don't you have a nickname?>

    Lugia: -Shakes his head.- <No...Er, well...Actually...>

    Lake: -Tilts his head.- <Oh? You do have a name?>

    Lugia: <Kinda'. But only my dad ever used it...>

    Lake: <Well...I would that the name your parents give you is the name everyone should call you by, right?>

    Lugia: <...Yeah, I guess so...>

    Lake: <So...Will you tell me what it is?>

    -The Lugia hesitated. Should he really be telling him his proper name? All the Pokémon at back at the reef always referred to him as "The Child of the Beast", "The Silver Prince", or "Heir of the Sea", amongst many other titles that he didn't really care for that much.

    If he told this nice Feraligatr Lake was it? Yeah, Lake about all that, then they would probably fuss over him the same way the Reef Pokémon did. And although he thought it was nice that they all looked after him...They treated him more like a God than an actual Pokémon.

    But his father hadn't. He was the only Pokémon to have called him by his proper name. He was the only one who had cared for him in that way. And although he was thankful for all the Reef Pokémon had done for him...

    ...He really wanted his Dad back.

    And while this Feraligar wasn't his Dad, he treated him the same way. So...He deserved to know his name, at least.-

    Lugia: <...My name's...Pleo.>

    -Has just landed on the northern beaches after about one hour of flight, and decides to let Copahue rest-

    Dintel — <Oh, long time we don't shoo Poachers, right? I, er.... wow, I have trouble remembering it.>

    — Yeah... it's never nice to meet them. Last time was slightly less than two years ago, Dintel. Near Chimmey, remember?

    Dintel (wonders for a moment, then blinks) — <No... I don't.>

    -shrughs, thinks back to that time with Iggy-

    — Perhaps it's better that way. So... Algira said tower with a Pokéball design this way?

    -We walk for some more minutes until we catch sight of a singular building with a Pokéball design on its tower. I send Ichtacya out, and after letting her get used to the surroundings, instruct her to go fetch Algira and Copahue, warning her to avoid the locals-

    ...

    -A couple of hours later, me and my team are back on the road. Algira and Ichtacya are heading, with Dintel walking behind. We decide to look for an isolated beach on the southern side of the island, somewhere shallow and with calm water.-

    That Afternoon

    -At a shallow body of water protected from the sea by some rocks and a couple of big tree trunks that must have fell there some time ago, I release Copahue and Shahinne too-

    Shahinne — <Oh! Wow, this is completely different!> — perches on Copahue and looks around — <It's all so green and blue!>

    Algira — <I like it because there are interesting fruits in the trees back there...> — points a random way — <I'd like to taste them later.>

    -While I remove my shoes and change my pants for shorts, Shahinne flies around with Ichtacya following to keep her under watch. I instruct Algira, Copahue and Dintel to come in-

    — Now, let's begin, slowly.

    -Dintel nods and stays sat on the sand, far from the water, while Copahue and Algira rest some distance by my side-

    -Releasing the Pokémon in the Dive Ball on the sand right where the waves are crashing-

    -Lileep appears-

    Lileep♂ — <...?> — looks around and makes a strange inintelligible noise — <Oh, back on land aga~ hey! There's the sea!>

    -waves tentacles around, then I call his attention-

    — Hey there...

    Lileep (stretches) — <H-Hey...>

    — Uh, I thought you'd like to spend some time on the water, although I can't let you go very far.

    Lileep — <Water, well... yes I'll be comfortable there...>

    -stands there motionless waving his tentacles-

    — Uh... do you want me to carry you there?

    Lileep — <No.>

    -Copahue steps in a bit closer-

    Copahue — <Or I can take you if->

    -Lileep turns to Copahue and makes a threatening gesture, as if attempting to shoot some Acid at him. I have to instruct Copahue to stay behind-

    Lileep — <...No.>

    -twiddling fingers-

    — ...Right... So you want to going to stay there, eh, sunbathing instead?

    Lileep (raises a tentacle) — <Yes.>

    — ...Oh.

    Wow, he's kinda terse. I guess I should have expected this.

    -I sit on the sand-

    — Look... I understand that this is all different from what you expect, but we can support you, OK? The little round thing resting there is Dintel, you already know him, see? There's Copahue — points to the Fearow — and the flying one is Algira.

    Dintel (away) — <Oh hai!> — does the animation, you know the one.

    -Lileep simply stares at him and turns back to focus on Copahue-

    — If you have any trouble just swim to the surface and call their attention. They'll be watching over you...

    Lileep — <I don't like them.>

    — ...Uh, they're there to ensure you don't run into danger, or out of oxygen while on land. So trust us, please? That way you can just go around and have fun on the shallow water.

    -Lileep stares at us for a moment, then makes another noise that I can't understand and reorients himself to stay there looking at the sea. He simply stays there.-

    -Algira flies over me, a bit frustrated-

    Algira — <He's filtering sunlight, right?>

    — I think so, Algira. I guess he still doesn't trust us enough...

    -From the eastern side of the beach, Ichtacya and Shahinne come over carrying a weird shell and a fruit, respectively-

    Ichtacya — <Wheee~! More partners! All around!>

    -Algira flies to the girls and instructs them to give Lileep some personal space. Since Shahinne seems to not be able (or not want) to grasp the subject, Algira sends them both to play with a random Krabby.-

    Algira — <Just remember...!>

    Ichtacya — <We won't engage the locals!>

    -Algira sighs, then turns to me and we spend the next hour simply watching Lileep spend time-

    Algira — <He's so different. Well, he's a bit like Helios, actually...>

    -I look at her and inquiry about the (apparently) random comparison, but she simply dismisses me and goes fly around Lileep's spot. I go pat Copahue's head-

    — It's not your fault.

    Copahue — <I hope not. What's his problem anyway? Does he not like our species or something?>

    — Hardly, Copahue. Your species didn't even exist when his was around, I think.

    -Copahue stays there dumbfounded trying to interpret that information. Seeing him worried, I tell him to go fly around and have fun-

    — Just remember to keep one eye on him, please. I'll stay close, but there are things humans can't do.

    Copahue — <I'll try to do it right this time, don't worry.> — flies ahead to the sea.

    Well, at least we can spend time on the outside...

In about three more hours, his foot is finally bathed by water, prompting the Pokémon to flail his arms around and let out another sort-of-screech, which again I can not interpret-
