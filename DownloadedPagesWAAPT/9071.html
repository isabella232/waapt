    Sometime, Somewhere

    Des is up in a tree, a sniper rifle in hand as he scopes out what appears to be some sort of safe house. The bright lights emitting from it make it stick out like a sore thumb in the otherwise dark green jungle. His breath is slow and steady. He waits. Circling around the base of the tree are an Arcanine and a Haunter. Sparkers and Vladimir. They keep watch as Des waits for his mark. Des's arm starts to get tired, so he lowers the rifle though making sure he can quickly bring it back up into position when the opportune shot came. He fiddles around with something in his pocket, pulling out a notebook. He flips through a few pages until he comes across a poem he was working on.

    Des: Why does Salvatore have to take so long. I pray that they didn't get an intern to do up the coordinates this time around. -sigh- At least I can amuse myself for the time being...

    He begins to read the poem.

    Des: I have beaten many villains from regions far and wide. I bested many baddies, from justice they cannot hide...

    Des is interrupted by the telltale sound of an engine. He quickly pockets the notebook and levels the rifle at the jeep that's making it's way to the safe house.

    Des: There you are...

    A bald headed figure in a white suit steps out of the jeep. He reaches inside his pocket and pulls out a cigar. After snipping the end he lights it up and takes a long haul.

    Des levels the crosshairs right on Salvatore's head, right where the moonlight bounce off his shiny bald head.

    Des: It would be a mighty big shame if you got a stain on that suit...

    He pulls the trigger.

    BANG!!!!

    Sometime else, Somewhere else

    Des is leaping across rooftops in hot pursuit of what appears to be a woman dressed in a purple and yellow catsuit.

    Des: If you surrender Lady Liepard, I'm sure we would be able to do something for your sister!

    Lay Liepard: Do not give me that bullshit! You wouldn't understand. She needs that operation!

    Des: -Grr- this isn't the way to solve it though, if you just stop I can help you!

    Lady Liepard comes to a gap in between two buildings. She stops. The gap is too far to jump.

    Des: Give up and hand the money over-AAHHH!

    Des is tackled by a trio of figures.

    Lady Liepard:...Well I guess I shouldn't question a good thing.

    She begins to scale down the side of the building, large sack of money in tow.

    Des opens his eyes to find a girl straddled on top of him. She appears to be wearing a Rocket Uniform, only with a big green D on the front.

    Des: Uuhh..

    Fangirl: Desi-chan!

    Des:...

    Two more fangirls appear behind her. They all give an evil smirk as one of them hauls out a roll of duct tape.

    Des:meep.

    Meanwhile, thousands of miles away and in the future.

    Des is in what appears to be a rundown carnival. He notices a weird figure wearing a top hat and a white suit with random equations on it out of the corner of his eye.

    Des: There you are...

    Des chases the figure into n abandoned funhouse.

    Des: The jig is up Matter Hat! You're trapped.

    The voice of the villain known as Matter Hat emanates all throughout the house.

    Matter Hat: I have to say, out of all my pursuers you are the most persistent by far. Y'know I just don't show my multi-dimensional funhouse to anyone.

    Des surveys the inside of the house too find a vast array of mirrors. He hears the creak of the door behind him. It slams shut and a mechanism locks it shut.

    Matter Hat: Stay a while and check out my mirrors.

    Des: Enough of the tricks and show yourself!!!

    Des hauls out a knife and chucks it at a mirror. His eyes widen in surprise as rather than shattering it to a million pieces, it enters the mirror causing a ripple effect.

    Des: More of your tricks?

    Matter Hat: Mere copies that do the original no justice.

    Des: Copies?

    Matter Hat: Yes, I once worked for a Megacorp called Pokefutures in their theoretical physics department. I was assigned to study a most peculiar artifact. A mirror that seemed to act as a portal to other worlds, other dimensions! I became obsessed with it, to the point where I wanted to take it for myself. My superiors saw my obsession as unhealthy for the project and promptly had me fired. I was outraged to have been parted from my true love...

    Des: Dude, you got issues.

    Matter Hat: Do not interrupt my monologue! As I was saying, I loved that magnificent mirror, so I decided to make my own. Unfortunately no institutes would hear me out and rejected my proposals. Desperate I turned to crime and became to person you know today. I am the Uncatchable King of Crime! I am Matter Hat, and I think with Portals!!!

    Des:...y'done?

    Matter Hat: Yes I think I am, now see if you can find me.

    Hesitantly Des pokes his head into a mirror. He sees what appears to be a couch sitting in a darkened room.

    Des: Huh, nice couch.

    He takes his head out of the mirror and turns around to try another one when he freezes in spot. In front of him is the couch.

    Des: Wha? But how? Matter Hat?

    Matter Hat: I'm just as surprised as you, I've never seen that couch before.

    Des:-sigh-It's gonna be one of those days.

    What time is it?

    Des has an intense look on his face. Sweat drips down from his brow and his breathing is heavy.

    This is it. Do or die...

    Des shakes a cup and tips it out onto a table. Several dice roll out.

    Des: Yahtzee!!!!

    Figure:-facepalms-
