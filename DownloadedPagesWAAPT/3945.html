    <There was! It was a monster! It looked like a grass Pokemon, but it breathed fire! It burned all of my Silcoon friends...and then it ATE them!> The Wurmple sniffled pitifully, Clover patting his head gently.

    <You poor thing...>

    Glaze looked sympathetic, but stepped forward, knowing that they needed the information the Wurmple had. <Did you see which way it went?>

    The Wurmple nodded, pointing with its barbed tail towards another section of the island. <That way...> He curled up fearfully, looking highly stressed. <What if it comes back for me?>

    <Don't worry; we'll take care of it,> Clover promised.

    <Y-yeah. We'll do something,> Glaze agreed. 'Can I even do anything against it? I might be worse off now that I'm part ice...'

    <Thank...thank you,> the Wurmple said, looking up at them gratefully.

    Clover nodded before turning to Kim, pointing in the direction the Wurmple showed them.

    "So, it went that way?" Kim asked. "Alright...let's follow it." She gulped down a bit of nervousness, steeling her resolve. Then the three of them headed into the woods, wondering just what they were facing.
