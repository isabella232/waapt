    -Frederick didn't trust me at first either way, just for different reasons... But heh, that's Fredrick for you. He was an ol' softie deep down, though, but more on that later, perhaps...-

    Chrom: -frowns- He certainly seems to be telling the truth and genuinely upset about what happened from his tone and attitude, and if it's fresh in his mind he's probably not ready to talk about it just yet. Besides, what kind of Shepherds would we be if we just left him here all alone?

    Fredrick: ...Just the same, m'lord. I must emphasise caution. 'Twould not do to let a wolf into our flock.&

    Chrom: Right then - we'll take him back to town and sort this out there.&

    -At this Robin grows a bit more flustered-

    Robin: Um... I'm n-not sure you'd want me...

    -Now note both here and in the game I was rather leery of just getting dragged along with these guys, but for different reasons. In the game I had no clue who these people were and not sure what they wanted with me. Here... As much as I didn't want to keep being alone, I wasn't sure of having new companions after what I'd done to my last one.-

    Chrom: -smiles- Don't be ridiculous. If you're genuinely lost and alone we're willing to help once we sort some things out. We'll tell you a bit more about ourselves once we get into town, if it helps.

    -Robin smiles again-

    Robin: Th-thank you...

    -The trio leave and he follows-

    Robin: The differences were subtle at first... But...

    -puts a hand to his chin-

    ...Your world calls it the Beautifly effect or something, right?
