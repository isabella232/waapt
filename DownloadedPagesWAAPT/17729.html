    The other girl smiles. "You're strange, you know that? No, I'm Alesha. I've been assigned to you via a foreign exchange system of sorts. You look like you need some help around the region, I can help with that. But, I'm not a trainer, as much as I'd like to be. So in exchange for helping you around, I'd ask for your protection."

    Teresa blinks again. "Wha... I don't know why or how you're here, but I'll take all the help I can get. My friends all kinda ditched me. ...You're not going to ditch me, are you?"

    Alesha grins. "If I did, may dragon fire kill me where I stand."

    Teresa smiles shyly. "All right then, Alesha, where should we go?"

    Alesha looks around. "Well, there's a gym in Mauville. We could go there next."

    Teresa smiles. "Ok! ...How do you plan on getting there? Do you have a boat? None of my mons know surf. That's why I'm kinda stuck here."

    Alesha grins. "Oh, consider that a non-issue." She leads them behind a house and removes her illusion.

    Teresa falls over again. "Wait, what? Y-you're a Latias? ...I should be less confused about this, one of my friends is Lady Yveltal."

    Alesha blinks. <...Lady Yveltal? Does that make you Teresa, Who Is Friends With Death?> Alesha ponders for a moment.

    Teresa sweatdrops. "I guess so? ...Shouldn't we get out of here, before people start asking questions?" Alesha nods and gestures for Teresa to climb on board. They quickly fly off.
