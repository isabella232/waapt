    -Irritated at being used, all three Trevenant suddenly vanish.-

    Ren: Dragon Dance again then get ready to Dragon Rush to reduce the damage to yourself, they're using Phantom Force.

    -Once again Bahamut dances wildly as his Attack and Speed are once again increased.-

    -After a tense moment, a wooden claw delivers a backhand swipe to Bahamut side.-

    -Taking this as His cue, Bahamut body is engulfed in blue draconian energy and uses the momentum to power rush.-

    -the second Trevenant tries to attack from the ground with a skywards attack, but Bahamut reduces the damage by using the claw as a springboard launching him into the air.-

    Bahamut: That was two, now where's three? Considering their pattern so far...

    -The third Trevenant appear in midair, pulling back its wooden arms to deliver a swipe to Bahamut that would send him crashing to the ground. However Bahamut grins.-

    Bahamut: I thought so~

    -the Draconian energy flares as Bahamut puts in some extra energy to fuel his momentum.-

    -The Draconian and Obscura energies of the two attacks collide, causing an explosion and creating a smoke cloud.-

    -Ren hold his breath and readies a Full Restore, only to gasp as Bahamut pushes the the KO'd Trevenant out the smoke as the Charmeleon's body starts to glow.-

    -As Bahamut glows, his frame expands and grow dramatically. As his arms grow shorter and thinner compared to the rest of his body as hands with three clawed fingers each develop. As Bahamut's neck extends, his horn seems to split into two as his snout becomes more squared. As his tail grows longer and more powerful, Two large, powerful draconian sprout from Bahamut's shoulders. As the glow fades, Bahamut's scales are shown to no longer the vibrant gold color they once were. Instead, now where once was an smallish golden reptile, a large black dragon with a grey scales covering his underside from his chest to the tip if his tail bobs in the air under the power of black, leathery wings with dark red undersides.-

    -Keys appears next to Ren's head, causing Ren to blink.-

    Keys: <Conqueror of the skies, the Hallowed Father, the First Sire. Soar now o mighty Wyrm Tyrant on the wings of night. Rise Dragon King, Bahamut!>

    Ren: Must you always be like this?

    Keys: <It's my job.>
