    Snowbelle Pokémon Center

    Sakura had just a few minutes ago been kidnapped into Hikari's room by the girl herself.

    "So! I'm done!" said Hikari, with a victorious expression

    Sakura raises an eyebrow "Done with...?"

    Hikari then tosses a costume complete with helmet, gloves, an utility belt, a pink scarf and a pair of tabi boots.

    "Put that on now!"

    Sakura sweatdrops and sighs, but goes along with it anyway

    About five minutes later

    Sakura was now dressed in a mostly dark blue suit except for a light yellow shuriken drawing in the chest.

    "Wow, did you do all of this yourself?" asked Sakura, her voice sounding more like that of Johnny Yong Bosch.

    Hikari seemingly freezes for a moment upon hearing Sakura speak "You're a completely different person now... But yeah! I did it!" she then does a victorious fist-pump "So, first of all the gloves you're wearing as well as the boots, I made them so they're able to stick to the walls, much like how actual Greninja can!"

    Sakura then looks at the gloves, they were dark blue in the back and light yellow in the front, similarly upon looking better at the boots she saw that they were dark blue except for their light yellow soles.

    She was quite impressed by the work put on it "You really want to keep the motif going huh?" she then touches the helmet, which had the same features of a Greninja's head, such as it's ears, and also an identical color scheme.

    "Yup! That's why the visor is red. It also has the same capabilites of my glasses, while the helmet himself has some fun functions as well such as a built-in Aura Jammer and communicator!

    "I don't think making the visor red was a good idea... Good that it hides my face though, it seems like it's opaque for anyone seeing it"

    "I can change that later. So, what do you think?" asked Hikari, with an almost hopeful expression

    "You really put a lot of effort into this, you even got my size right. Isn't the scarf a bit long though? It almost reaches my legs even when tied like this."

    "Well, Greninja's tongue is long, so I guess I had to represent that in some way..."

    Sakura shrugs "It's fine, by the way, hearing myself as a man is really weird."

    "You'll get used to it, don't worry!"

    "I guess. It's a pretty comfortable costume and well-designed costume but... Let's talk about this whole super-hero thing a bit..."

    Hikari winces a bit, already dreading what was going to come out of that talk.
