    adopting kitteh

    Johto Route 35

    The two Nidoran carefully move to the creek, to where it goes below the bridge.

    <Are you sure there are no elil?> — inquires the female.

    <What is the difference? We'll be under the open sky once we cross the bridge. Besides,> the male answers, brushing his whiskers, <do you want the scales or not?>

    The female growls, then follows after the male.

    Beyond the bridge, the creek becomes wider as it opens to a small lake. Some berry trees lie on the other side, which the Nidos will have to circle around to reach. They look up a couple of times and smell their surroundings, and then decide to get closer to the shore to inspect it.

    <No... I can't smell those around... rusati ma, what do we do?>

    The female looked up, then around, and grabbed the male.

    <We go that way, by the south shore, closer to the trees...>

    The two gulp at the openness of their surroundings and then focus on the way ahead, to adventure.

    Above, two Pidgeotto are circling the skies carefully, inspecting the ground for unsuspecting prey. Between the trees, Ariados have already set several traps. And an Umbreon is roaming around, looking for a fast mean before going to sleep for the day.

    The male turns to the female.

    <We have to get those heart scales before they reach the sea!>

    <Come on... let's go, brother!>

    The two high-paw each other.

    <It will be a great adventure! We'll be just like eldest brother!>

    And with that, Maekrite's youngest siblings darted following the course of the river, blissfully chasing the Heart Scales. First becoming shadows, then two dots, then a faint, blurry spot in the distance...



    And that was the last anymon had ever saw of them.
