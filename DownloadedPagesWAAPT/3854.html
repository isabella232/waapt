    -Inka the Nidorina is walking across a grassy landscape, the air filled with a strange dust, a scent that she thinks she knows but she can't put a claw on.     There she is... but, [   Inlé   ] grows weak... Where is she going?

    The kits and the explorers are running scared...

    Why are we allowing this to happen?

    -She looks around, smelling the grass blades and trying to catch some specks in the wind to examine them, until she reaches a ledge leading to a river below. Past the other side, a mesh of lights can be seen, a myriad of indistinct voices and the hidden sound of some hrududu can be heard-

    -The city of Goldenrod...-

    Wonder who else has noticed.

    Do the [humans] know? Do they care?

    Do they know who is responsible for this?

    -The wind changes direction to the southewest, to where the mesh of lights is. Inka catches the scent a bit better-

    ...Silent? What is this? I'm scared...

    You should not need to be scared...

    -Inka's ears flicker and she turns around to examine her surroundings, looking for the source of the voice. It is now that she faces towards the Colony, her senses more alert, that she can lick her paws covered in dust and identify the strange specks coming from the forests beyond-

    This, this fire. Fire?

    -Licks her paw, it tastes ashy. Watches the fields beyond, where the ashes have given the blades of grass a distinctive purple and red colouration-

    [Poison sweat] and...

    Blood! Blood in the fields!

    -Inka turns and tries to make a run towards the forest, but as she crosses the first line of trees she crashes headfirst against an invisible wall-

    Oooof... *paws around* What....? Where? Let me in! This is my place!

    -The spectral images of some Nidorina and Nidorino can be seen running around at the other side of the barrier; they don't pay any attention to her-

    Hey! Kits! Mayonaka! *flails* LET ME IN!

    ???? — Great work you have done with your place.

    -Inka turns around to see a big shadow walking towards her-

    ???? — A mess, it is. Unlike what we managed to do there!

    -A Nidoqueen in full armor appears in front of Inka, resting in a friendly position; however in her gaze and the way she moves around freely it can be seen that it senses Inka as a far lesser being-

    Inka — You? You can't be here... you belong somewhere else...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Really? It seems to me, in this moment this is where you want me to be.

    -Some laments can be heard coming from the city, and the wind around dies-

    Inka — No. I don't need you... — shakes head and pushes back against the invisible wall — That part of our history... or whatever it was. We got over it. We can take care for ourselves!

    -The Nidoqueen swings her tail, causing a tremor that lifts a strange blue fog around them both-

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Have you even heard the news? Who am I kidding, of course you have... — walks closer, menacingly — You just don't want to accept the truth!

    Inka — ...No...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Say it... you can always be honest with me, after all...

    -Inka pushes against the invisible wall harder, her spikes bleeding, she's scared of that creature so much-

    -But between her intense, yet still maternal stare, and the fog clouding every sensation, she can't help but admit it-

    Inka — In this world... now... I am too weak to do anything about it...

    -She bites her tongue; in contrast, the Nidoqueen relinquishes in a strange pleasure as she stretches and cracks her neck-

    Inka — about to cry — No! I'm not like this! And I'm not like you either!

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Yes! That's exactly the issue! This is why your colony suffers! Our Colony! — stretches arms wide and looks around — You want to so desperately [ignore] what is going on around you... even if that spells your doom! Because you no longer suffice!

    Inka — But I'm not alone! I-I... — looks around, seeing only the fog; tries to calm herself — I have the other breeders, and my mate too.

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Oh but that's so... inefficient. They lack, let's say, discipline. I mean, tell me, how many kits did the colony lose to stray [Houndoom] last week, eh?

    Inka — ...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Come on... — licks her paw — I know you know!

    Inka — Twelve...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Thanks! — blinks — Right... you realize that's as many as three fours! — growls — What would have happened if [Maekrite] didn't bring us warning of that wild pack from the west, those vicious monsters, eh?

    -Inka closes her eyes, on the verge of tears-

    Inka — We still could... could...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Someone has to do something about this! To protect the Colony! You know how much we suffered because in this other timeline we had no [gods]... — moves to the side, letting the faint moon show — and in your timeline, it turns out you no longer have the [devils] either!

    -Suddenly a hollow cry emerges from the city beyond. Both Inkas look ahead, to see the spectre of a [   Scolipede   ] emerging from the bloodstained mesh that comprised the City. The Scolipede flies to the sky, surrounding Inlé, encircling it, slowly blocking its light and dominating the sky...-

    -Then the Scolipede eats it.-

    Inka — ...No!

    Suddenly, the entire universe takes on a reddish tint-

    -The Nidoqueen Inka turns towards the city and assumes a battle stance. Those laments that could be heard from the city, now are accompanied by footsteps approaching the Forest-

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Soon those monsters could be here too, or perhaps some worse ones... Just imagine... how easily will this forest be underrun with your Nidos, all uncivilized, undisciplined, untrained...

    Inka — That is not going to happen. We have good peoplemons! And friends! There is going to be [someone, somewhere]...

    -The Nidoqueen snarks, then turns and punches Inka on the gut, pressing her against the wall. Inka falls to the ground, coughing blood-

    Nidoqueen!Inka — It is supposed to be you who has to be here! It is supposed to be you who has to protect this forest for us!

    -A shining light emerges in the Nidoqueen's left paw, while with the right paw she grabs Inka and neck lifts her-

    Inka — Aaahhh! — fights the grip, then she opens her eyes and sees the light materialize into a [Moon Stone] — You want me to...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Step forward and assume your rightful place. WHICH YOU WILL!

    -The Nidoqueen lifts the stone and draws it near Inka, who is trapped, unable to move. The Nidorina's pupils dilate fully, as she is aware of what she is going to become-

    Maekrite didn't want to... He always said, after he saw them in battle... those trained by [that man]...

    I can no longer be happy... I can no longer be myself... I can no longer have...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — smirks — ''Oh, what crosses your mind? I know: "if I evolve I can't have more kits?" And all that crap?

    -The Nidoqueen steps towards the barrier, Inka still in her grasp-

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Do I have to remind you your current situation? I mean... 23 kits is not bad at all I have to admit... — lifts Inka further, allowing her to see part of the forest — But how many this year?

    -Inka's eyes drift to the direction of her burrow, just some short distance away. What for, if it is now empty, no longer a place of safety and warmth for her. Unlike past years, there was no [litter] of kittens annoying her every day to make her life a delight-

    "Now I live alone..."

    "I tried, but it didn't turn out well..."

    "These memories... are a curse!"

    "Leave! I don't want to see you!"

    Nidoqueen!Inka — ...Do I hear "none"? Too bad... Perhaps your [mate] could help you make any? — chuckles, then strangles Inka further — Oh wait... he's left. I wonder why... Oh wait! You kicked him out of the burrow! Because you didn't want to admit the truth...

    That you are getting too old, and too weak. Fourteen years is a long time yes... Oh well... Perhaps he'll leave to get himself another [female]... that or they'll get onto him, if you know what I mean...

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Oh, and the better part? You are now going to be kicked off the breeding circle too! You'll have to watch from afar the pleasure the other females have mating with him! And I'm sure he will appreciate it too, they are all younger, even Mayonaka...

    Inka — No... they said they'd support me... They know why we picked only one mate...

    -The Nidoqueen tries to push the Moon Stone on Inka's face; as she does, the light of the stone covers Inka's field of vision completely, filling her mind and her body with sensations of unlimited power and of a strange loneliness only accompanied by the presence of a faint soulmate-

    Accept me!

    It feels so great, this power...

    It is my rightful place!

    Inka — Noooo! — wither in pain as her memories are invaded by an insinctual drive to become a Nidoqueen.

    -The red tint of the now Inléless universe fades to be substituted slowly by a greenish tint, as if the forest was expanded in every direction-

    Nidoqueen!Inka — You know where those stones are stored... All you need is to-

    "....AAAAHH!"

    Inka — I can protect this colony! I'm not useless and you know this too well! I... I see it clearly... You are just envious because of what you have had to sacrifice!

    -The Nidoqueen stares at her blankly, her tail swinging-

    Inka — I'm not going to take your place. Not here. You are not needed.

    Nidoqueen!Inka — Oh... well... — rubs arms — I would have thought...

    -The Nidoqueen moves to the side, as if it felt ashamed or hurt, however her stare was still defiant and burly to a point-

    Inka — ...What?

    Inka — ...No! Not him!

    Nidoqueen!Inka — That from a certain point of view, yes. All you need to do is to submit and let us be the you that we all want us to be.

    Inka — I still have... our [sons]...

    "We will take good care of them. Just imagine! The joy of seeing them all trained, dressed in full armor, guarding the colony borders... as far as we extend them... Surely [Maekrite] will proudly lead that group..."

    "It will make you so happy... even more than that day he was born... don't you remember it my love?"

    -Inka's mind fades as she remembers the days at the [old PEFE HQ], where she was spending the holidays with Silent's researcher friends. Of how despite Baluarkos's stress and complains, she would allow Iggy to cradle up Maekrite and hold him in the dent that [bullet] had left in his shell-

I didn't want to go because I thought they would demand I be their mate, I swear!> — sits on the ground, tilts head — <but when I got there there was only one and she explained to me that you were scared and in pain.
