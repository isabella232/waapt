    Himura: <It was. Took me quite a while to wash all the blood off, but it was worth it to scare that Gyarados off. That'll teach him to try and eat our schools...>

    Himura goes to sheath his sword, but pauses when he spots a certain Feraligatr and Dragonite approaching.

    Yerta: <Ooh, looks like we have visitors. Do they seem suspicious?>

    Hiruma doesn't answer, simply waiting for the two to swim up to them, stopping a few meters away.

    Lake: <Hello, do you mind if we come i->

    Lake is interrupted by the Samurott suddenly rushing forward, drawing both of his scalchops, and stopping just in front of him and Bozaka, holding the point of his blades right up to their throats.

    <WHOA!!! What's the big idea?!>

    Yerta is also shocked, not expecting her partner to act this way. However, she manages to keep her cool enough to ready her cannons, just in case Hiruma had somehow managed to rightly identify the two as a threat.

    Yerta: <What's the problem, Hiruma?>

    The Samurott ignores his partner, choosing to glare right into Lake's eyes.

    Hiruma: <You have some nerve showing up here again, Gator. Especially after the crimes you committed against our kingdom!>

    Lake: <What are you talking about?>

    Hiruma: <Don't play the fool with me! Do you really think I would forget the face of a Pokémon that not only kidnapped Prince Pleo, but put the entire Reef under threat of the wrath of a god?!>

    <And I'm pretty sure you were with him last time. Or at least, you were with a Gator...>
