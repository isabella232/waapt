    Treeo nods, the Sceptile once again basking on a rock, having paid another visit to her Dream Island.

    Treeo: <We had to go to this weird...outer space sorta' place, but yes, we found a shard. So yesterday wasn't a total loss, even if it took us hours to actually get anything done...>

    Comet: <Hmm. It's not a ship part, but at least it's progress.>

    With a frown, Comet leans back against a rather large rock, gazing up at the sky.

    <Well, if I was there...>

    Treeo: <You'd tell them to get on with it, I know.>

    Comet: <Oh, you don't want that?>

    Treeo: <No, I would love it. I like Mezzo, but her leadership skills need a little work.>

    Comet: <No doubt about it. I wish there were some way I could get to you though...>

    The Charizard let's out a low sigh...then suddenly has a brainwave.

    <Hey! What if I used one of those shards we've found?>

    Hearing this, Treeo sits up with a start, blinking in shock.

    Treeo: <What?! Are you joking? There's no way to know where you could end up!>

    This is answered with a dismissive shrug from the Charizard.

    Comet: <Hey, they haven't killed anymon yet. That we know of.> It's worth a try, no?>

    Treeo: <Well...I suppose...but it's a huge risk, Comet. Are you absolutely sure about this?>

    She nods confidently.

    Comet: <Of course I am. Besides, you never get anything in life without taking risks, right?>

    Treeo: <...Point. Oh, but what about the water? How are you going to cope with that?>

    Treeo is given a blank, somewhat fearful stare by Comet, the Charizard shifting uncomfortably at the thought of all that cold, suffocating water.

    Comet: <Er...m-maybe I c-could hitch a r-ride in a D-dive b-bubble...D-do you think they would l-let me?>

    Treeo: <Hmm...might be a bit cramped...but I don't see any reason why they wouldn't. Still, I need to ask you one more time; are you sure about this?>

    Comet hesitates...then nods again.

    Comet: <Yes...I'm sure. I might just die of boredom if I have to sit on this beach for much longer if I don't come along, anyway...>
