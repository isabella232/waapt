    Celadon City

    Rex was waiting, standing by. A lot had happened since she parted ways with the Daycare Center man on Route 5. Though not Kanto-born, she felt this place to be like a second home. Charybdis was bred. Now it may have been underhanded, but it was necessary for the family's goals. She was finally born. Her youngest sister, Hikari, was born a year ago on the day into the Busso family. She was given an Absol named Tau, an Absol caught by her older sister who had taken a trip into Sinnoh during this auspicious event. As the last born it was her duty to capture the Absol for her sister. Rex had spent months, while the Busso family continued their work in Kanto in the casinos and dipping their hands into any amount of business they could.

    The egg was sent to the family's main facility in Cinnabar Island and Rex returned in time for Hikari's birth with Tau.

    Currently the young woman was in the Pokèmont Center with Charybdis and was leaned up against the wall on the phone, calling for Mezzy. Rex's family came first, so her downtime came from working in the Busso family businesses, particularly for her, the Celadon Department store. Her cousin Usa had hold of the Casinos and her Uncle was leading the construction yards in Vermillion City.

    Zap sat by Rex's side, little Hikari on his lap. The adorable girl was bright eyed and was dressed in a white jumper with black shorts. She had a fuzzy hood that was pulled up to her bangs. She quietly suckled on her pacifier while she was held by the electric mouse.

    "Come on Mezzy.... pick up." She had been unable to connect to the video systems with her apps. Something, as of late, was interfering. Of course this unsettled the cosplaying trainer and she sought to get into contact with her friend.
