    465363 Herbert40k19th Aug 2015 06:32:39 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
    Imperial Commissar
    Thanks to Moon to spagging this with me.

    Sootopolis Pokémon Center

    Duke is currently browsing through a selection of magazines, looking for something to read. A short, well-dressed young boy walks into the lobby, stops upon seeing Duke, then looks him up and down for a moment.

    Shun: Tall, well-built, handsome... going by the description she gave me, I suspect that this is Sakura's sister's boyfriend.

    He walks up to Duke.

    Shun: Excuse me, sir?

    As Duke picks up a magazine he hears Shun and turns around to see him, though he takes a moment to notice that he had to look down a bit to see him.

    Duke: Hey there, are you looking for something?

    Shun nods, doing his best not to crane his neck too much as he looks up at Duke.

    Shun: I'm looking for an associate of Sakura Riese. You fit his description.

    Duke: Sakura? Yeah, I'm her sister's boyfriend. Did something happen to her?

    Shun shakes his head.

    Shun: Thankfully not. I'm a detective with the International Police. She told me to ask you for details regarding a kidnapping case you were the victim of.

    Duke blinks a couple of times.

    Duke: You're a detective? You look a bit too young and small to be one.

    Shun huffs.

    Shun: So I've been told on numerous occasions. Do you need to see my badge before you start taking me seriously?

    Duke shakes his head, looking a bit amused by Shun's response.

    Duke: No need, I can already tell you're not lying.

    His expression soon sours, however.

    Duke: Though... why are you investigating that?

    Shun: It's a personal favour for S- Miss Riese, in return for her assistance with other cases.

    Duke: Huh? Odd, she never told me that she worked alongside a kid detective.

    The colour rises in Shun's cheeks as he responds.

    Shun: ...Our partnership is a relatively recent one. In any case, neither my age nor my relationship with Miss Riese is relevant to my current line of questioning.

    Duke: Well... I don't even know where to begin, really

    He scratches the back of his head, looking quite bothered by the questioning.

    Shun: From the beginning would be ideal.

    Duke: Well...

    It's at this point that a girl very similar in appearance to Sakura walks over, carrying two hotdogs in her hands and using her arm to hold a can of soda near her chest.

    Lyuri: Hey, Duke, I got something for us to eat and drink. Can you please take one of these? The soda is pretty cold.

    With Lyuri's timely entrance, Duke smiles and takes one of the hotdogs from her, allowing her to hold the soda in her now free hand.

    Lyuri: Thanks, I thought I would end up dropping it.

    She then looks over at Shun.

    Lyuri: And who's the kid?

    Shun speaks up before Duke can reply.

    Shun: Shun Hontou, International Police. Before you ask, yes, it is somewhat unusual to see someone of my age in such a position and no, neither my height nor my relative youth keeps me from doing my job properly. Now, barring any further questions, may we continue?

    Lyuri: Whoa whoa. Why are questioning my boyfriend?

    Shun: He's not under suspicion for any reason, if that's your concern. I presume you are Sakura Riese's sister?

    Lyuri: Yeah? How do you know my sister?

    Duke: They're apparently working together.

    Lyuri tilts her head slightly.

    Lyuri:... Eh. Sakura was always good with kids so I guess not even kid prodigy here can resist her.

    Shun glares at Lyuri.

    Shun: Whatever you're insinuating, Miss Riese, your patronising remarks are not what I was hoping to hear. All I want is a few questions answered and then I will be on my way. This whole process will go by much quicker if we treat each other with respect. If you can't manage that, then I ask that you at least attempt to feign it.

    Lyuri: What are you even questioning Duke about?

    Shun: Your sister advised me to talk to him. According to her, the two of you could provide me with information regarding the Cipher kidnapping case.

    Lyuri: Cipher, huh?

    She let out a long sigh.

    Lyuri: And here I was hoping that I wouldn't have to deal with all this police interrogation stuff.

    Duke: We never did talk about any of this with the police before, now that I think about it.

    Shun raises an eyebrow, but his tone becomes less intense as he continues.

    Shun: ...If it makes you feel more comfortable, I'm neither judge nor jury. I'm investigating the case as a service to your sister. You can tell me as much or as little as you please, but in order to make progress towards any arrests I need all the help I can get.

    Lyuri: Well, if my sister can trust you I guess I can do the same.

    Duke: In any case, it would probably be better if we sat down. This talk could get a little long.

    Shun does as requested and takes a seat and is soon followed by the couple.

    Duke: So... It was all really sudden. I was just taking a walk through the outskirts of some city and I got ambushed. When I realized what was going on, it was already too late for me to do anything about it.

    Lyuri: And the guy who did it had the guts to record everything and then send it off to me just to mock me.

    Lyuri then takes a bite of her hotdog, looking quite grumpy. Shun begins making notes in his jotter, endeavouring to keep his expression level.

    Shun: ...Are you able to provide a description of the ambusher?

    Duke: At the time he was wearing a black outfit and a mask...

    Lyuri: But we saw him without it before. Blond guy with blue eyes, normally dressed in black. Average height. He used to work under one of my friends and seemed to be an ok guy, but I guess it was all a facade.

    Shun looks up, his curiosity piqued.

    Shun: So you knew the kidnapper before the incident?

    Lyuri: Only talked to him once.

    Shun makes another note.

    Shun: ...I see. You have no reason to believe that the two of you were targeted because of this personal connection, then?

    Lyuri: Nope. Our friend probably talked about us to him so that probably was how he decided to go after us. It didn't help that we were separated from the rest of our group for the time being.

    Shun: By "group" you mean the J-Team, I assume.

    Duke: Guess you're familiar with us already.

    Duke then opens the can of soda and begins drinking some of it before taking a bite out of his hotdog and handing the soda to Lyuri.

    Shun: I wouldn't be much of a detective had I not become familiar with you over the course of the last few weeks. Anyway, please continue.

    Lyuri: After it happened I wanted to save Duke so I set off to do so. I tried to do it by myself but I basically walked right into a trap and was blackmailed into helping him at Cipher with his shady business.

    Shun hesitates for a moment, his pencil stopping mid-sentence, before asking his next question.

    Shun: ...Would you be willing to elaborate on what you mean by "dirty business"?

    Lyuri: He never told me what he wanted to do, just what he wanted me to do. I guess he was smart enough not to give out his plans to me. All I know is that he wanted to steal some files from Cipher.

    Shun sighs.

    Shun: He must have told you something more specific than that. He wouldn't have used blackmail if there wasn't a particular service or object he wanted from you.

    Lyuri: I really don't know, ok? He probably just blackmailed me so he could have someone do the dirty work for him that he knew he could keep in check if I tried to do anything. Everybody in Cipher was just as awful, probably one planning to backstab the other.

    Shun realises he isn't going to get anywhere with this line of questioning, and changes his tack.

    Shun: Very well then... how long did this go on for, and under what circumstances did it come to an end?

    Lyuri and Duke both stay quiet, they exchange looks for a moment before looking to be at a complete loss. Shun arches an eyebrow.

    Shun: ...Is something the matter?

    Lyuri: It's... difficult.

    Shun: I understand. Take your time.

    There's more silence as Lyuri and Duke basically just try to think of something, until Lyuri begins speaking up, now looking quite despondent.

    Lyuri: I... I almost ended up killing a friend, while working under that guy. I thought if I hesitated Duke would... Uh... You know. I was so scared, but in the end I couldn't do it. It didn't change a thing, but I caused that friend a lot of pain and trauma just because I was afraid of what would happen to Duke.

    Duke: Lyuri, you shouldn't-

    Lyuri: It's okay, Duke.

    She sighs. Shun is silent for a long time. He doesn't write anything more in his notebook, and when he looks up to ask another question, there's a fire in his eyes that wasn't there before.

    Shun: ...I need a name.

    Duke: Of?

    Shun: The man responsible for all of this. The man who ought to be shouldering the blame and living with the aftermath.

    Lyuri: The name is Allen, I don't remember his surname though. It's been a long time now.

    Shun writes the name down and underlines it.

    Shun: ...Thank you for your cooperation, Miss Riese. And to you as well, Duke. I apologise for forcing you to recount that experience, but... rest assured that if there is anything I can do to see this man made to answer for what he made you undergo, I shall make it a priority.

    Lyuri: Did my sister ask you to do this?

    Shun shakes his head.

    Shun: I suggested it after she confided in me. She was... reluctant at first, but after discussing the matter we agreed that his crimes cannot go unanswered, especially given the possibility of a repeat offence.

    Lyuri: I guess. Somebody has to make him pay.

    She sighs.

    Lyuri: Ugh... This was not how I wanted to start my day...

    Shun: ...Apologies. That was never my intention, I assure-

    He stops, then gives a sigh of his own, the fire in his eyes extinguished.

    Shun: Never mind. I have everything I need. I won't waste any more of your time.

    He turns briskly on his heel and walks away.

    Duke: Hey, wait.

    Duke then stands up himself and begins walking towards Shun, who stops midway up the stairs to his room.

    Shun: ...What? I already said that I'm not going to make you tell me anything else. We're free to wash our hands of each other and go our separate ways.

    Duke: Thanks. We never asked you to do this and neither did Sakura, but even then you want to help us get our case solved. I dunno if it's because you just want to help us or if it's out of principle since you're a detective, but still, I just want to let you know that we're grateful even if having to talk about all of this again wasn't exactly pleasant. So, again, thank you.

    Shun blinks in surprise, then hesitates and looks away, cheeks flushing brightly.

    Shun: ...You shouldn't feel the need to thank me for doing my job.

    Duke folds his arms and gives Shun a smile.

    Duke: Credit is given where credit is due. No matter if it's your job or not, you still deserve credit for it, after all, it's the little things that can keep people going when things are rough. Act as you might, but I can easily tell that you lead a very stressful life for someone of your age.

    Shun: Yours has been harder, from the sounds of it.

    Duke: That was the dark time of my life, but I'm doing fine now. I'm being able to relax and I have Lyuri to help me get through any problems, as well as my other friends. You have a lot of responsibilities for someone so young, it must be hard to find time for yourself.

    Shun attempts to shrug in what he hopes is a nonchalant manner.

    Shun: ...I can deal with it.

    Duke: I doubt it. But I guess Sakura managed to make friends with you, so I'm sure you'll have someone talk to. If you're having trouble you can come talk to me, since you're helping us, I'd gladly give you some help even if it's something small.

    Shun goes even redder.

    Shun: Thank you. I... I'll be sure to keep that in mind. Now if you'll excuse me I have to deal very important police-y stuff that needs my full and undivided attention for very good reasons.

    He darts into his room. Soon afterwards, Lyuri walks over to Duke, giving him a smirk.

    Lyuri: Man, you just have a way with people huh? You even made the boy blush.

    Duke smiles back at her.

    Duke: I just figured he need a bit of help.

    Lyuri: You're too much of a nice guy, you know? Trying to help everybody with their problems... there's a point where you'll have too many people to help.

    Duke: I... guess.

    He rubs the back of his neck while Lyuri just chuckles a little bit.

    Lyuri: I love that about you though, so yeah, don't change, I think it's better for everyone if you don't. After all, you're good at dealing with people.

    Duke: Noted.

    Lyuri: Now how about we go do something like... I dunno, go to a restaurant? Better do something to make this day less of a downer.

    Duke: I'm down for it.

    The couple then heads out, hand in hand.
