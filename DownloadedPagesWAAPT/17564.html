    Meteor Falls - Presumably in the past

    Aline, Lara and Eve are walking through the cave, admiring it's interior

    "Woooow! This place is so beautiful for a cave." said Lara.

    "Yeah, but we need to get out of here quickly, who knows what kind of Pokémon live here?" said Eve, looking around nervously.

    Aline nodded and the three girls continued on their way. Lara was trying to keep herself busy by kicking some pebbles around.

    "Man, we were almost out of all this trouble and then your Cleffa just had to ruin it all." complained Lara, grumpily, kicking a larger pebble into the distance.

    Aline looked at the pebble fly and noticed that it seemed to land onto a black thing in the distance, worse yet, that something started moving.

    "She can't really control what she's doing you know..." said Eve, sighing as she petted the little star mon.

    As the two argued, Aline noticed the black thing start to get up, revealing itself to be a Hydreigon. It then turned around, spotting the three girls and looking quite angry. Blinking and dreading what was about to happen, Aline began tugging at Lara's shirt, who was blissfully unaware of what had just happened.

    "It's still no excuse though! I mean, what were the chances of what she did happening?!" said Lara, folding her arms.

    Aline then noticed that the Hydreigon was charging an attack from his mouth, and he seemed to be aiming directly at the unaware Eve.

    "I don't know, we haven't studied probability ye-"

    Just as the Hydreigon shot a Dragon Pulse, Aline dived into Eve to force her out of the way, the attack flew past the girls, hitting right above the entrance to the cave and making it collapse, leaving them trapped.

    "W-Wha..." said Lara, turning around and spotting the Hydreigon "O-Oh..." she said as she took a few steps back.

    "What happened?!" said Eve, getting up alongisde Aline and also spotting the Hydreigon "That's... Aline, I think we might need-" her eyes then went wide open "... Divoom was left behind."

    The girls just watched the Hydreigon get closer to them, with Aline sending out Scout, who was in a star trim, in hope that he could do something to help.
