    -The Jagers arch several eyebrows at her from across the campfire.-

    Maxim: <Eet looks like a backpack.>

    Oggie: <Oh, dat's probably her jetpack! Remember, dah vun she made a few veeks ago?>

    Dimo: <Hy dunno, eet don't look like it should...>

    -Phosphora puts it on - it's surprisingly small, fitting over her shoulder blades more like a metal harness than a real backpack.-

    Phosphora: Alright...

    -She takes out a pocket recorder and presses the "record" button.-

    Phosphora: Project A.W. Field Testing, Test 001. Harness fits well, slight adjustment on waist belt may be necessary due to clothing bulk. Activating hover properties... now.

    -She presses a button on the belt and the harness starts with a hum. She raises off of the ground a few inches as a brilliant yellow energy exhaust fires from a pair of energy coils on the back.-

    Phosphora: Hover mode activates as intended - long-term effects of Minovsky Particle usage will have to be tested on another date.

    -She starts shaking in anticipation.-

    Phosphora: Now... It's time to test the experimental properties of Project A.W. The target dummy has been set up, the energy flux issues from production have been addressed, and... now it's time to test this baby out.

    -She can barely keep the excitement from her voice as she presses a different button on her belt.-

    Phosphora: Here. WE. GO!

    -Energy begins pulsing through the harness as brilliant golden-yellow energy rushes through the harness into Phosphora herself. Her arms, hands, and torso are covered in golden radiance as the exhaust ports on the back release energy resembling golden wings of light.-

    Phosphora: LET'S DO THIS!

    -Phosphora rockets forward towards the target dummy and punches it with a golden-covered fist. The combined energy of the jetpack, Phosphora's own punching arm and the golden luminescence, the construct of straw and wood shatters into splinters and fabric shreds.-

    Dimo: O_O

    Maxim: =O

    Oggie: <WOOOOOOOOOH! HYU GO, GORL!>

    -Phosphra pants as the energy burst dissipates. She goes over and picks up the recorder.-

    Phosphora: Ha... ha... Project Avenging Wrath... Test 001... complete.
