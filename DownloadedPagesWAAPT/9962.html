    The Dream World - Last Night

    Even if it was disconnected from reality, time still seemed to pass at a normal rate. The endless sky around the small island the team was staying on for the time being was now dark as night, a large moon hanging in the inky sky dotted with countless stars, providing just enough light to see by.

    However, most of the team was now snoozing soundly, save a certain Charizard and Sceptile, who had stayed up late to speak in private.

    Treeo: <Alright them Comet, what is it you want to...>

    Treeo stopped as he opened his jaw wide and yawned rather loudly, a tried look on his face.

    <Oh my. Sorry about that, I'm just a bit tired, that's all.>

    Comet: <It's fine. It's been a long day.>

    Treeo: <Yeah. Alright, go on then.>

    The Charizard hesitated. Even though she and Treeo had, well...an intimate relationship, she was still a little hesitant to tell him what she had to say.

    Comet: <Well...>

    Treeo: <What's wrong? Come on, Comet. You can tell me. I promise I won't let anyone else know if you want to keep this a secret.>

    Comet: <It's not that, Treeo. It's just that I haven't spoken about this to anymon. I'm only even considering telling you this because me and you are so close.>

    Treeo: <Well, if you don't want to tell me, you don't have to. Everyone's allowed to have secrets, as long as they're not hurting anybody.>

    The Charizard shook her head.

    Comet: <No, no...I really should tell you. That, and the truth is probably gonna' come out sooner or later anyway. I'm just gonna' have to bite the bullet here.>

    Treeo: <...Alright. Whenever you're ready, then.>

    Comet nodded, then inhaled deeply to calm herself before she finally told the truth.

    Comet: <Okay. So, you remember how we met, yeah?>

    Treeo: <Back in the desert in Hoenn? Yeah, I remember. Even though it was a long while ago.>

    Comet: <Well then, remember how I told you I was from Pokémon Island near Kanto?>

    Treeo: <I remember. Why?>

    Still hesitant, the Charizard frowned.

    Comet: <Funny thing, that. I had been living on Pokémon Island, yeah. But it's not where I was born.>

    Treeo: <Huh. So you haven't been entirely truthful about where you come from?>

    Comet: <Not really.>

    Treeo: <That's not so bad. If you're not from Pokémon Island, then where are you from?>

    This was the hard part. What Comet was about to say next would likely blow Treeo's mind.

    Comet: <I'm not from anywhere. Not from your world, at least.>

    Treeo blinked. His world? What did she mean by that?

    Treeo: <Oh. So you're an alien?>

    The Charizard scowled at the Sceptile.

    Comet: <No! Does anything about me look alien to you?>

    Treeo: <Calm down! I was just joking!>

    The Sceptile let out a throaty chuckle.

    <Sorry! When you told me you weren't from my world, I just couldn't help myself. You couldn't possibly be an alien, Comet. You're a perfectly ordinary Charizard!>

    Comet: <That's what you think though. I may be a Charizard, but I'm far from perfectly ordinary.>

    Treeo: <Well. If you're not from my world, then which world are you from? That Pokémon only world?>

    Comet shook her head dismissively.

    Comet: <No, but you're in the ballpark. There aren't any humans where I come from.>

    She gave Treeo a frown.

    <Can I just tell you? I don't really feel like guessing games right now.>

    Treeo: <Oh, I'm sorry. Go ahead, Comet. Tell me.>

    Comet: <Right. Wanna' know where I'm from?>

    She spread her arms, gesturing at her surroundings.

    <Here. Right here. Perfectly ordinary Comet is a Dream World Pokémon. I was born right here, and then I came to your world for...>

    She stopped, biting her tongue before she got carried away.

    <For reasons that I don't want to get into right now. But there. The truth is out after all this time.>

    The Charizard breathed a sigh of relief as Treeo tilted his head in surprise.

    Treeo: <So you're a Dream World Charizard?>

    Comet: <Yes! I'm a Dream World Charizard. Oh, mon! I can't believe how good it is to actually have told somemon! It's like I've been carrying a pile of bricks on my back all this time, and now they're gone! Ha! Ahahaha!>

    Comet's sudden shift in behavior was starting to freak Treeo out a bit.

    Treeo: <...Are you alright, Comet? You're being a bit demented right now.>

    She responded by flashing a toothy grin.

    Comet: <I'm fine! Never been better, actually! I just...feel so good now that's over with.>

    She frowned slightly, tilting her head at the Sceptile.

    <You're not mad though, right?>

    Treeo: <Mad? Why would I be mad?>

    Comet: <Because I didn't tell anyone about this for nearly a year and a half? Not even you? The Pokémon I'm closest with?>

    Treeo: <Eh...>

    Treeo shrugged.

    <I'm not really sure how to feel. I guess I'm still coming to terms with the idea that you're a Dream World Pokémon. It's a bit of a shock, you know? Even though it does explain a lot of things. Like how none of the crazy stuff we're always going up against even phases you.>

    Comet: <Treeo. I'm from a world where dreams manifest themselves as reality. Even the craziest things you and the others have seen are positively tame compared to some of the messed up stuff I've seen.>

    Treeo: <...Point made.>
