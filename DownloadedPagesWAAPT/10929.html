    Baron Samedi: "This is your mindscape. Inside of your head. I'm a part of you. I'm your mental defenses."

    "Well then, you've been doing a pretty shitty job."

    Baron Samedi: "Hey! That rabid thing already got Morality. I was waiting for an opportune moment to strike."

    "Fine. Whatever. I'm sure this is just a hallucination."

    Baron Samedi: "Oh no. This is real."

    "Alright then. What do I need to do?"

    Baron Samedi: "To stop Madness? We need to get something from one of the shops."

    "Lead the way."

    They set off down the eerie streets, until they stop in front of a shop titled: "The Centerpiece".

    Baron Samedi: "In here."

    They enter. Inside, memories line the walls.

    "So, what are we grabbing."

    Baron Samedi: "This."

    He indicates a series of memories, forged into a katana of familiar shape.

    Baron Samedi: "It's what we will use to fight Madness."

    He also points to a harness, made out of other memories.

    Baron Samedi: "And once you damage him enough, we can yoke him with this."

    "Why not just kill him?"

    Baron Samedi: "Because it's impossible."

    "Shit. So we're just going to lock him up?"

    Baron Samedi: "Yeah. But this time, it'll work. You've gained control over the forces playing havoc with your brain. Now you just need to lock down the parasite."

    "Alright then. Let's do this."

    Pi grabs the harness and the sword.
