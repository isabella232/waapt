    Justy: <We've been doing good now, haven't we?>

    Justy: <I, uh, had your parents in my squad the other day.>

    Justy: <In a nutshell, yes.>

    -Derek grabs himself a glass of water before he starts.-

    Derek: <To start... they say that there are two kinds of family. The one you're born with, and the one you choose. I think this is a false dichotomy.
