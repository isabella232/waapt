    Laverre Town (sometime after Scoobyplot)

    -Despite complaining loudly about the mud and the weather, Leonard has elected to pay a visit to Laverre, and is currently wandering around the town, grumbling under his breath. Cleo, after spending the past few days solving mysteries involving people in silly Pokemon costumes. is currently having a frothy mug of soda at one of the local taverns in Laverre. Flanked, as always, by her devoted Delphox and Blaziken. It seems that Cleo is constantly checking the time on her Holo caster as if she's waiting for someone in particular. And as luck would have it, said someone happens to notice her during one of his circuits of the town and addresses her with an irritable yell.-

    Leonard: There you are. Arceus, how you can you tolerate this forsaken bog?

    -Cleo, dainty as ever, spits out the sip of soda she was currently drinking and gives Leonard an annoyed glare while thumping her collarbone.-

    Cleo: DANDELION! Don't fucking DO that! You WAIT for someone to finish their drink before you catch their attention! I could've choked yannow!

    Leonard: Well, that would've been just tragic. Should I have signalled my presence with a flare or suchlike before approaching you as well?

    Cleo: Don't give me your sass, Dandelion. Though, I guess you'll pass as company while I wait in this rinky-dink bar for Larry and Pip. Lovebirds are probably on one of those old-fashioned rowboat dates being the schmaltzy romantics they are >_>.

    -Cleo mutters something under her breath. Probably along the lines of "I wish I could go on a rowboat date", but can't be too certain. Meanwhile, Pakhet and Montu watch on.-

    Montu: -Big Sigh- <Beatrice and Benedick at it again...>

    Pakhet': -Looking around- <Where's Gallade? We can't comment on this properly without him! Or the liquor, but mostly him.>

    -There is a quiet blip somewhere behind Pakhet and Montu.-

    Gallade: <Oh, I'm here. Just needed to stock up on malt whiskey before providing a running commentary.>

    Pakhet: <15 or 25?>

    Gallade: <30. An occasion like this demands the good stuff.>

    Pakhet: <Grande~>

    Montu: <We can always count on you, old boy~>

    -Leonard, meanwhile, gives a huff.-

    Leonard: And I suppose you'll have to make do as a conversation partner whilst I wait for my cousins to return from whatever expedition they've elected to embark on. Then again, I suppose just happening to encounter you here worked out rather conveniently, since there's something I ought to tell you.

    Cleo: Oh? Pray do tell, Dandelion.

    -Meanwhile, Pakhet is ~winking~ in and out different types of alcoholic beverages to contemplate the 30 year old scotch.-

    Montu: -Taking one of the shot glasses- <Oooh~ Leo's got some big news to share~>

    Gallade: <I don't think it's quite what Cleo's expecting, though.>

    -Leonard glances away from Cleo.-

    Leonard: ...I'm moving away from Kalos.

    Cleo, instead of spitting out her beverage, swallows it in a rather pained gulp (complete with Wide Eyes and Shrunken Irises). Coughing and sputtering somewhat after the drink (while massaging her neck).

    Cleo: Ha... Good joke Leonard. You're hilarious.

    Leonard: I wish I was joking. As it were, Father's expanding the family business, and he needs someone to head the Almia branch. For whatever reason, he decided that that someone ought to be me.

    -He gives a sigh.-

    Leonard: Of course, the thought that I might not want to leave Kalos behind never seemed to occur to him...

    Cleo herself is speechless, and looks away for a moment. Pakhet shakily points a shot glass at the too.

    Pakhet: <Issh it me bein' inebriated, or are thsoe two sharin' a tender moment tagetherrr?>

    Montu: <Inn there own unique wayysh, of course.>

    Gallade: <Issh probably the closhest they're gonna get, anywaysh.>

    -Cleo seems to be digging her nails into the wooden table as she looks over to Leonard.-

    Cleo: Hey Leonard... I guess I should tell ya something...

    -This time Montu and Pakhet go wide-eyed, but they don't spit because that's perfectly good 30 year old scotch they're drinking.-

    Montu: <Issh she?>

    Pakhet: <I fink she issh!>

    -Leonard raises an eyebrow at Cleo, and for a moment his tone becomes almost hopeful.-

    Leonard: ...Yes?

    Cleo: I...

    ...I wish you the best of luck on your endeavours and I hope you do well, Leonard..

    -Pakhet and Montu slam their heads together.-

    Gallade: <Whersh the really strong sthuff...?>

    Pakhet: <I THOUGHT THISH WASSSH THE REALLY SHHTRONG STUFF D:>

    -Leonard gives Cleo a rather strange, almost taken aback look, but then manages a small smile.-

    Leonard: ...Thank you, Cleo.

    -His smile disappears as quickly as it came.-

    Leonard: For the record, I think it would be best if we didn't keep in touch. I don't think either of us want to go through the cumbersome process of maintaining a long-distance relationship. Erm... well, you know what I mean.

    -If one had a recording device, they could probably pinpoint the exact moment where Cleo's heart rips in half plummets.-

    Cleo: -With a faint blush on her cheeks- Yeah, I understand...

    -Cleo fiddles for a moment, before replying with a bit of a playful smirk.-

    Cleo: You will be back, right? After all, I don't want my winning of the Laverre Cup next season to be an entire cakewalk, Dandelion~

    Leonard: Of course. The moment a vacation slot opens up, I'll be on the first plane back here. Someone needs to make sure that the championship remains in the hands of an individual with decent pedigree.

    Cleo: -Leering- I don't like what you're trying to imply there, Dandelion. Didn't know you were one of those types. I expect that shit from the Perillouxs, not you >_>.

    Pakhet: <Yeah. he needsh to be a little more carful wif his word choice thar.>

    Gallade: <Maybe hesh being a dick so she doesn't missh him quite as mucsh?>

    Leonard: I was merely jesting.

    Cleo: Well when you hear comments along that line all the time, it becomes hard to decipher what's in jest and what's serious >_>

    Leonard: In that case, then, I'd suggest seeking out better company - the kind that you can expect to refrain from making such comments without a trace of sarcasm. Or perhaps you ought to just make an effort to try and read my intentions a little better.

    Gallade: <...Nah, I think hesh just being a dick for the shake of it.>

    Pakhet has passed out. Montu instead replies to Gallade.

    Montu: <Whelp. It wassh nice knowin' yeh mein friedn...>

    Gallade: <Likewisch.>

    Montu: <Piss poisoning is the best way tah go~>

    Cleo: A) I can't help what kind of company dad brings home for business and 1) That would involve spending time and effort on you, which I most certainly would not do.

    -There is a girlish giggle that can be heard in the background.-

    Pippi: Well count my stars, It seems that cus' has come to say his last goodbyes to the lady of his dreams before his rendezvous in Almia~

    Lawrence: -Mockingly- Isn't that sweet~

    -Cleo says nothing and instead goes deep red. Leonard, meanwhile, folds his arms and gives a huff.-

    Leonard: I figured that I ought to inform her of my departure whilst I was in the area. No reason to go out of my way for such an errand, after all.

    Pippi: Tch. You always come up with the worst possible excuses, cher. Pretty much everyone knows that you came here to see Cleo first and your own kin second. Even Papi thinks so.

    -Lawrence seemingly is about to raise an eyebrow, but Pippi buts in before he can even do that.-

    Pippi: Yeah really. He's not that far gone...

    -Lawrence decides to re-rail the subject before it goes somewhere unpleasant fast.-

    Lawrence: Well then, since you two parakeets have said your goodbyes. Cleo? It's about time we head on home.

    Pippi: -To Leonard- And you best be heading to the old manor. Pa's gonna be a might annoyed if your not on time for your own goodbye banquet

    Leonard: ...Of course.

    -He turns to Cleo, and for a moment looks as if he's about to say something more before turning and walking away without another word. The rather sloshed Gallade begins to stumble after him, before glancing back to Cleo and waving an armblade in her general direction.-

    Gallade: ~He's gonna mish you, by the way.~

    -Cleo closes her eyes and turns her head away, flushing deeper red. Meanwhile, Pippi plants a kiss on Lawrence's cheek.-

    Pippi: Later boo. -Whispering into his ear- I'll give you the update on Leo's condition later~

    -Lawrence smirks as Pippi picks up her and opens her parasol before walking outside, following behind Leonard. Meanwhile, Lawrence consols his little sister.-

    Lawrence: Buck up, kiddo. Goodbyes ain't forever yannow~

    -Cleo doesn't respond. Lawrence sighs and rolls his eyes, before shaking his head with a smile on his face.-

    Lawrence: Let's go home.

    -Lawrence helps his sister up and the two of them walk out of the bar, Cleo looking absolutely dejected. Montu finishes off the rest of liquor, before Pakhet suddenly wakes up.-

    Pakhet: <Wait, THEY FERGOT ABOUT USSSSSS D:>

    -And ~winks~ the two of them back to the de Nile Chateau.-
