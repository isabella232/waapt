    Canalave City, Sewers

    -Cid taps his foot against the slimy, algae covered concrete. A small dingy turns a far-off corner and approaches. Cid watches the boat close in, and air of mild irritation mingling around him along with the smells best left undescribed.-

    Cid: You're late.

    -A mousy young man pushes his glasses up the ridge of his nose as he pushes himself off the boat onto the concrete landing.-

    Quartermaster: Apologies, sir. The Monsiour Charlemagne was in the middle of a surprise quarters inspection. Thankfully, there was ample evidence of an imminent storm that I convinced the captain to call it short so I may not be delayed further.

    -Cid arches an eyebrow.-

    Cid: Interesting. And inventive. Two excellent bargaining chips. Now all I need is a name.

    -The quartermaster nods.-

    Quartermaster: My name is Zed. Zed Schwartz. And I want you to commandeer my ship.

    Cid: Your ship?

    Zed: Allow me to rephrase. I want you to reclaim my ship.

    -Cid's curiosity was piqued.-

    Cid: ... Interesting. Start from when the "captain" entered the picture.

    -Zed gives a slight nod in approval.-

    Zed: I was shipping gray market Pokeballs from Canalave to the Orange Islands. Charlemagne boarded my ship as we took a detour around Oblivia. He wore a different outfit, then. Dark gray and green.

    -Cid cocks his head.-

    Cid: Pinchers?

    -Zed nods.-

    Zed: He commandeered my ship and took me captive. He used me as a way of putting up a "legitimate" front to his smuggling business, eventually letting me to my own devices when he learned how good I am at numbers. At this point, he's forgotten I was ever a captive at all.

    -Cid remains silent for a moment as he mulls something over.-

    Cid: ... What is that boat to you?

    Zed: It is the last remaining legacy I have of my grandmother.

    -Cid nods. He rubs the stubble on his chin.-

    Cid: ... It was the Gyarados incident that incited this, wasn't it?

    -Zed's shoulders tense enough to cause Cid pause. The younger man eventually nods, stiffly.-

    Cid: ... Remove his power base. Sour his outside contacts and get whispers going in the crew. We don't want a fight that will damage the ship.

    -For the first time as long as Cid's known the young man, Zed smiles.-
