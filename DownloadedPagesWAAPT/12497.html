    Duke quickly gets downstairs where Lyuri and Sakura were just having a chat with each other, however they soon noticed him.

    "Hi girls, Im back." said Duke, with a happy smile.

    Lyuri immediately gets up from the chair she was on and stares at Duke for a few seconds, Sakura was initially surprised, but her expression quickly changed into a big, happy grin, but remained silent.

    "D-Duke... I..." said Lyuri, looking about to cry

    Lyuri then dashes at Duke, glomping him; he manages to catch her without falling though. Lyuri kept a very tight hug on him and by this point she was already crying.

    "Im sorry! Everything that happened was my fault, I wanted to help you but I-" said Lyuri, until she was stopped by Duke hugging her back pretty much as tightly ... I...

    "Listen, while I dont approve of the lengths you went, you sacrificed a lot just to protect me. So... Thanks Lyuri, that menas a lot for me. said Duke, already knowing what Lyuri was going to say.

    "But... I only made things worse for you in the end! And for Herbert too..." said Lyuri, burying her head on his chest and still crying

    "Things couldve gone a lot better, thats true. The road to hell is paved with good intentions, but you still put everything yourself on the line for me, Im sure you tried your best to get out of there with me in tow, but you underestimated how difficult things were going to be. It was a mistake, but Im sure youve already learned something from it." said Duke, stroking Lyuris hair.

    Duke tried to break off the hug, but Lyuri had a very strong grip on him and he didnt want to force anything, Lyuri was still crying and sobbing, he knew that because he felt that his shirt was getting wet and could hear her sobs. He lets out a sigh.

    "... Lyuri...?"

    Lyuri then looks up at him "... Yes...?" she said, along with a few sobs, her voice shaking and very weak.

    "Murder machine or not, I cant stand seeing you like that, could you please stop crying, for me?" Duke requested, wiping out some of Lyuris tears with one hand while giving her a small smile

    Lyuri then breaks off the hug and starts wiping out her tears herself, she still seemed rather sad, but she manages to give Duke a small, regretful smile

    "Duke... Do you really forgive me for everything that happened?"

    As long as you dont try pulling off that kind of stunt again, I guess I can give you a chance to atone. I know you did everything for me, but dont go overboard, I dont want to see you hurt either. he answered, he pauses for a little while before letting out a sigh ... Im sorry, while I was a Shadow, I probably mustve scared you. In fact, just thinking about what I did is enough to make me feel uneasy"

    "... You were very scary as a Shadow Pokémon, no doubt about it, but the thing I was most afraid of was being unable to make you come back to your senses. But I dont have to worry about that anymore." said Lyuri, letting out a genuine smile now.

    Duke gives a small smile, though he seemed rather troubled now "Im sorry, if I hadnt let myself be captured, none of this would-"

    He is then cut off by Lyuri suddenly hugging him again, very tightly.

    "I dont care. Everyone makes mistakes and yours cant even be considered one, unlike what I did. she then pauses for an instant "... Can I ask for a favor?"

    "Sure. Anything." said Duke, hugging back again and smiling

    "... Please dont leave me again."

    "... Just as long as you dont leave me either. answered Duke

    Meanwhile, Sakura was just watching their reunion with a big smile on her face that didnt seem like it would fade anytime soon.

    "After all the problems weve been having recently, this certainly is making me cheer up a bit" thought Sakura, breathing a sigh of relief.
