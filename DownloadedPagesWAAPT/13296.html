    Valorie rubbed at her eyes trying to get the sand out of them, some involuntary tears spilling out. She accepted the go goggles with gratitude. "T-Thanks..."

    Moving back towards the center of the storm she drew a pokeball and threw it, fully expecting to be attacked again. Dragonite emerged from the ball, nearly tall enough to be over the storm but not quite. The gigantic dragon shook her head, experiencing the same problem Valorie had had with the sand. She couldn't see! Another sonic boom was heard, though Dragonite appeared utterly unfazed compared to the sand that blinded her. Valorie formed a plan in her head. Sure, she could have Dragonite throw herself into the storm half blind and hope for the best, but maybe there was a better answer!

    "Dragonite, use Hurricane!" she called out over the roaring sand, coughing as some of it got into her mouth. How unpleasant was this storm?? Dragonite's wings seemed to grow multiple times their idle size, expanding and filling with a supernatural gale strength. Valorie stepped back to give Dragonite some room as she flapped her wings, launching powerful winds well exceeding a hundred miles an hour directly into the storm. The effect was immediate, overpowering the sandstorm and simply blowing it away!

    In the storm's wake was none other than Sonic! the very pokemon they'd been searching for. A flygon struggled to fly in the winds presented against it, it's wings being forced into uncomfortable positions by the powerful winds. It attempted to land, only to find that the wind easily yanked it right off the ground and sent it crashing several meters away as Dragonite let up.

    Valorie was ready. She tossed a second ball and Mamoswine emerged. "Ice Shard!" Valorie called out, Mamoswine's tusks becoming enveloped in a sub-zero frost. With a powerful twist of his head he whipped the icy spears at the heavily disorientated flygon, scoring a direct hit before it even had a chance to react. A wailing noise was heard, and Valorie immediately knew the battle was over. Cringing slightly, she would rush forward as a half-hearted flamethrower attack startled her pokemon by whizzing by.

    Closing in on the fallen pokemon Valorie came upon a painful looking sight. One of the icy tusks Mamoswine had launched had impaled one of Flygon's wings against the ground. Upon getting closer she came to the startling realization that Flygon was... enormous! It loooked like it must've been at least seven feet tall standing upright, not even counting it's tail. That made it Valorie's second largest pokemon. More importantly though, it was in -pain!-.

    Valorie got dangerously close and dropped to her knees as she began quickly thinking up a plan; thankfully the injured pokemon did not attack her for reasons unclear.

    Chiptune DJ
    Valorie sat on her legs, quickly switching out Dragonite and Mamoswine for Charizard. She needed to get that ice shard out, and the easiest way seemed like melting it. She second guessed herself however in favor of something less stupid. She didn't want to burn Flygon! With Flygon's insect-like body, she assumed it was probably easy to.

    Instead she ordered Charizard to crush the hollow spear, after which she got up and bent over to grasp what remained, using her handy dandy magical ability called thumbs to grasp and pull the tip out after several moments of physical struggle due to her rather pathetic strength.

    Falling on her rear she quickly shoved the ice-tusk tip away and got back up, brushing her skirt off. She then crouched down next to the fallen flygon, fishing in her bag for a potion.

    "I'll have you feeling better in just a moment, Flygon..." she said, tilting forward onto her hands and knees to hover over the wound and spray it. Flygon emitted a rather shrill cry at the stinging sensation that followed, but the medicine worked fast. Soon the wound began closing rapidly. Valorie continued spraying until it was difficult to tell there had ever been a wound to begin with. Flygon began to move and Valorie quickly stood back up as the insect dragon rose to her feet.

    For a moment the two looked at eachother, Flygon towering over Valorie, before Flygon slowly crouched down and opened her mouth. She dragged a big slobbery tongue over Valorie, covering the tiny girl with drool. Valorie was less than thrilled.

    In spite of this fact she restrained the urge to comment, moving forward and awkwardly produced a pokeball, priming it and holding it out towards Flygon. "I want you to be mine!" she explained awkwardly. It was suddenly very apparent that Valorie barely had any idea what she was doing any had never attempted to catch a Pokemon this way before.

    Flygon just sort of looked at her for a moment in a confused fashion. Had Valorie just lost her spine? What was happening?? Valorie subtly blushed, not much used to being lowered to asking permission. "...And I want you to choose that for yourself." she added. This time Flygon seemed to grasp what the problem was. She once again leaned down and totally began licking at Valorie's face in an affectionate manner. The shortstop sputtered as she was slobberified again and promptly pressed the ball to Flygon to get her to stop. To her relief it settled, signaling that Flygon had been caught.
    I dropped out of school to become a pokemon master, but then they pulled me right back in to get my doctorate!
