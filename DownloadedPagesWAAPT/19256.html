    Resort Area

    Elise is asleep in her family's villa, though she seems to be having a fitful rest.

    Elise's dream

    Esprit stands around a rocky cliffside, looking for something. He picks up a black rock that seems out of place with everything else, and it begins to melt into his hand, eventually spreading up his arm.

    Elise backs away from the sight, unsure what's going on, pixelated bolts of black electricity coming from him don't help things. When everything seems over, Esprit turns to look at her. The left half of his face is haphazardly shaded black, white, and seemingly every color in between.

    "...W-What happened to your face?" Elise murmurs fearfully, backing away faster.

    Esprit makes no sound, instead lunging for Elise, grabbing her arm, the effect spread to her. She scrambles to throw him off or get away, but is held firmly in place by his grasp. All she can do is scream out, at least until she too is muted.

    Resort Area

    Elise jerks awake, frantically scanning the room and her body, breathing a sigh of relief.

    "...That....that was something I guess. "

    She tries to lie back down, but the images she dreamt stay on her mind for some time.
