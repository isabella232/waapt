    Lake — <Hey, it won't be that bad. Besides, you know Bubble Shield now. And that's really helped, no?>

    Phyco — <Well, yes. At least now I don't have to worry about my bubbles popping if somemon so much as breathes on them...>

    The Kingdra swims forward and pokes his bubble with his snout, the watery sphere holding firm.

    <In fact, I haven't had to replace this one since I blew it a few days ago. Talk about durable.>

    Lake — <Well, they're not unbreakable, but they will take a lot of punishment.>

    Phyco — <Yes, now anyway...please tell me you have at least some idea of what Pokémon you want to catch?>

    Luke nods.

    "I do. Which is why we're heading this way. There's a cave up ahead with that's home to a species of Pokémon I'm interested in. If we're lucky, we should have one on the team by the end of the day."

    Phyco — <Well, what a relief! We should last a full ten seconds longer against Suicune, then!>

    Phyco — <Gee, you think?>
