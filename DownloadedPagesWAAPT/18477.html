    Goldenrod City, airport

    -Yale Zelner is sitting on a bench waiting for his luggage. He turns on his phone and a message he missed comes in.-

    To: Zelner

    From: Professor Motte

    Let me know when you're back in Johto. Don't want us to all be finished eating before you get here.

    -Zelner smiles at the thought.-

    To: Professor Motte

    From: Zelner

    Go ahead and order, I'm in Goldenrod now.

    -He looks it over, frowns, and adds another line.-

    My flight was delayed, sorry I didn't say anything before taking off.

    -He sees his bag on the conveyor belt and picks it up. He's rolling it out of the building when he gets another message.-

    To: Zelner

    From: Professor Motte

    Okay.

    Alvin wanted me to add that he doesn't accept your apology.

    -Zelner chuckles and resumes walking.-

    ???: Hold up.

    -Zelner turns around and sees a young woman in a police uniform heading his way.-

    Zelner: Can I help you, ma'am?

    ???: You're Yale Zelner, yes?

    Zelner: Yes, that's right.

    -She holds out her badge.-

    Yanowitz: Officer Mariana Yanowitz. I just have a few questions to ask regarding someone you may have encountered in Fall City.

    -She notices he's tense.-

    Yanowitz: Don't worry, you're not in any sort of trouble.

    -She points to a nearby bench.-

    Yanowitz: Do you mind?

    Zelner: No, not at all.

    -They sit down, and she pulls out a photograph of a familiar-looking blue-haired, red-eyed figure.-

    Yanowitz: Does he look familiar?

    -He takes the picture and looks at it.-

    Zelner: Yes. I met him, without a doubt.

    -He hands the picture back. Yanowitz puts it away.-

    Yanowitz: He spoke to you? Do you remember what he said?

    Zelner: Yeah... Let's see... He introduced himself, then told me he was going to die soon. He didn't explain, thanked me, and left.

    Yanowitz: You remember it clearly?

    -Zelner nods.-

    Zelner: Of course. It was a strange conversation. It stood out. Is he... did he...

    -Yanowitz shakes her head.-

    Yanowitz: Never mind that. You said he introduced himself. Do you remember the name he gave you?

    -Zelner thinks about it.-

    Zelner: Silvio Salvai. I'm almost certain that was it.

    -Yanowitz looks visibly angry at the name for a split second, but she regains her composure in the blink of an eye. She stands up and shakes Zelner's hand.-

    Yanowitz: Thank you. That was all I needed to know.

    -She nods.-

    Yanowitz: Enjoy the rest of your evening.

    -She walks away. Zelner dwells on the conversation for a moment, then pushes those thoughts aside, picks up his bag, and exits the airport.-

    Shoal Cave Lab, back room

    -Cara enters the room where Glavo's cage is kept and runs toward the cage.-

    Cara: <Hello again!>

    -She splits her meal as she has every day the past week and gives half to Glavo.-

    Cara: <Here you go!>

    -They eat.-

    Cara: <You're starting to look much better than you did.>

    Glavo: <I'm lucky Ansien hasn't caught on to that... If he did... Well...>

    -She looks at Cara.-

    Glavo: <You don't look as well as you did. Are you sure you're—>

    Cara: <I'm fine! I ate less than this all the time before I found this group. I'm used to it.>

    -Glavo tilts her head.-

    Cara: <It's not something I'm eager to discuss...>

    Glavo: <O-of course not, I'm sorry.>

    -Cara smiles.-

    Cara: <It's alright!>

    -She finishes her meal and stands up.-

    Cara: <Y'know, you're really nice for an immoral monster!>

    -Glavo blinks.-

    Glavo: <I take it you asked Ansien about me again?>

    Cara: <Yep!>

    -She tilts her head.-

    Cara: <He said your name's Zwerda, though, and you said it's Glavo... Maybe he just thinks lying is funny!>

    Glavo: <Or maybe he has the wrong Sneasel...>

    Cara: <Oh no, that's terrible!>

    -She looks around the room and notices a button.-

    Cara: <Maybe this'll free you...?>

    Glavo: <N-no! That's—>

    -Cara presses it, and the cage electrocutes Glavo. She screams.-

    Cara: <Eee! I'm sorry! I'm sorry! I'm sorry! Sorry, sorry!>

    -She curls up.-

    Cara: <I don't know how to stop it, I'm sorry, I'm sorry, I'm sorry, I'm so sorry!>

    -Glavo tries to choke out "Hit it again", but it comes out unintelligible. Her screaming continues as Cara continues to apologize. Another Sneasel enters the room and presses the button.-

    Cara: <I'm sorry, I'm sor— Huh?>

    -Cara looks up.-

    Cara: <Ansien! I—>

    Ansien: <We'll talk later.>

    -Cara looks downtrodden and runs out of the room. The moment she's out of his sight he presses the button again and steps toward the cage.-

    Ansien: <Have you made a friend, Zwerda? That's wonderful.>

    -As Glavo's screams continue, Ansien's face lights up with triumphant glee. Unlike many of Ansien's expressions, it would very clear to any onlooker that this is genuine.-
