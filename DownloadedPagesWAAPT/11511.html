    "The Purple Sword grants the wielder victory in battle, and endless courage, as well as the ability to hide in one's own shadow and reappear in another's shadow. However, it makes the user reckless and mono/megalomaniacal.

It also grants the user a sense of Moral Righteousness, in the sense that they will engage in Honor Over Reason because it would offend their morality otherwise. This causes them to become indecisive, or worse, decisive in ways that can't be possibly foreseen."
