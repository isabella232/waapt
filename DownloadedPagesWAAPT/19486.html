    CU - Forest

    -the group forge on ahead, making their way through the branches and thickets and bushes of the forest. It's quiet, actually-not an enemy or a poppy to be seen. At least, of course, until they happen upon a clearing, the faintest ring of evening sun showing...-

    -nothing-

    Joseph: ...you were singing just a minute ago...! You must have gone s-somewhere, you can't try and f-fool us...!

    -he's trying to sound Genre Savvy, but he mostly sounds very scared. After a moment, there's a rustle in the bushes, and a white-haired head pops out of one-

    Shadow!Joseph: Fine, if you really want to be that way...

    -this is identical in every way to the Joe that everyone knows and loves/tolerates/pretends to like but secretly loathes, except in one regard-

    Joseph: ...new contact lenses, I see.

    -a cautious attempt at a joke that the Shadow gamely giggles at-

    Shadow!Joseph: No, silly, these are just our natural eyes!

    Joseph: Our natural eyes?

    -he steps out of the bushes into the clearing, taking the lead before the group-

    Joseph: You say this like we're the same person.

    -Shadow!Joseph smiles, sitting down on a tree-stump, looking up at Joseph-

    Shadow!Joseph: Well, aren't we?

    -Joseph frowns, shaking his head-

    Joseph: Noooo, we're not. For one thing-hey, pay attention when I'm talking!

    -Shadow!Joseph was busy picking blades of grass. He looks up, with wide, childlike eyes-

    Joseph: You're just me if I was a man child. You know, innocent singing flower child me.

    -pause-

    Joseph: Actually, no, because if you were innocent, you wouldn't have used those damn flowers!

    -as he speaks, he gets more and more angry until he shouts the word "flowers". This makes Shadow!Joseph yelp and dive back towards the bush, staring up at Joseph like a cornered baby Lillipup-

    Joseph: ...I mean, you're just a kid. You're me, without any maturity-

    -Shadow!Joseph cocks his head-

    Shadow!Joseph: What maturity?

    Joseph: Oh, for-

    -he sighs, irritated again-

    Joseph: Maturity means that you're, y'know, grown up. Capable of handling things without acting like a little idiot. Understanding the world, adapting to fit it while still remaining...true to yourself. That's what maturity is.

    -Shadow!Joseph stares for a moment, before breaking out into cackling laughter. He flomps onto the ground, looking up at Joseph with that oddly vacant expression of mischief children have-

    Shadow!Joseph: It's funny when you grown-ups run your mouths!

    -he sits up-

    Shadow!Joseph: I didn't ask what maturity was. I was asking if you had any of it for your own!

    -Joseph's about to answer, but his Shadow plows on ahead-

    Shadow!Joseph: You think I'm acting any different from you? You, the weird little pixie with a head full of stardust and color? You, the tallest toddler in the world? You, the nutcase the rest of the team pretends to tolerate?

    -Joseph blinks, before growling under his breath. Shadow!Joseph continues, with the kiddish glee of a young boy tattling on his sister-

    Shadow!Joseph: Y'know why you keep acting like a stoned six year old, Joe? Because you're afraid of the passage of time. You're afraid of having to grow old and boring and normal, like everyone else.

    Joseph: Stop that.

    Shadow!Joseph: Because that's what you are! Normal! You can speak in gold and pick flowers and squeal like a kid all you want, but you're just like everyone else!

    Joseph: Stop that.

    Shadow!Joseph: And a normal person dies a normal death, and goes into a normal grave, and turns into normal, boring old dust!

    Joseph: STOP!

    -he doesn't say it with demanding anger, but as a scared boy pleading for torment to end-

    Joseph: You're not me. You're the parts I want to keep, poisoned. You're not me!

    -he speaks with desperation, and that only earns a cackle from Shadow!Joseph-

    Shadow!Joseph: Heeheeheeheehee~ You really are silly, you know that?

    -he smiles, his golden eyes narrow and cruel-

    Shadow!Joseph: That's OK...I like that.

    -a wind starts to kick up, leaves and petals gathering and blowing before Joseph, blocking the Shadow from view as it continues to talk-

    Shadow!Joseph: You want to be happy, don't you, Joseph? You want to be happy and free forever...

    -the wind kicks up to a gale, the petals and dead leaves forming a wall from view before it fades, showing its true form-

    -an unnaturally tall and thin Gardevoir, it doesn't look as though it should be upright. It looks like an old statue about to crumble at any moment, overgrown with vines and spores and flowers and leaves with no discernible pattern-

    Berserk!Joseph: ~Our verdant beauty shall become ETERNAL!~

    Battle! Berserk Joseph!
