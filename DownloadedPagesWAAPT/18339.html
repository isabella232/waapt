    Inside, Pyroar and Scolipeetle are curled up together at the foot of Levi's bed. Levi himself staring dreamily up at the ceiling, his head off somewhere in the clouds. Santiago is not far from Levi, sitting on a chair with on leg crossed over the other, reading a newspaper.

    Levi: *...Sigh~♥*

    Santiago: Heh...

    Levi turns his head Sandy with a scowl on his face.

    Levi: Hey! What's so funny.

    Santiago: Oh, nothing. Just you hombre~

    Levi completely turns over.

    Levi: You wanna fight me b-

    Santiago: Oh don't be tough with me, squirt, your heart's just not in it today. In fact it's somewhere else entirely~

    Levi: What are you try'n ta say here, boyo?

    Santiago: Simply put. You, amigo, are completely smitten.

    Levi turns over to reply, pauses, and then turns back over mumbling under his breath.

    Santiago: Levi, you wear your heart on your sleeve. We all know about it.

    Santiago turns a page.

    Santiago: Even if you're playing a dangerous game.

    Levi turns his head slightly and cocks an eyebrow.

    Levi: What?

    Santiago: Well, putting aside that despite the fact your physically 21 and mentally 15...

    Levi: Oi!

    Santiago: -You are probably decades older than the kid in question. Doesn't the though ever cross your mind that he will age, and you are ageless?

    Levi: What are yo-

    Santiago: Levi, we're immortal. Are you sure you can handle the mental stress of everyone who you love grows old while we stay young? Every mortal friend, family and lover you have dying?

    Levi: What about Vyvyan and Si-

    Santiago: You don't think Vyvyan racks his head over that every day? He's had three years to come to terms with the fact and he's still not over it.

    There is a silence.

    Levi: So that's why you froze and didn't contact them.

    Santiago: !!!

    Levi: Your giving me a lecture about responsibility when you can't even tell your own family that you are alive?

    Santiago: They're...

    They're better off thinking I've passed than having to deal with the emotional turmoil of me being alive and immortal.

    Levi: I see that as cowards way. If I found out Ihad a family, the first thing I would do! You're squandering life here Sandy!

    Santiago: We can't act treat life like mortals anymore, Levi.

    Levi: But we can't just observe it like one would a work of art either.
