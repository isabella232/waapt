    Blackthorn City, Pokemon Center

    The folks at the Pokemon center knew their stuff. They certainly had plenty of experience from all the years they'd spent doing what they did. Which made it no surprise that they had little trouble fixing up Jack's pokemon - Penny especially - back to tip-top shape.

    Jack: "Time to..."

    Whatever Jack was about to say, it was interrupted by a message.

    Jack: ?

    Jack: "Looks like it's from Michael."

    Or rather, it was a proxy message, one that looked like it had been sent to him due to his previous contact with the man.

    We haven't met up in ages, and there's important things that need talking about, so I'm asking that we all head over to [Shrodingers Port City in Kanjoh] ASAP. I'll explain more once everyone has gotten there. If you're wondering how you're even going to get there, don't worry, I have...connections.

    You're most likely wondering "Why me? Who the hell are you and how did you get this number? I'm not part of the J-Team!" While technically that's kinda true, you've likely been accosted by members of our batshit insane fanbase, and it's also quite likely that our enemies will target you as well due to your association via the show or otherwise. Since obviously I find the concept of your blood on my hands to be about as pleasant as getting Dark Pulsed in the back a second time, it would be in your best interests to make your way over.

    P.S. Bring Honey :3

    Jack: "Woah, J-Team? Michael is affiliated with the J-Team?!"

    That was big - clearly, Jack had stumbled upon something far bigger than he would have ever suspected. Going back to the ruins would have to wait - he was certain they'd still be there and waiting for him when he wanted to return. But he suspected that he wasn't an intended recipient of the message he'd just gotten, and didn't want to let an opportunity like this slip away.

    [Shrodingers Port City in Kanjoh]

    One trip for honey later, Jack and Runt appeared in [Shrodingers City], a large container of honey being held in his arms. If he wasn't supposed to be here, he felt it best not to take chances and bring a sizable peace offering with him.
