    134724 Crewe4th Feb 2012 06:55:00 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
    Li'l Punk

    Northern Sinnoh

    Three Sneasels trudge along a large rock wall, bent over against the wind and hail. Martin, for once, takes the lead, a pale pink Reflect screen shielding the three from the worst of the storm. After a little while, they find a shallow cave beneath an overhang and, after checking for inhabitants, settle down for a brief rest. With Martin straining to keep his Reflect over the entrance, it quickly becomes almost comfortable compared to the outside. Morana passes around the Leftovers and they make a small meal of the regenerating apple core. Martin lets down his screen to recover his strength, but as he's preparing to set it back up so they can move out, a large shadow appears looming over the entrance.

    Martin shrinks back, while Biton leaps to his feet and extends his claws, and Morana prepares to make a hasty retreat. After a moment, a disapproving voice booms through the cave.

    <You children! Back on my mountain! Why are you here?>

    The three freeze for a moment, then Biton relaxes a takes a step back, tilting his head.

    <Miss Stella?>

    With a grunt, a female Abomasnow stoops and enters the cave, taking up most of the empty space.

    <I brought you down from the mountain,> she says, glowering at the three Sneasel. <Don't make me do it again.>

    <Oh, don't worry, Miss Stella!> Morana assures her. She offers the Abomasnow the Leftovers, which is refused. <We won't get into trouble this time. You won't have to save us.>

    Stella snorts. <Words. Child, you will get into trouble. And then I will have to save you again.> She grabs Biton by the scruff and lifts him from his feet, shaking him gently. He protests, swatting at her hand, and she drops her. <This one, he is trouble.>

    <I am not!> Biton says indignantly.

    <Are,> Stella says, sounding almost amused. <Now back down the mountain, children.>

    <But, Miss Stella...> Biton begins, flinching slightly at the hard look he gets from the Abomasnow, but continuing anyway, <We're running out of places to look!>

    Stella hesitates for a moment, then sighs. She places a hand on Biton's arm, giving him a level stare.

    <Child,> she says gruffly, <Sister is dead.>

    Biton pulls him arm out of her grip, and she lets him. He shakes his head urgently, clenching his fists.

    <No. She's not dead. C'mon, guys, we're going up the mountain to look.>

    Morana nods and motions for him to take the lead. Martin pats Stella on the arm and also follows, erecting another Reflect screen.

    <Good bye, Miss Stella,> he says absent-mindedly. <Please ask Timmy not to eat us...>

    The Abomasnow sighs as the three set off again, then lumbers out of the cave and follows them at a distance.

    <Stupid children,> she mutters under her breath. <Have to save again, bring back down mountain...>
    Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
