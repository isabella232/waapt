    Over the Horizon Hotel, Petalburg

    Ray groaned as she lay flat on her back, the soft material of the bed nursing her aching muscles."Well, looks like I'm going to be in for some fun later on, aren't I.." She looked to the side of her bed, where her Prowler gear lay, and began to remember the events of before..

    Petalburg Streets, last night

    With one final tug, Ray placed her helmet on in front of the shop mirror, and Prowler grinned back at her from the mirror's reflection. She slid her batons in the leg holsters, and hopped on to her bike, whistling to herself as she revved up her bike, riding off on her patrol."Well, time for patrol."

    Petalburg Streets, last night

    Catherine groaned as she stepped out, a white harlequin mask on her face, looking at a museum in front of them."Well, I was told to get all of the Mega Stones that I could..but man, I hate doing this. Gaah..I'll explain this to the HQ later."

    Slowly but surely, clad in an all black combat gear that she had found on her desk, she slinked towards what seemed to be a rock museum, slowly and surely. The security guard, big, bulky and mean looking, was prowling around the main door, so she sighed and went to the side of the building, where she could spot a glass case, fitted out with Houndoom prowling around it. Digging around her belts, she brought out two Pokeballs, and as she did so, she admired the way the tiny Illusion "I" on her cuffs caught the moonlight."Surprising how Wesker's into fashion. Ah well, Kalosians seem to be pretty good at this.."

    Opening her Pokeballs, she lets out Verona the Mismagius, and Shifty the Parasect. Verona smirked at her."<Robbing a bank, are we? You're finally meshing in with Illusion, fair child.>" Her voice echoed in Catherine's mind, causing her to shake her head in distress."Yeah well, gonna have to, aren't I?" She looked down at the snoozing Shifty, and frowned."Okay, so Verona, could'ya please phase us through, and wake Shifty here for a bit so that he can use some Sleep Powder?"

    Verona nodded, and phased them through, however, as she did, she didn't realize that at they moment, the watchful eye of Prowler, watching them from across the street.

    Petalburg Streets, Five Minutes later

    Catherine phased out of the museum, a satchel containing the Megastone slung around her hip, sighing in relief."Finally, I can get back to the base with some things to show for it, hehe.." She then hears something, a revving of a motor, and her head shot up, witnessing Prowler racing towards her on a rapidly closer and closer motorcycle."Ah, crap."

    Scrambling, Catherine rushed forward, running as fast as she could, before looking back and realizing that there was no way she could make it back to the hotel on foot. Thus, panickingly grabbing at her Pokeball, she sent out Bradley the Golem, who rolled out towards Prowler, while she kept her head straight ahead, not daring to look behind her.

    Gritting her teeth under her helmet, Ray sent out Balthazar, who Double Kicked Bradley off to the side, over which Prowler rode over, hopping off of her bike to rush at Catherine, her batons out at the ready.

    Doing her best not to groan as her back was slammed by Prowler, flinging a fist wildly behind her. Upon hearing a sharp cry of pain, she pushed Prowler off of her, scrambling up the building in front of her, using every window sill and door handle as hand and foot holds as best as she could.

    Growling, Prowler rushed up the ladder next to the building after her, only to find no one there at the top of the building..as if the thief had vanished in thin air. Frustrated, she kicked at the air in anger, and slowly climbed down."Not letting you go this easy, Clown Thief. I'm gonna find you, believe me."
