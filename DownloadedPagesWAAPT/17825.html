    Robin: ...OK, now I know there's something wrong.

    -Nico sighs-

    Nico: It's just... I'm the one who wanted to be Gonzales. According to Arika whoever got it would be the hero of this story.

    -He frowns-

    But it's not like I can be a hero. Ever.

    Robin: Why?

    -Nico sighs again-

    Nico: When I was a kid... I was treated like a freak. People locked me in a haunted house because of my powers.

    So when I found out about the J-Team... I was amazed. They were strange, unusual people like me, but they were awesome for it! So I sought them out and joined them, because I wanted to be awesome too!

    ...But ever since then I've discovered something worse than being treated like a freak.

    Robin: And what is that?

    Nico: I've always been ignored, on the sidelines. Everyone else gets to do all the cool stuff while I'm off in my own little corner that Maggie and Team Umbra sometimes visits. And... I feel overshadowed.

    I mean, the J-Team has immortals, super-scientists, genderbenders, were-mons and such, pendant-girls and...

    -gestures to Robin-

    A living video game character. Me? I'm just a normal teenager with some psychic powers he can't get the hang of.

    To the J-Team or its fandom I'm nothing special.

    Robin: You're better off than me. I tried to save the world, say fate didn't matter... And I failed, where another me succeeded.

    Nico: You're pretty well-off here, though. Heck, you've got built-in recognition already.

    Robin: Look, just... Try to do what's best for you. I'm sure you'll find something.

    -He hugs. Nico hugs back-

    I'm going to bed. Take care, OK?

    -he heads off-

    -Nico looks back up at the stars-

    Nico: I just wanna be a hero. I just wanna be somebody. Is that so much to ask?

    -Silence-

    -Nico sighs and heads to bed-

    Alone in a world without true light...
