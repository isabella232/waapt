    Rowan: <Not mentally.>

    “.. True. Well, guess I’ll be going soon.”

    Rowan: -Giving the Ninetales equivalent of a small smile- <I’ll see you later, then.>

    “Yeah.”

    “Hey, guys-”

    The mons in Daydre’s party not mentioned previously turn their attention away from whatever they were doing and focus on her. Anna is oddly absent..

    “You’re… All okay with me going on another gym challenge without you?”

       The Punisher:       <Well, it would be nice if I could spread terror.. But even one such as I would enjoy a break..>   

    Percy: <I can’t speak for the others, but I’m fine with it. Do it if ya want.>

    ‘’’Azami:’’’ <Yeah, same here. Whatever floats your boat. Don’t worry about it so much.>

    “Are you absolutely-”

    Daydre feels a tug at her leg. It’s Mr. Bitey!

    “... You want to go, huh?...”

    He nods.

    “.. Yeah, I should stop dawdling.”

    Daydre finishes packing her stuff. With a final wave to her team, she leaves her house for the first time in, like a month.

    Unbeknownst to her, though, a Volcarona-y shadow follows her on her way...
