    Mirage Forest

    Nathan had decided to walk his own road for a while, heading to Mirage Forest to train his companions.

    It didn't take long before they were ambushed by a Ponyta, challenging him, or more precisely, his pokemon, to fight it.

    Nathan: "Sweet, a Ponyta! Harvey, You're up, show your talons!"

    Harvey nodded and swooped toward the Ponyta, but the pony was surprisingly agile and dodged the initial pecking attempts, doing it's best to kick at Harvey before connecting a fierce kick with the Pidgey's body, sending him flying and falling to the ground, seemigly out for the count.

    Nathan: "Are... Are you okay?" Nathan watched Harvey, dusty and ruffled, stumble up on it's two feet.

    Harvey: <Let's go again, blondie.> He taunted, quickly shaking the dut off his wings and steadily hopping about to raise his Agility.

    The Ponyta used this opportunity to charge at the bird and sent a vicious kick at him, only to miss because he was behind him, sending a Gust of wind to tear on the Ponyta and irritate it.

    Harvey: <Yes, throw your cool away and risk your aim, the thing that you most need against my stature!>

    Out of the blue the Ponyta spat out several Embers at airborne Harvey. To counter this, he flew sky high and charged at the Ponyta in a divebomb-like maneuver, very narrowly avoiding several shots of the rapid fire. Upon getting close enough, Harvey blew a Gust at the Ponyta again, visibly affecting it by now.

    Harvey: <I am the hot shot here! Me! I'll finish you off!>

    Sophia: <Stop.>

    Harvey: <Why should I stop?>

    At that moment he found out as the two witnessed a pokeball fly at the Ponyta, impacting with it and doing it's thing once again. After a nervous wait for the end of the ball wobbling, the mechanism clicked and the pokemon was caught.

    Nathan: "Hahahahaha, yeah! We got ourselves a potential future mount! Oh, and a companion, of course. A companion with potential practical benefits."

    Harvey flew on top of Nate's head and started pecking him in frustration.

    Harvey: <He was my opponent, nobody interrupts duels like that!>

    Nathan: <Ow ow ow ow ow! Stop! Stop that, you're tearing my hair out!>

    Harvey didn't stop immediately, but he halted upon feeling tingly, hopping off Nate's head and to the ground, making indeterminate, rapidly shifting noises as he began glowing. His normal tone of voice now transformed from an average bird to a more hawk like baritone, and in a flash of light, the transformation was complete. Harvey Evolved!

    Nathan: "You evolved! You're a lot bigger too, size of a hawk or something, could lift Sophia here off her feet. Maybe your dreams of flight may still be realized, huh?" Nathan nudged at Sophia teasingly.

    Sophia: <I'm not going to even respond to that. Anyway, You feeling werll, Harvey?>

    Harvey: <She had no choice...> Harvey spoke in an eerie, unnerved tone.

    Sophia: <Huh?>

    Harvey: <She had to do it to ensure our survival, she had to release the hounds at both sides... Nobody won, yet nobody lost. Yet. One day this will be dealt with.>

    Sophia: <I don't understand...>

    Harvey: <This is joyous day, my lady! I have become harder, better, faster, stronger, more mating ritually attractive!>

    Sophia: <That soudnds wonderful... I think.>

    Nathan: "Let's go celebrate this new capture! the food is on me!"

    And with that, the group heads toward civilization.
