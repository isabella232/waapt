    - Axel, now a Houndoom , walks up to the Gengar 

    Axel: You wanted me for something, Xemnas?

    Xemnas: Youve met our newest member, right, Axel?

    Xemnas: I want you to be his... mentor.

    Axel: Oh, come on! I'm still mad that you made me care for that lazy good-for nothing Corphish! I can't believe that slob managed to evolve...

    Xemnas: I feel this one will be... different.

    Axel: And that's how you open chests with your paws. Any questions?

    Axel: ...

    Roxas: ...I could do that blindfolded.

    Axel: -Grins- Woah! Kid's got a snarky streak. I like that.

    A few days later

    - Axel and Roxas are slurping Sea Salt vanilla ice cream out of bowls. Suddenly, Roxas senses something. He looks up and notices a Porygon approaching. -

    Roxas: Hello!

    Porygon: - in a feminine voice- Oh! Um... hi?

    Axel: Are you one of the new guys?

    Porygon: Yes...

    Roxas: Would you like to sit and watch the sunset with us?

    - Xemnas has called his fellow Pokemon to a meeting -

    Xemnas: I have good news, everyone!

    Xemnas: We're leaving our pathetic excuse for a trainer!

    - There is much murmuring among the Pokemon; only Xion, now a Porygon-2, looks concerned. -

    A few days later

    - Axel and Xion are arguing-

    Axel: Wait, you're leaving?

    Xion: Look... I've found out things about Xemnas... He's planning horrible ideas... Manipulating us all...

    Xion: But that's exactly what he wants! and I'm not going to let him suceed!

    - She floats off-

    Axel: Go on! You just keep running! But I'll always be there to bring you back!

    - Axel is talking with a Roserade and a Luxray -

    Larxene the Luxray: ...So me and Marluxia were going to overthrow Xemnas. You in on it?

    Axel: Um... sure... Something weird - and familiar - is going on...

    A few days later

    - Axel is confronting Xemnas -

    Axel: OK, first Xion leaves... Then I get sucked into conspiracies... and now Roxas leaves? Something is going on here, and it all seems strangely familiar. I want to find out why.

    Xemnas: Don't you see, Axel? It's all part of the game.

    Axel: You're starting to sound like that Dunspar-

    - He realizes something-

    ...You're emulating that game our old trainer loved so much, aren't you?!

    Xemnas: Indeed.

    - Axel snarls-

    Axel: You deserted our Trainer for a life of petty crime... you scared off some of my friends... you're turning our group against each other... all for the sake of some stupid GAME?!

    Xemnas: Ah, Axel. Always so rebellious...

    Axel: How's this for a rebellion?!

    Xemnas: Traitor! Traitor!
