    To Whom it May Concern in the Imperial Garrison at Almer:

    Hoenn's future is very much an open question at the moment. Parliament has remained as intransigent and unproductive as it was in my days as a mere Bagon. And now the very principalities that nominally swear allegiance to my throne are on the cusp of raising arms against each other and elevating their local rulers over that of my throne's rightful claim.

    I cannot pretend my ancestors have always been able to ensure unity amongst their subjects, indeed, there are periods of thousands of moons in the old world in which the counterparts of Hoenn's present principalities openly bore arms against each other.     What I ask you to do is not an errand for my throne... But rather an errand for the very heart and soul of Hoenn. Things in Hoenn cannot persist as they are without change. Do everything in your power to ensure that our portion of those islands is peaceable and fitting for a reception between monarchs.
