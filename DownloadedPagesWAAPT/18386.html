    A warm thanks to Umbra for composing this Odin based Spag

    Elsewhere in the Neo-Flare base, a fair distance away from Lex's Quarters

    The cold metal floors of one of the many hallways of the Neo-Flare base echo with footsteps, alongside the eager, giddy and positively maniacal childish giggles.

    Odin: Oh Constantin~ You won't belive what I've got~

    Constantin: Odin, your enthusiasm is earnest and well needed, but is it really strictly necessary for me to be wearing this blindfold?

    Maria: Really Odin. It's as if you forgot the boss -your own cousin I might add - is psychic and has Aura Sense. (nitwit)

    Constantin: ~Maria, do not try my patience~

    Maria: (Bitterly laced grumbles and mutters)

    Odin: -Sticking his tongue out at Maria- Oh don't worry dear cousin of mine~ Once you get a glimpse of what you're about to see I could see your heart bursting out of your chest into great bloody chunks~

    Constantin: -In a joking tone- You haven't forgotten that my heart is nothing but a stone husk, have you now?

    Odin: -Giggling- Of course not! But we're here now!

    Odin begins untying the blindfold.

    Odin: Constantin, I reveal to you THIS!

    Odin removes the blindfold, revealing a rather expansive, lavish room with strange machinery.

    At the center of the room is a strange machine with a smaller-than-normal Kyurem strapped in. On the Kyurem's head is a mind-control device similar to the one used on Ever.

    Rainbow confetti cannons also fire at that exact moment.

    Odin: -Leaping in front with jazz hands- Odin: TA-DAA! Pretty cool, huh?

    As soon as Constantin gets a glimpse of the admittedly smaller-than-the-average Kyurem, his eyes light up into great big stars◊...

    Before quickly melting away into a skull and crossbones with a fiery backdrop and matched by a rather sadistic grin.

    Constantin: Zut alors Odin! I knew I could trust you to perform beyond the call of duty, but this! THIS is over-excelling in your field!

    Constantin runs over towards the contained Kyure. He himself emitting a giggle that matches Odin's own high pitched and unnerving one. (It seems to be a family thing)

    Constantin: Well que c'est beau and oh la la! Quelle chance!

    Maria: Warum ist es kleiner?

    Odin: I knew you'd like it! :3

    Constantin: Like it? -Turning around with his hand clenched in excitement- I LOVE it!

    Maria: So, how do you keep the brute contained? Surely you would install a failsafe in case things go terribly wrong, and we wouldn't want that to happen now would we?

    One could pretty much hear the bile dripping from Maria's words. Odin's smile turns into a slight sneering one as he answers Maria's question.

    Odin: Relax! That Lex kid's great, he built destablizing barriers to keep her in line. Not to mention the Cipher brainwashing device I installed~

    Constantin: Thinking ahead, brilliant~ You keep impressing me today, cousin!

    Maria: (Pity.) Speaking of the little whelp, shouldn't your whipping boy be here right now?

    Constantin: Indeed, he should be here right now. Besides, I do wish to have a word with him on the project...

    Odin. Page him.

    Odin does so...

    Lex's Quarters

    Lex whines.

    Lex: Okay, okay. The jig is up here.

    The truth is I'm from the fu-

    Lex's mecha-arm starts buzzing and beeping.

    Lex: SHIT! I'M SUPPOSED TO BE IN THE CONTAINMENT FACILITY RIGHT NOW? OF ALL THE TIMES TO BE CALLED.

    Lex runs into his room, before running back out. Attempting to put on his labcoat in a hurry.

    Lex: Love to explain! But I have a place to be! You get free roam of the place, just don't go into my room, don't break my stuff and for the lov of all that is holy DO NOT LEAVE THESE PREMISES!

    I gotta zip! BYE

    Lex runs out of the lounge room in a hurry. The locking of a metal door echoing through the hall.

    Robonoid: -Beep-

    Back to the Admin group.

    Constantin turns his focus back to the contained Kyurem.

    Constantin: I can see this going very far... Yes.. very far indeed~

    Suddenly, the group of Flare admins are greeted by the panting of their youngest member of rank, dressed in his proper black Neo-Flare garb.

    Lex: -Leaning against the metal frame, panting- Hwaaah. Sorry... for being late... boss... Nyaaaargh....

    He trods over towards the group, breathing dramatically, before taking a place beside Odin.

    Lex: I had a bit of a... ahh... (Hmm) wardrobe malfunction (Among other things) earlier... but I'm fine now.

    Odin: -to Lex- (Ribs started to hurt?)

    Lex: (Yeah. I seriously don't understand how some people can go for 12 hours. Let alone 8.)

    While Maria leers and mutters with her arms folded; a grinning Constantin places his hands on Lex's shoulders, causing the smaller male to make an unceremonious yelp of surprise.

    Lex: WAH!

    Constantin: Lex, my dear boy! It's grand that you're here now! Tell me, you worked on the containment facility here, oui?

    Lex: Uuuh, oui?

    Constantin: Tell me, how far are you with Project Anantya?

    Lex: Well....

    Um....

    Lex looks over to Odin.

    Lex: You wanna tell him?

    Constantin gives a quite befuddled head tilt.

    Odin: Erm, well... -squeakyvoice- Not very far?

    Constantin: -Taken aback- Hwah?

    Lex: I mean, I'm assuming you think that this Kyurem would be a good substitute for one of the Kalosian Triumvirate?

    Constantin nods.

    Lex: I've been working on the generators and such, but we haven't done to much on the "legendary infinite energy extractor" side since this project took up our time and it's...

    Um...

    Not related to Project Anantya?

    Lex peers over to Odin with a look of both fear and "you owe me for this" while Maria makes a tch sound in the background.

    Maria: (Typisch) So you're both traitors to the badge and the project.

    Lex: -Flailing widly- NO NO NONONONONONONONONONONONONO NO!

    Constantin: Maria, dear. I'm pretty sure that Odin can... explain.

    The inflections on "explain" have enough edges to be knives

    Odin: Look, I'm sorry, I was too focused on this project to focus on the other one...

    Constantin: ...

    ... ...

    ... ... ...

    Constantin sighs as he rub his forehead.

    Constantin: Maria, Lex. Would you like to excuse us for a moment? We have family matters to discuss.

    Lex gives Odin a thumbs up with a "good luck" look while Maria sneers and harumphs as both exit the expansive room of science and captive, dangerous legendary Pokemon.

    Constantin: -Tapping his index fingers- So, Odin, explain. For what personal project of yours do you need this behemoth for? And how important was it for you to completely ignore your duties? I was excited that we got one of the biggest hurdles out of the way, but then I find you haven't even worked on the prototypes!

    Odin: But cousin! I need to use it to get the Lunar Pendant! D:

    Constantin: ...

    ...

    Odin, in what way does a Kyurem have to do with ancient fairytale jewelery?

    Odin: Well, there's this Arika girl, and...

    Constantin: So there is this girl you want.

    And you're going to woo her with mythical wish pendants.

    ...

    ... ...

    ...You're doing this to get a date ಠ_ಠ!?

    Odin: No, see, that Arika girl IS the pendant! I have proof!

    Constantin: Uh-huh. (He may be delirious, but be the good relative and amuse him) Fine. Show what you have.

    Odin pulls up all his video/DNA proof and research.

    Cons: Huh. Well colour me suprised. And the Kyurem is bait for this girl?

    ...

    Strange choice of bait.

    Odin: Apparently she's friends with the Kyurem's adoptive parents. Small world. -giggles-

    Constantin: Well, once you have the girl, we'll talk about what to do with the Kyurem.

    Odin: Okeydoke! Though ‎if I get the pendant we may not need it anymore...

    Constantin: Well, I trust your... strange intuition. Hopefully this goes well~

    Odin: -giggles-

    Hey! You're the Psychic one! Can't you tell my future? o3o

    Constantin: Funny thing about Psychics, we all have varying abilities. I have near to no natural talent or capabilities in the field of clairvoyance. So, I am useless if people get into trouble and I'm not immediately there.

    Odin: Duly noted. But I have it all under control~

    Constantin: -Placing a hand on his cousin's shoulder- I trust that you do.

    Odin: -smiles-

    Constantin yawns and stretches, the bones in his body cracking quite audibly.

    Constantin: While this plan is certainly... strange and off-course from our usual trappings. I still none-of-the-less have no doubts that you can find some clever or crafty way to make this oddball idea work. So for now, I'll leave you with your toys and your Legendary for I have paperwork to do. Adieu, dear cousin.

    As Constantin walks out of the room, the vixen-esque figure of one Maria von Boehm slinks into view from the shadows, folding her arms and snarling in Odin's directions.

    Maria: You know, one of these days dear old cousin Constantin isn't going to be around to protect you. And when that day comes, you better watch your back, because I'll be chewing on it rather than help you. Or your little lily-livered pet.

    Odin: -glares- We'll see about that.

    Maria: You know, if all goes horribly wrong and that beast of yours breaks free and tears you to shred. I wont shed any tears for you, in fact, I'll be silently laughing.

    The [Rhine] beauty turns around and begins walking off, picking her with a stiletto blade.

    Maria: You're just incredibly lucky that you're the Bosses favorite.

    And with her snide remarks out of the way, she leaves. Gritting her teeth

    Odin: Oh, go have your bitter fantasies somewhere else.

    When she leaves the room, he look back at his captive prize and rubs his hands giggling like a little kid in a candy store.

    Odin: That Arika girl is a good as MINE~!
