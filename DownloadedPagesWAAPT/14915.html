    Indigo League — Final Chamber

    -Lance is pacing back and forth, waiting for a challenger.-

    Ellen: I remember when I used to do that.

    -Lance jumps and spins toward the voice.-

    Lance: Aah! Who— ...Oh. Hello, Ivy.

    -He regains his composure.-

    Ellen: It's "Ellen" now, actually. Middle name. "Ivy"... It's kind of misleading, isn't it?

    Lance: It is kind of, yes. How did you get in here?

    Ellen: Let's just say I have my ways.

    Lance: ...Hm.

    Ellen: So! I heard my daughter — well, my son at the time — pretty much shut you up in the middle of one of your "Pokemon-Human bond" speeches.

    Lance: -raises an eyebrow- "Daughter"?

    Ellen: Trans. Point is, the sibling of the girl you gave that Gible to pretty much wiped the floor with you.

    Lance: Wait, how do you—

    Ellen: She caught me somewhat up-to-date.

    Lance: ...Er, that makes sense, yes. Anyway... yes. He did. It was your Metagross, though.

    Ellen: Mmm-hmm~ And he'll wipe the floor with you again in the next Gold Conference~

    Lance: -crosses his arms- That's not for a long time from now.

    Ellen: Your point? I could wait an eternity.

    Lance: And I won't be holding back in—

    Ellen: C'mon, I already gave the whole "hold back in the league" lecture a while ago.

    -Lance starts to say something, but hears footsteps coming down the hall.-

    Ellen: Oh, that's your cue. Good luck~

    -Lance glances at the hall, and looks back... at the space Ellen used to be.-

    Lance: ...Am I going mad or something?

    Meteor Falls

    -Wallace is with Steven in one of the tunnels within the cave.-

    Wallace: I still don't see how you can like this place.

    Steven: Oh, come on, it's a waterfall in a cave. Surely you have some appreciation for it?

    Wallace: ...We're in your personal self-dug cavern. Away from all the water.

    Steven: I gave you the option to stay out there.

    Ellen: Oh, am I interrupting something? Sorry about that.

    Steven: Oh, no, it's fine, we're just—

    -Beat-

    Steven: ...Um.

    Wallace: Hoo, boy, here we go with this again.

    -Ellen raises an eyebrow-

    Ellen: With what?

    -Steven clears his throat-

    Steven: Nothing. It's nice to see you again, Ivy—

    Wallace: Name change.

    Steven: Right, Ellen. It's nice to know the rumors aren't fake.

    Ellen: And it's good to see you again as well. I'm only visiting shortly, but perhaps we could catch up with each other another time?

    Steven: That would be nice.

    Ellen: Great! Well, I'll see you around, I guess.

    -She turns around and leaves.-

    Steven: ...She really is back.

    Wallace: Faaaaaaaanboy~

    Steven: No.

    -Creeeeeeeak.-

    Alder: I've been well. I decided to pass on my championship.

    Ellen: Oh? Who's the champion now?

    Ellen: And likewise. I wish I wasn't as out of the loop as I am, but I guess that can't be helped. Anyway, I guess I'll catch up later!

    Alder: See you around, Ellen!

    -Ellen once again leaves.-
