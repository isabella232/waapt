    Some time later...

    -Having had dinner, all of us have fallen asleep, tired out from the day's antics. Well...All of us except Pleo, that is. The young Lugia is still lying awake, too upset to sleep at that point.

    He couldn't bear it. Tomorrow, his friends were going to leave him. And then he would be alone again.

    Sure, the Reef Pokémon were always there. But Pleo felt that they were only being nice to him because he was the son of a god. Not because of who he was as a Pokémon. His Dad was the only one who truly cared about him.

    Dad...Why did he leave him? Did he not love him anymore? Was that why he hadn't come back yet? And was that why Lake and the others were going to leave? Harriet and Gene had said that it was the ocean that was making them uncomfortable, but Pleo couldn't help but wonder...Was it him, maybe?

    A few tears leaking from his eyes, Pleo covers hie head with his wings, crying to himself quietly.

    Not quietly enough to avoid waking Lake, though.-

    Lake: <Zzz...Any luck finding that Finneon yet, Nad- Huh?>

    -Lake glances around, easily spotting Pleo crying to himself.-

    Lake: <Pleo? What's the matter?>

    -The Lugia uncovers his head, gazing at Lake with teary eyes.-

    Pleo: -Sniffs.- <Lake...Dod you guys not like?>

    Lake: <What? Of course we do. Why would you think something like that?>

    Pleo: <...Then, why are you leaving, then?> -Sniffs.-

    Lake: <Oh...Pleo, come here.>

    -Lake beckons for Pleo to come over and sit next to him. The young Lugia does so, plopping himself down next to the Feraligatr and leaning his long neck and head against him.-

    Lake: <Pleo, we're not leaving because of you. We all like you.>

    Pleo: <But...my Dad like me too, and he...he...>

    -Pleo buries his head in Lake's scales.-

    Pleo: <He went away, Lake! And he still hasn't come back yet!> -Sniffs.- <And I can't help but think that it's because of...Me...>

    Nadia: <Zzz...Okay, I found the Finneon, Lake...Wait, Lake? Where are you? Uh, hang on Ponyta...>

    -Naida wakes herself up, wondering why Lake suddenly just dissapered from her their dream for some reason. As well as why a Ponyta was friends with a Finneon, of all things.-

    Nadia: <Lake? What's going-> -Notices Pleo.- <Oh!> -To Lake.- <What's the matter?>

    Lake: <Poor guy's really upset about us leaving.> -To Pleo.- <Hey, it's okay, little guy...>

    -Lake wraps his arms around Pleo and gives him a hug.-

    Lake: <I'm sure you're Dad had a good reason for leaving you. And I'm pretty certain it wasn't because he didn't like you. Honestly, I can't see how anymon wouldn't like you.>

    Nadia: <Yeah. You're a really nice Pokémon, Pleo. And I've really enjoyed spending time with you. All of us have.>

    Pleo: <Then...Why are you leaving, then?>

    Lake: <Well, lots of reasons. But...None of them have anything to do with you. We promise.>

    Pleo: <...You sure?>

    Lake: -Nods.- <Yep.>

    Nadia: -Also nods.- <We're sure.>

    Pleo: <Oh. But...I don't want you to leave. Then I'll be alone.> -Sniffs.- <I don't want to be alone...>

    Nadia: <Well, nobody wants to be alone. I think that's something all Pokémon have in common.>

    Lake: <Yeah, that's right. Besides, you won't be completely alone. You'll have the Reef Pokémon.>

    -Pleo shakes his head.-

    Pleo: <They don't like me. Not the way you guys. The Reef Pokémon worship me. But you guys...You're friends with me. And apart from my Dad...You're the first friends I've ever had.>

    Nadia: <Oh...>

    -Lake and Nadia couldn't believe it. They and the others had been the first Pokémon that seemed to actually care about him apart from his father, and they were just going to walk out and leave him alone by himself. Needless to say, they felt pretty bad.-

    Nadia: -To Lake.- <What can we do? I don't want to leave him alone.>

    Lake: -To Nadia.- <Me neither. We can't stay down here, though. Harriet can't bear it. Plus everyone else is probably wondering where we've gone by now.>

    -Lake pauses, thinking for a second.-

    Lake: <Hmm...We're going to be here in the Orange Islands for a while, aren't we?>

    Nadia: -Nods.- <Yeah...OH! Are you thinking of taking him along with us>>

    Lake: <Yeah, It couldn't hurt, could it?>

    Nadia: <But, that Delta Pokémon are still after him! He might not be safe with us!>

    Lake: <True...But, we won't be staying in one place for very long, will we? Whereas if he stays here, that Delta Feraligatr will always know where he is, and probably have a much easier time trying to catch him. He could very well be safer traveling with us then staying here.>

    Nadia: -Looks unsure.- <I don't know, Lake. This sounds pretty risky. And if something were to happen to him, the Reef Pokémon wouldn't be impressed.>

    Lake: <We managed to handle one Delta Pokémon easily enough, though. If we all stick to together, I'm sure we'll be able to keep him safe.>

    Nadia: <Well...Why don't we ask Pleo? See what he thinks?>

    Toothless: <I think I already know what his answer's going to be, though.>

    Lake: <Alright.> -To Pleo.- <Hey, Pleo?>

    Pleo: -Sniffs. <...Yeah?>

    Lake: <We're going to be staying on the Orange Islands for a little while yet. And I was wondering...Would you stay with us while we're here?>

    Pleo: -Shocked.- <...Really? You'd want me to come with you?>

    Nadia: -Nods.- <Yes, of course we would.>

    Lake: <Of course, we would have to ask the others and King Nautilus about it first, but I'm sure they would be okay with it. So...How about it? Would you like to come with us?>

    -Pleo says nothing for a second, simply staring in a mixture of shock and awe.-

    Pleo: <...> -Grins wildly.- <YES! Yes, of course I would!>

    -Pleo jumps up, wrapping his wings around Lake and hugging him tightly.-

    Pleo: <Thank you, Lake! Thank you, Nadia! I promise I'll be on my best behavior! And I'll do my best to make sure that the mean old Feraligatr doesn't get me!>

    Lake: -Smiles.- <I know you won't, Pleo. We'll all do our best to keep you safe. Right, Nadia?>

    Nadia: -Nods. <Right.>

    Pleo: <Thanks, guys.> -Yawns.- <This is going to be awesome...>

    -Pleo leans his head against Lake, sleepily.-

    Lake: <Look's like somemon's tired. Come on, little guy. We've had a long day, so you should be getting some sleep.>

    Pleo: -Sleepily.- <Okay...Oh, Lake? Nadia? Do...Do you mind if I sleep next to you two?>

    -The two glance at each other.

    Lake: <I don't mind...Do you?>

    Nadia: <Er...No, I don't mind either.>

    Pleo:' <Cool...>

    -Pleo lies down on the floor, Lake and Nadia setting themselves down on either side of him.-

    Pleo: -Yawns.- <Goodnight, guys.>

    Lake: -Smiles.- <Goodnight, Pleo.>

    Nadia: -Also smiles. <Goodnight.>
