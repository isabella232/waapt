    The False Skarmory used Drill Peck!

    Thinking quickly, Unite once again morphed his wings into steel...

    Unite used Steel Wing!

    Then crossed his wings in front of him to form a make-shift shield as the gel-Skarmory crashed into them, releasing a shower of sparks as it attempted to drill through.

    The bird winced as he tried his hardest to stop the attack. His metallic wings were holding firm, but he couldn't keep them that way forever.

    Sarah: Lucius, is that you? Please tell me it's you...

    -A raven-haired boy steps out of the mists, his face pale and somber.-

    ??? Lucius: It's me, yes. Lucanus has been telling me about what's happened since I... since I died, but I wanted to talk to you alone. I would've come earlier, but...

    -He trails off.-

    Sarah: ...Because of my nightmares. They were blocking you, weren't they?

    Lucius: Yeah. I'm sorry I couldn't reach you before now, because there's something that you really need to know...

    -Sarah gives Lucius a worried look.-

    Sarah: ...And that is?

    Lucius: ...You never actually killed Abel, Sarah.

    -Sarah blinks-

    Sarah: ...I what? I-I should know if I did and I did I...?

    Lucius: Ever since I was killed, Lucanus has been watching over you at my request. He saw everything. I'm still not sure what exactly that bastard did to you, but you never... here, I can show you. Lucanus taught me how.

    -He steps forward and places an icy-cold hand on Sarah's forehead.-

    Lucius: Close your eyes. This isn't going to be a pleasant feeling

    -Slightly scared, Sarah nods and closes her eyes-

    Route 216 — Past

    -Abel is deposited in the forest by a Beheeyem holding a slip of paper, who proceeds to disappear immediately afterwards.-

    Abel: May I ask what all this is about?

    ???: Does the name "Lucius Cain" mean anything to you?

    -A figure with a cloak, a staff with wrenchlike ends, and a mask that is distinctly less broken than its owner might have claimed it was, steps out of the shadows. Abel observes the figure curiously, his expression not faltering.-

    Abel: The name is familiar, yes. He was the one who was killed in that rather tragic video that's been circulating, correct?

    -Elda scowls-

    Elda: I'm sure you know plenty more than that about the incident. Spill.

    -Elda holds the staff toward Abel, who gently pushes it away.-

    Abel: Now, there's no need for that, I don't think. We don't want to perpetuate the cycle of revenge, do we?

    -Elda falters, but keeps the staff prepared.-

    Elda: ...I'm not here for revenge. I won't let myself sink to your level.

    Abel: Such a pity. Of course, I never really believed you had the stomach necessary to end me... especially not in the same manner as you almost killed your dear paramour. But I suspected that you would seek me out, hence why I arranged for some external assistance.

    -The same Beheeyem that brought Abel to the clearing proceeds to blip behind Elda, who quickly whips around just in time to have her mask kicked off of her face.-

    Elda Sarah: Gah! Wha— Brecht?!

    Brecht Grohl: <Sorry, wrong brother. Don't remind me of him.>

    -Grohl proceeds to psychically knock Sarah unconscious. Abel kneels down and picks up her mask, turning it over in his hands curiously.-

    Abel: I have to say, I'm almost disappointed... somehow I thought this would be more difficult. It would appear that love makes fools of us all. Still, it wouldn't do to have her or any of her companions come back to finish the job, as it were, so I'm afraid I'm going to have to request a second favour. Make her believe that I died at her hands. A quick, clean and mostly painless end... though if she is anywhere near as moralistic as her late friend purported to be, then the memory of the deed should haunt her all the same.

    Grohl: <Easy enough. I've done greater alterations in the past. This is almost child's play.>

    -Grohl psychically raises Sarah upright, and places a hand on her forehead. After a brief glow, he lets her fall again. Meanwhile, Abel studies Elda's mask and staff with a small smile on his face.-

    Abel: Such intriguing artifacts... and the mask looks about my size, as well. Sadly, anything I take could easily be traced back to us, so it seems we will have to put an end to the girl's shadowy alter-ego for good.

    -He takes the staff and slams it down on the mask, shattering it, before breaking the staff in two over his knee.-

    Abel: If only she'd paid more attention to the message within that video. No matter how hard people like her try, I'm never to be punished for the whole grisly affair... after all, the other fellow wasn't. Revenge is such a fundamental aspect of human nature that I'm rather surprised our masked marauder had no intention of seeking it. Anyway, shall we be off?

    Grohl: <It's about time. I have a trainer to play ignorant to, as much as I hate it.>

    -Abel is blipped away. Grohl awakens Sarah again, teleporting her away as well before she can fully return to her senses.-

    Dragnor

    -Lucius takes his hand off of Sarah's forehead and steps back, looking down.-

    Lucius: I'm sorry, but... I couldn't bear the thought of you living with another death on your conscience
