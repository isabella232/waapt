    Greenfield Grasslands

    Yarn: <So, you're telling me you want to go all the way to Azelea...>

    Woolly: <Yeah...>

    Yarn: <Sneak into an observatory...>

    Woolly: <Yeah...>

    Yarn: <And use the telescope to talk to some Space Ampharos?>

    Woolly: <Yeah, what's wrong with that?>

    Yarn faceflippers.

    Yarn: <We really need to get back to Route 32, the sun's going down!>

    He gestures at the skies above, cast in a fiery orange hue as the sun sinks on the horizon.

    Woolly: <That's the point though, Yarn. We need to be there at night or else it won't work. You can't use a telescope during the day.>

    Yarn: <But is this detour really a good idea? Why do you need me and the flock to come along, anyway?>

    Woolly: <Well...I might need your help when it comes time to talk, if you know what I mean.>

    Woolly frowns, her ears drooping.

    <Come on, Yarn. You're the one who told me there were Ampharos in space in the first place.>

    The shiny Ampharos shifts uncomfortably.

    Yarn: <Actually...I kinda' made them up just so I could tell you a story...>

    Woolly: <...You did?>

    Yarn nods.

    Yarn: <Yeah...I'd thought you'd have forgotten it by now...>

    Woolly: <...Does that mean you don't believe me?>

    Yarn: <What?! No...no! I'm not saying that at all...>

    Unamused, Woolly crosses her flippers, giving her brother a stern leer.

    <...Okay, I might doubt you just a little, Woolly. You can be a bit silly about things.>

    Woolly: <But I'm not being silly at all! There really are Ampharos in space!>

    She pauses, shaking her head.

    <But you know what? It's fine, I'll go to Azelea by myself, and you can go back to Route 32 with the flock. Okay?>

    Yarn: <Oh, come on Wool->

    Woolly: <NO. If you think this is too "silly" then you can stay out of it, okay?>

    Yarn says nothing.

    <Good.>

    With that, Woolly turns and walks away in a huff as she begins to head towards Azelea. Yarn watches her go, then sighs as he returns to the flock so he can begin to herd them back to Route 32.

    Yarn: <I bet Benny never has to deal with these kinds of issues...>
