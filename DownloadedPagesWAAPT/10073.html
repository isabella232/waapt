    251814 Herbert40k10th May 2013 12:42:36 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
    Imperial Commissar

    Mistralton City, Airport

    -Herbert is sitting alone on a bench in the Mistralton City airport, still fuming silently after his fight with Lyuri. He is so preoccupied with his own thoughts that he doesn't notice a red-haired girl with a Roserade by her side come up behind him.-

    Alice: What are you doing here? The flight doesn't leave for another three hours.

    Herbert: In that case, I might ask you the same question. And it's nice to see you too, Alice.

    Alice: Same to you.

    Arsenic: <So, have you gotten around to giving those puny mons of yours some training, sir?>

    Herbert: Be quiet, Arsenic. You're lucky I don't have a Fire-type yet, or else me and Alice could have a fair fight for once.

    Alice: ...Arsenic, go and check the departure timetable. Make sure our flight isn't delayed.

    Arsenic: <Yes, ma'am!>

    -Arsenic fires off a salute and marches off. Alice watches her go for a moment, impassive, before turning around and addressing Herbert in a dull monotone.-

    Alice: Is something the matter, Lucius?

    Herbert: No. I'm fine. Why are you asking?

    Alice: Because you're a terrible liar, that's why. Are you still embarrassed about being made to wear a dress in Lentimas?

    -Herbert stands up suddenly and stares at Alice in surprise.-

    Herbert: How do you know about-

    Alice: I keep up with the news. Especially when it concerns you. You caused quite a stir in Lentimas, you know. Oh, don't look so mortified. It's not the first time I've seen you in a dress, after all. Remember when we were both eight, and you were curious about-

    Herbert: Funnily enough, I really don't want to hear that story again right now, Alice. I am not in the mood.

    Alice: See? Bad liar. You couldn't convince a Deino that the sky was green.

    Herbert: ...Alright, I admit it. I'm a little worked up at the moment. But I'm not going to let it ruin your birthday weekend. After all, we're going home again for the first time in... it must have been at least a year now. What did you have planned?

    Alice: I figured we'd go back and see what makes us nostalgic. Watch a couple of contests in Hearthome, go visit the Great Marsh, perhaps even see if we can go Rapidash riding. And... we're going back to Eterna City, to visit an old friend.

    -Herbert goes pale upon hearing this, and takes a couple of steps back, shaking nervously. Alice tilts her head at him, her tone remaining impassive.-

    Alice: You still blame yourself for what happened, don't you? It wasn't your fault and you know it.

    Herbert: It was my fault. I just sat there and watched as he... as he... I couldn't do anything to help him. It's becoming a depressingly recurrent theme...

    Alice: What do you mean?

    Herbert: I'm useless, aren't I? Everything I do just ends up making things worse in some way. No matter how I try to help, people still get angry. People still get hurt. Usually because of me. I'm more trouble than I'm worth.

    -He looks down at his feet, his face falling. Alice lets him talk for a while, her expression unchanged.-

    Herbert: All I want to do is bring evil people to justice. It sounds so cliche, and cheesy, but... after seeing what Titan did, I can't stand the thought of him getting away without punishment. But some of the people the J-Team hang around with... tyrants and murderers, ex-Rockets, scientists... why haven't they gotten their punishment? Why haven't they been brought to justice? Is saying "sorry" really enough to invalidate a life of sick and evil deeds?

    Alice: I'm just as keen to hunt your father down as you are. He deserves to be brought to justice. But he carries on with his evil deeds. He refuses to acknowledge that what he's doing is wrong. In his eyes, all that matters is finishing what Cyrus started. He is completely unrepentant. But some people want nothing more than to be forgiven for the sins of their past. And perhaps you should be more willing to grant them that. Are you really in a position to be so judgemental? Are you in a position to turn away allies?

    Herbert: ...No. I'm not, but it's just... I can't stand the thought of someone getting away with something like that. Just... just a snap of a finger, and they're absolved of all their sins. What's to stop them from doing it all again? Why aren't they haunted by their past, when I am? I've done nothing wrong. So why do the nightmares keep insisting that I have?

    -He gives a half-groan, half-sob, and punches a nearby wall in fustration.-

    Herbert: Perhaps it's because I'm a bad person myself. All I do is insult and hurt people, and expect them to be there for me even when I've treated them like shit. I got into a massive fight with my Aura tutor and his girlfriend Lyuri today, and you know why? Because she told the truth. She called me out on being the stubborn little jackass that I am, and you know what I did? I stormed off. I told them I didn't want to learn Aura with them any more. All I did was prove her right. So why do I keep refusing to admit it? Is my ego really so fragile that I can't stand people telling me the truth? It's her birthday this Sunday, as well. I've probably ruined it for her...

    -He rests his head in his hands.-

    Herbert: I don't mean to say these things. I don't want to hurt people. I don't want to be a bad person. And yet all I can do is insult and belittle and mistrust people. They're all beginning to hate me, and they should. I can't do anything right. Why can't I do anything right?

    -Alice looks at Herbert for a moment, before pulling him into a tight hug. He buries his head in her mane of hair.-

    Herbert: I don't know what I'd be without you... you're the only person who kept me sane after what happened, the only person who stopped me from just lashing out at the world... without you, I'd be...

    Alice: You're a good person, Lucius. You would be even if it wasn't for me. You're too hard on yourself sometimes. You're trying to help, that's what counts. You're trying to do the right thing. You're trying to be a better person.

    Herbert: ...

    -Herbert continues to embrace her tightly, before eventually pulling away. He goes a vivid red, and scratches the back of his neck awkwardly.-

    Herbert: ...Um, I'm sorry, but now I have a... well, not a "girlfriend", per se, but-

    Alice: I know. But you still need someone. Someone who'll be there even when times get difficult. Someone to help you cope with the lows of life, and someone to share the highs. Someone you can talk to, someone you can confide in. And to be quite honest, someone to keep you in line when you're being a massive idiot. Someone to stop you from making mistakes that will come back to haunt you later. I'm willing to be that person. Just like I always was. I'm here for you, Lucius.

    -She gives him a small smile, her usually emotionless face glowing with affection. After a moment's hesitation, Herbert returns it.-

    Herbert: I don't deserve someone as good as you.

    -Alice punches him playfully in the shoulder.-

    Alice: Oh, don't go saying things like that. We're friends, Lucy. Just like we've always been. I'll always be there for you, and you'll always be there for me. Now then, are you ready to head back home? It's been too long since I last saw Sinnoh.

    Herbert: ...Yeah. Yeah, I'm ready.

    Alice: Let's get moving, then. I imagine you'll want to find a birthday present for that Lyuri girl at the duty-free.

    -Alice takes Herbert by the wrist, and the two friends walk off, ready to head back to their childhood home. After they are out of earshot, a man who up until this point was watching them silently gives a grunt of disgust, before folding up his newspaper and following behind them.-
