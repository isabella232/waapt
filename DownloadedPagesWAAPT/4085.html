    Guardé: <Then why did you try and help me? He could have killed you, too.>

    Chord: -Groans.- <Well Guardé, you may be a completely incompetent idiot who is a danger to himself and others as well as a huge pain in the abdomen. But...I'd rather you weren't dead.>

    -Upon hearing this, Guardé wasn't sure whether to be insulted or touched.-

    Guardé: <So, you like me, then?>

    Chord: <Let's put it this way. If I didn't like you, would I have done that to my Guitar in order to save you?>

    -Chord weakly points a foreleg to his guitar lying by his bed. Or at least, what's left of it. The body is nearly snapped in half, held together only by a few splinters of wood, and pretty much all of the strings have been snapped.-

    Guardé: <Oh, your Guitar! Chord, I'm so sorry. You've got nothing to play your songs on now because of me...>

    Chord: <Don't beat yourself up about it. I can easily make another one. Can't say the same about you, though, so I'd say it was worth it>

    Guardé: <...Thank you, Chord.>

    Chord: <Don't mention it.> -Yawns.- <Come on, let's try and sleep. We'll be better in the morning of we get a good night's sleep.>

    Guardé: <Yeah, good point...Goodnight, Chord.>

    Chord: <Night, Guardé. Oh! But one more thing, just before we sleep.>

    Guardé: <Oh? what is it?>

    Chord: <I just wanted to say...I still think you're an idiot.>

    Guardé: <...Gee, thanks.>

    Chord: <Let me finish. You may be an idiot, but...You're the good kind of idiot.>

    Guardé: <...Oh.> -Smiles.- <Thanks, Chord.>
