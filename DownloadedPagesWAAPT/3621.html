    -We watch as the tide subsides and Lileep emerges from the water, moving very slowly towards us. He is dragging a strand of... something, red, branched and alga-like and about as long as an Onix.-

    Algira — <Wow, look at that!>

    Copahue (flies around) — <Ugh, it smells... what is that thing?>

    ...Did he just drag some kelp? From where? It looks like mochoco...

    -Lileep simply stands some distance ahead of us, pulling that thing through. I approach carefully and kneel to about his level-

    — Hello... did you spend a good night?

    -Lileep doesn't answer; Algira buzzes, looking obviously annoyed-

    — Er... what is that?

    Lileep (keeps pulling) — <Food.>

    — Oh, you git it yourself?

    Lileep — <Yes.>

    Algira — <It looks like it has spots or something... can we see it?>

    Lileep — <No.>

    — ...

    Algira (sort-of-blinks) — <B-but...>

    -I make a Gesture for Algira to drop it; she "hmph"s and perches on my shoulder, while Ichtacya, Dintel and Shahinne look at us from a nearby thicket-

    — I guess you want to take your time to... eat it or something?

    -Lileep turns around and looks at us, then points to his Pokéball on my belt-

    — ..Oh, you mean?

    Lileep — <I'm taking it home.>

    -I let out an "oh" of disappointment since I wanted to see what did Lileep have planned with that big algae branch. I bring out the Pokéball and stare at it for a while.-

    -Looking at Dintel and Ichtacya, Lileep waves his tentacles around, then emits a sort of screech I can't understand and then stretches his trunk-

    Lileep — <Look, can I just take it to that ball place? I'm hungry.>

    — Oh? Well, if you want...

    At least it's a good thing that he spoke more. I guess at this point I'd rather call it a day.

    — If you want, yeah. Just, eh, remember to ask if you have any problem.

    Lileep (drops tentacles) — <Fine.>

    -I point the Dive Ball at him and he disappears with the kelp. I sigh, then point to Dintel and Shahinne to get ready. We are departing for the other side of the island-

    Copahue (following us) — <Such a charming fellow.>

    — He's, technically, pretty new, you see, — I present Algira her Ball,but she decides to ride with me — he's even younger than Shahinne.

    Copahue — <Say what? But, but, no egg, and... er... she... it... err...>

    -shakes his head then flies around-

    Copahue — <You know what? Screw it. I'm gonna stop trying to understand all the weird things that happen around this group and just take it for granted.>

    -Algira can't help but laugh at the comment-

    — It'd make you well. You haven't met Tagg or Fool, for one.

    Algira — <Oh! When are we returning? I want to see Helios...>

    — Fancy him a lot? — she buzzes — We're returning tomorrow, Algira. I just want to give Lileep some time to get used to stuff.
