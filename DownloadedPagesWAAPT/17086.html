    427146 Luke99247th Nov 2014 06:44:21 PM from England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Noddin' my head like yeah

    Some time later...

    There were Feraligatr everywhere. All shapes, sizes, and some in a different colour, too. A bunch of Totodile and Croconaw were scattered about too, but in lesser numbers. Still, this was one of the largest gathering of the line that most would see.

    After all, Feraligatr families were big. And when they got together for gatherings and reunions...things got wild.

    In the midst of the hub-bub, one of the largest and most battle-scarred of the females waited impatiently for the hunting party to return. The festivities couldn't get started without something for everyone to dig their jaws into.

    Deluge the Feraligatr (Lv.100): <Gah. I hope they get back soon. Otherwise I'm going to have to start eating peoplemons.>

    Knowing her well enough that she was ready, willing and waiting to make good on this, most of the nearby Feraligatr vacated the area immediately.

    Luckily for them, it was at this point that the hunting party finally returned, prompting quite the celebraton from the gathered crocodillians.

    Feraligatr #24: <Yo! Food's here!>

    Feraligatr: <YEAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!!!>

    Until somebody noticed just what the four had brought back.

    Feraligatr #32: <Hey, wait-a-minute!>

    Frowning, Deluge tilted her head in curiosity at Stratus, who the four Feraligatr had tied up so they could bring him back without a struggle.

    Deluge: <...Kids. We're not going to eat your Dad.>

    Stratus: <Oh, thank goodness.>

    Deluge: <Dragonite meat is terrible! Far too gamy!>

    Stratus: <HEY!>

    Ignoring her one-time husband's annoyance, she crossed her arms.

    Deluge: <So, why ya' trying to feed the big lummox to me, then?>

    The leader of the hunting party snorted in annoyance.

    Feraligatr #1: <This big lummox is the reason we don't have anything to eat!>

    The gathered crowed gasped in shock.

    Feraligatr #2: <That's right! Every time we tried to capture something, he tried to invite our dinner to dinner!>

    The Dragonite blinked in surprise in his bonds.

    Stratus: <Oooooh...I may have misunderstood the intentions here just a tiny bit.>

    Deluge: <I did tell you to explain everything to him slowly. And perhaps more than once just to be sure.>

    Stratus: <I'm ''right here, and my ears work perfectly fine you know!>

    Deluge: <Anyways, there's still plenty of light left, and I think I've got some carcasses stuffed away that we can nibble on until then. Think you would be willing to go and give it another try?>

    The Feraligatr scowled.

    Feraligatr #1: <Only once we've kicked this guy out of our swamp! Along with the 'Gatr that brought him here in the first place! Where is he, anyway?>

    The gathered Feraligatr glanced at each other, trying to find who it was that had brought Stratus there to seemingly ruin things.

    <"Okay, where is he?">

    <"What does he look like?">

    <"The same as everyone else, I'd bet!">

    <"Hey!">

    <"OI!">

    <"HEY!">

    <"Apart From the shinies!">

    <"Oh wait, I think I've found him!">

    A few of the Feraligatr pushed one of their kin forward. This one was just a tiny bit different, though. His scales were a much brighter shade of blue and yellow, seemingly to gleam in the later afternoon light. His stomach bore a large scar, the mark of a particularly nasty stab wound that had long since healed. Against the uniform darker blue and yellow of his brethren, he stood out like a sore thumb.

    Precisely what he didn't want right now.

    Lake the Feraligatr-EX (Lv.100): <Okay, be cool. I can fix this.>

    Glancing at Lake briefly, Deluge turned back to her other sun, somewhat annoyed.

    Deluge: <I'm all for kicking Stratus out, but we are not getting rid of Lake.>

    Feraligatr #1: <Why not? If it wasn't for him, he wouldn't have brought our loser of a Dad back here to ruin our dinner!>

    Some of the crowd roared in agreement. Deluge, remained firm.

    Deluge: <I said no. End of discussion.>

    The thing about Feraligatr is that they tend to be stubborn. Very few Gators would ever give up their side of the argument. Not even if it ended up to loss of limb. Or life.

    Feraligatr #1: <Fine then.>

    He, along with his three other siblings, crossed his arms.

    <Either they go, or we're not going hunting again.>

    This time, everyone cried out in shock, prompting a large degree of chatter from the crowd.

    <"Kick 'em out!">

    <"No way, they're war-heroes!">

    <"Seriously?">

    <"Okay, one of them is!">

    <"How about we eat the pair of them?">

    <"Ooh, good idea!">

    Feeling the situation was about to get out of hand, Lake hustled over to his mother to speak to her privately.

    Lake: <I appreciate you sticking up for me, Mom. But maybe I should just go...>

    Deluge: <NO! I'll go and catch food for everymon if I have to!>

    Lake: <I don't doubt that you could, Mom. But you would probably depopulate most of the swamp. And the guys at the PEFE said that you've almost manage to eat almost the entire ecosystem here. Several times.>

    Deluge frowned, the same way a concerned mother would frown for her son. Just with much more teeth.

    Deluge: <This is supposed to be a reunion! It won't be complete without you here.

    ...

    ...And your Dad, as much as I hate to admit it.>

    Stratus: <I don't know whether to feel touched or slighted.>

    Lake: <Thanks, Mom. But...>

    Lake glanced down at the muddy floor, sighing wistfully.

    <I don't think I fit in here. I got a lot of flack for not being on the hunting team since I don't really like the whole "eating innocent Pokémon thing" much. Barely anyone here knows who Nintendo are, let alone what a 3DS is, and everyone's just a bit too rowdy for my liking.>

    Deluge: <I know, I know, it's a bit rough.>

    Lake: <I saw somemon rip another mon's arm off and hit him over the head with it! And they told me that was play-fighting!>

    Deluge blinked, stunned.

    Deluge: <...DANG.>

    Lake: <I know! Anyway, it's fine. I'll just take Dad and go back to Goldenrod. Luke's probably missing me already.>

    Deluge: <...Okay. If you really want to go, that's up to you.>

    She sighed heavily.

    <There's always next year, I guess.>

    Lake: <Yeah, next year. We'll be here for sure.>

    Feraligatr #1: <He won't.>

    He pointed at the tied up Stratus.

    Stratus: <Okay, that is it! Once Lake unties me, I'm going to give you such a->

    Using his Strength (As in, actually using Strength. Even Stratus was too heavy for Lake to carry without help). Lake picked his Dad up, turned around and waved goodbye to the other Feraligatr.

    Lake: <Bye guys. See you next year...>

    And with a sigh, he turned and walked off into the swamp, everyone watching him go.

    Feraligatr #2: <Aw, there goes dinner.>

    Feraligatr #3: <Bro...>

    Feraligatr #2: <Dude, how often do you get the chance to eat a Dragonite?>
