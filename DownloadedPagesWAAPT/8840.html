    220990 Crewe13th Jan 2013 03:12:53 PM from Gravity Falls, Oregon Get RP Mod
    Li'l Punk

    AU, Valterra's cave

    -Valterra rests against the back wall, her eyes closed. Temeraire seems to be trying to talk James into playing some sort of convoluted strategy game involving quite a bit of math and scribbled drawing in the dirt of the cave floor. James scratches out a few moves, then gives up entirely and retreats to Valterra's side, while Temeraire curls up in the corner and sulks-

    Temeraire: <Why hasn't the meeting started already?>

    James: <We're waiting on Ormarr. He's been training off by that waterfall of his.>

    Temeraire: <If he's gonna train so far away, he could at least leave early so he'd make the meetings.>

    Valterra: <Dear Ormarr is very devoted to his troops now that he has a special assignment. But Temeraire, love, perhaps you could go and fetch him up here, if his tardiness is putting Beedrill in your bonnet.>

    Ormarr: -climbs in to the cave just as Temeraire uncurls himself and prepares to fly out- <No need, Colonel. I'm here. Colonel James. General.> -he nods to them. James nods back, Valterra yawns-

    James: <Good, everyone's here. We can get started. What do you need us for, General?>

    Valterra: <First off, Ormarr, how's our special secret coming, hm? James, Temeraire, loves, if you could leave us so Ormarr and I can discuss our secret—>

    Ormarr: <No.>

    -Valterra's eyes slowly open-

    Valterra: <What?>

    Ormarr: <I said no, General. We can not keep this a secret.>

    Valterra: <But it will make such a good surprise when everyone sees it put to use—>

    Ormarr: <With all due respect, General, I will not keep this a secret from the army or its other officers!>

    James: <You dare speak to your general in this way—>

    Valterra: -raises a claw to silence James- <My officers speak to me in the way that keeps me from making costly mistakes. Pray tell me, love, why can't we keep our little secret a surprise?>

    Ormarr: <Because the troops will be surprised. If they don't know it's coming, they'll be just as surprised as the enemy, and that leaves them vulnerable. But if they know it's coming, they'll take heart in it when it does and fight all the harder.>

    Valterra: <And if any of them let this secret out?>

    Ormarr: <These troops are just as likely to let it slip as my own. Besides, who will they talk to? Each other? They have no contact with the outside world.>

    Valterra: <... Oh, fine. You've spoiled my fun, but I suppose you're right. Temeraire, James, we'll go to Ormarr's training spot and see his little trick. But it's rather far... Temeraire, love, if you could call a pair of your Hydreigons to assist James and Ormarr in getting there, I'll go with you—>

    A few minutes later, a clearing with a small waterfall spilling down from the mountains

    -the four officers and the two Hydreigon that ferried Ormarr and James arrive. There is a large force of various Dragons (as well as a few scattered non-Dragons) either drilling or resting. Ormarr marches over and they all snap to attention-

    Ormarr: <Attention! We're going to be showing the General what we've all been working on. Major Druddigon, Major Nuzlocke, if you would.>

    -a female Druddigon and the Grovyle from the meeting with the Council salute and turn to the Pokemon under their command-

    Druddigon/Nuzlocke: <Ring up! Basic!>

    -the troops form a large circle around the clearing. James and Temeraire look around curiously-

    -Druddigon and Nuzlocke look to Ormarr questioningly, who holds up a paw and steps up beside them-

    Ormarr: <Protect wall!>

    -each of the Pokemon in the ring throw up a Protect shied, each one rather larger than the average shield, though it does vary somewhat depending on the size of each Pokemon. Each Protect meets the edge of the one beside it, forming an impenetrable wall on the ground level. James nods approvingly-

    James: <Impressive. What happens when the ring has to be bigger?>

    Ormarr: <My mons have been training nonstop in making various sizes of shields. This is the most basic, with the smallest shields. It can be held for longer, but covers a smaller area. We are working up to an arrangement that allows a shield around cities such as Celadon.>

    Temeraire: <Isn't it rather silly not to have a roof? Flying types can escape.>

    Valterra: -flicks her tail- <You're right, love. We'll need aerial support. Send a messenger to Austerium, and ask for fliers to train with these troops—oh, Ormarr, they can stop now if you like. Anyway, Temeraire, tell Aussie that he can either send me a whole troop of clumsy fliers, or just a few quick and agile ones.>

    -Ormarr gives the order and the Protect wall goes down-

    Valterra: <You're all doing fabulously, dears! Keep up the good work, and the Council won't want to execute any of us!>

    -she rejoins her officers-

    Valterra: <Well, loves, you'd best go spread the word! Now, then, if one of you could bring me back to my cave, I'll just take a nap...>

    -the officers all disperse, Temeraire ferrying Valterra back to her cave before going to spread the word of the Protect wall to his own troops-
