    Lyuri chuckles and gives Duke a cheerful look "A date huh? Sure! Just let me get ready." she said, with a quite pleased tone.

    She then heads upstairs and after a while she comes back in her Lucario coat and carrying a map of the city in her hand.

    "Okay, where do you want to go?" asked Lyuri.

    "How about one of the cafes around the city?"

    "Hmm... Why not just a restaurant? Im feeling kinda hungry right now so I need more food than a café can give me." said Lyuri, with a grin

    "Well... All right, lets go then?" asked Duke, with a more honest smile

    Lyuri just nods and the couples heads out on rollerskates, off to their date.

    Lumiose City

    They dash through the city right alongside each other, occasionally glancing at each other and smiling

    "By the way, youre the one paying right?" asked Lyuri, with a knowing smirk

    Dukes smile turns into a grimace immediately "Uh I dont have money on me All the money I have its you who gives it to me so..."

    "Oh yeah, I forgot my boyfriend was a hobo." said Lyuri, with a playful wink and smirk

    Duke just blushes in embarrassment, despite being well aware that Lyuri didnt really mind it.

    "So, hey, how about a race to the Sushi High Roller? The one who wins chooses what the loser has to eat!" proposed Lyuri, with a challenging grin as she showed the location of the restaurant to Duke in the map.

    "A race? Well, I accept it..."

    Due to being distracted, Lyuri didn't notice that she was about to crash into a tree, fortunately Duke quickly pulled her out of the way, almost making her lose her balance in the process.

    "... Just try to not hit any trees in the way." said Duke, with a smirk

    He then lets go of Lyuri and starts speeding up and leaving her behind

    "Cheater!" yelled Lyuri

    As they race, they end up taking different routes to get to the restaurant, of course, they were too focused in going fast to care about it. Upon reaching the Sushi High Roller, Duke and Lyuri both arrive at the same time in the alley where the restaurant was located. Duke came from the south and Lyuri from the north, they both dash towards the restaurant, but Duke decides to slow down a bit to not risk an injury, unfortunately Lyuri didn't seem to particularly care about what happened, she ended up going too fast and went right past the restaurant and crashing right into Duke, who holds her in his arms as they both fall to the ground. Lyuri's face was buried on Duke's chest for a moment before she raised her head and met with her smiling boyfriend.

    "I guess you won." said Duke, still holding Lyuri.

    "Yeah, I guess I did..." said Lyuri, with a small smile.

    They then get up and dust themselves before entering the restaurant.

    Sushi High Roller

    Upon getting inside, the place was surprising empty and Lyuri quickly got her wallet in hands and went to the counter.

    "So, how much do I have to pay to dine here?" asked Lyuri.

    "500.000." said the man in the counter with a bored look.

    Lyuri just jaw drops and stays still for a few seconds and then leaves without a word.

    "... How come this restaurant costs five times more than the best hotel in the city?" asked Duke as he followed Lyuri.

    Lumiose City

Duke's composure was complete lost as he tried to make Lyuri stop, which she soon did.

    "Why... Why did you do that?" asked Duke, his expression a mix of amusement and confusion as he tried to regain his composure.

    "I was just bored, besides..." said Lyuri, letting out a few chuckles "I wanted to hear you laugh. You usually just chuckle or snicker, I don't think I've ever seen your burst into laughter like that." she smiles.

    Duke just gives a rather exasperated sigh and ruffles his hair a bit. Lyuri gives him a cheerful look, but stays silent for a bit before opening her mouth

    "You should laugh like that. You have a great, fun laugh and it's contagious just for how unlike you it is to laugh like that."

    Duke gives her a rather surprised look "You think? Well... Thanks."

    Duke then wraps his arm around Lyuri and they just stand there waiting and noticing the odd glances they got from other people. Suddenly Lyuri enters a laughing fit as Duke had started to tickle her in the stomach with his other hand with a satisfied grin on his face.

    "Stop...! Stop...!" said Lyuri, as she barely could stop laughing.

    Duke then stops and just smirks at Lyuri as she recovers from her fit

    "I guess... Two can play this game." said Lyuri, chuckling a bit and looking downright exhausted.

    "You have a pretty fun laugh as well, though I suppose it doesn't take much to make you laugh." said Duke, chuckling
