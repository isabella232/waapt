    "I still prefer being a Pokémon, but it's not bad." answered Duke as he also ate.

    "Well, I think you look better as a human than a Lucario, though that's more because you Lucario look all the same so it really doesn't make a difference." she joked, with a chuckle.

    "I'm still wondering how long it'll take for me to go back to normal, being a human makes me feel so... Helpless." said Duke, with a small sigh "Humans are pretty weak after all, that's why you have to rely on us."

    "Hey!" said Lyuri, clearly offended.

    "Sorry, it's just that I'm used to all the moves I knew as a Lucario, turning into a human made me lose almost all of them, and I trained hard to learn them, you know."

    "... Yeah, yeah, I know, you used to train all the time, with or without my help. I guess it must be a bit frustating, but that's still-"

    "I know, I know. You're not helpless, I've been with you for what? Three or four years now? I'd know at this point." said Duke, half-joking.

    "Hmph... Well, to be fair, you did have to get me out of danger a few times, as did the others." said Lyuri, though not being very happy to admit.

    "That's to be expected, you being yourself." he joked as he ate a bit more flan.

    "What do you mean by that?" said Lyuri, now starting to glare at Duke

    "Nothing."

    "Say it already."

    "Nothing at all." Duke then started snickering

    "Spill it out!" said Lyuri, now becoming genuinely irritated.

    "It's really fun to mess with you, you know?" he answered, now chuckling a bit before stopping a few seconds later "In all seriousness, you don't depend as much on us as most do, in fact I'm pretty sure you'd do pretty well without us."

    "Heh... I guess, I still prefer to have you guys around though, especially you and Riku, I think meeting you back in Floccesy years ago was one of the best things that could've happened, after all, you did become my strongest teammember, period." she said, smiling, if a little embarrassed.

    "I guess so, you make me look better than I really am though, but I guess this just motivates me to try to meet your expectations, and really, I'm pretty sure I'd lose some battles if it weren't for you guiding me around."

    "What are we eating here? Humble flan?" joked Lyuri

    Duke chuckles a bit "I guess so."
