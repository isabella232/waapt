    In one of the vined hallways of Neo-Flare, two Pokemon lean on the wall by a water cooler. Idly chatting about literature.

    Thutmose (Lucario): <I tell ya, old boy. You're missing out on some grand cinematic surrealist imagery not giving Jean Cocteau's La Belle et la Bête a chance>

    Thutmose (Lucario): <H-Hey. I'm getting around to it!>

    During this, the tall, slender figure of a Gallade comes walking briskly down the corridor. Stopping over at the water cooler to get a quick drink.

    Thutmose: <Hey! Shaka!>

    Octavius: <Salutations ol' boy! How has life been treating you?>

    Shaka: -Bluntly- <Hello.>

    Thutmose: <Sheesh Shaka. Why must you always act like you have this great big chip on your shoulder?>

    Octavius: <With an attitude like that, it's no wonder you are the Master's wingman.>

    Shaka: <Oh stuff it you two. I have no time for this.>

    Thutmose: <Oh. Go to go out and pull some more teeth, eh?>

    Octavius: <No! He's going to sand off some more fingerprints, oui?>

    Shaka: *Eye Twitch*

    Thutmose: <I got it, I got it! You gotta cut up a corpse and then bury the sections separately with a dead dog over each one!>

    The Emboar and Lucario burst into laughter, but stop when they notice that Shaka is staring at them with a glare that could burn through a diamond.

    Shaka: <Don't. Joke. About stuff like that.>

    Octavius: <Humourless too.>

    Thutmose: <Not only are you as frigid as the boss, but you're as dour as him too. C'mon, can't you be anything but another copy of Constantin?>

    Shaka: <Peh, I am nothing like Constantin...>

    Octavius: <Pah.>

    Shaka stops again.

    Shaka: Pray tell, how long have you two been under Master's thumb?

    Thutmose: <Us?>

    Octavius: <Oh about... coming on three years now. Why?>

    Shaka: Tell me. What do you know, and I mean actually know about the man we call our trainer?

    Thutmose: <Erm....>

    Octavius: <Hard to tell. He's an elusive sourpuss, like you.>

    Shaka: <Yes, yes. Superficially, the old saying of a mon resembling it's master rings true. But while I have fallen into dark times, I haven't thrown my humanity out of the window in a fit of bitter rage and despair. I follow orders, not make them.>

    Shaka gets himself a cup of water.

    Shaka: <Also while I tend to use the same demeanour when faced with trauma and despair. Constantin... he's always coped with it in extremes. Whether that be in either wide-eyed optimism bordering denial or - as you know him - burning hate and rage.>

    Octavius: <He once was optimistic?>

    Thutmose: <17 years? Didn't know you were a long runner.>

    Octavius: <..."Us">

    Thutmose: <Brutus... That name rings a bell.>

    Octavius & Thutmose: <We didn't know you had a sister.>

    Shaka raises his eye at the two, and replies with a very bitter tone.

    Shaka: <You two weren't even eggs when Constantin abandoned her in the Pokemon Village south of Snowbelle.>

    The two swallow as they exchange sheepish glances.

    Thutmose: <Erm... Well then...>
