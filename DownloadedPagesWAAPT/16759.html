    -The PA system buzzes for a moment before Echo's voice rings through the halls.-

    Echo: Benjamina Zanzibar, I'd like to talk to you in my office.

    Benz: Hmm~?

    -Benz enters Echo's office, perhaps slightly sooner than the laws of physics would suggest given the location of her quarters-

    Benz: What's up? Find anything that needs excavating? ♥

    -Echo frowns slightly, looking over Benz analytically.-

    Echo: ...You can drop the act now. I know who you are, Mew.

    Benz: That is quite the accusation.

    Echo: Your disguise is mediocre at best, as are your so-called colleagues'. While methemoglobinemia is indeed a real skin condition that I might otherwise believe Mercedes to have, the odds of a trio of women who each theme themselves around a color that, by pure chance, happens to correspond with that of Mew right down to skin color is slim to none. In addition, you've done absolutely nothing that your supposed occupations indicate you would do, and the "research" that your green friend supplied is a complete load of crap.

    Benz: Hey! You'll hurt Daimler's feelings!

    Echo: ...Look. I know you've heard about our original goals, and that you want to hold on to this false identity as tightly as you can so that I don't carry them out. However there's no need for that anymore. Even if I could follow through, I no longer wish to. You're safe.

    Benz: Are you sure?

    Since, you know, if you break your word and go trying to do freaky science stuff to me under the assumption I am Mew, you'll have obviously lost the only person who knows how to maintain the faucets that run with coffee in the bathrooms~

    Echo: I may have twisted the truth in the past, but I refuse to break a promise. And I promise you that you will not be harmed, manipulated, experimented on, or anything of the sort as long as I have any authority in this organization.

    Benz: Well, as long as you've been warned...

    -blip!-

    -there is now a Mew floating upside-down in front of Echo, whose face shows a restrained expression of awe even despite having expected this.-

    Echo: ...Thank you for trusting me. As I said, you will remain free and unharmed as long as I have the power to keep you protected. However I fear I may not be able to remain and protect you for long.

    Mew: ?

    -Echo sighs, glancing at the door-

    Echo: Over time, the Illusion Project has started to become exactly what I feared it would. While I've always pushed for a greater good, there are far too many beneath me who seek to take the Project and lead it to their own selfish ideals in a cruel imitation of Galactic's methods. The arbiter of the deserving once again will be the one with power, not the one who is right...

    -She looks Mew in the eyes.-

    Echo: I want to change this. But I cannot do this alone. If you were to help, however...
