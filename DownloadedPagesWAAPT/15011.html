    Galahad: <Well okay…> -Rushes forward, and smashes through the Reflect, Light Screen, and then the the ice encasing the Gourgeist-

    Galahad: <Oooh cand-> -Turns transparent- <.... Awwww…>

    Galahad: <Is this what death’s realm is like? Everyone floating and ghosty? Oh well, despite the fact beating up a ghost sounds like an excellent way to waste time and be unproductive, it’s what I have to do today!> -Freezes one of his ghostly paws and tries punching the Gourgeist again-

    Galahad used Ice Punch!

    Gourgeist: <THE COLD! IT BURNS!>

    Bloop: <Bloop!>

    Jacob: “Okay then, Galahad, Play Rough!”

    Galahad: <Ooooouch, but fine!> -Rushes at the Goodra and, not too roughly so as not to incite more pain from the burn, but fairly rough, plays with the Goodra-

    Galahad took damage from his burn!

    -The Goodra falls to slightly less than half health, and roars-

    Diantha: 'Fire Blast, again!”

    -It fires more bloop and bloops more fire.-

    -He is then rendered speechless as Galahad is swiftly knocked out-

    “.... Ah. I see. It’s come to this then. My last Pokémon and yours. …. Huh. This will be interesting. No wonder you’ve been called such a talented Trainer. Having achieved Mega Evolution and being the Champion speaks volumes on both your kindness and closeness to your team and your talent.” -Smiles and shrugs-

    “In that case I suppose I have no choice but to rise to meet this challenge. Galahad, return! Go, Regina!” -He recalls Galahad and sends out Regina-

    Regina: -Looks over Diantha’s Gardevoir- <.... Like the dress. This’ll be fun, won’t it?>

    Jacob: “Indeed. Let’s see what we can do. This is where the game gets fun."

    "Regina! Let’s see what we can do, Calm Mind!”

    Regina: -Nods, closes her eyes and takes a deep breath, focusing herself and the energy inside of her, knowing getting excited now could make her slip up, and a single mistake here could undo the whole battle-

    Diantha: “You, too, Gardevoir. Calm Mind.”

    Mega Gardevoir: -focuses as well, planning and envisioning her success and whatever else might happen to motivate her to utterly destroy her enemy.-

    -...-

    -Did I say that? I meant she's definitely focusing on ponies and rainbows.-

    Jacob: “Okay…. Regina Trick Room!”

    Regina: -Her eyes snap open- <Right!> -She extends her arms and from them reality seems to warp and twists for a while before the distortion expands and completely engulfs the room-

    Regina used Trick Room!

    Diantha: “Interesting tactic… Shadow Ball!”

    Mega Gardevoir: -opens her eyes as well, an orb of Obscura forming rather slowly in her hands and being thrown similarly slowly-

    Gardevoir used Shadow Ball!

    Regina: -Is already aware of what Jacob is going to ask her to do next, so she does not move as she focuses, being hit by the Shadow Ball and barely holding on, but managing to do so-

    Jacob: “That was your one move. I’m glad I thought of it in this order, Regina, Wonder Room!”

    Regina: -Another distortion expands over the room, switching the Gardevoir’s natural defenses-

    Regina used Wonder Room!

    Diantha: “Hmm… Thunderbolt.”

    -Gardevoir attempts to strike Regina with lightning, which itself is slowed because Trick Room laughs at the speed of light-

    Jacob: Now, end it!

    Regina: -Narrowly dodges under and forwards, missing the tendrils of lightning just barely, before more quickly forming a ball of shadow-y obscura in her hands-

    Jacob: “Shadow Ball!”

    Regina: -Hurls the blob at the Mega Gardevoir, thrusting herself forward across the field doing so-

    Regina used Shadow Ball!

    -Mega Gardevoir is hit, staggering.-

    Mega Gardevoir: <Wha— you— how— >

    Gardevoir fainted!

    Diantha: "....." -Smiles and claps slowly-

    "Clever, impressive, and good job all around. You understood your Pokémon and they trusted you, and you knew how to fight alongside them. And it led you to a well deserved victory. Good job, Jacob. It was a good match, and you won. You know how it all feels, that positive hope for the future and the warm feelings that fill your heart... Those things can never be exhausted. They will never run out. Not ever. As long as you continue to live and walk towards that future you see, that I see that you see."

    Jacob: "It... well... yes. I suppose... there's nothing else I can say to that. But... thank you. It was fun."

    Diantha: -Smiles- "Come along, then Jacob. You know where we're going next."

    Jacob: "The Hall of fame...."

    Diantha: -Nods- "The Hall of Fame." -And on cue, the center platform shudders, then starts rising-

    Regina: -Looks over at Jacob and smiles-

    Jacob: "...."
