    Eridanus: Use Bite and finish Sneasel off!

    Wulfric: Block him with Metal Claw!

    As Sneasel's steel claws gleamed with reflected light, Fox opened its pincers to attack. The attacks clashes, putting both in an impasse... and then Fox threw away Sneasel.

    Sneasel...knocked out. Fox barely holds on.

    Wulfric: Humph. One down. But yours doesn't look it's going to go on much longer, either! So I'd say it's close to being even. Go, Snover!

    As Snover came out, a wave of snow appeared out of nowhere.

    Forge: <And that's Snow Warning for you. Watch out for the hail!>

    Fox: <Hail... Won't go much longer with hail around. I guess I can demonstrate a trick I learned!>

    Eridanus: ...Snow Warning.

    Wulfric: I see you're familiar with this Ability? Good. Let's see what you'll do.

    Eridanus: Hail will likely finish him off, so we need to go all out. Show what you learned! Rock Slide!

    Wulfric: In that case... use Ingrain to heal yourself!

    Snover firmly planted its roots into the floor, making it heal itself once in a while. Meanwhile, Fox summoned rocks from a ripple in the air, hitting Snover.

    And then the hail stroke, knocking him down.

    Eridanus: Good job, Fox. Now rest. And your replacement will be... Vivi! Go show them your best, canine!

    Vivi: <Woof woof! Oh look, it's all ice around here! Sparkly!>

    Eridanus: Use Flame Charge and build up speed!

    Wulfric: Loose an Energy Ball at this fox and stop it!

    The Snover put its hands together and formed a ball of green swirly energy, then tossed at Vivi. He meanwhile charged at Snover, and became covered in flames. The projectile collided, but he still rushed towards Snover, and crashed into him, then rushed back.

    After he finished the attack, there was faint pink glow surrounding him. Then the hail stroke, but not strong enough to knock him down.

    Eridanus: Use Flame Charge again to build up even more speed!

    Wulfric: That Fennekin gained speed... Use Ice Shard and take priority!

    As Vivi rushed at the Snover, noticeably faster... but Snover used ice shatters lying around all the place, and threw them at very fast velocity, even greater than the Vivi's. This didn't do too much, and Snover ended up being knocked out by the crash. Again, Vivi's Speed rose.

    Wulfric: Two down. I guess you know how to deal with Ice-type. Kudos to you on that! But I still have my last Pokemon. Vanillish! Let's end this together, my friend!

    Vivi: <Looks like an ice cone! ...I am hungry.>

    Denshi: <Never seen that before! It really looks like an ice cream.>

    Eridanus: Well, that's the last one. We need to go all we have! Use Heat Wave and hope for best!

    Wulfric: Ain't gonna work this time, buddy. Avalanche!

    As Vivi used its stick to summon a blow of hot wind, Vanillish prepared its attack... and dropped large pile of snow via ripple on Vivi, knocking him out. But then, it started to glow red! It's burned!

    The hail stroke it. While Ice-type do not really care about it, this Vanillish seemed to absorb the hail, regained the HP lost via burn.

    Eridanus: And once again... I am left with only one Pokemon to finish. That leaves... Forge.

    Forge: <Well, that's third Gym I partake in. Guess I need to prove my skills, or something.>

    Eridanus: It's the last finish, so it'll be straightforward... Thunderbolt!

    Wulfric: Counter the jolt of electricity with Ice Beam!

    As Vanillish took in a breath and releases ray of cold energy, Forge released jolt of electricity... Both attacks partially got through each other, dealing damage.

    The hail stroke, the burn damage got negated by Ice Body.

    Eridanus: One more time. Thunderboltttt!

    Wulfric: ...I guess this is where the fun ends. Ice Beam for the last move!

    The two attacks went against each other once more, pierced through... and caused explosion. Leaving Vanillish knocked out.

    Wulfric: Well, that was a good battle. Take this!

    Eridanus obtained the Iceberg Badge!

    Wulfric: And this!

    Eridanus obtained TM 13 Ice Beam!

    Wulfric: You may know or not, pal, but what I just gave is the Iceberg Badge and TM 13, containing Ice Beam. Ice Beam is quite powerful, as you've just seen! It can freeze solid your enemy, too.

    Eridanus: Thank you for this battle. Guess I can go ahead.

    Wulfric: Before you leave... Y'know what? I can tell that you're on rather good terms with your team. Especially this lil' mouse over here, am I right?

    Eridanus: Can't say no or deny.

    Wulfric: It's good to see you're friendly towards your team, as not everyone is in these weird times. I hope you will do well on your journey. May we meet again.
