    -A man is standing on the edge of the port with an Infernape at his side, wearing a clean white lab coat and watching the ships come into the harbour. He has his eyes focused on one ferry in particular, which comes into port nearby, disgorging a crowd of passengers onto the dock.     Titan: Deimos. It has been a while.

    Deimos: Yeah, it bloody well has. Probably would've gotten here sooner if I hadn't had to switch to the damn ferry. And lugging this wheelchair around with me is a right pain.

    Titan: That is something which we can remedy later. For now, though, there is much to talk about. Come, walk with me a while.

    -Deimos glares at Titan.-

    Deimos: ...Is that some kind of fucking joke?

    Titan: No. I apologise, that remark was unintentional and in poor taste. But you understand my meaning. Follow me. Omega, come along as well.

    -The Infernape nods and lopes behind the two as they disappear from notice underneath Titan's newly summoned SEP field.-

    Deimos: So, we're back to working in Orre, are we? Shit, I hate this place.

    Titan: I wouldn't say that. I have always found the desert to provide something of a calming atmosphere. Almost mediative.

    Deimos: Whatever. So, what's the plan?

    Titan: The "plan"? The "plan" is the same as it has always been.

    Deimos: Well, yeah, I know that. But how exactly are you planning on going about it now that our operations in Unova are dead and buried?

    Titan: Step one is to find the resources and personnel required to restart our operations. Thankfully, that has been taken care of, courtesy of our new associates.

    Deimos: So we're working with Cipher now?

    Titan: Not working with. Working for.

    Deimos: What?

    Titan: My contacts were generous enough to provide me with a position as a Cipher Commander. Whilst I have some degree of autonomy, I am still required to answer to-

    Deimos: Whoa, whoa, slow down there! What's all this bullshit about you working as a Commander? Shouldn't you be an Admin or something? You know, one of the fuckers who's actually in charge of things?

    Titan: Cipher do not grant the position of Admin to anyone who walks in and asks for it. I was lucky to be given so much as the title of Commander, considering that I have no history of prior service with the organisation. Besides, I am still an officer, just one who answers to those higher on the chain of command. And after all, did you and I not both work for Cyrus at one point?

    Deimos: Well, yeah, but-

    Titan: Precisely. I am in ideal position to make use of the facilities and resources that Cipher provides. And whilst I may find some of the Admins' methodology to be... questionable, my immediate superior is one I have no issues with. Indeed, our relationship is closer to a partnership than anything.

    Deimos: So you've been sucking up to the new boss already. What's he like?

    Titan: I never specified that they were a he.

    Deimos: Well, are they?

    Titan: ...Yes.

    Deimos: Then what was the fucking point of-

    Titan: I was simply pointing out the fact that you were making assumptions. In any case, our new employer's main skill lies in his talent for acquiring goods whilst avoiding the notice of their original owners.

    Deimos: Thievery, in other words.

    Titan: I very much doubt that he would use those words, but that is indeed what it is. An ability that we can make good use of, certainly. Having said that, he is certainly an... interesting individual. To describe him as eccentric would be something of an understatement. He is in Holon currently. At least, I think he is. In any case, I am by default in charge of affairs until he returns. He has a tendency to disappear like this.

    -The faintest hint of a smile appears on Titan's face.-

    Titan: You will almost certainly hate him.
