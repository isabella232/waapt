    Puppet: No living thing could go up there to fetch the crown and survive, but I could. Maybe. Elizabeth wasn't having any of it, though. She said that she didn't want me to get hurt, so she went in herself, and...

    -he trails off, shuffling his feet. His pale wooden skin appears to be gathering some sort of grayish, metallic dust-

    Joseph: ...look. I don't know how this is going to affect you, Puppet, and you've been good to us for leading us through the factory, but I need you to go up and get her. I'm not ordering you, I'm asking you. It's going to hurt you a lot, I can tell, but I need you to. Please?

    -Puppet stares at Joseph, the effect of a doppelganger staring back at him unnerving the red-clad boy. Puppet heaves a sigh-

    Puppet: Oh, alright, then.

    -he trudges up the stairs, slower than usual for Puppet. After what seems like an eternity of wooden steps hitting metal stairs, he reaches the top, grabbing Elizabeth's ankles-

    Puppet: See you don't bump your head...
