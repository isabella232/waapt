    Anistar

    The radio feed emanating from the IPSS halted. Locke stood in solemn silence, a tear leaking from the corner of his ear. However, the teenaged girl, a red headed hologram on his X-Transciever, was openly sobbing.

    Locke and the others were all outside the cafe, now. He was standing a bit apart from them all for the moment, a little bit of privacy was necessary.

    Finally, Locke said to the hologram:"<...I'm sorry you had to hear that, Maria, but I thought that you had a right to know this much at least."

    Maria:(sniffling a bit):"..Yeah, I guess. Oh, Arceus, why...?"

    Locke:"....I don't know, Maria. People die, and that's a part of life, no matter how awful it is. I know how you feel, believe me, I've been in the exact same boat as you are now."

    Maria looked at Locke sadly."Empathy is a strange thing, isn't it?"

    Lock:"Yeah.I'm not gonna sugarcoat it, I don't believe in that very much. They're gone, nothing can change that. What's important, however, is how you remember them. They died with your safety on their minds, Maria.As Geo would say if he wasn't in the Pokeball;Honor that memory, and honor them by doing so."

    Maria:(she wiped her eyes, and nodded.):"...You're right."

    An extra arm entered the hologram, and then a whole body, as a brown haired girl around Maria's age came into the hologram and hugged Maria tightly."It's going to be okay, Mar. It''s going to be okay." Maria hugged back even tighter, crying.

    The brown haired girl kissed her, and then gently said:"I'm here for you, Maria, we all are. Do you want to lie down? I'll make you some hot chocolate, and then we can do whatever you want. Except for.." she whispered into Maria's ear, who actually started laughing. Locke smiled at the sight.

    Maria smacked the brown haired girl a bit, and then kissed her again, smiling."Ray, you doof!" She then looked at both of them and said:"...Thank you, both of you." Ray smiled and gently pushed her, and Locke waved back as Maria left the hologram, but before she did, he said:"You were there when it happened to me, for me not to be there for you when it happens to you would make me a huge sleazeball." Maria smiled, with a twinge of melancholy,and waved to him as she left, with only Ray left in the hologram.

    Locke:"She's going to need you now more than ever, Ray."

    Ray:"I know. I have a favor to ask."

    Locke:"What is it?"

    Ray:"For Maria...hunt that bastard down."

    Locke:"You don't need to tell me that. That's what I've been planning to do ever since I heard about it.'''

    The call ended, and Locke straightened up and started walking towards Robin and Zappa. As he passed them, he clapped both of them on the shoulder and got into the drivers seat of the jeep."Thank you again, both of you, for being ready to fight alongside me.Get as ready as you want, friends. For this time, we hunt ghosts."

    Does this count as any sort of Crowning moment?
