    The Beach Part IV: A New Hope

    Levi comes back with a massive, massive pile of various fried food goods.

    Levi: Sup nerds, I'm back with the goods

    Vyvyan and Santiago blink.

    Santiago: ...Where on earth are you gonna put all that Levi?

    Vyvyan: Sand in food is horrible and no-one likes it.

    Levi: Glad you asked.

    Suddenly picnic blanket next to Levi's empty lounger.

    Levi: I always come prepared.

    Santiago: Oi.. That grease stain is going to be a killer to get out @_@

    Vyvyan: That's going to be Levi's job, not ours.

    Levi places the food on the blanket and flops onto his lounger. Sighing in content with a giddy smile on his face.

    Levi: -Sticking his hand in the fried food pile and pulling out a fry- I'm telling you guys. This beach day right here? This is the life -Closing his eyes in content and tossing the fry in mouth and chews- Mmmm~

    ???: *chrip*

    Levi's eyes snap open as he sees colony upon colony of Wingulls and Pelippers staring straight at his food pile, Irises shrinking and eyelids twitching

    A cut later

    Levi is perched atop his fried goods pile like a dragon on it's hoard, hissing and snarling like a cat at any seabird that dares venture near his treasures.

    Vyvyan: Levi, get off of that. You're going to get a burn Not to mention how unsanitary that is. And you're going to put that in your mouth? Yeesh.

    Levi: No.

    Meanwhile, Santiago is busy cuddling and petting the Wingulls and Pelippers around and on him.

    Santiago: Aw, Wingulls and Pelippers aren't bad! They're cute and friendly! You see, back in the old country, we used to keep Pelippers and Wingulls as companions! They were a fisherman's best friend!

    [Snuggling Intensifies]

    Wingull Line Cuddlepile: ♥

    Santiago: ♥ (I'm calling you Raúl and you Julio...)

    Levi: Gulls are nothing short of pure, unadulterated    EVIL   

    Santiago: Why are you so-

    Levi:    THE BASTARDS STOLE MY FRIES, THE SELFISH PRICKS   

    Slateport - Around two months back

    Levi is sitting on the pier with a small cup of fries, humming to himself.

    Levi: We all need to treat ourselves, so what better treatment that a double-deep fried, extra salted Topo based starch stick~

    Levi is about to place a fry in his mouth, when a Wingull comes down and snatches the fry out of his fingers.

    Levi: -Bites down on nothing-

    Wingull: *chirp*

    Levi's eyes glow red as dark clouds and lightning strike form on the horizon.

    Back to the TS'd present

    Vyvyan: Oh stop being a drama king, Levi.

    Levi: No. Now I'm going to enjoy my Onion Rings WITHOUT being harassed by little thieves

    Levi places an "onion" ring in his mouth and chews.

    Slowly.

    Levi: These Onion Rings taste funky...

    Santiago: You're angry at them now, but over time - as members among your subjects, you'll find a place for them in due time, won't you Levi?

    ...Levi?

    Levi's face has gone blue.

    Vyvyan: What now?

    Santiago tilts his head and walks over to the pile, and tries one of the "onion" rings for himself.

    Santiago: These are calamari rings.

    Santiago looks back up at Levi.

    Vyvyan: So either eating Calamari triggers a transformation reaction-

    Santiago: Or he stopped breathing.

    Santiago and Vyvyan exchange glances.

    Vyvyan: Hospital?

    Santiago: Hospital.

    A while later outside the Slateport Hospital.

    The three walk out of the hospital doors. Levi with a scowl on his face and Santiago laughing like a big dofus

    Levi: It's not funny, Sandy.

    Santiago: I know, but still *snerk* I can't believe the Sea God of all creatures is allergic to seafood! HAH!

    Levi: Hey! That means I have to respect all creatures under my domain!

    ... Still, I can't believe I had those things in my mouth! Urgh.

    Levi sticks out his tongue and begins brushing it with his fingers.

    Levi: Urgh, I feel so unclean now.

    Vyvyan: Regardless, now we know for the future.

    Santiago: Well, I want to go back to Mount Chimney. You guys fine with that?

    Vyvyan: I'm fine with that

    Levi: Have no qualms.

    Vyvyan: Well then, to Mt Chimney we go.

    The trio head on out to Route 110 en route to Mt Chimney.

    That's all, folks!
