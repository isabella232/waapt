    "Lyuri... I never really thought much about it until the festival, I never really thought about human and Pokémon relationships, even less of you of all people doing it, I guess that it's because of that that I thought our relationship was like the one Seth and Sakura have, maybe it's because I never thought it could go any farther than that." he said, now putting his hands behind his head "You know, you're one of the most important persons I've ever met in my life, if not the most important, so, of course I want to make you happy."

    "But are you being ho-"

    "I'm being as honest as possible, Lyuri, I know that I'm very slow when it comes to noticing romantic feelings, even considering my Aura sense, but that doesn't mean that I don't feel anything for you. Sure, at least for now, I may not be one of the best boyfriends and be rather insensible towards you at some points, but it's because this is a completely new experience for me, I never thought anyone would develop feelings for me since the festival." he continued, giving a few chuckles as he now was holding Lyuri's hands.

    "Funny, you've been attracting some attention for a while now, I'm pretty sure other girls would kill to be your girlfriend..." said Lyuri, with a small, if rather sad, smile.

    "That may be the case now, yes, but I can assure you: I'm never going to leave your side no matter what happens, that's just how much I like you." he said, with a honest tone and giving a small pause as he starts to think for a bit and runs his hand through her hair as he began to talk again "I'm not doing this not just because I want you to be happy, but also because I'm also happy being your boyfriend, because I also love you."

    "I... I really don't deserve you, you know." said Lyuri, now getting a bit teary-eyed as she gave a smile

    "Really, after those years we spent together and all the care you had for me and the others, I'm the one that's worried that I may not be worthy of you." responded Duke, with a chuckle "You're not only a very good looking girl, you're also a fun person to hang around with, even if you do tend to be rather aggressive at times, I know that it's just an act that you sometimes take too far, because I'm well aware of the real you and, personally, I don't see why you have to keep the tough facade, you're strong enough just being yourself."

    "... Yeah, you may be right, sometimes I wonder if people are going to see me as weak if don't keep it going, but I suppose there's nothing to worry about, especially when I see my sister, maybe it'll actually be better if I just stop trying to act so tough." said Lyuri, now with a wide smile on her face "Thanks Duke, I'll think about it, seriously."

    "All right, that's good enough for me... So... Any remaining doubts about my feelings towards you?" asked Duke, with a slightly playful smile.

    "W-Well... It's a lot of info to process and-"

    Duke then gave a pulled Lyuri towards him slightly to shorten the distance between them and then gives Lyuri a kiss, one that caught her by surprise, however, she was more than fine with it, the two kept it going for a few seconds before breaking the kiss.

    "And now?" asked Duke, with a smile and a few chuckles.

    "I'm not even going to say anything anymore." said Lyuri, with a very happy and wide smile as she hugged Duke as tightly as she could.

    Duke then hugged her back, gently "Should we go watch the stars now?" he asked, enjoying the moment.

    "All right, but just wait a bit." said Lyuri, trying to enjoy the moment as much as she could.

    In the meantime, the Deerling was just watching everything from afar, looking very happy for the couple and trying to remain quiet.
