    Vyvyan: Oh for heaven's sake

    He is greeted by the sight of Levi flopped on the bed, sprawled unceremoniously, with his face buried in a pillow and his own Personal Raincloud raining down him.

    Most of the room is waterlogged now.

    Vyvyan: -Shaking the water off his boots- I hope we don't get charged for this. Are you still moping around Levi?

    Levi: Go awaaaaaaaaaaaaay... Can't you let me wallow in misery in peace?

    Vyvyan: Considering what happened at S- on the abandoned rig, I don't think that's a good idea.

    Levi: Poooint... *sniff*

    Vyvyan: Besides, you're gonna eventually flood the hotel if you keep whimpering like this. Or at least make everything soggy.

    I think you need a change of scenery.

    Vyvyan goes over to pick up and move the whale boy. Due to his rather small and light human frame, it's a task that's not too difficult to achieve.

    Levi: Nooooooooooo...

    His heart isn't in it for the crashing and thrashing a that would be typical of his personality as Vyvyan carries him to the porch.

    Vyvyan: He's really done a number on you, hasn't he?

    A slow nod and a whine is all Vyvyan gets in response.

    Vyvyan: Maybe some fresh air will do you good

    He plonks Levi down on one of the deck recliners.

    Levi: And the big ol' softy deciding now to go and rekindle ties with his old family ain't helpin matters much.

    Vyvyan: Hey, this time of year is really important for the old fella. Besides, you can lean on yer sis for emotional support.

    Levi: Touché.

    But what about you? Compared to Sandy and me, you don't seem to have the closest relationship with your folks.

    ...in fact, you barely talk about 'em. Why is that?

    Vyvyan: That is a talk for another time.

    Vyvyan sighs.

    Vyvyan: Hey, Levi?

    Levi: Yeah stickler?

    Vyvyan: What are we gonna do with eternity? I mean, what niche are we gonna fill?

    Levi shrugs in response.

    Levi: Dunno, be the Groundon/Kyogre/Rayquaza trio we kinda are?

    Vyvyan: Yeah, but there exists one of those already. And they're stronger and more experienced than us too.

    Vyv sighs

    Vyvyan: I dunno, ever since I've come to terms with my immortality, I just seem to be plagued with more questions...

    Levi waves his hand in response

    Levi: I wouldn't worry about it kid. I mean, if we were truly outclassed and useless the higher ups would have retconned us out of existence by now. -Makes a pew noise- Gaster style

    Vyvyan sinks into his own chair

    Vyvyan: Maybe I should stop complaining and just be thankful for the lot I've been given

    Levi: Nah mate.

    Vyvyan: ?

    Levi: Think! Ask! Question! Argue! Haggle! Complain! Heck, even wrestle with the powers that be and tell 'em you won't be taken for a chump! Know yer place, yeah. But never stop questioning anything, taking shit at face value and never asking crap about it is boring and leads to stagnation if ya ask me.

    Vyvyan: ...

    ...When have you ever dished out good life advice, Levi?

    Levi shrugs with a smirk.

    Levi: I don't know all to much about a lot of things, but you'll find that I have a few pearls of wisdom lodged in my knuckleheaded brain that come out from time to time.

    Vyvyan: I'm guessing that following your advice on the regular is a bad idea.
