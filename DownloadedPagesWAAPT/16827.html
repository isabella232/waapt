    The eternal dusk returns
    This post is part of my effort to make part of Chrome's backstory gel better with canon. I don't outright regret the base idea, (if I did I would have rettconed it into something completely different) it's just when I first did it I was like "I'm adapting from a different canon with different rules, I can do whatever I want!" but later on I was like "Er, no." Except with more paranoia I'd inadvertently made Chrome into a Sue. And more legitimate concerns I'd inadvertently ruined something ''really frigging important about a key element with my initial handling.

    That said... I'm developing a fic idea for after WAAPT that gives Chrome's time with the Aura Guardians a lot more context, and my amendments to his backstory are just as much about the idea evolving in my head as the need to fix things! With that in mind I'm introducing one of the fic's major characters to WAAPT as of this post. I've had fun plotting her out, and I hope you guys enjoy her.

    tl;dr I'm trying to handle something I did early in my RP career a bit better + build up to a future idea; have a new character that helps with both.

    Somewhere

    - Lina, Arika, and Human!Chrome are relaxing at a cafe and talking; Nico is nearby playing Fire Emblem Awakening-

    -Eventually he looks over to the other three and taps one on the shoulder-

    Nico: Hey Chrome?

    Human!Chrome: Hmm?

    Nico: You said a couple times you knew Sir Aaron and his Lucario way back when; were you... Partners?

    Human!Chrome: ...Actually no. Sure, we were pretty close, but they were always far closer friends with each other than they ever were with me.

    Nico: Then who was your partner back then? I know it was before Lina was born...

    Arika: He's told us before! A lady with Psychic powers named Polly.

    Lina: She and him got into quite a bit in their time.

    Human!Chrome: Oh yes, Polly and I had... Interesting adventures...

    About A Thousand Years Ago

    -A familiar Metagross and a blonde woman in Aura Guardian gear are fighting a horde of Mankey-

    Metagross: -flings several Mankey aside with a Psychic- <Tell me how we got into this situation again?>

    Polly: -thwacks several Mankey aside with her staff- I'm pretty sure it was your fault.

    Metagross: -Meteor Mashes more Mankey away- <And I'm pretty sure you were the one who wanted to destroy that cursed relic in favor of the other Aura Guardians who volunteered.>

    Polly: -Psycho Cuts more Mankey away- Tell me, then, if you already knew the answer to your question why did you ask it?

    Metagross: <To mess with you, of course.>

    Polly: Ha ha. -Backhands a Primeape-

    Now

    Human!Chrome: ...We were kinda like the platonic equivalent of an old married couple. Except with more asskicking.

    Arika: -smirks- So you and me in a few years?

    Human!Chrome: ...Maybe. I have mixed feelings on that.

    Lina: -thumbs to herself and also smirks- Oi. He's my asskicking partner.

    Human!Chrome: You two be quiet. >_<

    -Nico snerks, though at the same time thinks a bit to himself-

    [[pnk:Nico:]] Weird... This Polly lady sounds oddly... Resonant...

    Thoughts of Polly yourselves?

    You know, I kinda wish for a few things. First, I wish my internet didn't suck. Second, I wish my dad would tell me about stuff in advance. With both of those in mind, here's another episode of Dune's Gaiden Corner.

    Dunescape, Tower

    The Lorekeeper: You know, I can't help but feel that I should be reminded of something, here...

    The Pilot: Why is that?

    The Lorekeeper: Well, as we've been preparing for this last battle, you and your Weapon have gotten a lot... brighter. And the Heroine has been... well...

    The Pilot: ... I see what you mean. You think the two are connected?

    The Lorekeeper: I'm not sure what to think just yet. Every time I try and broach the question to the Captain, he gets... evasive. Like when I ask about the supposed "Frozen King" that's come out of freaking nowhere.

    The Pilot: Yeah, what's his deal? He says he's "the manifestation of anguish and frustration", but he's only become active recently... was it because I was taking in all of it until recently, and it needed a new place to "pool"?

    The Lorekeeper: Now, that's on the assumption that he's a new character in this whole scheme of things.

    The Pilot: What do you mean?

    The Lorekeeper: Well, think of it this way: What if, and bear with me here, he's been here, the whole time? Laying dormant, either to protect himself from the    Thing    or because you had come along and began usurping what gave him purpose?

    The Pilot: What inspired this theory?

    The Lorekeeper: Earlier, on a whim related to Deziree's recent difficulties...

    The Pilot: Ugh... I wish there was more we could do for her...

    The Lorekeeper: ... I tried looking through some of our own nasty memories. Despite the Scribe's assistance, I could not find certain, highly traumatic memories. Such as the last... incident with Mother, among several others. We know the memory exists, we know what took place, but we still couldn't find it.

    The Pilot: And?

    The Lorekeeper: Now link that back to my earlier theory as to whether the Frozen King is new or not.

    The Pilot: ... You think he's stealing these memories away.

    The Lorekeeper: Not stealing, no. I say that because everything in this mindscape has a purpose. This tower we stand upon is now, much like before the Invasion, the representation of civilization, as well as the new entry point for mental visitors.

    The Pilot: So... the Frozen King is supposed to cordon off painful memories? For what purpose?

    The Lorekeeper: I'm not one hundred percent sure myself, but I believe that he acts as... a sort of pus.

    The Pilot: Pus?

    The Lorekeeper: Bear with me here. Think as to what pus in a wound is supposed to do. I draws in infection, absorbs it, neutralizes it, and allows it to be cleaned out so the wound can heal.

    The Pilot: What sort of mentality would require something like that?

    The Lorekeeper: We've had to experience: a childhood of parental conflict bordering all-out war, our town being constantly harassed by a ruthless criminal organization, our social life at every turn be torpedoed because of our parents' shenanigans, the two of them having the audacity to disapprove of our lifestyle as if it was some teenage "phase", and then we get mind raped by an ancient abomination of evil. Someone without a way of mentally coping with that would not be as stable as we are now.
