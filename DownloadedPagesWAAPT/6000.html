    Route 34 - Johto

    <"zZz...">

    A Wild Abra is asleep in the shade of a tree, having chosen the spot both due to that the shade kept the annoying sunlight out of his face, and that he would be hard to spot by any potential predators.

    Not that predators were an especially huge problem, seeing as like any Abra, he could simply sense them coming and teleport far away to safety before they even came close. Even in a unconscious state, an Abra's psychic abilities were incredibly potent.

    As this Abra continued to doze, he began to pick up the psychic signature of a Pokémon not commonly found on the route, and it was trying to sneak up on him.

    A more thorough scan revealed this Pokémon in particular to be a huge river lizard. Perhaps one of the ones that lived in the swamps nearby? He had read much from the minds of the other Pokémon on the route about them. Particularly how one of them would devour anything that came her way...

    He wasn't entirely sure if it was that one or not - his psychic scans could only reveal so much at this stage of evolution - so he wasn't going to risk sitting there and leaving himself and easy target if it was her.

    So he would only wait until the river lizard was right on top of him before teleporting away. Sure, it was a risk...but a few more seconds of sleep was always worth the potential danger...

    Finally, an Abra! And this one didn't appeared to have noticed him.

    Lake had been trying to find one to ask for a quick Teleport to the Orange Islands, but the few he had managed to come across so far had teleported away from him before he could even approach them, let alone ask for help.

    However, as the Feraligatr began to approach this one, he was pleasantly surprised that it didn't teleport away from him. Maybe this time he would be lucky?

    Trying not to seem like an apex predator looking to swiftly devour him (no easy feat), Lake began to try and speak to the dozing Psychic Pokémon.

    Lake: <Um, hey. Would you mind if I asked for your help wi->

    Blip!

    <...>

    The Feraligatr twitched, feeling a mix of frustration and sheer RAGE building up inside of him. At that point, he was honestly tempted to go on a rampage. Knock a few trees over, smash some boulders, carve some chunks out of the ground...

    But he wasn't that sort of Feraligatr, so settled for letting out a bellowing roar, then taking out his frustration on a the tree the Abra had been sleeping under by clamping his jaws down hard on the bark.

    Biting things always made him feel a little better.

    Lake angrily chewed on the tree bark for a few seconds before letting go, spitting a few broken pieces of bark out of his mouth afterwards. Blurgh. Wood tasted horrible.

    He didn't feel so angry now, but he was still fed up. Letting out a long sigh, Lake sat down where the Abra had been just a few moments ago and leaned back against the tree, feeling rather sorry for himself.

    Since the fiasco with Blitz trying to forcibly steal the spotlight from him a few days back, Lake hadn't had any luck finding a means of getting to the Orange Islands, and his repeated failings were beginning to weigh down his confidence.

    As much as he wanted to find Tempest, and hopefully learn where his family was, he was starting to doubt whether he could do it by himself. Perhaps he should have taken Tracer's offer for help?

    It wasn't like there was any way he could get to the Orange Islands under his own power. It was too far to swim, he couldn't take a boat or anything like that, and many of the other potential ideas he had come up with were just silly.

    (Although he had practiced flying with his Air Balloon a few times, but he had given up on that idea when he found that he could only move through the air at a Shuckle's pace without outside assistance from a certain Salamence or another flying Pokémon.)

    So, it seemed like that he was going to need help with this one...
