    Sophian Road - Oblivia

    Comet — <Okay, so let me get this straight: you met this dude called Vyvyan at Mt. Pyre.>

    Stratus — Nods. <Yep.>

    Comet — <He was once a human, but Team Ozone captured him and turned him into a Rayquaza.>

    Stratus — <Yeah.>

    Comet — <Not only that, but he also had a Baby Groudon and Kyogre with him, too?>

    Stratus — Nods. <Buggle and Depthcharge.>

    Comet — <And this apparently was all going on in Hoenn while our backs were turned?>

    Stratus — <I think so.>

    Comet pauses, exchanging a glance with Lake, Woolly Treeo, and myself, who are also listening to Stratus' explanation.

    Lake — <If this is the sort of thing that happens behind our backs...> Sighs. <...Then we really need to pay more attention. Bad things happen, otherwise.>

    Comet — <Hey, I don't think it's that we weren't paying attention. If you ask me, we didn't know because nobody else bothered to even tell us this was going on.>

    Treeo — Rubs the back of his head. <To be fair, Comet, we did spend most of the time off by ourselves or with Tracer. So maybe they just didn't have the chance to let us know?>

    Comet — Snorts, crossing her arms. <Luke has a cell-phone, doesn't he? So why couldn't they have sent him a text that said something like "Hey, there's this guy who's now a Rayquaza, just thought you should know?">

    "I dunno if that would work, I think there might be something wrong with my plan or something. I've been getting texts that claim my soul is now forfeit or something."

    Woolly — <Well, the whole thing with Sierra and Vyvyan is cute.>

    Comet — Blinks. <...Cute?>

    Woolly — <I mean, it's the classic love story, isn't it? Boy meets girl, boy falls in love with girl, boy is kidnapped and turned into a sky deity, boy escapes from his kidnappers, boy and girl live happily ever after!>

    Lake — <Well, I suppose it is kinda' sweet when you think about it.>

    Comet — <What kind of love stories have you been reading?>

    Treeo — <One more thing that's been worrying me, though. Stratus, Rayquaza told you that the only for them to stop the volcano was for him, Groudon and Kyogre to...?>

    Stratus says nothing, simply nodding glumly.

    Lake — <...So...that means they're-?>

    Stratus — <...Gone...>

    A horrible silence falls over the group, none of us not sure what to say. After a few moments, however, Lake gets up, goes over to Stratus, and gives him a hug. The Dragonite says nothing, simply hugging the Feraligatr back.

    Comet — <shouldn't Groudon have been able to handle a supervolcano by himself? Without dying?>

    Treeo — <Apparently not.>
