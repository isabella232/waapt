    Pakhet: Pakhet: ~Seems Mummy and his kook are back in town, and are planning a bigger bonfire than our grog induced party~

    Cleo: True, Mohummad is a spineless, whiny spoiled sport. But that Delphox that hangs around him like a bad stench always seemed troublesome to me. I wouldn't be surprised if he offed Mohummad by now and went straight for the kill. Or arson. Knowing them.

    Cleo: That would be appreciated. You may leave now.

    Pakhet places her paws on her hips and rocks playfully.

    Pakhet: ~So cold. What happened to the untempered fiery spirit of yours~? Anyway, Monty?

    Pakhet: <Let's go be watchdogs~>

    Montu: <Lead the way, your highness~>

    The two saunter out of the room.
