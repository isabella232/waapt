    437146 Uilleam2nd Jan 2015 10:26:17 AM from Bonnie Scotland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Pining for the fjords
    little silver knives

    11:59 AM, New Year's Eve

    (In the far corner of a rather large house is an incubator containing a black and red egg.)

    ???: ...zor?

    (Well, okay. The remains of a black and red egg.)

    Jakobsson family manor

    (As the little dark fox crawls his way through an air vent, he hears the distant sound of bells chiming, followed by a muffled cacophony of overjoyed human voices.)

    Zorua: ?

    The dining room

    (All the most important white-collar employees of Jakobsson Inc are gathered around a huge rectangular table, clicking glasses with every other person they can reach as the bells on the giant plasma TV screen chime midnight.)

    Employees: SKAAL!

    (The cries of "skaal" undulate across the table in both directions for a minute or so. As they die down, the man at the far right end - a broad-shouldered, middle-aged blond with a prominent beard - stands up and clears his throat into a megaphone.)

    Mikael Jakobsson, Sr.: A-hem!

    (murmur, murmur)

    Mikael: Settle down, settle down! Now, New Year's Day is a time of merriment, and we here at Jakobsson, Inc have every right to be merry - business is booming! Our early forays into the Holocaster market have had an excellent reception so far, in spite of a certain group of fashion disasters trying their hardest to ruin it for us...

    (General chuckling and nudging.)

    As for familial matters, I'm pleased to announce that my eldest son is finally undergoing the training he needs to succeed me in the future!

    (Mikki tries half-heartedly to look happy, but...)

    Mikkiscape

    Whichever Aspect of his controls work ethic, I'm not sure I haven't thought about what this place would be like: (trapped under a pile of crumpled research papers) Kill me.

    Papers:    Later.   

    Back to reality

    Mikael: And as for all of you, without whom none of this would be possible? Well, what can I say? Happy new year!

    (The ensuing cheers are easily loud enough to mask the sound of tiny paws falling from the air vent and scampering away.)

    Jakobsson Manor Garden

    (A male Aggron - normal-sized, I hasten to add - patrols in front of the mansion's large door with a look of revulsion on his face.)

    Skyraker: <2015 already, and I only got to brutally eviscerate two things last year. What a waste of time.> >_>

    (He continues to pace and mutter about wanton destruction for about thirty seconds, only stopping when he hears a very faint sound...)

    ???: Zor~

    Skyraker: ...

    <GAAAAH INTRUDEEEEER>

    (And in an instant, the neatly-mowed lawn is torn up by a flurry of blades.)

    Skyraker: <COME HERE I ONLY WANNA RIP YOU TO SHREDS>

    (In his fit of bellowing fury, Skyraker never even comes close to noticing a tiny black quadruped scampering off down the garden path.)

    The next morning

    Alex: (peering out the window) ...what happened to the garden? o_O

    Svavel: <I always said he would snap someday, didn't I?>

    ("He" is the metal triceratops currently making half-hearted jabs at the desecrated earth with his fist.)

    Skyraker: <Guuuuuuhh...> @_@

    Petalburg Woods, around 12:10 AM

    (The Zorua is about to head off into the depths of the trees here, but stops when he hears voices.)

    Simon: Well, of all the terrible years I've had so far, last year was by far the worst.

    (The ragged-looking hipster is sitting on the stump of a recently felled tree, his Pokémon gathered around him. Whether or not they're paying any attention to him whatsoever is a different story.)

    Simon: I mean, aren't you supposed to celebrate your achievements on New Year's Day? What was there about 2014 to celebrate? Now, my sister and her boyfriend, I'm sure they have plenty to be cheerful about!

    (He gets up and starts to pace frantically, his tone rapidly growing angrier. The Zorua, not really used to the concept of rage, shrinks back slightly in fear.)

    Simon: No, don't focus on the fact that you had to ruin poor old Simon's life to improve your own! Nobody cares what happens to him, it's all fine, now let's go out and do whatever it is happy people do when they celebrate!

    Nimueh:    ~Don't you think you're being a bit ridiculous? Were you to head back to R'lyeh right this instant, I doubt anything would change.~   

    Simon: One, stop calling the university that. Two, I... I...

    (Simon pauses as the actual scale of his problems, or rather the lack thereof, tries to permeate his brain. Unfortunately for rational thought, he shrugs the sensation off.)

    Simon: I just know it'd be awful! Now stop patronising me!

    Nimueh:    ~Then stop needing it.~   

    (Simon sighs and plods over to a spot of long grass. When he aims a kick at it, a Zorua suddenly cries out in shock, leaps out of the way of his foot, and flees in the direction of Petalburg.)

    Simon: -exasperated- See? Even the wildlife hates me!

    (He turns to leave, heading for Rustboro. Most of his mons turn to follow, but his Honchkrow remains behind, working out something with lots of fancy motions on his Feather Fingers.)

    Corleone: <Petalburg Woods... wild Zorua...>

    (Cue sigh and facepalm.)

    Corleone: <Can ya tell he don't get out much?>

    Simon: (in the distance) It makes me sick, thinking about how overjoyed she must be...

    Petalburg, 12:15 AM

    Belinda: ...I don't feel happy, Thera.

    (The Liepard, who as usual is impossible to read and therefore is making determining whether or not she's being attentive difficult, is curled up beside Belinda on a hotel bed. Her trainer swings her legs off the side, looking downcast.)

    Belinda: I should be, but it's so hard! Am I a member of the J-Team now? Will I have to follow them? What if I have studies or something and I can't follow them? What about Jason?

    Thera: <Jason is an accomplished Auric with something like fifty other people watching his back. I'm sure he'll be fine.>

    Belinda: I suppose, but then there's still my biggest worry..

    (She waits for Thera to ask what this biggest worry is, but the Liepard has fallen back into her usual stony silence.)

    Belinda: (sigh) Simon. Okay, so we weren't exactly close at any point, but we're still siblings - heck, still twins. What did I do to make him hate me?

    (Thera actually shows a hint of emotion on her face. Exactly what is hard to determine, though.)

    Thera: <You took the one chance he's ever had to be in any way relevant and chucked it right back at him, and you know what? You didn't even mean it. It's his damn problem.>

    Belinda: ...I'm sorry, but that's not exactly helpfooh Zorua!

    (Indeed, the little Zorua has somehow made his way into the hotel and nudged open the door to Belinda's room. Grinning, she pads over and bends down to the babymon's level.)

    Belinda: Hai there! What's your name, sweetie? :3

    Zorua: ...zor?

    Belinda: Zor- oh, oh! You must still be a baby!

    (She picks him up and cradles him.)

    Belinda: And if a baby's here without a trainer, that means the poor widdle thing must be lost and alone... 3:

    Zorua: (confused) ...zor.

    (Without warning, he squirms out of Belinda's arms and runs off down the corridor...)

    Belinda: Hey, where are you going? D:

    Alex's room, the exact same hotel

    (...nudges open the door to this room, empty due to the presence of its inhabitants in the Jakobsson manor, curls up on the bed, and slowly drifts off to sleep.)
