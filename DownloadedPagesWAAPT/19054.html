    Marie is currently writing an article for her blog while Hikari is just playing around.

    "Did you know? There's an asteroid coming down! Isn't that awesome?!" asked Hikari, excitedly

    Marie turned on her chair to face Hikari, looking baffled "Imminent death is awesome?"

    "Not that! Imagine going to space and heroically destroy the threat to all life on our planet! I'm already starting to build my mecha for that!"

    Marie blinked a few times and then sighed "Hikari, what happened to you?"

    Hikari stopped, being a little taken aback "... Eh?"

    "I know a lot can change as time goes, but you're way too hyper and peppy." Marie sighed again "... You're faking it aren't you?"

    Hikari flinched for a moment "Of course not, it's just been a long time since we last saw each other. A lot happened since then!"

    "You're lying." Marie bluntly stated "When I first arrived, you were acting a lot differently than from now. You knew I would notice something was off, didn't you?"

    "U-Uh..."

    "Figured as much." Marie paused "... Have you ever talked to anyone else about your problems?"

    "I-I don't really have any problems." Hikari giggled a little then noticed Marie giving her a stern look "... Not buying it?"

    "Not in the slightest." Marie tapped her fingers on the chair's arm "Does your boyfriend actually know anything about you other than the basics?"

    "Uh... Well... He never asked..."

    Marie facepalmed "I'll just say this now. If you just pretend to be someone you aren't all the time while dating him, someday he's going to find out."

    She then turned back to the computer and went back to writing, meanwhile, Hikari just sat on the bed and sighed, there's a good amount of silence between them until someone broke it

    "You're actually really afraid of that asteroid aren't you?" asked Marie, still focused on her work

    "Yes..." said Hikari, now lacking any sort of energy while doing something on her phone.
