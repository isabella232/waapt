    Coumarine Gym

    Just hang in there, buddy. We're almost through.

    I'll pull through for you. I promise.

    Ramos: I've got one more for yeh, but I don't think yer bird can take out my last tough Pokemon before Toxic takes out yers.

    We'll see, old man.

    Ramos: Confidence! I like that, sprout. All right, then, here's my oldest friend. Go, Gogoat!

    -he sends out the tough-looking ram Pokemon, who eyes Clockwise-

    Gogoat: <You're in for more than you bargained for, bird.>

    Clockwise: <I like pancakes!> ^_^

    Gogoat: <... Hm.>

    Here we go.

    Clockwise! Use Psycho Shift!

    Ramos: Eh? Gogoat, Wild Charge!

    -Clockwise shoots upwards as high as he can reach in the library. His eyes glow brightly, and the Gogoat's do likewise. His wings beat a little stronger as his poison disappears and the Gogoat staggers a step as he is hit by the poison-

    -regardless of the poison, the Gogoat lights up his fur with sparks and starts leaping from wall to wall to get at Clockwise. While they watch him, Ramos furrows his brow-

    Ramos: What's that yeh've done to him?

    It's called Psycho Shift! Not many Pokemon can learn it, but the Noctowl line can. It heals status effects—by transferring them to the opponent.

    Ramos: … Ah. I think I understand yer earlier confidence, young'un. But my Gogoat is tough. We'll pull through.

    Clockwise and I are just as tough as you two! I believe in him, and he trusts me to lead him to victory!

    Ramos: We'll see, young'un! We'll see!

    -the Gogoat reaches Clockwise's level and hurtles towards him. Clockwise locks eyes with him, then folds his wings and drops like a stone, leaving the Gogoat to slam into the wall. Clockwise lands hard on the ground, and Gogoat skitters back down to ground level, shaking his head. He doesn't flinch as he takes damage from Toxic-

    Clockwise, Roost!

    Ramos: Take advantage of it with a Seed Bomb!

    -Clockwise settles himself down, healing off nearly all the damage he's taken-

    -the Gogoat shakes his mane hard and launches several explosive seeds at the sitting Clockwise. He makes a token effort to duck out of the way, but is hit in the side and loses about a third of his health-

    -Clockwise opens his wings and takes off again, hovering above Gogoat as it takes more Toxic damage-

    Air Slash!

    -Ramos tosses a bowl to Gogoat-

    Ramos: Milk Drink!

    Gogoat: <... Only for you, Ramos.>

    -Clockwise lifts off and hits Gogoat with an Air Slash as he's lapping milk from the bowl, which just about neutralizes the damage he takes. He takes further damage from Toxic, leaving him at just above ¾ health-

    Ramos: Wild Charge!

    Protect!

    -Gogoat leaps up at Clockwise, who throws up a Protect screen in his face-

    -Gogoat loses almost half his health to Toxic-

    Ramos: We can still pull this through, Gogoat! Milk Drink!

    Gogoat: <Ramos…>

    Ramos: I believe in yeh, Gogoat!

    Gogoat: <I'll do all I can.>

    Air Slash, Clockwise!

    -Crewe keeps her eyes on Ramos as Clockwise circles Gogoat-

    If he keeps stalling like this… soon the Toxic damage will overwhelm him… I can stall him out better than he can stall me out!

    -the Gogoat bends to drink more milk, keeping an eye on Clockwise as he circles overhead. Clockwise fires an Air Slash off as soon as he's behind the Gogoat, but he dances to the side to avoid it-

    Dodge all you want, you big goat!

    -the health Gogoat regained is nearly negated by his Toxic damage-

    Air Slash!

    Ramos: Milk Drink!

    Aha, yes! Force him into trying to vy for his own survival!

    -Clockwise circles the Gogoat again, who watches him closely while drinking again. He dodges another Air Slash but the health he regains is negated by his Toxic damage-

    If he doesn't get any more health, he can last two more turns. If he attacks this turn, and heals next, he can survive… it shouldn't be more than two. If he heals both turns… he could double that time. Either way, he's done for. His only hope is to take Clockwise out in two turns. Time to make sure that doesn't happen.

    -Ramos is clearly thinking hard-
