Super sorry guys, promise it won't happen again. You'll have to just trust me, though, 'cause I can't pinkie swear.

    Anyway, Ashley's meandering along some footpath with her Pokemon all around. Cole's hippity-hopping along next to her, and Ion's bumbling around on the other side. James is, like, trying to set the forest on fire or something, I'm not sure. Maybe he has a cold. Can Heatmoors get colds? Anyway, Six is trying to be all ninja in the back, and also he apparently really doesn't like leaves. Domino's somewhere else. Probably in the air. I can't see everywhere at once, okay? I'm still new at this. I'm not one of those fancy-pants omniscient narrators. They always get so uppity about everything.

    But Thunder Equals Downpour! There's someone coming! See, when you rotate the view just so, you can see the sky, and there's a guy on a Staraptor! Oooh, I hope he's friendly. But Staraptors are really super-scary, and I've already seen a super-scary Pokemon today!

    Oh, wait. This guy looks pretty familiar. Where have I seen him before? Um... maybe it was at the—no, that can't be it, there weren't any characters there... And this is my first narration, so I can't have told his story before...

    Oh! I remember! He's from earlier, back when Narrator was in charge. His name is... Um...

    "Hi, I'm Hunter," the boy says, hopping off his Staraptor and very rudely cutting me off. He extends a hand, and Ashley shakes it.

    "Hey. I'm Ashley. What brings you here?" she asks. Cole hops forward and cocks his head, eyeing the Staraptor. The Staraptor glares back at him, half-extending a wing to shield the boy.

    "Chill, Cesar, she's not like, evil or anything." Hunter gently elbows his Pokemon's wing, and it is reluctantly retracted. "Anyway, we wanted to find this girl, and we thought she might be here, but I guess not. And seriously, I'm thinking at this point I should maybe solve my own problems."

    Ashley and Hunter fall into step, chatting amiably. Cole and Cesar stay behind, strutting along beside each other, each eyeing the other and fluffing up their feathers and puffing out their chests, clicking their beaks and straining to be bigger than the other.
    Victory! Honor! Destiny! Mutton!
