Nick's condition is stable, Francine and Damien have a whole new play out they're trying to make, they almost have enough money to rebuild that orphanage, Cosette has that beach side bar in Dewford working like a dream and she's not bothered by what happened any more! You don't need to hold on to it all."

    Jacob: ".... I know. But still. All those things happened because of me... sure they're getting better but it's my fault..."

    Lity: "Hey, you can't come back just to mope. You are not allowed to mope, okay?"

    Jacob: -Sighs- "Okay... So, hey I'm thinking about trying to be the Champion-"

    Lity: "And no one is surprised! Heehee~ Took you long enough!"

    Jacob: "But I'm worried I'm not cut out for it."

    Lity: -Tilts her head as she gets out of the blankets and steps into the lake to swim around- "Oh?"

    Jacob: "Well... I used to think I was good enough to be the Champion but... well I've learned from several people I'm still missing things. Power and planning aren't enough."

    Lity: "Who gave you that idea?" -She dives under the water and flips back up, shaking her hair out-

    Jacob: "Well, the de facto leader of this group I'm in and my rival did. Tagg taught me I need purpose, and that the difference between an Ace trainer and a Great trainer is not one's skill or mind but rather the strength of one's soul... and Eskay... Eskay is still teaching me."

    Lity: "Oh I remember Eskay! He was nice. I liked him! Wait, rival? You m-"

    Jacob: "Yeah, I know it's shocking but yes. And no it's not like the bitter teeth gnashing "I'm going to beat you one daaaaaaaaay" rivalry. We just happen to have similar skill levels and know we can push each other to be better. And I think we're both interested in how far we can go. The son of the Steel Champion and the self made tactician."

    Lity: "Can this be a TV show now?"

    Jacob: "Last time I was in a TV show I was a villain so no please. Also-" -Turns around- "You know even if no one comes over towards this part of the forest it'd still be nice if you wore something while you swam?"

    Lity: "I know but all my clothes are drying and it's not like anything you haven't seen before! I mean... you know..."

    Jacob: "How is that going, by the way?"

    Lity: "Wonderful! Doctors said if I can gain 15 more pounds I'll be back in the lower quartile for healthy height-weight range!"

    Jacob: "That's good to hear..."

    Lity: "Yeah! And... well... I mean it was awkward at first but... I mean, those cuts... if you hadn't treated them-"

    Jacob: "Your lacerations were harmless but cleaning everything and and treating then preexisting infection made it necessary. And if I hadn't, I wouldn't have found out how close to death you already were from malnutrition. So yes I suppose it was awkward but worth it. Still just because I've seen it before doesn't mean I want to look."

    Jacob: ".... I see..."

    Jacob: "I can't imagine why..."
