    Cortez: <I like what the diosa del sol is saying! Holidays at a tropical resort sound nice!>

    Seishirou: Good idea, yeah... Question is which facility and how the hell we rent out the place.

    Arika: -smirks- I have a plan.

    RIGHT NAOW

    -The whole J-Team gets a text-

    To: J-Team

    From: Nico:

    Hey guys! Nico here! As Maggie probably told you all she I, and later Arika were on this crazy Paper Mario adventure for nearly a year, but we're all back now! I haven't been feeling all that well since the end of said adventure due to circumstances involving me using the Crystal Stars to deathlaser an ancient evil, but I'm getting better and both Lina and a Latias friend of ours named Kaguya (who was also on the adventure) say I'll make a full recovery soon! Plus, I have Grodus, one of the main foes we were up against, as a sweet ride until I can walk better again:

    -Insert pic of Nico wearing his helmet and raising his staff in the air with an excited expression, sitting atop a much grumpier-looking Grodus-

    As further good news I have the much more willing aid of many of the rank-and-file X-Nauts, and with their help I, the rest of Team Umbra, and a few others are gonna host the annual J-Team Christmas party, and we're doing it in style in the Battle Frontier, specifically the Battle Tower's expansive penthouse! Getting the place reserved was... Tricky, to say the least...

    Battle Tower Lobby, this afternoon

    -A receptionist is staring incredulously at Umbra, Lina, Ammy, Nico, Robin, Seishirou, and Fuuma...-

    -Who are all haphazardly disguised as Noland, Lucy, Greta, Spenser, Anabel, Brandon, and Tucker, respectively-

    Receptionist: ...You sure you guys are the real Frontier Brains?

    -Arika and Cortez are watching discreetly from afar-

    Cortez: <You sure this plan will work, luna colgante chica?>

    Arika: Let's see how everyone plays their part.

    Noland!Umbra: -just dressed in Noland's clothes- Erm, yeah, we've had a stressful season and like to relax up there with some friends to blow off steam...

    Lucy!Lina: -dressed in Lucy's clothes and has her hair dyed black except half her bangs; trying to pose seductively- You wouldn't deny your bosses that privilege, would you~?

    Greta!Ammy: -is dressed in Greta's clothes and has her hair styled and dyed to match- Yeah! We'd whoop your ass if you did! -makes a bunch of gratuitous karate poses and noises-

    Spenser!Nico: -is dressed in Spenser's outfit, wearing a fake beard, leaning on his staff, and hiding all his hair in his X-Naut helmet; trying to sound old and semi-feeble- D-Don't mind me, just experimenting with new headwear this holiday...

    Anabel!Robin: -is dressed as Annabelle with hair styled and dyed to match but is staying dead silent for several reasons-

    Brandon!Seishirou: -is wearing Brandon's clothes and a cheap wig resembling his hair; sighs- Look, it's been a rough year... You can afford to do us this favor-

    -Nudges Fuuma, who's dressed in Tucker's clothes and has the Flygon-y parts of his hair styled to resemble Tucker's hair-

    Your turn, brother.

    Tucker!Fuuma: I-I'm not sure if I can do-

    Brandon!Seishirou: Think of your daughter, my niece... She's the one you need to need to channel for this... Think Spectrum-y thoughts...

    -Fuuma nods, closes his eyes a bit, gulps...-

    -Then opens them, grins, and strikes the most flamboyant pose he can muster-

    Tucker!Fuuma: You all heard my fine Frontier Brain freinds, my dear receptionist! We all need some R-and-R, darling~ And how could you ever doubt I'm the one and only Tucker?! I've got so much fab I can speak in motherfucking RAINBOWS!~
