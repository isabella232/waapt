    Psyche's Mindscape

    Ren's family will notice they are in a large hall surrounded by a vast star field.

    Someone who looks like Psyche, but wearing a blue and black starry cloak walks up. "Welcome to the Mindscape. This is a representation of my inner turmoil, my thoughts, my desires, every waking thought and unconscious notion that passes through my head."

    Suddenly two large titans clash behind them. "Ignore them, they are but fragments of my shattered mind. They cannot hurt you... Unless you provoke them."

    She leads them on a walk. "Follow me, and I will show you the way out of the realm of the Mindscape."

    The world changes to a dark, barren world, littered with skulls and clay masks. "This is the land of broken dreams, and forgotten nightmares. Do not linger here, lest you attract the attention of the ghosts of my past."

    She leads them down a narrow pass and out into a field, bright as the morning sun. "This is where I must stop. Go out, and let my compatriot lead you out into your world."

    A second version of Psyche, this one clad in a green and white dress, towers over them. "Welcome to my field of dreams. This is where I sow my plans for days to come. Hurry, leave that place of darkness quickly." As they approach her, she appears to get smaller and farther away, as if retreating. She walks up to them, however and takes the down the path. "We are approaching the end of the mindscape quickly, but first you must cross the rivers of thought that encircle the world."

    A large waterfall comes into view. It becomes obvious that below it, there lies a swirling void. A last version of Psyche comes into view, completely star covered, green and blue hued, and flickering in shape. "Beyond my veil lie secrets and memories that man was not meant to know. I have seen thousands of years worth of experience, read the minds of countless, and devoured the consciousness of more than you would like to know. If you wish to leave, know only this. Doubt not the words of those with more power than you. Doubt only their actions." The boat that the group was in suddenly falls out of the mindscape and they wake up.
