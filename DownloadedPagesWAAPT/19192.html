    Drunken Master

    Battle Tower

    -Ding!-

    -The elevator opens up, and Sarah walks out into the battlefield. At the other end is Anabel, who grins at the sight of her challenger.-

    Anabel: Finally! Nice to finally get to meet the girl Old Man Spenser dragged in. Gotta say, I like your style.

    Sarah: Thanks. Good to finally be up against a Frontier Brain!

    Anabel: Promise not to let me down?

    -Sarah grins-

    Sarah: Promise.

    Anabel: Then you better not break it. Let's get this started!

    Battle! Palace Apprentice Sarah vs. Salon Maiden Anabel!

    Anabel sent out Alakazam!
    Sarah sent out Myra!

    Alakazam: <~Interesting.~>

    Anabel: Not a lot of trainers have the nerve to use a regular Rotom without an appliance.

    Sarah: Not a lot of trainers try to use their environment to their advantage. Myra, you know what to do!

    Myra: <Bye-bye!>

    -She jumps into one of the lights and disappears. The Alakazam frowns.-

    Alakazam: <~Anywhere else, that would probably be illegal.~>

    Anabel: Lucky for you, Scott loves Trainers who think outside the box. Or in this case, outside the battlefield. So that's exactly what we're going to do, too!

    -She raises a Key Stone, and her Alakazam glows-

    Anabel's Alakazam Mega-Evolved!

    Anabel: Pinpoint that Rotom and use Psychic!

    -The Mega Alakazam closes its eyes and focuses. Within a split second, a Shadow Ball is lobbed out of a light at the Alakazam, and then that light explodes.-

    -Then it happens again, this time at a different light.-

    -Then again.-

    Sarah: Myra, don't stop in any one place! Use Ominous Wind instead!

    -The air in the arena gains an unnatural chill. As it starts gusting, a series of small explosions trail through the ceiling, destroying all electronic equipment except for the ones operating the elevators. Myra is finally forced out through a light switch, but at that point the Alakazam is severely weak. The room is in total darkness. Before the Alakazam can attack again, Myra blows it over with a final Ominous Wind, then looks around at the damage, sweatdropping.-

    Myra: <...Um, we might have gotten a bit carried away?>

    Anabel: Anywhere else in Hoenn, you'd be kicked out for property damage.

    Sarah: Good thing you like my guts, isn't it?

    -She winks. Anabel smirks-

    Anabel: You bet I do. You're lucky I'm not Lucy, she'd probably kill you for testing her patience, and flirting with her right afterward.

    -Sarah tints-

    Sarah: Hey, hold on a moment, I'm not flirting!

    Myra: <Omigosh you totally were flirting aaaaaaaaa>

    Anabel: Sure, you weren't. Try not to get too flustered!

    Anabel sent out Snorlax!

    Sarah: ...Hmmm Myra, Toxic!

    -Myra lobs a glob of poison at the Snorlax, who grumbles-

    Snorlax: <Damn kids with yer poisons and stuff>

    Myra: <I'm probably at least fifty years older than you. You're way more of a child than I am.>

    Snorlax: <...>

    Anabel: Yawn!

    -The Snorlax lets out a big sigh that apparently counts as a yawn, and Myra gets sleepy. It then winces from poison.-

    Myra: <Ooohhh>

    Sarah: Keep yourself awake with Uproar!

    -Myra shakes her head, and starts screeching. The Snorlax recoils, clutching its ears.-

    Snorlax: <Shut up shut up!>

    Anabel:' Crunch!

    -The Snorlax tries to shut Myra up personally by lumbering over to the screeching Rotom and biting her.-

    -It doesn't quite shut her up.-

    Snorlax was hurt by poison!
    Myra used Uproar!
    Snorlax used Crunch!
    Snorlax was hurt by poison!
    Myra used Uproar!
    Snorlax used Crunch!
    Snorlax was hurt by poison!

    -Both of them finally go down.-

    Anabel: Nice, nice, great work so far! Before we wrap this up, though care to come with me for a moment?

    -Sarah blinks-

    Sarah: ...Uh, sure.

    -She follows Anabel into the next elevator.-
    "She's drunk, but she's no fool. She knows what she's doing better than some of us do sober."

    Battle Tower

    -They come out on the roof.-

    Sarah: I'm guessing you just needed a change in battlefields?

    -Sarah gapes-

    Sarah: You Entei—

    Anabel: The one from Oblivia, specifically. We've got a sort of agreement that he shows up from time to time to help us with our battles. He's not caught or anything, just an ally.

    -She grins-

    Anabel: You didn't think the J-Team were the first bunch of misfits to get friendly with Legendary Pokemon, did you?

    Sarah: Well, I mean, of course not! It just didn't occur to me, I guess?

    Entei: <ENTEI FRIENDS WITH ALL BRAINS! ANABEL IS ENTEI'S FAVORITE THOUGH.>

    Anabel: Well, after your performance against Steven and his Latios, I hope you can handle this!

    Sarah: ...Yeah. I can. We can!

    Phase Two! Palace Apprentice Sarah vs. Oblivia Entei!

    -Sarah sends out Ria.-

    Ria: <...Oh, hey, we're on the— >

    -She goes wide-eyed at Entei-

    Ria: <...Roof?>

    Entei: <YOU REMIND ENTEI OF LAKE!>

    Ria: <He, uh, h-he's my brother, uh— >

    Entei: <LAKE HAS A SISTER? HE NEVER TOLD US! ENTEI HASN'T SEEN LAKE IN WAY TOO LONG APPARENTLY.>

    Sarah: Mind chatting later? We kinda have a battle to do!

    Ria: <R-right, uh hoo, boy relax, Ria, you've got this, he's a Fire-type>

    -She yelps as she barely jumps out of the way of a giant pillar of flame erupting from the ground. A number of similar flames erupt elsewhere around the arena.-

    Entei used Sacred Fire!

    Ria: <Eep!>

    -She uses the momentum to go into a Dragon Dance, and dances out of the way of a Fire Blast.-

    Entei: <ENTEI SORRY, WILL TRY NOT TO BURN TOO MUCH!>

    -Ria keeps dancing out of the way of Entei's attacks-

    Sarah: (Well, on the bright side, this is at least warming Ria up. In more ways than one.) Ria, when you get a chance, go in for an Aqua Tail!

    Ria: <Easier said than done— eep!>

    -She is grazed by a Fire Blast, and she flails to regain her balance. She stumbles right into a Sacred Fire jet, yelping in pain-

    Ria: <AAH!>

    -She tumbles out of the Sacred Fire, burned.-

    Ria: <I-I I have to do something at least!>

    -She fights her burn and rushes toward Entei, swinging a water-cloaked tail at it. Entei staggers-

    Entei: <OW! LAKE'S SISTER IS STRONG!>

    -He regains his footing and uses another Fire Blast, right as Ria comes around with another Aqua Tail. Both attacks connect.-

    Ria: <...Ow>

    Ria fainted!

    Sarah: Looks like I just have one more shot at this

    Sarah sent out Holly!

    Holly: <...Another encounter with a Legendary, huh>

    Sarah: It seems so. You can finish this, though!

    Holly: <...I can try.>

    Holly Mega-Evolved!

    -Holly doesn't even have a chance to react after Mega-Evolving before she's Fire Blasted. She staggers back in shock, and uses Psychic on Entei-

    Entei: <AAH! ENTEI'S HEAD HURTS!>

    -Entei roars, and Holly jumps out of the way of a Sacred Fire from below. She uses another Psychic on Entei-

    Entei: <AAAAAAAAAARGH!>

    -Entei teeters a bit before bowing.-

    Entei: <ENTEI CONCEDES DEFEAT. YOU WIN.>

    -Sarah blinks-

    Sarah: You aren't fighting anymore?

    Entei: <ENTEI DOES NOT WANT TO TURN ENTIRE TOWER MELTY. CAN'T PAY FOR PROPERTY DAMAGE.>

    Anabel: I suppose that settles it. It was certainly a battle worth having, Sarah. Congratulations.

    -Entei jumps off the tower and disappears. The flames die down, and Anabel approaches Sarah.-

    Anabel: Can I see your Frontier Pass?

    -Sarah nods, and hands it over. Anabel presses a silver Ability Symbol into the pass.-

    Anabel: I'd suggest heading to the Battle Pike and facing Lucy next.
