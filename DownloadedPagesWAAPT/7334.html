    At the very top of the tower perch two birds; one of Silver and one of Gold, who have spent the past few days catching up with each other.

    Pleo the Lugia: <...And then I said "It's a very nice bubble sculpture, but why does it only have one spike on it's eyes?" And they said that it must have popped somehow. But apart from that, it was a very good likeness, I must admit.>

    The other bird chuckles slightly at this.

    Nix the Ho-oh: <It's wonderful to see that the citizens of Bluescale still appreciate the arts! I wish I could have gone to have a look, but there was so much here that needed to be done.>

    Pleo: <Yeah, such a shame...>

    Pleo looked away briefly, a far off look in his eyes. He had known Nix for the majority of his long, long existence, and yet...there was something about her that made his heart race just by being around her.

    By legendary standards, Pleo was still relatively young, but he couldn't help but wonder if he had...feelings for her.

    Before he could continue the conversation, though, a certain group of Pokémon arrived via bubble as Suicune carefully floated up, then gently down onto the roof of the tower, allowing the Pokémon inside to exit.

    Suicune: <Lady Nix. Lord Pleo. I have brought you the Pokémon you requested.>

    The beast bows politely as Nix gives her a friendly nod.

    Nix: <Thank you very much, Suicune.>

    She then turns her attention to the group, staring at them with interest.

    <My goodness, Pleo here was right!>

    Pleo looks away shyly at this.

    <It's as if you've been reborn to walk the Earth with flesh and blood once again...or at least, it would appear that way.>

    Lake nods.

    Lake: <Yeah, I'm guessing that Pleo told us we're not really the Pokémon you knew all those years back?>
