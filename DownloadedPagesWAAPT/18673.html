    ...But what Iulianus said about Choice here is a bit weird.

    Gui: Oh?

    Zeal: He put together profiles with what he knew about the history of all the Octet members, aside from Wound and Traitor. And with Choice... he's got nothing. He couldn't find anything about his past, his real identity, anything. It's like the guy just appeared out of nowhere one day.

    Gui: That is weird. ...Why didn't Wound and Traitor have profiles, again?

    Zeal: Yeah... I really hope we see Dr. Kraus again soon, so I can ask him to help clear all this up.
