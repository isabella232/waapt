    -Crewe (very carefully) stores the eggs near the group, then takes and deep breath and walks off into the woods in the direction Biton went. After a short walk, she notices a very familiar shadow on the ground beneath a tree and smiles to herself-

    Oh Bittykins... You're gonna have to work harder than that to surprise me.

    -the older Sneasel drops from the branches to the ground, chuckling-

    Biton: <And you'll have to work harder than that to come up with a nickname I'll answer to.>

    -smirks-

    Apparently not.

    Biton: -chuckles- <No, apparently not...>

    -Crewe suddenly reaches down and hugs him-

    I'm sorry, Biton...

    Biton: -hugs her back, then sighs- <Glint, what are you doing? You belong at home, with me and Martin and Morana. Not with these people.>

    -she lets go of him and sits back against the tree. Biton does likewise-

    That's the thing, though... I don't. I lived my entire life in this world, in the world of Trainers and cities and TV and Gyms. I can't just leave it all behind to live in a cave.

    Biton: -looks at her sadly- <I thought you were happy with us.>

    -hugs her knees-

    I was, Bitty, I love you and Martin and Morana and Miss Nellie and Miss Stella and Scooter and all of them. But I don't belong in your world like I belong in this one.

    Biton: -sighs- <I can't believe I spent most of my life searching the world over for my baby sister... And I'm about to leave her with a bunch of strangers.>

    -Crewe's eyes widen, and she turns and hugs her brother-

    Biton! Thank you, thank you!

    -he smiles a little and hugs her back-

    Biton: <I'll miss you, Glint.>

    -Crewe hesitates, then pulls back from the hug-

    You don't have to.

    Biton: <What are you talking about? I should just forget about you?>

    No, no, of course not. Like you be able to.

    -he punches her shoulder-

    Ow! But no, seriously, listen. It's like Straw said: you can travel with me and be part of my team!

    -Biton stares at her, bewildered-

    C'mon, it's not that crazy an idea. Look, the only two Pokemon I have right now are a pair of eggs. Two baby Pokemon can't fight that well. But I know my big brother Biton can. This way you can look after me, I never forget about my family—

    Biton: <Like you could.>

    -punches him-

    Biton: <Ow!>

    —And the babies have someone to help them fight! Everybody wins!

    Biton: <... I don't know. What about Martin and Morana?>

    Oh, come on. You left them alone at home to follow me all the way here, you can't be that worried about them.

    Biton: <Hey, a trip is one thing. But traveling with a Trainer for who-knows-how-long? That's something else entirely.>

    Morana's a big girl, she can take care of herself. And Martin! And Miss Nellie's there to protect them if something goes wrong, and if they're in real trouble they always have Miss Stella to go to!

    Biton: <Well...>

    Come on Biton, please? I need you.

    Biton: <What else is new?> -he smiles- <Alright, Glint, I'll stay with you. But I am not going into one of those balls, you hear me? I need to be able to keep an eye on you.>

    All right, all right. But you have to call me Crewe, okay? I'm only Glint when I'm at home.

    Biton: <Fine, fine. It doesn't suit you, but fine.>

    Yeah, whatever. Welcome to the team, Bittykins.

    Biton: <Say Bittykins one last time, and I'm leaving.>

    Biton: <Oh, and who was that Sneasel guy you were—>
