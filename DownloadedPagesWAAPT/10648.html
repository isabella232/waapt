    Stratus: <I'M SORRY, OFFICERS! THIS IS A SERIOUS MATTER THAT I CAN'T JUST GIVE UP ON!>

    Police Officer on Loudspeaker: "An act of aggression! ALL UNITS! ARM FREEZE CANNONS AND OPEN FIRE!"

    Stratus: <...Oh, Karp.>

    Hydreigon: <Way to go, genius!>

    Several policeman leaned out of the windows of their vehicles - all armed with nasty looking weapon that seemed slightly frosted over - took aim at the two dragons, and opened fire, launching bolts of super-chilled energy at the two in a similar fashion to an ice type attack.

    <Stuff it, I'm outta' here!>

    The Hydra Pokémon shot up, avoid the first wave of shots as Stratus quickly rolled to the side, one of the icy projectiles just barely clipping a wing.

    Stratus: <EEP!>

    Police Officer on Loudspeaker: "KEEP FIRING!"

    Stratus: <WAAAAAH!>

    As they unloaded another volley of shots, Stratus turned and shot up just as they reached an intersection, the ice shots all missing the Dragon and instead colliding with the building at the end of the road. Which just so happened to be the Pokémon Musical Theater.
