    Seth used Acrobatics!

    The attack hits and send the Heracross against a tree, however, he soon got up again and rushed at Seth to hit him with his horn with double the power from before.

    Heracross Used Counter!

    The attack does a lot of damage to Seth and knocks him back a bit, making him fall on his back, still counscious but really hurt.

    "<Thought it would be easy to take me down?>" said the Heracross with a weak smile, despite all that, he seemed like he was having his fun.

    Jordan used Hypnosis!

    Pinsir used Close Combat!
    Pinsir's Defense and Special Defense Fell!

    Jordan is hit by the attacks and also knocked back, but he manages to take the hit better than Seth, however, it still caused a lot of damage to him.

    "<Hah! I thought you were better than just that! What a wimp.>" said the Pinsir, overconfident.

    Seth then got up and taking advantage of the Pinsir's overconfidence, he completely ignored the Heracross and instead repeatedly strikes the Pinsir while he wasn't paying attention.

    Seth used Acrobatics!

    The attack of course hit and did a good damage on him and distracted him long enough for Jordan to do his own attack.

    "Strike with Waterfall!" ordered Lyuri.

    Jordan then gathered a lot of water around him and shot himself like a missile at the Pinsir, who, thanks to Seth's interruption, wasn't able to see the attack coming.
