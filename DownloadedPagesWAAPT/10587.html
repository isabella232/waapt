    <Cracked Femur: "Namely, that you and Rising Star here will be making a new clan... Time will tell who absorbs it... Or if it will stand firm on its own.">

    <Rising Star: "I... Guess that seems fair...">

    <Kat: "Eh?! But I'm still undergoing training as a lore-!">

    <Cracked Femur: "Not anymore. Effective immediately, you are the head lorekeeper... For whatever Clan name you two give your union.">

    <Rising Star: "A name? Hrm... Is there something that would have a 'muh' sound that fits?">

    <Kat: "Or something related to images...?">

    -Shaking head-

    <Cracked Femur: "Don't worry so much about fitting into one convention or another... We already recognized that your clan would be a peculiar one... Just pick the name that you feel best symbolizes yourselves.">

    <Kat: "Huh... Well... We sure seem to wind up going places... Heh... 'Traveller'?">

    <Rising Star: "Eh... I don't know... Always wandering isn't good for a young one, is it...? What about 'Hearth'?">

    <Kat: "... Sounds kinda stodgy...">

    <Cracked Femur: "Hmm hmm... Perhaps something that relates to how you two were brought together?">

    <Rising Star: "... 'Union'?">

    <Kat: "... Nah, sounds too dry... 'Bond'?">

    <Rising Star: "... It seems to be missing something...">

    -Both thinking... And then spitting out a word together-

    <Kat + Rising Star: "'Link'!">

    <Cracked Femur: "Hmm hmm... If that's what you both desire... I'm sure it would be an honor to refer to you two as such...">

    -Teasingly-

    <Cracked Femur: "Now... Should we expect your lore to be merged as swiftly, Rising Star and Chain of Spades of the Link Clan?"> ^_^

    <Kat: "... Uh..."> O_O;
