    Unova League, outside

    -Herbert has not returned the lobby after his defeat, and is in fact sitting on the edge of one of the cliff faces with his head in his hands. He does not look up, even when he feels a hand on his shoulder.-

    Titan: You did well, son.

    Herbert: You... what are you doing here?

    Titan: Doing my duty as a father. As I should have done a long time ago.

    -There is silence for a moment.-

    Titan: I wanted to say sorry. About a lot of things. About-

    Herbert: Stop. I don't want to hear it.

    Titan: That's understandable, given the circumstances. But-

    Herbert: You're not sorry, are you? You're not sorry about anything. You're just saying that to try and get me to stop chasing you. If you were sorry, you'd give up on it all. On Galactic. Do you think apologising somehow brings back all the people you've killed?

    -Titan gives a sigh, and looks over the cliff with an unreadable expression.-

    Titan: No. The things I have done are unforgivable. I am an evil man, Lucius. I am not afraid to admit that much. But my path has been set. My crimes, terrible though they are, are necessary. To create a new world, a perfect world... that is all that matters. It does not forgive the crimes I have committed in this world. But one day, every life I have taken will be born anew. In a world free of the impurities of the human spirit.

    -He looks back at Herbert, whose eyes widen as he notices that Titan is tearing up.-

    Titan: You are no failure, Lucius. I am proud of you. Prouder than any father could ever be. To have seen you grow this much, to have come this far... it doesn't matter that you lost. What matters is that you had the drive and determination to reach that point in the first place. If you continue improving as you have done over the last few months, then soon you will be ready to face whatever the world may throw at you. You will not be perfect. No-one can be perfect. Perfection is impossible in this broken world of ours... but you can aspire towards it. And that is what matters. Not the outcome of a single battle.

    -He stands up.-

    Titan: Do promise me that you won't let this bring you down. Time spent regretting your last defeat is time not preparing for your next victory. And... one last thing, Lucius.

    Herbert: Yes?

    -Titan sends out a Bronzong and an Alakazam.-

    Titan: ...You look tired, son. Best to get some sleep.

    Bronzong used Hypnosis!

    Herbert fell asleep!

    Titan: Goodnight.

    -He kneels down and rummages through Herbert's bag for a moment, retrieving his rift machine and his dream goggles. He leaves the rest of Herbert's possessions untouched.-

    Titan: And that concludes my business here in Unova, I believe. Sidious?

    -There is a blip, and Titan and his Pokemon are gone.-
