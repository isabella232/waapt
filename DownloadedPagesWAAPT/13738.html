    Mirage Island Man: Aye, and I might still have been wasting away looking out at the ocean in naive enthrallment with that demon island! I can only hope yer mates 'n crime made such a turnabout of their lives. But how can we know, eh?

    Pink!Weavile: -Looking forlornly out into the distance- <Yes... Who can know?>

    Elsewhere, one of Amber's Pokeballs rustle.

    Amber: Oh! It looks like Corelss want's to come out and play~

    Amber releases the Metagross from his Pokeball, who seems to be a bit huffy at the moment.

    Coreless: <Oi Amber m'lass! Y'promised you'd let me out to explore the docks here->

    Coreless looks around as his eyes go all big and sparkly

    Coreless: <Look at all this marvelous FOOD!>

    Coreless zips around at all the stands, checking out all the different kinds of foodstuffs available.

    Coreless: <Oh this is like a dream come true! Why didn't you tell me about this stuff ?>

    Amber: Oh dear! I'm so sorry I forgot Coreless D:!

    Coreless: <No Worries! Now to go on a FOOD ADVENTURE!>

    Coreless starts walking long the Boardwalk checking out the various stands and food varieties. Meanwhile, back with Mystery Island Man and Pink Weavile...

    Mirage Island Man: Ah, people be startin' to flow in! Time to draw a crowd with our secret weapon, eh friend?

    Pink!Weavile: -Smirking- <You said it, mate!>

    Mirage Island Man: Then let us break out...

    Both: THE MIRAGE PIZZA!>

    Mirage Island Man:: (or, as I like to call it, Liechizza!)

    Pink!Weavile: -Smirking- <Aww yeah!~>

    Mirage Island Man: -grabs a dough ball- Now, the spin! -Begins tossing the dough in a conspicuous manner-

    Pink!Weavile: -Smirking- <Let's watch the crowds draw in> -Starts dicing the the Wynaut Fillets in an over the top fashion-

    Meanwhile, Coreless explores the stalls, when he takes notice of MIM and Pink!Weavile. His eyes get bigger and sparklier than ever.

    Coreless: <Ohmygoshohmygoshohmygosh! Eeeee~ It's them!>

    Coreless scuttles over to the stand and places his huge, metallic forelegs on the front bench.

    Coreless: -Giving the biggest Puppy-Dog Eyes a Metagross can muster- ~You guys are like, MY BIGEST CULINARY HEROS EVER. I never though I would meet you (Mindsquee)~

    Coreless: ~Perfection~

    Pink!Weavile: <Thanks man!> -Holding unsliced pizza toppings- <You ready ol' chum?>
