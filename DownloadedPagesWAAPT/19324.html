    Drunken Master

    Battle Pyramid Peak

    -Sarah stumbles out of the final teleporter onto the roof. She is covered in dirt and soot.-

    Sarah: Geez… Not even the Battle Pike was this brutal.

    -She blinks a few times to get used to the light now that she's not in near-total darkness.-

    Brandon: Well, I can't say you look like you'll manage to stand much of a chance against me, considering the state you're in…

    -Sarah finally regains sight well enough to see Brandon shrug from the very top of the pyramid-

    Brandon: But hey, denying a battle just because of that would be rude. Besides, from what I've heard, you're persistent.

    Sarah: Ugh… you bet I am… Gimme a minute…

    -She sits down to rest.-

    Brandon: Take your time, you'll need the energy.

    -Brandon sits down next to her.-

    Brandon: So, you're next in line to be a Frontier Brain yourself, huh? Another girl from another team of heroes.

    Sarah: Everyone keeps mentioning that you all used to be your own team back in the day. What all did you guys do?

    Brandon: Oh, you know, travel the world, get dragged into deadly situations, save the world, all that.

    Sarah: Same stuff, different generation, then.

    Brandon: Indeed.

    -Sarah stretches, and stands up.-

    Sarah: Okay, I think I'm about as ready as I'll ever be.

    Brandon: Excellent.

    -He stands up and takes his position. As he does, the entire building starts to shake.-

    Sarah: Wait, what— what's going on?

    -Sarah's question is answered by a massive stone creature climbing into view behind Brandon.-

    Regirock:⠀⠁⠀⠝⠑⠺⠀⠉⠓⠁⠇⠇⠑⠝⠛⠑⠗⠹ ⠸⠺ A new challenger?

    Sarah: ...Oh.


    Battle! Pyramid King Brandon vs Palace Apprentice Sarah!

    -Sarah hesitates, before sending out a slightly battered Cassius-

    Cassius: <...Well, I can't say I was expecting this.>

    Sarah: I really should have, but there's only so much we can do about that. You just have to survive this, and we'll be golden!

    Regirock: ⠨⠨⠨⠎⠓⠑⠀⠙⠕⠑⠎⠝⠄⠞⠀⠓⠁⠧⠑⠀⠁⠝⠽⠀⠊⠙⠑⠁⠄⠀⠙⠕⠑⠎⠀⠎⠓⠑⠹ ⠸⠺ ...She doesn't have any idea, does she?

    Cassius: <You know, I thought these guys were supposed to be a lot more intelligible.>

    Sarah: It was when I met it with Dune and that blind kid. But— but anyway, uh, Iron Head!

    Cassius: <You're doing it again…>

    -Cassius shakes his head, and slams headfirst into the Legendary golem. Regirock is barely fazed.-

    Regirock: ⠊⠄⠍⠀⠁⠋⠗⠁⠊⠙⠀⠽⠕⠥⠄⠇⠇⠀⠓⠁⠧⠑⠀⠞⠕⠀⠞⠗⠽⠀⠎⠕⠍⠑⠞⠓⠊⠝⠛⠀⠑⠇⠎⠑⠨ ⠸⠺ I'm afraid you'll have to try something else.

    -Brandon shakes his head-

    Brandon: A decent effort, but that won't do you any good. Superpower.

    Cassius: <...Shit.>

    Sarah: <Shit indeed.>

    -Regirock grabs Cassius by the legs, and promptly slams him down into the ground.-

    Cassius fainted!

    -Sarah's hand shakes as she sends out Ria, who has a burn from their time exploring the Pyramid.-

    Ria: <...Oh.>

    Sarah: Yyyyeah. There's no way we're gonna pierce its physical defense, so you're gonna have to try hitting it with as strong a Special Attack as you can. Hydro Cannon!

    -Ria pauses, then charges and fires the biggest jet of water she can manage, as long as possible. Regirock staggers back slowly as it's hit by the blast, until Ria sputters and collapses to the ground, regaining her breath. The attack leaves Regirock in the red.-

    Regirock: ⠊⠍⠏⠗⠑⠎⠎⠊⠧⠑⠨ ⠸⠺ Impressive.

    -Regirock approaches Ria, and once again, uses Superpower on the now-helpless Feraligatr.-

    Ria fainted!

    Sarah: (Shit! It's a good thing I saved all my healing items for Holly… Can she survive whatever his next two Pokemon are, though…)

    -She sends out Holly.-

    Holly: <Desperate times, huh?>

    Sarah: Yeah. Honestly, I don't know if we'll make it, but we'll damn well try!

    -She raises her Mega Ring, and Mega-Evolves Holly. Immediately, vines break through the roof of the Battle Pyramid and latch onto Regirock's legs, yanking them out from under it and sending it crashing to the ground.-

    Regirock: ⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮⠮ ⠸⠺ !!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

    -Regirock takes a while to move, but when it does, it returns to behind Brandon, solidifying into a dormant statue.-

    Sarah: ...Okay. Okay! We did it! We beat a Regirock! We can handle the rest! What— what's next?

    -She shivers slightly. Brandon smirks.-

    Brandon: You shouldn't assume we all follow the same patterns, kid.

    Sarah: What do you—

    -The arena starts icing over. Sarah shivers more.-

    Sarah: ...Oh no.

    -A second, crystalline form slides into view.-

    Regice: ⠊⠞⠄⠎⠀⠁⠃⠕⠥⠞⠀⠞⠊⠍⠑⠮ ⠐⠺⠒ It's about time!

    -Sarah stares… then closes her eyes and sighs-

    Sarah: ...I forfeit. There's no way we'll win at this point, even if we do manage to pull an impossible miracle and defeat Regice.

    Brandon: Wise choice. I admire your guts, at least, trying to take us on with no idea what to expect.

    Sarah: Well, now I know, don't I? I'm willing to bet that after Regice, you would have called a Registeel up as well.

    Brandon: Well, yes. Sticking with the theme and all.

    Holly: <And Regigigas probably would just crush the pyramid.>

    Sarah: I… I'll be back. When we have a plan. Thank you for the battle.

    Brandon: Of course. Come back when you're ready, kid.

    -Sarah recalls Holly, and stumbles away.-
