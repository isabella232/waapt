    High Priestess of Fabulous

    Illusio

    Aiura is meditating, undergoing self-diagnostics

    Aiura's Mindscape

    Aiura's mindscape appears, somewhat anachronistically, as a modern military base.

    An aspect that appears like Aiura, but dressed in breakaway armor, armed to the teeth, and callous beyond all else, bursts into the control room. "Status report."

    Her generals line up. One dressed in her eatery garb pipes up, "Lady Head, There is something you need to attend to in-"

    "I know. I'm heading there next, Abdomen. Vitals, I need vitals on the screen."

    A large screen flashes. "Heart rate, normal, Hunger, slightly elevated, noted, Hydration, low, but within normal parameters. Breathing, slowed, but normal for trance status. No injuries of note. Good, everything's normal."

    She turns back. "Where's Heart?"

    Extremities (Dressed in casual wear, for her everyday exercises) steps back, as do Abdomen and Senses (Armor clad, but blindfolded).

    Finally, a figure steps out of the shadows. It appears like Aiura, but clad in her full armor. "Ah, Skin. Why are you not on border control? We have many visitors lately."

    "Indeed. And that is what's wrong with Heart. You sent her to the infirmary lately, remember? Those vicious assaults by that Chandelure were not good for her health. I'm surprised Abdomen is still here. Go check on her."

    Head sighs. "You're right, of course."

    They all head to the Infirmary. Nerves (appearing as Aiura's servants) escort them, as White Blood Cells (Nurses and Doctors) rush around nervously, attending to Heart.

    Heart, appearing as Aiura, but dressed in her evening finest, whimpers. "Lucanus..."

    Head scoffs. "He betrayed us. Why do you linger."

    "The Heart wants... What the Heart wants..."

    Head sighs. "Indeed. Well, get better. Griselbrand has been successfully repelled."

    Heart's eyes cloud over in memory.

    Abdomen smacks Head upside the head. "Normally, I wouldn't insubordinate like this, but what were you thinking? She's going to be Shell Shocked for the rest of her life, and if this kept up any longer, it wouldn't be long!"

    Head narrows her eyes. "We are at war. Every Commander must make sacrifices. They only hope they can limit them." Abdomen scoffs.

    "Abdomen, don't take that tone of voice with me, just lead me to the mess hall."

    She shrugs. "As you wish, Commander."

    In the mess hall, only a single patron resides. A small girl eats voraciously. She takes a whiff at her desert and vomits, undoing all of her hard work.

    Abdomen scoffs. "See what you're doing?"

    Head hangs her head. "I see."

    Abdomen crosses her arms. "Is this the sacrifice you wanted?"

    "No. I'm glad we have at last repelled the invader."

    Illusio

    Aiura sheds a few tears. Quietly.
