    468986 usherp20th Sep 2015 04:35:38 PM from General Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Seeking boyfriend-free girl
    Shade
    While meditating near the shores of Sootopolis, Shade turns to see Sunny napping again. His thoughts drift elsewhere....

    Flashback, 3 months ago.

    Shade is walking though the Lilycove Cove cave system looking for an entrance.

    (Hmmm, Now if I remember right, my contact said he saw a few peculiar gems in one of these caves...)

    Shade continues to look, when a Dugtrio pops up.

    <We have not found anything worthy of your attention.>

    (Hmmm, wow, they agree on something for once.)

    "Ok, thanks Link. Please continue to look, why don't you try that cave down there."

    Shade points to a cave a little further up the cliff.

    Shade finds a cave that seems out of place among the current cave system. He can't figure out why.

    (I wonder where this goes....)

    Shade travels down the cave to find a lagoon.

    (Hmmm.... This seems dangerous.)

    Shade pulls out a pokeball and summons Lance.

    "Hey buddy, be careful, there is a very suspicious lagoon down there."

    <Hmm, maybe a lady of the lake to hand us Excalibur?>

    "We both know the she doesn't like to be handled by strange women."

    Lance chuckles softly. The duo move torward the lagoon to find that deep in the lagoon is a cluster of stones. Some seem to fit the Keystone bill.

    (Ah, there is the stash that he told me about. I just hope its a convenient stash and not being guarded by anybody in particular.)

    Shade approaches closer, only to be ambushed by an attacking Shiny Relicanth.

    "Gaaahhhh!"

    <Lord Shade, are you ok?>

    Shade gets up and dusts himself off.

    "I'm fine Lance, I just had a nasty scare from a charging creature of the deep."

    Shade takes a look at the lagoon again to see a shiny Relicanth guarding the keystones.

    (Hmmmm, this Relicanth seems to be rather irritated...)

    Shade reaches out to the Mon, hoping to tell it his intentions.

    "Hello, royal creature of the deep. I come in peace. I wish to ask your blessing, so that I may walk into your beautiful lagoon and grab one of your precious stones."

    The Relicanth replies.

    <No. I have lived too long, and survived from too little to let someone take my precious friends away. They are all I have from my humble beginnings in the ocean.>

    (Hmmmm seems like it was stuck here during hightide, before the water levels receded down the beach....)

    "I'm sorry for your friends, may we battle? If I win, I take one stone from your collection, If you win, I leave and never return?"

    The Relicanth swims around in contemplation.

    <Sound fair enough, a battle after so long in this gloomy cavern will be the highlight of my life.>

    Shade looks at Lance.

    "Lets make this quick, use Brick Break!"

    Lance jumps into the water to smash the mon with a hardened Karate chop.

    <No, I will not go easily.>

    The Relicanth dodges and uses Head Smash. It's head takes on a shiny gleam before he rushes torwards Lance with great speed.

    "Oh crap... Lance, use Spiky Shield!"

    Lance is too slow, and takes decent damage, while the Relicanth moves on like nothing happened.

    (Hmmmmm... Rock Head then.)

    "Lance, use Seed bomb over the entire lagoon!"

    The Relicanth rushes in with Headsmash only to take a full on hit from the move, and his health goes down to almost nothing.

    "Hmmm, pour soul. I think he needs a new home, Pokeball go!"

    Shade throws the ball into the lagoon, and the ball envelops the Mon. The ball wiggles, before finally settling down. Shade picks up the ball, wades into the lagoon and grabs a Keystone, and heads out the way he came.

    "Good work Lance, lets go grab something to eat, and some rest."

    Shade overhears Link arguing in one of the caves. He sighs.

    "After we get Link and his argumentitive self into a ball..... I need some aspirin."

    Present

    Shade smiles knowingly. He may still be approached.
