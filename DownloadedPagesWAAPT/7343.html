    I hope you all have as much fun reading this post as I had writing it.

    Ecruteak

    Eskay and co. are asleep.

    Eskay's Dream

    Funny how it all started here.

    "Here" meaning Ecruteak. It's where I was born, where I was raised, where I lived for the past... almost 15 years of my life. In exactly a week, it will be 15 years...

    I lived right under the wings of Ho-oh himself. Pretty amazing once you think about it. I live my life in a town devoted to one great bird, and it turns out I'm somehow connected to him. And so was Spectrum.

    I remember the day Luke first fought Ho-oh. I don't idolize him as much as Skye does, but still, you come to respect someone who managed to earn the respect of your home's patron deity.

    Luke also knows the dragon who led the Uprising that turned this universe into what it is, did he not? Alduin? Huh... He was the mon that ended up dragging me into this mess... If I hadn't met him, I wouldn't have met Umbra. And then I probably would've ended up going about the last four months like I had for 14 years. The inciting event of the rising action of my life's story...

    Umbra... his girlfriend, Lina, looks vaguely familiar, somehow... I could have sworn she looked like my sister, Alli, but that's just ridiculous...

    Alli... she's been the only family I've had for five years. Almost six. Ever since my mom died, me and Skye have acted sort of like the guardians in our family... Alli's only a year younger than me, but still, she can act kind of childish... Although that's not really saying much. Technically, I'm a child too...

    My mom was actually pretty awesome, as far as parents go. It's kind of like she always knew exactly what I was worried about, and she didn't pester me about anything I didn't want to say. That said, we were always pretty open to speaking our minds anyway. There's one thing I've always wondered, though... Why didn't she catch Skye rather than me? She's a Steel-type specialist, you'd think a Skarmory would be appealing to her. And even moreso with her personality, now that I can actually understand what she's saying.

    Perhaps she let me take care of Skye to kind of follow in her footsteps... I never actually did, of course, but I can't help but wonder... In fact, I would've followed in her footsteps more if she had taken Skye, and waited for Jazz to appear before I got my first mon. A shiny Ekans as my first mon might have inspired me to specialize in a single type, just like her. Her first mon was shiny as well. It was a shiny Metagross... Pallad was his name... Being a Psychic-type, we could understand him, and he kind of filled the "father" role for us...

    Oh, if only I knew my real father... He left us when I was only 3... All I remember about him is a glimpse of his face. If I could actually find wherever he went off to after he left us, I could recognize him just by that glimpse of his face... All I know is, he went off to somewhere in Hoenn...

    Hoenn. Three of my mons are from there. Dax, Kong, and Spectrum. The first two, I caught during my first where-oh-where-could-my-father-be visit... and I found Spectrum just a couple months ago...

    Right, Spectrum... the mon I share a link to Ho-oh with...

    Funny how it all ends here.
