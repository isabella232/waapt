    Ruins of the Alph, several months ago

    The Natu's eyes gleamed darkly, having decided to stop playing around. Rex and the others looked on in worry as it spread its wings, its obsidian colored eye lighting to an azure blue.

    Natu: <   NO MORE GAMES. PREPARE TO WITNESS MY PSYCHIC POWER.   >

    Rex: Oh no! Look out, Ohm!

    Unfortunately, the sheep was still fighting off the image of the Night Shade, completely unfocused on the little bird at the moment. As such, her vision returned to normal just in time for her to witness the tiny bird's suddenly radiant aura.

    Ohm: <Eeep!>

    Natu: <   BEHOLD. COWER BEFORE THE MIGHT OF MY...   >

    Both Trainer and Pokemon watched in horror as the tiny bird's aura flared...and then disappeared, the little bird now in front of Ohm.

    Natu: <   TELEPORT!   >

    It pecked at her feet, the group staring at him blankly.

    Natu: <Are you in awe? Behold the power of movement by sheer thought!>

    Rex: ...

    Rascal: <...>

    Anya: <...I wish I had hands so I could bury my face in them.>

    Regaining her composure, the little sheep launched herself forward, tackling the little bird yet again with a Frustration.

    Natu: <Argh!>

    The Natu twitched weakly, dazed from the sudden attack.

    Natu: <You are not...in awe of my power? ...Philistine!>

    With a groan, the Natu flopped to the ground, dropping into a dead faint. Shrugging, Rex sorting through his Pokeballs and settling on a Heal Ball, tossing the capture device at the Natu. As expected, it was sucked into the ball, the orb wiggling once...twice...thrice...and then it was done.

    Rex: Alright! Now I have six again!

    He picked up the ball, looking at the little bird inside. The Pokeball's one-usage recovery system had kicked into effect, several tiny nanomachines circling the little bird and healing any scrapes or wounds it had suffered in the battle.

    Rex: You look like a smart bird. I think I'll call you... Alfonse.

    Placing the ball on his belt, he turned to Ohm with a grin.

    Rex: You did a great job, O-

    The sheep shuddered slightly, staring at Rex in confusion. Then, to everyone's surprise, a white light emanated from her wool, eventually growing to envelop her entirely. Rex shielded his eyes, the glow subsiding after a while. When he lowered his hands, he was surprised to see a now bipedal Ohm, tufts of her fur missing and her wool now a whitish color.

    Ohm evolved into Flaaffy!
