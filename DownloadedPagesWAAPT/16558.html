    Seven Star Samarilla

    Marie steps into the hotel, and looks around. There was a melody in the air from a piano player on a podium, a little bit away. The walls had paintings and a section of the roof depicted Oblivia's night sky, while a whole long wall did the same for the town at nighttime.

    There were decorated and well furnished tables all around the room, and there seemed to be people from all corners, some fashionably dressed men and women in sparkling attire, while others wearing normal and even a bit ratty tee shirt and jeans.

    Marie tugged uncomfortably at the collar of her shirt. Ray had pleaded her into dressing cleanly this time, so she was wearing grey jeans, her Chuck Taylors, a navy blue dress shirt, and after some gaslighting on the part of her girlfriend, and much sorrow on hers, Marie had left her leather jacket at home, and was wearing a black coat instead. Overall, by her standards, it was quite formal.◊

    She slowly tried to walk down the aisles, avoiding the gaze of everyone and avoiding hitting anyone,until she comes across a table at which only a single person sat. They were reading a menu, which Marie tapped."Excuse me, um, have you seen a person around yea tall, big doe eyes, and.."

    The menu came down, and Ray smiled at her."Go on~"

    Marie smiled, and bent down to give Ray a kiss on her extended hand."Why hello, mademoiselle. And might I just say, you look simply stunning."

    Ray giggled as she got up and twirled like a princess, showing off her clothes like a newly made princess.◊"I'm glad you liked them, I picked ones that I thought you would~"

    Marie sits down, looking around in awe. Ray waves her hand a bit, and sits back down."Start picking food, Mar, there won't be much time to order when the waiter gets here."

    "Ray.." Marie waves around.."Care to explain all...this?" Ray giggled a bit."Oh, yeah, uhm...that's actually one of the reasons I wanted to do this.." she took a deep breath, and held Marie's hands."...I sold a painting."

    Marie's eyes widen, as she tightly grips Ray's hands."Seriously?" She lets out a little squeal as she moves forward to tightly hug Ray and kisses her full on the mouth."''Congratulations!!" Ray had a beaming smile on her face, but before she can enjoy it more, Ray looked around and sees people looking at them strangely, and is scared that Marie will pull back, which she does, after lingering for a little bit longer, which made Ray's eyes go wide, Marie's hand still squeezing her's.

    "Which one is it?" "It was a landscape portrait of Almia that I did from the airship, I'll show it to you later." The waiter she had waved to was rapidly approaching.

    Ray took a shaky breath, and looked at Marie with her big doe eyes."I also wanted to do this because...because I'm sorry."

    Marie looked at her, confused. Ray continued."I..I've been so unfair to you this past while, and we really haven't spent much time together, have we? I know that you must feel-"

    "Like I'm being ignored," Marie whispered, tears in her eyes, her hand squeezing Ray's even tighter."Ray, it's not your-"

    "Marie, I know what you're feeling, and I'm really sorry for it. So this..is my attempt to make amends." Ray kisses Marie's hand this time.

    Marie looks at her, her eyes sparkling."Oh, Ray.." Suddenly, the waiter stood next to their table, awaiting their orders.

    "Oh, great. I know what you'll be getting, Marie, I got this."

    While she was ordering, Marie propped up the menu in front of her to hide her face behind as she acted as if she was reading, when in actuality, she was discreetly crying with a beaming smile.
