    430183 Luke992422nd Nov 2014 01:34:36 PM from England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Noddin' my head like yeah

    Gun Deck - 1:17 to Cannon Fire

    With a ding, the elevator doors slid open. Cautiously, Unite stuck his head out to check for any more false Pokémon. But fortunately for him, there were none to be seem.

    Unite: <Clear!>

    The bird padded out as Luke followed. The duo now found themselves in a chamber overlooking the cannon, which had been lowered out of a large trapdoor into firing position on the underside of the airship.

    Straight in front of them was a complex looking control panel full of computer screens, a targeting display, and many buttons and levers. The targeting display also showed how much time they currently had until it was fired.

    Unite: <Still an hour to go! We're making pretty good time!>

    Luke: We shouldn't dawdle, though! We've got to figure out how to turn it off and eject the power core!

    Unite: <Hey, is that it?>

    Unite pointed a wing at the right wall. Embedded within it was a glass chamber, where two electrical beams held what looked like some sort of strange rainbow ore in place.

    Luke: Is that-?

    The human and Braviary moved in to take a closer look. Of course, a rainbow rock wasn't something you normally came across. But the two had seen it before.

    Unite: <I've seen a rock like this before. It looks just like that key stone Sarah had. No, it is a key stone! And they've been powering their gun with it!>

    Luke: I knew these things were special, but I didn't think they could be used as an energy source.

    Unite: <Hey, if they can change Pokémon into new crazy-powerful forms, powering a giant laser doesn't seem too out of the question. Now, how about we swipe this thing and keep if for ourselves?>

    Luke gave his friend a doubtful look.

    Luke: Is that a good idea?

    Unite: <Well that's what we came here for, didn't we? To get rid of the core? Better that it ends up in your hands than with the guy who wants to blow up the city. Even better, you'll be able to do that awesome Mega Evolution thing. Not that I would be able to take advantage.>

    Luke: Ooh, good points. We just need to find a way to get it out of here then.

    Unite nodded, then pointed a wing at a small control panel embedded in the wall next to the chamber.

    Unite: <This looks like a good place to start.>

    Luke took a closer look at the panel. Unfortunately, the buttons were all in an alphabet that didn't look like any one he'd ever seen.

    Luke: I can't read this! Is this even a language?

    Unite: <I guess it was silly to expect that an airship built by a Pokémon would have things written in any human language...>

    Deciding to try a different approach, the Braviary raised a wing.

    <Alright, stand back. I'm gonna' smash it.>

    Luke: NO! It's too dangerous! That un-caged electricity will kill the pair of us! You especially.

    Lowering his wing again, Unite shot Luke an annoyed glare.

    Unite: <So what are you gonna' do? Hack it?>

    Luke shook his head as he pulled out his Pokégear.

    Luke: I can't.

    He flipped it open and began to dial a number.

    But I know somemon who can.
