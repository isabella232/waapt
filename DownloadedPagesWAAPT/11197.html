    Holon Lake

    On their way to Holon Lake, the group got themselves aqcuainted with their newest Ponyta member, explaining what's going on and why he was captured and was given a name he was comfortable with.

    Ellis: <I hear trainers make pokemon like us fight other pokemon. Is that true?>

    Sophia: <Yes, I understand it's to strengthen both sides' Pokemon and increase their capabilities, even evolve to a stronger, bigger form. I was much smaller at first, but I evolved a while ago and my powers have improved.>

    Ponyta: <Do you think I'll evolve too?>

    Sophia: <I don't know, but It sounds possible.>

    The group finally arrived to the lake. Scouting the beach, Nathan could see, some distance from the shore, different kinds of Water types hopping about and Flying types landing to rest. Feebas, Mantine, Magikarp, Swanna, Tentacool... the whole list. Getting one of them would be a considerable assert.

    Nathan: "How are we gonna get close enough to fight one?"

    Sophia: <There's no boats anywhere, so that's out of the question.>

    Anita: <Don't bears slap fish from uphill waterfalls? Maybe we could try something similar.>

    Harvey: <Tackle them from mid-air? I got->

    Anita had thought of the same thing, beating Harvey to the group of marine creatures, hovering above the largest bunch.

    Harvey: <...This. Whatever, she won't get to battle it if this works.>

    Keeping her senses sharp, the Yanma waited for one of the fishes to leap from the water. In a moment, a Feebas hopped to the air. Anita instantly hit it with Wing Attack to send it toward shore for easier battling.

    Sophia: <Great hit, Annie!>

    Anita: <Could you call me that in the future? I'd like that. Anyway...>

    Annie poised in the air in preperation for the Feebas' attack. W Hich was... flailing about, splashing on the ground.

    Nathan: "That was it?"

    Sophia: <Really?>

    Anita: <Well what are you waiting for? Throw a ball!>

    Nathan threw his Pokeball with precision at the fish pokemon... for no effect. The ball refused to activate. Was it busted?

    Nathan: "Why didn't the ball work? What happened?"

    While Nate tried to figure out what's wrong with his ball, Sophia, meanwhile, inspected the struggling fish. It looked like it had a camouflage pattern to it, probaby to fool any predators from above. Blue fins were a typical part of any fish, including this one, but the eyes were creeping her out just a little. It was looking depressed, now that it had stopped flopping around.

    Feebas: <What do you want? Am I next in line?>

    Sophia: <What?>

    Feebas: <If so, then please make this quick. I just want some closure to my life...>

    Sophia: <I'm not going to hurt you. None of us are going to.>

    The Feebas' eyes lit up.

    Feebas: <Why not? Everybody wants my kind gone, why not you? Is your trainer going to capture me?>

    Sophia: <Well...> Sophia looked at Nathan's direction.

    Nathan: "I can't figure this out, let's see what we can do to this fishie and get going.

    Sophia: <We can't really do that. Our Pokeballs don't work.>

    Nathan: "What are you two talking about?"

    Sophia: <I did my best to assure we don't want to harm it.>

    Feebas: <Her.>

    Sophia: <Sorry, I couldn't tell your gender at first.>

    Nathan: "Okay, I don't generally like to force Pokemon to work for me, so I'm gonna ask, please translate for me, Sophia: Would you like to join our group? We could use a water type like you. We'll keep you fed and get you some confidence to that additude."

    Feebas: <Why would you want to have me travel with you? I'm an average fish, there are more powerful water types.>

    Nathan: "But you've survived in their midst regardless. That's gotta mean something."

    The Feebas considered for a moment. This could be her chance to see places outside of the dangers of the lake and travel in the safety of several powerful Pokemon.

    Feebas: <Okay, I could join you people. I'll do my best. That is all I can promise.>

    Sophia: <It's enough for me. I think others think so too. Right, guys?>

    Anita: <Yeah, sure.>

    Harvey: <There's everything to gain. So yes.>

    Ellis: <Wat she said.>

    Nathan: "We'll get aqcuainted better on the way to the city, lets go."

    With that, the group headed for the city, hoping to find some of the J-Team.
