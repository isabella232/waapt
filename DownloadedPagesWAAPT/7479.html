    The Man who could Understand the Languages of Pokemon

    "Many moons ago, there lived a farmer who had the most awful luck. When he tried to sow seeds, they were eaten by the birds, when he tried to make tools, they were broken by the larger pokemon, when he tried to store wheat, it was eaten by the Patrats and the Rattatas. He was poor an miserable."

    "One day his wife told him "We are too poor! What are we going to do for money?", the farmer thought about it, and told his wife "I'll make palm wine for the man down the street, I'll ask permission to bleed his trees in return I make him wine". His wife agreed with this plan and the next day, he went to ask."

    "He was given permission to bleed some of the trees, in return, the man was to take 60% of the profits made from the sale of the palm wine. The man worked hard and long in the fields that day, and came home with 20 pots of Palm Wine for brewing. "We shall take the pots to market tomorrow!" and the wife was proud of his work."

    "But the next day, when the man woke up OH NO! ALL HIS POTS EXCEPT THREE WERE BROKEN! Neither could explain what happened, other than he would need to work harder to make ends meet. Yet every-night when he came home with the pots, they were broken the next morning. So he came up with a plan to catch the culprit."

    "After work the next day, instead of going to sleep, he stayed up and hid behind a few boxes to watch over his pots of Palm Wine. He waited and waited and waited until he saw an unusual site, a Deerling in a green tunic was pouring his wine into empty bottles and breaking the pots afterwards! I'm not so sure why he had to BREAK the pots, seems like a rather dick move if you ask me."

    "The man jumped out of his hiding place and started to run towards the Deerling, the Deerling said "HIYAH!" and tried to run away. The two chasesed and chased eachother until the man realised that the place he was running towards was a place he'd never seen before. It was a kingdom carved entirely out of OPAL and that the Deerling stopped to bow down at a Shiny Liepard. That was draped in red. And was walking on his hind legs."

    "The King of Red Liepards said "O man! What are you doing here!" The man said to the king that this Deerling was stealing his Palm Wine! The Liepard King considered this, and told the man "I believe you, I gave my subject lots of money to buy the Palm Wine, but he comes back with lots of wine and his money untouched. As forgiveness, I grant upon you the ability to understand the language all creatures. But you must never tell anyone about your gift or you'll die"

    "The man left the kingdom wondering what possible use being able to understand all Pokemon would be. When he arrived home, he was going to tell his wife about the Liepard King, but remembered what the King of Red Liepards said to him. So told his wife that he saw the thief, but the thief escaped from his clutches."

    "The next day, while on his way to the Palm plantation. The farmer overheard his Torchics gossiping about some sacks gold buried on the side of his house, so after he came back from his day at the Palm Plantation, he dug up the many sacks of gold that was buried underneath his house"

    "He was now RIIIICH!"

    "The next day, he overheard the Spineraks talk about some fields not far away that no bird or four-leg would eat from, so the man purchased these fields and grew all sorts of healthy crops on them. Being a nice man, he and his wife gave out food and money to the poor and you'd think this is where the story ends BUT WAIT! THERE'S MORE."

    "One day the man said to himself, why have one superfab wife when you can have TWO! So the man went an married another wife, only, on the way to the marriage ceremony, WAS ABDUCTED BY ALIENS and replaced with a SUPER-MUTANT ALIEN LOOKALIKE of his future second wife. So now this guy had a normal wife and an alien wife"

    "The alien wife was a total bitch, she hated all the nice things her husband did and was superjealous of her rival wife so she made things as miserable as possible for the two. She made it so that there was to be NO LAUGHING, NO TALKING at night lest meet the wrath of her eyebeams"

    "One night, the man overheard the Patrats telling each-other a funny joke and laughed. When he laughed, the second wife stormed in and yelled "YOU'RE MAKING FUN OF ME AGAIN!" and the farmer said "No I wasn't! I was laughing at something else!" the alienwife then told him "Then what were you laughing at?" The former was caught between a rock and a hard place, and he told his wives "gather the whole town. Party at my place at 8"

    "So the next day there was a SUPERFANTASTIC banquet party at the farmers house. Everyone was having a jolly good time. When the farmer said he was making a special announcement, everyone clustered around to hear the news."

    "The man told everyone about everything, the Deerling who broke the pots, the city of opal, the King of Red Liepards who gave him the ability to understand Pokemon, the Torchics who spoiled him about the gold, the Spinarks who blabbed about the fields, the Patrats who said a funny joke that made him laugh last night and the fact he would drop dead when he told everyone all of this."
