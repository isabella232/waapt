    Gah I suck at this Summer Camp baloney. Have the closest thing to a good idea I could come up with.

    !!!A hotel, Floaroma

    (Svavel's initial reaction to learning the room they would be staying in had no kettle was pure, unbridled rage. He screamed. He ranted. He raved. He accused the hoteliers of being cheapskates, bastards, disgusting pond scum, blasphemous embodiments of vileness, and whatever other insults came to mind.)

    (The receptionist didn't understand at all. She simply glowered and requested that Alex control his "aggressive Pokémon" better.)

    !!!The hotel room

    (Svavel hadn't had a caffeine fix in several hours by the time they entered their room. He'd been waiting for the opportunity to pour himself a cuppa, so this was quite the blow... but never mind. It wasn't as if he was an addict, right? The worst he'd get was a little drowsy...)

    !!!Ten minutes later

    (A goggle-eyed Svavel claws frantically at the varnished wooden table.)

    Svavel: <need caffeine need caffeine need caffeine need caffeine need caffeine>

    (From her usual perch on the side of the bed, Vattenfallet watches with disdain. She herself is rather miffed, because the room also turned out to lack a television.)

    Vattenfallet: <Snowy?>

    (After a few seconds, the terrified Sneasel pokes her head out from under the bed.)

    Snowy: <A-aye?>

    Vattenfallet: <You could easily build a kettle yourself, couldn't you?>

    Snowy: <Um, I s'pose...>

    Vattenfallet: <Then do so immediately.>

    (Snowy initially balks at Vatt's uncharacteristic crankiness, but gets over herself and hurries to find parts.)

    !!!Around an hour later

    (As he hangs from the chandelier, sucking whichever claw happens to be closest to his mouth in the absence of thumbs, Svavel suddenly feels an inexplicable tingle of excitement.)

    Svavel: <Is that...>

    (It takes him a few seconds, once he looks down, to realise the object held in Snowy's claws is a kettle.)

    (To say the ensuing reaction is unexpected would be an understatement.)

    Svavel: <...EEEEEEEEEEEEEE~>

    Spalje: <...did he just.>

    (Kettle glomp~)

    (No, seriously. Svavel leaps down, wrenches the kettle from Snowy's claws, and proceeds to hug it with all his considerable strength.)

    Snowy: <Um.>

    Vattenfallet: <What.>

    Skugga: <I ship it.>

    Svavel: <Don't care, have kettle~> X3

    !!!Shortly afterwards

    (Thanks to Snowy's ingenuity, the kettle doesn't even need an electric power source. Using the miraculous power of Plotdevicium Flash Fire-based heat absorption technology, it allows Fire-types to heat up a combination of water and teabags to acceptable levels while preventing accidental fire damage to their surroundings.)

    (Svavel has mostly calmed down as Snowy explains the processes at length. I say mostly because he's still twitching violently as he waits for the technobabble to cease.)

    (At last, Snowy gets to the juicy bit.)

    Snowy: <In short, all you need t' do is shoot fire at it! It's simple, it is!>

    Svavel: <Really? That's it?!>

    Snowy: <Uh, yeah, but->

    (By the time she registers the Fire Blast, it's already gone soaring across the room.)

    !!!The fields outside Floaroma

    (It's broad daylight, so the whistling fireball streaking across the sky away from town attracts no attention.)

    (For now.)

    !!!Back in the hotel room

    (Everyone present is staring in shock at the huge hole the kettle made in the wall as it took flight, none more horrified than Svavel, who looks as though he just witnessed a violent murder. It's Snowy who eventually breaks the silence.)

    Snowy: <I was gonna say an Ember woulda sufficed...>

    Svavel: <Yes, thank you, Snowy. I sort of realised that when my only source of caffeine turned into a bloody firework.>

    (He promptly goes blank-eyed, with no response to any attempts to rouse him. Not even a tight hug from Eldräv, usually a surefire way to elicit a cry of "gerroff me!" and violent arm gestures, can drag him from his caffeine-deprived stupor.)

    !!!Over the field

    (Had the kettle hit the ground normally, there would be a smouldering hole in the field, but no further damage.)

    (Unfortunately, things are never quite that simple. You see, heading for that same field, at that same moment, on a trajectory that brings it right into the path of the rogue kettle, is a vaguely humanoid purple shape.)

    (Could Entropy tell this was going to happen? Who knows? The Latii are mysterious and powerful beings; a certain degree of future sight on their part wouldn't be too surprising. Alternatively, the dude simply lucked out.)

    (Regardless of why it happened, the results are impossible to ignore.)

    (*FWOOSH!*)

    (Upon contact, the volatile concoction explodes with enough force to kick up a small mushroom cloud. The bright purple blast awakens the entirety of Floaroma in time to see one of their prized flower fields vapourised, leaving nought but a smoking crater.)

    !!!???

    (In fact, the blast is powerful enough to catch the attention of someone camping in the woods about a mile away...)

    Gabriel: Ah-ha! If there's one thing that screams "J-Team", it's bizarrely coloured explosions!

    (...who promptly sets off to investigate.)
