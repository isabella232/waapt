    Beach - PMD-A

    Since most everymon else seemed to busy with other things, Lake had decided to go wandering off by himself for a while, eventually following the path south of the crossroads and coming to a secluded beach lit by a vivid sunset over the ocean.

    Lake: <Oh wow...what a nice place.>

    Smiling at the view, the Fealigatr goes over to the sand and lies down in the wet sand, allowing the gentle waves to wash over him as they flowed onto the beach.

    Feeling relaxed, Lake began to go over what had happened that day. He had to admit, that when he got up this morning, he hadn't expected to end up in another universe altogether. He was rather annoyed that this seemed to be yet another obstacle on his quest to find his family's whereabouts.

    At the rate things were going, who knows how long it would take to find them? And what would happen if something happened to Tempest while he was gone? There'd be no chance of ever finding them then...

    Still, was this really the time to be worrying about that? Especially since that Time Space Orb had been shattered. Lake wasn't sure what the "Balance of the Universe" was supposed to be, but it didn't sound like the sort of thing that you would mess around with without breaking something.

    Hmm...everyone else was working on putting it back together. There was no reason why he shouldn't either...

    <Perhaps I'll give everyone else a hand tomorrow. 'Cause it sounds like they could use all the help they could get...>

    The Feraligatr couldn't help but let out a sigh, though. Although pretty much everyone was here, his trainer and his teammates were notably absent, as was Tracer, his Pokémon...and Nadia.

    And he couldn't help but feel every so slightly homesick because of that.
