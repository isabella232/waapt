However, we need money to survive. If I can train that motley group of ex-Illusionites in the art of infiltration and embezzlement we could send them in as employees to high profit companies and banks and have the siphon off funds to us. 0 Maria von Boehm is reclining on the bed, playing with a paddle pong.

    Constantin: Hmm, could work. But we have an image to cultivate, one that would not be helped by being seen as a group of violent thugs.

    Constantin: No matter what, we need to get funding. Our foundation isn't solid, making our project risky from the start...

    Maria: Aww~

    Maria: You worry too hard. Try to relax, we will figure this out~

    Constantin: You may be my only admin at the moment, but do not treat me as your personal pet von Boehm.

    Maria pouts

    Maria: Apologies sir, I was just having your best interests in mind~

    Constantin: I know what you're playing at von Boehm and the answer is still no. I have no interest in you.

    Maria: Harsh. Why must you be so dismissive ma petit prince?

    Maria walks up and moves behind Constantin, massaging his shoulders and talking into his ear.

    Maria: Maybe you should consider this from a different angle, Constantini~ We both like messing with people's mind, no?

    Constantin considers Maria's offer and slowly nods.

    Maria: Well, why don't we pretend - or give off signals - that we're a couple. Never actually say anything - mind you - but give off the impression none of the less.

    Constantin: ...

    Maria: So what do you say~

    Constantin smirks.

    Constantin: ...You're good at this von Boehm, I'll consider the offer.

    Maria slinks her arms down his chest and kisses Constantin on the cheek.

    Maria: Bon. Now, I shall leave to go and organize the boys~ You can sit here an mope for a bit, or come up with a concrete plan. Whatever floats your boat~

    Maria walks out of the room with a slight wiggle in her walk.

    Maria: I'll see you later ma petit prince~
