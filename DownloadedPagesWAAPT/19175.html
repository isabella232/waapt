    -I'm really not sure what I'm doing. I don't know if I need an increase in my Zoloft doses, or whether it's seasonal affection disorder, or whether I'm just being some insufferably existential college student who should shut up before he embarrasses himself any further, but I don't feel happy right now. I like being home, and I like being in my spot on the couch listening to music, but I feel fidgety, bored, empty, unfulfilled. Anhedonia, I think the word is-when you don't feel enjoyment from activities that would normally cause it. But it's not even complete numbness, it's just...lessened. I don't get as much joy from the RP or F-list or listening to my music as I used to. I don't know how negligible the difference is, but it's noticeable, and I desperately want to get it back, want to feel everything. I want to be emotionally invested in this again, I want to feel useful and appreciated, I want to get out of my own mind and start living again! Because that's really what it is-I feel locked in my own mind, telling me that I'm useless and boring and annoying and that no one likes me, and I believe it, because what else can I do? It's a constant impulse of uselessness, and I want it to stop, and maybe if I brute force it with enough writing I can feel better and maybe stop judging myself so much and let it happen! Maybe I can do it! But even now, at this quasi-epiphany, I'm doubting myself. Is anything going to change? Will I ever do anything useful? Will I actually achieve something in my life? I don't know. Maybe I should stop trying to figure that out and work towards achieving it...but that's such a massive undertaking, isn't it? Working towards being remembered after you die, instead of just having fun on an RP for Pokemon...I should narrow my scope. Or maybe I shouldn't? I don't know.-
