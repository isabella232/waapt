He wears a red t-shirt and black pants, accessorized with a red scarf and black fingerless gloves. Often, he wears a black, high-collared jacket over top. He always has his black sack slung over his shoulder.

    Any ties to an organization: None.

    Region of origin: Downtown Saffron City, Kanto.

    Backstory: Aitruss early life was nothing too remarkable. He was a friendly, if a little timid, boy with an insatiable thirst for knowledge. His family would often take trips to different regions. It was on a trip to the Hoenn region where he met his first Pokmon. The family found a small Ralts, beat up, passed out and completely alone, and adopted it. Aitrus spent the most time with the injured Pokemon, and the pair formed a bond. The two grew up together, and their confidence increased with each passing day. Eventually Aitrus was ready to depart on his own adventure. A year later, after the death of his beloved Espeon, Aitrus gave up Pokmon training. He moved back to Saffron with his parents and sister and lived a quiet life for almost a decade, but the desire for experience grew too strong. He set out travelling, making friends and casually studying Pokmon; it was a clean slate and a fresh start.
