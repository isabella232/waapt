    Alright, hi everyone. Saw this and it looked cool. So I'm going to apply.

    • Name: Scheherezade 'Zod' Alizadeh
    • Age: 29
    • Gender: Female
    • Ethnicity: Persian
    • Appearance: Electric-blue hair which fades to red, then purple, then black (dyed, naturally brown); dark tan skin, grey eyes, wears glasses. Generally wears a black, knee-length dress with a long, combat boots, white trench coat with a blue stripe down the back, across the shoulders and down the outside of the sleeves over it. Has a (fairly pronounced) limp, and, while she can walk, albeit not well, without it, carries and uses a cane because of it.
    • Affiliations: None really. She's a freelance archaeologist and amateur astronomer.
    • Region of Origin: Jubilife City, Sinnoh.
    • Backstory: Scheherezade Alizadeh grew up as the daughter of a two geeks. Her mother worked for a small game company as a writer and programmer, and her father worked as a Pokemon researcher in the local university, and had a hobby of training Pokemon, specializing in steel-types. When she was six, her father went on a business trip and did not return. Years later, she found out his expertise had been requested by a colleague in Pokefutures (later the PEFE), and took a plane to Goldenrod City... but never made it there. After she finished school, she enrolled in college, and her mother gave her Shahryar, which used to be her father's. She studied to become an archaeologist, always having been fascinated with the ruins around Sinnoh. She usually took a telescope with her, as she was also an avid amateur astronomer. When not delving into ancient ruins, she often took hikes into the central mountains to escape the light pollution in the city. It was on one of these hikes that she lost nearly lost her right leg. A rock slide had occured, and had smashed her leg. Dunyazade and Shahryar carried her back to the city, where she was delivered to a hospital. Her leg, while healed, was crippled, and forced her to walk with a cane. The doctors told her she was fortunate not to have lost her other leg, or she might have been confined to a wheelchair for life. On another one of the excursions, she met Sinbad. Four years ago, while she was visiting Snowpoint to converse with a colleague, the Infinity Keystone incident happened, which was rather irritating in the whole "shutting down technology" sort of way. So, instead of taking notes on her tablet, as she would have normally, she took notes on the experience on paper. She doesn't talk about it much. It was also around thus point that she picked up Marjanah.
    Of note: She takes notes on basically everything. Including, rather, especially things charging at her. Much to, well, everyone's irritation.

    Pokemon 1:
    • Species: Gardevoir, Shiny
    • Nickname: Dunyazade 'Dune'
    • Gender: Female
    • Ability: Trace
    • Personality/Bio: A gift from her (Zod's) father, Dunyazade has been with Zod since she was six (Though Dune was a Ralts at that point), shortly before he left to Arceus-knows-where. The two of them, if they weren't different species, could easily be mistaken for sisters, and they often think of each other as such.

    Pokemon 2:
    • Species: Metagross
    • Nickname: Shahryar 'Shah'
    • Gender: Agendered. Dislikes being called anything other than 'it', to basically everyone else's discomfort.
    • Ability: Clear Body
    • Personality/Bio: Extremely intelligent, and likes to lord it over just about everyone. Scheherezade uses him as a portable calculator, much to his irritation and her amusement. Still, as he puts it, "keeps her around and humors her insults to my vast intellect", because it means that Dunyazade stays around, and annoying Dunyazade is, in his opinion, one of life's great pleasures. He does appear to have some feelings for Zod beyond the contempt he shows, as he has endured bodily harm on her behalf and saved her life multiple times.

    Pokemon 3:
    • Species: Luxio
    • Nickname: Sinbad
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Intimidate
    • Personality/Bio: Egotistical, Bravadic (Yes, that's a word, whether or not Spellcheck agrees), flamboyant. Sinbad is, or wishes he was, a swashbuckler. Much to his chagrin, he is both quadrupedal and lacks opposable thumbs to swashbuckle with. This is a point of irritation and making fun of. Easily angered.

    Pokemon 4:
    • Species: Froslass
    • Nickname: Marjanah
    • Gender: Female
    • Ability: Snow Cloak
    • Personality/Bio: A kleptomaniacal Froslass who likes to think herself a strategic genius (but isn't), who started following Scheherezade around while trying to steal her stuff, she doesn't get along with the rest of the party, especially not Dune. Sinbad tolerates her the most, but he doesn't like her "underhanded" tactics. Tends to wander off and bring back trouble. Regardless of her overinflated opinion of herself, she is actually decent at Xanatos Speed Chess, and can usually talk or gambit her way out of the situations she causes. Usually.

    And: Egoistic Electrode and Artistic Ambipom

    So there. That turned out to be longer than originally anticipated.
