    Trainer

    • Name: Lucius Cain (goes by the nickname of Herbert)

    • Gender: Male
    • Appearance: Pale with scruffy black hair. Skinny, frail, and around medium height. Wears a thick black scarf that conceals most of his face.
    • Organisational Ties: Son of a Team Galactic Commander.
    • Region of Origin: Eterna City, Sinnoh
    • Backstory: Herbert’s parents split shortly after he was born and he was raised by his father, a commander of Team Galactic. He was infatuated with the organisation as a child, but as he witnessed more of the team’s questionable methods he grew to hate it, eventually seeking to take it down from within. Thankfully Team Galactic was disposed of without him – but not before his father disappeared with a fair chunk of their research and personnel. Herbert now seeks to find his father and put an end to whatever he is planning. He is quiet and often sarcastic, which can cause him to come off as rude during first encounters, but tends to grow friendlier over time.

    Pokemon

    • Species: Lucario (Starter)
    • Nickname: Kirk
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Inner Focus
    • Personality: Unlike most Lucario, Kirk is headstrong, impulsive and never shies away from a fight. He rarely focuses on his trainer’s orders, preferring to do what he feels is best.

    • Species: Kadabra
    • Nickname: Fett
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Synchronize
    • Personality: Despite being a capable telepath, Fett never communicates except in cryptic half-sentences. Instead of the spoon used by most Kadabra, he wields a fork instead.

    • Species: Sneasel
    • Nickname: Ripley
    • Gender: Female
    • Ability: Keen Eye
    • Personality: Ripley doesn’t seem to care much for battles or training, and seems to view Herbert as something of an annoyance. Though he can’t tell, she is just as snarky and abrasive as he is.

    • Species: Scyther
    • Nickname: Bishop

    • Personality: Bishop is perhaps the one genuinely likable Pokemon on Herbert’s team, being both fiercely loyal to his trainer and friendly towards his opponents.

Not bad for the eldest son of two scientists, one of them involved in research at the Mossdeep Space Center, who still finds time to come home and help with his parents' research, much to the eternal consternation of his younger siblings, who often challenge him to battles over dinner whenever they are all at home.     Alright, a few comments:

    First, please work on Anderson's backstory. Most of it seems to just be talking about how awesome he is.

    About Helen, Ralts tend to avoid things like cockiness and lust for battle. As these things are fairly typical in novice trainers, maybe it would be better if she were a later capture.

    Hector doesn't seem to have much personality. He sort of comes off as a textbook Let's Get Dangerous.

    Galaxy seems to be mostly a job position, there's nothing on who she is or why she's like that

    For Lysander, you might want a "hook to the team" like the one Hercules has.

    Also, could you try defining the relationships the characters have to each other better? How do they get along? Are there any in-team rivalries, romances, quirky hobbies or such?

    Overall, could you try toning down the awesome and work on making your characters more interesting characters?
