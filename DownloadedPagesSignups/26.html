    You know what? I've honestly come up with a better idea based on blu beri pi's input. In other words, I've scraped Ren's original bio and doing a rewrite. The new bio should be up in a couple of hours at most.

    EDIT: posted new bio
    EDIT 2: Posted revised mon bios reflecting the rewrite.

    Backstory: Quirky, hammy, a bit snarky, and a fan of pop culture, Renard Valenum Clavis is the son of Erik Tobias Clavis and Clara Sophia Desmond Clavis and younger brother to Martin Reese Clavis.

    Ren grew up in comfort due to him coming from a long line of highly successful lawyers and business men. From a young age Ren showed an aptitude for the arts. Ren's parents ecouraged Ren and his talent (Martin was already well on his way on on becoming an excellent business man). In his teens, Ren had a bad accident while riding a Rapidash, the aftermath of which left Ren with his left leg heavily scared and a trick ankle. Ren now walks with a cane in cold and/or stormy weather or whenever he needs extra support because of this. Ren parents, wanting to make sure Ren would be able to provide for himself, allowed Ren to attend the La Rousse College of Art and Technology.

    During his third year of college, Ren developed a love of pokemon coordinating. And opted to be come a professional coordinator. Ren finished is B As in graphic design and fashion design - the latter of which would serve him well in his coordinator carreer. After a few years of running The Johoto, Hoenn, and Sinnoh contest circuits, Ren started to gain recognition as a coordinator; known for his pokemon's highly visual appeals and designing and making accessories for his pokemon. Ren has since branched off from contests and is currently making a name for himself in pokemon musicals. Ren currently resides in Castelia City.

    Species: Lucario
    Nickname: Rio
    Gender: Male
    Ability: Inner Focus
    Personality/Bio: As Ren's first pokemon, he's fiercely loyal and highly protective to him. Rio shows respect to anyone who he perceives a "friendly". After Ren's accident, Rio sticks close to Ren, seeing himself as a bodyguard only leaving his side for battles or appeals. While Rio may have a strong sense of right and wrong, don't expect him to actively go and stop a mugging unless Ren wants to stop it as well - unless the mugger is tottering on the edge of a Moral Event Horizon. Rio is also one of Ren's star contest pokemon. Rio is used for making Tough appeals

    Species: Ninetales
    Nickname: Kitsune
    Gender: Female
    Ability: Drought
    Personality/Bio: An extremely large Ninetales, Kitsune is Ren's mode of land transportation when traveling on two feet is inconvenient or when Ren wants to make an impression. Kitsune is also one of Ren's most prominent contest pokemon, used for making a variety of appeals. Kitsune's personality has shades of a Clingy jealous girlfriend, and can be a bit of a diva at times, but once she warms up to someone don't expect to insult that person the while she's in earshot and to walk away without being b*tch-slaped by a few (if not all) of her tails.

    Species: Alakazam
    Nickname: Copperfield
    Gender: Male
    Ability: Magic Guard
    Personality/Bio: As one of the most powerful psychic type pokemon, Copperfield uses his abilities to connect the minds of Ren with Gamma or Seamstress to allow Ren to produce the signature accessories Ren's pokemon are known for. Copperfield is at his core a Physically Weak, But Skilled Badass Bookworm. Slightly sadistic in battle, Copperfield likes to analyze his opponent's psyche, and carry out Ren's orders while simultaneously inflicting as much psychological damage as possible. Copperfield never gets used in contests for this reason.

    Species: Porygon-Z
    Nickname: Gamma
    Gender: unknown
    Ability: Download
    Personality/Bio: As a member of the Porygon line, Gamma uses his digital body to allow Ren to create models for the accessories he designs. Gamma is in all but physical form, a robot buddy. He can occasionally be literal minded, yet he likes to give made up (yet somewhat believable) ludicrously precise calculations just to hear annoyed groans. Gamma can communicate with human via electronic displays and voice synthesizers.

    Species: Leavanny
    Nickname: Seamstress
    Gender: Female
    Ability: Chlorophyll
    Personality/Bio: Polite and caring, Seamstress is very much the team mom of Ren's pokemon. Seamstress uses her innate abilities as a Leavanny produce the accessories Ren has designed. Seamstress is also very picky about unkempt clothes and clothes that are showing wear. She has been known to pull people aside just to fix a loose button, and will never let Ren out in public if he so much misses a belt loop. In battle, Seamstress loves to be paired with Kitsune, as both of them can showoff their full abilities. Girl Power indeed!

    Species: Dragonite
    Nickname: Zanna
    Gender: Female
    Ability: Inner Focus
    Personality/Bio: Be it flying, performing, or fighting - she'll do it with with style! Zanna is one of Ren's star contest pokemon. Zanna is used for making cool appeals. Zanna may act a bit tom-boyish at times, but over all she is kind, sensitive, and slow to anger. But her fury is terrible to behold.
