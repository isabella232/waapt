        Pokemon Trainer dossier 

    Alexander Volkov

    • Name: Alexander Volkov
    • Age: 18
    • Birthday: 7th October 1997
    • Gender: Male
    • Height: 60, (184cm)
    • Weight: 95kg, (209.4Lbs)
    • Hair colour: Black
    • Eye colour: Silver-grey, sometimes blue-grey
    • Ethnicity: Caucasian

    • Appearance: Alexander is a young man of slightly above height and compact yet muscular build. His hair is short, black and often seen to be somewhat spiked at times but maintained enough to be presentable in social situations. One of the more noticeable of Alexanders features is his serene silver-grey eyes that can at times if provoked, deliver withering death glares in certain circumstances. He keeps a red scarf, black goggles which he keeps wrapped around his neck when not in use and white gloves suited for outdoor use. His attire consists of a blue bomber jacket, blue cargo pants, black t-shirt, black jump boots and in more colder conditions, a fashionable grey ushanka (fur hat with ear flaps that can be tied up to the at the top, or fastened at the chin to protect the ears, jaw and lower chin from the cold).
    • Affiliation: None
    • Region of Origin: Sinnoh
    • Hometown: Snowpoint City
    • Relations:
      • Father: Dima Volkov
      • Mother: Eva Volkov
      • Sister: Nina Volkov
    • Bio: Alexander was born in the wintery city of Snowpoint to where he spent most of his early life with his parents Dima and Eva along with his sister, Nina. His parents were formidable pokemon trainers whose strengths and weaknesses complimented either others during their first encounters. In his early years, he started training at the local gym alongside other promising trainers under the tutelage of the gyms leader Candice. While his tactics were unrefined at the time, Alexander expressed a keen enthusiasm to learn more about pokemon and discover what it took to be a talented Pokemon Trainer. However his time under Candices teaching was cut short after the Team Galactic incident where his parents decided to emigrate to Icirrus City, Unova. Far away from the danger that Team Galactic posed at the time before they were reportedly defeated. Being in strange new surroundings had left Alexander disorientated for a short time as he left numerous good friends behind in Sinnoh. This time of confusion did not last however as he soon found himself adapting to his new surroundings culminating towards a decision to embark on his own expedition and make his own way in life. Since starting his journey officially, he has amassed a small fortune of wealth, knowledge and conquered the Unovan league.

    Nikita the Blaziken (Starter)

    Gender: Female
    Ability: Speed Boost
    Nature: Calm
    Characteristic: Alert to sounds
    Nikita was a Torchic that was given to Alexander by his parents in Sinnoh. She is named after the Greek/Slavic word Nike meaning victory and her reputation as a lightning fast hard-hitting pokemon is nothing to laugh at either. Her combat style is quick and devastating, with the emphasis on quick as her unique ability allows her to gather more speed as a fight drags on. Yet despite your standard hot-headed boisterous Blaziken, Nikita maintains a calm and composed demeanour most of the time and only cracks whenever her comrades are severely hurt.
    Relationship with Alexander: Nikita was the first pokemon Alexander received as a Pokemon trainer, she is the most experienced pokemon of the team and the one Alexander entrusts his safety to the most. Nikita has also displayed absolute unrelenting loyalty towards her trainer in the firm belief that the bond between a trainer and their pokemon is be symbolically and ideologically sacred.
    Relationship with other pokemon: Nikita is the defacto leader of Alexanders pokemon team and acts as both a mentor and motherly figure towards the other pokemon. Other than Alexander himself the others look up to her for leadership, inspiration on the battlefield and respect her the most out of the team. Nikita sees great potential in John as she acts as a mother-figure towards him, guiding and training him so he can eventually realise his potential.

    John the Gallade

    Gender: Male
    Ability: Justified
    Nature: Careful
    Characteristic: Proud of its power
    John was once a Ralts in the Unovan region when a fateful encounter with Alex changed his life entirely. John was one of the newer members of the team and found himself discouraged as he kept questioning his place in a powerful team as Alexanders. This began to change as Nikita took him under her wing and guided him to reach his potential the end result was him playing a key role in the team's victory over Brycens Ice-type pokemon and at least two members of the Elite Four. After his triumph against the Elite Four Nikita became more proud of him and their bond grew into her becoming like a mother to him.
    Relationship with Alexander: John is loyal to Alexander and trusts his judgement as a Pokemon Trainer but has a great desire to truly prove himself to Alexander despite his training.
    Relationship with other pokemon: John is supportive towards his teammates, often more than willing to fight alongside them and give it his best. Yet despite his power he is one of the newer members of the team and secretly finds himself doubting his place among a powerful team as Alexanders. Nikita encourages and guides him along their journey, acting as a stern but caring mother-figure towards John in hopes that he can one day become strong enough to hold his own in a fight.

    Patton the Garchomp

    Gender: Male
    Ability: Rough Skin
    Nature: Jolly
    Characteristic: Likes to fight
    Patton was just a Gible when he met Alexander in Unova and actually was one of the only pokemon to do physical harm towards the Pokemon Trainer. After a brutal battle between him and Alexanders team he realised that this trainer could help him fight greater foes and allowed himself to be captured. Since then Patton has yet to be proven wrong as his fighting prowess has increased constantly along with the quality of his opponents.
    Relationship with Alexander: Patton respects Alexander as his pokemon trainer and follows him anywhere for the promise of good fights and even better foes. To Alexander he is like a rowdy, destructive teenage son who has the tendency to get into often ridiculous accidents.
    Relationship with other pokemon: Patton gets along with most of the team with the exception of Perun, whose attitude often cause him to antagonise his steel-type comrade. He respects the martial prowess of his teammates and is always eager to fight alongside them in battle.

    Gender: Genderless but is referred to by other members of the team as a male
    Ability: Clear Body
    Nature: Modest
    Characteristic: Highly curious

    Relationship with other pokemon: Odin is considered the wisest and most intelligent of the group and is usually friendly and honest to a fault. Yet for his intimidating appearance he is by far the most eccentric of the group and his seemingly endless curiosity doesnt really help him either.

    Perun the Magnezone

    Gender: Genderless/Referred to as male
    Ability: Magnet Pull
    Nature: Adamant
    Characteristic: Somewhat vain

Now he is a powerful Magnezone with an ego twice the size of his body.

Yet underneath all of the saber-rattling and chest pumping lays a Magnezone with a large inferiority complex that refuses to admit defeat.

    Relationship with other pokemon: Spitfire enjoys the company of her comrades and strives to protect them in her own way. She is openly friendly and easy to get along with towards other pokemon although her outgoing personality could wear thin on more serious and stoic individuals.
