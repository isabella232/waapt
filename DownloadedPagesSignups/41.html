    • Gender: Male
    • Description: Surprisingly tall for his age, nearing almost six foot even with shaggy, surprisingly long red hair, often at times, tied up in a pony tail and dark green eyes. His attire usually consists of a hoodie of some kind, despite the temperature of most places, and a simple pair of jeans with a belt, strapping his Pokemon to it.
    • Organization Attachments: Yaiwen, despite his outward intimidating appearance, is actually NOT a member of any evil organizations, but rather a wandering member of a religion his parents are a part of. It's small, not at all cult like, and worships the likes of Mew, Celebi, and Jirachi.
    • Region of Start: He's originally from the Hoenn Region, specifically from Fallarbor City
    • Backstory: Yaiwen is a surprisingly normal young man. He started out as all Pokemon Trainers do, at the young age of ten and acquiring a Pokemon for his journey's. However, there's a reason he travels so often, and so far. See, his little sister is sick, very, very sick and has been for years. So, in his journey's, he's made it his mission to find the Patrons of his Religion, and, somehow, get them to help make her feel better.

    • Species: Blaziken
    • Nickname: None
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Blaze
    • Personality: Yaiwen's very first Pokemon. Almost as intimidating looking as his trainer, but Blaziken is one of the softest, sweetest Pokemon on his team. He considers everyone his family, and is fiercly loyal and protective of them, even so far as going Papa Wolf on anyone who would dare hurt him, and is almost as devout as Yaiwen in his religion.

    • Species: Heracross
    • Nickname: None
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Swarm
    • Personality: Brash. Hard Headed. Fiery. These are all perfect ways to describe Heracross. He's a battler through and through, and wants nothing more than a good fight, to feel the thrill of someone as strong as he is. Though he's not at all cocky, or condescending, or even a jerk, he just...really, really, really likes a good fight!

    • Species: Duskull
    • Nickname: None
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Levitate

The only problem...he's not scary. At all. Despite his efforts, he just can't make anyone scream or cry, or jump! He has an inferiority complex as a result...despite his attempts.
