    And now, here's my updated signup sheet!

    • Name: Corrin
    • Age: 16
    • Gender: Male
    • Ethnicity: Caucasian
    • Brief description of his appearance: A relatively average looking boy. Has brown hair and brown eyes, and wears a green hoodie, red scarf, and grey goggles. (Full picture here◊, made with the Trainer Creator.)
    • Any ties to an organization: None, though he does work frequently with various Professors.
    • Region of origin: Unova
    • Hometown: Nuvema Town
    • Backstory: A young man who lived with his loving mom, dad, and brother in Nuvema Town. When he turned 13, he was allowed to start his own Pokemon journey, which has spanned across many different regions, with his first destination being Johto. His defining skill is his ludicrously extensive knowledge of Pokemon and how they work. He lives for exploring and researching, and he often goes on assignments given to him by various different Professors. He is overall kind and compassionate to his comrades, though he often uses Brutal Honesty, and he has a hard time interacting with people hes not familiar with. He has an extremely low self esteem, and prefers to do things at his own pace. Though he is a decent battler, he doesnt have any desire to become a Pokemon champion, fearing the publicity that he could gain. He treats all of his Pokemon like his own family. Loves video games and various other geeky things. His current objectives are to acquire the ability to use Mega Evolution, and to find and research a Legendary Pokemon. The most passionate about cosplay.

    Corrin's Current Team

    Skipper the Empoleon

    ->"[ As delinquent humans would normally respond: Ill bust a cap in yo ass. ]"
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Torrent
    • Nature: Calm
    • Characteristic: Strong willed
    • Notable Moves: Scald, Roar, Stealth Rock, Defog
    • Skipper was a Piplup that was given to Corrin by Professor Rowan in Sinnoh. Hes somewhat of a pacifist and prefers to go through things in a calm and slow manner, planning out his actions carefully. As such, hes rather quiet. When he does talk, he's either a mentor-figure or a Deadpan Snarker. Makes the best comebacks.
    • Relationship with Corrin: Unlike most Piplup, Skipper got along rather well with Corrin, even when they first met. This is because of what Skipper was taught by Professor Rowan: That he should care for his new trainer, no matter who it may be.
    • Relationship with his teammates: He acts as a sort of wise advisor towards his comrades. If they are having trouble with something, anything at all, they usually go towards him. Even Luka asks him for advice when he or Corrin arent sure about something.

    Lance the Dragonite

    ->"[ What if that ice were to crack! I hate ice! Oh Arceus, no!! ]"
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Multiscale
    • Nature: Adamant
    • Characteristic: Alert to sounds
    • Notable Moves: Dragon Claw, Extremespeed, Fire Punch, Roost
    • Named after the famous Dragon trainer, Lance was given as a Dratini to Corrin during his trip in Johto. Despite being extremely powerful, Lance would much rather prefer to stay out of fights, due to his cowardness. He Does Not Know His Own Strength. Hates Jump Scares the most.
    • Relationship with Corrin: At first, Lance was terrified by Corrin, since he was a complete stranger towards him. Over time, Lance became much more trusting towards Corrin, often hiding behind him for protection.
    • Relationship with his teammates: He overall isnt afraid of his comrades and he trusts them greatly. He acts as a sort of little brother towards them, often asking for help and protection. Severa berates him because of his cowardice. Despite this, he still trusts her, and he thinks that she might actually have a point.

    XD-01 the Porygon2

    ->"[ Lucario resemble jackals, a type of canine; canines enjoy bones. Amusing on two levels. ]"
    • Gender: N/A (Referred to as male)
    • Ability: Trace
    • Nature: Bold
    • Characteristic: Capable of taking hits
    • Notable Moves: Thunderbolt, Ice Beam, Recover, Toxic
    • Item: Eviolite
    • During his trip in Kanto, Corrin was (forced to be) a part of a small raid by a small group of former Team Plasma members, who were against the Celadon Game Corner and wished to free the Pokemon that they were giving out as prizes. One of the Porygon that were freed decided to join Corrins team, and later evolved into a Porygon2 with Corrins brothers assistance. XD-01s name stands for Extra Defence, Model #1, due to his status as a Mighty Glacier. Spends his time calculating data for Corrin, and tends to interpret things literally. Hes referred to as XD for short. Has the least emotions.
    • Relationship with Corrin: XD has a sense of loyalty towards Corrin after Corrin saved him at the Game Center. Since he doesnt have a lot of emotions, its mostly a sort of User Loyalty sense rather than any emotional connection.
    • Relationship with his teammates: Hes rather neutral towards his teammates, due to his lack of emotions. He still has a desire to protect them, of course. Notably, he is trying to learn sarcasm and overall social communication from Skipper.

    Severa the Sylveon

    ->"[ Hmph! Im only going with you just to see places beyond this one. Its not like I think youre a nice person or anything! Imbcile! ]"
    • Gender: Female
    • Ability: Pixilate
    • Nature: Bold
    • Characteristic: Somewhat vain
    • Notable Moves: Hyper Voice, Wish, Heal Bell, Protect
    • Severa was an Eevee that Corrin caught during his time in Kalos. She joined Corrins team semi-willingly, wanting to see the outside world. Shes a Tsundere of the highest order, and loves to be pampered and showered with gifts and praise. Shes rather confident about herself and her abilities, and can thus come across as arrogant. The most sadistic when fighting Dragon-types.
    • Relationship with Corrin: Severa shows her dere side towards Corrin more than anyone else. She tends to suck up to him a lot, if only to receive attention and praise from him.
    • Relationship with her teammates: Shes a Tsundere towards everyone; No surprise there. Notably, she often berates Lance for his cowardice. She may seem mean spirited in doing this, but she just wants him to overcome his fears.

    Zack the Weavile

    ->"[ ^w^ ]"
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Pressure
    • Nature: Jolly
    • Characteristic: Likes to relax
    • Notable Moves: Knock Off, Icicle Crash, Ice Shard, Poison Jab
    • Zack was a Sneasel that Corrin found during his time in Johto. Zack was separated from his pack and was saved from starvation by Corrin and Luka. Zack is completely mute and loves nature and beautiful scenery. Hes constantly cheerful and has an optimistic outlook on life. He is a skilled tracker and hunter, and hes often seen out of his Pokeball due to his hatred for confined spaces. His dialogue consists of Emoticons. The most photogenic.
    • Relationship with Corrin: Zack is ridiculously loyal and friendly towards Corrin, since he essentially saved his life.
    • Relationships with his teammates: He is overall very friendly towards the rest of his teammates, acting as an Annoying Younger Sibling of sorts. He specifically sees Luka as a father-figure.

    Luka the Lucario

    ->"[ I swear that I will protect my partners and serve Corrin until my very last breath. ]"
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Justified
    • Nature: Hasty
    • Characteristic: Highly persistent
    • Notable Moves: Close Combat, Aura Sphere, Flash Cannon, Dark Pulse
    • Corrins first Pokemon was a Riolu, who was bred and hatched from an Egg specifically for him. As the leader of Corrins team, he is a kind and just ally who puts his friends lives above his own. Despite his impressive strength and skill, he has an incredibly low amount of self-confidence, possibly more so than Corrin himself. Though he acts cheerful and optimistic, he only does this to boost his friends morale. Hes much more open about his emotions and feelings to Corrin. Has a habit of making references to various Anime when hes fighting, usually Fist of the North Star and JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. He has limited Telepathy that he uses to talk to humans and translate things that a Pokemon or human says. The most persistent.
    • Relationship with Corrin: Hes extremely close to Corrin, since he was his Starter. Corrin acts as a sort of confidant for Luka, hearing out his worries and hidden emotions. It wasnt always like this, though. When Corrin first received Luka, Luka didnt really trust him. Over time, they grew to like and trust each other. They solidified their friendship when Corrin nearly lost his life while defending Luka from a wild Tyranitar in Johto, which lead to Luka, who was a Riolu at the time, evolving into a Lucario and defeating it.
    • Relationship with his teammates: He trusts his teammates greatly, but doesnt want them to worry about him or his emotions, hence his Stepford behavior. He acts as a father-figure to Zack, and thus he worries about him and what he thinks of him.

    The secret words are still Despondent Delibird and Exalted Electabuzz.
