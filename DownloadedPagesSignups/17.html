While he plans to become a trainer eventually, circumstances may be about to put a change to all that...

    • Name: Lagoo
    • Species: Totodile
    • Age: 9
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Torrent
    • Bio: Lagoo's line have served the Mare's family as friends and companions for generations. As such, he was given to Mare to serve as his starter Pokmon shortly after his birth. Having been together since then, they've become pretty much inseperable, despite the language barrier. Being part of a lineage of some of the strongest of Feraligatr, Lagoo yearns to follow in their footsteps and become strong himself. Now if only if his trainer would just head off on a journey already.

    Personality wise, Lagoo is generally pretty approachable, if somewhat sharp of tongue. He never means ill with his quips, however, and is pretty easy to get on with. That said, he tends to get somewhat flustered around feminine Pokmon of his age, making it rather hard for him to hold a conversation with them. His interests include battling - since that's what pretty much all Pokmon enjoy - but he has a vested interest in exploration as well. After all, it's a big world, and there's so much of it he has yet to see.
