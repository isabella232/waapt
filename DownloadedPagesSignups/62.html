    And yes, someone did draw this for me.
    Timezone: Eastern Time Zone

    Name: Emily
    Age: 14
    Gender: Female
    Appearance: Short dark brown hair, really pale skin, empty-looking light blue eyes, wears a knee-length purple dress underneath a black hooded cloak, usually with the hood up, black gloves, black leggings, and sturdy boots.

    Personality: Quiet, but not exactly shy. Very eerily calm almost all the time. She doesn't seem to understand what is socially accepted as "normal" in society. For example, she hasn't exactly heard of personal space, and she often comes off as rather insensitive, and doesn't understand it when people get upset with her. Despite this, she cares deeply about the well-being of her Pokemon, thinking if she can establish a strong enough connection with them, she'll develop the ability to communicate with them using telepathy. As for humans, she hasn't been around them much, so what may seem like normal behaviour to a Pokemon might seem strange to humans.

    Affiliations: Was a member of Team Rocket for about a week, before deciding she didn't like the way they operated and left. Currently wanders the regions on her own accord.

    Region of Origin: Ecruteak City, Johto. Currently resides in Laverre City in Kalos.

    Backstory: Emily's parents always felt unnerved by her stoic behaviour when she was very young and essentially neglected her. Due to this, she left on her journey to find other people that she can eventually grow to like and trust. For most of her life, she's had little to no contact with other humans. A while after she left Team Rocket, she happened upon the Kalos region in her travels. There, she met up with a strange man who gifted her with a Fennekin and Pokeballs for her own protection, and basically told her if she wanted to survive, she'd have to learn to make a life out of stealing from people to get what she needs. Needless to say, she believed every word he said, and stole everything from food to clothing for herself. After a while, the man left, leaving her alone once again. She still wanders through the Kalos region, though she doesn't know when she'll move onto another region.

    Pokemon:

    Starter Pokemon
    Name: Fara
    Species: Braixen
    Gender: Female
    Ability: Blaze
    Personality/Bio: Evolved from the Fennekin Emily received when she arrived in the Kalos region. Level-headed and tries to act wiser than she really is. She is Emily's go-to Pokemon when it comes to battles. Combines strength with deadly precision, especially when it comes to using Flamethrower, but is not very fast. Fara sees herself as a bit of a bodyguard figure to Emily, and trains with both her and on her own so that she is always ready to protect her trainer in case of an emergency. She is also protective of Emily's other Pokemon, even in spite of Ivory's hotheaded behaviour and Orin's inability to focus on training.

    Name: Orin
    Species: Espurr
    Gender: Male
    Ability: Infiltrator
    Personality/Bio: Her closest companion, Orin is very loyal, and like Emily very quiet and distant, and does not stray from Emily's side unless she tells him to. He is almost always perched on her shoulders or on top of her head, since he does not like being in a Pokeball. While he rarely ever fights, Emily tries to keep his skills as polished as possible, but fails due to Orin's tendency to zone out from time to time. Due to this, he is an unskilled battler, Psybeam being the only move he is capable of using well, but if Emily is threatened, he will use all of his available strength to protect her. He hopes to evolve one day to prove himself worthy of being on Emily's team.

    Name: Ivory
    Species: Sneasel
    Gender: Female
    Ability: Inner Focus
    Personality/Bio: Hatched from an Egg that Emily stole from the Daycare Centre in Kalos, Ivory is a bit of a spoiled brat, always demanding of her trainer and other Pokemon. Even though she's young, she knows she is a good choice if Emily needs a fast Pokemon to face her opponent. While not overly strong, her attacks are quick and precise, especially when it comes to Ice Punch (Egg Move). She is also very arrogant and headstrong, never doubtful of her strengths, and believes she is the strongest of Emily's team, and sometimes belittles the other members. If anyone calls her out on her behaviour, she does not hesitate in challenging them to a battle. She is known to sometimes get Emily's other Pokemon into trouble when battling due to her frequent recklessness.
