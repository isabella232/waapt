Her whole life, besides Obscura, she had to help her families textile shop, and therefore knew a relatively good deal about money.

    One night, she left in the night, taking one of her parents pokemon, some money from the register, her favorite dress, and ran off into the night. She became a traveling salesperson, offering to grant a person's desire for a price. With her powers, she created illusions of memories, easing people's minds, before moving onto the next town.
