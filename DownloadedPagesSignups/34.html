    Trainer Name: Solomon Davids

    Age: 70

    Gender: Male

    Appearance: Solomon indeed looks like an elderly man, but fortunately for him, not decrepit. His face is reasonably wrinkled, but usually with a smile on it. He wears a black blazer over a green shirt along with some beige jeans, and some reading glasses just in case he has any trouble with certain labelling. There is normally a mint leaf hanging out of his chest pocket. Any ties to an organization: Back in his hay-day, Solomon was an ace trainer, and even became a member of the Elite Four for a few years in the Sinnoh Region, but stopped for an urge to go travelling. Many years since, and the only “organization” he is a member of, is the UMLA… “The United Mint-Lovers Association”.

    Region of Origin: Twinleaf Town from the Sinnoh Region.

    Backstory: Solomon was born in the Sinnoh Region by Lake Verity 70 years ago, in the small town of Twinleaf. Ever since he was young, he loved herbs, and Pokémon, both equally in measure, and he wanted to become a master and friend of both of them. So, of course, he set out to become the best grass-type trainer the world had ever seen. Strangely, a day before he set out to speak to Professor Rowen about his dream, a Treecko appeared outside his house, and just wouldn’t go away. As soon as his parents opened the door, early in the morning, the Treecko ran in and up to little Sol’s room, waiting by his bed until he woke up, when he hugged him at the exact moment and wouldn’t let go for at least an hour. Solomon automatically loved this Treecko, and called him Sammy, before taking Sammy along with him on every adventure. On his journeys, Solomon even picked up an Eevee-which he later evolved into Leafeon and called her Memento (Mento for short)- and a Turtwig named Twif-that later evolved all the way to a Torterra. Eventually, at the age of 23, after many adventures, Solomon became a member of the Sinnoh Elite Four, and tested many challengers on their way to the Champion. He beat them more often than not, but helped them on their way to become better trainers with some pro advice, and even helped some of them outside the Pokemon League. Solomon became a renowned trainer, and after around 7 years, he decided to retire from the League start a family with a girl he’d met recently called Carly, whilst keeping all of his Pokémon close by still, especially Sammy. He even started work on a Mint Farm. However, a year after his retirement, when his wife was giving birth to his first son, there were complications and both of the lives were lost. Strangely, a day later, Sammy the Treecko he had met all those years ago evolved from Grovyle into Sceptile, something Solomon would’ve loved his newborn son to see. Ever since then, Solomon wasn’t entirely the same. He was slightly delusional on quite a few accounts, for example, he would believe beyond a doubt that Sammy was his son, and think of him as a growing child constantly, also that there was a milkman who came by every Saturday with a Giratina behind him. However, he retained many of his skills as a trainer, and only lives now to help young trainers with their dreams. Strangely, there is a Litwick that follows Solomon constantly, which for some reason he doesn’t seem to notice, and would even outright deny its presence to anyone who mentions it.

    Mints Time-zone: GB

    Pokemon

    Species: Sceptile

    Nickname: Sammy

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Unburden

    Personality: Sammy is completely loyal to Solomon in every way, and watches over him constantly to make sure is safe, not to mention combatting anything that makes a hostile move against him. He is the designated “Leader” of the Pokémon group, and is good friends with the others, even though he can never be heard talking. Well liked and caring. Was never technically “caught” so therefore roams free, without a Pokeball. Possesses a Life Orb.

    Species: Leafeon

    Nickname: Memento (Mento for short)

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Leaf Guard

    Personality: Mento is a little bit ditsy, although quite loyal and helpful. Makes silly mistakes sometimes, but is a fierce battler. Has some funny situations with the old man, and loves him dearly whatever happens, even though she may not show it sometimes when annoyed.

    Species: Torterra

    Nickname: Twif

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Shell Armour

    Personality: Torterra is very quiet, and doesn’t say much or show much, except for very short sentences. Like the other two, he loves Solomon very much, and battles as well as he can possibly do for him, doubled. Not easily swayed.

    Species: Litwick

    Nickname: None

    Gender: Unknown

    Ability: Unknown

    Personality: This Litwick follows Solomon constantly, and has done for many years, without any indication of a reason. Solomon doesn’t even seem to believe it exists, even though everyone else can see it clearly. Does not get involved in battles, and says nothing.

    FALLACIOUS FLETCHLING

    I operate in GMT, by the way.

    Name: Alexander Mikael "Alex" Jakobsson

    Age: 19

    Gender: Male

    Appearance: Tall and rather thin, in the "are you sure he eats enough?" sense. Extremely ruffled blond hair. Blue eyes; he rarely blinks, instead opting to stare uncomfortably at things. Wears loose-fitting, rather crumpled khaki clothing, a la Steve Irwin.

    Ties: Nope. In fact, he abhors evil teams even more than the average trainer, but we'll get to that.

    Region of origin: Hoenn. No specific town; he grew up in living quarters that were basically stapled to an isolated office block.

    Backstory and personality: Alex is the second, and youngest, child of Mikael Jakobsson, a filthy-rich businessman who specializes in the manufacture of Pokémon-related tech. His older brother, Mikael Jr., was the designated heir to his father's business, so the young Alex had the freedom to do almost anything he wanted.

    And what Alex wanted to do was be a Pokémon Trainer. Ever since he'd first seen top-tier Trainers on TV as a little kid, Alex had been obsessed with them, and especially with being just like them. His parents were supportive of this goal, and they hired a private tutor to teach Alex about, in his own words, "training and maybe other stuff". As it turned out, there was very little "other stuff" involved. Alex would spend every minute of his free time indoors, learning all about Pokémon and how to train them. "Every minute" isn't a euphemism, either. Alex did so little else that his parents began to seriously worry about his mental health, but to pull the training away by that point would have had even more disastrous effects on their son's psyche. So they let him continue, and Alex would end up spending his entire childhood indoors with 'mons.

    Eventually, Alex hit 16, and decided it was about time he left for the wider world and put his skills into practice. His parents were glad - maybe now, Alex would talk to humans for a change! They gave him a small part of his inheritance to fund this goal, along with advice not to spend it in one go. Alex clearly didn't take that in, because he blew half his fortune on a well-reared Torchic for his starter, and another 49% on random crap for him. After aimlessly wandering Hoenn for half a year, Alex heard rumours of the ghost of a deceased Sinnoh champion stalking the land of Orre. That last 1% of his parent's gift was immediately blown on travel fees, along with several different forms of life insurance in case Orre's many dangers killed or injured him.

    In terms of personality, Alex could be described by a formal, polite person as "having an innocent quirkiness about him". A more blunt person would say he "has No Social Skills at all". Due to his self-imposed isolation from his fellow man, Alex feels a much greater allegiance to Pokémon than other humans, and often comes off as blissfully unaware of basic human social conventions. He's even been seen engaging in Mon-esque behaviour. Alex speaks three languages: Swedish (native tongue), Monese (highly fluent), and English (good, but occasionally rusty).

    Team

    Name: Svavel (Swedish trans.: "brimstone")

    Species: Combusken (Soon to become Blaziken. Svavel is that Torchic Alex bought for ridiculous moneys. Therefore, he's his starter.)

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Blaze

    Bio: For Svavel, nothing is more important than battle. To defeat your Worthy Opponent is an honour beyond compare; to lose to some wild upstart, a crushing divine punishment. He constantly preaches about the benefits of medieval-style chivalry in battle, though whether he takes his own advice is up for debate. Due to this personal emphasis on battling, Svavel is not a social 'mon at all, and he tends to come off in person as a grumpy git who sarcastically rags on all things non-combat-related.

    Name: Spalje (Swedish trans.: "trellis")

    Species: Venusaur

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Overgrow

    Description: Spalje is a little less powerful than Svavel, but he makes up for it by being much more likable as a person(?). He has an almost pathological desire to help others, be they human or 'mon, and enjoys talking to others in his down-time. He's far from complacent when fighting, but he's amiable in both victory and defeat. Basically, where Svavel preaches chivalry, Spalje practices.

    Name: Vattenfallet (Swedish trans.: "waterfall")

    Species: Vaporeon

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Water Absorb

    Description: The Quiet One. She rarely talks, but this is because she prefers listening and observing to talking, rather than because she's shy. When she does talk, she seems to be an intelligent, empathic 'mon.

    You may wonder why someone as obsessed with training as Alex only has three 'mons. Well, one of Alex's many weird obsessions is the desire to own a four-Pokémon party that corresponds to the Platonic elements. Heaven knows why he wants to do this. He has three of the solids so far - Fire (Svavel), Earth (Spalje) and Water (Vattenfallet). He's still looking for the ideal representative for Air.

    EDIT: I retconned away "only Svavel understands English" to prevent problems.
