    First Trainer Name- Allria Menemi

    Age- 21

    Appearance- 5 foot 7.5 inch woman with wild wavy red hair that reaches the upper most section of her thighs. She is fairly thin, with a serious appearance, with her face seeming to be set in a crooked frown. She wears usually dark blue jeans, a grey t-shirt, a maroon scarf, and a black cloak with golden lining that has matching knee high boots.

    Occupation/Organization- Pokemon trainer, and inheritor of a billion dollar arms manufacturing business.

    Region of Origin- Goldenrod City, Johto

    Ethnicity- Caucasian

    History- Allria hails from a wealthy family, who anticipated her to rise up and claim the company. They have been preparing her since she was a child, giving her an education, gifts, and many opportunities. Denying most of them, Allria was able to sway their opinions, so she could become the pokemon trainer she had been dreaming to be, and the company owner, along with her close friend, Caelius. Seeing how they could easily take care of one another, Allrias parents gifted them both with two pokemon, and sent them off into the world, with plenty of resources.

    Pokemon

    Species- Typhlosion

    Nickname- Ignis

    Gender- Male

    Ability- Blaze

    Personality- Brave. Ignis acts like a total gangster. He is awkward about affectionate situations, and can be sarcastic at times. He enjoys observing others, studying their quirks and personalities.

    Species- Mamoswine

    Nickname- Anya

    Gender- Female

    Ability- Snow Cloak

    Personality- Hasty. Anya is very short tempered, but, she is forgiving. She enjoys pranking others with Gris, as she is usually the distraction. Anya is surprisingly very fast for a Mamoswine, and loves the company of others.

    Species- Staraptor

    Nickname- Nikolai

    Gender- Male

    Ability- Intimidate

    Personality- Sassy. Lokaar gets all the ladies, but he usually doesnt enjoy the attention.He spends a majority of his time with Ignis, commentating on the behaviors of others, practicing battle strategies, and just snooping around their surroundings.

    Species- Roserade

    Nickname- Kalinka

    Gender- Female

    Ability- Poison Point

    Personality- Naughty. Kalinka is a total flirt, she is also very social, though her appearance gives her an intimidating appearance. She loves playing with younger pokemon, though sometimes she accidentally hurts them.

    Species- Grumpig

    Nickname- Gris

    Gender- Male

    Ability- Own Tempo

    Personality- Jolly. Gris is a total comedian. He loves the spotlight, and enjoys showing off. He has a weak spot for poffins, and can easily be bribed with them.

    Species- Mienshao

    Nickname- Combatta(Batta)

    Gender- Female

    Ability- Inner Focus

    Personality- Calm. Combatta watches over everyone, and only speaks when spoken to. She is seen as the mediator for almost all fights, arguments, or those who just find peace in her calm demeanor.

    Egg Move- Me First

    Second Trainer Name- Caelius Fusenik

    Age- 23

    Appearance- Caelius is a 6 foot 2 inch man with shoulder length platinum blonde hair, that is swept back, away from his stormy grey eyes. He usually keeps a rough and intimidating demeanor so people and pokemon stay away from Allria. Caelius wears a tight fitting dark grey button up shirt, with blue pants. He usually is seen with a dark blue trench coat, with black combat boots. He always wears a light grey scarf, which matches his eyes. He also has a small stubble, that he likes to keep well trimmed.

    Occupation/ Organization- Co-owner with Allria, and a trainer as well.

    Region of Origin- Unknown

    Ethnicity- Caucasian

    History- Caelius mother worked as a maid for Allrias family, and when she died, they adopted Caelius into their family, even though he had been doing some pretty shady things. He and Allria had known one another years before that though, after bumping into one another on a business trip. They then finished growing up together, and set out to face the world when they were about 17. Although he can be, well. Scary, Caelius actually is one of the biggest softies in history.

    Nickname- None
