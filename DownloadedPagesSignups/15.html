    Um, I'm really hoping the form is ok, and if not I'll change anything you tell me to... And Acidic Flamingos.

    Your Trainer's name: Alexis K. Jackson

    Age: 17 years old.

    Gender: Female.

    Brief description of his/her appearance: Alexis stands around 5'1, with a skinny figure and skin that isn't pale or tan, somewhere in between the two opposing shades. Her eyes are light brown and kind of almond shaped. Her hair is light brown and cut at her chin, and is often messy. On her upper right shoulder is a large scar, a slightly more pink version of her skin, and while seven years old is still highly noticeable. Wears a red short sleeved t-shirt, with light blue denim jeans and simple, black and white sneakers. Her backpack is a plain red, and has a yellow jacket inside, among other supplies.

    Any ties to an organization(i.e Pokemon Rangers, Team Rocket, International Police, etc): None at the moment.

    Region of origin: Snowpoint City, Sinnoh.

    Backstory: Up until she was ten, life was easy for Alexis. Hang out at the gym, explore the town, nothing complex. But on her birthday, everything changed in a heartbeat. Among the presents was a backpack, basic supplies for trainers, and a young Houndoom pup, who is now known as Copper. The two left that day, saying farewell to her parents, and leaving the town. However, Copper ran into the wrong den, angering a Froslass inside and causing the Pokemon to attack both with an Ice Shard. Alexis was hit pretty badly on her shoulder and Copper fainted. If not for a woman and her Arcanine to hear the girl's screams she probably would be dead now, and she was taken to the Pokemon Center to get the wound fixed up and Copper restored to full health. Since the attack, Alexis feared ice types greatly, and Copper couldn't forgive herself for letting her trainer get hurt. The trainer who found them offered to take them down the mountain to some of the earlier routes that would be much more well suited to her Pokemon's level. Her parents outright refused, and said they would prefer their daughter not to leave anytime soon due to what happened. Alexis didn't agree with them. In the middle of the night, she went to the woman's room and asked for her to take her out of here. The trainer agreed, and they left in the early morning, with Alexis being dropped off at Sandpoint Town before she left. Through the rest of her journey, nothing huge has happened, and recently she decided to see what the far off region of Unova was like.

    Species: Houndoom

    Nickname: Copper

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Flash Fire

    Personality/Bio: Copper is loyal to her packmates to the last breath, and even when told to would never betray a friend, despite any risks. However, she is also possessive, untrusting, and has a fiery temper. Messing with her team, especially Alexis, will guarantee you some bite marks. However, if you get past all of that, you have a kind, loyal and energetic friend. She often violently rushes into a situation if she senses a threat, though luckily her teammates and trainer help her tone down her habit of being trigger happy... Most of the time. When determined, she won't stop until she can't breathe, and Arceus help you if you even slightly hurt Alexis.

    Species: Mismagius

    Nickname: Pandora

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Levitate Personality/Bio: Pandora is a trickster, to say the least, and her ability to bless/curse as a Mismagius makes this worse, though she tends to get tired and forget about revenge. She's most likely to stay out of range or try running away instead of one on one combat. Pandora has an odd friendship with Alexis, though, and enjoys finding new ways to startle people, though her trainer has been conditioned to not be very startled as their time together went on. With strangers Pandora is often quite loud, swirling around someone to see if she can pull off a little prank before Alexis notices. With the rest of her party and their friends her pranks are more silly and less troublesome... Most of the time. She is teasingly affectionate to the rest of the team, and prefers hanging out around Rosalie.

    Species: Roserade

    Nickname: Rosalie

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Poison Point

    Personality/Bio: Rosalie is definitely curious, and probably the least battle hungry of the group. She likes helping the rest of the team, and is not quiet at all about her opinions on matters. Her favorite activity is probably making friends, and is a social butterfly. To her, the worst thing to do is lie and cheat, or break a promise. Rosalie will often be found assisting Pandora with her more silly pranks, and has a great sense of humor and fondness of puns. However, sometimes she is too distracted by making friends to notice that someone isn't the best person to make friends with.

    Species: Lopunny

    Nickname: Alice

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Cute Charm

    Personality/Bio: Alice is competitive and energetic fighter, eager to leap into any battle. She is loud, slightly naive and can be considered as dim as a 50 year old light bulb. Don't say that, though, because it's up there with yanking on her ears. Alice has a large pride, and will sulk for days if she's defeated easily. The rabbit also loves sweets, and would do near anything for Sweet Poffins especially. However, she is quiet and rather shy, hiding from strangers behind her trainer. Due to a dual training strategy from years ago when she was just a Buneary, Alice and Copper are close friends and battling partners, and are almost never with somemon else in a double battle. Only in battle and around close friends is her more curious and friendly nature brought out.

    Species: Floatzel

    Nickname: Marisa

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Swift Swim

    Personality/Bio: Marisa is a bundle of energy, and has no sense of personal space. At all. Whatsoever. She is playful, and talks one hundred miles an hour. No, she isn't very mature or serious, and is the child of the team to say the least. To say the least she's a bit spoiled, and is kind of up in the clouds about normal things. Her views on the rest of her friends are sunny as a Sunny Day, and she views Alexis as an adoptive mother. She loves to be around others, and hates being alone. Marisa, however, tends to be a bit nippy, as Alexis found out when she first hatched.

    Species: Gardevoir

    Nickname: Nina

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Synchronize

    Personality/Bio: Nina is calm and patient, in addition to being the last addition to the team. She is also rather observant, and takes humor in the small things. The psychic is definitely the sane lady of the team, and has a good memory with bad multi-tasking skills. She also is rather wary of others, and takes a while to warm up to others, though at the same time she isn't stubborn and will ask for help often. The Gardevoir loves to learn new things and see new places, though one of her favorite activities is trying new foods. At the bare minimum she is highly protective and loyal to all of her teammates and their friends, and would rather die than lose them. Her nurturing nature makes Nina try to assist all members, though she often forgets about taking care of her own needs because of this.

    Alright, so, I did, in fact, discuss the various problematic parts of my sign-up last Tuesday, but for reasons known only to me (sounds cooler than saying "There happened to be a heck of a lot of tests and English papers due last week"), I'm just getting around to posting the changes now. Anyways...

    • The Almighty Pink Jirachi doesn't actually exist (But Ben's grandpa still believes in Him, and thinks that He is the cause of Ben's bad fortune). Ben himself does not believe in His Almighty Pinkness, but is actually deep in denial about His existence, even though He doesn't really exist in the first place.
    • Andromeda isn't actually the Elgyem and Beheeyem princess, nor was their planet (necessarily) blown up by spacefaring Clefairy. She WAS jettisoned to Earth in a single-Elgyem pod when her planet was destroyed in whatever vague incident actually led to the destruction of the Elgyem homeworld, and she hit her head really hard in the crash-landing. After that, she became convinced that she was actually the Elgyem princess, due in part to her purple eyes and sparkly, bluish skin.
    • Solomon, alas, no longer has the ghost of the internet in his head (nor is he able to talk in anything other than Monese). Also, since he seemed to be somewhat lacking in personality, I've decided that he used to work as a famous philosopher's bodyguard/assistant in ancient times. As a result of this, he constantly tries to sound wise despite having the approximate intelligence of the average lump of clay. He is quite fond of Ice Cream Koans, and is often Too Dumb to Fool. Though he is quite unintelligent, he can perform extremely complex mathematical calculations in his head and remembers everything that he did over the course of his life. While this does mean that he is extremely knowledgeable about things that he was able to learn about in ancient times, he's still quite clueless about many modern technological advancements and scientific discoveries, though he tries to sound like he knows all about them, often failing horribly (not that Sandy and Andromeda know any better...). He TALKS LIKE THIS, calls people "SIR," has a habit of both using long words (many of which he makes up on the spot) to sound more intelligent, is extremely clumsy, and has a weakness for cute- WHO'S A GOOD KITTY? YES, YOU ARE!
    • Notch has been cast into the great fiery pit where all rejected characters go to die. She shall be missed. By me. And probably no one else. Instead, I now have...

    Bruce the Audino

    • Gender: Male

    • Ability: Healer (normal), Regenerator (enraged)

    • Description: Oran the Audino lived a happy, carefree life as the town gardener in a hidden Audino village where everyone was kind to one another, candy grew on trees, a rainbow filled the sky by day, and community songs filled the air by night. Then, the Houndoom came. Drawn there by the allure of countless living exp. sacks, a pack of Houndoom calling themselves the Hounds of Cerebus descended upon the peaceful village, killing, looting, burning, and destroying everything and everyone in sight. Something in Oran's mind snapped that day. Roaring with rage, the once cheerful, pacifistic gardener turned into a giant, hulking monster of an Audino (Well, for an Audino. He was actually only about 4'6"), bulging with muscle, colossal arms dragging on the ground, two glorious, flowing tufts of fur spouting from his upper lip by sheer force of will alone. In the years following that fateful day, he wandered the barren deserts east of Orre, out for revenge on the ones who murdered his village. By day, he would calm himself with the move Return, spreading cheer and goodwill like any Audino, but when night fell, he would rekindle his anger with Frustration and don an old luchador mask, wrestling the mightiest of human and Pokemon wrestlers alike under the stage name of El Vengador Rosado, The Pink Avenger. He did this both to earn a living and dig for information regarding the Hounds of Cerebus, two tasks made infinitely more difficult by the fact that he couldn't actually speak to humans (though there was sometimes a Psychic type around to serve as an interpreter). In the dead of night, he prowled the shadowy rooftops and alleyways of whatever city he happened to be in at the time, hunting down those who would dare harm an innocent human or Pokemon. This nocturnal crime-fighting did not stop when Ben captured him (at which point he also acquired the nickname of "Bruce"), though he seems to think that no one (aside from his self-appointed sidekick, Andromeda) has caught on to the fact that the mild-mannered Audino in Ben's party is actually the not-quite-legendary Pink Avenger. Even though he's usually the only Audino for miles around. And he wears a blue bandana that looks suspiciously like a luchador mask turned inside-out and tied around the neck. Ben and his other Pokemon typically humor him.

    When not in "Enraged Mode," Bruce is as kind, gentle, and mild-mannered as any Audino, spreading joy throughout the world and refusing to physically harm anyone except in defense of someone else. He acts as a Team Mom to the assorted idiots, weirdos, and misfits that make up Ben's party, grows flowers, berries, and vegetables on Terry's back, serves as a doctor for anyone who is injured far from civilization, and dabbles in cooking, painting, sewing, and violin-playing. On the other hand, when he is in his El Vengador Rosado persona (which he turns into when he gets angry (not easy to do for an Audino, though mentioning the Hounds of Cerebus usually helps) or uses the move Frustration (which makes him violently angry), turning back when he calms down, falls asleep, is recalled to his Pokeball, or uses the move Return (which forcibly calms him down)), he is a shining pinnacle of utter manliness, leaping across skyscraper rooftops, punching through walls with his bare hands, wrestling Feraligatr in midair after jumping out of an exploding helicopter, and beating lesser men at arm-wrestling with his mighty 'stache alone. He can also speak Spanish... in Monese, and any dramatic action that he performs is inexplicably followed by a brief guitar chord.

    • Unusual moves (normal form, learned back when he was the village gardener and used primarily for helping plants grow): Sunny Day, Grass Knot (can make any sort of plant grow larger (within reason, and has no noticeable effect on big things like trees)), and Rain Dance.

    • Unusual moves (El Vengador Rosado): Fire Punch, Thunderbolt (throws an exploding bolt of lighting, Zeus-style), Blizzard (chills the surrounding area and encases his foes in a block of ice with merely a cold glare).

    • Unusual moves (both): Return (When used when already calm, attacks the foe by radiating weaponized Incorruptible Pure Pureness, circumnavigating his normal policy of pacifism. His opponents usually end up curled in a fetal position, trembling from the sheer Tastes-Like-Diabetes-ness, but are not physically harmed.) and Frustration (His basic attack as El Vengador Rosado, which basically amounts to beating people up forcefully, repeatedly, and with extreme prejudice.). As mentioned above, they are also used to switch between alter-egos.
