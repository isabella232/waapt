    That said, I don't think everyone is entirely opposed to the idea so far if she's not likely to star going Crush. Kill. Destroy! on peoplemon without it being a publicly planned plot event or something. But there are a couple of concerns I can see even without the bad experiences:

    • We're going to need a more detailed backstory for a robot girl than we'd need for a random human trainer. I'm not certain where you'd find the technology for her to start with (though we've got a cyborg villain and other high-tech goodness, so it might exist somewhere), and I don't think there are any robots like that in any pokemon canon so far. So meshing her with existing lore might be kind of hard
    • We're probably going to have to look a lot closer at what she's capable of compared to your average human. The name of the RP is "We Are All Pokemon Trainers", even with all the crazy things that tends to happen, pokemon battles are supposed to be the go-to response for overcoming threats. If your naive robot girl ends up being able to fight and beat high-level pokemon on her own, that might be a bad sign, especially with the vaguely mentioned bad experiences.

    This Guy 481, well in short, the armbands are EXCEEDINGLY rare artifacts and come in three varieties - although the third is so rare there's only one of them as far as I know.

    • Pokemon to Human; allows any Pokemon to become a human version of themselves as long as they wear the armband.
    • Human to Pokemon; allows a human to shift into their Pokeform as long as they wear the armband.
    • Borked; this type of armband is capable of only partial transformations and usually resulting in Petting Zoo People to various degrees. (I doubt there is another one in the RP other than the one already owned by a PC who is often willing to share if asked nicely under proper circumstances)

    There are also and bracelets made by pure SCIENCE! by a certain Spark-y PC that function for specific characters in the in a similar fashion to the first two types of armband. But be aware that both armband and bracelets are not something brand spanking new players' PCs can get there hands on. You would need to be part of the RP for a bit before we'd let you ask to even apply to get one. Armbands are meant to be rare so new ones appear less than once a year now. However, some PCs keep hold of the current Armbands for personal reasons, but might be convinced to let your PC borrow an armband for a few hours before you need to return it. Bracelets may even be harder to get because of the exceedingly strict application process. I say "may" because only two PCs got them before easier alternative methods came about.

    Said alternative methods basically are lycanthropy; Pokeform viruses; and Supernatural powered, non-Legedndary mon inflicting a PC with a polymorph spell, these methods are easier to come by, but they would need planning and approval before it happens in the RP.

    This Guy: Well, I do like species-changing plots that get into questions like that, but I've got a few reservations that may or may not apply

    • I'm kind of iffy about someone starting out with a plot like that out of the gate or having a character mostly based around that. I feel like it's better to explore that kind of thing with an established character, then make that another facet of how they are, if that makes sense

    • Unown having that knowledge isn't really an 'inherent' thing, to my knowledge, so a human turned Unown would need to accrue that knowledge rather than get it upon transformation. Likewise, to my understanding, Unown aren't inherently linked, but they do link and lose individual traits when several of them congregate, to my knowledge.
