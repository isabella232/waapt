    (People still being accepted? I'm in~! Also, my character will only have 1 Pokmon till she finds the Pokmon that destroyed her life eight years ago. Pyrrhic Pyroar)

    Name: Pana

    Age: 19

    Gender: Female

    Description: 5'4", purple dyed hair with right eye covered, giving her the emo look. Always wears either black or red clothing, her only two favorite colors.

    Origin: Kanto

    Backstory: Pana grew up within a town that was always excited, always having fun and simply never letting anything stop happiness. Everyone knew each other, battles were fought every hour, and laughter was shared. Though, one event changed all of that... and made Pana who she is today.

    It was during her 11th birthday, a day where her parents thought it was time for her to get her first pokemon. They brought her to their local Professor and she was able to pick out a Squirtle to call her own. The Squirtle was nicknamed Squit, due to her unable to truly pronounce its name correctly and thus, kept it. The day went by greatly till night time came along. An unknown Pokmon came through the town, destroying everything in its path. Families were destroyed and children left alone. She was one of the children, who witnessed this strange Pokmon strangle her parents after defeating their pokemon within seconds.

    This one Pokmon, it was a pokemon that she has never seen in her town before. It was a Pokmon with three heads, each one breathing fire and burning down everything. So much fire, so many screams of both people and Pokmon burning. She was able to hide, able to remain out of sight till the aftermath. Since that day, she left the remains of her town and went to find the Pokmon, to get revenge.

    After a few years of wandering around, she learned the Pokemon was named Hydreigon. She learned what types it was, even got to meet one that was nice. With this knowledge, she went forth through each region, battling trainers every where she could find the.

    Though she is furious towards Hydreigon, she is able to make friends with ease and is a nice person once people get to know about her.

    Team

    Species: Squirtle

    Name: Squit

    Personality: Squit is Pana's most trusted Pokmon and a friend to her. She is more open to Squit than anyone else and Squit is always protective around her. Just like her, Squit is determined to hunt down the Pokmon that destroyed their home and family.

    (Time Zone is EST, -5)
