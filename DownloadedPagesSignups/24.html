    Bronwin the female Hoppip
    Bio: Arlyles first Pokémon, met at a PokéSpot on vacation. Protective of Arlyle, hates his mom, and wishes hed look after himself better. Shes frenetic and stubborn, and if she were human shed be a revolutionary in a first-world country.

    Sertorius the male Gligar
    Bio: Sertorius saw Arlyle at a PokéSpot, silently clung to his hair for five minutes, and then spit in his ear. Ever since hes been a fixture on the back of his trainers head. His childishness grates on everyone but Arlyle, who serenely lets him nap in his coat. Surprisingly profane when he feels like talking.

    Henry the Dusknoir
    Bio: Arlyles moms most trusted mon, who she sent as backup, with instructions not to use unless in danger. He knows a mountain of moves, understands Arlyle well from playing with him as a child, and can drive stick. A little imperious and controlling.     Trainer's name: Renard "Ren" Kaleb Clavis

    Age: Young Adult, between 25-30

    Gender: Male

    Appearance: Ren is a big, but not overweight, man with a height of around 6 feet. Ren has short, dark brown-almost black hair usually combed back. Ren has dark green eyes and wears glasses do to astigmatism. Ren prefers dark colored clothes and to dress smartly, wearing vests over his shirts with a pair of matching pants and sensible shoes and occasionally, a matching hat. Ren's left leg is heavily scarred do to a riding accident in his teens and carries a collapsible cane with him for extra balance when needed.

    Any ties to an organization: Ren is often commissioned by various people, groups, and organizations of varying degrees of importance to design and produce everything from clip art to magazine ads to the outfits to be worn by families at their holiday parties.

    Region of origin: Born in Goldenrod City, Raised in Celadon City, Attend college in La Rousse City. Currently lives in Castelia City.

    Backstory: Quirky, hammy, a bit snarky, and a fan of pop culture, Renard Valenum Clavis is the son of Erik Tobias Clavis and Clara Sophia Desmond Clavis and younger brother to Martin Reese Clavis.

    Ren grew up in comfort due to him coming from a long line of highly successful lawyers and business men. From a young age Ren showed an aptitude for the arts. Ren's parents ecouraged Ren and his talent (Martin was already well on his way on on becoming an excellent business man). In his teens, Ren had a bad accident while riding a Rapidash, the aftermath of which left Ren with his left leg heavily scared and a trick ankle. Ren now walks with a cane in cold and/or stormy weather or whenever he needs extra support because of this. Ren parents, wanting to make sure Ren would be able to provide for himself, allowed Ren to attend the La Rousse College of Art and Technology.

    During his third year of college, Ren developed a love of pokemon coordinating. And opted to be come a proffesional coordinator. Ren finished is B As in graphic design and fashion design - the latter of which would serve him well in his coordinator carreer. After a few years of running The Johoto, Hoenn, and Sinnoh contest circuts, Ren started to gain reconition as a coordinator; known for his pokemon highly visual apppeals and designing and making accessories for his pokemon. Ren has since branched off from contests and is currently making a name for himself in pokemon musicals. Ren currently resides in Castelia City.
