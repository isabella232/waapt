    2564 Umbramatic30th Dec 2015 01:24:55 AM from WAAPT or PEFE Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: In another castle

    Alright, we talked a bit in the chatroom and... Unfortunately, you have a lot of problems. Namely:

    • As Corvus mentioned you don't have the required indicators you read the essays, which... probably explains a lot of the other problems if you didn't.

    • The armor/bowgun aren't particularly problematic in and of themselves but stick out like a sore thumb as his default gear in the RP's setting, and the "uniqeness" bit of it is eyebrow-raising, like you're trying TOO hard to make him stand out - which is a bad way to go, and even noted as such early in the OP.

    • It should be noted that Modern!Ransei's status is currently ambiguous and we're still not sure how/if to un-ambiguize it, so that isn't gonna fly as his hometown, especially the way you're handling it - we're not going to let newbies worldbuild to that level as they're signing up. (Also, Aurora/Dragnor/etc. are treated as countries in RP canon, not cities.)

    • Pretty much everything about the Hunter's Guild, particularly the name, being a high ranking one, the 'only good one' thing, and how it's supposed to 'protect People and Pokemon from the wild mons,' especially with how being a mercenary group doing that doesn't seem like it'd end well. Overall it's confusing, worrisome, doesn't really fit the RP's universe, and definitely not the sort of thing we'd let a new player introduce right out.

    • The Snivy's coloration - Pokemon with non-Shiny alternate coloration aren't unprecedented in the RP, but there's always a reasonable in-universe justification whereas this Snivy has zilch.

    • The class system may or may not be a problem - if it's just for flavor it's probably fine, if rather out-of-place but if there's a deeper meaning to it you're keeping quiet to us about that raises a lot of alarm bells.

    • Back on the note of the Hunter's Guild... Being a high ranking mercenary at age 14 is a bit of a stretch, and kinda reeks of trying to be overpowered. (Also, not really a problem per se but on the note of stretching 5'8 seems a bit tall for a 14-year old, though someone else correct me if I'm wrong since I'm a 5'9 20-something).

    • Also, you have a lot of grammar and spelling issues you need to be more careful about.

    Overall... This whole signup reads like it was meant for another RP - like, one of Pokemon crossed over with some other JRPG series or something. This RP has no shortage of crossover elements, yes, but ultimately it's rooted most in the main series games and things directly based off their world (certain spinoff games, the anime, Adventures/Special, etc) and your signup as is doesn't really gel with that.

    Please read the required essays and other things about the RP and its universe linked in the OP of this thread and read them all very carefully to see if this is the sort of RP you're willing to work with; if so, I and/or the others can help you overhaul your signup to be a better fit, so please be reasonable and cooperative.
